  <p>“明虾”又称作“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%96%91%E8%8A%82%E8%99%BE.md#1">斑节虾</a>”，其特征就是身上带有横条花纹，体型大的明虾在日本料理中，不论是在寿司还是炸虾天妇罗料理中，都是不可或缺的食材，而新鲜的明虾作为生鱼片或沙拉更能品尝到虾的鲜味与甜味。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10685921" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5c4d97ff1cca21203701f7a5adb42819.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10685921 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5c4d97ff1cca21203701f7a5adb42819-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">新鲜Q弹的大明虾搭配微辣的酸菜，带点酸酸辣辣的口感，相当开胃。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <h2>【材料】</h2>  <p><strong>食材：</strong><br />  草虾 6支<br />  酸菜 20克<br />  蒜末 5克<br />  姜末 5克<br />  辣椒末 5克<br />  锅巴 6片</p>
  <p><strong>调味料：</strong><br />  酱油 1茶匙<br />  糖 1茶匙<br />  花椒油 少许<br />  辣椒油 少许<br />  胡椒粉 少许<br />  太白粉<br />  米酒</p>
  <h2>【作法】</h2>  <p>1.将蒜、姜、辣椒、酸菜切成末，明虾剥壳后去除泥肠，背部划刀，加太白粉及米酒腌渍备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10685925" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/f87f8cf5232b73e4df481a3ca5f6b832.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10685925 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/f87f8cf5232b73e4df481a3ca5f6b832-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">明虾剥壳后去除泥肠。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2.将虾入锅川烫，不煮到全熟，关火用余温泡到全熟。</p>
  <p>3.锅里倒入一点点油，加入蒜末、姜末、辣椒末爆香，再加入酸菜末拌炒，之后加入糖、胡椒粉、酱油调味，再来拌入川烫好的虾，最后淋上花椒油、辣椒油。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10685924" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/98b4565108fc4fbb4d0307ada9f5d136.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10685924 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/98b4565108fc4fbb4d0307ada9f5d136-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加入蒜末、姜末、辣椒末爆香。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10685923" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/987792206b7ddfc85bb39b1167f04a86.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10685923 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/987792206b7ddfc85bb39b1167f04a86-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">爆香调味后拌入虾球（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4.炸锅巴垫底，将虾摆盘上桌。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10685922" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/cd997a9f9c5be0b334bf4d9681d8f002.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10685922 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/cd997a9f9c5be0b334bf4d9681d8f002-450x446.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="446" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">炸锅巴垫底再将虾摆盘。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <h2>贴心小提醒：</h2>  <p>1. 明虾价钱高，虾头、虾壳不要丢弃，可以拿来熬汤。</p>
  <p>2. 挑虾重点看虾头，如看见虾头里绿绿的，表示可能有用药，须斟酌使用。</p>
  <p>3. 炸锅巴的油温一定要高，至少180度，锅巴才会酥透膨涨。</p>
