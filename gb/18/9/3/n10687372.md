<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>TGC 2018 A/W 铃木爱理及渡边直美等众星出席</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903132610100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>前℃-ute成员铃木爱理于TGC 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月04日讯】日本年度最大的时尚周活动“第27届TOKYO GIRLS COLLECTION 2018 A/W”（简称TGC 2018 A/W）9月1日于埼玉超级竞技场举行，众星云集。</p>
<p>“マイナビ presents 第27回 东京ガールズコレクション 2018 AUTUMN／WINTER”是结合时尚及音乐的大型活动，本次的活动邀请了上百名模特儿，如<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E6%9D%A1%E5%BD%A9%E6%9C%AA.md#1">中条彩未</a>、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B7%9D%E4%BC%98%E7%88%B1.md#1">新川优爱</a>、池田依来沙（池田エライザ）等人登台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10687685" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903131435100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10687685" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903131435100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E6%9D%A1%E5%BD%A9%E6%9C%AA.md#1">中条彩未</a>于TGC 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10687687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903131413100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10687687" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903131413100707-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">池田依来沙及<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B7%9D%E4%BC%98%E7%88%B1.md#1">新川优爱</a>于TGC 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>活动还邀来韩国人气女团TWICE、日本人气组合“柚子”、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A1%E8%BE%B9%E7%9B%B4%E7%BE%8E.md#1">渡边直美</a>、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B3%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D%E6%A6%89%E5%9D%8246.md#1">平假名榉坂46</a>的队长佐佐木久美、成员高本彩花、小坂菜绪，以及GARNiDELiA成员メイリア等知名歌手和艺人参与。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10687688" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903131424100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10687688" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903131424100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A1%E8%BE%B9%E7%9B%B4%E7%BE%8E.md#1">渡边直美</a>于TGC 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据ATC Taiwan报导，乃木坂46因为正在举行巡演的仙台场演出，因此本次未能参与活动，但“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B3%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D%E6%A6%89%E5%9D%8246.md#1">平假名榉坂46</a>”（けやき坂46）共有三位成员出席。其中，曾经在部落格上透露想站上伸展台的佐佐木久美，这天也实现了愿望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10687678" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903125407100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10687678" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903125407100707-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“平假名榉坂46”成员佐佐木久美、小坂菜绪于TGC 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了实现愿望站上伸展台的佐佐木久美以绿色的长大衣登场，还有现担任时尚杂志《Seventeen》专属模特儿的小坂菜绪，也以桃色的毛开衫搭配紫色上衣及格子裙登台，又于“WEGO”舞台以褐色的渔夫帽配上高腰身的裙子出场。而高本彩花在就定位后，还于舞台上给观众飞吻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10687693" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903125423100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10687693" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903125423100707-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“平假名榉坂46”成员小坂菜绪及高本彩花于TGC 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10687692" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903125430100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10687692" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903125430100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“平假名榉坂46”成员高本彩花于TGC 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，去年在偶像团体℃-ute解散后单飞出道，并发行个人首张专辑《Do me a favor》，还于今年7月唱进日本武道馆的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%93%83%E6%9C%A8%E7%88%B1%E7%90%86.md#1">铃木爱理</a>，这天则是穿着紫色的飞行夹克，配上褐色贝雷帽及连身的格子裤裙登场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10687708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903132614100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10687708" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903132614100707-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">前℃-ute成员<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%93%83%E6%9C%A8%E7%88%B1%E7%90%86.md#1">铃木爱理</a>于TGC 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10687699" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903132621100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10687699" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/180903132621100707.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">前℃-ute成员铃木爱理于TGC 2018 A/W活动登台。（ATC Taiwan提供）</figcaption></figure>
