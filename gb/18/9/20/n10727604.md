<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>喜从天降！发射荷兰“花式大炮”现场超震撼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/b3d0fdc3992a079200a3e8b2f210ef56-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10610147 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-17-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月14日讯】中国传统新年时节会放<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9E%AD%E7%82%AE.md#1">鞭炮</a>，许多国家则会用烟火来庆祝，那么你知道在荷兰会放什么吗？传统上，荷兰农村新年时会放“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E5%BC%8F%E5%A4%A7%E7%82%AE.md#1">花式大炮</a>”（Carbid Schieten），这种外型看起来有点像小钢炮的装置，在抛物线射出<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9A%AE%E7%90%83.md#1">皮球</a>的同时发出巨大声响，非常有趣！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10820846" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/c30ae97869b1ba2a666ed429913a3322.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10820846 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/c30ae97869b1ba2a666ed429913a3322-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">新年除了放烟火，荷兰人还会发射一种传统的“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E5%BC%8F%E5%A4%A7%E7%82%AE.md#1">花式大炮</a>”。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10820848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/fdfe6e987f19ec69a73942ea8ca056f5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10820848 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/fdfe6e987f19ec69a73942ea8ca056f5-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">只要找来各种钢瓶还有大小适中的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9A%AE%E7%90%83.md#1">皮球</a>，几乎就准备就绪，非常方便。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10820849" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/da8c1486e6fd452a2fe2c9fe1b886da4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10820849 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/da8c1486e6fd452a2fe2c9fe1b886da4-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">钢瓶里加入灰石，然后点火加热，这样会产生化学反应，释放大量二氧化碳。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“花式大炮”的钢瓶其实是大型的牛奶罐，里头放入灰石（碳酸钙），然后把大小适中、刚好可以卡住牛奶瓶口（但不会掉进瓶子里）的皮球稍微压紧，接着加热牛奶罐，不一会儿，皮球就会“砰”地一声飞出去啰！</p>
<p>由于灰石加热后会释放出二氧化碳，原本牛奶瓶里的气体体积是固定的，突然间多了二氧化碳，但皮球将瓶口堵住，牛奶瓶里的气体压力越来越大，最后就把皮球射喷，类似摇晃酒瓶软木塞也会被射喷的道理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10820854" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/14b203df62f75498c823e7b3dee666b2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10820854 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/14b203df62f75498c823e7b3dee666b2-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">钢瓶里的压力变大，不久就会把皮球“砰”地一声射向高空。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10820853" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/392e0a15c3a03e6e590670544c67d47b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10820853 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/392e0a15c3a03e6e590670544c67d47b-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">发出声响又充满喜气，感觉和中国传统的放<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9E%AD%E7%82%AE.md#1">鞭炮</a>很像！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>通常一般花式大炮会稍微倾斜，这样皮球才能飞得又高又远，并且在天空划出一条优美的抛物线。而影片中的荷兰好友们，新年时节在自家院子里发射小型的花式大炮，但他们却把瓶身“直通通”地瞄准天空。</p>
<p>准备就绪点火，然后“砰”地一声，皮球飞走啦！大伙儿欢呼庆祝新年到来，结果下一秒皮球居然也是“直直落”地掉到小野餐桌，把啤酒给打翻了，真是出乎意料之外的“喜从天降”呀！看得大家既有趣又惊喜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10820859" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e41d204aee71fb860081536598cf8994.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10820859 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e41d204aee71fb860081536598cf8994-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大家摀住耳朵，钢瓶要“爆炸”啦！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中国传统<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%BF%87%E5%B9%B4.md#1">过年</a>也有放鞭炮的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B9%A0%E4%BF%97.md#1">习俗</a>，这项历史约流传了两千年。古时没有火药，人们砍竹子做成竹筒，火烧了竹筒产生爆裂声，用以驱除山魈恶鬼。到了唐代则将长竹竿从头到尾逐节燃烧，使之断续发出爆破声。后来火药发明出来，竹筒里装火药产生双重燃爆，声响更大。</p>
<p>到了现代，华人在传统节日、各类庆典和庙会活动仍保持放鞭炮的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B9%A0%E4%BF%97.md#1">习俗</a>。其实这些年俗活动，除了表达对天地与神祇的感谢与敬畏，也包含慎终追远的文化传承。例如：除夕前要大扫除，除旧布新，地板、墙面、窗櫺都清理干净，贴上书写吉祥语句的春联，呈现浓浓的年节气氛，然后燃放长串的大红爆竹，新年的序幕就此拉开来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10820875" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/cbc5fc37e9dfe6056cffe7f3155fdde3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10820875 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/cbc5fc37e9dfe6056cffe7f3155fdde3-600x333.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="333" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">真是有趣的活动，大家都在找寻皮球的踪迹。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10820879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/6905941d486253078cd353ec0af20502.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10820879 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/6905941d486253078cd353ec0af20502-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">忽然皮球霸气回落，而且正中啤酒“红心”，真是名符其实的“喜从天降”！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
