<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中秋亮起来 爱老人才艺大赛暨爱心餐会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/78c8c7259ac51ad342341136792ec28a-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>	由市长涂醒哲带领厨师们亲自为长者上菜，大家边看才艺表演边享受佳肴。（李撷璎／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月05日讯】（大纪元记者李撷璎台湾嘉义报导）<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%B1%B1%E5%9F%BA%E9%87%91%E4%BC%9A.md#1">华山基金会</a>以全心照顾三失的老人为志业，在中秋节前夕5日，邀请常年服务的沈阿嬷及散居嘉义县市各角落的240位独居弱势长者齐聚嘉义市明月圆婚宴会馆，尽情享受一年一次难忘的美食料理招待。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10692118" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/77cfa83c744d6c7e635a5cf807afe18e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10692118" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/77cfa83c744d6c7e635a5cf807afe18e-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">市长涂醒哲以正确喂食方法，亲喂103岁仁瑞美食。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>供应美食料理拥有900多位会员的的嘉义市厨师工会，在理事长许坤生的率领下，会员齐心把这场活动办得有声有色，许坤生不但是市府食安委员会的一员，还是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%83%AD%E5%BF%83%E5%85%AC%E7%9B%8A.md#1">热心公益</a>的领头羊，厨师工会与<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%B1%B1%E5%9F%BA%E9%87%91%E4%BC%9A.md#1">华山基金会</a>携手合作所办的这场爱老人才艺大赛暨<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%BF%83%E9%A4%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">爱心餐会</a>，老人在愉快的气氛下用餐、看表演，整个会馆笑声不断，中秋果真亮起来了。嘉义市长涂醒哲也到场和长者们同乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10692123" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/a0c868856aaaa3aa4fa4960a8037b387.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10692123" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/a0c868856aaaa3aa4fa4960a8037b387-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">市长涂醒哲非常感谢华山基金会及嘉义市厨师工会长期以来对于长者的关怀。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年表演特别结合传统故事；盘古开天、后羿射日、嫦娥奔月、玉兔祷药、吴刚伐桂、指月割耳、阿姆斯壮登陆月球及兰潭泛月等8个主题，期望能从自娱互娱中重建阿公阿嬷们的自信心与欢笑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10692132" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/7b15e7a0e93374bc38db04ccdeee7a69.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10692132" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/7b15e7a0e93374bc38db04ccdeee7a69-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">让长者们享用团圆餐宴的美食佳肴的同时，藉由表演走上舞台塑立信心，为大家带来热闹团圆的欢笑气氛。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>涂醒哲表示，非常感谢华山基金会及嘉义市厨师工会长期以来对于长者的关怀，每年举办<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%BA%86%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B.md#1">庆中秋</a>的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%BF%83%E9%A4%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">爱心餐会</a>及才艺表演活动，让长者们享用团圆餐宴的美食佳肴的同时，藉由表演走上舞台塑立信心，为大家带来热闹团圆的欢笑气氛。涂醒哲也热情邀请长者们一同参加9/15弥陀映月桥开通典礼，以及9/21中秋晚会活动，并预祝大家中秋佳节快乐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10692126" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/334efd2e205207fa1ce894327bdaa5b8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10692126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/334efd2e205207fa1ce894327bdaa5b8-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">华山基金会义工妥善安排阿公阿嬷享受美食。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10692128" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/84b8e0ad7e138bcacc50266a3b9d48c5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10692128" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/84b8e0ad7e138bcacc50266a3b9d48c5-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市厨师工会义厨工作队忙得不亦乐乎。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本次爱心餐会活动，席开30桌，各总铺师准备手路菜，理监事与会员们踊跃捐鸡捐鱼提供食材，一起为在地高龄长者贡献爱与关怀。现场更有高龄103岁长者前来参加。由涂醒哲带领厨师们亲自为长者上菜，大家边看才艺表演边享受佳肴，场面非常温馨热闹。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_10692134" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/25134ff6e68e77aaab87976c6364ddc7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10692134" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/25134ff6e68e77aaab87976c6364ddc7-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">市长涂醒哲等人与103岁人瑞（1排右）合照。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10692136" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/62388e1ef26f7cb3328100ad383266c6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10692136" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/62388e1ef26f7cb3328100ad383266c6-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市厨师工会理事长免费提供自家的明月婚宴会馆，又免费招待弱势老长辈吃一顿中秋美食。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10692137" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/24789c5be498abcaffdfdcca104b1253.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10692137 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/24789c5be498abcaffdfdcca104b1253-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">嘉义市厨师工会会员中也训练出擅长歌唱的表演者。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
