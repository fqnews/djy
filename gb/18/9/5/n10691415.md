<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>“缤纷胡麻贡寮鲍”搭配特调和风酱汁</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-26-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>缤纷胡麻贡寮鲍。（影片截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10611616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月27日讯】<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%8D%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲍鱼</a>，古称鳆、鳆鱼、海耳，又名镜面鱼、九孔螺、明目鱼，鲍鱼栖息于石质河岸，以藻类为食。</p>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%8D%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲍鱼</a>在明清时代即被列为八珍之一，鲍鱼含有丰富的球蛋白和钙、铁、碘、维生素A等营养元素。鲍鱼种类甚多，有少部分鲍鱼新鲜急冻，亦有制成罐头，或晒制成干货。</p>
<p>鲍鱼是中国传统的名贵食材，是山珍海味中的一种。新朝王莽爱吃鲍鱼，曹操也喜欢吃鲍鱼，曹操死后，曹植曾向臧霸要200只鲍鱼来祭祀。</p>
<p>今天这道“缤纷<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E9%BA%BB.md#1">胡麻</a>贡寮鲍”，先将活鲍鱼用刷子刷洗其壳后，先汆烫约2分钟，再将鲍鱼肉整粒挖出，并以卤汁卤约30分钟，卤汁要淡味，避免鲍鱼缩水使肉质变硬，如果能再将鲍鱼与卤汁一起冰一晚，会更入味、好吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10691465" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-26.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10691465" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-26-450x407.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="407" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">缤纷<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E9%BA%BB.md#1">胡麻</a>贡寮鲍。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【准备材料】</h2>
<p><strong>●食材：</strong><br />
贡寮鲍 500g<br />
绿卷须<br />
红卷莴苣<br />
新鲜<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E8%8A%B1%E6%9E%9C.md#1">无花果</a></p>
<p><strong>●调味料：</strong><br />
柴鱼酱油<br />
葱<br />
姜<br />
冰糖<br />
胡麻酱<br />
和风酱</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1. 将鲍鱼洗净，汆烫约2分钟捞起，并挖出鲍鱼肉、去除口器。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10691466" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10691466" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2-24-450x407.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="407" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将鲍鱼洗净，汆烫约2分钟捞起。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10691464" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10691464" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5-13-450x413.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="413" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">挖出鲍鱼肉、去除口器。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 放入由柴鱼酱油、葱、姜及冰糖调成的卤汁，用小火卤渍30分钟。<br />
3. 将卤好的鲍鱼切片摆盘，以<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E8%8A%B1%E6%9E%9C.md#1">无花果</a>、生菜装饰。<br />
4. 淋酱使用胡麻酱、和风酱以1：3调制而成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10691467" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3-22.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10691467" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3-22-450x413.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="413" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲍鱼以柴鱼酱油、葱、姜等调成的卤汁，小火卤30分钟。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10691468" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10691468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-14-450x413.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="413" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将卤好的鲍鱼切片摆盘，以无花果、生菜装饰。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
