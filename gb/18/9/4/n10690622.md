<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【折纸】挂钩花雨伞</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1187-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（摄影／陈柏颖）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>美丽的洋伞很吸引路人的目光。台湾美浓的纸伞保有传统做法，但工序繁复，纸张折一折做成的小纸伞，迷你又可爱，很适合作为假日午后陪小朋友成长的亲子活动。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>材料：</strong>正方形纸张、长方形纸张、剪刀、白胶。</p>
<p><strong>做法：</strong></p>
<p>1、将正方形纸张正面朝上对折，再对折1次。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1141.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695767 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1141-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1143.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695768 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1143-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>2、将上图中的角A立起，往下压成平面（如下图）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1144.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695769 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1144-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1146.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695770 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1146-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>3、翻面，重复步骤2将正方形打开，往下压平成三角形。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1149.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695771 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1149-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1151.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695772 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1151-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>4、将下图中的角C、丙边往中央线翻折，使丙边对齐乙边，再摊开，得折线戊（如下图）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1153.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695773 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1153-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1154.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695774 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1154-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1155.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695775 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1155-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>5、以戊折线为基准，将角C、丙边打开向右翻折，压平。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1158.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695776 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1158-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1159.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695777 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1159-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>6、将上图中的角C往上翻折，使新三角形的底边与整体大三角形的底边对齐，再将此新三角形往内侧翻折，再将丙线右侧的三角形往左侧翻折使其重叠。（如下图）</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1160.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695778 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1160-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1161.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695779 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1161-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1163.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695780 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1163-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1164.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695781 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1164-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>7、重复步骤4到步骤6的动作，将其余3个边逐一折好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10695785" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1180.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10695785 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1180-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">全部折好的样子。</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1176.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695782 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1176-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1177.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695783 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1177-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1179.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695784 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1179-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>8、拿剪刀将伞顶端约0.5公分处剪开。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1182.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695786 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1182-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>9、取长方形纸张，此为<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%8A%98%E7%BA%B8.md#1">折纸</a>伞正方形纸张的一半大，在一长边涂上胶水或白胶，另一边用双手将其卷起成细细的纸卷后黏合。用手指将一端卷弯成钩状（如下图）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1183.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695787 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1183-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>10、将上图中的支架中间段涂上白胶，穿过伞身，再用双手各手指协助固定伞与支架，约1分钟后，白胶稍干，将伞放在桌上待干，一把有挂勾的花雨伞就完成啰。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1184.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695788 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1184-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1185.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10695765 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1185-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_10695766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1188.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10695766 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/IMG_1188-600x400.jpg" alt="完成。" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">完成。</figcaption></figure>
