  <figure id="attachment_10747486" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/58cb7c27bf03ec9c4409ec7def1194f1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10747486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/58cb7c27bf03ec9c4409ec7def1194f1-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">旧城国小学生担任<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E4%BD%BE%E8%88%9E.md#1">八佾舞</a>生。（方金媛／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10747484" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5f115161113d6d2c6b1b90390e2a92be.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10747484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5f115161113d6d2c6b1b90390e2a92be-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鼎金国中学生担任乐生。 （高雄市立历史博物馆提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>释奠礼在庄严的钟鼓乐声中，举行启扉、迎神、进馔、上香、献帛、献爵、献花、饮褔、受胙及送神等仪节，仪式结束后，民众拔“智慧毛”、过“聪（葱）门”、领“智慧糕”，象征聪明伶俐、满腹墨水，事事如意。</p>
  <p>今年释奠典礼由左营旧城国小担任<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%AB%E4%BD%BE%E8%88%9E.md#1">八佾舞</a>生、鼎金国中担任麾生、乐生及歌生；左营高中担任祭典通赞、亚赞、礼生，共近250位师生组成仪礼队伍，高市府各级长官及校长分别担任正献官、分献官、陪祭官，代表高雄市政府、全体教职员生对万世师表<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AD%94%E5%AD%90.md#1">孔子</a>最高的敬意。现场同时有许多家长带领孩童观礼，透过<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%A5%AD%E5%AD%94.md#1">祭孔</a>大典，传承珍贵的中华传统文化，也让民众认识尊师重道的精神。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10747488" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5fbc79727efdf36d4de44b9368a5fc16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10747488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5fbc79727efdf36d4de44b9368a5fc16-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">送神。 （方金媛／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>高雄市自1980年孔子诞辰2530周年起举办祭孔释奠典礼，至今已38届，旧城国小师生从首届担任八佾舞生至今，几乎年年出席，指导老师曾望超与父亲曾宏一，以保存及发扬此项仪礼为使命，是八佾舞传承的重要推手。</p>
  <p>曾望超说，过去，家世清白的男生才有资格担任八佾舞生，现在八佾舞已融入学校课程中，每位5、6年级学生都有机会练习。因八佾舞严肃庄重，学生必须静心才能学好，并得经过一个多月的练习，才能参与每年一度的祭典盛会。◇</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10747485" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/a405e2b5a069829ab266803ea7c5f570.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10747485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/a405e2b5a069829ab266803ea7c5f570-450x274.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="274" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">民众拔象征智慧的牛毛笔。 （方金媛／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10747487" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/714bb51cbe52012b8a1aa0f534dc83a2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10747487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/714bb51cbe52012b8a1aa0f534dc83a2-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">过聪 （葱）门。（方金媛／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>责任编辑：昱作</p>
