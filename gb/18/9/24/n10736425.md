<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>PGA巡回锦标赛 伍兹封王终结冠军荒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1038900198-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>伍兹（右）。(Photo by Kevin C. Cox/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年09月24日讯】（大纪元记者尚上礼综合报导）美国高球名将、前世界球王老虎<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%BC%8D%E5%85%B9.md#1">伍兹</a>（Tiger Woods），在巡回锦标赛（Tour Championship）最后一轮缴出71杆，以4天269杆封王，拿下5年多来首座美巡赛<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%86%A0%E5%86%9B.md#1">冠军</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10736447" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1038900282.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10736447" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1038900282-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Tiger Woods of the United States poses with the trophy after winning the TOUR Championship at East Lake Golf Club(Photo by Sam Greenwood/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10736448" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1038905080.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10736448" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1038905080-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Tiger Woods of the United States celebrates making a par on the 18th green to win the TOUR Championship at East Lake Golf Club (Photo by Tim Bradbury/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10736445" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1038619908.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10736445" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/GettyImages-1038619908-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Tiger Woods of the United States plays his shot from the third tee during the final round of the TOUR Championship at East Lake Golf Club(Photo by Kevin C. Cox/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>现年42岁的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%BC%8D%E5%85%B9.md#1">伍兹</a>上一次夺冠是在2013年的普利司通邀请赛，如今再夺冠成就职业生涯第80座<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%86%A0%E5%86%9B.md#1">冠军</a>，仅次美国已故传奇老将史尼德（Sam Snead）创下的82冠历史纪录。伍兹坦言：“在最后一洞差点哭出来，我强忍着泪水打完的。”</p>
<p>伍兹首轮第18洞抓下老鹰并列领先，次轮吞下双柏忌后仍勇保第一，第3轮更是焕发青春，前7洞抓下6鸟，把对手全抛在后头，独居领先。最后一轮，虽缴出高于标准杆1杆的71杆，仍以2杆的优势封王。</p>
<p>伍兹胜出后举臂庆祝，果岭边数千名粉丝欢声雷动。播报员感叹：“我以为我永远再看不到这个画面，所有球迷请注意，老虎伍兹又是冠军了！”</p>
<p>老虎伍兹这几年由于背部的伤势，他甚至无法像正常人一样好好坐着跟走路，去年才因背伤几乎被迫退休，排名最惨时落到1199名。在动了4次背部手术，今年年初重返PGA赛场后，状态越来越好。</p>
<p>也曾经是世界球王的米克森表示，对于生涯已拥有14座大赛冠军的伍兹再拿下冠军，他从未有过怀疑，“老虎在非常困难的高尔夫球场地也都打得很好，我们几乎都有期待…只是时间问题。”两人11月23日将在拉斯维加斯一对一单挑，采赢者全拿方式，获胜者将独得900万美元。</p>
