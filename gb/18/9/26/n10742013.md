  <h2><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>成功的餐饮业典范</strong></span></h2>  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/candeloris.md#1">Candelori’s</a>餐厅是意大利第二代移民Ross亲自筹建的，经营者Ross，从小受家庭的熏陶和培养，领悟到美食的精粹，建立了独自的经营理念。经过十九年的辛勤经营，成为了意大利裔人在澳洲成功经营餐饮业的典范，已经连续三年荣获三顶厨师帽的最高荣誉。</p>
  <p>Ross有着甜美的童年，小时候，每天都是母亲烹调的香味从厨房飘出，唤醒了梦中熟睡的他。母亲煮的咖啡香，面条香，鱼香，肉香，母亲煮什么都特别美味，母亲就像一个魔术师一样，无论什么食材到她手里都会变成可口的美食。对美食的鉴赏伴随着Ross的成长。Ross的母亲教导他说：“烹调在于精，小小的烹调细节会改变食品的口味”，“注重细节”奠定了Ross今天的成就。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10742205" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/98d82e6b5317311a6387319a31adb273.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10742205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/98d82e6b5317311a6387319a31adb273-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>独具慧眼 成功之人必有过人之処</strong></span></h2>  <p>Ross不仅有对食品的高标准要求，他还有独到的眼光。十九年前，Ross在Smithfield的工业区投资了三百万打造了豪华的Candelori’s餐厅，并用家族的名字予以命名。Ross坚信用家族的名字更能激发自己的勤奋和努力，不允许自己有半点的懈怠。正如Ross所料，在偏远远离闹市的Smithfield，很多大企业公司老板和管理高层不仅成了他的客户，还为他带来了澳洲各地的商贾名流，过世的澳洲首富纸箱大王Richard Pratt也曾是Candelori’s的常客。Richard在世时，每周三次从墨尔本特地赶到Candelori’s用餐。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10742209" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-38.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10742209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-38-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>Ross精心尽力打造的Candelori’s餐厅，要求的是一流的食材和品质，进口最好的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8E%9F%E4%BA%A7%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84%E9%85%92.md#1">原产意大利葡萄酒</a>。Candelori’s只采购未加工的食品原料，所有的意大利面，酱料，面包均由厨师当日准备，烹调。Candelori’s海鲜来自澳洲各地，许多是渔民直接空运到悉尼，减少了中间环节，海鲜品质更高。Ross要求Candelori’s每道菜都要做到极致和提供一流的服务。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10742208" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ac87499618185fd6cf5c1435302269ec.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10742208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/ac87499618185fd6cf5c1435302269ec-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10742210" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3-63.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10742210" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3-63-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>现在Ross和他的儿子Christian一起经营这家餐厅，Ross充满激情的说：我们热爱美食，喜欢服务客人，喜欢和朋友做伴，客人的满足是我们最好的回馈。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>品尝</strong><strong>Candelori’s</strong><strong>垂涎欲滴的意大利美食</strong></span></h2>  <p>Candelori餐厅的菜单体现了正宗意大利美食，菜品表现出传统家庭的朴实与真诚的风格。当日新鲜制作的面包，各种木炭烤比萨饼，各式意面令人垂涎欲滴。Candelori按每个季节更换菜单，为来宾供应时令，新鲜美味的佳肴。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10742203" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/79072e5e3e2ae2e5b7af58b38e5622e8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10742203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/79072e5e3e2ae2e5b7af58b38e5622e8-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>餐前食品Wood Fired Grissini是把用烤比萨饼面团搓手指条，放入意大利的木炭烤炉烘烤，待烤至酥黄芳香时，撒上海盐，香料和辣椒碎，再淋上低温榨取的高品质橄榄油。等不及了吧！</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10742201" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9ccde81e40e187277edb026a80745e25.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10742201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/9ccde81e40e187277edb026a80745e25-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>意面是Candelori的看家食品，各种不同的意面全是厨师们自己制作，例如海鲜Linguine和墨汁Tagliolini都是华人的最爱。海鲜Linguine味道赋予了丰富的口感，柔软，滋润而微辣，新鲜的老虎虾，青口，覆盖半个盘子的琵琶虾令人不可抵挡。这道意面完美到不需要再加入任何的调味料。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10742202" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/869e374735dbe23bb485c59b3108b45c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10742202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/869e374735dbe23bb485c59b3108b45c-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>墨汁Tagliolini是用墨鱼的墨汁和面调和压成像阳春面的细条，是意大利特有的佳肴，其独特的口味是其它品种意面不可代替的。于炸新鲜带子和意大利蔬菜Cime Di Rapa一起烹调是完美的配搭。且不可错过品尝墨汁Tagliolini的香浓。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10742207" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5e25ec6ff571567ca7fbd9db4f910a42.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10742207" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5e25ec6ff571567ca7fbd9db4f910a42-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>甜品是榛果威化巧克力，味道柔软香甜，薄薄的一层巧克力和碎果仁恰到好处，爽口酥脆。来Candelori用餐，切记要预订。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10742211" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2-83.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10742211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2-83-450x453.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10742212" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-114.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10742212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-114-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（商家提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>（工商稿）</p>
