<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>浪漫水果玫瑰塔 简单几步骤华丽上桌</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2018-09-06_160940-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>季节性的缤纷水果为主，打造浪漫满分的玫瑰造型的水果玫瑰塔吧！（影片截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10611616 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月15日讯】想吃的健康又能养颜美容的甜点，哪里找？快试试以季节性的缤纷<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>为主，打造浪漫满分的玫瑰造型的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C%E7%8E%AB%E7%91%B0%E5%A1%94.md#1">水果玫瑰塔</a>吧！简单几步骤，居家甜点DIY就能轻松上手。</p>
<p>完成后，别忘了！放进冰箱冰镇一下。在炎热的季节里，尝一块冰凉<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>塔，那口感真的太幸福啦！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10694853" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2018-09-06_160940.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10694853 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2018-09-06_160940-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">打造浪漫满分的玫瑰造型的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C%E7%8E%AB%E7%91%B0%E5%A1%94.md#1">水果玫瑰塔</a>吧！（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>【食材准备】</strong></h2>
<p>新鲜水果（芒果、水蜜桃、李子、奇异果、草莓）<br />
全麦饼干粉<br />
奶油<br />
奶油起司 8盎司（225克）<br />
蔗糖 1/2杯<br />
香草精 1/2汤匙<br />
柠檬皮 1/2茶匙</p>
<h2><strong>【作法】</strong></h2>
<p>1.先将新鲜水果：芒果、水蜜桃、李子、奇异果、草莓切片；并将新鲜水果片，横向排列，再从其中一端向内卷到底端，将呈现玫瑰花的形状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10694857" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2018-09-06_160714.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10694857 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2018-09-06_160714-450x355.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="355" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">先将新鲜水果切片，再从其中一端向内卷到底端。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.<strong>饼皮制作</strong>：全麦饼干粉、奶油均匀搅拌后，倒入派盘中，使用汤匙将饼皮均匀按压。</p>
<p>3.<strong>馅料制作</strong>：依序加入奶油起司、蔗糖、香草精、柠檬皮，使用搅拌器将容器中食材均匀搅拌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10694859" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2018-09-06_160831.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10694859 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2018-09-06_160831-450x437.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="437" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">馅料制作，使用搅拌器将容器中食材均匀搅拌。（授权影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.接着，用刮勺将奶油均匀铺在饼皮上。</p>
<p>5.最后，将水果花摆入，美丽的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C%E5%A1%94.md#1">水果塔</a>就完成啰！</p>
