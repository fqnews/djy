<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>缤纷甜甜圈 午后时光增添小确幸</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6-7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>百变千层酥皮，配上疗愈酱料和梦幻摆设的缤纷甜甜圈，为难得的午后时光增添好心情吧！（影片截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10611616 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年11月13日讯】最近繁忙的工作或课业，一波接一波来袭，心情也受影响了吗？不妨放松一下，快来试试诱人的百变千层<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%85%A5%E7%9A%AE.md#1">酥皮</a>，配上疗愈酱料和梦幻摆设的缤纷<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%94%9C%E5%9C%88.md#1">甜甜圈</a>，为难得的午后时光增添好心情吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10843254" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6-7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10843254 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/6-7-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">百变千层<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%85%A5%E7%9A%AE.md#1">酥皮</a>，配上疗愈酱料和梦幻摆设的缤纷<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%94%9C%E5%9C%88.md#1">甜甜圈</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【食材准备】</h2>
<p>酥皮 2张<br />
奶油 1/2杯<br />
香蕉布丁馅<br />
巧克力酱<br />
草莓糖霜<br />
装饰小点心</p>
<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.酥皮均匀刷上奶油。</p>
<p>2.将酥皮分成三等分，下半部分往中间折，并刷上奶油，最后将上半部分往中间折，呈现长条状。</p>
<p>3.使用圆形模具进行按压，将旁边多余酥皮拿掉；接着，拿小型圆形模具，在圆的中心进行按压，并将中间酥皮去除。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10843248" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-134.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10843248 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-134-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">使用圆形模具进行按压。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10843250" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2-4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10843250 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/2-4-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">拿小型圆形模具，并将中间酥皮去除。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.将完成的甜甜圈下锅油炸，呈现金黄色浮起来，即可把油沥干，起锅啰！</p>
<p>5.香蕉布丁馅1杯适量的挤在甜甜圈的侧边，裹上巧克力酱或草莓糖霜上方可以随意摆上自己喜欢的装饰，独一无二的缤纷甜甜圈就完成啰！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10843251" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3-27.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10843251 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/3-27-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将完成的甜甜圈下锅油炸，呈现金黄色浮起来。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10843252" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-17.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10843252 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/4-17-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">裹上巧克力酱。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10843253" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5-15.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10843253 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/5-15-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">或者裹上草莓糖霜，并随意摆上自己喜欢的装饰。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
