  <figure id="attachment_10697201" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/20180907-Yilan-Zeng-Handong-104.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10697201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/20180907-Yilan-Zeng-Handong-104-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">文化部在国立传统艺术中心宜兰园区展示馆102室举办的“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E8%97%8F%E5%8C%BB%E5%AD%A6%E5%94%90%E5%8D%A1%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF.md#1">西藏医学唐卡艺术</a>特展”。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>文化部在展览期间也安排专题讲座及文化体验活动，邀请民众一同探索西藏医学的古老智慧与文化意涵，详细资讯欢迎至西藏文化艺术节活动网站查询(https://tibetanfestival.moc.gov.tw )。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10697198" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/20180907-Yilan-Zeng-Handong-102.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10697198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/20180907-Yilan-Zeng-Handong-102-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E8%8B%B1%E5%B3%B0.md#1">黄英峰</a>馆长导览解说。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>黄英峰馆长表示，西藏医药学至今已有二千多年的历史，在其民族医疗基础上，吸收汉医的理论与实践精神，并融合印度阿育吠陀医学及尼泊尔、阿拉伯医学的概念，逐步形成独特的医学体系。而唐卡是一种卷轴画，为藏族独具特色的绘画艺术形式，题材包罗万象，包括宗教、医药、天文历算等。本次展出的医学唐卡是将生硬难懂的“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9B%9B%E9%83%A8%E5%8C%BB%E5%85%B8.md#1">四部医典</a>”变成一幅幅通俗易懂的连环漫画。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10697199" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/20180907-Yilan-Zeng-Handong-103.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10697199" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/20180907-Yilan-Zeng-Handong-103-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">唐卡画师现场绘制。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>完整的西藏医学唐卡共有80幅，每一幅唐卡中，小图少则十几个，最多达一百六十多个，80幅唐卡中将近有九千多个小图，描述西藏医学理论，包括药物学、生理学、治疗学，以及临床病症、病因与诊断的现象，这套创意十足的医学唐卡，历经历史洗礼流传至今。◇</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10697202" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/20180907-Yilan-Zeng-Handong-105.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10697202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/20180907-Yilan-Zeng-Handong-105-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“西藏医学唐卡艺术特展”展品之一。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>责任编辑：昱作</p>
