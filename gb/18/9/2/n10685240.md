<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>绩优企业汇聚多伦多台商会2018首届就业博览会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/7-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>记者采访台商会徐弘益会长。
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月03日讯】<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A%E5%8F%B0%E5%95%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">多伦多台商会</a>举办的2018首届<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%B1%E4%B8%9A%E5%8D%9A%E8%A7%88%E4%BC%9A.md#1">就业博览会</a>（Job Fair），于8月31日在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E9%83%BD%E4%BC%9A%E5%B9%BF%E5%9C%BA.md#1">大都会广场</a>（Metro Square Mall）成功举行。<span lang="ZH-TW">台商会感谢各大企业参与摆设摊位招募人才，解答求职者疑问。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10685245" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10685245" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/1-6-450x273.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="273" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">招聘会现场。（主办方提供文图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div id="yui_3_16_0_ym19_1_1535771001978_14926">台商会称，首届<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%B1%E4%B8%9A%E5%8D%9A%E8%A7%88%E4%BC%9A.md#1">就业博览会</a>为求职者提供机会，充分了解了企业 的人才需求，并将履历表递交给企业。</div>
