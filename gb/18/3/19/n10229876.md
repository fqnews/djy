<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>PANDORA Shine 2018春季系列闪耀登场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/BEE_MINE_01_Crop_resize-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Bee Mine系列：搭配迷人蜜蜂主题饰品，令您的造型脱颖而出。(图片由PANDORA提供 )
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年03月19日讯】全球销量第三的珠宝商——丹麦国际珠宝品牌PANDORA，今年春季首度发表令人惊艳的2018全新PANDORA Shine系列珠宝。Shine系列为<ahref="/gb/tag/925%E9%93%B6.md#1">925银</a>材质外层镀18K金，由人手缀饰，至臻完美，是PANDORA最新的珍贵金属手工制作完美系列。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10229955" style="width: 284px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/01-RAYS_OF_SUNSHINE_05_CMYK_resize.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10229955" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/01-RAYS_OF_SUNSHINE_05_CMYK_resize-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="284" b="284" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">丹麦国际珠宝品牌PANDORA，今年春季首度发表令人惊艳的2018全新PANDORA Shine系列珠宝。(图片由PANDORA提供 )</figcaption></figure>
<p>《大纪元时报》近日获邀参加了最新PANDORA Shine系列珠宝的发表会，现在就跟大纪元记者一起来欣赏一下这个全新系列吧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10229932" style="width: 313px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/BEE_MINE_02_CMYK_resize.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10229932 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/BEE_MINE_02_CMYK_resize-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="313" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Bee Mine系列：搭配迷人蜜蜂主题饰品，令您的造型脱颖而出。(图片由PANDORA提供 )</figcaption></figure>
<p>全新PANDORA Shine系列珠宝，以PANDORA一贯工艺水准铸造而成，工序结合传统匠艺和当代科技，先于饰品的<ahref="/gb/tag/925%E9%93%B6.md#1">925银</a>核心表面镀上一层钯，再镀上一层深厚丰浓的18K金。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10229935" style="width: 297px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/BEE_MINE_08_CMYK_resize.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10229935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/BEE_MINE_08_CMYK_resize-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="297" b="297" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Bee Mine系列：搭配迷人蜜蜂主题饰品，令您的造型脱颖而出。(图片由PANDORA提供 )</figcaption></figure>
<p>PANDORA Shine散发的优美黄金色泽，彰显个性，让女性发挥创意尽情展现魅力，从内到外闪耀迷人光彩。PANDORA Shine非常适合创造引人注目的璀璨阳光外观，从串饰、手链、戒指、项链与吊坠及耳环，搭配Shine系列新款让你更加光彩照人 。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10229937" style="width: 319px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/BEE_MINE_10_CMYK_resize.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10229937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/BEE_MINE_10_CMYK_resize-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="319" b="319" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Bee Mine系列：搭配迷人蜜蜂主题饰品，令您的造型脱颖而出。(图片由PANDORA提供 )</figcaption></figure>
<p>作为Shine系列的延伸，Pandora还推出Bee Mine和Rays of Sunshine珠宝系列，这些珠宝系列仅在有限的时间内提供。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10229945" style="width: 294px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/RAYS_OF_SUNSHINE_03_CMYK_resize.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10229945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/RAYS_OF_SUNSHINE_03_CMYK_resize-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="294" b="294" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Rays of Sunshine系列：佩戴耀目迷人的太阳主题饰品，能突显您个性的每一面。 (图片由PANDORA提供 )</figcaption></figure>
<p>漂亮蜂姿，设计灵感则来源于蜂巢的几何美态，礼赞大自然和它的夺目图案。搭配迷人蜜蜂主题饰品，令您的造型脱颖而出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10229941" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/BEE_MINE_19_CMYK_resize.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10229941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/BEE_MINE_19_CMYK_resize-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Bee Mine系列：搭配迷人蜜蜂主题饰品，令您的造型脱颖而出。(图片由PANDORA提供 )</figcaption></figure>
<p>灿烂艳阳，佩戴耀目迷人的太阳主题饰品，能突显您个性的每一面。</p>
