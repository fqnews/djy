  <figure id="attachment_10878950" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/995dfef59ce1a0e9a62bc6f548f5774f.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10878950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/995dfef59ce1a0e9a62bc6f548f5774f-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2018云林软食力竞赛决赛第一名获奖者。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10878951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/bc54a3d862f0d46d5727eeb7325c1168.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10878951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/bc54a3d862f0d46d5727eeb7325c1168-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2018云林软食力竞赛决赛第二名获奖者。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10878952" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/f16dbe63361be0e143bb8fe4c91b49cc.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10878952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/f16dbe63361be0e143bb8fe4c91b49cc-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2018云林软食力竞赛决赛第三名获奖者。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10878953" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ceb7d7667e03f2da662e2ad4ffa56b9f.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10878953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ceb7d7667e03f2da662e2ad4ffa56b9f-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2018云林软食力竞赛决赛获佳作团队。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>秘书长黄玉霜表示，长者在牙齿及肠胃蠕动与食欲等各项机能退化下，食用的餐点必须利用流质、泥状、切碎、软食等方式特别烹调；为了提醒大家对于长辈专属餐食的重视，今天卫生局特别举办“软食力竞赛”，除了为长辈们打造适合进食的美味佳肴，更希望让参赛的长青食堂、社区团体、餐厅业者相互交流，共同精进软食力餐点的色、香、味，让长者们也能吃得<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%81%A5%E5%BA%B7.md#1">健康</a>美味又营养！</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10878954" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/40ddd913b2b6a864065b3d360fef105d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10878954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/40ddd913b2b6a864065b3d360fef105d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">参加竞赛的软食力餐点 色、香、味俱全。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10878956" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/5bbb6c9b726abdb808c0a342e17c7a72.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10878956" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/5bbb6c9b726abdb808c0a342e17c7a72-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">色、香、味俱全的软食力餐点。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>卫生局长吴昭军表示，云林县内已经有90余间长青食堂，为了让长辈们在吃饱的同时也吃得健康，已在东势乡成立“社区营养推广中心”，并把中心里的营养师能量带进各长青食堂，让长辈们的吃食能够更佳均衡。</p>
  <p>另外，卫生局也致力打造友善长辈的卫生单位，云林县辖内的20个卫生所在3年内全数通过<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E9%BE%84.md#1">高龄</a>友善健康照护机构认证2.0，而今年，台西卫生所更获得107年高龄友善健康照护机构典范选拔，希望借此让长辈得到更好的照顾。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10878946" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3ac86b0f6a866e759b22f701351634b0.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10878946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3ac86b0f6a866e759b22f701351634b0-600x348.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="348" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2018云林软食力竞赛决赛暨保健成果发表会 28日在卫生局举行。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10878948" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/8b84d91fd1f0ff55daafe287b55e9577.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10878948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/8b84d91fd1f0ff55daafe287b55e9577-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台西卫生所今年获得107年高龄友善健康照护机构典范选拔。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10878949" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/51507e947897da0ebfd1a003869d76a5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10878949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/51507e947897da0ebfd1a003869d76a5-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">虎尾卫生所今年通过高龄友善健康照护机构认证2.0，接受卫生局长吴昭君表扬 。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>除软食力竞赛优胜者，今日颁奖项目也包含50家健康优良职场暨无槟榔职场、6家癌症筛检品质精进计划医院，以及18组活跃老化竞赛队伍。</p>
  <p>健康优良职场暨无槟榔职场计有宇荣高尔夫科技股份有限公司、成大医院斗六分院、虎尾糖厂、台塑石化公司、俊鼎机械厂有限公司麦寮厂等50家获奖。? 癌症防治业务绩优医疗院所得奖单位计有台大医院云林分院、中国医药大学北港附设医院、天主教若瑟医院、成大医学斗六分院、彰基云林分院，及天主教福安医院等6家。</p>
  <p>活跃老化竞赛则有麦寮乡海风社区日托学院队、北港镇乐活辣嬷队等18组参赛队伍。</p>
