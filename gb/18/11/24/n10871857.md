<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>郑文灿认为投票率提高  陈学圣认为公投可分开</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/206583b6eb334cb923981ba450877eb3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>民进党桃园市长候选人郑文灿圣投下神圣一票。（郑文灿新闻团队提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月24日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）九合一投票，桃园市长候选人<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E6%96%87%E7%81%BF.md#1">郑文灿</a>、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%AD%A6%E5%9C%A3.md#1">陈学圣</a>一早出门投票，郑文灿认为感觉应该投票率会，上一届选举投票是五成七左右，这一届应该会有更高的投票率；陈学圣上午8点53分到中坜新明国小排队，到9点38分完成投票，他认为现在选民对<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E6%8A%95.md#1">公投</a>有信心，可以将大选与公投分开举行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10871859" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/39b0e45817dc6786a9b3b63ff5cf3928.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10871859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/39b0e45817dc6786a9b3b63ff5cf3928-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">国民党提名桃园市长候选人<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%AD%A6%E5%9C%A3.md#1">陈学圣</a>投下神圣一票。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E6%96%87%E7%81%BF.md#1">郑文灿</a>到母校八德大安国小投票，他说，户籍也一直都在这边，回来自己的故乡投票，其实感觉特别亲切，大安国小围墙那些画的交通号志都是我画的，这学校只有2班，因为国小只有二十几个，同一班只有二十几个，大部分都还留在桃园，</p>
<figure id="attachment_10871861" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3b87384fa7b87c403d078f1b597a9ddb.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10871861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/3b87384fa7b87c403d078f1b597a9ddb-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">九合一投票因为天气好，大批排队人潮。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10871863" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/5f3a9a5b33666d0b6cd348967db245f2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10871863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/5f3a9a5b33666d0b6cd348967db245f2-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">选民对国民党提名桃园市长候选人陈学圣表达支持。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>24日天气很好、很阳光，很多选民都投完票才要出门，投票是人民的权利，一定要投票，今天感觉应该投票率会，上一届选举投票是五成七左右，这一届应该会有更高的投票率。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10871866" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a85e0fa8c5358a3841568ebbd58cb81d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10871866" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/a85e0fa8c5358a3841568ebbd58cb81d-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">民进党桃园市长候选人郑文灿圣慰勉选务人员辛苦。（郑文灿新闻团队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10871865" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ec24a5159c33cc3d1330504a76cb3d91.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10871865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ec24a5159c33cc3d1330504a76cb3d91-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">民进党桃园市长候选人郑文灿圣排队投票。（郑文灿新闻团队提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈学圣上午8点53分到中坜新明国小排队，到9点38分完成投票，他认为选民投票率高，对“翻转桃园”是一大利多，但因为<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E6%8A%95.md#1">公投</a>绑大选，时间耗时太长，对老人家体力负荷是一大考验。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10871860" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ecb0058fc0a0509cfe80a7e7545e3364.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10871860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/ecb0058fc0a0509cfe80a7e7545e3364-450x353.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="353" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">国民党提名桃园市长候选人陈学圣投下公投票。（徐乃义／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈学圣认为，现在选民对公投很有信心，应该可以将大选与公投分开举行。◇</p>
