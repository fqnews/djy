  <p>11日晚间，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%BC%A6%E6%95%A6.md#1">伦敦</a>的威斯敏斯特教堂点燃了一盏灯塔，接着英国各地点燃了另外1,000多个灯塔。这些灯塔象征着黑暗战争年代的结束，和平的曙光重返。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10854269" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1060279042-e1542290668784.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10854269" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1060279042-e1542290668784.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Paul Grover- WPA Pool/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>女王和德国总统施泰因都参加了在威斯敏斯特教堂举行的活动。施泰因还成为第一位在伦敦的阵亡将士纪念碑献花圈的德国领导人。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10854270" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1060197504-e1542290755611.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10854270" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1060197504-e1542290755611.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Matt Cardy/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>奥斯卡奖导演博伊尔（Danny Boyle）策划了英国及爱尔兰33个海滩举行的名为Pages of the Sea纪念活动。人们将一战中阵亡士兵的头像画在沙滩上，然后任海浪将画像冲散，因为这些士兵当年离开家乡后再也没有归来。全英国有几千人参加了这个活动。图为康沃尔的Porthcurno海滩上的士兵画像。这名士兵来自康沃尔，阵亡的时候年仅26岁。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10854273" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1066219664-e1542290821107.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10854273" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1066219664-e1542290821107.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（ Jeff J Mitchell/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10854274" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1066146990-e1542290902467.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10854274" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1066146990-e1542290902467.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Jeff J Mitchell/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>英国其它地方也举行了纪念活动。苏格兰几百名现役和退役军人游行到Spean Bridge的纪念碑，向在两次世界大战以及之后战争冲突中死亡的人表示怀念。还有老兵向纪念碑献花圈。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10854278" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1059485158-e1542291229390.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10854278" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1059485158-e1542291229390.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（ Dan Kitwood/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>著名的劳埃德保险公司（也称劳合社）举行了一分钟默哀，同时有红色的罂粟花从天而降。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10854280" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1065346690-e1542291291722.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10854280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/GettyImages-1065346690-e1542291291722.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Jim Dyson/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>伦敦King’s Cross地铁站使用了画有罂粟花的站牌。</p>
