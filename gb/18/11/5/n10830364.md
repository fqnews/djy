<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Cupertino新餐馆 米其林级烹饪 平价享受</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/06_Kumino-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>湾区中餐馆Kumino China：店内很有中国风味，融合西式摆盘。（旧金山吃货/百合摄）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年11月04日讯】文：百合?美食专栏作家<br />
Kumino又重新开张，兴奋不已，于是立马前往。记得两年前它还是山景城一家小店铺，地方不大却十分的温馨，去了几次都是人满为患、大排长龙，它的每一道菜我都品尝过。这一位大厨曾经在米其林餐厅做过厨师，现在是新开张餐馆——Kumino China的主厨。<figure id="attachment_10830383" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/05_Kumino.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/05_Kumino-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" class="size-small wp-image-10830383" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%AD%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">湾区中餐馆</a>Kumino China：主厨刘昊辰。（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>/百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<h2>从工程师到大厨</h2>
<p>主厨刘昊辰原本是一名电脑工程师，来到美国并没有继续学习电脑，而是去纽约的美国烹饪学院（Culinary Institute of America，CIA）学厨艺。他热爱美食，热爱烹饪，把家常食材，用米其林的制作方式呈现给大家，让普通人也能享受到米其林品质的餐食。</p>
<p>今日走进餐厅，原来就是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B2%B8%E8%85%BE%E9%B1%BC%E4%B9%A1.md#1">沸腾鱼乡</a>的旧址，沸腾的老板十分重才，特地邀请他前来合作。我们叫了几道菜：</p>
<p>小龙虾包、软壳蟹包、甜菜根沙拉、烟熏鸡肉和抓饼，以及茄汁海鲜汤，甜点上了脆皮年糕芝士。<figure id="attachment_10830384" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/01_Kumino.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/01_Kumino-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10830384" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%AD%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">湾区中餐馆</a>Kumino China：甜菜根沙拉。（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>/百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10830385" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/02_Kumino.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/02_Kumino-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10830385" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湾区中餐馆Kumino China：烟熏鸡肉和抓饼。（旧金山吃货/百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<h2>融合各家风味的独创菜品</h2>
<p>刘师傅的食材新鲜，咬下去便知道，高品质呈现。曾经吃过他的海胆蒸蛋，那真是世上一绝。他不停地研发各种食材，就如今日的海鲜汤（Cioppino）有意大利口味，又有日本的寿喜烧滋味，再和原本<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B2%B8%E8%85%BE%E9%B1%BC%E4%B9%A1.md#1">沸腾鱼乡</a>的海鲜汤的结合。<figure id="attachment_10830387" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/03_Kumino.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/03_Kumino-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10830387" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湾区中餐馆Kumino China：茄汁海鲜汤。（旧金山吃货/百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10830389" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/04_Kumino.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/04_Kumino-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10830389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湾区中餐馆Kumino China：脆皮年糕芝士。（旧金山吃货/百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>烟熏鸡肉和抓饼，鸡肉用上好的Madeira红酒烹调而成。脆皮年糕芝士外脆内软还有芝士流出来，上面加上一大匙绿茶冰淇淋，冰火相容，口感极佳。</p>
<p>这一次重新开张，听说最有名的强打主菜就是北京烤鸭，但是必须预约，所以今天没有吃到，等着来日尝尝。各位饕客们有空不妨品尝不同的料理方式，听说每道菜都是独家设计的不二菜色。#</p>
