  <figure id="attachment_10867419" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867419" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-10-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Citroen官网。</figcaption></figure>  <p>这是一款既能使您日常生活轻松便利，又能为您提供宽敞空间和舒适体验的小型SUV。除了引人注目的风格外，C3 Aircross 还提供了一整套先进的安全装备及最高五星级安全评级。Citroen 1.2升 Pure Tech发动机不仅能够提供高效动力，而且还能帮您将油耗降至最低。简而言之，C3 Aircross是非常适合您的完美SUV。</p>
  <h2>独特的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AA%E6%80%A7.md#1">个性</a>化外观</h2>  <p>C3 Aircross 凭借其独特的车身造型，在日益流行的小型SUV市场脱颖而出。其丰富的调色板包括8种车身颜色、3种车顶颜色（墨水黑、天然白、辛辣橙）和3种颜色套装（橙色、白色、黑色）。颜色套装适用于车顶杆、后视镜盖、前照灯饰圈和车轮中心盖。内装方面，您可以选择五种装饰主题的内饰氛围。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10867422" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-2-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-2-1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Citroen官网。</figcaption></figure>  <p>C3 Aircross 标配麦卡灰（Mica Grey）面料座椅、黑色仪表板装饰和镀铬装饰条。$500大都会灰（Metropolitan Grey）选项打造的是浅色内舱色调。该选项在座椅、驾驶员座椅扶手、灰色面料包覆的仪表板和橙色装饰插件上搭配橙色条纹。</p>
  <p>$750元城市红（Urban Red）设计将灰色调与红色条纹、橙色缝线和黑色仪表板装饰配搭。article content begin -->000 Hype Mistral选项加入了真皮装饰，座椅饰面混搭灰色皮革和布料，同时搭配黑色仪表板。article content begin -->500 Hype Colorado选项则采用浅棕色皮革，搭配雅致的灰色布料饰边及同色造革仪表板。</p>
  <h2>Citroen高级舒适内装</h2>  <p>C3 Aircross 的重要特色是全新Citroen高级舒适R计划。这项创新可增强乘员的内部空间感和舒适感。凭借其独创的巧妙架构，C3 Aircross 不仅具有城市友好的紧凑性，而且还在舱内提供了出色的空间感。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10867424" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-16-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867424" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-16-1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Citroen官网。</figcaption></figure>  <p>横向设计的仪表板、释放的大量空间及宽大舒适的座椅营造出温馨的环境氛围。后排乘员不仅方便进入，而且腿部和头部空间都很宽裕，还有可以遮阳的百叶窗。想要更多自然光线的话，您可以选配开放式全景玻璃天窗。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10867425" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867425" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-3-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Citroen官网。</figcaption></figure>  <p>7英寸中控屏是各项功能的控制中心，包括自动空调、收音机、电话等功能，同时搭载3D卫星导航、Android Auto、Apple CarPlay和MirrorLink车载系统。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10867433" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-12-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Citroen官网。</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10867428" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867428" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-13-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Citroen官网。</figcaption></figure>  <p>行李箱标准容量为410升，当后排座椅处于最靠前位置时，容量可增至520升。如将后排座椅按60/40比例折叠，总负载量将增至1289升。除此之外，前排一个座椅还可折叠放平，从而实现2.40米的负载空间。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10867429" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867429" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-11-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Citroen官网。</figcaption></figure>  <h2>无缝使用的安全功能</h2>  <p>荣获欧洲NCAP五星级安全评级的C3 Aircross，搭载一系列专为无缝使用而设计的安全功能，高配车型更是搭载了直观技术，确保您的旅程更轻松、更安全。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10867432" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867432" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-9-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Citroen官网。</figcaption></figure>  <p>C3 Aircross全系标配或选配无钥匙进入及一键启动、平行和车位泊车辅助、自主紧急制动、车道偏离警告、盲点监控、限速标志识别和建议、带前后驻车传感器的倒车摄像头、智能远光辅助、驾驶员注意力警报和咖啡休息提醒等功能。</p>
  <h2>高效节能动力系统</h2>  <p>全新C3 Aircross 采用最新一代荣获大奖的PureTech发动机，该发动机在油耗和效率方面表现突出。经来自31个国家的65名评委评定，PureTech发动机连续四年荣获1.0至1.4升发动机类别国际年度最佳发动机大奖。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10867435" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-15.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867435" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-15-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Citroen官网。</figcaption></figure>  <p>C3 Aircross搭载三缸1.2升PureTech发动机，可输出110马力（81千瓦）最大功率和205牛米峰值扭矩。得益于启/停技术，其百公里综合油耗只有5.5升，二氧化碳排放量为每公里126克。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10867437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-14.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10867437" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/11/citroen-c3-aircross-suv-photo-gallery-14-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片来源：Citroen官网。</figcaption></figure>  <p>PureTech标配Citroen 高效自动6速变速箱（EAT6）。该变速箱采用Quick Shift快速换挡技术，可实现更快、更平稳换挡及最佳驾驶性能，从而为您带来极致驾驶体验。</p>
