<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>H组第二轮：哥伦比亚3:0淘汰波兰</h2>

<h6>米纳的进球为哥伦比亚先拔头筹。 (SAEED KHAN/AFP/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月25日讯】（大纪元记者天宇报导）6月24日，2018<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E6%9D%AF.md#1">世界杯</a><ahref="/gb/tag/h%E7%BB%84.md#1">H组</a>第二轮赛事中，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%93%A5%E4%BC%A6%E6%AF%94%E4%BA%9A.md#1">哥伦比亚</a>3:0击败本组实力最强的波兰，出线可能扔把握在自己手中，而两连败的波兰则惨遭淘汰。</p>
<p>上半场双方长时间陷入僵持，互有攻防，但都迟迟不能破门，一直到第39分钟，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%93%A5%E4%BC%A6%E6%AF%94%E4%BA%9A.md#1">哥伦比亚</a>在一次角球机会中，刚刚被巴塞罗那签下的中卫米纳（Yerry Mina），在无人盯放下，于门前头球破门，打破僵局，上半场比分也以1:0结束，哥伦比亚暂时领先。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10508947" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982938606.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10508947 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982938606-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">米纳（Yerry Mina ）门前头球破门，上半场比分1:0结束，哥伦比亚领先。(Photo by Alex Livesey/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10508948" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982938812.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10508948 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982938812-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">米纳（Yerry Mina ）门前头球破门，上半场比分1:0结束，哥伦比亚领先。(Photo by Julian Finney/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>易地再战后，波兰用全场紧逼战术，大举压上，但也令后防上出现空虚，哥伦比亚的几次反击更具威胁。</p>
<p>结果在第69分钟，金特罗（ Juan Quintero）中路送出精妙直塞球给禁区，队长法尔考（Radamel Falcao）拿球后形成单刀球，在门将出击前射门得手，比分改写为2:0。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10508906" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982962146.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10508906 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982962146-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法尔考进球瞬间。(JEWEL SAMAD/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10508904" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982961006.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10508904 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982961006-450x293.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="293" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法尔考进球瞬间。 (Julian Finney/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第74分钟，哥伦比亚卷土重来，在中线的夸德拉多（Juan Cuadrado ）接队友J罗（James Rodriguez）精准传中球后长驱直入，一直带球至大禁区外延，推射右侧入网，比分改写为3:0，哥伦比亚已经胜券在握。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10508901" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982960780.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10508901 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982960780-450x312.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="312" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">夸德拉多打入第三粒进球，锁定胜局。(Richard Heathcote/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后比分锁定在3:0，哥伦比亚全区三分，落后本组的日本和塞内加尔1分，这意味着三支队伍都要在最后一轮上演生死战，而两连败的波兰则提前出局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10508902" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982989520.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10508902 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-982989520-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">莱万多夫斯基失望至极，自己领衔的波兰连败而遭淘汰。(BENJAMIN CREMEL/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
