<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月23日讯】想来点咸食吗？选这道饱足感满分、奶香味浓郁、口感绵密又牵丝的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF%E7%81%AB%E8%85%BF%E5%8D%83%E5%B1%82%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E6%B4%BE.md#1">马铃薯火腿千层起司派</a>，利用起司和<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a>层层相叠，加上炒过的蘑菇与洋葱调味，增添<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%83%E5%B1%82.md#1">千层</a>派的香气，绝对让人无法抵抗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483701" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/7-15.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10483701 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/7-15-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF%E7%81%AB%E8%85%BF%E5%8D%83%E5%B1%82%E8%B5%B7%E5%8F%B8%E6%B4%BE.md#1">马铃薯火腿千层起司派</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【材料】</h2>
<p>蘑菇 500ml<br />
洋葱 1颗</p>
<p>培根 5片</p>
<p>莫札瑞拉起司 2杯（丝）<br />
切达起司 2杯（丝）<br />
火腿 8～10片<br />
<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF.md#1">马铃薯</a> 5颗</p>
<p>奥勒冈香草粉 2茶匙<br />
黑胡椒粉 2茶匙<br />
胡椒粉 1茶匙<br />
鲜奶油 1杯<br />
盐 2茶匙<br />
蛋 2颗</p>
<p>香芹末 1/4杯</p>
<p>（以上的材料、口味可随意调整、变化。）<br />
※“1杯”=16汤匙=240 ml；“1汤匙”=1 Tbsp=15 ml=3茶匙；“1茶匙”=1 tsp = 5 ml</p>
<figure id="attachment_10483691" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-120.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10483691 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-120-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马铃薯片铺在底层，火腿片铺在第二层，薄铺切达起司。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>【事前准备】</h2>
<p>将培根切碎，炒熟。<br />
将洋葱和蘑菇切块，炒到变软。<br />
马铃薯去皮、切片。<br />
烤箱预热至摄氏180度／华氏350度。<br />
方形烤盘的底部均匀涂上奶油。</p>
