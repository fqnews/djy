【大纪元2018年06月29日讯】许多动物跟主人相处久了自然会“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A8%A1%E4%BB%BF.md#1">模仿</a>”主人，常常分享宠物趣事的粉丝专页UNILAD贴出一个有趣的短片。</p>
<p>影片中，男子和<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>对准相机镜头，狗狗坐在车子的后座，而主人戴着墨镜坐在前座。只要主人<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%81%BF%E7%AC%91.md#1">灿笑</a>，狗狗就傻傻地吐出舌头，主人弄出帅气冷酷表情，狗狗也变得<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%A5%E8%82%83.md#1">严肃</a>，不断这样上演<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A8%A1%E4%BB%BF.md#1">模仿</a>戏码。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10519460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4643e47308c5cbca82239fbb59a4d613.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10519460" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4643e47308c5cbca82239fbb59a4d613-600x461.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="461" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">主人<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%81%BF%E7%AC%91.md#1">灿笑</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>就<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%90%90%E8%88%8C.md#1">吐舌</a>头。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>仔细观察可能会觉得奇怪，狗狗在后座耶，怎么能看到主人表情呢？可能是看“后照镜”吧？！</p>
<p>虽然狗狗有时会慢半拍……而且动作好像也模仿不是非常像，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%90%90%E8%88%8C.md#1">吐舌</a>头与灿笑差很多呀！但是毕竟狗狗的生理结构和人类不一样，它很厉害噜！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10519462" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/92ae1a76fd0560f8664eeb07add74163.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10519462" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/92ae1a76fd0560f8664eeb07add74163-600x432.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="432" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">狗狗有会慢半拍，主人<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%A5%E8%82%83.md#1">严肃</a>时还吐着舌头。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
