  <p>【大纪元2018年08月26日讯】吃饭没胃口吗？不妨放松心情，一起来玩料理吧！意想不到的新创意，增添料理乐趣，还能揪家中小宝贝一起蒐主意，一起同乐增进亲子间默契。让这份创意满分的趣味料理，享用起来更加美味！</p>

  <h2>【食材准备】</h2>  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E8%85%BF.md#1">火腿</a><br />  鹌鹑蛋<br />  白饭<br />  三色豆<br />  泡菜<br />  奶油<br />  葱末<br />  胡椒粉<br />  海苔</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10516949" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/eed85b43f824ad4ba80cf2f453d4debf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10516949" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/eed85b43f824ad4ba80cf2f453d4debf-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">吃饭没胃口吗？不妨放松心情，一起来玩料理吧！ （影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <h2>【作法】</h2>  <p>1.先将<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%81%AB%E8%85%BF.md#1">火腿</a>中间切掉一个长方形，并将其部分切丁。</p>
  <p>2.外圈边框下锅煎到酥脆，在中间打入鹌鹑蛋，盖上锅盖1分钟，即可起锅备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10516947" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e3687d03193d0dcf57805cdc323d26b9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10516947" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e3687d03193d0dcf57805cdc323d26b9-450x281.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="281" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">香煎火腿鹌鹑蛋。 （影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3.<strong>蔬菜<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炒饭</a>：</strong>热锅抹上一层奶油，将切丁的火腿放入锅中拌抄，再加入混合蔬菜、白饭、葱末，翻炒至锅内食材煮熟，撒点胡椒粉提味。</p>
  <p>4.<strong>泡菜<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%82%92%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炒饭</a>：</strong>热锅放入点食用油，加入火腿丁拌炒，再放入泡菜、白饭均匀翻炒。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10516950" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f44970b328674a98b8f9a101c6908c0b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10516950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/f44970b328674a98b8f9a101c6908c0b-450x423.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="423" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">双色香Q炒饭。 （影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5.将两种口味炒饭，放入铺有保鲜膜的容器中，并轻微按压铺平，再将炒饭连同保鲜膜一同取出。</p>
  <p>6.在中间裹上一片海苔，放上火腿煎蛋，即可美味上桌。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10517051" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e0140528ffe969bb373d056d368d5263.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10517051" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/e0140528ffe969bb373d056d368d5263-450x386.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="386" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在炒饭中间裹上一片海苔 。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10517050" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/97c79b23d3174c2e6f3c6002346fe6a4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10517050" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/97c79b23d3174c2e6f3c6002346fe6a4-450x452.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="452" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">最后，放上火腿煎蛋，即可美味上桌。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>【备注】口味咸淡及食材，皆可依个人喜好调整变换。</p>
