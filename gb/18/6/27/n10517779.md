<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>期待中的第三届万锦于人村街头豪车展7月将至</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/PrettyThingsexoticcars-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>超跑云集的街头豪车展将如期而至（主办方提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月27日讯】7月的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">多伦多活动</a>不断，最令众人瞩目的无疑是在Unionville Main Street举办的超跑云集的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A1%97%E5%A4%B4%E8%B1%AA%E8%BD%A6%E5%B1%95.md#1">街头豪车展</a>了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10518377" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/b8da06d2e309b079ab0749e2bf350ad0-sz_227880-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10518377 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/b8da06d2e309b079ab0749e2bf350ad0-sz_227880-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10518381" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-216.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10518381 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-216-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span class="button" style="background-color: #c51c21;pointer-events: none;">活动时间：7月29日?上午11点~下午4点</span><br />
<sapn class="button" style="background-color: #c51c21;pointer-events: none;">地点： 157 Main Street Unionville, Markham,ON</a></p>
<p>今年多伦多本地的各色豪车纷纷前来参展，参加车展的车型包括兰博基尼、阿斯顿·马丁、迈凯伦、保时捷、法拉利等近百辆超级跑车、豪车及老爷车。<br />
<figure id="attachment_10518370" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/b8da06d2e309b079ab0749e2bf350ad0-sz_227880.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10518370 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-181-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure></p>
<figure id="attachment_10518383" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/835eb362bf4b47cf405d63cc9c9cdc1a-sz_226536-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10518383 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/835eb362bf4b47cf405d63cc9c9cdc1a-sz_226536-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10518385" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/d6a43cf0d055405c74cb10ceb0857a86-sz_230752-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10518385 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/d6a43cf0d055405c74cb10ceb0857a86-sz_230752-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_10517891" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-10517891 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/48e641079428c6608f7e545e29355c70-sz_235683-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（主办方提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span class="button" style="background-color: #c51c21;pointer-events: none;">活动时间：7月29日?上午11点~下午4点</span><br />
<sapn class="button" style="background-color: #c51c21;pointer-events: none;">地点： 157 Main Street Unionville, Markham,ON</a></p>
<p>据称这个露天车展是由豪车的车主们自发组织的，已经成为当地车迷的年度盛事。每年车展都将吸引数千名豪车爱好者和家人前来一同观赏，并且与心爱的名车拍照留念。此外，Unionville Main Street<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A1%97%E5%A4%B4%E8%B1%AA%E8%BD%A6%E5%B1%95.md#1">街头豪车展</a>亦为Hand Over Hand这个非盈利机构筹款，用来帮助社区内的患有发育障碍的青年人恢复健康。<br />
豪车展上除了百余辆炫目的跑车、豪车、古董车，还设有DJ、抽奖活动、食品摊位、礼品赠送等等。不仅能够让前来参观的朋友们大饱豪车眼福，更能够有让您拥有一个完美的周末体验。</p>
