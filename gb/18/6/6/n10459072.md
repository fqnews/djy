<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>尖沙咀日本沾面</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JJG0N"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/aopcrh390/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月06日讯】每次经过K11也会经过<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E7%94%B0%E5%88%B6%E9%9D%A2%E6%89%80.md#1">三田制面所</a>，几年前它从日本进军香港的时候，每天门外都排着长长的队伍，我听到都怕。现在热潮已过、稳定了，分店增加了，变得方便多了，上周六我终于都去试了。</p>
<p>从玻璃窗便看到店家的制面机在制面，为确保质素，制面机都是从日本运来的，面条每日新鲜制作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10459389" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052222141366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10459389" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052222141366-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">从玻璃窗便看到店家的制面机在制面。（影片撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10459392" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052222171366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10459392" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052222171366-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">制面机都是从日本运来的，面条每日新鲜制作。（影片撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>面店面的种类选择种类不多，主打<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B2%BE%E9%9D%A2.md#1">沾面</a>，自家制极粗面配上特浓沾汁，特浓沾汁强调用上大量猪骨、鸡骨、蔬菜和鱼介等材料，用强火熬制超过12小时而成。</p>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B2%BE%E9%9D%A2.md#1">沾面</a>有冷热选择，冷食是最正宗的，面条口感会更加爽滑、烟韧、有嚼劲。这里的沾面大中小份量同价，份量分别是140克、210克、280克，不论面条的份量多少，沾汁份量都是一样。</p>
<p>我点了一碗沾面（大）、一碗<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%BE%9B%E8%BE%A3%E4%B8%89%E7%94%B0%E6%8B%89%E9%9D%A2.md#1">辛辣三田拉面</a>（中）外加两款小食分别是炸鸡翼（黑胡椒味）和起司虾球。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10963277" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1806052210061366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10963277" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1806052210061366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">沾面（大）、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%BE%9B%E8%BE%A3%E4%B8%89%E7%94%B0%E6%8B%89%E9%9D%A2.md#1">辛辣三田拉面</a>（中）、炸鸡翼（黑胡椒味）和起司虾球。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>首先上来的是沾面，我先试一口沾汁，沾汁很浓沃、略咸但很鲜味。可惜不够热，上桌时的温度已经可以立刻入口，一般的沾汁都需要热到烫口的程度，尤其沾面是冷的话，沾汁必须要够热先才好吃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10459329" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052209591366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10459329 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052209591366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">沾面 （大）。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>沾汁里有一颗叉烧粒，叉烧粒吸收了大量沾汁，好吃但不够嫩滑。到沾面了～沾面入口有微微的小麦面香，烟韧、爽滑、有嚼劲，好吃！</p>
<p>配菜有一片叉烧，半颗溏心蛋和几条笋条。叉烧干身吃起来有点干沾上沾汁会食吃点，溏心蛋见到蛋黄一半呈果冻状，味道都不错，笋条清淡没有渣。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10459380" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052210161366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10459380" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052210161366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">溏心蛋的蛋黄一半呈果冻状，味道都不错，笋条清淡没有渣。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于沾汁浓厚，很容易沾在面上，所以大碗的面食到最后会不太够沾汁，幸好我尽量沾不多，所以可以吃到最后，（后来听朋友说，原来虽然不可以加添沾汁，但可以加汤割，不过回想现场看不到汤壶，也没人提醒，大家下次可以主动问店员。）</p>
<p>辛辣三田拉面就是一般拉面口感，辛辣度一般，也是配叉烧、笋条，不过没有蛋，虽然是中份量，但也颇大碗的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10459351" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052210021366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10459351 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052210021366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">辛辣三田拉面（中）。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黑胡椒味炸鸡翼以为是辣胡椒味，但没想到入口是甜的，甜味完全盖过黑胡椒的味道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10459346" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052210091366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10459346 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052210091366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">炸鸡翼（黑胡椒风味）。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>芝士虾球咬开里面是软身芝士，挺弹牙爽口。后来点的那杯蜂蜜绿茶就淡而无味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10459348" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052210131366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10459348 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052210131366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">芝士虾球。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然面店格局不像要加一服务费的餐厅，但它是需要收加一的！沾面真的好吃我想我还会再去光顾，而且还会更聪明一点，试试加上它们的汤割，看看味道如何！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10459394" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052209551366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10459394 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1806052209551366-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
