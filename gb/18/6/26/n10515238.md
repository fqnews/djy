  <p>比赛一开场，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E6%A0%B9%E5%BB%B7.md#1">阿根廷</a>展开积极攻势，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%BC%E6%97%A5%E5%88%A9%E4%BA%9A.md#1">尼日利亚</a>回收防守。阿根廷本场排出433阵型，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%85%E8%A5%BF.md#1">梅西</a>（MESSI）领衔锋线，迪马利亚（DI MARIA）和伊瓜因（HIGUAIN）登场。巴内加（BANEGA）、马斯切拉诺（MASCHERANO）和佩雷斯（PEREZ）构成中场。</p>
  <p>后卫线上，奥塔门迪（OTAMENDI）和罗霍（ROJO）搭档中卫，塔利亚菲科（TAGLIAFICO）和梅尔卡多（MERCADO）配合双塔，阿玛尼（ARMANI）担任门将。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10515433" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-984480120.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10515433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-984480120-600x379.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="379" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">梅西起脚破门瞬间(Photo by Michael Steele/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10515345" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-984487804.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10515345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-984487804-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">让梅西进球的先决条件是要让他舒服的拿到球。(Photo by Alex Livesey/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>阿根廷队员第14分钟敲开对手城门，巴内加（BANEGA）中场送出过顶长传球，梅西反越位成功，突入禁区形成单刀，起脚打门得分1:0。</p>
  <p>这也是本届杯赛的第100粒进球。凭借此球梅西成为继马拉多纳和巴蒂斯图塔之后第3位在3届世界杯进球的阿根廷球员。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10515339" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-984617940.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10515339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-984617940-600x567.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="567" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马斯切拉诺力拼到血染绿茵场。(Photo by Gabriel Rossi/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10515431" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-984529850.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10515431" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-984529850-600x364.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="364" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马斯切拉诺被主裁判出示黄牌，视频回放里巴洛贡倒地确实有“碰瓷”嫌疑，但是马斯切拉诺的手势太多余。(Photo by Michael Steele/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>下半场第49分钟出现有争议的一幕，马斯切拉诺禁区内拉倒巴洛贡（BALOGUN），主裁判判罚了点球，并且出示黄牌警告马斯切拉诺。</p>
  <p>随后摩西操刀主罚命中右下角，尼日利亚将比分扳成1比1平。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10515430" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-984529904.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10515430 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-984529904-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">摩西给尼日利亚队扳平比分。(Photo by Michael Steele/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>阿根廷队立刻发动反击，迪马利亚带球长途奔袭切入禁区左侧，他在摩西防守下摔倒，裁判却毫不理会，本场阿根廷至少4次因为身体碰撞倒在禁区内都被裁判忽略。</p>
  <p>第86分钟比分再被改写，阿根廷梅尔卡多（MERCADO）右路突破传中，尼日利亚防守漏人，罗霍点球点附近凌空推射命中右下角，阿根廷2比1成功击败尼日利亚，以小组第二身份晋级16强，淘汰赛中对阵法国。</p>
