<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>初生之犊不畏虎 小猫的大玩具竟然是这个</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-2-3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>天然逗猫棒。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10428695 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/complete-video-link-600x65.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="65" /></a><br />
【大纪元2018年06月11日讯】<span class="s1">养猫的朋友家里必备的物品之一就是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%80%97%E7%8C%AB%E6%A3%92.md#1">逗猫棒</a>，而对“喵星人”来说，任何会摇来晃去的东西都能激发它们狩猎的本能，就连自己的尾巴都不例外。相信不少人都曾看过猫咪追着自己的尾巴团团转，实在是深谙自得其乐之道呢！</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">不过，这段影片里的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8C%AB.md#1">小猫</a>是吃了雄心豹子胆吗？居然玩到狗狗身上！</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10471255" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-1-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10471255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-1-3-600x428.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="428" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8C%AB.md#1">小猫</a>玩<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E5%B0%BE%E5%B7%B4.md#1">狗尾巴</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">只见大狗坐在地上，它的尾巴像牛尾赶苍蝇一样甩个不停，感觉颇有力道；但是胆大又<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E7%8E%A9.md#1">爱玩</a>的小猫没在怕，直接把它当成天然<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%80%97%E7%8C%AB%E6%A3%92.md#1">逗猫棒</a>，躺在地上玩得不亦乐乎。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">看来狗狗的个性很好，否则早就发飙啦！小猫对此情有独钟，有时伸出小手左右开弓，有时整个攀在上面死抓不放。后来狗狗被闹得受不了，三十六计走为上策，结束这场毫无火药味的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E7%8B%97%E4%B9%8B%E6%88%98.md#1">猫狗之战</a>。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10471256" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-2-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10471256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5-2-3-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">天然逗猫棒。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">说到猫与狗和谐相处，美国加州有一只原本遭到遗弃的小猫罗西被幸运救回后，由主人家里的三只哈士奇带大，此后这“三犬一猫”形影不离的故事，温暖了许多网友的心。</span></p>
