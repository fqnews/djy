  <figure id="attachment_10449674" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/eb2d2b8d04e23b3990f2c71d37726d86-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10449674" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/eb2d2b8d04e23b3990f2c71d37726d86-1-600x463.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="463" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">像小蛋卷一样的炒冰，看起来好吃又好玩，真想赶快也来一杯～（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>有些人知道，有些人不知道，其实这“泰式炒冰”其实应该叫作“台湾炒冰”才对喔！原本炒冰就发迹于台湾，不过传到东南亚之后，在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%9B%BD.md#1">泰国</a>被发扬光大。欧美人士一看到这新鲜有趣的冰凉小点，马上爱不释手心都给了它，后来炒冰就被叫作“泰式炒冰”了。</p>
  <p>制作方法是，炒冰要先把乳状液体倒入冷冻平底锅，据说维持在零下17度最美味，接着，客倌想要什么配料，例如：各式新鲜水果、巧克力饼干、不同口味的糖果等，任君挑选～老板加入蓝莓和草莓等浆果，可以想见<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%86%B0%E6%B7%87%E6%B7%8B.md#1">冰淇淋</a>成品将呈现可爱的粉紫色！</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10449449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4a1f05f13bc23132fdf071fd3b909e4d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10449449" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4a1f05f13bc23132fdf071fd3b909e4d-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">客倌想要什么配料，各式新鲜水果、巧克力饼干、不同口味的糖果等，任君挑选～（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10449804" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/0e27e021004abeec7b08117ae5ace0aa-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10449804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/0e27e021004abeec7b08117ae5ace0aa-1-600x459.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="459" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">不断冷冻拌炒，最后压成扁平的长方形薄片，然后“卷”起来～做成一个个冰卷 （影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>接着是一系列的来回翻炒，两支锅铲不断夹起再压平，将乳制品和甜滋滋的配料均匀搅拌。经过反复拌炒，最后把已着色的冰淇淋平均压在铁板上，制成长方形的薄片。然后“卷”起来，变成一卷卷短短的“蛋卷冰淇淋”！</p>
  <p>看起可爱又可口的卷卷冰，装到纸杯里之后，还会进行最后的“美味加工”！老板，舀一匙蓝莓果粒浇在卷卷冰上，又挤了一大团鲜奶油，把鲜奶油当成圣诞树，塞几粒草莓，再淋些甜酱！哇～这一份“炒冰”也太豪华缤纷了～好想吃一口尝尝味道喔！</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10449680" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/c3d0c925e7159b17f2ba3d1452ee7a14-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10449680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/c3d0c925e7159b17f2ba3d1452ee7a14-1-600x396.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">像小蛋卷一样的炒冰，看起来好吃又好玩，真想赶快也来一杯～（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>观看完整影片?</strong></span><div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-a8871a32-1532-4b22-7c1b-d6a9575e5671" data-id="player-a8871a32-1532-4b22-7c1b-d6a9575e5671"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/a8871a32-1532-4b22-7c1b-d6a9575e5671?r=16x9&amp;s=720x640&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F6%2F2%2Fn10449306.md#1"></a></p>
