<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>福气片！人老心不老 老奶奶从容弹琴 老爷爷开心跳舞</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月26日讯】当有一天年纪很大了，你还会继续保持自己喜爱的兴趣吗？头发雪白的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E5%A5%B6.md#1">奶奶</a>和佝偻着背的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%88%B7%E7%88%B7.md#1">爷爷</a>，在客厅里“载歌载舞”好不开心。来看看这对可爱的爷爷<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E5%A5%B6.md#1">奶奶</a>如何自得其乐！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10504797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5aa8cba87365af66a75390a3c4787e52.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10504797 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/5aa8cba87365af66a75390a3c4787e52-600x522.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="522" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">奶奶从容弹琴，奏出轻快的乐章。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>奶奶从容弹琴，奏出轻快的乐章，可以想像奶奶的十只指头正灵活地在琴键上跳跃。<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%88%B7%E7%88%B7.md#1">爷爷</a>更是兴致勃勃，随着旋律扭扭腰，跟着节拍把叫伸出去、点一下、再缩回来，十足“舞王”架势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10504819" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3766e7e9213f994ba70c08a4abb232ad.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10504819 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3766e7e9213f994ba70c08a4abb232ad-600x521.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="521" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">爷爷随着旋律扭扭腰，十足“舞王”架势。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>听了就让人心情好的小曲，还有看了也让人会心一笑的舞步，爷爷和奶奶非常投入，也很快乐，欣快的气氛感染所有人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10504833" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/764b74ed146c5935e497ab216768fd22.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10504833 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/764b74ed146c5935e497ab216768fd22-600x521.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="521" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">最后爷爷作势一个“滑步”，滑到奶奶背后拍拍她的肩，奶奶吓了一跳！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后爷爷作势一个“滑步”，滑到奶奶背后拍拍她的肩，奶奶吓了一跳！不过也很开心～她表扬爷爷：“最棒的舞者！”完美的结束了精彩表演。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10504831" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/0bf0d60039f212a57a33a97901d9015b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10504831 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/0bf0d60039f212a57a33a97901d9015b-600x527.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="527" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">她表扬爷爷：“最棒的舞者！”完美的结束了精彩表演。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
