<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>夜深嘴馋 来碗鲜虾馄饨汤吧！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/s-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>鲜虾馄饨汤 健康清爽一碗接一碗（影片截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10442784 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-3-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月10日讯】夜深嘴馋肚子饿怎么办？想尝点不一样的吗？别犹豫了～来碗鲜甜清爽的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E8%99%BE%E9%A6%84%E9%A5%A8%E6%B1%A4.md#1">鲜虾馄饨汤</a>吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10446812" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/s4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446812" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/s4-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">夜深嘴馋肚子饿怎么办？那就来碗鲜甜清爽的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E8%99%BE%E9%A6%84%E9%A5%A8%E6%B1%A4.md#1">鲜虾馄饨汤</a>吧！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>鲜虾<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A6%84%E9%A5%A8.md#1">馄饨</a>材料：</h2>
<p>去壳虾仁 250克<br />
<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A6%84%E9%A5%A8.md#1">馄饨</a>皮 数张<br />
盐 1小匙<br />
胡椒 0.5小匙<br />
薄盐酱油 1小匙<br />
太白粉 1大匙<br />
芝麻油 1大匙<br />
红萝卜 1大匙<br />
竹笋 1大匙<br />
香菇 1大匙<br />
香菜 1大匙</p>
<h2>馄饨汤底：</h2>
<p>油 1大匙<br />
葱段 适量<br />
虾米 适量<br />
姜片 2片<br />
鸡高汤 2杯<br />
青江菜 少许<br />
红萝卜 少许</p>
<p>首先，制作这道料理的主角：鲜虾馄饨。制作馄饨馅料：先将买来的新鲜虾仁拍打成碎块，或切成小丁状后，放入容器中。</p>
<p>接者，依序将盐、胡椒、薄盐酱油、太白粉、芝麻油搅拌均匀后；再加入红萝卜、竹笋、香菇、香菜各1大匙，再次将容器里食材搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10446870" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/s3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446870" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/s3-450x386.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="386" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲜虾馄饨汤 健康清爽一碗接一碗（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>包馄钝：</strong>取稍许馄饨馅料放在馄饨皮中间，将馄饨皮边缘沾湿，再将皮两端黏在一起。</p>
<p>将少量填充物包裹在馄饨皮内。将手指浸在水中并弄湿边缘。将两端粘在一起。包好后，先放在一旁备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10446811" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/s2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446811" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/s2-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲜虾馄饨汤包好后，先放在一旁备用。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>制作馄饨汤底：</h2>
<p>热锅加入1大匙的油，接着再依序放入适量的葱段、虾米，及姜片和鸡高汤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10446869" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/s1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/s1-450x439.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="439" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">制作馄饨汤底。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水滚后，就可以将刚才包好的鲜虾馄饨下锅，并将锅内葱段和姜片捞起；接着，放入青江菜和红萝卜煮熟后，即可起锅，放入容器中，享用啰！</p>
<p>口味咸淡及配料食材皆可依个人喜好调整。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10446810" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/s.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10446810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/s-450x406.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="406" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲜虾馄饨汤美味上桌。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
