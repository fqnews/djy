<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>活虾上身！爸爸醒来发现全身贴满“除毛贴”</h2>
<a href="https://combinatronics.com/klgej2021/vd/master/jump2.htm?id=MXQoA"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/klgej2021/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-1-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10544860 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-1-2-450x58.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="58" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月17日讯】妈妈手里拿着一个小东西，蹑手蹑脚地走来，还露出一抹坏笑，仔细一看，原来是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%99%A4%E6%AF%9B.md#1">除毛</a>贴！再看看客厅，爸爸躺在沙发上睡着了，妈妈原来想<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%81%B6%E4%BD%9C%E5%89%A7.md#1">恶作剧</a>呀！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498766" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/a3073aed344e1b5e12a89e96809e4d11.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10498766 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/a3073aed344e1b5e12a89e96809e4d11-600x533.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="533" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">妈妈手里拿着<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%99%A4%E6%AF%9B.md#1">除毛</a>贴，偷偷摸摸走进客厅。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妈妈小心翼翼地撕开一片又一片的除毛贴，轻轻黏在爸爸的胸膛、肚子还有腋窝。这<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%81%B6%E4%BD%9C%E5%89%A7.md#1">恶作剧</a>真的玩很大欸！身体那么一大片面积都被贴满了，爸爸醒来之后不知要如何面对……</p>
<p>终于，苦主回神了（睡醒了），他发现自己浑身都是奇怪的透明贴纸，马上意识到事态严重！因为妈妈跟女儿已经在旁边笑到不行，爸爸隐约知道自己待会惨了，“听说除毛这玩意儿很痛啊……”爸爸眉头紧蹙，无可奈何地看着身上的除毛贴。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498772" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/a3aa91c4010aeb943dd6a7373b1de7bc.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10498772 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/a3aa91c4010aeb943dd6a7373b1de7bc-600x532.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="532" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">苦主终于回神睡醒了，但是，他马上也会发现自己惨了……（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这时，妈妈“自告奋勇”要帮爸爸撕掉肚子的一张贴布，爸爸虽然内心不愿，也很紧张，也一直拒绝，可是也没有办法！还没等撕，爸爸就先预支恐慌拼命哀号！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498747" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/0fa363d927c93a470cc107f2a4215367.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10498747 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/0fa363d927c93a470cc107f2a4215367-600x535.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="535" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">爸爸不敢相信地看着妈妈，“这全部都要撕下来？！”会不会出人命啊？（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妈妈改变策略，“那换撕掉胸口的好了！”爸爸虽然说不要，但来不及了……“啊！！！！！！！！”撕下来的瞬间仿佛发生惨案！爸爸整个人痛得表情歪七扭八，反应超大，根本是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%BC%B9%E6%B6%82%E9%B1%BC.md#1">弹涂鱼</a>还是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B4%BB%E8%99%BE.md#1">活虾</a>被抓到，蹦个没完！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498774" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/d8ef596b7d89f43910f6f81301554eb4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10498774 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/d8ef596b7d89f43910f6f81301554eb4-600x533.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="533" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">只是撕开一点点就感觉超痛的。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妈妈趁爸爸痛累了，稍微休息时，再撕掉一片胸口的除毛贴。“啊！！！！！！！！”爸爸又是一阵哭天抢地，双手抱头，感觉超崩溃啊！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498782" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/749079eb3d4b8e8f6c3ed67a385eb672.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10498782 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/749079eb3d4b8e8f6c3ed67a385eb672-600x531.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="531" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">妈妈突然撕掉一片除毛贴，痛得爸爸瞬间飙泪！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>但是，更悲剧的还在后头，爸爸突然发现，“这啥？！胳肢窝底下也有？？？！！”不！！！简直不敢相信！应该说，爸爸已经痛到流泪，不想面对了，用手掌掩面。这时，妈妈又趁机再斯一片！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498784" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/aa8c6e312188f8cb3a7c7a1a239b94c9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10498784 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/aa8c6e312188f8cb3a7c7a1a239b94c9-600x534.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="534" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">更悲剧的是，居然连腋窝处也被贴上除毛贴啦！爸爸已崩溃。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>爸爸把五官放到最大！但已经痛到无法发声了。爸爸的情况，跟渔夫把渔获捞到船上，但是画面静音差不多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10498797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/0c5ec828f210a294f28fed8f1fc5fac0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10498797 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/0c5ec828f210a294f28fed8f1fc5fac0-600x536.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="536" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">妈妈又趁爸爸不注意，再撕下一片除毛贴，痛得表情都扭曲了，好可怜啊！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>真是很惨烈啊……虽然女生多少会有除毛的经验，其实并没有像爸爸表现出的那么痛，可是爸爸一觉睡醒就被迫除毛，感觉真的好恐怖喔！妈妈还一直笑，很恶趣味欸！</p>
