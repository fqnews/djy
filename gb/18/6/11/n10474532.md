<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>捡到受伤的雏鸟 换成是你会怎么做？</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/g"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tx281/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10445614 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年06月18日讯】这位仁兄从邻居那得知，邻居饲养的猫叼回了一只小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%8F%E9%B8%9F.md#1">雏鸟</a>，玩了玩却没有兴趣吃掉它，就扔到一旁了，好心肠的他就把这只雏鸟带回家照顾了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10474476" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-92.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10474476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/1-92-600x518.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="518" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一个好心人救了一只<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%9F%E5%91%BD.md#1">生命</a>垂危的小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%8F%E9%B8%9F.md#1">雏鸟</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>回去发现小雏鸟既瘦弱又一身伤，只好赶快给他温暖舒适的地方，都安顿好了之后，看见瘦小的小雏鸟一脸<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A5%A5%E8%82%A0%E8%BE%98%E8%BE%98.md#1">饥肠辘辘</a>的样子，就马不停蹄地为他准备了食物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10478234" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-74-600x515-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10478234 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/2-74-600x515-1.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="514" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">好心人试图喂食，小雏鸟却不肯吃。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>谁知道都放在嘴旁了，这小家伙却不赏脸，怎么样喂就是不肯吃，东摆摆头、西摆摆头，躲食物躲得远远的，无可奈何之下，他只能先暂时放下小雏鸟去找办法，找来找去，似乎方法都不对，始终无法让小家伙把这口食物咽下去。</p>
<p>最后走投无路之际，他灵光一现，决定观察母鸟如何喂食小鸟，就在鸟巢旁架了摄影机，在几次尝试下小雏鸟总算是把食物吃下肚了，原来不是不爱吃，而是方法不对呀！实际上可是个大吃货。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10474479" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-69.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10474479 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/3-69-600x518.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="518" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">好心人观察野外母鸟如何喂食小鸟后，小雏鸟愿意一口接一口吃。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了成长就是要不停的吃。就这样一天天下去，小雏鸟越长越大，身上的羽毛也一根根的开始长齐，他也越照顾越有心得，总算是让一个小小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%9F%E5%91%BD.md#1">生命</a>茁壮了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10474480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-63.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10474480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/4-63-600x518.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="518" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小雏鸟原来是个大吃货。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>常常有小动物在野外受了伤，没办法自愈，虽然偶然地被人捡回照顾，但往往都有案例，在不熟悉动物习性又没有寻求专业的帮助下，往往每况愈下甚至造成遗憾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10478212" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/admin-ajax.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10478212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/admin-ajax.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="521" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小小生命茁壮啰！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>生命无论大小都是生命，这位好心的人虽然在一开始遇到了不少困难，但他不气馁并寻求真正的“专业”经验，成功地让生命垂危的雏鸟，长大成美丽的飞鸟，他的精神值得我们学习呀。</p>
