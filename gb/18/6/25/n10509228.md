<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10603615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年08月17日讯】<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B9%8C%E9%B8%A6.md#1">乌鸦</a>是一般人印象中不太正面的鸟类，被抓到了喔！没事就爱<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%AC%BA%E8%B4%9F.md#1">欺负</a>别人，尤其是无害的猫猫<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>，更成了它们捉弄打压的对象。不知有多少次，网友抓到乌鸦就是偷啄小动物的“现行犯”！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10509234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/dadb5fa1b3ea7c1eef12bd6fa9e34212.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10509234 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/dadb5fa1b3ea7c1eef12bd6fa9e34212-600x519.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="519" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一只<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B9%8C%E9%B8%A6.md#1">乌鸦</a>心怀不轨，偷偷从背后接近小狗。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这支影片中的小狗，其实只是网路上众多“受害者”之一，冰山一角而已吧！小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8B%97%E7%8B%97.md#1">狗狗</a>跟着主人在散步，坏心眼的乌鸦偷偷地从背后靠近，用鸟喙瞄准狗狗的小屁屁用力一啄！痛得小狗狗瞬间弹跳！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10509235" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/56491e62a734c2e8501cc0a2dc91a8cf.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10509235 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/56491e62a734c2e8501cc0a2dc91a8cf-600x518.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="518" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">趁狗狗不注意，乌鸦马上偷袭它。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看小狗狗被啄到的瞬间，反射性地全身缩起弹跳避开，完全不只是单纯的惊吓而已！它是真的被乌鸦狠狠啄了一下呀！小狗狗张大嘴巴似乎是没想到会被喙了一下，吓了一跳赶快跟乌鸦保持距离。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10509236" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/87231567c8d633fecbe83a888b0ad126.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10509236 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/87231567c8d633fecbe83a888b0ad126-600x519.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="519" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用力一啄！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>狗狗的主人连忙站到乌鸦和小狗中间，也不能说居中调停，但至少希望乌鸦识相一点别再<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%AC%BA%E8%B4%9F.md#1">欺负</a>人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10509255" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/c998f5a8c5ce81be50935173ac3c6fda.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10509255 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/c998f5a8c5ce81be50935173ac3c6fda-600x517.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="517" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">狗狗惊到，完全弹跳起来！（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次虽然就此消停了，但主人自己最好也小心一点！因为乌鸦基本上不怕人，也有过飞抓人的各种物品、啄人头顶、拉人眼皮等各种攻击前科。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10509244" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/370e3298285f09d27f68b11b551bf033.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10509244 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/370e3298285f09d27f68b11b551bf033-600x519.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="519" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">无辜的狗狗吓坏了，只好赶快躲到主人身边。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其实乌鸦基本上应该是不怕任何动物吧？除了人跟小动物，老鹰、猫头鹰、大型犬甚至北极熊，都是乌鸦捉弄欺负的对象！看来为了“人身安全”还是不要随便招惹这只很狂的黑色小鸟比较好！</p>
