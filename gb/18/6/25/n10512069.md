  <p>上半场夸雷斯马（Quaresma）外脚背撩射为<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84%E7%89%99.md#1">葡萄牙</a>队首开纪录，下半场C罗（Cristiano Ronaldo）点球被扑出，终场前<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%BC%8A%E6%9C%97.md#1">伊朗</a>队的安萨里法德（Ansarifard）点球扳平救主。</p>
  <p>比赛一开场，葡萄牙队迅速占据场上主动，第3分钟，C罗插入禁区后突施冷箭，皮球被贝兰万德（Ali Beiranvand）扑出。4分钟后，伊朗队反击，贾汉巴赫什（Jahanbakhsh）右路反击中突破后分球，阿里米（Amiri）没有打上力量偏出。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10512151" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-983788040.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10512151" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-983788040-600x376.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="376" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">C罗转身拿球时，身后同时有3名伊朗队员防守。(Photo by Hector Vivas/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>第9分钟，若奥．马里奥（Joao Mario）传中，埃扎托拉希（Ezatolahi）解围和门将贝兰万德相撞成空门，快马赶到的马里奥面对近在咫尺的空门放了高炮，错失最佳进球良机。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10512153" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-983728160.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10512153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-983728160-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">开场10分钟伊朗队一次回防酿成失误送给对手打空门的机会，随后两人都有推搡的动作，波斯队的内讧不稀奇，2006年世界杯就曾爆出内讧新闻 。(Photo by Hector Vivas/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>第45分钟，阿德里安．席尔瓦（Andre Silva）巧妙的脚后跟分球，夸雷斯马接球后稍做调整，在禁区前沿右脚外脚背撩射，皮球在空中划出弧线飞进网内，葡萄牙队以1比0取得领先优势结束上半场。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10512121" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/983809452.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10512121 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/983809452-600x347.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="347" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">C罗面对伊朗队的挑衅，笑脸领黄牌。(Photo by Shaun Botterill/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>下半场易地再战。数据统计显示葡萄牙队场上控球时间一度高达70%，完全压制伊朗队。第50分钟出现有争议判罚，埃扎托拉希在禁区内绊倒C罗，主裁判卡塞雷斯经过VAR认定改判点球。</p>
  <p>C罗亲自操刀主罚，皮球却被贝兰万德扑出抱在怀里。伊朗队逃过一劫。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10512142" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-983789452.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10512142" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-983789452-600x360.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">C罗点球被扑出，粉丝们高呼“没关系，梅西也没罚进”。(Photo by Jan Kruger/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10512152" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-983787086.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10512152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/GettyImages-983787086-600x358.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="358" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">伊朗队传奇门将贝兰万德（Ali Beiranvand）扑出C罗的点球，凭借这一精彩扑救必定身价大涨。(Photo by Jan Kruger/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>第82分钟再次出现争议判罚，C罗在无球状态下遭遇波拉里甘吉（Pouraliganji）故意身体阻挡，C罗转身摆脱对方摔倒在地，裁判在第二次查看VAR后给C罗出示黄牌警告。伊朗队全体队员高举双手十分兴奋。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10512116" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/983816652.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10512116 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/983816652-600x391.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">巴拉圭主裁判卡塞雷斯全场比赛三次查看VAR也是累得满头大汗。(Photo by Shaun Botterill/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>第90分钟，阿兹蒙（Azmoun）在禁区内头球打在防守球员塞德里克（Cedric）的手臂上，主裁判第三次查看VAR认定改判点球。随后安萨里法德操刀主罚命中右上角，伊朗终场前将比分扳成1比1平。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10512108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/983817060.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10512108 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/06/983817060-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">伊朗球迷绝望的眼神，不过这届伊朗国家队的世界杯之旅成绩已经很好了。(Photo by Laurence Griffiths/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>与此同时，另一赛场比赛结果传来，西班牙以2比2战平摩洛哥，与葡萄牙队同为1胜2平积5分一同出线，伊朗3战1胜1平1负积4分，与摩洛哥1平2负积1分同时被淘汰。#</p>
