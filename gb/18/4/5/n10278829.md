<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：没到日本赏樱 不要说你看够了樱花！</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7536-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>生命的花季。东京立川樱花满开。（容乃加／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<h2><span style="color: #ff99cc;">?一夜东风发千树 万粉千红总是樱</span></h2>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>并不是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC.md#1">日本</a>特有的，然而日本的确形成了特有的樱花文化。说真格的，没有到过日本<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E6%A8%B1.md#1">赏樱</a>花，不要说你已经看够了樱花！</p>
<p>今年大自然季候的改变很明显，从<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A8%B1%E8%8A%B1.md#1">樱花</a>身上尤其看得见。<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC.md#1">日本</a>的樱花期比去年提早了4天，比起历年的平均纪录，更是提早了9天左右。日本京城<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E4%BA%AC.md#1">东京</a>一地，像是上野公园、隅田川、立川、千鸟渊……等等樱花名胜之所，水边、公园的染井吉野樱都提早开放了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10278893" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7527.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10278893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7527-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E4%BA%AC.md#1">东京</a>立川染井吉野樱盛放。（容乃加／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7637967" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/b8c402703e1fae4520d7628b85929897.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-7637967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/b8c402703e1fae4520d7628b85929897-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">日本东京武藏野染井吉野樱初开。（容乃加／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10278914" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7531.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10278914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7531-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">东京立川樱<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E5%AD%A3.md#1">花季</a>（容乃加／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一夜东风发千树，万粉千白总是樱。春樱早放，美丽不减；然而，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E5%AD%A3.md#1">花季</a>错落不得赏的心情，比起悄悄然的落樱，又多几分愕然、平添几声叹息！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10278993" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/25ef1bae0b1f6cfab1bfd3b4bfac4beb.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10278993" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/25ef1bae0b1f6cfab1bfd3b4bfac4beb-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">东京野川公园樱花。（容乃加 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>东京新宿闹区的新宿御苑里有近千株的樱花；今年清明时节许多追逐樱花的雅客聚集到此来<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B5%8F%E6%A8%B1.md#1">赏樱</a>。御苑内云集着四海的赏花客；放眼园内，此处、彼处芳树上，一团团的樱花朵窃窃私语，芳树下一簇簇“花见”的醉花客谈天说地。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10279008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7581.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10279008" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7581-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">东京新宿御苑樱花季，芳树下的醉花人。（容乃加 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10279004" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7568.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10279004 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7568-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">东京新宿御苑樱花季，芳树下一簇簇“花见”的醉花客。图为粉嫩的“一叶樱”。（容乃加 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #ff00ff;">生命的花季</span></h2>
<p>清明时节，染井吉野樱花木上已经长着嫩绿新叶，本来，这是寻常的吉野樱满开时分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10279013" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7595.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10279013 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7595-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">染井吉野樱花木上已经长着嫩绿新叶。（容乃加 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新宿御苑的樱花有30多种，除了常见的粉白染井吉野樱之外，不同种类的樱花次第绽放，花期持续一个月。今年清明时节的御苑，一叶、普贤象、关山、郁金等等暮春樱花花种盛放着，嫩粉、粉红、深粉红和浅黄，浅浅深深、深深浅浅，铺天盖地，交织出大自然的锦绣和规律。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10279012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7590.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10279012" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7590-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">东京新宿御苑普贤象樱花。（容乃加 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10279007" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7580.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10279007 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7580-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">东京新宿御苑“关山樱”满开。（容乃加 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10278942" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7574.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10278942 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7574-e1522908617884-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">东京新宿御苑“郁金樱”，花色嫩黄映着微绿。（容乃加 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10279010" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7584.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10279010 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7584-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">东京新宿御苑里，嫩粉、粉红、深粉红和浅黄，浅浅深深、深深浅浅，铺天盖地的樱花，交织出大自然的锦绣和规律。（容乃加 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>盛放时华华硕大之美、纤纤可怜之美，在樱花木上都看得见。樱花的花季，花蕾初绽雀跃、欢颜盛放夺彩、倏忽飘然急落，承载着大自然、天宇间成住坏灭的规律，那一朵朵悠然的粉靥底面，切切诉说着生命精彩尽放在一瞬<span style="font-weight: 400;">──</span>不可多得。从悠悠天宇来看人间，人的生命不也如同樱花一般吗<span style="font-weight: 400;">──</span>不可多得、不待多留！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10279005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7570.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10279005" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/IMG_7570-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">东京新宿御苑樱花季，生命精彩尽放在一瞬－－不可多得。（容乃加 ／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
