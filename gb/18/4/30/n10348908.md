<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>让人受不了的滑嫩 日式煎蛋包快来尝一口</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/4fc0d011e899a5eb8654717156cb41da-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10611616 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年04月30日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">这道看似绵软蓬松的日式<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%85%8E%E8%9B%8B.md#1">煎蛋</a>包，究竟有什么玄妙之处？它令人为之惊艳的厉害独到之处，就在于切开的那一瞬间。秘</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">密揭晓前，先看看料理师傅怎么处理这道“功夫菜”吧！</span></p>
<p>制作过程，首先是寻常的炒饭备料。看着大厨熟练翻腾着炒饭，一派轻松，还面带微笑，随着厨具和食材舞动的声响，搭配四处飘散的美味香气，想必厨房里的氛围让人忍不住食指大动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10348973" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/408ad3de601b9f060a8ec8b559823625.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10348973 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/408ad3de601b9f060a8ec8b559823625-600x531.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="531" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大厨熟练翻腾着炒饭，一派轻松。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">完成料理后的盛装摆盘，热腾腾的湿润炒饭，不断冒出香气逼人的蒸气，接着，大厨准备了一个“透明”的大盘子，把炒饭扣过来，让炒饭在盘子上呈现容器的弧度，基础的料理完成了。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10348976" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/2da4a33c78be177596c2a92c31162ce2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10348976 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/2da4a33c78be177596c2a92c31162ce2-600x533.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="533" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">热腾腾的湿润炒饭，不断冒出香气逼人的蒸气。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">紧接着要准备绵软蓬松的“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E5%8C%85.md#1">蛋包</a>”，师傅以熟练的技巧烘蛋，没多久时间，蛋包已经可以出炉，准备放到炒饭上。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10348980" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/a0115fb59cce32a8cf7ffe1b0fa9c51f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10348980 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/a0115fb59cce32a8cf7ffe1b0fa9c51f-600x536.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="536" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">把炒饭扣过来，让炒饭在盘子上呈现容器的弧度，基础的料理完成了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">看起来明明是炒蛋的料理，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E5%8C%85.md#1">蛋包</a>却像海绵蛋糕一样蓬松，那份软绵绵的触感，仿佛西式蛋糕一样让人感到甜蜜、可爱；鲜嫩宜人的色泽，又像芒果布丁、又像克林姆，但它却是货真价实的烘蛋蛋包。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10348983" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/ace87b14f6c6cfaea8021062eb697de3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10348983 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/ace87b14f6c6cfaea8021062eb697de3-600x506.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="506" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蛋包却像海绵蛋糕一样蓬松，那份软绵绵的触感，又像芒果布丁、又像克林姆。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">看了这么久，到底能不能开吃了呢？乔好绝佳角度，大厨要准备进行最后一个步骤——切开蛋包！轻轻划开，湿润鲜美的起司蛋包就这么绽放在饕客眼前。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10348987" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/b7dd0902a90e17ed40019e34f9efcc95.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10348987 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/b7dd0902a90e17ed40019e34f9efcc95-600x532.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="532" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">仿佛西式蛋糕一样让人感到甜蜜可爱的蛋包，被轻轻划开。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">淋上特制的酱汁，金黄滑嫩的蛋包与色泽厚实饱满的酱汁，调和出富有视觉冲击的美味飨宴。而蛋包柔嫩的内里此时更是完美呈现，酱汁从上方缓缓落下的微微冲击，也能改变软嫩蛋包的形状，可见其入口即化的鲜嫩。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10349000" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/74f1895c2a4f11f1506d1c06865ef6cd.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10349000 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/74f1895c2a4f11f1506d1c06865ef6cd-600x532.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="532" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湿润鲜美的起司蛋包绽放在饕客眼前！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">看起来真的好好吃喔！一旁的镁光灯和此起彼落的惊呼声，已经让饕客快要受不了，好想赶快咬一口，大快朵颐！</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10349004" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/7445e69afa186a40f2fb1ed5c23b2fab.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10349004 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/7445e69afa186a40f2fb1ed5c23b2fab-600x536.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="536" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">酱汁从上方缓缓落下，改变软嫩蛋包的形状，可见其入口即化的鲜嫩。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
