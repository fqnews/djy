  <p>在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%92%99%E7%89%B9%E5%88%A9%E5%B0%94.md#1">蒙特利尔</a>以北的一个河滨豪宅社区里，Ambienti Design创意团队完成了一个法式乡村风格的软装项目，从脚下的地毯到头顶的水晶吊灯，全部由Ambienti Design提供。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10289123" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180409_HuaXue_Montreal_Ambienti1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10289123 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180409_HuaXue_Montreal_Ambienti1-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="302" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Ambienti Design的豪宅<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%B1%85%E8%AE%BE%E8%AE%A1.md#1">家居设计</a>。（Ambienti Design提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>按照屋主的期望，家的氛围要温馨和放松，格调上要优雅和古典。创意团队确定了以法国乡村风格为基调，采用古典而简约的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%B7.md#1">家具</a>，加上明亮淡雅色调的铺陈，配上雅致的干燥花和各类艺术品。</p>
  <p>弯曲线条是法式乡村风格<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%B7.md#1">家具</a>的特点。椅腿、桌脚、靠背、扶手、柜面等部位，均呈现各种不同形状的弯形造型。曲线轮廓和细节雕饰，给人以古典的雅致和华贵感，凸显屋主的生活品味。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10289125" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180409_HuaXue_Montreal_Ambienti3-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10289125 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180409_HuaXue_Montreal_Ambienti3-1-450x678.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="678" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Ambienti Design的豪宅家居设计。（Ambienti Design提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10289126" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180409_HuaXue_Montreal_Ambienti4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10289126 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180409_HuaXue_Montreal_Ambienti4-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Ambienti Design的豪宅家居设计。（Ambienti Design提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10289127" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180409_HuaXue_Montreal_Ambienti5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10289127 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180409_HuaXue_Montreal_Ambienti5-450x617.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="617" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Ambienti Design的豪宅家居设计。（Ambienti Design提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10289128" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180409_HuaXue_Montreal_Ambienti6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10289128 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/20180409_HuaXue_Montreal_Ambienti6-450x481.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="481" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Ambienti Design为豪宅家居提供的吊灯。（Ambienti Design提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>从起源上看，法式乡村风格是讲究工艺美的法国宫廷风格流入民间后发展而成的，在民间它逐渐去掉了繁复的细节，趋于质朴和内敛。</p>
  <p>这些曲线、弧线的运用，可以为屋内的空间感增添趣味，在方正感很强的居室里，多了一分流畅而又和谐多变的美感。</p>
  <p>Ambienti创意团队发挥灵思妙想，精心挑选多个不同品牌的法式乡村家具，从客厅到卧室，从书房到餐厅，优雅与温馨融为一体，精致无处不在。</p>
  <p>在蒙特利尔家具市场上经营20年的的Ambienti Design，定位于豪华、优雅和精致的家居设计。从魁北克到美国，他们为很多家庭提供了豪华住宅软装的全包服务。</p>
