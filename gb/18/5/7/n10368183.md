  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%AB%8B%E5%A4%8F.md#1">立夏</a>时，自然界的变化是阳气渐长、阴气渐弱；相对人体脏腑来说，是肝气渐弱，心气渐强。所以立夏养生重养心。“色赤入心”，吃些红色食材可养心，像是番茄、红萝卜、苹果、红豆。</p>
  <p>“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E7%8F%AD%E7%89%99%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84%E5%86%B7%E6%B1%A4.md#1">西班牙番茄冷汤</a>”，是很适合立夏的料理。番茄是养心的红色食材，而冷汤的清凉爽口也可以解燥热。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">5步做<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E7%8F%AD%E7%89%99%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84%E5%86%B7%E6%B1%A4.md#1">西班牙番茄冷汤</a></span></h2>  <p>西班牙番茄冷汤源自西班牙南部安逹鲁西亚，是当地夏季的常见料理。因为那里的夏季来得很早，而且非常炎热，温度可高逹40度以上。因此当地人喜欢在饭前先来碗冷汤，喝下后暑气全消，而且是用新鲜蔬果制成，非常营养健康喔。</p>
  <p><strong>食材：</strong>大番茄 1颗、小番茄 4～5个、青椒 1/2个、红甜椒 1/2个、小黄瓜 1/2条、洋葱 1/4个、面包几块、蒜瓣 1个。</p>
  <p><strong>调味料：</strong>橄榄油 250ml、清鸡汤 250ml、50ml 红酒醋、孜然粉 1小匙、玫块盐 1小匙、黑胡椒适量。</p>
  <p><strong>用具：</strong>果汁机一台。</p>
  <p style="text-align: center;"><div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" src=""></a><a href="https://git.io/JUHZ1"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/gvzhui3753/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/wjfulmwAttg?wmode=transparent&#038;wmode=opaque" allowfullscreen></a>  	</div></p>
  <p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #808080;"><em>（完整制作视频，制作步骤如下。）</em></span></p>
  <p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
  <p>1. 准备好果汁机。将洋葱、青椒、红甜椒、小黄瓜、大小番茄切成小块，和面包一起放入果汁机。面包用于增加汁的浓稠度，可选法棍或欧式面包。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10368336" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Lengtang1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10368336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Lengtang1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">西班牙番茄冷汤做法：食材切块，放入果汁机。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 依次倒入初榨橄榄油、清鸡汤及红酒醋。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10368343" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Lengtang2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10368343" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Lengtang2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">西班牙番茄冷汤做法：依次倒入初榨橄榄油、清鸡汤及红酒醋。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 洒上玫瑰盐、黑胡椒，放些许孜然粉增添风味，再丢入一粒蒜瓣。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10368345" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Lengtang3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10368345" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Lengtang3-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">西班牙番茄冷汤做法：放调味料。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4. 启动果汁机，将所有食材打至浓稠滑顺。随后，将打好打冷汤倒入碗中。倾倒前可以先过滤汤渣，增加口感的细腻度。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10368347" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Lengtang4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10368347" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Lengtang4-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">西班牙番茄冷汤做法：打好的冷汤倒入碗中。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5. 在冷汤上洒上面包丁、番茄丁，最后滴几滴橄榄油。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10368351" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Lengtang5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10368351" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/Lengtang5-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">西班牙番茄冷汤做法：食用前滴几滴橄榄油。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">·?<a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/18/5/4/n10362780.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">立夏吃瓜 补水又减肥 5种瓜各有养生功效</a></span></h2>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">·?<a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/18/4/20/n10321798.md#1">医师用超简单“番茄减肥法” 一个月瘦11公斤</a></span></h2>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">·?<a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/18/1/8/n10038651.md#1">思虑太过小心血虚！中医：吃红黑食物补养血</a></span></h2>  <p>&lt;点阅<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #008000;"><a style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;" href="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光系列 节气+营养料理</a></span></span>&gt;</p>
