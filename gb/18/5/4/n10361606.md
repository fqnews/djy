<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>野生蜘蛛人飞檐走壁 徒手攀墙连4蹬迅速攻顶</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/01/150104152549100574-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>优秀的跑酷人，令人热血沸腾。（MARK RALSTON/AFP/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10611616 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/complete-video-link-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月06日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">想像蜘蛛人一样飞檐走壁？看过现代版忍者吗？眼前这位帅气小哥，徒手爬墙（确定没有吊钢丝），展现惊人的爆发力！</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10364486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-27.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10364486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1-27-600x533.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="533" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">这位帅气小哥，徒手爬墙。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10364488" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-14.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10364488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/3-14-600x534.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="534" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小帅哥助跑几步，右脚蹬上右侧墙。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>眼前三面建筑墙柱，是小哥这次的征服目标，经过数次练习后，小哥这次要展现成果啦！预备备～GO！</p>
<p>小帅哥助跑几步，右脚蹬上右侧墙，顺势转身，两脚踩上中间墙面用力蹬，再转向左侧踩墙后，伸出右手准备勾住上方墙面，“哎呀！差一点点。”别气馁，再一次。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10364487" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-21.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10364487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/2-21-600x533.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="533" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用双手力量将全身往上拉，顺利站上墙上啦～（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小哥开始第二次挑战，再次助跑，连四蹬后，伸出长长右手臂，用五指与掌心力量紧紧抓住了墙面，左手再伸出勾墙，用双手力量将全身往上拉，顺利站上墙上啦～身手真不赖！</p>
<p>挑战成功后，再来进阶版，这次免助跑，直接来！看小哥面对墙面用力一跳，蹬右墙、左墙、右墙，最后再蹬一下左墙，伸出手一勾，成功达标，小哥露出喜悦的成就感，看了令人热血沸腾！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10364490" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10364490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5-10-600x533.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="533" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">看小哥面对墙面用力一跳，蹬右墙、左墙、右墙，最后再蹬一下左墙。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>事实上，这种利用城市间各类建筑设施当障碍或辅助，迅速跑跳、穿行的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9E%81%E9%99%90%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8.md#1">极限运动</a>被称为“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B7%91%E9%85%B7.md#1">跑酷</a>”，是在1980年代晚期，从法国兴起的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A1%97%E5%A4%B4.md#1">街头</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9E%81%E9%99%90%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8.md#1">极限运动</a>“Parkour”。</p>
<p>与武术不同之处在于，武术注重格斗反击，而<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B7%91%E9%85%B7.md#1">跑酷</a>起源于“紧急脱逃”。想成为优秀的跑酷人，需要有很高的身体素质，包括爆发力、弹跳、柔韧性等，练习时要非常专注，需拥有强大的毅力与信念，克服恐惧感！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10364489" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4-11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10364489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/4-11-600x534.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="534" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">伸出手一勾，成功达标！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
