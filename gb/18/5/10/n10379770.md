<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小猫伸出猫爪 坐车享受惬意午后时光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/01975db7db79bdfc950d341741ce3a4d-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>猫咪静静的享受悠哉的午后时光。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/video-link-3-450x38.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10327568 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/04/video-link-3-450x38.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="38" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年05月13日讯】一台正在驶向前的车上，车子内正躲着一只小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E5%92%AA.md#1">猫咪</a>，小猫咪其实躲在窗边的小架子上，正在观望车上的乘客，对着他们摆动着小小的尾巴，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%BA%AB%E5%8F%97.md#1">享受</a>着饲主对它投射的怜爱目光，且静静的看着窗外，享受<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%82%A0%E5%93%89.md#1">悠哉</a>的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%88%E5%90%8E.md#1">午后</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%85%89.md#1">时光</a>。</p>
<p>车子往前迈进，市区的道路平整，因此车子并不会震来震去，这只小猫在车子内感到安稳，并不会因为是在车子内，所以感觉到紧张与难耐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10379940" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/01975db7db79bdfc950d341741ce3a4d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10379940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/01975db7db79bdfc950d341741ce3a4d-600x425.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="425" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AB%E5%92%AA.md#1">猫咪</a>窝在车内窗边的小架子上，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%BA%AB%E5%8F%97.md#1">享受</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%82%A0%E5%93%89.md#1">悠哉</a>的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%88%E5%90%8E.md#1">午后</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%85%89.md#1">时光</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>往来的人们看见这只小猫，感到惊喜与奇妙，没想到车子内居然会有只猫。看见可爱的小猫，饲主仿佛忘记生活里的紧张感，想与小猫互动。</p>
<p>这只小猫愿意帮助人类纾解紧张情绪，小猫只要一眨眼一开嘴，人们都为它为之疯狂，觉得实在太“卡哇伊”。在这个悠哉的午后，这只小猫享受着与人类互动的时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10379941" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/fc9ec2e677c0ef8e8bf4fc089bfacf1b.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10379941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/fc9ec2e677c0ef8e8bf4fc089bfacf1b-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">猫咪窝在车内窗边的小架子上，充满好奇的看着周围。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10379944" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5191f67d51caced35ba92ee16121aae4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10379944" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/5191f67d51caced35ba92ee16121aae4-600x436.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="436" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">猫咪静静的享受悠哉的午后时光。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小猫在车子内不会乱跳，不会干扰驾驶开车，因次饲主也放心让小猫留在车子里面，塞车时看着小猫伸出淘气的猫爪，焦虑的心情就会一扫而空，舒缓紧张情绪比听音乐还有效果。</p>
<p>有时车子等红绿灯时，路人看见小猫忍不住投射目光，小猫仿佛成为一位大明星，饲主也愿意与人分享这只小猫的可爱身影，愿意带着他一起开车上路。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10379945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/37efede41c9ce56db21617bb469d38b2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10379945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/37efede41c9ce56db21617bb469d38b2-600x415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">猫咪优雅的侧躺，充满好奇的看着窗外街景。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
