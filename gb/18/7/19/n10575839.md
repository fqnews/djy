  <figure id="attachment_10575881" style="width: 445px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_1163-e1532039991873.jpeg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_1163-e1532039991873.jpeg" alt="" width="445" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图：厨师Chi获奖。（Chi越南时尚餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">坐在富有浪漫情调的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">越南时尚餐馆</a>（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi Modern Vietnamese Kitchen</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，环顾周围的壁画、中国瓷器与越南器皿，品尝着地道美味的越南餐，不仅体验到厨师的烹饪技艺，而且仿佛沐浴在家的温暖中。</span></p>
  <h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">无心插柳柳成荫?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></h2>  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">朋友家人都喜欢她的烹饪，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2014</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年，她的朋友背着她在越南报名，让她参加</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">MasterChef</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">厨艺比赛。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">居然脱颖而出成为大赛亚军，比赛期间，已经有真人秀节日与她签约，要她撰写越南菜谱书。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></p>
  <figure id="attachment_10575876" style="width: 497px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/14110425328-e1532039902753.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/14110425328-e1532039902753.jpg" alt="" width="497" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Chi越南时尚餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10575883" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_1178-e1532039738604.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575883" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_1178-e1532039738604.jpg" alt="" width="338" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Chi越南时尚餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10575885" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_2250-e1532039771134.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_2250-e1532039771134.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Chi越南时尚餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10575886" style="width: 338px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_2251-e1532039789498.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/IMG_2251-e1532039789498.jpg" alt="" width="338" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Chi越南时尚餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“有时，我们并不清楚自己的天赋和才能。”</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">对此感慨很多，比赛后，她的人生也因此重新定位。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">11</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个兄弟姐妹中排名最小，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">从小就喜欢观看母亲煮食；结婚生子后，家人朋友都称赞她的厨艺，儿女嚷嚷着要吃妈妈做的。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">迈进饮食业，许多机遇接踵而来。电视台找她做节目，她写了两本烹饪书，教授烹饪技巧，在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">温哥华美食</a>节上展示烹饪技术，等等。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2016</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年秋，她开办了自己的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">越南时尚餐馆</a>；</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2018</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月，餐馆荣获《</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Where</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">》杂志读者选出的最佳温哥华亚洲餐馆。</span></p>
  <h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">“越南菜系其实很丰富” </span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></h2>  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">提到越南菜，人们可能想到常见的越南菜系。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">却认为，越南菜受到两千年中国文化的熏陶，以及一百年法国美食的影响，演绎成为一个精美丰富的菜系。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">想再进一步发扬光大，选用卑诗健康食材，传统越南食谱，加上传统与创新的烹饪方法，烹制出更好的美味佳肴。她分享说，能做到这一点，就不能偷工减料与简化烹制程序了。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“品尝起来是一样的越南菜，质量更好，食材更佳，烹饪方法有所不同，带给人升华后的味蕾享受。”</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">她对此信心满满，“非常美味！”</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></p>
  <figure id="attachment_10575880" style="width: 497px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/14110424944-e1532039815390.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/14110424944-e1532039815390.jpg" alt="" width="497" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Chi越南时尚餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10575878" style="width: 497px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/14110424766-e1532039870949.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10575878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/14110424766-e1532039870949.jpg" alt="" width="497" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Chi越南时尚餐厅提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">越南菜深受中国菜的影响，她解析说，一道真正美食是需要功夫的，比如名厨烹饪的汤，熬汤时间很长，烹饪技巧也远超过一般菜系等。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">餐馆提供了各类越南特色餐，比如蛋咖啡就是一越南特色饮品，用蛋黄加炼奶搅拌，再导入咖啡而成，也是战时缺电又没有机器时期的独创品。</span></p>
  <h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">星期天米其林星级贵宾餐 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></h2>  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“如果你恨谁，就让他开餐馆！”</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">说后哈哈大笑。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">则选择了经营餐馆，推出自己钟爱的时尚美食，只因为她太爱烹饪，太喜欢美食，渴望与人分享。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">15</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日起，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">越南时尚餐馆将再推荐：星期日的米其林星级贵宾餐，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">款菜肴仅需</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">80</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">加元，不是平时的菜谱，而是</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">特意精选，采用本地新鲜健康食材，期望给顾客带来惊喜与星级享受。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></p>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chi</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">表示，应该是时机成熟了，顾客也一再催促她，终于能够实现自己一直梦寐以求的这个愿望，为顾客烹制一些特别美味餐饮。</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />  </span></p>
