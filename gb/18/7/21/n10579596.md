<h2>食材预制：</h2>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">一、盐水浸<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1.md#1">鸡</a></span></h3>
<p>4汤匙盐（1汤匙合15毫升）<br />
2汤匙糖<br />
4汤匙意大利调味料<br />
1只全鸡</p>
<p>放入水中混合后将鸡放入盐水中，冷藏12至18小时后取出，晾1小时。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">二、蔬菜烤盘</span></h3>
<p>2整个胡萝卜<br />
2个生玉米<br />
?块芋头<br />
?花椰菜（西兰花）<br />
1洋葱<br />
1茶匙盐（约合5毫升）<br />
?茶匙黑胡椒<br />
1汤匙橄榄油</p>
<p>蔬菜切块，洋葱切片，混合调味料，在烤盘上“搅拌”一下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10581054" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/56fd78ea8abc2cc57eaf9be7a8f3f76a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10581054 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/56fd78ea8abc2cc57eaf9be7a8f3f76a-450x423.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="423" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">正在制作“蔬菜烤盘”。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">三、大蒜奶油</span></h3>
<p>?杯无盐奶油（一杯约等于240毫升）<br />
1茶匙盐<br />
2茶匙洋香菜<br />
2茶匙大蒜（切碎）</p>
<p>搅匀后，用手分离鸡胸部位的肉、皮，塞入中间，压一压，使分布均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10581055" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/db3ab666a4b687bdd30e958612a20b0c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10581055 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/db3ab666a4b687bdd30e958612a20b0c-450x424.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="424" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大蒜奶油。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">四、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a>水与柠檬</span></h3>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a>和水混合均匀，刷于鸡的表皮。柠檬一颗切两半，放入鸡的胸腔内。</p>
<h2>烤制方法：</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10581050" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/e68c11cb65ca69af407e14d82f73e154.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10581050 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/e68c11cb65ca69af407e14d82f73e154-450x414.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="414" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">记得将鸡脚绑起来。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>把鸡的两腿用线绑起来，与蔬菜烤盘放一起，翅膀转于鸡身下。</p>
<p>放进烤箱，温度设为摄氏200度（华氏400度），烤40分钟后，挑出煮熟的花椰菜（西兰花）。</p>
<p>把烤盘水平旋转180度，继续烤约40分钟，温度不变，时间可根据鸡的大小和口感喜好调整。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #993300;">大功告成啰！</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_10581034" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/5cf6451c1675510041a19e06471d7fa2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10581034 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/5cf6451c1675510041a19e06471d7fa2-450x416.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%83%A4%E9%B8%A1.md#1">烤鸡</a>完成啰！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>说明：</h2>
<h3><span style="color: #808000;">1. 关于味道</span></h3>
<p>预先浸泡调料水，不仅可为烤鸡增添风味，还可使鸡肉更加柔嫩滋润。最少要泡4小时，泡一整晚更好。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #808000;">2. 关于外观</span></h3>
<p>金色的烤鸡看起来非常开胃，其中蜂蜜水有很大功劳，抹在鸡的表皮，能促进<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%85%A5%E8%84%86.md#1">酥脆</a>金色外观的形成。</p>
<p>制作过程中的“绑脚”也很重要，有助于保持烤鸡形状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10581044" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/10908822fa224b296d5676ba8a91fc19.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10581044 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/10908822fa224b296d5676ba8a91fc19-450x401.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%85%A5%E8%84%86.md#1">酥脆</a>的外皮。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #808000;">3. 如何判断烤鸡熟了？</span></h3>
<p>如果各位有“肉类温度计”（meat thermometer），可以探测鸡肉最厚的部分，若读出165℉，代表烤鸡已经完成啰。如果没有这种特殊的温度计，可以将烤鸡倾斜，让汁水流出，如果是“清澈”的，那就代表完成啰！</p>
