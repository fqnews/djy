<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大厨示范马铃薯炖鸡 原来鸡小腿是这样去骨的</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/40d811be5357568458c4f2c4b773b359-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美味的马铃薯炖鸡。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月24日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%85%BF.md#1">鸡腿</a>是鸡肉中最美味的部分之一，如果和其它好吃食材结合炖煮，那味道真是令人垂涎。今天就为各位介绍一道“可乐<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF%E7%82%96%E9%B8%A1.md#1">马铃薯炖鸡</a>”。大概需要花一小时料理，影片中大厨还会展示鸡小腿去骨的刀功。各位准备好了吗？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10584101" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/145a808eee68e9a5941d4f762bf17386.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10584101 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/145a808eee68e9a5941d4f762bf17386-450x436.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="436" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">本篇的主角——<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%85%BF.md#1">鸡腿</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>首先，要备好以下食材：</strong></p>
<p>&#8211; 鸡腿<br />
&#8211; 黑胡椒<br />
&#8211; 玉米淀粉（1汤匙）<br />
&#8211; 橄榄油（3～4汤匙）<br />
&#8211; 酱油（1汤匙）<br />
&#8211; 青葱1或2棵<br />
&#8211; 生姜末（1茶匙）<br />
&#8211; 2、3个蒜瓣（需切碎，1茶匙）<br />
&#8211; 糖（0.5汤匙）<br />
&#8211; 可口可乐（一小罐）<br />
&#8211; 马铃薯（300克）<br />
&#8211;?罗勒叶（20克）</p>
<p>注：1汤匙约15毫升，1茶匙约5毫升。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10584082" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/121d482c621303c09bfaf7679dbc9819.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10584082 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/121d482c621303c09bfaf7679dbc9819-450x360.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="360" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">首先将<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%85%BF%E5%8E%BB%E9%AA%A8.md#1">鸡腿去骨</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>烹饪流程：</strong></p>
<p>1. 用刀将<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%85%BF%E5%8E%BB%E9%AA%A8.md#1">鸡腿去骨</a>。注意大厨的手法哦！</p>
<p>2. 将鸡腿肉切成小块。</p>
<p>3. 将鸡肉放入碗中，加入黑胡椒、玉米淀粉，用手搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10584202" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/d1595fef0cad7561ce03299928907cf2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10584202 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/d1595fef0cad7561ce03299928907cf2-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用手搅拌肌肉。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将橄榄油倒在锅上，加入蒜末、姜末、葱末“爆香”。</p>
<p>5. 加入鸡肉，拌一拌，等到鸡肉变成淡褐色，洋葱变半透明，此过程最多需要3到5分钟。</p>
<p>6. 将酱油、糖和可乐加入锅中，加热一段时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10584093" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c85e9999fc582abe1312124a8fefbf73.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10584093 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c85e9999fc582abe1312124a8fefbf73-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加入可乐调味。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 将以上加热后的食物，取出放入可密闭容器中，加入300克马铃薯片，炖煮20分钟。</p>
<p>8. 打开盖子，放进20克罗勒调味，继续炖煮一小会儿使食材更入味。</p>
<p>9. 加入新鲜的香菜末或柠檬末。</p>
<p><strong>到此完成！香喷喷的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%93%83%E8%96%AF%E7%82%96%E9%B8%A1.md#1">马铃薯炖鸡</a>出锅了！</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_10583438" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/40d811be5357568458c4f2c4b773b359.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10583438" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/40d811be5357568458c4f2c4b773b359-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美味的马铃薯炖鸡完成啰。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
