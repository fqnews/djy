<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小犀牛不哭！南非犀牛孤儿院要照顾宝宝长大</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/89c47bc954ad9ae66f425f5864537ef0-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>爱撒娇的犀牛宝宝，对“人类妈妈”都很依赖。（影片截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10442513 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/1532506d04b41a9e_ttl7daydED_complete_video_link-2-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月05日讯】这里是南非，NPO团体在这里设立了世界上第一座犀牛宝宝的孤儿院。<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9B%97%E7%8C%8E%E8%80%85.md#1">盗猎者</a>因觊觎<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8A%80%E7%89%9B%E8%A7%92.md#1">犀牛角</a>，将成年犀牛杀死，留下这些失去父母的犀牛宝宝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10527525" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/081bde0db306ab468fc754077dd0c32e.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10527525 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/081bde0db306ab468fc754077dd0c32e-600x601.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="601" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">好奇的犀牛宝宝对着镜头用口鼻碰了碰，发出微微地咿呜声，非常可爱。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>好奇的犀牛宝宝对着镜头用口鼻碰了碰，发出微微地咿呜声，眼神单纯可爱。它们原本应该和爸爸妈妈一起在野外生活，学会生存技能；但现在，这些孤儿<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8A%80%E7%89%9B.md#1">小犀牛</a>只能暂时被人工保护起来。</p>
<p>位于南非林波波省（Limpopo Province）的犀牛宝宝庇护所，是世界上第一座犀牛孤儿院。在这里，饲育员每天带着<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%8A%80%E7%89%9B.md#1">小犀牛</a>在原野散步，也帮小犀牛准备奶水喂食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10527527" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/89fea65ad6209ad06538151a065238dc.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10527527 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/89fea65ad6209ad06538151a065238dc-600x601.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="601" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">位于南非林波波省（Limpopo Province）的犀牛宝宝庇护所，是世界上第一座犀牛孤儿院。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饲育员甚至还会念睡前故事给犀牛宝宝们听，让小犀牛看看大图画书里面其他野生动物的图样。虽然我们并不知道小犀牛能理解多少，但可以肯定的是，它们真的很喜欢跟“人类妈妈”腻在一起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10527529" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2170ee0f37b56ccb3b7c323f1d8ae6cd.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10527529 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/2170ee0f37b56ccb3b7c323f1d8ae6cd-600x598.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="598" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">饲育员每天带着小犀牛在原野散步，也帮小犀牛准备奶水喂食。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>爱撒娇的犀牛宝宝，舒服地躺着让饲育员当靠枕，或是让饲育员摸摸下巴，非常亲昵。有时候，饲育员还会跟犀牛宝宝在院子里玩追跑游戏！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10527531" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/89c47bc954ad9ae66f425f5864537ef0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10527531 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/89c47bc954ad9ae66f425f5864537ef0-600x603.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="603" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">爱撒娇的犀牛宝宝，对“人类妈妈”都很依赖。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嬉闹玩耍过后，来到了犀牛宝宝的“洗澡时间”，它们可不喜欢清水或肥皂泡泡，犀牛的最爱当然是“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A5%E5%B7%B4%E6%B5%B4.md#1">泥巴浴</a>”！饲育员为了帮小犀牛洗澎澎，自己也弄得浑身是泥巴！偶尔也要帮小犀牛抓抓痒，真是尽责的“妈妈”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10527533" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/636367020e3a4ac2f92ac1a2b3222505.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10527533 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/636367020e3a4ac2f92ac1a2b3222505-600x602.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="602" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">饲育员甚至还会念睡前故事给犀牛宝宝们听。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>犀牛孤儿院庇护所的主旨，是要帮助无法自己生活的犀牛宝宝，照顾到它们成年，就让它们回到野外和其他犀牛及野生动物生活在一起。毕竟，它们从来就不是宠物，庇护也只会是暂时的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10527537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/990b257c6f458e11db1a82f1a3d28786.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10527537 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/990b257c6f458e11db1a82f1a3d28786-600x601.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="601" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">犀牛孤儿院庇护所的主旨，是要帮助无法自己生活的犀牛宝宝，照顾到它们成年。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>饲育员洁咪（Jamie）分享，犀牛其实是一种很温暖又社会型的动物。有些小犀牛看久了人类的行为还会主动“帮忙”（如拎袋子，虽然只移动几公分）。“犀牛真的很特别。”洁咪也希望人们更多了解犀牛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10527539" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/7c82b818a98135332cd0ca05a6bf1ff0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10527539 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/7c82b818a98135332cd0ca05a6bf1ff0-600x602.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="602" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">它们从来就不是宠物，庇护也只会是暂时的，成年后，犀牛们就会回到野外和其他犀牛及野生动物生活在一起。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
