<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月30日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）大啖美食，是许多人天天追求的享受，可是大家会着迷到无法吃到美食而<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B3%AA.md#1">泪</a>流满面吗？美国加州索拉诺县（Solano County）有位小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E5%AD%A9.md#1">女孩</a>对“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%A5%BC.md#1">松饼</a>”非常着迷，那夸张程度真是令人匪夷所思。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10598449" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/83ae632c652c6f2ab03f71410d598ad3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10598449 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/83ae632c652c6f2ab03f71410d598ad3-450x651.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="651" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B3%E5%AD%A9.md#1">女孩</a>激动紧闭双嘴。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>女孩的妈妈茱莉亚．罗兰（Julia Rowland）在今年1月8日于脸书上传一短片。只见女孩眼睛泛<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B3%AA.md#1">泪</a>，难过地说：“我一直想着<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9D%BE%E9%A5%BC.md#1">松饼</a>。”说完还激动到嘴唇紧闭上下震了几下。</p>

<p>这时妈妈冷静回应：“你已经在晚上、早上吃过松饼了，所以我们去吃其它的吧！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10598452" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c1b1b95a3c250b516263dc3031d17180.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10598452 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/c1b1b95a3c250b516263dc3031d17180-450x656.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="656" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">女孩不想接受无法吃到松饼的事实。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>没想到这时女孩崩溃了，一副不敢接受这残酷事实的样子，她回应道：“喔，我一直&#8230;&#8230;为什么我一直<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B9%BB%E6%83%B3.md#1">幻想</a>松饼？”<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%93%AD.md#1">哭</a>得好像吃不到松饼人生就无望的感觉，呜呜，好可怜呀……</p>
<figure id="attachment_10598464" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/501d2701a52a2362c242c5280037c50a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10598464 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/501d2701a52a2362c242c5280037c50a-450x658.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="658" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">女孩姿体语言很丰富。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>妈妈态度依然冷静，回应道：“我不知道，我不知道。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10598455" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/35105c3352f05a63ae77662cc5447d51.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10598455 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/35105c3352f05a63ae77662cc5447d51-450x655.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="655" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">听到老妈冷回“我不知道”，女孩更加放生大<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%93%AD.md#1">哭</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>没想到，女孩听到妈妈冷酷的回应，立刻更加崩溃，无止尽地哭<span style="font-weight: 400;">……</span>妈妈再次冷回，说道：“女孩儿，这有点搞笑。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10598465" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/d7ee5cb5117077bf4760ddef637945a7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10598465 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/d7ee5cb5117077bf4760ddef637945a7-450x658.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="658" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">女孩难过低头。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此时妈妈可能已意识到场面控制不住了，赶紧问她：“你需要小睡一下吗？”</p>
<p>小女孩语无伦次回应：“不，我不知道我！＠＃＄％＾＆＄想松饼。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10598463" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/0428fc7cbf3ddac3632d76625e3982d2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10598463 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/0428fc7cbf3ddac3632d76625e3982d2-450x663.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="663" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">女孩已经难过到极点了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此片传至网上后爆红，至少几千万人观看。</p>
<p>有网友看后留言：</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“如果我是位母亲，我会把她送到她的房间让她在那里哭。如果我是奶奶，我会去做她喜爱吃的松饼。”</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“我理解你，宝贝女孩。我马上去做一些松饼，加一些香蕉、巧克力脆片、糖浆，好吃！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10598460" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/e8536e7675e57429408ef5017cce9703.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10598460 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/e8536e7675e57429408ef5017cce9703-450x657.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="657" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">女孩眼泪流下了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也许影片里的妈妈不给女孩吃松饼是因担心宠坏她或影响健康。</p>
<p>不过有些网友持不同看法，留言道：</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“就给她一些松饼，她之后就会吃腻，自然会去吃其它东西，她会哭是因为她不了解自己。”</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">“我看到这我哭了，就给她一些松饼，童年很短暂的。”</p>
