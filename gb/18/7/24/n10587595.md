  <p>不一会，答案就揭晓了。一条小鱼在碎石上方游过，突然在沙里埋着的鱼像子弹一样射出，瞬间吞噬了小鱼，然后立刻就又回到了沙中，跟环境重新融为一体。</p>
  <p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看完整影片?<br />  <div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-d019b168-4fc1-44f3-4b70-f1b8ef2fffe3" data-id="player-d019b168-4fc1-44f3-4b70-f1b8ef2fffe3"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/d019b168-4fc1-44f3-4b70-f1b8ef2fffe3?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F7%2F24%2Fn10587595.md#1"></a></span></strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_10587802" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Stargazers-640x353.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10587802 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/Stargazers-640x353-600x331.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="331" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">怪鱼瞬间吞噬了小鱼。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>影片中可以看到，这只鱼几乎是百发百中，动作像闪电一样快。大一点的鱼它也敢出手，也能咬住，但是因为嘴太小，一口吞不下去，大鱼最后还是逃脱了。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10587799" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fish2-632x353.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10587799 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fish2-632x353-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大一点的鱼它也敢出手。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>这种鱼叫<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9E%BB%E6%98%9F%E9%B1%BC.md#1">瞻星鱼</a>（stargazer fish），因为两只眼睛在头顶（有如望天）而得名。它没有牙，怪不得身体那么鼓，猎物都是囫囵吞下去的。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10587812" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/4100563845_911c03080b_b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10587812 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/4100563845_911c03080b_b-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">藏身砂石中的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9E%BB%E6%98%9F%E9%B1%BC.md#1">瞻星鱼</a>。(<ahref="https://www.flickr.com/photos/14002143@N00/4100563845" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Mark Harris/Flickr</a>)</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10587800" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fish3-630x353.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10587800 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/fish3-630x353-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">猎物都是囫囵吞下去的。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>有些此科的鱼可以发电、喷毒。此鱼分布在世界各地的大洋里，在澳大利亚州菲利普港湾的潜水者们经常能碰到它。幸运的是，这些鱼相对来说还是小，对人类无害。想想它要是两米长，人也就别想在去海洋里游泳了。</p>
