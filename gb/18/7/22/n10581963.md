<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>硅谷上市房 向往优质生活圈</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月22日讯】（大纪元记者李欧旧金山湾区报导）本周回到工程师重镇、华人最爱的南湾、硅谷。这里因为大量的人口移入和工作机会，造成房价高涨、房源不足，一百万房对很多家庭来说已经不能满足空间和优质生活的需求，甚至两百万房也仅仅只是应付过关的水准。而且硅谷有更多的旧房新建，或全新社区，毕竟这里的阳光和科技是全世界向往的地方！</p>
<p>一、10341 North Portal Ave., Cupertino, CA 95014</p>
<figure id="attachment_10581995" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3.12.20.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10581995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3.12.20-600x331.png" alt="" width="600" b="331" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">湾区地产</a>：10341 North Portal Ave., Cupertino, CA 95014。</figcaption></figure>
<p><figure id="attachment_10581997" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/01_242131323-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10581997" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/01_242131323-1-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E8%B5%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区赏房</a>：此房产超过百年历史。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10582000" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/02_ISyrrzm4e7xmp01000000000.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/02_ISyrrzm4e7xmp01000000000-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10582000" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">房产分为主屋、客房、工作室等建筑。（MLS）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>令人叹为观止的家园超过百年历史，经过大面积的豪华改造！地理位置优越，适合购物、餐饮，并有优质校区。此物业有多栋建筑与草地，成了私人般的公园场地，配有客房、车库和工作室。法式门、华丽的硬木地板和高耸的书柜营造出精致的世界。<br />
孩子可以步行到柯林斯小学和劳森中学，或骑自行车去库比蒂诺高中（买家自行验证资格）。这里距离Apple新园区、大华超市都在5分钟的路程，还可漫步到公园、星巴克、主街等等！主屋面积为1,563平方英尺，客房面积为1,058平方英尺。</p>
<p>二、20696 Greenleaf Drive, Cupertino, CA 95014<br />
<figure id="attachment_10582001" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3.12.33.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3.12.33-600x333.png" alt="" width="600" b="333" class="size-large wp-image-10582001" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%8A%E5%B8%82%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区上市房</a>：20696 Greenleaf Drive, Cupertino, CA 95014。</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10582002" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/03_ISqpk6fkpaj9c61000000000.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/03_ISqpk6fkpaj9c61000000000-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10582002" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">湾区地产</a>：此房产以全新建筑上市。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10582003" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/04_240497933-47.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/04_240497933-47-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10582003" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">精致的室内设计，享受优质生活。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
此房产2016年底售出，经过完全重建，如今以托斯卡纳风格展现，附带2车位车库，1个超大入口，为户外娱乐提供了理想的空间。全屋经过设计师精细打造，给予现代Cupertino家园的高水准需求。<br />
此处有最受欢迎的学校：Monta Vista High、Lawson Middle和Garden Gate Elementary。房子位于安静的街道上，可步行到Apple的Infinite Loop园区，邻近有众多商店，包括永和超市、全食超市、Target等。轻松进入高速公路85和280，享受完美的硅谷生活。</p>
<p>三、1641 Eagle Drive, Sunnyvale, CA 94087<br />
<figure id="attachment_10582005" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3.12.41.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3.12.41-600x397.png" alt="" width="600" b="397" class="size-large wp-image-10582005" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B8%8A%E5%B8%82%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区上市房</a>：1641 Eagle Drive, Sunnyvale, CA 94087。</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10582006" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/05_ISmu5z1hv6w2tx1000000000.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/05_ISmu5z1hv6w2tx1000000000-600x421.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="421" class="size-large wp-image-10582006" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湾区上市房：可停多辆车的前门入口。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10582008" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/06_ISif73del3g3c00000000000.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/06_ISif73del3g3c00000000000-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10582008" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">带泳池的后院，拥有私人空间。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
高耸的客厅天花板，全屋采用大型窗户，以及新硬木地板，营造出开放和通风的感觉。宽敞的主人卧室包括两个步入式衣柜，一楼还有一个孝亲的主卧室，以及宽敞的二楼卧室。厨师风格的厨房设有花岗岩台面、双烤箱、5个炉灶和带橱柜的中岛，以及砖砌的Jenn-Air室内烤架。<br />
后院有凉亭庭院、波光粼粼的游泳池和果树。家人可以漫步到Ortega公园，孩子们可以就读排名靠前的Stocklmeir小学和库比蒂诺中学（买家自行验证资格）。同样，大华超市、Apple新园区就在附近，有便利的华人生活圈。</p>
<p>四、1150 Norval Way, San Jose, CA 95125<br />
<figure id="attachment_10582010" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3.12.47.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/3.12.47-600x346.png" alt="" width="600" b="346" class="size-large wp-image-10582010" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B9%B0%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区买房</a>：1150 Norval Way, San Jose, CA 95125。</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10582011" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/07_237312833-25.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/07_237312833-25-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10582011" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湾区上市房：2018年新建房产，全新地中海风格。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10582012" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/08_237312833-3.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/08_237312833-3-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10582012" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">不銹钢专业厨房。（MLS）</figcaption></figure><br />
这也是一个拆除老房，新建的2层楼住宅，带有部分地下室，靠近未来的Google Campus。室内融合地中海和现代风格；起居、用餐室设有典雅的壁炉，为举办正式派对营造了氛围。相邻的办公室设有衣柜和楼下卧室套房，可用于工作、游戏。宽敞的客房设有开放式厨房、用餐区。<br />
楼上主卧室设有法式门，通往露台阳台，主浴室配有高档独立式浴缸。这里是优质的Willow Glen社区，距离圣荷西市中心仅7、8分钟的车程；到Gilroy Outlets只要半小时，或是往南享受更多自然公园、林地！#</p>
