  <figure id="attachment_10622182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807-shan-FTV-007.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807-shan-FTV-007-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" class="size-large wp-image-10622182" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E7%9A%93%E6%98%87.md#1">吴皓昇</a>对<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B9%94%E5%8D%8E%E5%9B%BD.md#1">乔华国</a>的道歉对谈被观众誉为“父亲节的礼物”。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%82%B1%E7%90%A6%E9%9B%AF.md#1">邱琦雯</a>在剧中饰演歌女廖秋月，跟孙如观（吴皓昇饰演）10年之情，碍于歌女身份，让两人婚姻之路走的艰辛，邱琦雯说，想到剧中秋月一直不被夫家接受，所以渴望有个风光的婚礼，才能让大家知道她终于被认可了。</p>
  <p>这次邱琦雯在《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E6%97%B6%E4%BB%A3.md#1">大时代</a>》中披<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A9%9A%E7%BA%B1.md#1">婚纱</a>，造型师梵纬特地找来近两百万的珠宝让她穿戴上身，让她直说压力很大，深怕把珠宝弄丢，但也夸赞剧组用心，让她又惊又喜。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10622186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807-shan-FTV-004.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807-shan-FTV-004-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" class="size-large wp-image-10622186" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">邱琦雯为《大时代》披婚纱，并戴上台币200万的珠宝。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10622189" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807-shan-FTV-005.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807-shan-FTV-005-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="800" class="size-large wp-image-10622189" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《大时代》剧中吴皓昇、邱琦雯将步入礼堂。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>被问到择偶条件，邱琦雯冷静的表示现在没有特别要什么样的对象，基本上就靠感觉，感觉对了，事情就会自然发展。而期待的婚姻生活，“夫妻生活有时充满艰辛，但是莫忘初心，重点是要包容、付出、接受生活里的不完美，退让、妥协有时候能让关系更完美。”</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10622196" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807-shan-FTV-003.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807-shan-FTV-003-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="900" class="size-large wp-image-10622196" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">剧中吴皓昇、邱琦雯将步入礼堂。戏外吴皓昇说结婚太累，人生一次就好。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>而已婚的吴皓昇在拍戏时仍心系老婆文汶，拍戏空档常开摄影机看老婆在工作的模样，收工也赶回家收店整理，拍戏家庭两头跑，直说一点也不累。这次吴皓昇再穿起新郎服，他笑说当新郎真的好累，还好这是演戏，人生经历过一次美好就好了。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10622190" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807-shan-FTV-006.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807-shan-FTV-006-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10622190" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《大时代》剧中吴皓昇、邱琦雯与童星全家福照。（民视提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>责任编辑：辛幼安</p>
