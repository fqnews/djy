  <p>首先送来的是黄瓜沙拉，他的黄瓜腌制让我想到在台湾吃金园排骨面，排骨旁边的那些腌渍过的黄瓜，吃起来十分爽口。这黄瓜也就是清一清口准备接着吃大餐。</p>
  <p><figure id="attachment_10635140" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/01_Cucumber-Sonomono-Crab-Salad.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10635140" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/01_Cucumber-Sonomono-Crab-Salad-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%8D%B7%E8%A5%BF%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E9%A4%90%E9%A6%86.md#1">圣荷西日本餐馆</a>：黄瓜沙拉Cucumber Sunomono Crab Salad。（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_10635143" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/02_Maguro-Hamachi-Hay-Sake-Nigiri.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/02_Maguro-Hamachi-Hay-Sake-Nigiri-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10635143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">湾区寿司</a>店：生鱼片寿司Maguro, Hamachi, Hay Sake Nigiri。（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E5%90%83%E8%B4%A7.md#1">旧金山吃货</a>，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
  <h2>和尚鱼肝</h2>  <p>接着上来的寿司看起来很普通，但是吃起来味道确实不同，所有的鱼都很新鲜不说，就连饭也很好吃。再下来上了一道“和尚鱼肝”Monkfish Liver，我在网上查一下和尚鱼长得什么样，说实在还真丑。老美朋友吃了一口便不再吃，只有我一个人把它吃完。感觉上有点像鹅肝酱，但是又没有那么黏巴巴的在舌头上。<figure id="attachment_10635145" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/03_Monkfish-liver.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/03_Monkfish-liver-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10635145" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">圣荷西日本餐馆：和尚鱼肝Monkfish Liver。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
  <h2>鲜虾与和牛</h2>  <p>接着就是用最新鲜的虾，以及和牛做的寿司，就像前面的鱼寿司一样新鲜得没话说，入口即化。然后就是蛋卷鳗鱼，轻巧制造，可是为什么只有给我们5片呢？3个人不是该6片吗？另外一片是被侍者吃掉，还是大厨不小心掉在地上？或还是包得不好看？反正总有一个人得牺牲少吃一片。<figure id="attachment_10635146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/04_Shrimp-and-Beef-Sushi.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/04_Shrimp-and-Beef-Sushi-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10635146" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md#1">湾区寿司</a>店：虾与和牛寿司Shrimp and Beef Sushi。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
  <h2>巧克力麻糬蛋糕</h2>  <p>最后上来的是巧克力麻糬蛋糕，做得十分实在，比我想像的要硬，但是倒是挺好吃的。<figure id="attachment_10635147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/06_Chocolate-Mochi-Cake.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/06_Chocolate-Mochi-Cake-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" class="size-large wp-image-10635147" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">圣荷西日本餐馆：蛋卷鳗鱼Unagi Tamago Roll。（旧金山吃货，百合摄）</figcaption></figure></p>
  <p>这家店看起来漂亮雅致，侍者说在旧金山以及南加州也有分店，所以南北吃货想要试试“相信大厨”的日式套餐不妨可以试试，但是百合先要警告一下，荷包得要够深，免得到时大出血。#</p>
