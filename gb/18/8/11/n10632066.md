  <p>闲暇之时，也来做一碗香喷喷的台湾美食“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A4%E8%82%89%E9%A5%AD.md#1">卤肉饭</a>×<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B4%A1%E4%B8%B8%E6%B1%A4.md#1">贡丸汤</a>”吧。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">卤肉饭×<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B4%A1%E4%B8%B8%E6%B1%A4.md#1">贡丸汤</a>，这样吃营养更均衡</span></h2>  <p><strong>陈小薇营养师的贴心小叮咛：</strong></p>
  <p>由于卤肉饭单纯只有猪肉皮丁与白米饭的组合，一碗200公克米饭的卤肉饭，热量约400多大卡。因此在食用时，建议多搭配一份烫青菜、一份小菜如凉拌小黄瓜，增加饮食中膳食纤维量的摄取，让营养更加均衡。</p>
  <p>如果是家里自制卤肉饭的酱汁，记得在酱汁冷却后，将表层白色的浮油轻轻刮除，减少口感的油腻，降低油脂摄取。</p>
  <p>另外，卤肉饭的黄金拍档“贡丸汤”，以一般单纯含二颗贡丸的贡丸汤来看，一碗的热量通常约150多大卡，喜爱贡丸汤的朋友，一餐享用2颗贡丸为宜，升级成“紫菜贡丸汤”或是“蛋花贡丸汤”，能提升营养摄取来源的丰富度。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">卤肉饭×贡丸汤做法</span></h2>  <h3><span style="color: #800000;">卤肉饭</span></h3>  <p><strong>食材：</strong>带皮三层肉 200g、红葱头 50g、蒜头 3～4颗、蛋 1盒、酱油 6大匙、清水 600cc、冰糖 40g、白饭。</p>
  <p style="text-align: center;"><div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" src=""></a><a href="https://git.io/JUAJ3"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/whhoub340/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/PCVGT_E4VZ0?wmode=transparent&#038;wmode=opaque" allowfullscreen></a>  	</div></p>
  <p style="text-align: center;"><em><span style="color: #008000;">（完整视频）</span></em></p>
  <p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
  <p>1. 三层肉切细条。在锅里加入酱油、水、冰糖，放入肉条，腌制约20分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10632157" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1-74.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10632157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1-74-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A4%E8%82%89%E9%A5%AD%E5%81%9A%E6%B3%95.md#1">卤肉饭做法</a>之：腌肉。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 开火炖煮，将剥好的水煮蛋放入锅中。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10632158" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/2-33.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10632158" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/2-33-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%A4%E8%82%89%E9%A5%AD%E5%81%9A%E6%B3%95.md#1">卤肉饭做法</a>之：开火炖煮。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 将红葱头、蒜头切片，取另一只锅，炸出香气后，捞起放入卤肉锅中。中小火卤1个半小时。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10632159" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/3-20.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10632159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/3-20-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">卤肉饭做法之：炸香红葱头和蒜。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4. 开锅盖，加一点米酒、半碗水，再煮30～60分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10632160" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4-17.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10632160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4-17-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">卤肉饭做法之：加入米酒后继续炖煮。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #800000;">贡丸汤</span></h3>  <p><strong>食材：</strong>贡丸 5颗、盐少许、芹菜适量、白胡椒适量、柴鱼粉适量、香油少许。</p>
  <p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
  <p>1. 在贡丸上方划十字。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10632165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/11-12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10632165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/11-12-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">贡丸汤做法之：在贡丸上划十字。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 水煮开，放入贡丸，再加入盐和柴鱼粉调味。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10632166" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/22-9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10632166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/22-9-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">贡丸汤做法之：水煮。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 盛出贡丸汤，加入芹菜末、少许胡椒粉。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10632167" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/33-6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10632167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/33-6-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">贡丸汤做法之：盛出后放入芹菜末。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>将卤肉和肉汁浇在白饭上，配着贡丸汤，就可以享用了！</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10632161" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5-10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10632161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/5-10-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白饭浇上肉汁。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">·?<a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/18/2/27/n10177133.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">5步做出“烧肉丼饭” 加2种蔬菜营养更均衡</a>?</span></h2>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">·?<a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/18/3/12/n10211690.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">猪大肠补铁锌、增体力 简单5步煮大肠面线</a>?</span></h2>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">·?<a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/18/3/30/n10264441.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">马铃薯皮都被你扔了？这样一拌营养又好吃</a></span></h2>  <p>&lt;点阅<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;" href="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光系列 节气+营养料理</a></span></span>&gt;</p>
