<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>南向圣荷西 百万优质房</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月01日讯】高昂的硅谷房价，令众多高薪工程师都难以买房，不少人因而举家搬迁，甚至异地谋生。不过，硅谷的邻近处仍是有不少好房等待买家上门，这次就从圣荷西一路南向找房，看看有何高性价比好房适合您。</p>
<p>一、989 Michigan Ave., San Jose, CA 95125</p>
<figure id="attachment_10683304" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/01_ISeord00nxhvkq0000000000.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10683304 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/01_ISeord00nxhvkq0000000000-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%8D%B7%E8%A5%BF%E6%88%BF%E4%BA%A7.md#1">圣荷西房产</a>，童话般的独立屋。（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10683308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/02_243032533-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10683308" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/02_243032533-5-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">典雅不过时的客厅。（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个1930年兴建，童话般的独立屋位在Willow Glen区，属于Lincoln Ave.街区的优质社区，生活便利，附近就有精品店和高级餐馆。交通靠近280、87公路。<br />
房产的窗户、空调、灯具、花园和厨房的设备与橱柜都经过更新，看起来换然一新。正式客厅配有燃木壁炉；正式餐厅配有新安装的Milgard法式门，方便户外生活空间；庭院配有燃气烧烤炉、火坑、座椅等设施。另外，还有地下娱乐室、大空间主卧、优雅的露台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10683310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/11.51.51.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10683310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/11.51.51-600x317.png" alt="" width="600" b="317" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">圣荷西地产：989 Michigan Ave., San Jose, CA 95125。</figcaption></figure>
<p>二、4770 Clydelle Ave., San Jose, CA 95124</p>
<figure id="attachment_10683312" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/03_36521293-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10683312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/03_36521293-1-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E6%88%BF%E4%BA%A7.md#1">硅谷房产</a>，类似联排屋的独立屋社区（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10683314" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/04_236521293-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10683314" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/04_236521293-3-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">社区有儿童公园。（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里是优秀的独立屋社区，类似联排屋设计，拥有较小的庭院。交通方便前往CA-17/880、85，邻近Los Gatos，不远处有医疗中心Good Samaritan Hospital。社区还设有带儿童游乐场的公园。<br />
华丽的起居室设有壁炉和拱形天花板，正式的餐厅可通往起居室，营造出开放而豪华的氛围，配有葡萄酒冰箱和储藏室。厨房配有花岗岩台面和不銹钢用具，包括燃气灶具和大型法式门式冰箱。书房位于厨房旁边，拥有充足的光线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10683315" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/11.52.01.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10683315" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/11.52.01-600x356.png" alt="" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">圣荷西地产：4770 Clydelle Ave., San Jose, CA 95124。</figcaption></figure>
<p>三、6552 Camden Ave., San Jose, CA 95120</p>
<figure id="attachment_10683320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/05_251353313-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10683320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/05_251353313-3-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E6%88%BF%E4%BA%A7.md#1">硅谷房产</a>，位在转角上的学区房。（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10683321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/06_251353313-26.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10683321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/06_251353313-26-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">后院泳池设计有岩石喷泉。（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这处重新装修的家，已经准备好迎接新主人！拥有高API顶尖学校，包括Simonds小学/ Bret Harte Middle /和Leland高中（买方核实）。靠近高速公路入口、购物中心和Almaden Lake公园。<br />
新屋顶、室内外新油漆，新硬木地板、新枫木柜，新石英台面、新的管道工程??等等，全户经过大面积升级。客厅、厨房均有天井窗享受充足自然光，户外泳池更搭配喷泉、岩石山水，营造野地流水的意境。<br />
您可以在庭院区享用咖啡，同时放松身心并聆听定制喷泉或在后院清凉的游泳池畅游！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10683323" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/11.52.12.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10683323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/11.52.12-600x311.png" alt="" width="600" b="311" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湾区上市房：6552 Camden Ave., San Jose, CA 95120。</figcaption></figure>
<p>四、2775 Cantor Drive, Morgan Hill, CA 95037</p>
<figure id="attachment_10683327" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/07_ISu4vfup1xc4a60000000000.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10683327" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/07_ISu4vfup1xc4a60000000000-600x289.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="289" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B9%B0%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区买房</a>，此房产空间大、房间多（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10683328" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/08_ISu0seogyu8ib60000000000.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10683328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/08_ISu0seogyu8ib60000000000-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白色优雅的厨房（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<p>买下这个房产除了入住，这里没什么可做的！房产位在摩根希尔东部山麓美丽的绿树成荫街道上，这个更新的住宅充分展示了优质生活。到达Gilroy Outlets只要15分钟。虽然距离硅谷稍远，但价格相对便宜。<br />
厨房配有高端S / S设备，带石英台面和步入式厨房的白色橱柜。这里非常适合户外用餐和娱乐。户外有许多成熟的果树，包括橙子和油桃果树。所有卧室和洗衣房均位于二楼，楼下有容纳3辆车的车库。房产有6间房间，对喜欢大家庭或招待朋友的买家是一个好选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10683329" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/11.52.22.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10683329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/11.52.22-600x311.png" alt="" width="600" b="311" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湾区上市房：2775 Cantor Drive, Morgan Hill, CA 95037。</figcaption></figure>
