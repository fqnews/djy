  <p>男子像做CPR一样，为小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%94%E5%AD%90.md#1">兔子</a>按压，并且轻轻呼唤，希望它能醒过来。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10664874" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie.-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10664874" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie.-1-600x308.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="308" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>过了一会儿，小兔子默默睁开了眼睛，看来已经没有生命危险了。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10664875" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10664875" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie-2-600x308.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="308" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>男子拿了一块布，想擦干兔子湿漉漉的身体。就在准备给兔子换个位置的时候，小兔突然尖叫起来。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10664876" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10664876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie-3-600x308.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="308" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>在很多人的印象中，兔子总是默默无声的，以至于有人以为兔子不会叫。其实兔子并不是哑巴，在害怕或者遭受痛苦时，兔子也会尖叫。这只兔子可能是苏醒后感到害怕，所以叫了起来。</p>
  <p>不过这叫声在男子听来，就是脱离危险的最好说明，所以他开心地大叫：“它活过来了，活过来了！”</p>
  <p>小兔子虽然醒了，但很虚弱，动都动不了。于是男子把兔子捧回了家，找了个盒子给它当窝，里面还特别铺了一条小毯子，让兔子躺着舒服点。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10664878" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10664878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie-4-600x308.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="308" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>男子细心地用电吹风帮兔子吹干了毛，又找来蔬菜给它吃。经过这样无微不至的照料，小兔子终于彻底活了。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10664884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10664884" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie-5-600x308.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="308" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>看着小兔子可爱的模样，男子已经有点舍不得让它走了。可是如果不让它回家，兔妈妈该多着急啊！</p>
  <p>男子捧着康复的小兔子回到了当时发现它的湖边，叮嘱它“以后不要再去游泳”之后，才不舍地目送它消失在茂密的树丛中。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10664886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie-6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10664886" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bunnie-6-600x308.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="308" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>看完整影片 （ Credit: Jukin Media）&gt;&gt;<br />  <div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-5a040884-557e-4b10-4c59-bab1c9be3d30" data-id="player-5a040884-557e-4b10-4c59-bab1c9be3d30"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/5a040884-557e-4b10-4c59-bab1c9be3d30?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F24%2Fn10664872.md#1"></a></p>
