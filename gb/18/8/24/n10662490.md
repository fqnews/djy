<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2018台湾美食Taste of Taiwan盛宴不同凡飨</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5261-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>青商会创始人Kai Hsu致词。（汉民／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月24日讯】（<span lang="ZH-TW">大纪元记者汉民亚特兰大报导）</span><span lang="ZH-TW">8<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>22<span lang="ZH-TW">日</span><span lang="ZH-CN">（星期三）</span><span lang="ZH-TW">晚，“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">台湾美食</a>飨宴”的重头戏</span><span lang="ZH-CN">在亚特兰大正式登场。</span><span lang="ZH-TW">中华民国侨委会自从</span>2010<span lang="ZH-TW">年开始举办的“台湾美食厨艺巡回讲座”（</span>Taste of Taiwan）<span lang="ZH-TW">在世界各地的热烈反响，已经成为全球各地侨界每年翘首以待的盛会。侨委会今年再度从全国厨艺界选出手艺高超的名厨巡回世界，这次来临美南地区的讲师是号称“亚州食神”的黄宝元及助理讲师梁语宸。黄宝元为醒吾科技大学餐旅管理系系主任，曾在丽景四季饭店、美国希尔顿饭店及凯悦饭店担任行政总主厨及客座主厨，梁语宸为醒吾科技大学餐旅管理系专任讲师，两人均获国内外多项厨艺竞赛大奖。</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10662514" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5263-e1535078485966.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5263-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加州小姐李万晴主持晚宴。（汉民／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10662523" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5298-e1535078583682.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662523" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5298-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">经文处处长刘经岩致词。（汉民／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="NoSpacing1" align="left"><span lang="ZH-TW">本年巡回讲座以“台湾美食 不同凡飨”为主题，并再度由乔治亚州台湾商会青商会主办在亚特兰大的活动，包括</span>8<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>21<span lang="ZH-TW">日的厨艺教学，以及</span>8<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>22<span lang="ZH-TW">日的重头戏，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">台湾美食</a>飨宴。美食飨宴的场地在</span>The Westin Atlanta Perimeter North <span lang="ZH-TW">的豪华会场，席位共开四十多桌。各界名人绅士与名媛盛装出席，整个晚宴并穿插着现场音乐演奏，热闹非凡。去年主持晚宴的加州小姐李万晴</span>(Crystal Lee) <span lang="ZH-TW">再度光临接下主持重任，将晚宴节目的承接掌握得十分出色。台北经济文化办事处处长刘经岩也上台致词，介绍台湾扬名国际的美食文化。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10662532" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5334-e1535078712313.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662532" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5334-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Brad Raffensperger致词。（汉民／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10662537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5345-e1535078818419.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5345-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">王祥瑞致词。（汉民／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="NoSpacing1" align="left"><span lang="ZH-TW">两位大厨为大家带来了七道菜，头台冷盘是三阳开泰鸿运</span>（<span lang="ZH-TW">酒香醉鸡、五味鲜虾、五香牛腱</span>）<span lang="ZH-TW">，然后五道主菜是</span>XO<span lang="ZH-TW">酱芦笋鲜干贝</span><span lang="ZH-CN">、</span><span lang="ZH-TW">台式风味芋香米粉汤</span><span lang="ZH-CN">、</span><span lang="ZH-TW">上汤百果鲜食蔬</span><span lang="ZH-CN">、</span><span lang="ZH-TW">锦绣蒸海鲈</span><span lang="ZH-CN">、</span><span lang="ZH-TW">以及樱花虾米糕。最后的甜汤是甜酒酿枸杞芝麻汤圆。众人吃得赞不绝口，齿颊留香。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10662544" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5350-e1535078938937.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662544" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5350-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">奥运金牌得主Jen Lee致词。（汉民／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="NoSpacing1" align="left"><span lang="ZH-TW">晚宴中青商会并颁赠文化大使奖给两位得主，分别是亚特兰大侨界名人侨务委员王祥瑞，以及活跃于商界及政界的</span>Brad Raffensperger<span lang="ZH-TW">先生。</span>Raffensperger<span lang="ZH-TW">是现任乔治亚州议会议员，代表亚裔集中的约翰溪市（</span>Johns Creek）<span lang="ZH-TW">，并且正在参选今年的州务卿一职。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_10662554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5364-e1535079076142.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10662554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/DSC_5364-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">青商会两位会长戴广晟（左一）及Dean Wang（左二）介绍两位主厨，黄宝元（右二）及梁语宸（右一）。（汉民／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="NoSpacing1" align="left"><span lang="ZH-TW">这次还有一位特别来宾是出生于台湾而住在加州的美国陆军中士</span>Jen Lee<span lang="ZH-TW">。</span>Jen Lee<span lang="ZH-TW">是在</span>911<span lang="ZH-TW">事件之后加入陆军，并参加过伊拉克战争。在派驻于乔治亚州的萨凡纳市时</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-TW">他不幸经历了一起摩托车车祸，导致双腿截肢。但是他不但没有被这场车祸击倒，反而被激起了意志力而参与残障奥运，并在</span>2014<span lang="ZH-TW">和</span>2018<span lang="ZH-TW">的冬季奥运获得金牌。</span>Jen Lee<span lang="ZH-TW">上台时以充满活力的语气带起了全场热烈的气氛，并借此机会感谢他的挚友</span>Jerry<span lang="ZH-TW">。</span></p>
