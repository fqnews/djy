<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>鸟妈妈在花环里筑巢 大自然的神奇就在家门口</h2>
<a href="https://ghcdn.rawgit.org/rsllp275/vd/master/jump2.htm?id=tztGw"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rsllp275/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年08月06日讯】（大纪元记者陈安编译报导）?有只<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9F%A5%E6%9B%B4%E9%B8%9F.md#1">知更鸟</a>于一户人家门上的花环里筑了个巢，并把蛋下在了巢里。随着时间推移，幼鸟破壳而出，不断成长……夫妻俩发现后，开始用影片记录这“门上奇缘”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10618106" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c9b506d83de154b0256dc5c8de51aa7f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10618106 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c9b506d83de154b0256dc5c8de51aa7f-600x340.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="340" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">家门上的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E5%9C%88.md#1">花圈</a>，有着一窝<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E5%B7%A2.md#1">鸟巢</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这户人家的房门上挂了个装饰花环，可能是鸟儿见到这“花草”外型觉得特别亲切，竟在花环的下凹处筑了一个小巢。</p>
<p>奇怪，门不是常常有人进进出出，鸟儿怎么会敢在上面<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%AD%91%E5%B7%A2.md#1">筑巢</a>呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_10618120" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4474d3d32c4902c3137c9b7781372c05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10618120" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/4474d3d32c4902c3137c9b7781372c05-600x394.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="394" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">巢里有三颗鸟蛋。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>原来，那阵子夫妇刚好出去渡假，不知鸟儿是不知这个住了人，还是看准时机在“空巢”落户，总之，它认为这里是繁育后代的最好地方，把几颗小鸟蛋生在了花环<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%9F%E5%B7%A2.md#1">鸟巢</a>里。</p>

<p>就在主人度假回来第二天，鸟蛋就开始孵化，于是巢里添了三只小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9F%A5%E6%9B%B4%E9%B8%9F.md#1">知更鸟</a>。这些小朋友体上没什么毛，身形小却有一个“大嘴巴”。随着时间推移，它们的羽毛越来越长，颜色也越来越鲜艳。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10618122" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/53794449e2d90c3aee9cd0ff29dafae3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10618122" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/53794449e2d90c3aee9cd0ff29dafae3-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸟儿身体小，嘴却很大。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>很特别的是，由于它们的巢是筑在大门的透明<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8E%BB%E7%92%83.md#1">玻璃</a>上，夫妇俩可以通过“透视”来观察鸟儿的生活样貌。</p>
<p>从门内透视拍摄的画面中，我们还可看到鸟妈妈的有趣喂食过程。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10618129" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/697cd3bbc997be3c1c945859b71ada0f.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10618129" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/697cd3bbc997be3c1c945859b71ada0f-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">窝里可爱的鸟儿。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鸟妈妈将食物衔到孩儿身边时，宝宝们好似都有“知道开饭时间”的本能，个个伸长脖子张开大嘴，争先恐后殷切期盼着把小肚肚吃饱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10618124" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/b07b188b533a4524e1592cd28e24605c.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10618124" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/b07b188b533a4524e1592cd28e24605c-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">从<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8E%BB%E7%92%83.md#1">玻璃</a>内观察鸟儿喂食过程。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而鸟妈妈的嘴就像插秧一样，将食物“种入”孩儿嘴里，待之长成鸟宝宝身上的一部分。</p>
<p>等到鸟儿长了许多羽毛后，它们就开始飞离小巢啰！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10618127" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bf7b9ab478d6fec4697b3c9944e000e6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10618127" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/bf7b9ab478d6fec4697b3c9944e000e6-600x336.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸟儿飞离巢啰。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网友Rachel Langevin对此影片做了贴切的留言：“大自然的神奇就在你家门，如此美好的一场秀，令人惊叹。”</p>
<p>而Frank Orozco则很熟悉这种鸟的性格：“知更鸟喜欢人类，很好交际的鸟儿，它们在我们的凉亭（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%AD%91%E5%B7%A2.md#1">筑巢</a>），而且从未搞得一团乱。”</p>
<p>从英文大纪元脸书网友留言看，不难感受到知更鸟与人类相处融洽，很多网友都谈论著谁家哪里曾有知更鸟，甚至不少网友也分享说自家门上的花环里也曾有知更鸟筑巢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10618138" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/d54ef65745d30bdda0774ccc16f7a4f9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10618138" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/d54ef65745d30bdda0774ccc16f7a4f9-600x355.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="355" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">飞离巢的鸟儿。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
