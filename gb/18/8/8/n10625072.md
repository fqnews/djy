  <figure id="attachment_10625125" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807GS110e.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10625125 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807GS110e-300x401.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">旁边有两条狗和一只猫围观。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p><strong><span style="color: #008000;">看完整影片?</span></strong><br />  <div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-29ca7fb0-d097-432e-7f0d-fe107ea842a5" data-id="player-29ca7fb0-d097-432e-7f0d-fe107ea842a5"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/29ca7fb0-d097-432e-7f0d-fe107ea842a5?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F8%2Fn10625072.md#1"></a></p>
  <p>令他无法忍受的是，同两只狗狗只是坐在面前、静静地等着主人施舍不同的是，这只名叫尼根（Negan）的小猫直接就跳上了男子的肚子，并在肩上，臂上、肚子上追着、抢着吃主人手中的汉堡包。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10625124" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807GS110d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10625124 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807GS110d-300x479.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="479" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小猫直接就跳上了男子的肚子。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>男子一边像玩篮球时运球躲避对手一样，把汉堡包左右前后躲闪腾挪，一边还语气坚定地喊道：“不行！”“走开！”“这是我的汉堡！” 结果……</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10625122" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807GS110b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10625122 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807GS110b-300x533.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="533" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">男子把汉堡包左右前后躲闪。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>结果，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%96%B5%E6%98%9F%E4%BA%BA.md#1">喵星人</a>仍然把汉堡咬下了一块。主人一脸震惊，难以置信地望着相机。</p>
  <p>在两只狗狗渴望的目光中，男子自己终于有机会咬了一口汉堡包。但之后，吃完了上一口的猫猫又跳了过来，继续在男子身上追逐者汉堡，并不断地一块一块地吞咬着汉堡。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10625121" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807GS110a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10625121 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807GS110a-450x474.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="474" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">猫猫又继续在男子身上追逐者汉堡。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>男子很无奈地对着镜头说：“这就是我每次、每次、每次吃饭时都会发生的事情！”</p>
  <p>有网友对两条狗狗的自律能力点赞，它们在美食的诱惑下，仍然能够把持住自己，不干不该干的事。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10625123" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807GS110c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10625123 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/20180807GS110c-600x360.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">你对猫咪的抢吃怎么看呢？（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>不过，也有网友问道，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%96%B5%E6%98%9F%E4%BA%BA.md#1">喵星人</a>抢男子的食物真的是在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B2%E5%9D%8F%E4%BA%8B.md#1">干坏事</a>吗？请再想想看。</p>
  <p>该网友解释说，这位男子的体重都已经严重超标了，小猫咪根本就是在帮他嘛！根本目的是让他注意饮食、少吃垃圾食品。喵星人甚至不惜牺牲自己的健康，不惜主人可能对自己产生负面想法，也要主动扑上前去，尽量多地抢着吃一些汉堡，以便让主人能够少吃一些、不再超重。</p>
  <p>这……多么的感人啊！</p>
