  <p>这只家在洛杉矶的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>，它抓痒的工具很特别，竟是一支“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BE%BD%E6%AF%9B.md#1">羽毛</a>”。只见它单脚站立，伸起爪子抓着羽根，往头部搔呀搔，一下子搔头顶，一下子搔脖子、脸颊，一下子又搔眼睛周围。</p>
  <p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>看影片完整版…</strong></span><br />  <div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-f9d56163-e680-4803-7711-9e5d80341018" data-id="player-f9d56163-e680-4803-7711-9e5d80341018"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/f9d56163-e680-4803-7711-9e5d80341018?r=16x9&amp;s=1080x1080&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F8%2F18%2Fn10648488.md#1"></a></p>
  <p>鹦鹉还会用嘴咬羽根，这不是要吃<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BE%BD%E6%AF%9B.md#1">羽毛</a>，而是鹦鹉很聪明，懂得用嘴把羽毛转向脸的另一侧，继续搔。它搔了一阵又换脸的另一侧，这“自助抓痒”过程，鹦鹉表情看起来真是好享受。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10648867" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c3a358855987879736c9388555eedfc7.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10648867 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/c3a358855987879736c9388555eedfc7-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鹦鹉表示：“太舒服了。”（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>其实，会拿工具抓痒的还不只这只，这只白鹦鹉就是一例，它会拿树枝抓痒<span style="color: #0000ff;"><span style="color: #000000;">（</span><a style="color: #0000ff;" href="https://www.facebook.com/djy.news/videos/%E6%8A%93%E7%99%A2%E4%B8%8D%E6%B1%82%E4%BA%BA%E7%9C%8B%E7%9C%8B%E8%81%B0%E6%98%8E%E7%9A%84%E6%88%91%E6%89%BE%E5%88%B0%E4%BA%86%E4%BB%80%E9%BA%BC-via-viralvideouk/1731350733543457/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点击播放</a><span style="color: #000000;">）</span></span>。</p>
