<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>幸福感！口感绵密“焦糖慕斯蛋糕卷”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/342b20866341c7c53e6983cc51e418b4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>焦糖慕斯蛋糕卷。（影片截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-10603615 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/complete-video-link-16-450x49.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="49" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2018年09月06日讯】如何让美味的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">蛋糕卷</a>增添风采？首先加入绵密可口的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96.md#1">焦糖</a>慕斯内馅，最后装饰再摆上<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%AE%94.md#1">金箔</a>及坚果增添高级感，铺上正方形镂空纸模，撒上<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%AF%E5%8F%AF%E7%B2%89.md#1">可可粉</a>，可爱的英式格纹就诞生啰！下午茶时间，绵密的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%84%A6%E7%B3%96.md#1">焦糖</a>慕斯蛋糕配上一杯咖啡，气氛刚刚好！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10669664" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/342b20866341c7c53e6983cc51e418b4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10669664 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/342b20866341c7c53e6983cc51e418b4-450x448.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">焦糖慕斯<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">蛋糕卷</a>。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong>【食材准备】</strong></h2>
<p><strong>●蛋糕面糊：</strong></p>
<p>蛋白 4个<br />
糖 52g<br />
蛋黄 3个<br />
低筋面粉 70g<br />
沙拉油 20g<br />
香草精 1g</p>
<p><strong>●焦糖：</strong></p>
<p>糖 100g<br />
鲜奶油 100g<br />
牛奶 180g<br />
水 10g</p>
<p><strong>●焦糖慕斯：</strong></p>
<p>焦糖同制作份量<br />
蛋黄 40g<br />
糖 25g<br />
吉利丁粉 6g<br />
水 25g<br />
鲜奶油 180g</p>
<p><strong>●装饰果物或者坚果：</strong></p>
<p>莓果<br />
坚果<br />
薄荷叶</p>
<h2><strong>【作法】</strong></h2>
<p>●蛋糕面糊：</p>
<p>1. 蛋白打散，分次加入糖，先用高速打至有点奶昔状，再加入剩下的糖，再用中速打至挺立。<br />
2. 将蛋黄加入打发好的蛋白霜盆中，低速搅拌5秒至均匀。<br />
3. 过筛低筋面粉至盆内并搅拌至有些余粉。<br />
4. 从边边加入沙拉油再搅拌均匀，加入少量香草精搅拌至完全均匀为止。<br />
5. 面糊倒入正方形烤盘模后刮平。<br />
6. 放入烤箱，180度烤10分钟左右即完成。</p>
<p>●焦糖：</p>
<p>1.糖放入盆内加少许水。<br />
2.放置锅内煮焦色后，分次加入加热好的鲜奶油与牛奶，搅拌均匀即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10669665" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/08bb5cb2977c709f7b0384fbc9e356ae.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10669665 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/08bb5cb2977c709f7b0384fbc9e356ae-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">奶油糊加入焦糖糊。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>●焦糖慕斯：</p>
<p>1. 蛋黄与糖放入钢盘内，用打蛋器打至鹅黄色。<br />
2. 将焦糖分次加入蛋黄糖盆内搅拌均匀。<br />
3. 过筛后再煮至80度。<br />
4. 将还原好的吉利丁（吉利丁+水）加入“步骤3”的材料后放凉备用。<br />
5. 打发鲜奶油至七成发（霜淇淋模样）。<br />
6. 接着，将“步骤5”打发鲜奶油，分次加入“步骤4”的材料中，使用打蛋器搅拌均匀为止。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10669667" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/2d977b2407bc5ea645a9a9318ae152a4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10669667 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/2d977b2407bc5ea645a9a9318ae152a4-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放上格纹篓空纸，让<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%AF%E5%8F%AF%E7%B2%89.md#1">可可粉</a>均匀洒落。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>●装饰果物、坚果：</p>
<p>组装时会将戚风蛋糕皮裁切为高约2.5公分的长条状（约可做7个），将蛋糕条围成一个圈，底部放上圆状蛋糕皮后将慕斯倒入，将之冰至冰箱30分钟左右，再将上面装饰菓物或坚果即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10669666" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/832c63efc86e470510244f5ffeb22a26.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10669666 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/832c63efc86e470510244f5ffeb22a26-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">撒上坚果及<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E7%AE%94.md#1">金箔</a>做装饰。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
