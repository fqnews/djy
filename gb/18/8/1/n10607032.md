<h2>【作法】</h2>
<p>1.酪梨削皮后，从侧面剖半切开；并将中间的籽去掉，使用小勺子各挖出鸡蛋大小的形状，放置一旁备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10607145" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9e3e45184f2ccd7a2dd872dc367f9869.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10607145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/9e3e45184f2ccd7a2dd872dc367f9869-450x389.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将酪梨中间的籽去掉后，使用小勺子各挖出鸡蛋大小的形状。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将生鸡蛋放入水中煮熟，蛋壳冷却后剥开。</p>
<p>3.接着，放入刚刚酪梨挖空的中心，再将另一半酪梨合上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10607146" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1acdcad16711c45e8ac972fd981f3a34.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10607146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/1acdcad16711c45e8ac972fd981f3a34-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">酪梨外层均等裹上培根，下锅油炸。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.酪梨外层均等裹上培根，下锅油炸，等培根呈现金黄色即可捞起锅。</p>
<p>5.准备酱料：番茄酱、蜂蜜、酱油均匀混合后，均匀刷在培根上，最后，切开就可以大口享用啰！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10607069" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/27a0cda8958cc120bded0888701428bf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10607069" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/08/27a0cda8958cc120bded0888701428bf-450x388.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">酪梨外面裹上一层金黄培根，切开里头还有<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E7%85%AE%E8%9B%8B.md#1">水煮蛋</a>，口口充满惊喜，给你满满的饱足感。（影片截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>备注：食材口味皆可依个人喜好调整变换。</p>
