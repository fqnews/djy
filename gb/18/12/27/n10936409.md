  <p>德拉华河畔赏烟火的最佳地点包括：</p>
  <p>1. Great Plaza at Penn’s Landing　（宾恩广场）<br />  地址：101 COLUMBUS BOULEVARD</p>
  <p>2. Blue Cross RiverRink &amp; Waterfront Winterfest（蓝十字河滨溜冰场和海滨冬日节）<br />  地址：Market Street and Columbus Boulevard</p>
  <p>3. Race Street Pier（芮士街码头）<br />  地址：Columbus Blvd. &amp; Race Street</p>
  <p>4 Independence Seaport Museum（独立海港博物馆）<br />  地址：211 S. COLUMBUS BOULEVARD</p>
  <p>5 SugarHouse Casino（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B3%96%E5%B1%8B%E8%B5%8C%E5%9C%BA.md#1">糖屋赌场</a>）<br />  地址：1001 N. Delaware Avenue</p>
  <p>6. Adventure Aquarium （水族馆）<br />  地址：1 RIVERSIDE DRIVE CAMDEN, NJ</p>
  <p>7. Battleship New Jersey(新泽西战舰)<br />  地址：100 Clinton Street, Camden, NJ</p>
  <p>8. Spirit of Philadelphia （<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B4%B9%E5%9F%8E.md#1">费城</a>精神游船）<br />  地址：401 S. Columbus Boulevard</p>
  <p>9. Liberty Fireworks Cruise(自由号烟花巡航)<br />  地址： 211 S. Christopher Columbus Boulevard</p>
  <p>10. Wiggin’s Park（威金公园）<br />  地址：Ulysses Wiggins Waterfront Park, 2 Riverside Drive, Camden, NJ</p>
  <p>11. One Liberty Observation Deck （观景台）<br />  地址：One Liberty Observation Deck, 1650 Market Street</p>
  <p>12. Penn Treaty Park（宾恩条约公园）<br />  地址：Penn Treaty Park, 1301 N. Beach Street</p>
  <p>13. South Street Bridge over I-95（南街桥）<br />  地址：The South Street Bridge on the eastern edge of the city passing over I-95</p>
  <p>14. Ritz East parking lot（丽思电影院停车场）<br />  地址：125 S. 2nd Street</p>
  <p>15. Front Street（前街）<br />  地址：Front Street between the South Street and Market Street bridges</p>
