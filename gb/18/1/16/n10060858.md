<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>日本餐的时尚 品味Yayoi轩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>YAYOI轩餐厅宽敞明亮（House of Cameo提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年01月16日讯】拥有两家分店的YAYOI轩日本餐还是不能满足悉尼众多YAYOI粉丝们的需求，现在，YAYOI轩又在悉尼市中心的Westfield Sydney开了第三家分店，如同The Galeries和Chatswood的分店一样生意繁忙兴隆，每天爆满、优质、可口的传统日式餐和出色的服务使得Yayoi轩日本餐口碑广传。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10060923" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-07.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10060923 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-07-450x276.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="276" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">YAYOI轩餐厅品种多样的定食（House of Cameo提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>YAYOI轩像一股新鲜空气，令人耳目一新，其餐式带给美食爱好者新的体验。YAYOI轩的魅力是YAYOI餐的品质，大多数食品原料是从日本直接运输到悉尼，甚至包括大米。吃过YAYOI轩的米饭，再回家吃吃自己家里做的米饭，真的，那是没法比的了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10060918" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-02.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10060918 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-02-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">YAYOI轩餐厅品种多样的定食（House of Cameo提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>YAYOI轩经营的是Teishoku 餐式， 日语的汉字为“定食”，即是一种日式套餐。套餐有味噌汤，食品托盘内配有米饭，主菜和配菜，是一种食品口味，营养搭配均衡的用餐方式。YAYOI轩是日本最大，最著名的传统连锁餐厅，目前在日本就有337家分店，并且在海外的台湾，新加坡，泰国，马来西亚，美国有分店。</p>
<p>位于悉尼市中心Westfield Sydney新开的YAYOI轩餐厅用日本式的木制装修成的，整个餐厅显得干净，明亮，时尚。这里设施可以提供100人团体用餐，平时也适合散客就餐。YAYOI轩餐馆采用了先进的数字电脑点餐系统，方便而又快捷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10060922" style="width: 795px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-06.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10060922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-06.jpg" alt="" width="795" b="463" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">YAYOI轩餐厅品种多样的日本梅酒（House of Cameo提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为了让来悉尼市中心Westfield Sydney分店用餐有不同体验，该店提供仅在这里有的各种各样特色的日本清酒和葡萄酒，还包括超过10种日本梅酒，日本梅酒全部仅售8澳元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10060928" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-10.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10060928 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-10-300x347.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="347" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">YAYOI轩餐厅品种多样的甜食（House of Cameo提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>来悉尼市中心Westfield Sydney用餐，一定不要错过品尝特色的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%92%8C%E7%89%9B%E5%AF%BF%E5%96%9C%E7%83%A7%E5%A5%97%E9%A4%90.md#1">和牛寿喜烧套餐</a>，这是一菜三吃的套餐。这套套餐可以只品尝高级和牛原有的牛肉本来带有的咸、甜口感，还可以与蛋浆沾食，享受柔和的味道， 也能与面条同食。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10060924" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-08.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10060924 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-08-450x268.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="268" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">YAYOI轩餐厅<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%92%8C%E7%89%9B%E5%AF%BF%E5%96%9C%E7%83%A7%E5%A5%97%E9%A4%90.md#1">和牛寿喜烧套餐</a>（House of Cameo提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“定食”套餐种类繁多，如：鱼套餐Salmon Teriyaki,鳗鱼套餐，鸡肉套餐，猪肉套餐，御膳套餐等，另外还有系列乌冬面，系列点餐和甜品等等。Yayoi轩供应的食品非常全面，适合不同口味的日式餐爱好者。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10060926" style="width: 878px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-09.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-10060926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/20180116-Alfred-yayoi-09.jpg" alt="" width="878" b="444" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">YAYOI轩餐厅品种多样的定食（House of Cameo提供）</figcaption></figure>
