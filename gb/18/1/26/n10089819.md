  <h2>独立党内斗一塌糊涂</h2>  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/8-15.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-10089865" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/8-15.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="404" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/3-50.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10089863" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/3-50.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="405" /></a></p>
  <p>Christopher Furlong, Matt Cardy/Getty Images</p>
  <p>独立党领袖博尔顿（Henry Bolton，左）刚刚上任不到四个月，就被党内高层亮了红牌。该党的全国执行委员会在上周通过了对他的不信任案。</p>
  <p>但是博尔顿拒绝下台。独立党副领袖帕克（Margot Parker，右）和移民问题发言人宣布辞职，以示不满。独立党的党员将投票决定他的去留。</p>
  <p>引发此次危机的是博尔顿的女友。她在短信中称哈里王子的未婚妻是“美国黑人”，将会给<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B1%E5%9B%BD.md#1">英国</a>王室带来“污点”。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10089849" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/2-54.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10089849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/2-54.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="791" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">?Ian Forsyth/Getty Images</figcaption></figure>  <p>冬天下海是需要勇气的。戴着火鸡形状的帽子，想像自己在火炉里，可能就没那么冷了。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10089879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/UK844_A_04_photo.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10089879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/UK844_A_04_photo.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">LUDOVIC MARIN/AFP/Getty Images</figcaption></figure>  <p>熊皮帽子真暖和，但是能见度差了一点。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10089858" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/7-17.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10089858" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/7-17.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="406" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Jeff J Mitchell/Getty Images</figcaption></figure>  <p>感谢主人把我们涂成了橘黄色。在这白茫茫的雪地里，我们多显眼啊！</p>
  <h2>落马</h2>  <figure id="attachment_10089856" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/6-22.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10089856" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/6-22.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Alan Crowhurst/Getty Images</figcaption></figure>  <p>近日在Ascot举行的青少年骑马障碍赛上，一匹赛马在最后一圈跌倒，连累另外一匹马也跌倒了，两名骑手也被摔下马背。</p>
  <h2>大雪天，有人高兴有人愁。</h2>  <figure id="attachment_10089848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1-71.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10089848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1-71.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Jeff J Mitchell/Getty Images</figcaption></figure>  <p>在刚刚过去的周末，苏格兰降下了八年以来最大的一场雪。部分地区连续下了五天雪。</p>
  <p>大雪给许多人的出行带来不便。但是，滑雪者乐坏了。难得大雪还赶上了周末，于是通往苏格兰高地滑雪场的公路上塞车了，长达六英里。</p>
  <p>接着，问题变得更糟糕了。附近的停车场满了，人们就把车留在了公路上，扫雪车无法通过。警方只好警告滑雪者，尽管“天气很好”，也不要开车去高地的滑雪场了。</p>
  <h2>Lumiere 灯光秀重返伦敦</h2>  <figure id="attachment_10089851" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/4-36.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10089851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/4-36.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Chris J Ratcliffe/Getty Images</figcaption></figure>  <p>1月17日至20日，Lumiere灯光秀在伦敦多个不同的地点同时举行。在五颜六色的灯光之下，你还能认出威斯敏斯特大教堂吗？</p>
  <h2>中国有灶王爷，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8B%B1%E5%9B%BD.md#1">英国</a>有果园仙。</h2>  <figure id="attachment_10089853" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/5-26.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10089853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/5-26.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Matt Cardy/Getty Image</figcaption></figure>  <p>伍斯特郡乡下的村庄里，每年1月份都举行祝酒仪式。</p>
  <p>人们把用苹果酒浸泡过的圣诞蛋糕放在苹果树上，在苹果树的根部撒上苹果酒，然后点燃火把，在果树周围载歌载舞，请求管理果园的神仙让他们在今年有一个好收成，酿出好喝的苹果酒。◇</p>
