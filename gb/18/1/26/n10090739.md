<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：抱怨</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/Fotolia_54451613_Subscription_L-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（fotolia）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>人类是会抱怨的动物，而且，何止会抱怨已尔？还是很会抱怨的动物呢！<br />
谓之不信，且听我道来！<br />
冬天，天气冷了，人类就抱怨老天不好，说老天为什么会让天气那么冷？希望冷赶快过去；可是，到了夏天，天气热了，又抱怨老天不好了，说老天为什么让天气那么热？都要热死了！希望热赶快过去。<br />
同样地，干旱时，人类便抱怨老天不好，说老天为什么不下雨，是不是要把人类干旱死了？等雨季来时，多下几天雨，他便又抱怨老天不好了，说老天为什么不停下雨，把人困在屋里，骨头都要发霉了，如造成水灾，那老天更不知要被骂成什么样子了。<br />
人类抱怨这个抱怨那个，好像万物必需皆备于我，听我主宰；否则便不满意，万物有负于我，活该有罪，非给抱怨一下不可。<br />
其实，人类会抱怨没什么不好，不但没什么不好，而且还有好处。<br />
人类之所以抱怨，是人类天生有不满足、求好的性格。没有的时候要求有，有了以后要求好，好了以后要求更好，有缺点就要求改进，所谓“精益求精”就是了。人类的文化进步就是这样来的。<br />
那么，就去抱怨吧！就去不满吧！就去求好吧！我们将在抱怨、不满、求好中更加精进！</p>
<p><strong> Complaint </strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>Human being is an animal who can complain, and far more than complain? He is great in complaining!<br />
Let me tell you if you won’t to believe.<br />
The weather is cold, human being complains the heaven is no good, says why the sky let the weather so cold? Wishing the cold will pass quickly; but when summer is come, the weather is hot, he also complains the heaven is no good, says why the sky let the weather so hot? It’s burning! Wishing the hot will pass quickly.<br />
It’s the same, when it’s drought, human being will complain the heaven is not good, says why the heaven don’t rain, as if drought brings human being into death. When rain season is come, as rain is drop more days, he also complains the heaven is no good, says why it’s rain ceaselessly, surrounds person in the house, the bone will become mouldy, if it causes flood, the heaven will to be blamed to death.<br />
Human being complains hither and thither, as if every thing must ready for me, dominated by me, or it will be discontented, every thing is fail to come up to me, it deserves the punishment, it’s no choice but to complain.<br />
In fact, human being to complain is not bad, not bad but also has benefit.<br />
Why human being complained is human being has the nature of dissatisfy and forward to good. Want to have something when there is nothing, will search to good when they have something, search better when it’s good, must revise when there is some shortcomings, this is so called “to always endeavor to do still better; to strive for perfection”. The improving of the culture of human being is the fruit as thus.<br />
Then, to complain! To dissatisfy! To search for good! We will improve in the complaint, dissatisfy and search for good more improvement! @</p>
