  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E8%B1%AA%E5%AE%85.md#1">硅谷豪宅</a>一、15995 Cerro Vista Drive<br />  Los Gatos, CA 95032</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10076551" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1.41.09.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10076551" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1.41.09-450x196.png" alt="" width="450" b="196" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B9%B0%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区买房</a>。</figcaption></figure>  <p>在Los Gatos宁静的山脚下，占地1.14英亩，迷人的当代设计和非凡建筑。住宅周围被树林包围，每间房都有各自的风景；挑高的客厅天花板，配上大面玻璃窗，整个视野开阔舒畅！全不锈钢的高档厨房家电，搭配白色台面，创造出厨师级的工作厨房。室内还包含图书室、媒体室、品酒室、3个壁炉。</p>
  <p>室外有970平方英尺的泳池、水疗室和独立客房，同样可俯瞰硅谷景致，树林环绕，同时享受加州阳光。这里虽然位在郊区，但很轻易就能开往17、85公路。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10076553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/01_146356123-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10076553" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/01_146356123-5-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">挑高的客厅空间，室外风景一览无遗。（Alain Pinel Realtors）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10076554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/02_146356123-7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10076554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/02_146356123-7-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">厨师级的厨房设计。（Alain Pinel Realtors）</figcaption></figure>  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E8%B1%AA%E5%AE%85.md#1">湾区豪宅</a>二、40 Selby Lane<br />  Atherton, CA 94027<br />  <figure id="attachment_10076559" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1.41.21.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1.41.21-450x195.png" alt="" width="450" b="195" class="size-medium wp-image-10076559" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B9%B0%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区买房</a>。</figcaption></figure><br />  在中半岛最繁华的地带，享受1英亩的土地，这绝对是难得的机会！尽管建造于1939年的双层房子，看起来设计有些老旧，但硬木地板、瓷砖地板和花岗岩厨房仍是高级的象征。室内有宽敞的空间，经过适当布置会是相当温馨的家园。</p>
  <p>室外空间开阔，泳池旁有独立泳池屋，825平方英尺的空间比主楼房的房间还大上许多。户外土地广大，可供屋主规划庭院或其他休闲空间。这里毗邻多所私立学校，邻近斯坦福大学（Stanford University）和沙山道（Sand Hill Road）商业中心。<br />  <figure id="attachment_10076560" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/03_110342763-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/03_110342763-1-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10076560" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">3图：私人入口大门，这是Atherton高档房产的象征。（Alain Pinel Realtors）</figcaption></figure><br />  <figure id="attachment_10076561" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/04_110342763-9.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/04_110342763-9-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" class="size-large wp-image-10076561" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">4图：泳池周边土地开阔，却保有隐私。（Alain Pinel Realtors）</figcaption></figure></p>
  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%A1%85%E8%B0%B7%E8%B1%AA%E5%AE%85.md#1">硅谷豪宅</a>三、490 Loma Verde Avenue<br />  Palo Alto, CA 94306<br />  <figure id="attachment_10076562" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1.41.42.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1.41.42-450x201.png" alt="" width="450" b="201" class="size-medium wp-image-10076562" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湾区买房。</figcaption></figure><br />  这是2017年建成的全新房产，尤为注重细节的精美设计，挑高的门厅、气派的大门、弧形楼梯、现代式厨房，还有与后院互通的家庭起居室。“绿色设计观”的导入不仅体现了一个自然、舒适的家居环境，还倡导着一种崭新的生活。</p>
  <p>El Carmelo小学（加州排名第1小学）近在漫步距离之内，骑行可到中城购物中心（Midtown Shopping Center），学区内还有JLS Middle（加州＃2中学）和Gunn High（加州＃1高中），此地是湾区的著名学区（买家自行验证入学资格）。<br />  <figure id="attachment_10076563" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/05_ExtFront_01_AH-3-1030x686.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/05_ExtFront_01_AH-3-1030x686-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10076563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">5图：2017落成崭新的房产。（Deleon Realty）</figcaption></figure><br />  <figure id="attachment_10076565" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/06_Kitchen_02_AH-1030x686.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/06_Kitchen_02_AH-1030x686-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10076565" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">6图：白色雅致的厨房。（Deleon Realty）</figcaption></figure></p>
  <p>硅谷豪宅四、121 Park Avenue<br />  Palo Alto, CA 94306<br />  <figure id="attachment_10076566" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1.42.34.png"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/1.42.34-450x205.png" alt="" width="450" b="205" class="size-medium wp-image-10076566" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湾区买房。</figcaption></figure><br />  这座上下三层的高尚住宅建于2012年，以回收木材为灵感，当代的设计融合了时尚的设施，与开放式概念的生活空间。前院有舒适的走道，前门设有户外座椅；后院休闲区和厨房相通，也能轻易通往车库。</p>
  <p>户外可漫步到斯坦福大学、圣丹斯牛排餐厅（Sundance the Steakhouse）、Caltrain火车站，并邻近Escondido小学（加州# 11），Jordan中学（加州# 4）和Palo Alto高中（加州＃5）（买方自行验证入学资格）。<br />  <figure id="attachment_10076567" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/07_ExtFront_03_AH-3-1030x686.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/07_ExtFront_03_AH-3-1030x686-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10076567" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">7图：以环保木材打造房屋外观。（Deleon Realty）</figcaption></figure><br />  <figure id="attachment_10076569" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/08_ExtBack_05_AH-1030x686.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/01/08_ExtBack_05_AH-1030x686-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10076569" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">8图：温馨的后院直通厨房，生活便利。（Deleon Realty）</figcaption></figure></p>
