  <p><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">《广告报》报导，根据<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E6%BE%B3.md#1">南澳</a>州总检察长劳（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">John Rau</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）接到的这一简报：阿市西部派对转化为暴力现场的几率是其它地方的两倍。仅就辛德利街而言，</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">2016</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年每个周末就有平均</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">9</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">起暴力事件，如殴打、抢劫、贩毒等等。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">去年年底，警方利用简报中的情报，获得特权以遏制周末暴力事件。但周末暴力频发问题直到现在才首次曝光，反对党使用信息自由法，得以弄清真相。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">警署专员史蒂文生（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Grant Stevens</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）向劳递交的“警察局风险评估”书透露，在西区，遭到来自正常人的人身攻击的可能性大于其它区两倍，而遭受来自思维低于正常人行为标准者攻击的可能性则为三倍。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">在公共区域，警方有权使用金属探测仪搜身，在一定时间内禁止进入该地区，轰走肇事者，将幼儿转移出该地区，对行为不端者罚款可高达</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">1</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">千澳元。警方特权仅限于阿市西区周五周六晚</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">6</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">点至凌晨</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">6</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">点。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">在野党警方发言人</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Stephan Knoll</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">说，数据显示暴力高峰在午夜</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">12</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">点左右，与</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">2013</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年引进的法规规定早上</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">3</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">点店铺关门在时间点上不符，</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">2014</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年审阅该法规时发现，其作用并不大。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">“西区多数<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8A%AF%E7%BD%AA.md#1">犯罪</a>发生在大约</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">12</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">点，</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">3</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">点钟关门是否晚了点儿？”他说，“如果州政府想减少南澳犯罪，那就该面对事实，南澳毒品泛滥，应支持自由党打击毒品走私策略。”</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">阿德莱德西区协会发言人</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Andrew Wallace</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">说，希望公共区域法规，</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">3</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">点关门法规能将夜间的安全带回来。“给予警察特权来治理这一地区是件好事。”他说。“关门政策解决了一些问题，希望新法律能将失去的平静送还我们。”</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">南澳警务厅厅长皮克顿（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Chris Picton</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）说，</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">3</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">点关门法规施行后，该地区犯罪有所下降。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">深夜场地协会（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Late Night Venues Association</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）主席</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Tim Swaine</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">说，从趋势上来看，南澳警员协会（</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">SAPOL</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）使用的数据显示，从</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">2015</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">到</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">2016</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年犯罪有所下降，基于此点，他质疑“公共区域”的决定。然而，他又说，公共区域法规给警察的权利并不比许可证场地（酒吧等）的多，而协会支持酒吧法规。“我们并不反对宣布某地为公共区域，问题是，根据什么来确认某处属于公共区域？”他说，“从文件上来看，并无太大的秩序和安全问题。”。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">辛德利街斗殴致死的</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">22</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">岁男子</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Jack Hanley</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，死于凌晨</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">5.20</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，开禁</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">40</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">分钟之前。公布公共区域时，即</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Hanley</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">死亡</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">1</span></span><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;">个月前，警方说，该地区对守法者来说安全，但对那些醉汉和罪犯来说则是禁地。</span></span></span></span></p>
