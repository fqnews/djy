<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>超萌小小邮差送信去  民众直呼超可爱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/a6b8f40ff7cb2548c9991655a234e313-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>宜兰邮局107年10月11日举办的小小绿衣天使体验营活动。（曾汉东／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年10月11日讯】（大纪元记者曾汉东台湾宜兰报导）宜兰邮局为了让小朋友更进一步认识邮差的工作内容，于107年10月11日在宜兰邮局大礼堂特别举办了两场<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%B0%8F%E7%BB%BF%E8%A1%A3%E5%A4%A9%E4%BD%BF.md#1">小小绿衣天使</a>体验营的活动，吸引超过30组家长报名。</p>
<p>穿着绿色的制服，背着迷你邮包袋，手拿着邮件，已准备好要去送信。您没看错，这些都是来报名体验邮差工作的小朋友们。<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E8%90%8C%E5%B0%8F%E5%B0%8F%E9%82%AE%E5%B7%AE.md#1">超萌小小邮差</a>送信到附近商家，收信民众直呼超可爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10776221" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/dbe4cdc7b1d624254499031b7177f77b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/dbe4cdc7b1d624254499031b7177f77b-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">穿着绿色制服的小邮差，从邮件进口开始体验。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10776223" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e67bcff5e03ce7dcd52db12b914f2014.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/e67bcff5e03ce7dcd52db12b914f2014-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">穿着绿色制服的小邮差，做邮件大区分体验。 （曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10776227" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/3accff61c355a8349db38e67fa42d4b6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/3accff61c355a8349db38e67fa42d4b6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小邮差们骑乘电动儿童摩托车至邮筒收信。 （曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>带着3岁儿子参加宜兰邮局举办的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%B0%8F%E7%BB%BF%E8%A1%A3%E5%A4%A9%E4%BD%BF.md#1">小小绿衣天使</a>体验营活动的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E9%9B%85%E9%92%B0.md#1">张雅钰</a>妈妈表示，儿子每天在家喜欢玩邮差送信游戏，认为自己是邮差，他的第一志愿是当邮差。每当看到真的邮差送信来，会很兴奋。另一位<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E4%BD%A9%E7%9C%9F.md#1">吴佩真</a>妈妈表示，她也带小朋友参加不同类型体验活动，她觉得小小绿衣天使体验很不一样、很好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10776235" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2de73f7722c4563a828da2847fd99fa6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/2de73f7722c4563a828da2847fd99fa6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E9%9B%85%E9%92%B0.md#1">张雅钰</a>妈妈带着3岁儿子参加宜兰邮局举办的小小绿衣天使体验营活动。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10776239" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/b05e7a047b61573a9ceffdcec298037f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776239" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/b05e7a047b61573a9ceffdcec298037f-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%90%B4%E4%BD%A9%E7%9C%9F.md#1">吴佩真</a>妈妈带着3岁女儿参加宜兰邮局举办的小小绿衣天使体验营活动。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小邮差们一早就来邮局报到，换上特制的儿童版邮差制服，邮局以“一分信的旅程”卡通影片带领小朋友进入邮政世界，从邮件进口开始，小朋友必须大区分后，再将信件分拣，一一排信放入到袋内，才能出发投递。活动中也宣导了简易交通规则，让小邮差们骑乘电动儿童摩托车至邮筒收信，最后再将信件汇整后，亲自到邻近住家递送信件，超萌的模样让民众直夸超级可爱。活动结束后小朋友都说邮差叔叔真的很辛苦，以后看到邮差叔叔一定要向他们说声谢谢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10776229" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/a690d93e9f77b0aaff6fc2f480e7fff4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776229" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/a690d93e9f77b0aaff6fc2f480e7fff4-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">穿着绿色的制服，背着迷你邮包袋，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E8%90%8C%E5%B0%8F%E5%B0%8F%E9%82%AE%E5%B7%AE.md#1">超萌小小邮差</a>送信去。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10776232" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/9a4b314de80c5effe9ed37cf09141374.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10776232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/9a4b314de80c5effe9ed37cf09141374-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">超萌小小邮差去送信，收信民众直呼超可爱。（曾汉东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>宜兰邮局周昆法局长表示，小朋友从信件进口到递送都能亲身体验，认识邮政服务。邮差们不畏风雨在巷弄中穿梭，只为将信件及包裹准时送到顾客手上，希望藉由小小邮差的活动，让顾客明白邮差的工作内容。◇</p>
