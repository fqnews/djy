  <h2>众<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B9%A6%E9%B9%89.md#1">鹦鹉</a>表演<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%89%B9%E6%8A%80.md#1">特技</a></h2>  <p>看看影片里的众鹦鹉，有的会随着主人释出的旋律，用自己特殊的叫法陶醉其中；有的会随着音乐跳舞，舞姿千奇百怪，有走来走去的，还有会狂转头跳“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E9%A3%8E%E6%89%87.md#1">电风扇</a>舞”，转到头都晕了……</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10776550" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/568c73cfcfc9d15f0664bd83d1925168.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10776550 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/568c73cfcfc9d15f0664bd83d1925168-450x455.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="455" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">正在跳“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E9%A3%8E%E6%89%87.md#1">电风扇</a>舞”的鹦鹉。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看完整影片（Credit: JukinMedia）?</strong></span><br />  <div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-008b6d38-a714-4d27-521d-3f0e2249e164" data-id="player-008b6d38-a714-4d27-521d-3f0e2249e164"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/008b6d38-a714-4d27-521d-3f0e2249e164?r=16x9&amp;s=720x720&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F10%2F11%2Fn10776091.md#1"></a></p>
  <p>影片中段的黄鹦鹉则会变身“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%BF%E5%9C%B0%E6%9C%BA.md#1">凿地机</a>”，咬起杯子，全身疯狂上下震动，这是凿地工人转世吗……？！</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10776551" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/0bf4d8532fe504f92def2279a5e025c3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10776551 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/0bf4d8532fe504f92def2279a5e025c3-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鹦鹉变身“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%BF%E5%9C%B0%E6%9C%BA.md#1">凿地机</a>”。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>紧接在“凿地工人”后面的鹦鹉，也是令人惊讶，一般情况都是鹦鹉学人说话，这只鹦鹉竟学起猫语，不断在猫群里喵喵叫。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10776556" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/4c331c21b2619d1891b2c5c2bc8a2f15.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10776556" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/4c331c21b2619d1891b2c5c2bc8a2f15-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鹦鹉<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AD%A6%E7%8C%AB%E5%8F%AB.md#1">学猫叫</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>它也许是要试图融入猫群，可是猫猫们可没那么笨，一看就知道这毛茸茸的尖嘴家伙是“异类”，恐怕不是什么好东西，纷纷敬而远之。</p>
  <p>最后两只鹦鹉的技能则是溜滑板、啄镜子打节拍。下次看到鹦鹉，不要再只说鹦鹉会学人说话啦，它们可是很多才多艺的。</p>
