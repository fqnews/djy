  <figure id="attachment_10776638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1046498866-e1539247903832.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10776638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1046498866-e1539247903832.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="379" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（ IAN KINGTON/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>10月6日，英超球赛中Bournemouth以4:0战胜Watford。Bournemouth的后卫Nathan Ake（白色球衣）被媒体评为这场比赛表现最优秀的球员，因为他没有给对方球员任何机会。</p>
  <h2>这是小人国吗？</h2>  <figure id="attachment_10776641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1049811516-e1539248021751.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10776641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1049811516-e1539248021751.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Christopher Furlong/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10776642" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1049309786-e1539248048848.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10776642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1049309786-e1539248048848.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="900" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（ Christopher Furlong/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>在刚刚过去的周末，一个“巨人”光临利物浦的海边，它躺在沙滩上，情景如同《格列佛游记》里面格列佛在小人国的经历。</p>
  <p>这是巨型牵线木偶第三次光临利物浦，引来超过十万人观看。</p>
  <p>同时展出的还有各种巨型物品，包括这个被刀子“切开”的公交车，像不像是巨人的早餐？</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10776649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1045962902-e1539248106937.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10776649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1045962902-e1539248106937.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（ Leon Neal/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>充满童趣的蛋糕，让人舍不得吃掉！</p>
  <p>近日，伦敦举行了蛋糕展，图为一个参赛作品，名为Up Up and Away，顶部是一个热气球，下面是以海洋为主题的多层蛋糕。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10776651" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1045931794-e1539248152356.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10776651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1045931794-e1539248152356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（ Jack Taylor/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>草地，鹿群，真是难以想像这是在伦敦市内。图为伦敦里士满公园的秋日景色。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10776652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1045023524-e1539248200848.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10776652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1045023524-e1539248200848.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="901" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（ ANDY BUCHANAN/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>世界最贵的威士忌，猜猜一瓶多少钱？</p>
  <p>近日，一瓶1926年酿造的麦卡伦Valerio Adami标牌威士忌创下新的拍卖纪录。这一瓶威士忌的成交价是70万镑，外加支付给拍卖行约15万镑的费用，买家一共需要支付近85万镑。</p>
  <p>进行拍卖的邦瀚斯拍卖行（Bonhams）表示，这批威士忌自从1926年以来一直储藏在一个酒缸里，1986年被装瓶，麦卡伦的老板请来了两位著名的流行艺术家设计它的商标，分别是Valerio Adami和Peter Blake。当时一共装了24瓶，两人设计的商标各占12瓶，目前不清楚还有几瓶存世。</p>
  <p>这次拍卖中，来自中国的竞价者非常踊跃，拍卖行没有披露买家的身份。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10776653" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1045416522-e1539248255647.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-10776653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/GettyImages-1045416522-e1539248255647.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Jack Taylor/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>上周，麦当劳、连锁酒吧JD Wetherspoons和连锁餐馆TGI Fridays的员工以及UberEats和Deliveroo的外卖小哥举行了罢工，因为他们要求公司加薪，达到每小时10镑钱。</p>
  <p>有些顾客在社交媒体上开玩笑说：“所以，麦当劳、TGI Fridays还有Wetherspoons今天罢工了。嗯，那我的早饭、午饭和晚饭吃什么？”</p>
