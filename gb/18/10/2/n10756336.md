  <p>在MV中<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E5%8A%BF%E5%AE%89.md#1">陈势安</a>扮演一位被工作、生活琐事压抑的上班族，某天他终于鼓起勇气决定放下手边的杂事，骑着重机到海边放空，途中遇到好友<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B6%E7%8E%AE%E5%BA%AD.md#1">叶玮庭</a>为他加油打气，两人更一起听着音乐、唱歌跳舞，享受了一个愉快“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%88%92%E6%9C%8D.md#1">舒服</a>”的假期。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10756381" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/6n2a2207.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/6n2a2207-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10756381" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">陈势安骑重机拍摄《舒服》MV。（华纳音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10756383" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/IMG_5421_s.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/IMG_5421_s-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10756383" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">陈势安骑重机拍摄《舒服》MV。（华纳音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10756385" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/IMG_5707_s.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/IMG_5707_s-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10756385" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">陈势安到海边拍摄《舒服》MV。（华纳音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>这次陈势安也挑战了骑重机绕沿海公路，同门师妹<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B6%E7%8E%AE%E5%BA%AD.md#1">叶玮庭</a>更义气客串演出，个性开朗的她在片场中令大家笑声不断，让陈势安笑说：“这次整个拍摄过程都让我觉得很‘舒服’！”</p>
  <p>叶玮庭也透露为了拍摄压力不小，因此失眠了两、三天，甚至一度担心自己肤色比陈势安黑，两人先前虽然私交不深，却常在工作场合遇到，对彼此的印象都是“很会唱歌”，叶玮庭更笑说：“之前商演听到师兄在我前面唱，我在后台压力超大的！”</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10756370" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/IMG_4472_s.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/IMG_4472_s-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10756370" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">陈势安拍摄《舒服》MV，同门师妹叶玮庭义气客串MV一角。（华纳音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10756378" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/IMG_4116_s.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/IMG_4116_s-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10756378" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">陈势安与同门师妹叶玮庭在MV里一起听着音乐、唱歌跳舞。（华纳音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10756372" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/IMG_4367_s.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/IMG_4367_s-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-10756372" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">陈势安拍摄《舒服》MV，同门师妹叶玮庭义气客串MV一角。（华纳音乐提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>被问及是否会像MV剧情一样放下一切去度假，陈势安笑说自己很有经验：“我曾经在工作空档突然发现隔天开始有三天假期，半小时内确定没事，就立刻订机票、饭店，工作一结束就马上回家收行李，直奔机场！”疯狂程度让身边同事当场傻眼。</p>
  <p>第四波主打《舒服》MV于10月2日在YouTube《陈势安官方频道》全球正式首播。</p>
