<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：玻利维亚红湖 呈现红白相间奇观</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/1024px-Andean_Flamingos_Laguna_Colorada_Bolivia_Luca_Galuzzi_2006-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>玻利维亚的科罗拉达湖呈现红白相间的奇特景观。（维基百科）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年10月02日讯】（大纪元记者陈俊村报导）科罗拉达湖（Laguna Colorada，又称<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E6%B9%96.md#1">红湖</a>）是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8E%BB%E5%88%A9%E7%BB%B4%E4%BA%9A.md#1">玻利维亚</a>西南方的一座浅水盐湖，它的湖水既不是蓝色，也不是绿色，而是少见的淡红色。在白色沙洲的映衬下，这座湖泊呈现红白相间的奇特景观。</p>
<p>科罗拉达湖位于<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8E%BB%E5%88%A9%E7%BB%B4%E4%BA%9A.md#1">玻利维亚</a>的阿尔蒂普拉诺高原（Altiplano），湖面海拔为4,278公尺，接近该国与智利的边界。此湖的面积约有15,000英亩，但深度不到3英尺（0.9公尺）。</p>
<p>阿尔蒂普拉诺高原素以色彩缤纷的大型泻湖著称，因其湖水中含有矿物质，但科罗拉达湖是该地区唯一一座红色的湖泊。</p>
<p><a title="Laguna Colorada" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/breenjones/7358324706/" data-flickr-embed="true"><img src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7242/7358324706_ce82152cf8_z.jpg" alt="Laguna Colorada" width="600" b="401" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></p>
<p>科罗拉达湖之所以呈现淡红色，是因为湖水中生长着多种<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%97%BB%E7%B1%BB.md#1">藻类</a>，而湖中大量沉积的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%A1%BC%E7%A0%82.md#1">硼砂</a>则呈现白色，与湖水的颜色相互争辉。</p>
<p><a title="laguna colorada" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/53888266@N02/14888817318/" data-flickr-embed="true"><img src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3850/14888817318_be36130e32_z.jpg" alt="laguna colorada" width="600" b="338" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></p>
<p><a title="Borax @ Laguna Colorada" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/plucciola/8910858104/" data-flickr-embed="true"><img src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3791/8910858104_92d0bd2bd1_z.jpg" alt="Borax @ Laguna Colorada" width="600" b="398" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></p>
<p>此外，该湖吸引了大量红鹤栖息，只见它们成群结队点缀于湖面上，成为该湖的另一个亮点。</p>
<p><a title="Laguna Colorada, Salar de Uyuni tour, Bolivia, May 2014" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/whereisyourtoothbrush/14144294757/" data-flickr-embed="true"><img src="https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2914/14144294757_8832471da7_z.jpg" alt="Laguna Colorada, Salar de Uyuni tour, Bolivia, May 2014" width="600" b="338" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></p>
<p><a title="Laguna Colorada, Bolivia" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/dmbrickman/6799365909/" data-flickr-embed="true"><img src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7017/6799365909_0b77758dd0_z.jpg" alt="Laguna Colorada, Bolivia" width="600" b="399" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></p>
<p>科罗拉达湖四周大多是沙漠、岩石和盐矿。其气温经常在清晨时降至摄氏零度以下，但在夏季会比较温暖，这使得观光客得以造访该景点。</p>
<p><a title="Laguna Colorada" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/dudufigueiredo/305020460/" data-flickr-embed="true"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/113/305020460_622c6597c2_z.jpg?zz=1" alt="Laguna Colorada" width="600" b="450" /></a><a async src="//embedr.flickr.com/assets/client-code.js" charset="utf-8"></a></p>
