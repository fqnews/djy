  <figure id="attachment_10795681" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/buying-flowers-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10795681 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/buying-flowers-1-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">奥力佛用自己的钱买花儿。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>这位来自美国堪萨斯州奥弗兰帕克?（Overland Park）的六岁男孩对<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%AD%A6%E5%AF%9F.md#1">警察</a>充满热情的向往，最爱穿警察制服。</p>
  <p>他给自己的新年新希望就是要走访<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%BB%E8%80%81%E9%99%A2.md#1">养老院</a>， 给老爷爷、老奶奶们发放鲜花和违规单，单据上这样写道：“您太可爱了，所以拿到这张罚单！”他还问每个人要不要一个抱抱。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10794569" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/ticketting.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10794569" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/ticketting-150x120.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="426" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小奥力佛在家努力开罚单。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10794578" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/opa.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10794578 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/opa-450x235.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="235" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">献给老爷爷一朵鲜花。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>看完整影片?</strong></span><br />  <div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-c07c792a-46bc-45fb-4f44-0c4043265ea7" data-id="player-c07c792a-46bc-45fb-4f44-0c4043265ea7"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/c07c792a-46bc-45fb-4f44-0c4043265ea7?r=16x9&amp;s=1280x720&&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F18%2F10%2F19%2Fn10794304.md#1"></a></p>
  <p>“我只想让一些人微笑。”</p>
  <p>母亲布兰迪．戴维斯（Brandi Davis）写道：“人们感动得落泪，并在街上拦住我说这是他们看到的最好的故事！”</p>
  <p>2月份起，奥力佛就吵着妈妈要实现他的新年新愿望，布兰迪说：“他问我：‘我们为什么还不去拜访养老院呢？’我喜欢看到他与市区居民互动。他也乐此不疲！”</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10795707" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/ready.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10795707 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/ready-450x424.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="424" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲜花满载，奥力佛准备要出任务了。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>妈妈还说：“奥力佛心目中认为自己是一名真正的警察！他现在的职责就是服务社区！当地警察部门给了他一个荣誉徽章。”</p>
  <p>很多养老院的老先生老太太都说，这是他们一天中最开心的片刻！大家都毫无例外地接受了拥抱的邀请，有的向他敬礼，有的与他握手，还有的假装投降。</p>
