<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2011年预言川普当总统 美国男子：他会连任</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/GettyImages-632237092-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美国男子泰勒（Mark Taylor）预言说，川普将会连任该国总统。图为2017年1月20日，川普摄于就职典礼。(Mark Wilson/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年02月09日讯】（大纪元记者陈俊村编译报导）<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD.md#1">美国</a>男子泰勒（Mark Taylor）曾在2011年<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A2%84%E8%A8%80.md#1">预言</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B7%9D%E6%99%AE.md#1">川普</a>（特朗普）会当选该国<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%80%BB%E7%BB%9F.md#1">总统</a>。他著有《川普<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A2%84%E8%A8%80.md#1">预言</a>》（<ahref="https://www.amazon.com/Trump-Prophecies-Astonishing-Tomorrow-Coming/dp/0998142670" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">The Trump Prophecies</a>）一书，陈述他对这位第45任<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%80%BB%E7%BB%9F.md#1">总统</a>的预言。他表示，在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B7%9D%E6%99%AE.md#1">川普</a>任内，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD.md#1">美国</a>国力会空前强大，再次赢得世人的敬重。而在下次大选中，川普将顺利胜选，获得连任。</p>
<h2>泰勒：神选定川普当美国总统</h2>
<p>据基督教广播网（Christian Broadcasting Network）报导，泰勒是一名退休消防队员，住在佛罗里达州奥兰多（Orlando）。他自称受到神的启示，从而得知有关川普的一些预言。</p>
<p>报导说，2011年4月，泰勒在电视上看到川普接受访问。当时他对川普所知不多，只知道他是个很有权势的商人，建立了自己的商业帝国，而且考虑出马竞选美国总统。正当他在听川普讲话时，他突然听到上帝对他说：“你现在听到的是一名总统的声音。”</p>
<p>此时，泰勒立即冲进办公室，拿起纸和笔，将上帝告诉他的事情记录下来。</p>
<p>泰勒表示，基本上，上帝告诉他说，他选定川普这个人，将荣耀、尊敬和复兴带给美国。美国会达到前所未有的繁荣；美元会出现史上最强劲的走势；而以色列和美国之间的关系会比以往都好等等。</p>
<p>（编者按：这与川普上任后，美国<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E6%B5%8E.md#1">经济</a>复苏、就业率大增、美元走强、外资大举进入的情况吻合，而川普也在去年12月宣布承认耶路撒冷为以色列首都，进一步强化美国与以色列之间的关系。）</p>
<p>泰勒原以为川普会在2012年赢得总统大选，但当年他并没有参选，所以泰勒怀疑这预言失准了。后来，川普在2015年6月宣布参选，而且在2016年11月胜选，让很多不看好他的人“跌破眼镜”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10129109" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/GettyImages-632231266.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10129109 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/GettyImages-632231266-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2017年1月20日，川普伉俪观看就职典礼当天的游行。(Patrick Smith/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>泰勒：川普会连任美国总统</h2>
<p>泰勒说，上帝藉由川普来阻止正在占领美国的反基督教议程（编者按：应指无神论），而经常对川普有意见的主流媒体，也会开始同意他的观点或做法。</p>
<p>他说：“上帝将清理媒体。他正在寻找他的子民。”由于现在的媒体被富豪收买并占有，没有真实报导的精神，上帝将加以改造。</p>
<p>泰勒相信，川普的胜利只是刚开始。他同时预言说，教堂会茁壮发展（编者按：应指宗教或对神的信仰大兴），美国将见证上帝的伟大之举。</p>
<p>当被问到川普是否会赢得连任时，泰勒说：“绝对会。”当人们见到美国走向好的一方面时，包括<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BB%8F%E6%B5%8E.md#1">经济</a>、工作等，他自然能在第二任中胜出。</p>
<p>以下是泰勒在2011年4月28日写下的预言全文：</p>
<p>&#8220;The Spirit of God says I&#8217;ve chosen this man Donald Trump for such a time as this. For as Benjamin Netanyahu is to Israel, so shall this man be to the United States of America, for I will use this man to bring honor, respect and restoration to America. America will be respected once again as the most powerful, prosperous nation on Earth other than Israel. The dollar will be the strongest it has ever been in the history of the United States and will once again be the currency by which all others are judged. The Spirit of God says the enemy will quake and shake, and fear this man I have anointed. They will even quake and shake when he announces he is running for President. It will be like the shot heard across the world. Then you will say what shall we do now? This man knows all our tricks and schemes. We&#8217;ve been robbing America for decades. What should we do to stop this? The Spirit says, ha, no one shall stop this that I have started, for the enemy has stolen from America for decades and it stops now. For I will use this man to reap the harvest that the United States has sown for and plunder from the enemy what he has stolen, and return it back sevenfold to the United States. The enemy will say, &#8216;Israel, Israel, what about Israel?&#8217; Israel will be protected by America once again. The Spirit says yes, America will once again stand hand in hand with Israel and the two shall be as one, for the ties between Israel and America will be stronger than ever and Israel will flourish like never before. The Spirit of God says I will protect America and Israel, for this next President will be a man of his word. When he speaks the world will listen and know that there is something greater in him than all the others before him. This man&#8217;s word is his bond and the world and America will know this, and the enemy will fear this, for this man will be fearless. The Spirit says when the financial harvest begins so shall the parallel in the spiritual for America. The Spirit of God says in this next election they will spend billions to keep this president out. It will be like money down the toilet. Let them waste their money, for where it comes from is being used by evil forces at work, but they will not succeed. This next election will be a clean sweep for the man that I have chosen. They will say things about this man, the enemy, but it will not affect him and they will say it rolls off of him like a duck. For even as the feathers of a duck protect it, so shall my feathers protect this next president. Even mainstream news media will be captivated by this man and the abilities that I gift to him and they will even begin to agree with him, says the Spirit.&#8221; ＃</p>
