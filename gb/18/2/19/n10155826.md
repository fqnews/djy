<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>雨水节气菜蔬甘甜 熬制翡翠海鲜羹</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JJOU1"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bfenwa3188/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>到了“<span class="highlightNode"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%A8%E6%B0%B4.md#1">雨水</a></span>”时节，空中的水汽增加，所以容易下雨。而<span class="highlightNode"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%A8%E6%B0%B4.md#1">雨水</a></span>的到来，正好符合农夫们的春耕播种的需求。天地的节奏就像是老天安排好的一样，春天就是给与适合播种的气候，让种子发芽，万物成长。</p>
<p>二月正处于季节交替之际，这时候不稳定的气候变化很容易感冒，在中医养生中<span class="highlightNode">雨水</span>节气养重在“调养脾胃”，是一年之中调理脾胃最佳时期。调理好脾胃，能有效增强抵抗力，预防感冒。</p>
<div class="text_exposed_show">
<p><strong>饮食小叮咛：</strong>应少吃酸味，多吃甜味。饮食清淡不油腻</p>
<p><strong>时令蔬菜：</strong>韭菜、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8F%A0%E8%8F%9C.md#1">菠菜</a>、香椿、茼蒿、荠菜、春笋、萝卜</p>
<p><strong>当季水果：</strong>水梨、甘蔗</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%A8%E6%B0%B4%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">雨水料理</a>“翡翠海鲜羹”</span></h2>
<p>春天的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8F%A0%E8%8F%9C.md#1">菠菜</a>最鲜嫩，雨水时节吃菠菜，有助于解毒、防春燥。此时的菠菜不但价钱便宜，而且味道甘美。将菠菜打成汁调和蛋白炸成的翡翠羹，更是好吃喔！</p>
<p><strong>翡翠食材：</strong>菠菜（用食物处理机榨成汁）50g、鸡蛋（蛋白） 约 50g、太白粉1匙</p>
<p><strong>羹汤食材：</strong>虾仁200g、透抽（中尾）一条、红萝卜少量、白萝卜1/3条</p>
<p><strong>调味料：</strong>盐、柴鱼粉、白胡椒、蒜末、香油</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" src=""></a><a href="https://git.io/JJOU1"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bfenwa3188/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/bMFFRFJBfTU?wmode=transparent&#038;wmode=opaque" allowfullscreen></a>
	</div></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #808080;"><em>（完整制作视频，制作步骤如下。）</em></span></p>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>1. 菠菜切段，加水，打成汁。滤掉菠菜渣，将汁倒入同等量的蛋白液中，加一匙太白粉搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10155971" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/jade-soup-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10155971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/jade-soup-1-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%A8%E6%B0%B4%E8%8A%82%E6%B0%94.md#1">雨水节气</a>料理翡翠海鲜汤做法之：菠菜打汁，滤渣。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 油锅低温，将调好的菠菜蛋白汁倒入油中，用锅铲搅动，炸成颗粒状“翡翠”。捞起投入冰水，将多余的油腻洗去，泡在冰水中备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10155975" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/jade-soup-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10155975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/jade-soup-2-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%A8%E6%B0%B4%E8%8A%82%E6%B0%94.md#1">雨水节气</a>料理翡翠海鲜汤做法之：翡翠入冰水去油。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 汤锅加入清水约1.5公升，放入红白萝卜丁，用太白粉略勾薄芡，加入盐、柴鱼粉，再加蒜末和胡椒添香气。汤熬成羹状后，倒入川烫熟的虾仁、透抽丁，最后将冰水中的翡翠捞出，撒入羹汤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10155979" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/jade-soup-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10155979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/jade-soup-3-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">雨水节气料理翡翠海鲜汤做法之：制作羹汤。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 起锅装盘，在羹心滴入少许香油，就可以开动了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_10155983" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/jade-soup-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10155983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/jade-soup-5-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">雨水节气料理翡翠海鲜汤做法之：滴入香油。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>伴着窗外淅淅沥沥的雨水声，喝一口鲜香翠嫩的翡翠海鲜羹，是否有感受到春的绿意呢？</p>
