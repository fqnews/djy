  <p>位于悉尼岩石区Circular Quay的五星级酒店InterContinental 117 dining特地为大家准备了中国新年特别版的Fine dining，结合中国传统美食元素，要大家来品尝不一样的中国新年大餐！</p>
  <p>InterContinental 117 dining环境幽静，特别适合家人朋友小聚怡情。如果有喜欢小酌品酒的朋友，那来这家店特别合适，InterContinental 117 dining 会为每道菜搭配最合适的酒，一套晚餐就会搭配5-6种精品葡萄酒哟。而且，这里的酒来源于澳洲小酒庄，每年精品产量很少，在市场上很难买得到。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10124909" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_01.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10124909 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_01-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">InterContinental 117 dining 餐厅一角(鑫鑫/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <p>InterContinental 117 dining 今年特地推出了中国新年版的Fine dining，结合了中西方美食元素，带给大家一个高调的中国新年晚餐。接下来我们一起来看看菜单吧：</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10124922" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_11.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10124922 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_11-450x325.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="325" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法式小面包配海盐与口感丝滑的黄油(鑫鑫/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <p>前菜法式小面包：搭配着口感丝滑的黄油和优质的海盐，入口香脆。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10124913" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_03.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10124913 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_03-450x279.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="279" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">形如元宝的茶香带子馄饨，招财进宝(鑫鑫/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <p>Tea-Smoked Scallop Dumpling 茶香带子馄饨：馄饨如元宝，都说新年吃馄饨可以招财进宝。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10124914" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_04.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10124914 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_04-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">搭配白芝麻和青椒丝，口感香滑鲜美(鑫鑫/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <p>InterContinental 117 dining 精心准备了茶香带子馄饨，搭配着白芝麻和青椒丝，口感香滑鲜美，值得一试哟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10124918" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_08.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10124918 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_08-450x270.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="270" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烤老虎虾，嫩而多汁(鑫鑫/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <p>Grilled Tiger Prawn 烤老虎虾：新鲜的老虎虾搭配着XO黄油酱和水萝卜，虾肉烤的刚刚好，嫩而多汁，搭配着水萝卜和香菜，清爽鲜香。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10124920" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_10.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10124920 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲜蒸盲曹，年年有余(鑫鑫/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <p>Steamed Cone Bay Barramundi 鲜蒸盲曹：年年有鱼（余），新年晚餐鱼是必不可少的。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10124919" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_09.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10124919 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_09-450x326.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="326" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">姜葱酱汁蒸盲曹，搭配酱青豆脆葱花(鑫鑫/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <p>InterContinental 117 dining准备了姜葱酱汁蒸盲曹，搭配着酱青豆和脆葱花，吃一口，满口清香，鲜滑不腻。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10124917" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_07.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10124917 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_07-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红烧肉口感软绵香脆(鑫鑫/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <p>Hong Shao Rou Pork Belly 红烧肉： 红烧肉搭配着栗子、小白菜和腌制的脆洋葱一起吃，丝毫感觉不到肉的油腻，口感软绵香脆。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10124915" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_05.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10124915 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_05-450x281.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="281" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">梅子味烤冰激凌，可以帮助消除口中菜香(鑫鑫/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <p>甜点：前面几道菜是不是口感有点重？这个时候吃甜点会尝不到甜点的味道。所以 InterContinental 117 dining 特地准备了一款小甜点，梅子味的烤冰淇淋，口感酸甜，帮助消除口中的菜香。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10124916" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_06.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10124916 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/20180208_Alfred_RST_06-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">精致美味的甜点，甜中带一点酸(鑫鑫/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <p>然后才上正式的甜品：Sweet Emotion 搭配着荔枝果冻、树莓、菠萝片、百香果、芝麻碎和柠檬乳酸冰糕，甜中微带果酸，帮助开胃，超级赞。</p>
  <p>传统印象中的Fine Dining都是3道菜，可InterContinental 117 dining为中国新年足足准备了5道菜和小甜品，每位$99，如配对美酒则为特价每人article content begin -->59。仅在2月15日周四、16日周五和17日周六晚上时段供应。</p>
  <p>菜肴精美，环境优雅，价格超值，特别适合亲人朋友在相聚团圆时一起享受的大餐。喜欢的朋友请打电话预定哟！</p>
