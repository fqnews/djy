<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>爸爸夜晚听到怪声 惊见浴室有“怪影晃动”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY2-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>狗爸听到浴室有怪声，随即看到狗的水碗旁边有一大团黑影在晃动。 (Courtesy of?<span style="color: #000080;"><a style="color: #000080;" href="https://twitter.com/mythicalwndfish" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sarah Colvin
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年02月03日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳编译报导）养过狗的人都知道，和<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B1%AA%E6%98%9F%E4%BA%BA.md#1">汪星人</a>一起生活，它们绝不会让你觉得无聊。而最有趣的事情往往不是发生在出门探险途中，而是它们不经意间带来的小惊喜。最近，小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E3%B9%B4%E7%8A%AC.md#1">?犬</a>莱西（Lacy）就让宠爱它的饲主大卫乐不可支。</p>
<p>上周四晚上11点，家在美国旧金山的大卫（David Colvin）准备去睡觉了，这时他感觉到家里有异常响动。因为莱西早就睡了，他怀疑是不是有外人或是野生动物潜进了家里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10110017" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10110017" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY1-450x514.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="514" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: #000080;"><a style="color: #000080;" href="https://twitter.com/mythicalwndfish" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sarah Colvin</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>他的心一时狂跳起来。“浴室很黑，当我听到放莱西碟子的地方发出很奇怪的声响，像是有什么东西在大声撩水，心里感到害怕。”他告诉宠物媒体“渡渡鸟”（The Dodo），“我能看到的就是它水碗旁边的一大团黑影，不知道那是什么。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10110018" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10110018 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY2-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: #000080;"><a style="color: #000080;" href="https://twitter.com/mythicalwndfish" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sarah Colvin</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>莱西的白毛就是在暗室里也会很显眼，所以这显然不是它。那是浣熊吗？还是臭鼬？灯一亮，他脑中闪过一个念头，那……是鬼吗？</p>
<p>“我把灯打开之后，发现好像是莱西的毯子在卫生间里漂浮晃动。”不是鬼，也不是别的什么，正是莱西！</p>
<p>狗狗在夜里醒来感觉口渴，于是就向着碗那边去了。不过，它还是对舒适的小床铺恋恋不舍，于是就披挂着毯子蹭过去了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10110019" style="width: 527px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10110019 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY3.jpg" alt="mythicalwndfish" width="527" b="490" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: #000080;"><a style="color: #000080;" href="https://twitter.com/mythicalwndfish" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sarah Colvin</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>莱西这只“小懒虫”！大卫既如释重负，又忍俊不禁：“它不紧不慢地喝着水，于是我跑着取回了手机 ，拍了几张照片。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10110020" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10110020 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: #000080;"><a style="color: #000080;" href="https://twitter.com/mythicalwndfish" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sarah Colvin</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>喝饱了水的莱西，又披着毯子往床边走，一点也没有让微寒的室温打搅到睡意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10110021" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10110021 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY5-600x445.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="445" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: #000080;"><a style="color: #000080;" href="https://twitter.com/mythicalwndfish" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sarah Colvin</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过走到一半，毯子还是滑落到地上去了。好在有大卫跟在后面，他笑着给它披上了身。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10110022" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10110022 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY6-600x448.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="448" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: #000080;"><a style="color: #000080;" href="https://twitter.com/mythicalwndfish" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sarah Colvin</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>对“狗爸”来说，这或许是很普通的一个夜晚，不过狗狗的“保暖有方”还是让他很惊讶，也觉得“太可笑”。他禁不住和女儿莎拉分享自己拍到的景象。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10110065" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10110065 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY9-450x458.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="458" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（<span style="color: #000080;"><a style="color: #000080;" href="https://twitter.com/mythicalwndfish" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sarah Colvin推特</a></span>截图)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“爸爸经常给我传莱西的近照，一大早，他就给我发了几张照片，我觉得真是太逗趣了。”莎拉说。而她对爸爸如此宠莱西也习以为常了：“我总说，那是他最爱的小孩。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10110024" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10110024" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY8-450x598.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="598" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: #000080;"><a style="color: #000080;" href="https://twitter.com/mythicalwndfish" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sarah Colvin</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>网友看到莎拉的分享，纷纷留言：“这个小隐形人笑死我了！”“可笑得不可理喻。”</p>
<p class="mol-para-with-font">还有网友说：“我家的波士顿<ahref="/gb/tag/%E3%B9%B4%E7%8A%AC.md#1">?犬</a>也喜欢这样。只是它的毯子掉下去时，总是等着人给它披上，我说丈夫‘都是你惯的’。”“冬天屋里很干的！我的狗夜里渴得直喘。幸好狗妈喜欢在水里加冰，经常分给它！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_10110057" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY7-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10110057 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/LACY7-1-600x380.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="380" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: #000080;"><a style="color: #000080;" href="https://twitter.com/mythicalwndfish" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sarah Colvin</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
