<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>从护肤品到品牌香水 德国Aldi再次打响价格战</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/GettyImages-81811182-594x400.jpg" />
<h6>2月14日情人节（Valentinstag）这一天，德国连锁超市Aldi要低价出售品牌香水。(Christopher Furlong/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年02月13日讯】<span class="s1">（大纪元</span><span class="s1">记者樊伊德国报导）</span><span class="s1">继推出优惠价的妮维雅护肤品后，德国连锁超市巨头</span><span class="s2">Aldi</span><span class="s1">发布消息表示，将在</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">14</span><span class="s1">日情人节（</span><span class="s2">Valentinstag</span><span class="s1">）这一天低价出售品牌香水。首先进入</span><span class="s2">Aldi</span><span class="s1">超市的是</span><span class="s2">Calvin Klein</span><span class="s1">、</span><span class="s2">Joop</span><span class="s1">和萧邦（</span><span class="s2">Chopard</span><span class="s1">）等高档品牌。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">Aldi</span><span class="s1">推出的品牌香水价格为</span><span class="s2">75</span><span class="s1">毫升</span><span class="s2">29.99</span><span class="s1">欧元、</span><span class="s2">100</span><span class="s1">毫升</span><span class="s2">39.99</span><span class="s1">欧元，一般情况下，同样的香水价格约为</span><span class="s2">50</span><span class="s1">欧元至</span><span class="s2">60</span><span class="s1">欧元。一位香水业界经理对《食品报》（</span><span class="s2">Lebensmitteilzeitung</span><span class="s1">）表示，这种价格很令人心痛，它会肆无忌惮地破坏香水产品的市场。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在</span><span class="s2">Aldi</span><span class="s1">将要低价出售品牌香水的消息传开后，一些网上香水卖家和实体香水店都纷纷降低香水的价格，使得香水商家们几乎没有什么利润可赚了。</span></p>
<h2 class="p1"><span class="s1">日用品连锁店</span><span class="s2">dm“</span><span class="s1">跟风”?</span><span class="s1">出售优惠价品牌香水</span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">日用品连锁店</span><span class="s2">dm</span><span class="s1">也做出了反应，它将扩充自己的中、低档品牌香水的产品品种，同时还将以</span><span class="s2">59.95</span><span class="s1">欧元的低价出售像阿玛尼（</span><span class="s2">Giorgio Armani</span><span class="s1">）、高缇耶（</span><span class="s2">Jean Paul Gaultier</span><span class="s1">）和大卫杜夫（</span><span class="s2">Davidoff</span><span class="s1">）等高端品牌的香水。也就是说，</span><span class="s2">Aldi</span><span class="s1">此次的特价活动是暂时的，但</span><span class="s2">dm</span><span class="s1">计划持续提供廉价的品牌香水。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">2018</span><span class="s1">年才过去一个多月，这</span><span class="s1">已经是Aldi第二次低价推出品牌化妆品了。今年1月中旬，</span><span class="s2">Aldi</span><span class="s1">超市将</span><span class="s2">18</span><span class="s1">款妮维雅（</span><span class="s2">Nivea</span><span class="s1">）化妆品全面减价</span><span class="s2">25%</span><span class="s1">出售，前提是顾客必须消费</span><span class="s2">9</span><span class="s1">欧元以上才能享受此优惠价格。当时专家就预测，这可能只是</span><span class="s2">Aldi</span><span class="s1">进军名牌化妆品市场的一个开始。#</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">责任编辑：穆华</span></p>
