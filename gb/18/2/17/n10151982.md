<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>开春抢房 首购族勿失良机</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年02月17日讯】整理：李欧<br />
<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B9%B0%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区买房</a>竞争激烈，尤其即将进入开春的抢房战，有意购房者千万不可轻忽。本周精选几处房价宜人的小型房产，开价在60万至160万之间，适合首购族或小家庭，或有意投资者参考。（资料来源：MLS）</p>
<h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E8%B5%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区赏房</a>一、2584 Knightsbridge Ln<br />
Santa Clara, CA 95051</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10152013" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/01.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152013" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/01-600x282.png" alt="" width="600" b="282" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">湾区地产</a>：2584 Knightsbridge Ln., Santa Clara, CA 95051。（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10152017" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/01_2584-KNIGHTSBRIDGE-LN.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152017" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/01_2584-KNIGHTSBRIDGE-LN-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">1图：附有两车库的Condo。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10152019" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/02_2584-KNIGHTSBRIDGE-LN1.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152019" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/02_2584-KNIGHTSBRIDGE-LN1-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2图：挑高的客厅天花板感觉宽广。</figcaption></figure>
<p>这处Condo位在街道的底端，享有宁静的氛围。室内空间宽敞，光线充足，而且经过大幅度的改造，挑高的客厅天花板、硬木地板、双层玻璃窗、壁炉和新漆，厨房换上白色台面与橱柜，一切显得精致迷人。<br />
楼上有两间房，主卧室宽敞附有完整卫浴，次卧也有独立厕所。Condo附有容纳2车的车库、私人小庭院和阳台。这里交通便利，靠近公园、图书馆和圣托马斯阿基诺步道，距离El Camino Real和101高速很近，购物、上学都很方便。</p>
<h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E8%B5%8F%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区赏房</a>二、522 Davenport Ct<br />
Sunnyvale, CA 94087</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10152033" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/02-1.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152033" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/02-1-600x295.png" alt="" width="600" b="295" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">湾区地产</a>：522 Davenport Ct., Sunnyvale, CA 94087。（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/02.png"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-10152014" alt="" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_10152025" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/03_522-DAVENPORT-CT2.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/03_522-DAVENPORT-CT2-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">3图：后院能与厨房或房间互通。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10152026" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/04_522-DAVENPORT-CT4.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/04_522-DAVENPORT-CT4-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">4图：客厅、餐厅、厨房贯通，屋顶高耸。</figcaption></figure>
<p>这一处Sunnyvale独立屋享有Cupertino的好学校，因此价格较高。房屋经过升级，除了少见的深蓝色外观，车库也增加电动车充电设备、全新的前院草坪。这户独立屋有不同一般平面布局，客厅、餐厅、厨房贯通，屋顶是三角尖顶，使空间不压迫，而且有天窗可通风、采光。<br />
后院与屋檐下户外座椅区相连，不管在厨房工作或用餐，都能透过玻璃清楚看到户外。所有房间安排在进门后的左手边，空间不大但精致舒适，而且家庭室被安置在中间，家人相聚更容易。</p>
<h2>湾区Open House三、1090 Main St 307<br />
Redwood City, CA 94063</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10152015" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/03.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/03-600x307.png" alt="" width="600" b="307" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E4%B9%B0%E6%88%BF.md#1">湾区买房</a>：1090 Main St., #307, Redwood City, CA 94063。（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10152035" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/05_1090-MAIN-ST-307-1.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152035" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/05_1090-MAIN-ST-307-1-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">5图：雅致的客厅。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10152036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/06_1090-MAIN-ST-307-2.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/06_1090-MAIN-ST-307-2-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">6图：温馨的小餐桌和厨房。</figcaption></figure>
<p>此Condo坐落在红木城的市中心，1卧1浴是单身贵族首购最佳房产。享有市中心的便利，餐厅、车站就在附近。热闹的商店都在几条街附近，不用担心开车去购物的问题，邻近也有好几间中餐馆。<br />
硬木地板的客厅，与磁砖地板的小厨房和餐厅相连，还有一个精致的吧台，方便单身或两人生活。卧室有附有步入式衣橱，卫浴分成淋浴间和化妆间，使生活更人性化。该住宅社区配有车库、电梯、洗衣间。</p>
<h2>湾区Open House四、Satuma Dr<br />
Redwood Shores, CA 94065</h2>
<figure id="attachment_10152016" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/04.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152016" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/04-600x289.png" alt="" width="600" b="289" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湾区买房：Satuma Dr., Redwood Shores, CA 94065。（MLS）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10152038" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/07_SATUMA-DR1.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/07_SATUMA-DR1-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">7图：社区户外空间宽广。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_10152039" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/08_SATUMA-DR2.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10152039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/02/08_SATUMA-DR2-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">8图：家庭房、厨房享受充分阳光。</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个豪华和现代化的联排别墅2010年完成，位于红木海岸最理想的地区之一。社区毗邻风景优美的保护区，户外就有大草地、社区公园，不远处就是海岸泻湖，周遭有水域相连，非常适合户外活动。<br />
房屋一楼分成底部的入口客厅，上层的家庭房、厨房与餐厅，二楼有3间卧室与洗衣区。主卧享有大浴室和大型步入式衣橱。室内更有2分区空调和供暖系统。这是个现代化迷人的社区，不可错过！</p>
