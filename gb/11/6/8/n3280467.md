<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：烈日的锤炼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/06/1011070745211758-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（摄影：龙芳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>夏天的烈日是一支支无形的铁锤<br />从稻子结穗开始<br />它们便无时无刻都在给稻子锤炼了<br />它们使出其所有的力量给锤炼着<br />把一颗颗稻谷锤炼得坚实<br />锤炼得越来越有重量<br />锤炼得谦虚弯腰有礼<br />锤炼得一颗颗金黄而贵重<br />最后它们还不忘在晒谷场上给锤炼<br />并且不忘给锤炼出清香来<br />成为色香味俱全而有用</p>
<p>你羡慕夏天的烈日给稻谷锤炼吗？<br />你愿意冒着汗给夏天的烈日锤炼吗？<br />你愿意被夏天的烈日锤炼得<br />和稻谷一样色香味俱全而有用吗？</p>
<p><b>Tempered under the Burning Sun<br />　　　　Hsu ChiCheng</b></p>
<p>The burning summer sun is an invisible hammer<br />From the rice have grown out heads<br />It begins to do its upmost<br />To temper them all the time<br />It tempers the rice stronger and stronger<br />And their heads turn heavier and heavier<br />Golden and valuable as gold<br />Elegant and refined in manner<br />Even on the threshing floor<br />The sun still tempers them<br />Into fragrant and delicious</p>
<p>Do you envy the rice tempered by the summer sun?<br />Are you ready to be tempered by the summer sun?<br />Do you like to be tempered as golden and fragrant as rice<br />By the summer sun?@</p>
