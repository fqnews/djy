<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：拯救屈原</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/06/1008091035561973-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（摄影：戴兵 / 大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>鼓声咚咚，鼓声咚咚<br />呐喊声声，呐喊声声<br />龙船向前，向前再向前<br />大家戮力划着<br />我们不是在玩游戏<br />是为了拯救屈原<br />是为了拯救诗</p>
<p>今天是端午<br />不要只知道吃粽子<br />却不知道屈原受尽委屈<br />抱持耿耿忠心，僻处一隅<br />竟至投江而死<br />更不要不知道时至今日<br />诗也受尽委屈<br />划龙船多有诗意都于事无补<br />屈原已成旷古冤魂<br />诗也已经只有小众参与<br />更要我们戮力拯救<br />不能让它也与屈原同沉江底</p>
<p>咿呀嘿，咿呀嘿<br />我们戮力向前划<br />不怕千辛万苦<br />我们要拯救屈原<br />也要不停地拯救诗<br />让忠义诗花随时随处绽放<br />千秋万世，香火永续传承……</p>
<p><b>Saving Qu Yuan　Hsu chiCheng</b></p>
<p>Gongs and drums<br />Whooping up<br />Dragon boats are marching forward<br />We all try our best to paddle<br />We are not playing<br />But saving Qu Yuan<br />And saving poetry</p>
<p>Today is the Dragon Boat Festival<br />Some ones only like to eat rice dumpling<br />But don’t know today is the date of Qu Yuan’s black Friday<br />On which Qu Yuan drowned himself into a river with stone<br />While in exile from a corrupt court<br />Some ones do not know<br />That poetry today sustains grievance<br />It is no use dragon boat racing<br />Qu Yuan has been an unjust-treated soul for thousands of years<br />And poetry has got into a narrow lane<br />Therefore, we must do our best, with one mind in joint<br />To save Qu Yuan and poetry</p>
<p>Oh alas! Oh alas!<br />Paddle! Paddle!<br />No fear of toils. No fear of sweating<br />We must save Qu Yuan<br />We must save poetry<br />To let the flowers of poetry appear everywhere<br />From generation to generation…@</p>
