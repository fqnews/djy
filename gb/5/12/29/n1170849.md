<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>素食新煮意 什锦豆腐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Unknown-11-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>原料：豆腐 豌豆 玉米粒 香菇 木耳 胡萝卜 酱油 盐 糖 鸡精 淀粉<br />做法：豆腐切片。锅中放水，大火烧开，将豆腐和(香菇豌豆玉米胡萝卜)焯一下。<br /><center><br />
<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/12/512290223381469.jpg" alt="" title="" class="size-large wp-image-7037204" /></a></center><br />倒入油，烧至6成热，将豆腐片下锅煎至两面金黄，盛出控油备用<br /><center><br />
<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/12/512290223411469.jpg" alt="" title="" class="size-large wp-image-7037197" /></a></center><br />起锅，放葱，炒出香味后，将(香菇豌豆玉米胡萝卜)和木耳入锅煸炒一下，然后再放入豆腐片。<br /><center><br />
<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/12/512290223441469.jpg" alt="" title="" class="size-large wp-image-7037194" /></a></center><br />随后倒入泡香菇的汤、一点点老抽、少许糖和少许盐，继续翻炒一分钟。最后，用水淀粉勾芡，散点鸡精即可。<br /> <center><br />
<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2005/12/512290223461469.jpg" alt="" title="" class="size-large wp-image-7037188" /></a></center><br />(来源：搜狐)<font color=#ffffff>(http://www.dajiyuan.com)</font></p>
