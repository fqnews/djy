  <p>本则英语影音新闻及出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻 <ahref= > http://english.ntdtv.com/?c=145&#038;a=6156</a>（可线上观看）</p>
  <p>Anchor: NTD’s first International Chinese Culinary Competition <b>wrapped up</b> last week. And it’s still being praised by members of the culinary profession. NTD caught up with the owner of one of Hong Kong’s most <b>prestigious </b> restaurants to hear what he had to say.<br />新闻主播：新唐人电视台首届厨艺大赛上周结束，参赛的厨艺专家成员们至今仍赞不绝口。新唐人电视台赶上了香港最富盛名的餐厅老板，来听听他怎么说。</p>
  <p>STORY:The Hsu Rongxuan restaurant is a well-known <b>seafood</b>establishment in Hong Kong. Its head <b>chef</b> , Oyang Zhinhui, has more than 30 years experience in the business. He believes NTD’s International Chinese Culinary Competition is a good opportunity to share the secrets of the <b>trade</b> . <br />新闻内容：著名的海鲜餐厅叙榕轩建于香港，主厨欧阳敬辉在这个行业已经超过三十年，他认为新唐人电视台的厨艺大赛是同业分享秘密的好机会。</p>
  <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b> </p>
  <p>1.<b>Culinary Competition</b> : n. ph.厨艺大赛</p>
  <p>2.<b>wrapped up</b> : ph.结束</p>
  <p>3.<b>prestigious</b> : adj.有名望的</p>
  <p>4.<b>seafood</b> : n.海鲜</p>
  <p>5.<b>chef</b> : n.主厨</p>
  <p>6. <b> trade</b> : n.同业</p>
  <p>[Oyang Zhinhui, Hsu Rongxuan Restaurant]:“By looking to others, we can <b>strive</b>for perfection. It is good to learn from others and to <b>improve</b> . I want to give it a try for the next competition.” Zhinhui says cooking should be done from the heart. <br />叙榕轩餐厅欧阳敬辉说：“藉由观摩别人，我们会努力达到完美，向别人学习并改进是很好的事，下次比赛我还要试试。”敬辉说烹调应该要用心完成。</p>
  <p>[Oyang Zhinhui, Hsu Rongxuan Restaurant]: “I am glad if people enjoy the food I cooked. I like to see people eating&#8211;personally I am not a person who really likes to eat&#8211;but I get great enjoyment out of seeing my customers finish a dish I have made.”<br />叙榕轩餐厅欧阳敬辉说：“如果人们喜欢我煮的食物，我就很高兴，我喜欢看着别人吃，我个人不喜欢吃东西，但是我非常喜欢看我的客人把我做的菜吃光光。”</p>
  <p>Zhinhui says that Cantonese <b>cuisine</b>emphasizes traditional flavors and sauces. He prepared a seafood feast for us, including a delicious cheese lobster&#8211;rich in flavor but not <b>greasy</b> . This dish is the hundred-flowered <b>grilled</b>seafood, made by cooking fresh shrimp that is soaked with fresh crab. <br />敬辉说广东菜强调传统的口味和酱料，他准备了一道海鲜，包括美味的起司龙虾，美味但不油腻，这道菜是百花烤海鲜，由新鲜的虾子浸泡新鲜螃蟹烹煮。</p>
  <p>The executive manager of the Hsu Rongxuan restaurant, He Yaoji, was also excited to hear about the competition. “The competition <b>exalts</b>Chinese cuisine and Chinese culinary art”, he said. <br />叙榕轩餐厅的执行经理何耀吉听到这项比赛也很兴奋，他说：“这个比赛提升了中国烹饪及中国厨艺。”叙榕轩餐厅的经理何耀吉说。</p>
  <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b> </p>
  <p>7. <b> strive</b> : v.努力</p>
  <p>8. <b> improve</b> : v.改进</p>
  <p>9.<b>cuisine</b> : n.料理</p>
  <p>10.<b>greasy</b> : adj.油腻的</p>
  <p>11.<b>grill</b> : v.烤</p>
