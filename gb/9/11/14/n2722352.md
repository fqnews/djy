  <p><embed src="http://www.youmaker.com/v.swf" width="450" b="358" bgcolor="#FFFFFF" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" allowfullscreen="true"flashvars="file=http://www.youmaker.com/video/v%3Fid%3Deb478c9973b44d8097739c1dc1970e56001%26nu%3Dnu&#038;showdigits=true&#038;overstretch=fit&#038;autostart=false&#038;rotatetime=12&#038;linkfromdisplay=false&#038;repeat=list&#038;shuffle=false&#038;&#038;showfsbutton=false&#038;fsreturnpage=&#038;fullscreenpage=" /> <br /><--ads--></p>
  <p><b> 【新闻关键字】</b><br />1.<b> jockey</b>: n. 骑师<br />2. <b>pride oneself on sth</b>：ph.以…自豪<br />3. <b>credentials</b>: n. 证书<br />4. <b>fleet (of sth)</b>: n.车队、舰队<br />5.<b> substantial</b>: adj.实质的<br />6. <b>consumption</b>: n.消耗<br />7.<b> emission</b>n.排放<br />8.<b> address</b>: v. 设法解决，处理 </p>
  <p>The <b>Jockey</b> Club Kau Sai Chau Public Golf Course in Hong Kong <b>prides itself on</b> its green <b>credentials</b>. It has the world&#8217;s first and biggest <b>fleet</b> of solar-cell powered golf carts. The SolarDrive golf carts will also lead to <b>substantial</b> savings for the club.<br />香港赛马会窖西洲高尔夫球场以其环保得奖证书而自豪。它有着全世界第一支也是规模最大的高尔夫球车队。这些太阳能高尔夫球车也将为赛马会省下庞大的开支。</p>
  <p>[Cameron Halliday, The Jockey Club Kau Sai Chau]:<br />&#8220;We estimated that we would save 50-70 percent on power <b>consumption</b> of the fleet&#8230; We anticipate that every year we&#8217;ll save HK$400,000 (US$51,610) on the operation of the 205 car fleet. Those are impressive numbers.”</p>
  <p>[克麦隆．哈乐戴，窖西洲赛马会]<br />“我们估计能节省50-70%的车队电力消耗…在205辆球车一年的运作上，将能省下40万港币(合51,610美元)。这是一笔可观的数字。”</p>
  <p>The SolarDrive project is part of a $45 million citywide environment project in response to global warming and climate change. The golf club estimates that the switch to solar power golf carts will reduce carbon <b>emissions</b> by up to 30 tons per year.<br />这项太阳能驱动车计划，是全市因应全球暖化与气候变迁所采行的环保策略中的一部分，其总预算高达4,500万元。高尔夫俱乐部估计，转换成太阳能车队，每年将能减少碳排放量达30吨。</p>
  <p>[David Powers, Golfer]:<br />&#8220;Many more steps like this would be wonderful, <b>address</b> our air pollution problem and make Hong Kong a much greater city than it already is, so it&#8217;s a very good step.&#8221; <br />[大卫．鲍尔，高尔夫球运动员]:<br />“多一点这样子的措施很好！不但能处理我们空气污染的问题，也把香港变成一个比现在还棒的城市，它真的是一个不错的点子！”</p>
  <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />9. <b>convert</b>: v. 转换<br />10.<b>as far as sth is concerned</b>: ph. 就…而言<br />11.<b>viable</b>: adj. 可行的<br />12. <b> perspective</b>: n. 观点<br />13. <b> out-perform</b>: v. (效益上)胜过<br />14.<b>patron</b>: n. 熟客</p>
  <p>The SolarDrive has a solar-cell roof panel and is able to capture and <b>convert</b> daylight into sufficient energy, even on cloudy days. The cart charges while being driven. <br />太阳能驱动车的车顶有一片太阳能板，它能够吸取光能，并将之转换为充足的能量，即使在阴天也没问题。这种车辆在行进时会自动充电。</p>
  <p>[William Yiu, Exec. Dir., Hong Kong Jockey Club]:<br />&#8220;So when the golf carts come back after an 18-hole day the charging at night will still have to happen but it will be a lot less requirement <b>as far as</b> the recharge <b>is concerned</b>. And having less recharge every night, the battery actually runs a lot longer, so from that <b>perspective</b> it&#8217;s actually commercially <b>viable</b>.&#8221;<br />[尤威廉，香港赛马会执行主任]:<br />“当打完一天18洞的行程回来后，这些高尔夫球车在夜间还是得要充电，但充电所需的电量则减少了许多。当夜间的充电量减少之后，电池可以维持更久的寿命，从这个观点看来，它的确具有商业应用上的可行性。”</p>
  <p>The SolarDrive is more efficient and can <b>out-perform</b> battery-powered carts.<br />太阳能驱动车比起一般电池驱动的车辆而言，更有效率，表现也更好。</p>
  <p>The solar powered golf carts are also a hit with <b>patrons</b>.<br />太阳能高尔夫球车也是熟客的最爱。</p>
  <p>[Charles Regan, Golfer]:<br />&#8220;It&#8217;s a great idea. You’ve got plenty of sun and they can easily run four hours very quickly, running through the course. I think many golf courses should adopt the same thing. They&#8217;re fantastic!&#8221;<br />[查理斯．雷根，高尔夫球运动员]：<br />“这是很棒的点子。有充足的阳光，这些车就能轻快的在球场上开上四个小时。我想很多的高尔夫球场都应该采用这样的设备。它们真是棒呆了！”</p>
  <p>Earlier this year, the golf course received the &#8216;Asian Golf Course Environment Award’. <br />今年稍早，这座高尔夫球场才获颁“亚洲高尔夫球场环境奖”。</p>
  <p>The project is collaboration between a Hong Kong university and Denmark-based engineering company SolarDrive.<br />这项计划由香港的一所大学和丹麦一家名为SolarDrive的工程公司所共同合作。<br />@*</p>
