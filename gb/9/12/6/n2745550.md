  <p>节目一开始由员工林淑华女士讲解感恩节的由来拉开序幕，紧接着就是会员与员工的表演。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051520381627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="299" b="448"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602013" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">王奉恺自拉自唱</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>第一个节目是黄正勤先生的京剧演唱: 法门寺，由王奉恺先生拉二胡伴奏。王奉恺也独唱了一曲。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051520351627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="336" b="448"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602014" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">李天恒女士弹琴伴奏</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>第二个节目是中心会员男女声小组合唱，由李天恒女士弹琴伴奏，曲目是: 老年的朋友来相会，夕阳红，反映了他们在中心的经验与气势。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051520571627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602015" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">周慧玉 独唱</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>第三个节目是会员周慧玉女士的独唱: 一朵小花，她的专业素养，美丽音质，唱得大家心花朵朵开。</p>
  <p>第四个节目是严人觉先生的诗词朗诵，他因佩服会员史固华先生的文武涵养，撰写了一首诗以志之，并与众人分享他的仰慕。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051520371627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602016" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">黄正勤先生的快板</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>第五个节目是黄正勤先生的快板，唱说感恩节的感怀。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051521221627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602017" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">刘给平女士独唱</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>接着是会员刘给平女士的独唱: 红湖水，展现她音乐学院耳濡目染的歌唱水平，迎得满堂彩。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051520591627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602018" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">邹秉雄夫妇社交舞表演</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>第七个节目是中心主任邹秉雄夫妇的社交舞表演，他夫妻俩的默契，体现了社交舞二人随从韵律和谐舞动的绚丽。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051521001627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602019" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">刁松变魔术</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>接下来是轰动中心的魔术表演，由员工刁松所演出，他那一付神秘不可说的神情，引起耆老莫名的好奇与期待，最终因与其他员工默契不足而穿帮，逗得耆老呵呵大笑，比完美的演出还逗趣。魔术表演过后，有会员刘槐荫的独唱: 牧歌；</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051521011627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602020" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">刘槐荫的独唱</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051521211627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602021" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">吕思慧女士与员工Christina 合唱</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>义工吕思慧女士与员工Christina 合唱东方之珠；会员马继美女士与员工合唱野地的花；</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051520581627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602022" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">马继美女士与员工合唱</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>员工林淑华女士更率领所有员工大跳Macarena, 还有主任夫人带员工跳Line Dance，双双把庆祝会带到最高潮。然后小山东和会员玩比手划脚。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051521231627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602023" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">员工共舞</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051521241627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602024" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">会员与员工共舞</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051521201627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602025" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">林一霹独唱</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051520391627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602026" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">马继美 吴艳萍 刘金莉 刘给平 刘槐荫 林一霹合唱</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>节目结束前穿插抽奖，奖项有九，寓意“欢乐长长久久”。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/12/912051521281627.jpg" alt="" title="" width="448" b="299"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7602027" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">八宝鸭好吃</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>午餐除了平时餐点外另有八宝鸭，用饭时耆老可随意点唱，被点名的员工因此引吭高歌，无不尽欢。这样的欢乐时光岂不是 “欢乐”人所该有? 大家都正期待着下一个节庆呢。 </p>
