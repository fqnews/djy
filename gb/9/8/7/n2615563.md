  <p><b>A材料:</b></p>
  <p>中筋面粉 600公克（1斤）</p>
  <p>水 300公克</p>
  <p>酵母发粉 12公克</p>
  <p>泡打粉 12公克</p>
  <p>糖50公克</p>
  <p><b>B材料</b></p>
  <p>葱花 少许 (只取绿色部分；葱白不用)</p>
  <p>油 少许</p>
  <p>盐 少许</p>
  <p><b>做法:</b></p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/08/908070009121466-450x338.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="338"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7445400" /></a><img width="14" b="12" border="0" alt="高精度图片" src="//www.epochtimes.com/images/highRes.jpg"/><br /><span class="bn12">(摄影：杨美琴/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>1、将A材料揉成面团，发酵4小时。(发酵后之面团会膨胀至双倍大，因内含空气，用手插入会留下手指痕迹，即表示发酵成功)</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/08/908070012201466-450x338.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="338"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7445401" /></a><img width="14" b="12" border="0" alt="高精度图片" src="//www.epochtimes.com/images/highRes.jpg"/><br /><span class="bn12">(摄影：杨美琴/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>2、将发酵面团擀成长薄片，加入B材料，即刷上油、葱洒上盐。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/08/908070014341466-450x338.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="338"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7445402" /></a><img width="14" b="12" border="0" alt="高精度图片" src="//www.epochtimes.com/images/highRes.jpg"/><br /><span class="bn12">(摄影：杨美琴/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>3、将面团擀成长薄片的面皮对折，再盘绕成花苞形状的花卷。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/08/908070016571466-450x338.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="338"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7445403" /></a><img width="14" b="12" border="0" alt="高精度图片" src="//www.epochtimes.com/images/highRes.jpg"/><br /><span class="bn12">(摄影：杨美琴/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>4、成型后的花卷放在蒸笼或蒸盘上醒15分钟。（这样花卷皮才会膨胀变大：蒸盘底部抹少许油或放油纸或蒸布才不会黏住外皮）</p>
  <p>5、再将蒸笼或蒸盘上的花卷放入已经水滚的蒸锅蒸，用中火隔水蒸10-12分钟即可。蒸好熄火勿马上掀盖，让余温再闷2分钟后再掀开。</p>
  <p><b>另外两款花卷做法：</b></p>
  <p><b>细丝状花卷做法：</b></p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <table border=0 align=center>  <tr valign=top>  <td>  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/08/908070329181466-450x338.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="338"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7445404" /></a><img width="14" b="12" border="0" alt="高精度图片" src="//www.epochtimes.com/images/highRes.jpg"/><br /><span class="bn12">1.将面团擀平抹点油(摄影：杨美琴/大纪元）</span></div>  </td>  <td>  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/08/908070329191466-450x338.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="338"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7445405" /></a><img width="14" b="12" border="0" alt="高精度图片" src="//www.epochtimes.com/images/highRes.jpg"/><br /><span class="bn12">2.洒上加了少许盐的葱花(摄影：杨美琴/大纪元）</span></div>  </td>  </tr>  </table>  <p><!-- --></p>

  <table border=0 align=center>  <tr valign=top>  <td>  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/08/908070329221466-450x338.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="338"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7445408" /></a><img width="14" b="12" border="0" alt="高精度图片" src="//www.epochtimes.com/images/highRes.jpg"/><br /><span class="bn12">5.压平后用面刀切成约0.5cm的细丝数条 (不切断)，按需要大小于8条-10条处切断(摄影：杨美琴/大纪元）</span></div>  </td>  <td>  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2009/08/908070355551466-450x338.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="338"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7445409" /></a><img width="14" b="12" border="0" alt="高精度图片" src="//www.epochtimes.com/images/highRes.jpg"/><br /><span class="bn12">6.将切断的面皮略为拉长后卷成细丝的螺旋造型(摄影：杨美琴/大纪元）</span></div>  </td>  </tr>  </table>  <p><!-- --></p>

  <p>将面团擀平，洒上加了盐的葱花，上下折成三层压平，用面刀(切蛋糕的刀子即可)切成约0.5cm的细丝 (不切断)，按需要大小于8条-10条处切断，略为拉长后卷成细丝的螺旋造型。</p>

  <p><b>螺旋状花卷做法：</b></p>
  <p>也可将面团上下折成三层压平再切成数等份，再将每个等份卷成螺旋造型。</p>
