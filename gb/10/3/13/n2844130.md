<p>The Chinese <B> regime</B> has <B> banned </B>independent reporting on the latest <B> melamine </B>toxic milk <B> scandal</B>.<br />中共政权已禁止媒体独立报导最新三聚氰胺毒奶粉的传闻。</p>
<p>The Guandong Province<B>Propaganda </B>Department issued an order stating, all media are only allowed to use information formally released by authorities.<br />广东省传播部门发布命令表示，所有媒体都只准许使用当局正式公布的信息。</p>
<p>General Secretary of The International Federation of Journalists, Aidan White, says this latest media ban in China “raises further concerns about <B> prioritizing censorship</B> over the wellbeing of citizens.”<br />国际记者联盟秘书长艾登?怀特表示，这项在中国最新的媒体禁令引起了进一步的关切：将新闻审查的优先性置于人民的福址之上。”</p>
<p>8. <B>contaminated</B>：adj. 受污染的<br />9.<B> state-run</B>：adj. 国营的<br />10.<B>kidney</B>：n. 肾脏<br />11. <B> tainted</B>：adj. 腐败的、有毒的<br />12. <B> Province</B>：n. 省份 </p>
<p>In 2008, six children died and more than 300,000 fell ill from consuming melamine-<B> contaminated </B>milk products, according to <B> state-run </B>media.<br />根据国营媒体的报导，在2008年，有6名儿童死亡，另外还有30多万名因食入受三聚氰胺污染的奶类制品而感到不适的病患。</p>
<p>Adding melamine to milk products makes the protein content appear higher. When ingested by humans, it can cause <B> kidney </B>failure and other serious health problems.<br />添加三聚氰胺的奶类制品，可使蛋白质含量变得较高。当被人体摄取后，将会导致肾功能衰竭以及其他严重的健康问题。</p>
<p>In this most recent melamine scandal, <B> tainted</B> products have been produced by companies across China, including Shanghai, Guizhou and Liaoning <B> Province </B>in the northeast.<br />在这最近的三聚氰胺丑闻中，这些受污染的产品是由中国各地的公司所生产，包括上海、贵州以及东北的辽宁省。<br />@*</p>
