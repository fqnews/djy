  <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />1.<b> magnitude</b>：n. 地震强度的级数<br />2.<b>panic </b>：v. 使惊慌失措<br />3. <b> collapse </b>：v. 倒塌、坍塌<br />4.<b>trap</b>：v. 受困、陷入困境<br />5.<b>retaining wall</b>：n. 挡土墙<br />6.<b>shallow</b>：adj. 浅层的<br />7.<b>aftershock</b>：n. 余震<br />8. <b> epicenter</b>：n. 震央 <br />9. <b> jolt</b>：v. 震动、摇动<br />10. <b> tremor </b>：n. 震动<br />11.<b>wake-up call</b>：n. 警示<br />12.<b>tremor-prone</b>：n. 地震好发的地带</p>
  <p>WORD NUMBER： 2328</p>
  <p>The 7.2<b>magnitude</b> earthquake rocked buildings and<b>panicked </b>residents from Tijuana to Los Angeles.<br />从提瓦纳到洛杉矶的7.2级地震，让建筑物都开始晃动，也让当地居民惊慌失措。</p>
  <p>One person died when a house in Mexicali<b>collapsed</b>, according to Alfredo Escobedo, a director of emergency services in Baja California state. He says that others are reportedly<b>trapped</b> in elevators.<b>Retaining walls</b> have collapsed in some places and electricity was out in several parts of the state.<br />根据下加利福尼亚州紧急救灾处的组长Alfredo Escobedo表示，在Mexicali有一幢房子倒塌，造成一人死亡。他说，据报导有些人被困在电梯里。有些地方的挡土墙倒塌，好几个地区停电。 </p>
  <p>The relatively<b>shallow </b>quake was centered in a lightly populated area in northeastern Baja California, knocking out power in parts of the city and cutting off most telecommunications.<br /> 这次相对浅层的地震中心，是在下加利福尼亚州东北部一个人烟稀少的地区，部分城市地区电力中断，大部分通信被切断。 </p>
  <p>A series of<b>aftershock</b>s rocked the area around the<b>epicenter</b>, 30 miles (50 km) to the southeast of Mexicali, and close to the town of Guadalupe Victoria.<br />一连串的余震发生在震央附近的地区，距Mexicali东南方30英里（50公里）的地方，且离Guadalupe Victoria这城镇很近。 </p>
  <p>In Tijuana, about 135 miles (200 km) away from the epicenter, the quake visibly<b>jolted </b>cars in a parking lot and shook computers. A highway between Tijuana and Mexicali was badly damaged.<br />在提瓦纳，离震央约 135英里（200公里）处，剧烈的地震让那些停在停车场的车摇个不停，也使得桌上的电脑不停的摇晃。提瓦纳和Mexicali之间的高速公路也受到严重损坏。 </p>
  <p>[Tijuana Earthquake Witness]: <br />&#8220;It was terrible; terrible; terrible. But it&#8217;s ok, everything is calm.&#8221;<br />[提瓦纳地震的目击者]： <br />“这真的是太可怕，太可怕，太可怕了！但还好，现在一切都平静下来了。” </p>
  <p>Another local resident said that his entire home shook due to the <b> tremors</b>.<br />另一位当地居民说，他整个家因为地震剧烈的振动，都在不停地摇晃着。 </p>
  <p>[Tijuana Earthquake Witness]: <br />&#8220;I was at my house and I felt it strongly. Everything shook. It was a serious<b>wake-up call</b>.&#8221; <br />[提瓦纳地震的目击者]： <br />“当时我正在家中，我感觉地震非常强烈。一切都在晃动，这是一个很严重的警示。” </p>
  <p>Local civil defense officials inspected apartment buildings and ordered residents away.<br />当地的民防官员视察所有的公寓大厦，并下令居民离开。 </p>
  <p>[Marta Arce, Tijuana Earthquake Witness]: <br />&#8220;They told us to walk away and out of the buildings. We have a big building and it shook at least three times, and then once very slowly.&#8221;<br />[Marta Arce，提瓦纳地震的目击者]： <br />“他们告诉我们离开这栋大楼。这是一幢很大的大楼，它摇晃了至少三次，然后还有一次持续非常久。” </p>
  <p>Devastating earthquakes in Haiti and Chile this year have left many people nervous across<b>tremor-prone</b> Latin America.<br />今年在海地和智利发生了毁灭性的地震，已让位于容易发生地震的拉丁美洲居民很紧张。 <br />@*</p>
