<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1. <B>Pop-up Book </B>:ph. 有立体图片的动画<br />2. <B>animate </B>:vt. 栩栩如生的动作<br />3.<B> feast </B>: n. 盛宴<br />4.<B> goggles </B>:n. 护目镜<br />5. <B>two-dimensional </B>: a.2D<br />6.<B> confinement </B>: n. 限制<br />7. <B>illustrations </B>:n. 插图<br />8.<B> embed </B>: vt. 镶嵌、嵌入<br />9. <B>cue </B>:n. 信号<br />10. <B>absorb </B>: v. 吸收<br />11. <B>diversification</B>:n.多样化</p>
<p>South Korean Researchers Create Pop-up Books in 3D<br />南韩研究学者创作了3D立体书。</p>
<p><B>Pop-up books </B>have been around in various forms for hundreds of years, but the version created by South Korea’s Gwangju Institute is a computer-<B>animated feast </B>for the eyes.<br />立体书已经以不同的形式呈现数百年，但南韩的光州研究院所创作的版本是”电脑动画的” 视觉飨宴。</p>
<p>With the aid of specialized <B>goggles </B>attached to a computer, the actors can break out of their <B>two-dimensional confinement </B>to do battle in 3-D.<br />在连接电脑的护目镜的协助下，演员们能够打破2D的限制，挑战3D。</p>
<p>The <B>illustrations</B> in the book are <B>embedded</B> with <B>cues </B>that communicate with a sensor in the goggles that bring the characters to life.<br />书中的插图嵌入提示，可与护目镜的感应器沟通，就能带给书中角色以生命。</p>
<p>The Institute’s Sang-Cheol is the project leader.<br />领导这项研究计划是金尚哲。</p>
<p>[Kim Sang-Cheol, Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology]:<br />“We applied paper book’s sensorial aspect that they are touchable. And then we <B>absorbed </B>strengths that digital books have, such as <B>diversification </B>of content, and easy access to animated pictures by just connecting to the computer.”<br />光州科技研究院金尚哲说：“我们应用纸本书可接触的外观，然后吸收数位书籍的长处，例如满足多样化，藉由只要连接电脑就能很容易地进入动画图片的世界中。”</p>
<p>The team has animated two Korean children&#8217;s books but says the technology could also be applied in other ways to display images on smart phones for example, or enhance museum exhibits.<br />研究团队已将两本韩国童书，以动画方式呈现，但他们表示这项技术也能够运用在其他方面，例如在智慧型手机上展现影像，或提高博物馆的展示吸引力。</p>
<p>Kim says he hopes it won&#8217;t be too long before the books become commercially available, so readers of all ages can fully appreciate stories that really do leap of the page.<br />金尚哲说，他希望不会等太久（3D）书籍就能用在商业上，这样任何年龄层的读者都可以欣赏到真的从书中“跃出纸上”的故事。<br />@*</p>
