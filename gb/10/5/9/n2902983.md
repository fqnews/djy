<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻学英语：美国人在台南观赏神韵演出</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>Americans See Shen Yun in Tainan, Taiwan<br />美国人在台南观赏神韵演出<br /><embed src="http://www.youmaker.com/v.swf" width="450" b="358" bgcolor="#FFFFFF" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer" allowFullScreen="true"flashvars="file=http://www.youmaker.com/video/v%3Fid%3D1afdc260babe42688efb431cb5b89b9a001%26nu%3Dnu&#038;showdigits=true&#038;overstretch=fit&#038;autostart=false&#038;rotatetime=12&#038;linkfromdisplay=false&#038;repeat=list&#038;shuffle=false&#038;&#038;showfsbutton=false&#038;fsreturnpage=&#038;fullscreenpage=" /> </p>
<p>本新闻影音出处：新唐人电视台英语新闻:<br />http://english.ntdtv.com/ntdtv_en/ns_arts/2010-04-06/253188852010.md#1</p>
<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.<B>performance</B>：(n.)表演<br />2.<B>gorgeous</B>：(adj.)华丽的<br />3. <B> tenor</B>：(n.)男高音<br />4. <B> baritones</B>：(n.)男中音<br />5.<B>soprano</B>：(n.)女高音<br />6.<B>incredible</B>：(adj.)极妙的<br />7.<B>amazing</B>：(adj.)惊人的<br />8. <B> depiction</B>：(n.)描述<br />9. <B> persecution</B>：(n.)迫害<br />10. <B>religious</B>：(n.)信仰</p>
<p>Two Americans were in the audience for Shen Yun’s recent run in Tainan, Taiwan<br />神韵艺术团最近在台南举行演出，观众当中有二名美国人。</p>
<p>Rod Spindler and his colleague came to Taiwan on business, and after hearing about Shen Yun, they decided to buy tickets.<br />罗德．史宾德勒和他的同事到台湾来洽公，在获悉神韵艺术团将演出后，他们决定去买票。</p>
<p>[Rod Spindler, Audience Member]:<br />“It was just beautiful. The colors brought out all the<B> performances</B>. I really enjoyed it.”<br />罗德．史宾德勒，观众：<br />“表演实在很美，各种色彩带动了所有的演出，我真的很喜欢。”</p>
<p>[Rod Spindler, Audience Member]:<br />“This is just unique, it’s different. The backgrounds are <B>gorgeous</B>. The fact that the backgrounds could move in (sync) with the live players was really cool. I thought that was nice. The music was fantastic. The singers, the<B> tenors</B>, the <B>baritones</B>, <B>soprano</B> were great. Lots of power in their voices.”<br />罗德．史宾德勒，观众：<br />“它真的非常独特而且与众不同。背景极其华丽，而且能够配合现场的演出者同时变动，实在酷毙了！我认为这很棒。音乐美极了，歌手们，包括男高音、男中音和女高音都唱得超好，他们的声音充满了力度。”</p>
<p>Audience member Gary Stone says the show was<B> incredible</B>.<br />观众之一的盖瑞史东说这个演出实在棒极了。</p>
<p>[Gary Stone, Audience Member]:<br />“Yes I am very lucky. I am very lucky to be able to see a performance like this in Taiwan. The dancers are <B>amazing</B>; the whole show was just incredible. I have never seen this kind of dance before, ever. The Chinese traditional dance. So it was very, very pleasing to be able to see that and experience it.”<br />一位观众盖瑞史东说：“是啊！我实在太幸运了！我何其有幸能在台湾观赏到像这样的一场演出，舞者们令人惊艳，整场表演真的棒极了。我以前从来没有观赏过这一类中国传统舞蹈的表演，这次能够欣赏到并且体验到，实在是非常非常的开心！”</p>
<p>Shen Yun’s <B>depiction </B>of the <B>persecution</B> of Falun Gong practitioners in China made an impression on Mr. Spindler.<br />神韵艺术团对于法轮功学员在中国被迫害的描述，让史宾德勒先生印象深刻。</p>
<p>[Rod Spindler, Audience Member]:<br />“We see many religious people around the world murdered for their beliefs and it would be nice if that would just stop.”<br />观众罗德．史宾德勒说：“我们见到世界各地有许多宗教人士，因为信仰而被杀害，如果这类事情可以停止的话，该有多好啊！”</p>
<p>Shen Yun’s next Taiwan performances are in Taichung City and Yunlin County. .<br />神韵艺术团的下几场演出，将会在台中市与云林县。<br />@* </p>
