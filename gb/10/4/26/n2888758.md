  <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />1. <b> crops</b>: n. 农作物<br />2.<b>inaccessible</b>: adj.难取得的<br />3.<b>grapple </b>:vi.搏斗<br />4.<b>devastating</b>:adj.重创的<br />5. <b> struggle</b>: vi.挣扎<br />6.<b>cope with</b>:ph.对付</p>
  <p><b>Crops</b> are failing and clean drinking water is<b> inaccessible</b> to more than 16 million people as southwest China <b>grapples </b>with a <b>devastating </b>drought.<br />农作物无法收成，超过1,600万人民无法得到干净的饮用水，中国西南正与重创性的旱灾搏斗着。</p>
  <p>More than 50 million people in southwest China are<b> struggling</b> to <b>cope with</b> what is being called the worst drought in living memory.<br />在中国西南方，超过五千万人正奋战应付着被称为记忆中最严重的旱灾。</p>
  <p>Here in the village of, local people are awaiting their only source of drinking water.<br />在这个村里，当地居民正在等待他们唯一的饮用水来源。</p>
  <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />7.<b>cultivate</b>: vt. 耕种,耕作<br />8.<b>plot </b>:n.小块土地<br />9. <b> millet </b>: n. 小米<br />10. <b> shoot</b>:n. 幼芽<br />11.<b>supplement </b>:n.补充<br />12.<b>manual </b>: a.手工的</p>
  <p>The trucks come every other day or so, bringing only enough to supply drinking water to residents, but not enough for farmers to<b> cultivate</b> their fields.<br />运水车大约每隔一天来一次，带来仅够供给居民的饮用水，但对于农民农作物的耕作还是不够的。</p>
  <p>73 year-old farmer Zhang Zhenlong has given up on his<b> plot </b>of land, which used to produce good crops of<b> millet</b>, beans and corn.<br />73岁的农夫张清龙已经放弃他的那小块用来生产小米、豆类及玉米的土地。</p>
  <p>[Zhang Zhenlong, Farmer]:<br />&#8220;The last time it rained properly was last May. The crops we planted before May all failed. The crops after May produced a little. This year we don&#8217;t know what to do, it&#8217;s so dry. We have planted, but there are no <b>shoots</b>. Any shoots that come up just die.&#8221;<br /> 农夫张清龙说：<br />“上次下雨大约是在去年五月。五月以前所种植的农作物都枯死了。五月过后生产的农作物也只有一点点收成。今年我们真的不知道该怎么办，太干燥了！我们有播种，但是没有发芽。所有的幼苗刚长出来就都枯死了。”</p>
  <p>Villagers in Pubin are faring no better.<br />浦滨村的状况也没有比较好。</p>
  <p>With the year&#8217;s first crops failed and no rain in sight, farmers like Wang Fu have been left on their own to try their luck at the spring and summer crops. <br />随着今年第一季的农作物无法收成，又不见有降雨的迹象，和王富一样农夫只能靠运气期待春季及夏季的农作物收成。</p>
  <p>The drought has forced young people to leave the village to <b>supplement</b> their income.<br />干旱迫使年轻人必须离开家乡去增补收入。</p>
  <p>[Wangfu, Farmer]: <br />&#8220;Because the drought has been so serious, young people have all gone to find <b>manual</b> work. There&#8217;s nothing to do in the village, so they&#8217;ve gone to make a living. Only the old people are left behind.&#8221;<br />农夫王富说：“因为干旱很严重，年轻人全都离开去找靠手工的工作。村里没有事情可以做，所以他们出去求生。只有老人家留了下来。”</p>
  <p>Some areas have received 90 percent less rainfall than usual for this time of year, and weather forecasters<b> predict</b> that no <b>significant</b> rainfall is expected for at least another 10 days.<br />有些地区比往常少了90%的降雨量，天气预报员预测未来的10天里，也没有降雨迹象。<br />@*</p>
