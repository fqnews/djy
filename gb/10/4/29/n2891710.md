  <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />1.	<b>Ethical </b>:a. 道德的<br />2.	<b>fur </b>: n. (兽类的)软毛<br />3.	<b>obtain</b>:vt. 得到,获得<br />4.	<b>cruel </b>:a. 残忍的,残酷的<br />5.	<b>garment</b>:n. 服装,衣着<br />6.	<b>mushroom </b>: n. 【古】暴发户<br />7.	<b>bulk up</b>:vi扩大,膨胀<br />8.	<b>gradually </b>: ad. 逐步地,渐渐地<br />The animal rights group People for the <b>Ethical </b>Treatment of Animals – PETA – launched its new campaign &#8220;Be an angel, don&#8217;t wear<b> fur</b>.&#8221; <br />善待动物组织-PETA发起一项新活动：“做个天使，莫穿皮草”。</p>
  <p>Their star attraction is Chinese actress Sun Li who rose to fame through popular television dramas. <br />这项活动的明星代言人，是颇受欢迎的电视剧中国女艺人孙俪。</p>
  <p>Li posed in wings with a white rabbit in order to encourage China&#8217;s one-billion-strong population not to wear fur.<br /> 孙俪装扮成带着翅膀的天使，怀抱一只可爱的小兔子，为了鼓励十亿多的中国人不要穿动物毛皮。</p>
  <p>[Sun Li, Actress]:<br />&#8220;Today, the methods used to<b> obtain</b> furs are really<b> cruel</b>. But because people want these precious furs they will sometimes skin them alive and that is an even crueler method. Even when the animals don&#8217;t have skin, they can still feel and move. There really aren&#8217;t any ethical ways to do it. So I hope that everyone, especially in the wintertime, when they feel warmth, will let other things feel warmth, and not wear fur.&#8221;<br /> 女艺人，孙俪说：““今天，用来获得动物毛皮的方法非常残忍。但是人们为了想要这些珍贵的动物毛皮，他们将动物活生生剥皮，那是更残忍的方式。甚至当那些动物没有了毛皮，他们还是有感觉，还是会动。这样对待它们那真的是很不道德的方式。所以我希望每个人，尤其是在冬天的时候。当他们感到温暖时，让其他的事物也感到温暖，不要穿动物的毛皮。”</p>
  <p>PETA says its new anti-fur campaign results from a recent investigation into the conditions of Chinese fur farms.<br />善待动物组织说，这项新的反毛皮运动，源自最近一项对中国毛皮牧场的调查。</p>
  <p>They say they discovered &#8216;awful conditions,’ with animals often being skinned alive. <br /> 他们说他们发现“情况糟透了”，动物常常活生生地被剥皮。</p>
  <p>China is a major exporter of fur <b>garments</b> and the number of fur farms has <b>mushroomed</b> in the past ten years, especially in the cold northern region of Heilongjiang.<br />中国是毛皮大衣的主要出口国，皮草牧场的数目在过去十年中暴增，特别是在北方寒冷的黑龙江地区。</p>
  <p>According to local industry experts, the level of fur production in northeastern China has been increasing steadily by 10 percent per year since 2004.<br />根据当地的产业专家说，自2004年以来，中国北方动物毛皮生产量，每年稳定地以10%增加。</p>
  <p>Many of the buyers come from neighboring Russia, where many people<b> bulk up </b>against the bitter winter by wearing fur.<br />许多的买家来自于邻近的俄罗斯，那里有越来越多的人靠着穿动物毛皮度过寒冬。</p>
  <p>[Sun Li, Actress]:<br />&#8220;Because I&#8217;m Chinese, I hope to use my language and my feelings to tell my country&#8217;s people that perhaps they can use other methods to beautify themselves and keep themselves warm and not use fur.&#8221;<br />女艺人孙俪说：“因为我是中国人，我希望用我的语言和我的感受告诉我国家的人民，也许他们可以用其他的方法让自己更美丽且保暖，而不要使用动物的毛皮。”</p>
  <p>According to a China Leather and Fur Association forum in November 2007, a growing number of international fur traders, processors and fashion designers have <b>gradually</b> shifted their business to China<br />根据中国皮革及动物毛皮协会，于2007年11月的一项论坛上，愈来愈多的国际动物毛皮贸易商、制造商及时尚设计师，逐渐地将他们的事业转移到中国来。<br />@*</p>
