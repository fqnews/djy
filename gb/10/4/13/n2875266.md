  <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />1.<b>controversial</b>: adj. 受争议的<br />2.<b>municipality</b>: n. 自治市<br />3.<b>reservoir </b>:n. 蓄水库<br />4.<b>seismic</b>: adj. 地震的;因地震引起的<br />5. <b> compensation </b>:n. 补偿，弥补;赔偿<br />6. <b> hydroelectric</b>: adj. 水力发电的</p>
  <p>In China, the <b>controversial</b> Three Gorges Dam project continues to push forward. <br />在中国，备受争议的三峡大坝工程案子持续进行。</p>
  <p>An additional 300,000 residents in the southwestern <b>municipality</b> of Chongqing are being forced to relocate, state-run media reported on Thursday.<br />国营媒体星期四报导，在重庆市西南方又有30万居民被迫重新安置。</p>
  <p>Officials say relocations are to prevent communities from polluting the<b> reservoir</b> and to protect them from potential <b>seismic</b> disasters, according to state-run China Daily.<br />根据国营的中国日报报导，官员表示居民重新安置，是为了预防社区污染水库，以及保护他们免于受到潜在的地震灾害。</p>
  <p>Opponents of the 22.5 billion-dollar dam have long criticized the project because of its huge environmental and social costs.<br />花费225亿元的大坝工程，长期以来因为其巨大的环境及社会成本，饱受反对者的批评。</p>
  <p>Villagers who’ve lost their homes complain they have not been given any <b>compensation</b>.<br />那些失去家园的村民抱怨，他们并没得到任何的补偿。</p>
  <p>Officials have said 1.4 million people have already been forced to relocate. <br />官员表示已经有140万人被迫重新安置。</p>
  <p>The dam is the largest <b>hydroelectric</b> project in the world. It’s more than 7,500 feet wide and almost 280 feet high. <br />三峡大坝是世界最大的水力发电工程，宽度超过7,500 英尺，高度几达280英尺。</p>
  <p>The dam itself is located in Hubei province, but most of the reservoir is in Chongqing.<br />水坝座落于湖北省，但大多的蓄水库位于重庆。</p>
