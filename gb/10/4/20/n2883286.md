  <p> <b>【新闻关键字】</b> <br />1. <b> chubby</b>: a. 圆胖的<br />2.<b> liken </b>: vt. 把…比作<br />3. <b>diva</b>: n. 女主唱<br />4. <b>rendition</b>: n. 诠释<br />5. <b>floor</b>: vt. 使震惊<br />6.<b> pitch-perfect </b>: adj. 完美高音的<br />7. <b>show-stopping</b>: a. 特别受人欢迎的</p>
  <p>He&#8217;s everything Whitney Houston is not—Taiwanese, male, <b>chubby </b>and unknown—but Lin Yu-chun is being<b> likened to</b> the pop <b>diva </b>after wowing a talent show with his <b>rendition </b>of one of her most popular songs. <br />他什么都是，但就不是惠妮休士顿&#8211;他只是一个胖胖的台湾男生，且默默无名，但是在他以诠释惠妮休士顿最受欢迎的歌曲、并且惊艳选秀节目后，林育群被比作流行歌手惠妮休士顿。</p>
  <p>Lin, a competitor on popular singing show &#8220;Super Star Avenue,&#8221; <b>floored</b> the judges this week with his<b> pitch-perfect </b>rendition of Houston&#8217;s &#8220;I Will Always Love You.&#8221;<br />林育群，一个歌唱选秀节目“超级星光大道”的参赛者，这星期，以一首惠妮休士顿的&#8221;I Will Always Love You.&#8221;完美高音的诠释，震惊了所有评审。</p>
  <p>A video clip of his singing on Youtube attracted millions of hits within 48 hours. <br />他唱歌的影像片段在Youtube上，在48小时内吸引了数百万人点阅。</p>
  <p>[Lin Yu-chun, Singer]:<br />&#8220;Unbelievable, and it&#8217;s just like a dream. I was just a normal chubby boy yesterday, and now I become someone all over the world looking at. I really really appreciate all the support from my family, and my friends, especially the TV production team.&#8221;<br />【歌手，林育群】<br />”真不可思议，就跟作梦一样。昨天我还是一个普通的胖男孩，现在我成了全世界所注目的人。我真的、真的很感谢我家人，和我的朋友，尤其是电视制作团队，他们对我的支持。”</p>
  <p>The 24-year-old is also being touted by many bloggers as this year&#8217;s Susan Boyle who became a musical superstar after her <b>show-stopping</b> performance on the &#8220;Britain&#8217;s Got Talent&#8221; TV program.<br />24岁的林育群，就如同今年苏珊大审一样，她在”英国选秀”的电视节目中，特别受欢迎的演出后，就成为音乐界的超级巨星，吸引许多部落迷。</p>
  <p> <b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />1. <b>overnight</b>: adj. 一夜之间<br />2. <b>sensation</b>: n. 引起轰动的人物<br />3.<b> tip</b>: v. 提议<br />4. <b>glamorous </b>:adj. 有魅力的<br />5.<b> conquer </b>:v. 征服<br />6.<b> single</b>: n. 单曲<br />7.<b> album</b>: n. 专辑</p>
  <p>Like Boyle, Lin has become an <b>overnight</b> Internet <b>sensation</b>, with videos of his performance watched by thousands of people. He is also<b> tipped</b> to take home the Taiwan talent show&#8217;s top prize of article content begin --> million.<br />如同苏珊大婶，林育群一夜之间成为网际网路的轰动人物，他的表演影片受到数千人的观看。他同时也被看好，是最有希望能把台湾选秀节目的第一名奖金一百万元带回家。</p>
  <p>[Lin Yu-chun, Singer]:<br />&#8220;Actually, the most important reason was that I saw Paul Potts and Susan Boyle, my look is similar to them in the way that we&#8217;re not <b>glamorous</b> like others, but they <b>conquered </b>everyone with their voices. They gave me a lot of courage, that&#8217;s why I stepped up the stage and gave myself the challenge.&#8221;<br />【歌手，林育群】<br />”事实上，最重要的原因是，我看到保罗及苏珊，我的相貌与他们相似，我们都没有像其他人有迷人的外表，但是他们用歌声征服了每一个人。他们给了我很多的勇气，这就是为什么我能站上舞台，让自己接受挑战。”</p>
  <p>&#8220;I Will Always Love You&#8221; was written by U.S. singer Dolly Parton in 1973, who released it a year later as a <b>single</b>. Houston&#8217;s 1992 version has become one of the best-selling singles of all time.<br />&#8220;I Will Always Love You&#8221;这首歌是由美国歌手Dolly Parton于1973创作，一年后发行成为单曲。惠尼休士顿1992的版本成为有史来最畅销的单曲之一。</p>
  <p>Lin hopes to release his first<b> album </b>by the end of this year. He is also invited to appear on TV shows in the United States, Japan, and Korea.<br />林育群希望他能于今年底发行他的第一张专辑，他同时也被邀约参加在美国、日本及韩国的电视节目演出。<br />@* </p>
