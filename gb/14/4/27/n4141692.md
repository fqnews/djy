  <p>大陆所用的“简化字”，是中共废除汉字、实行文字拼音化的过渡性产物。由1956年1月大陆所公布的《汉字简化方案》正式推出。至1964年5月《简化字总表》出版时，总数已达2236个。</p>
  <p>中共“文字改革委员会”估计，汉字难以在短时间改为拼音文字，中间会有新旧文字的过渡时期，因此在汉字改革上奉行的策略是：破坏汉字形体，削弱汉字效用，以简体字创造汉字内部利于拼音化的条件。（中国语文杂志社编《简化汉字问题》，中华书局，1956年，第98页）</p>
  <p>既然“汉字改革要走世界文字共同的拼音方向”，而奉行的策略又是“以简体字创造汉字内部利于拼音化的条件”，因此，大陆“文改”专家们在对正体汉字进行简化时，可谓不择手段，极尽戕害变乱之能事，无所不用其极。</p>
  <p>以下，是简化字变乱正体汉字的主要手段。</p>
  <p><b>一、剜肉抽筋，空余皮囊（所谓保留原字轮廓）。如：</b> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270818172223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725118" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270818172223.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="67" /></a><b></b></p>
  <p><b>二、随意肢解，致残成伤</b></p>
  <p>1.所谓保留原字的特征部分而删除其它。如： <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270820232223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725121" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270820232223.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="197" /></a></p>
  <p>2.删减原字的一部分再加以变形。如： <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270821552223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725125" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270821552223.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="132" /></a><b></b></p>
  <p><b>三、草鸡硬充金凤凰（草书楷化）。如：</b> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270823072223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725127" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270823072223.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="132" /></a><b></b></p>
  <p><b>四、俗野压雅正，复辟启旧章（采用俗字、罕用异体字或归原古字）。如：</b> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270824202223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725128" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270824202223.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="295" /></a><b></b></p>
  <p><b>五、麻雀配鸳鸯，乱滚屎壳螂（偏旁改造，符号替换）。如：</b> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270825282223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725135" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270825282223.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="328" /></a><b></b></p>
  <p><b>六、珠目相混，鹊巢鸠占（同音替代）。如：</b> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270814402223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725137" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270814402223.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="198" /></a><b></b></p>
  <p><b>七、趋陋就简，李鬼当家（声符以简换繁）。如：</b> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270831392223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725140" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270831392223.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="197" /></a><b></b></p>
  <p><b>八、冒名顶替，不伦不类（加入声符，替换原字的一部分，余下的部分未必是形声字里的形旁）。如：</b> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270835372223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725143" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270835372223.jpg" alt="" width="559" b="36" /></a><b></b></p>
  <p><b>九、僵李代桃，稻稗不辨（借用与部分合并）。</b></p>
  <p>1.借用：用一个音意不同、历史上很少通用的字替代一个通用字。如： <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270839192223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725146" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270839192223-600x405.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a>2.部分合并：将某字部分意义载入到另一个笔划简单的字上，该字其他意义仍然保留的。如： <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270843392223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725156" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270843392223-600x502.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="502" /></a><b>十、弃宗背祖，离经叛道（全面改造，另造新字）。</b> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270846562223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725163" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270846562223-600x185.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="185" /></a><b></b></p>
  <p><b>十一、谬种流传，余毒泛滥（类推简化）。如：</b> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270850012223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725168" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270850012223.jpg" alt="" width="434" b="132" /></a><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270851252223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725170" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404270851252223.jpg" alt="" width="434" b="165" /></a></p>
  <p>既然汉字简化的目的，是为“走世界文字共同的拼音方向”，而简化的策略，又是“破坏汉字形体，削弱汉字效用，以简体字创造汉字内部利于拼音化的条件”，所以“二简字”更加荒唐，以至简化者自己后来也难以接受，不久夭亡。</p>
  <p>下面，我们大致地扫视一下“二简字”的荒谬乱相：</p>
  <p>◎声符改造：<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272129342223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725172" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272129342223.jpg" alt="" width="150" b="60" /></a></p>
  <p>◎会意改造：<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272131422223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725174" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272131422223.jpg" alt="" width="150" b="30" /></a></p>
  <p>◎字形假借：<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272135502223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725176" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272135502223.jpg" alt="" width="150" b="30" /></a><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272135582223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725177" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272135582223.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="60" /></a></p>
  <p>◎部分删减：<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272140032223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725180" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272140032223.jpg" alt="" width="200" b="60" /></a></p>
  <p>◎符号替换：<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272140162223.jpg"><br />  <img class="size-large wp-image-5725182" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1404272140162223.jpg" alt="" width="150" b="30" /></a></p>
