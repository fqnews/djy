  <p>&#8220;Yangming Spring Morning&#8221; is a very popular flute solo. I have a student named Ho Li-mien, who is currently in the third year of the arts program at Yangming High School. When, during the year before last, I held a joint exhibition of paintings and calligraphy with my fellow teacher Wu Ying-kuo, I asked her to play the flute to liven things up. She ended up playing &#8220;Yangming Spring Morning&#8221; in front of an audience of 200 (her performance was uploaded to YouTube). </p>
