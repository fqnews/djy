  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">古早味<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%A0%82%E7%B3%96.md#1">砂糖</a>卷卷</span></h2>  <p>蓬松柔软，最纯粹的蛋香与奶香，古早味最能呈现这样简单的幸福。</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">份量</span></h3>  <p>39.5cm，长2卷</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <h3><span style="color: #000000;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>体</span></h3>  <p><strong>A 蛋 黄 面 糊</strong></p>
  <p>沙拉油－35g<br />  水－35g<br />  细砂糖－7g<br />  低筋面粉－57g<br />  玉米粉－5g<br />  泡打粉－1g<br />  蛋 黄 －77g</p>
  <p><strong>B 蛋 白 霜</strong></p>
  <p>蛋白－150g<br />  细砂糖－60g</p>
  <h3>内馅</h3>  <p><strong>奶油霜</strong></p>
  <p>无盐奶油－150g<br />  （室温软化） 细砂糖－30g</p>
  <p><strong>装饰</strong></p>
  <p>细砂糖－适量</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>  <p>1. 将沙拉油、水与细砂糖放入钢盆中拌匀。<br />  2. 加入过筛的低筋面粉、玉米粉与泡打粉搅拌均匀。<br />  3. 再加入蛋黄拌匀即为蛋黄面糊。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12724533" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/63be225be99cec1ef70f1b3a5f585008.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/63be225be99cec1ef70f1b3a5f585008-450x298.jpg" alt="鸡蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蛋黄面糊。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4. 另取一锅，将蛋白放入钢盆中，打至粗泡沫。<br />  5. 分次加入细砂糖。<br />  6. 将蛋白打至勾起有鸟嘴状，即为蛋白霜。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12724534" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/c22d100d76f605e84a1a00936885e5cf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/c22d100d76f605e84a1a00936885e5cf-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蛋白打至勾起有鸟嘴状，即为蛋白霜。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>7. 取1/3份量的蛋白霜加入蛋黄糊中拌匀。<br />  8. 再倒回剩余蛋白霜中，用刮刀划“J”字形搅拌均匀。<br />  9. 将面糊倒入铺有白报纸烤盘中。<br />  10. 用软刮板将面糊表面轻轻抹平。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12724535" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1bb3856fc1d6ac49cd6349786bbc6fb3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1bb3856fc1d6ac49cd6349786bbc6fb3-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用软刮板将面糊表面轻轻抹平。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>11. 烤箱预热后，烤时，盘轻敲一下，再将烤盘放入上火 190℃，下火170℃ 的烤箱中烘烤。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12724536" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7727b5c83000175146cb44a5d5dda4e0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7727b5c83000175146cb44a5d5dda4e0-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烤箱预热后，烤时，盘轻敲一下，再将烤盘放入烤箱中烘烤。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>12. 烘烤至12分钟时，取出烤盘调头，再放回烤箱。<br />  13. 烘烤约6分钟后取出，重摔一下烤盘释出热气。<br />  14. 迅速脱模撕除四边白报纸，放置网架上冷却备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12724537" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/0628e746052923db1163874b8da15de1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/0628e746052923db1163874b8da15de1-450x276.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="276" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">迅速脱模撕除四边白报纸，放置网架上冷却备用。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>15. 将奶油霜所有材料放入钢盆中打发泛白后备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12724538" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/539f562000acdcca7758058751b0ccef.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/539f562000acdcca7758058751b0ccef-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将奶油霜所有材料放入钢盆中打发泛白后备用。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>16. 先覆盖一张新白报纸，翻面将蛋糕纸膜撕除。<br />  17. 再将蛋糕对切。<br />  18. 先取其中一片，抹上薄薄的奶油霜馅。<br />  19. 然后在长边先轻划三刀。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12724539" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7ab6f06ebacc1f2cf02a3671dfa1296a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7ab6f06ebacc1f2cf02a3671dfa1296a-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在长边先轻划三刀。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>20. 在前端的白报纸底下，用湿抹布擦一下防滑。<br />  21. 接着将擀面棍置于底部白报纸下。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12724540" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/22304fc353bc5006a9da338728543344.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/22304fc353bc5006a9da338728543344-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将擀面棍置于底部白报纸下（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>22. 双手轻轻卷起白报纸。<br />  23. 直到将<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95%E5%8D%B7.md#1">蛋糕卷</a>卷好。<br />  24. 稍微用力推回擀面棍，让蛋糕卷更紧实。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12724541" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/561f6a7427439d09e0010ccd5b696293.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724541" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/561f6a7427439d09e0010ccd5b696293-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">稍微用力推回擀面棍，让蛋糕卷更紧实。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>25. 用双手整理一下蛋糕卷。<br />  26. 两端扭紧，放入冰箱冷藏定型。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12724542" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/da74899fa449807919d92fae85b87f9d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724542" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/da74899fa449807919d92fae85b87f9d-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">两端扭紧，放入冰箱冷藏定型。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>27. 将蛋糕卷在砂糖上滚几圈即可。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12724543" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1c570f7ef658fd4dae54f9daf6881d90.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12724543" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1c570f7ef658fd4dae54f9daf6881d90-450x298.jpg" alt="蛋糕" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蛋糕卷在砂糖上滚几圈即可。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12714332" style="width: 125px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2dca9e35145163663ffd81ce3bb58406.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12714332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2dca9e35145163663ffd81ce3bb58406-450x540.jpg" alt="蛋糕卷" width="125" b="150" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《卷！卷！卷！蛋糕卷的百变造型与风味》书封。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3>本文摘自：<span style="color: #0000ff;">《<a style="color: #0000ff;" href="https://www.books.com.tw/products/0010823795" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">卷！卷！卷！蛋糕卷的百变造型与风味：从基础风味、创意风格到缤纷手绘，蛋糕卷装饰与造型技巧全搜罗！</a>》</span>，日日幸福提供。</h3>  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">· <a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/21/1/27/n12714264.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> 【简单做蛋糕卷】香葱肉松卷 咸甜奶香好滋味</a></span><br />  <span style="color: #008000;"> · <a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/18/8/31/n10680061.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">巧克力优格蛋糕卷 甜而不腻送礼自用两相宜 </a></span><br />  <span style="color: #008000;"> · <a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/12/3/18/n3543817.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">【刘老师烹饪教室】葡萄干戚风蛋糕卷</a></span></h3>  <p>责任编辑：伊芙</p>
