<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小狮儿子太萌 威严狮王摇身一变“暖爸”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/c2d7bab3f77b1e8df2f9fccbd7056697-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Tatu跟爸爸有着最萌体型差。（Denver Zoo授权提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年11月29日讯】（大纪元记者赖依琴台湾台北报导）美国科罗拉多州的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B9%E4%BD%9B%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E5%9B%AD.md#1">丹佛动物园</a>（Denver Zoo）今年出现了一位备受瞩目的小明星，它就是小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90.md#1">狮子</a>“Tatu”。它一出生的萌样就吸引了不少粉丝，还有Tatu爸爸与儿子互动时也不同于狮子威猛、严肃的形象，摇身一变为“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E6%9F%94%E7%88%B6%E4%BA%B2.md#1">温柔父亲</a>”，让粉丝们看了直呼：“这是暖爸！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11688696" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/fafd52311eba136cae7d760b06fb8317.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11688696 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/fafd52311eba136cae7d760b06fb8317-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Tatu的萌样圈了许多粉丝。（Denver Zoo授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11688699" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/492172816600921b4eeb5ef6291a3365.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11688699" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/492172816600921b4eeb5ef6291a3365-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Tatu凝望发呆中。（Denver Zoo授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%B9%E4%BD%9B%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E5%9B%AD.md#1">丹佛动物园</a>，是科罗拉多州最受欢迎的景点之一，园方经常在社群网站上发布动物可爱的特写照片，吸引不少网友前去朝圣。今年夏天诞生的“Tatu”更吸引大批游客前来观看。</p>
<p>小幼狮刚出生时，必须和其它成年<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90.md#1">狮子</a>隔离开来，等长到够大、经兽医判断后，才能与其它狮子合笼，也因此这只明星小幼狮一开始没有和妈妈、爸爸住在一起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11688701" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5d522c048c655f1b51d5bf0b498f89c8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11688701" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/5d522c048c655f1b51d5bf0b498f89c8-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Denver Zoo授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11688698" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/27a6b7265a96b71d9cca702f3f96aa42.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11688698" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/27a6b7265a96b71d9cca702f3f96aa42-450x348.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="348" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Tatu与爸爸初次见面时，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E7%8E%8B.md#1">狮王</a>爸爸严肃形象瞬间转萌。（Denver Zoo授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11688697" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/cf7fa91e254fb38190c068d867c49ea7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11688697" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/cf7fa91e254fb38190c068d867c49ea7-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Tatu也十分黏着妈妈。（Denver Zoo授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有一天，园方仔细评估后，认为小幼狮可以和爸妈互动，在工作人员的安排和全程监控下，小幼狮和<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AE%E7%8E%8B.md#1">狮王</a>爸爸见面了，而狮王的反应出乎众人意料，当它看到自己的孩子时，竟卖萌般地把屁股翘高高，且显得十分温柔、细心，互动相当温馨！</p>
<p>今年感恩节（11月28日）时，园方再度贴出Tatu一家人的照片，短短几个月明显看到Tatu又长大了，跟家人的互动也越来越紧密。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11688702" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/b350ac0823154d2f7fd730293411c60b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11688702" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/b350ac0823154d2f7fd730293411c60b-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">感恩节时，园方po出狮子一家人的合照。（Denver Zoo授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11688703" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/c127c63de68c21e2af0d01143c6d4692.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11688703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/c127c63de68c21e2af0d01143c6d4692-450x450.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Denver Zoo授权提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>丹佛动物园在许多地区都有许多关于动物保护及发展的活动，而且还有许多教育计划以及接受志愿者工作。</p>
