<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>李心洁登金马红毯 与王净以白礼服各展风情</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1123-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>王净（左）为了典礼特地瘦身，她穿着纯白抓皱单肩礼服，相当高雅时尚。李心洁穿着白色蕾丝度假风礼服亮相。（陈柏州／大纪元制图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年11月23日讯】（大纪元郑宜芬综合报导）第56届<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%A9%AC%E5%A5%96.md#1">金马奖</a>23日晚间在台北举行颁奖典礼，金马影后<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%BF%83%E6%B4%81.md#1">李心洁</a>穿着白色蕾丝度假风礼服亮相。睽违3年拍戏，她以《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%95%E9%9B%BE%E8%8A%B1%E5%9B%AD.md#1">夕雾花园</a>》再次角逐影后，透露拍戏时一度紧张到崩溃大哭，不过回到家乡马来西亚拍戏，格外有意义。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11676138" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230726082384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11676138" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230726082384.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第56届<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%A9%AC%E5%A5%96.md#1">金马奖</a>颁奖典礼于11月23日晚间在台北举行，金马影后<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%BF%83%E6%B4%81.md#1">李心洁</a>穿着白色蕾丝度假风礼服亮相。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>以《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%BF%94%E6%A0%A1.md#1">返校</a>》走红并入围最佳女主角的的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%87%80.md#1">王净</a>，为了典礼特地瘦身，她穿着纯白抓皱单肩礼服，相当高雅时尚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11676141" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230725302384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11676141" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230725302384.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%87%80.md#1">王净</a>穿着纯白抓皱单肩礼服，相当高雅时尚。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>国际影后杨贵媚穿着宝蓝色斜肩礼服，以黑色腰带点缀，高贵端庄；同样是熟女时尚范本的金钟视后柯淑勤，穿着宝蓝色缎面礼服搭配亮片披肩，散发端庄典雅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11676153" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230726292384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11676153" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230726292384.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第56届金马奖颁奖典礼于11月23日晚间在台北举行，国际影后杨贵媚穿着宝蓝色斜肩礼服，以黑色腰带点缀，架势高贵。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>金马影后谢盈萱穿着黑色单肩连身裤，简约俐落；金马得主李千那受邀担任颁奖嘉宾与表演，以黑色蕾丝礼服搭配超高马尾，她近来为了筹备表演忙得瘦成22腰，相当敬业；为新戏晒黑的温贞凌，也选择黑色缎面礼服搭配低马尾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11676152" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230726532384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11676152" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230726532384.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">金马影后谢盈萱穿着黑色单肩连身裤，简约俐落。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11676140" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230724342384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11676140" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230724342384.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">李千那以黑色蕾丝礼服搭配超高马尾。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>金马影展执行委员会主席李安致词，希望将电影界众多前辈56年来的努力传承下去，“电影不管在台湾还是全世界，都受到很多挑战，但电影界永远有很多人在努力，我们就是给大家一个竞争的平台与工作的机会，我相信很多梦想家会表达他们的见解”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11676139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230724132384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11676139" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230724132384-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">金马影展执行委员会主席李安。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>杨千霈迈入主持金马奖星光大道第十年，以一袭粉色系亮片礼服展现高雅秀丽，她开心表示：“见证华语片的璀璨时刻！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11676137" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230723402384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11676137" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230723402384.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">杨千霈以一袭粉色系亮片礼服展现高雅秀丽。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，今年典礼的颁奖嘉宾，也邀请到获得金棕榈奖的日本大导演是枝裕和、日本影帝永濑正敏助阵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11676157" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230726382384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11676157" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230726382384.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">日本大导演是枝裕和。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11676156" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230727072384.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11676156" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1911230727072384.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">日本影帝永濑正敏。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
