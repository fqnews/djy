<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>老年人的大脑健康讲座</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月19日讯】老年健康对每个人都非常重要，更重要的是不要忘记<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E8%84%91%E5%81%A5%E5%BA%B7.md#1">大脑健康</a>！这<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%AE%B2%E5%BA%A7.md#1">讲座</a>鼓励参加者积极地保护及维持大脑健康。了解更多有关<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%85%B9%E6%B5%B7%E9%BB%98%E7%97%87.md#1">阿兹海默症</a>及脑退化症的知识，与及改善您大脑、身体及心灵健康的方法。欢迎任何有兴趣人士参加。</p>
<p>Healthy aging is important for everyone, and it is essential not to forget the health of your brain as well!</p>
<p>This workshop encourages participants to actively engage in protecting and maintaining their brain. Learn strategies and set goals for improving the health of your mind, body and spirit. Anyone interested in brain health is welcome to attend.</p>

时间: 下午 1:30 至3:00 PM<br />
地点: Richmond Caring Place列治文加爱中心 (294)，7000 Minoru Blvd, Richmond</p>
