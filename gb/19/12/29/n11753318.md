<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【C2食光-节日饼干】意式脆饼</h2>
<img width="600" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/db9792b4b3b182aa660e6cf6c462dcc8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Biscotti 这个字来自中世纪拉丁文，有二次烘焙的意思。而这个意式脆饼也需经由两次烘烤而成。 (C2食光提供)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月30日讯】（大纪元记者<a href="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md">C2食光</a>报导）Biscotti是意大利传统点心，非常简单易做，吃起来的口感偏硬、脆、香。</p>
<p>Biscotti这个字来自中世纪拉丁文biscotus，有二次烘焙的意思。而这个意式脆饼也需经由两次烘烤而成。</p>
<p>这种饼干容易保存，很适合外出旅行携带。而且相较其它的饼干，Biscotti较低脂低卡，因此很受到大众欢迎。</p>
<p>美好的午后，搭配咖啡或茶，享受美好的午茶食光。</p>
<p><strong>更多作法，请参考小影片：</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11753329" style="width: 148px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/eb27155dd2babfabdeb3b00ea32fddc9.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11753329" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/eb27155dd2babfabdeb3b00ea32fddc9.jpg" alt="" width="148" b="148" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">更多作法，请参考小影片： (大纪元制图)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>食材：</strong>无盐奶油20g糖50g蛋2颗盐少许泡打粉1/4小匙中筋面粉200g杏仁角60g其它食材：黑巧克力酱白巧克力酱杏仁角干燥莓果碎</p>
<p><strong>作法：</strong></p>
<p>1.将糖及奶油打散，之后打入一颗蛋，打匀后再将另一颗蛋打匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11753321" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/250db1b42a02e0ebb9bb168891af9bce.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11753321" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/250db1b42a02e0ebb9bb168891af9bce-450x315.png" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<a href="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md">C2食光</a>提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.混入过筛面粉及泡打粉拌匀，加入少许盐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11753323" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/c62bff674b619d4d02be4934c01685a2.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11753323" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/c62bff674b619d4d02be4934c01685a2-450x315.png" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(C2食光提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.再混入杏仁角拌成团，压平后放入预热好的烤箱以200℃烤30分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11753324" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3045cbbc233c7b1da9a42dff0c70d2c5.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11753324" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/3045cbbc233c7b1da9a42dff0c70d2c5-450x315.png" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(C2食光提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.取出，待冷却切0.8公分厚片铺平，再以160℃烤15分钟即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11753325" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/0ba97c2cf535d313ec427a358b5973a2.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11753325" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/0ba97c2cf535d313ec427a358b5973a2-450x315.png" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(C2食光提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.冷却后可享用，酥酥脆脆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11753326" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/e90f4f770630907a3838ce0d94eacb89.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11753326" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/e90f4f770630907a3838ce0d94eacb89-450x315.png" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(C2食光提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.淋上巧克力酱也好美味！◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11753327" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/23badaec9abd9497397d16580caa50a4.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11753327" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/23badaec9abd9497397d16580caa50a4-450x315.png" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(C2食光提供)</figcaption></figure>
