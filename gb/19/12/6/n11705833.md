<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>刘奕儿演霸气千金 与刘以豪组“双刘CP”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/Screen-Shot-2020-07-10-at-10.37.14-AM-320x200.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年12月06日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台北报导）导演吴子云率领主角<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E4%BB%A5%E8%B1%AA.md#1">刘以豪</a>、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E5%A5%95%E5%84%BF.md#1">刘奕儿</a>以及<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9B%B9%E4%BD%91%E5%AE%81.md#1">曹佑宁</a>、天心、黄仲崑、蔡思韵今天（6日）出席Netflix华语自制原创影集《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9E%81%E9%81%93%E5%8D%83%E9%87%91.md#1">极道千金</a>》上线记者会。<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E4%BB%A5%E8%B1%AA.md#1">刘以豪</a>与<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9B%B9%E4%BD%91%E5%AE%81.md#1">曹佑宁</a>戏中是情敌，现场被拱争夺刘奕儿芳心，三人玩起壁咚，让刘奕儿脸红害羞。<br />
<figure id="attachment_11705977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/20191206-Netflix-08.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/20191206-Netflix-08-600x664.jpg" alt="刘以豪《极道千金》刘奕儿" width="450" b="664" class="size-large wp-image-11705977" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">刘以豪（左）与<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E5%A5%95%E5%84%BF.md#1">刘奕儿</a>（右）戏里戏外都有好默契。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
<figure id="attachment_11705798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/ad43782e38f78660d77532b990d13222.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/ad43782e38f78660d77532b990d13222-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="900" class="size-large wp-image-11705798" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">刘以豪带玫瑰花送给刘奕儿，而刘奕儿也准备剧中定情信物大象马克杯回送。（中央社）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>刘奕儿在戏中饰演黑帮千金，再度挑战武打戏码，她说自己从小就是田径队员，爸爸也是跆拳道教练，现在终于有地方展现这一身好武艺，也笑说自己记者会当天见到刘以豪就打他一拳，逗得哄堂大笑。</p>
<p>刘以豪有着“台湾最萌男友”封号，这次剧里的角色融合了“厌世巨星”与“宅男”，还与黑道迷妹千金激起爱情的火花。他现场带着一束玫瑰花送给刘奕儿，而刘奕儿也准备剧中两人的定情信物大象马克杯回送。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11705975" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/20191206-Netflix-17.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/20191206-Netflix-17-600x318.jpg" alt="《极道千金》" width="600" b="318" class="size-large wp-image-11705975" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9E%81%E9%81%93%E5%8D%83%E9%87%91.md#1">极道千金</a>》上线记者会6日在台北举行，主持人黄子佼（左起）介绍导演吴子云与主要演员黄仲崑、天心、刘以豪、刘奕儿、曹佑宁、蔡思韵，众人开心合影。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>主持人黄子佼这次客串演出八卦周刊主管一角，与饰演王牌经纪人的天心成为死对头，掀起一波经纪人与八卦狗仔的大战。</p>
<p>而以《返校》“殷老师”一角受到瞩目的女星蔡思韵，特地从香港飞来台湾参加记者会，饰演大明星的她聘请刘奕儿当保镳，促成“双刘CP”的诞生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11705980" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/20191206-Netflix-01.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/20191206-Netflix-01-600x381.jpg" alt="曹佑宁 蔡思韵" width="600" b="381" class="size-large wp-image-11705980" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《极道千金》6日举行上线记者会，（左起）演员曹佑宁与蔡思韵亮相。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11705982" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/20191206-Netflix-16.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/20191206-Netflix-16-600x404.jpg" alt="《极道千金》上线记者会" width="600" b="404" class="size-large wp-image-11705982" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《极道千金》上线记者会主创齐聚，（左起）天心、导演吴子云、黄仲崑。（Netflix提供）</figcaption></figure>
