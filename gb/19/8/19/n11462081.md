<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>炸肉丸佐酪梨酱 孩子最爱点心</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_108-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>炸肉丸佐酪梨酱。（图/联经出版提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>酪梨能解油腻，非常适合各种油炸料理。<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E8%82%89%E4%B8%B8.md#1">炸肉丸</a>口感清爽，是很受孩子喜爱的点心，如果搭配<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8%E9%85%B1.md#1">酪梨酱</a>享用，除了美味还增添了健康。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">食材（2人份）</span></h3>
<p>牛绞肉 150 克<br />
猪绞肉 50 克<br />
清酒 2 大匙<br />
蘑菇 4 个<br />
洋葱 1/4 个<br />
红萝卜 1/4 个<br />
蒜泥 1/2 大匙<br />
鸡蛋 1 个<br />
面包粉 4 杯<br />
香芹粉 适量<br />
帕马森起司 适量<br />
盐 适量<br />
黑胡椒 适量<br />
奶油 1 大匙<br />
橄榄油 适量</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;">蔬菜汤</span></p>
<p>水 3 杯<br />
洋葱 1/2 个<br />
大葱 1 根<br />
红萝卜 1/4 根<br />
月桂叶 1 片</p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8%E9%85%B1.md#1">酪梨酱</a></span></p>
<p>酪梨 1 个（磨成泥）<br />
原味希腊优格 1/4 杯<br />
罗勒叶? 1/2 杯（磨成泥）<br />
墨西哥辣椒 1 根（切丁）<br />
寡糖 1 大匙<br />
柠檬汁 4 大匙<br />
盐 适量</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1. 将蔬菜汤的食材装入锅中，以中火炖煮30 分钟后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11462189" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_101.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11462189 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_101-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E8%82%89%E4%B8%B8.md#1">炸肉丸</a>佐酪梨酱作法。（图/联经出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 碗中倒入猪绞肉及牛绞肉，倒入清酒与黑胡椒后抓腌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11462191" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_102.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11462191 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_102-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">炸肉丸佐酪梨酱作法。（图/联经出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 蘑菇、洋葱及红萝卜都切碎，橄榄油倒入平底锅中，放入刚才切碎的食材，加点盐和黑胡椒调味后炒熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11462198" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_103.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11462198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_103-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">炸肉丸佐酪梨酱作法。（图/联经出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 把步骤2. 跟步骤3. 的食材倒入盆中，加入蒜泥、鸡蛋、香芹粉、面包粉，搓揉到肉泥变软，然后再捏成肉丸形状备用。接着将橄榄油倒入平底锅，以中火热锅后再抹上奶油，放入肉丸煎至外皮变得有点焦黄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11462201" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_104.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11462201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_104-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">炸肉丸佐酪梨酱作法。（图/联经出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 将肉丸放入步骤1. 的蔬菜汤中，煮至肉丸完全熟透后即可起锅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11462204" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_105.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11462204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_105-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">炸肉丸佐酪梨酱作法。（图/联经出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 拿一个碗，倒入酪梨酱食材并调好酱料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11462207" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_06.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11462207 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_06-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">炸肉丸佐酪梨酱作法。（图/联经出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 肉丸盛盘，撒上刨碎的帕马森起司，再搭配酪梨酱即可上桌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11462211" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_107.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11462211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/08/20190819_shouhuihsu_food_107-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">炸肉丸佐酪梨酱作法。（图/联经出版提供）</figcaption></figure>
