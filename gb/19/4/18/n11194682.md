<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>优惠价钱享受高质正宗片皮鸭及各式京川沪菜</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JvS3U"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/xlget2929/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年04月18日讯】近来乌溪沙迎海荟进驻了很多食肆，其中一间<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9C%88%E6%98%A0%E7%9A%87%E5%BB%B7.md#1">月映皇廷</a>，听说食物水准高而且价钱大众化，最吸引人的是正宗北京<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%89%87%E7%9A%AE%E9%B8%AD.md#1">片皮鸭</a>，星期一至六每只只需188元，听说比一些大酒楼更正宗好吃，喜欢片皮鸭的我当然不会错过。</p>
<p>酒楼傍特别增设了一个小厨房，透过玻璃可看到师傅即场拉面。酒楼的面积不大，但装潢简洁明亮。侍应和部长都笑容满面，招呼周到。坐下看到很多台都点了特价<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%89%87%E7%9A%AE%E9%B8%AD.md#1">片皮鸭</a>，由于每晚供应数量有限，因此我一早便预留了，避免迟了而售罄。我和朋友除了片皮鸭外，还点了<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%AC%BC%E5%8C%85.md#1">小笼包</a>，四川担担面，砂锅华亭云吞鸡，鸡丝伴粉皮，四喜烤麸，上海炒年糕，孜然寸骨和高力豆沙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11194769" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246231366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194769" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246231366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">皇廷<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%AC%BC%E5%8C%85.md#1">小笼包</a>、四川担担面和砂锅华亭云吞鸡。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>先上来的是小吃鸡丝伴粉皮，四喜烤麸和小笼包。粉皮很滑身，麻酱香浓。四喜烤麸很入味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11194719" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247231366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194719" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247231366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小吃鸡丝伴粉皮的粉皮很滑身，麻酱香浓。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11194724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247181366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194724" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247181366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">四喜烤麸。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>小笼包皮薄馅，一口咬下去满满的汤汁溅出来，肉味香浓很好吃，比很多自称小笼包专门店的实在是好吃太多了，我忍不住吃完再吃，竟然一个人就吃了3只。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11194725" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246281366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194725" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246281366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小笼包皮薄馅，一口咬下去满满的汤汁溅出来，肉味香浓很好吃。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>紧接期待已久的片皮鸭出场了，侍应用餐车推到我们面前，再由部长亲自片皮。整只鸭烤得胀卜卜，香味四溢，部长一边片我们一边垂涎着。部长说加88元便可两食，就是把余下整只鸭斩件用酱爆上桌，这么便宜我们当然说好。跟片皮鸭上的馍馍皮跟传统一样，每一片也用底纸隔着，现在已经很少酒楼有这样做，因为比较麻烦成本又高。这里能保持传统做法，值得一赞。馍馍皮口感很滑，皮脆肉嫩的片皮鸭加上大葱，青瓜条和烤鸭酱，用馍馍皮包着吃，又是一道好吃到停不下来的菜式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11194745" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247041366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194745" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247041366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">整只鸭烤得胀卜卜，香味四溢。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11194732" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247081366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194732" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247081366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">侍应用餐车将推烤鸭到我们面前，再由部长亲自片皮。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11194722" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247131366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194722" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247131366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">片皮鸭皮脆肉嫩。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11194760" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172332581366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194760" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172332581366-600x339.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">跟片皮鸭上的馍馍皮跟传统一样，每一片也用底纸隔着。（影片撷图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11194764" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247361366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194764" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247361366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">酱爆鸭。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其它食物方面，砂锅云吞鸡汤底很甜，奶白色应该有用鱼来熬过。孜然寸骨炸得香脆，如果孜然粉再多点，味道会更浓郁更惹味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11194771" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246321366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194771" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246321366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">砂锅云吞鸡汤底很甜，奶白色应该有用鱼来熬过。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11194773" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246411366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194773" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246411366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">砂锅华亭云吞鸡。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11194774" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246451366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194774" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246451366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">这次点的砂锅云吞鸡汤内有半只鸡。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11194775" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247321366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194775" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247321366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">孜然寸骨炸得香脆，如果孜然粉再多点，味道会更浓郁更惹味。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>四川担担面不辣，但麻酱香浓，面条坚韧，好吃。上海炒年糕味道则一般。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11194781" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246371366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194781" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246371366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">四川担担面面条坚韧、好吃。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11194794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247281366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194794" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172247281366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">上海炒年糕味道一般。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后是甜品高力豆沙，卖相虽然不是胀卜卜，但吃着有惊喜，口感Q弹充满蛋白香，内层豆沙蓉香甜而不腻，为这一餐画下完美句号。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11194796" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246501366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194796" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246501366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">高力豆沙卖相虽然不是胀卜卜，但吃着有惊喜。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11194797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246541366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11194797" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/1904172246541366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">高力豆沙口感Q弹充满蛋白香，内层豆沙蓉香甜而不腻。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这里设有春季优惠，星期一至五除特价菜式外，其余全单享有八八折，真的很吸引。更重要的是食物水准真的很不错，服务又好，喜欢<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%BA%AC%E5%B7%9D%E6%B2%AA%E8%8F%9C.md#1">京川沪菜</a>的您值得一试。#</p>
