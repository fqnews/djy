<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>马路幼犬获救后美滋滋 甜笑影片萌翻网友</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JfNrq"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/erg2932/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年04月12日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳编译报导）收容所的狗狗会有各种各样的情绪反应，取决于它先前的境遇。近期，一只初生小母狗在收容所绽放微笑的影片在网上热传：来到温饱无忧的安全处所，听到充满爱意的话语，它的内心显然是甜蜜蜜的。</p>
<p>春寒料峭的2月末，美国德州东部博蒙特市车水马龙的路上，有人发现了还不到2个月大的蕾拉（Layla）。当地动物收容所“<span style="color: #008080;"><a style="color: #008080;" href="https://www.facebook.com/beaumontanimalcare/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Beaumont Animal Care</a></span>”闻讯立即赶来将其接管。刚上车时，弱弱的莱拉显得紧张敏感，但到达收容所后，它的表现却让工作人员非常意外。</p>
<p>当时，兽医技师蕾切尔·巴伦（Rachel Barron）走近狗窝，开始和蕾拉说话，只见小狗眯缝着小亮眼，嘴角上翘，露出幸福微笑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11182405" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/layla-all-smiles.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11182405 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/layla-all-smiles-450x475.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="475" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/rachel.barron.376/videos/vb.100001173514213/2038455836203514/?type=2&amp;video_source=user_video_tab" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Rachel Barron/Facebook</a>视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>巴伦向动物网媒“渡渡鸟”（The Dodo）描述道：“当我开始用奶声奶气的腔调和它讲话，它非常喜欢。它开始摇尾巴，然后绽开了它那已名声在外的微笑。我就拿出手机拍了一段视频。真太可爱了，不能不分享。”</p>
<p><center><a src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/video.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Frachel.barron.376%2Fvideos%2F2038455836203514%2F&amp;width=450&amp;show_text=false&amp;height=804&amp;appId" width="450" b="804"></a></center>&nbsp;</p>
<p>巴伦说，蕾拉的表情，似乎知道她被好人接管了。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/smile-pup-layla-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11182407" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/smile-pup-layla-1-450x449.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="449" /></a></p>
<p>而今，小狗甜美的表情让众多网友为之着迷，连小歌后亚莉安娜（Ariana Grande）也在推特为影片点赞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11182381" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/layla-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11182381 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/layla-1-450x662.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="662" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=2041492462566518&amp;set=pb.100001173514213.-2207520000.1555082784.&amp;type=3&amp;theater" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Rachel Barron/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>巴伦将视频传上网后不久，询问领养蕾拉的讯息和电话就像滚雪球一样涌来。最终，当地一个爱狗家庭如愿中选。</p>
<p>妮可·托尼（Nicole Toney）和丈夫已经收养了3只狗和2只猫，一看到莱拉的影片，就确信这只小狗很适合加入他们的大家庭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11182417" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/pup-layla-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11182417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/pup-layla-3-450x600.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of Rachel Barron)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/rachel.barron.376" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Rachel Barron</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>托尼回想在收容所亲见蕾拉的情景：“起初它很害羞，但你可以看出它心里非常甜蜜和兴奋。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11182415" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/pup-layla-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11182415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/pup-layla-2-450x600.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of Rachel Barron)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/rachel.barron.376" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Rachel Barron</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>不出所料，小蕾拉立即和托尼夫妇熟络起来，很快就被批准跟他们回家了。到家后，蕾拉很快就和其它狗儿在一起追跑玩闹了。</p>
<p><center><a src=""></a><a href="https://git.io/JfNrq"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/erg2932/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/JBhcsvI1cG8" width="600" b="338" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></center>托尼家有只狗名叫“夫人”（Lady），被领养时刚刚在收容所生完宝宝，它立即想要靠近蕾拉。女主人说， “‘夫人’是个妈妈，她真的被蕾拉吸引了。”</p>
<p>对家里这些毛孩，小蕾拉也同样感兴趣，可能是经历过无依无靠的时光，如今，它抓住一切机会和家里的猫猫狗狗依偎在一起。</p>
<p>托尼说，蕾拉完全适应了环境，会挨着她先生的脸颊卧下来睡着，“它真是个小甜心，我们看到它连睡梦中都会微笑呢！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11189052" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Layla.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11189052 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Layla-450x450.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;https://www.facebook.com/LaylaTheSmilingPup/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Layla Toney&lt;/a&gt;)" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="https://www.facebook.com/LaylaTheSmilingPup/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Layla Toney</a>)</figcaption></figure>
