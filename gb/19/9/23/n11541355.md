<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：幸喜</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/01/09050511291910003-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>白头翁 (摄影:王嘉益 / 大纪元) 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>久久，冷冬携寒风留连不去<br />
有时还飘些霪雨<br />
这天气叫人瑟缩不前<br />
连高大建筑和树木都发抖</p>
<p>我等得愈来愈不耐烦<br />
幸喜今早一只白头翁<br />
在屋旁那棵樱花树上<br />
呼唤出朵朵春花</p>
<p>我瞻望春天很久了<br />
但愿将来的日子是晴的<br />
把前程彩绘成<br />
一片万紫千红</p>
<p> <strong>Luckily </strong> &emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>The cold winter carries the cold wind lingering about for a long time<br />
Some time there wafting the excessive rains in addition to it<br />
It huddles us up with cold of this weather<br />
Even the mansion and the trees are trembling</p>
<p>I’m waiting for more and more impatience<br />
Luckily, there is a light-vented bulbul this morning<br />
On the cherry tree by the house<br />
Calls a flower and a flower of spring flower</p>
<p>I’m looking ahead the spring for a long time<br />
Just wishing it’s fine in the future<br />
Paints the future into<br />
A piece of innumerable flowers of purple and red@</p>
<p>责任编辑：林芳宇</p>
