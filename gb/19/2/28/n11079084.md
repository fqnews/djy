<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欣赏贵族油画 品赏各式甜品</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118241366-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（米芝Gi提供） 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年02月28日讯】朋友介绍元州街有间<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E5%93%81.md#1">甜品</a>店推出甜品放题，价钱合理，甜品卖相、质素和味道也不错，对我这个甜品控真是佳音，便和朋友约定去试。上星期六虽然下大雨，但是也无损我们的心情。</p>
<p>到达<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E5%93%81.md#1">甜品</a>店后才知原来甜品放题原来要先预约，虽然做足一星期7日，可是先预约先得，星期一至五每日每个时段只供应15张台，星期六、日及公众假期每个时段只供应30张台。虽然吃不了放题，但我们也照试试他们的甜品。</p>

<p>打开餐牌，先到甜品种类颇多，而且色彩丰富，除了一般甜品也有些现在流行养颜的桃胶甜品，还有糯米糍等等都很吸引。看到花多眼乱，最后拣了原盅椰皇炖桃胶、鲜杂果桃胶、紫薯牛奶飞雪、白雪黄金卷和黑糖红豆团子（4串）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11079315" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118501366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079315" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118501366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">打开餐牌，先到甜品种类颇多，而且色彩丰富。（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>等候甜品时看看店内的布置，店的面积不大，墙上挂了几幅中世纪以贵族为题材的油画，很贴合餐厅贵族甜品的名字，其它装饰不多。因为天雨关系，当天的客人也不多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11079317" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118441366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079317" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118441366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">墙上挂了几幅中世纪以贵族为题材的油画，很贴合餐厅贵族甜品的名字。（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11079318" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118541366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079318" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118541366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">店的面积不大，其它装饰不多。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了黑糖红豆团子外，其它甜品几乎同一时间送上。打开原盅椰皇炖桃胶，可能焗了一段时间，里面水有点多，桃胶放在炖奶上，晶莹爽口，炖奶比较稀身不够香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11079311" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280119041366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079311" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280119041366.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">原盅椰皇炖桃胶。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11079313" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280119001366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079313" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280119001366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">原盅椰皇炖桃胶里面水有点多，放在炖奶上的桃胶晶莹爽口，但炖奶比较稀身不够香。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鲜杂果桃胶有火龙果、奇异果、西瓜、木瓜、芒果和不少的桃胶，下面是椰奶西米，味道不错甜度适中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11079309" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280119101366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079309" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280119101366-600x430.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="430" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲜杂果桃胶有火龙果、奇异果、西瓜、木瓜、芒果和不少的桃胶，下面是椰奶西米。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11079310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280119181366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079310" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280119181366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲜杂果桃胶味道不错甜度适中。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>榴梿销魂上面是绿色的水晶蒟蒻，下面有很多榴梿肉，还有云呢拿雪糕和芋圆，榴梿很甜很香很好吃配上雪糕口感更香滑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11079306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118191366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079306" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118191366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">榴梿销魂上面是绿色的水晶蒟蒻，下面有很多榴梿肉，榴梿很甜很香很好吃配上雪糕口感更香滑。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>白雪黄金卷上面铺上芒果沙冰，下面白色皮卷着芒果肉，芒果肉很香甜，但包着芒果用甜品做成的皮却很甜，芒果沙冰也溶得很快。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11079300" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118341366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079300" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118341366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白雪黄金卷上面铺上芒果沙冰，下面白色皮卷着芒果肉，芒果肉很香甜。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11079302" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118121366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079302" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118121366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白雪黄金卷的芒果沙冰溶得很快。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>紫薯牛奶飞雪是紫薯雪花冰加上紫薯粉末，上面放了几粒香芋雪米糍。卖相一流，入口淡淡紫薯香，口感和味道都很不错，吃到里面竟然还有我喜爱的芋圆，而且雪花冰出奇地不会溶得很快。香芋雪米糍吃得出是用维记的雪米糍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11079296" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118291366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079296" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118291366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">紫薯牛奶飞雪上面放了几粒香芋雪米糍，入口淡淡紫薯香，吃到里面竟然还有我喜爱的芋圆。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后上来的是黑糖红豆团子，一碟共有四串，除了黑糖和红豆，其余两串分别是花生碎和曲奇碎，团子口感像糖不甩，烟烟韧韧，口感和味道也很不错。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11079292" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118391366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11079292" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1902280118391366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黑糖红豆团子（黑糖浆、花生碎、曲奇碎、红豆）。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然这次我们吃不了放题，但是对于爱好甜品一族来说，即使是平常食甜品可考虑。</p>
<p>1.5小时任食甜品放题，优惠时段：星期一至五3pm-5:30pm（最后入座时间4pm）/5:30pm-8:30pm（最后入座时间7pm）</p>
<p>星期一至五每时段每日只供应15张台，星期六、日及公众假期每时段每日只供应30张台#</p>
