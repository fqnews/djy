<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>2019诸事顺心 12星座幸运色</h2>

</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年02月09日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）年后上班，新的一年运势如何？星星王子大弟子<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%98%9F%E5%BA%A7.md#1">星座</a>专家辰宇力老师，特别推荐读者<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%BC%80%E8%BF%90.md#1">开运</a>居家小物，让新的一整年都闪闪亮眼、好运四射。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #0000ff;">牡羊座</span></h2>
<p>今年幸运色为<strong>蓝色</strong>，打开心胸，踏出舒适圈。特别推荐胡桃木休闲椅，对生活放轻松，休闲片刻，接受更多的新事物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11034305" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/5a5f8705466f2b5a5339a50bac3dc3d9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11034305" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/5a5f8705466f2b5a5339a50bac3dc3d9-600x495.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="495" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">牡羊座-胡桃木休闲椅。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>今年幸运色为<strong>棕红、暗红</strong>，运势大好大坏，面对各种机会都要做好事前功课，并谨慎行事，聪明选择。特别推荐机能系加长L型沙发，多和家人沟通，也能帮助问题的解决。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11034306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/3747dc3bdf894fc4cdb1921db043e245.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11034306" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/3747dc3bdf894fc4cdb1921db043e245-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">金牛座-机能型加长L型沙发。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_11034298" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/408f81887b55341ac5721e2d435e2fef.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11034298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/408f81887b55341ac5721e2d435e2fef-600x600.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">双子座-浅紫色猫滚滚。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
