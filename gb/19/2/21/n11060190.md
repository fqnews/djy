<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>刺绣？油画？俄女子手绣梦幻景色风靡IG</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/Vera-Shimunia-embroderies-7-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>薇拉和她的创意刺绣。(<a href="https://www.facebook.com/shimunia/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Vera Shimunia/Facebook
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年02月27日讯】（大纪元记者裴晏综合报导）在机绣织物唾手可得的今天，俄罗斯姑娘薇拉·席慕妮娅（Vera Shimunia）的手工绣品不仅稀有，而且独树一格：缤纷的色彩，富于质感的肌理，宛如一幅幅意境优美的精致油画，为她在IG赢得25万粉丝。</p>
<p>薇拉透过柔软的丝线，搭配鲜明而又精准的色彩，勾描出灿烂的大自然景致。比起讲究色彩柔和、细节匀称的图案风格，薇拉的绣品更注重强烈的画面感，以及绣线堆叠的力与美，第一秒就抓住观众视线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11073257" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/vera-embroderies-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11073257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/vera-embroderies-1-450x570.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="634" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">薇拉的创意刺绣。(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/shimunia/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Vera Shimunia/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11073258" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/Vera-Shimunia-embroderies-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11073258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/Vera-Shimunia-embroderies-2-450x559.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="621" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">薇拉的创意刺绣。(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/shimunia/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Vera Shimunia/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11073263" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/Vera-Shimunia-embroderies-6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11073263" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/Vera-Shimunia-embroderies-6-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="667" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">薇拉的创意刺绣。(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/shimunia/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Vera Shimunia/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>每一幅可爱的袖珍创作，都像在诉说关于大自然美景的感动，这也是薇拉创作这系列的初衷——用针线记录自然美，跟有缘人分享。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11073264" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/Vera-Shimunia-embroderies-8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11073264" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/Vera-Shimunia-embroderies-8-450x444.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="493" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">薇拉的创意刺绣。(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/shimunia/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Vera Shimunia/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>小小的圆形画布里，是一个个广阔的世界，一丝一线引导着观众的视线，感受大气的流动和光的变幻。不论是日出<span style="font-weight: 400;">、</span>日落，高山<span style="font-weight: 400;">大海，或是一片炫丽夺目的花海，都引人入胜。</span></p>

<p>每当薇拉发布新作品，都会赢来众多粉丝的喝采赞叹，更有很多人感谢她身体力行传承古老文化。其实薇拉完全是自学出身，刚开始只为赢得“意中人”的肯定，没想到从此引发兴趣深入探索，风格日渐成熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11073261" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/Vera-Shimunia-embroderies-4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11073261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/Vera-Shimunia-embroderies-4-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="625" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">薇拉的创意刺绣。(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/shimunia/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Vera Shimunia/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>小试刺绣半年后，薇拉开始上传作品到社群平台，网友感到惊艳之余，也令她备受鼓舞。在陆续有人询问是否出售以后，薇拉开始接受订单，在掌心大的画布上，一针一线“画出”对方喜爱的梦幻景致。</p>
