<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>BTS梦想成真担任葛莱美奖颁奖人 K-POP首例</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211022159100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>防弹少年团（BTS）于2019年第61届葛莱美奖颁奖典礼颁发Best R&B Album奖。(Kevork Djansezian/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年02月11日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）人气席卷全球的韩国男团（BTS），今日（美国当地时间10日）受邀出席第61届<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%91%9B%E8%8E%B1%E7%BE%8E%E5%A5%96.md#1">葛莱美奖</a>（61st Annual GRAMMY Awards）颁奖典礼，担任“Best R&amp;B Album”奖颁奖人的他们，于典礼中表示能够踏上葛莱美的舞台是“梦想成真”，而且他们还会再回来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11037332" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014549100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11037332" title="BTS attends the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014549100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS attends the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E5%BC%B9%E5%B0%91%E5%B9%B4%E5%9B%A2.md#1">防弹少年团</a>（BTS）出席第61届<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%91%9B%E8%8E%B1%E7%BE%8E%E5%A5%96.md#1">葛莱美奖</a>颁奖典礼红毯照。(Neilson Barnard/Getty Images for The Recording Academy)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11037326" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014628100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11037326" title="BTS attends the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014628100707-600x376.jpg" alt="BTS attends the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="376" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E5%BC%B9%E5%B0%91%E5%B9%B4%E5%9B%A2.md#1">防弹少年团</a>（BTS）出席第61届葛莱美奖颁奖典礼红毯照。(Rich Fury/Getty Images for The Recording Academy)</figcaption></figure>
<p>防弹少年团是第一个受邀出席葛莱美奖颁奖典礼并担任颁奖人的韩国歌手，这也是他们继美国告示牌音乐奖（Billboard Music Award）与全美音乐奖（American Music Awards）后，再度登上全球瞩目的国际颁奖典礼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11037329" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014608100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11037329" title="BTS attends the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014608100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS attends the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）出席第61届葛莱美奖颁奖典礼红毯照。(Neilson Barnard/Getty Images for The Recording Academy)</figcaption></figure>
<p>防弹少年团本日以颁奖人身份出席葛莱美奖，队长RM（金南俊）表示：“我们一直梦想站上葛莱美奖的舞台，谢谢所有粉丝让我们的梦想成真，我们会再回来。”接着他们便颁发“Best R&amp;B Album”奖给女歌手H.E.R.。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11037119" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014643100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11037119" title="BTS speak onstage during the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014643100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS speak onstage during the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）于第61届葛莱美奖颁奖典礼颁发Best R&amp;B Album奖。(Kevin Winter/Getty Images for The Recording Academy)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11037335" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014652100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11037335" title="BTS speak onstage during the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014652100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS speak onstage during the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）于第61届葛莱美奖颁奖典礼颁发Best R&amp;B Album奖给“H.E.R.”。 (Kevin Winter/Getty Images for The Recording Academy)</figcaption></figure>
<p>而在颁奖典礼结束后，防弹少年团也透过经纪公司表示，此前他们多次在节目上表示希望能参与葛莱美奖，但真的没想到能踏上这样的舞台，今日他们达成了这个梦想，能够参与葛莱美奖真的感到非常光荣，与全世界的歌手们一同欢乐地参与活动，也让他们感到很开心、很幸福。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11037345" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014726100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11037345" title="BTS speak onstage during the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014726100707-600x410.jpg" alt="BTS speak onstage during the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）于第61届葛莱美奖颁发Best R&amp;B Album奖。 (Kevork Djansezian/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11037339" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014656100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11037339" title="BTS speak onstage during the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014656100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS speak onstage during the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）于第61届葛莱美奖颁发Best R&amp;B Album奖。 (ROBYN BECK/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11037338" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014647100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11037338" title="BTS speak onstage during the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/190211014647100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS speak onstage during the 61st Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）于第61届葛莱美奖颁发Best R&amp;B Album奖。(ROBYN BECK/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，防弹少年团的成员们也于官方推特上传了自己的照片与影片。其中，V（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E6%B3%B0%E4%BA%A8.md#1">金泰亨</a>）在影片中说道：“ARMY（官方粉丝名），谢谢大家帮我们创造了美好的回忆，谢谢你们让我们能来参加葛莱美奖，会努力成为好人、好的歌手，展现更多好的面向给大家。谢谢大家！”</p>
