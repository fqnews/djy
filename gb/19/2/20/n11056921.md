<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>池上庆元宵 传统担饭文化重现浓厚人情味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/74bc476ec2004c202b555abfcd17ee58-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>池上乡公所前张灯结彩，艺阵展演欢庆元宵。 (池上乡公所提供)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年02月20日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾台东报导）台东<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B1%A0%E4%B8%8A%E4%B9%A1.md#1">池上乡</a>庆<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5.md#1">元宵</a>，今年乡公所首次于公所前广场布置大型灯笼墙，张灯结彩喜庆佳节。而宫庙绕境及担饭文化，是池上乡民及游客期待的元宵盛会，元宵绕境今年由池上玉清宫主办，15队宫庙、近百台车阵绕境，阵容庞大，晚间于乡公所前展开热闹的民俗艺阵表演，场面欢欣热闹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11056924" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/aaa7b7b7897e1bd9f5357abb1d11f84c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11056924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/aaa7b7b7897e1bd9f5357abb1d11f84c-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">池上<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5.md#1">元宵</a>节传统的担饭飨宴，让乡民及游客感受传统人情味。 (<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B1%A0%E4%B8%8A%E4%B9%A1.md#1">池上乡</a>公所提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11056925" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/760a8100f034e813a8d04f0e61ff7cab.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11056925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/760a8100f034e813a8d04f0e61ff7cab-450x309.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="309" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">池上元宵绕境队伍。 (池上乡公所提供)</figcaption></figure>
<p>慕名而来的各地游客也充分体验池上人的担饭文化－buffet晚宴，担饭文化是池上元宵节传统，由池上各村轮流主办，今年值年村落为锦园村，晚宴会场设在伯朗大道旁的保安宫，锦园村民以在地食材烹煮免费餐食担饭，既慰劳踩街人员饱餐一顿略作休息，也提供游客品尝池上好食材与浓厚人情味。</p>
<p>晚间6时开始艺阵展演晚会，市区中山路封街，绕境各队伍行经池上乡公所前广场，为乡民及游客祈安，同时各民俗艺阵展开精彩表演。乡长张尧城表示，近年各宫庙为争取艺阵评比的最高荣誉及奖金，纷纷自我要求严守纪律提高素质，展现池上元宵优良传统文化内涵。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11056926" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/a4d561fbe839f910ee60bb2210d358e5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11056926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/a4d561fbe839f910ee60bb2210d358e5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">池上乡艺阵展演欢庆元宵。 (池上乡公所提供)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11056927" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/f12b0c4b082b5dd7fa7d27d43b18166f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11056927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/f12b0c4b082b5dd7fa7d27d43b18166f-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">池上元宵绕境热闹场景。 (池上乡公所提供)</figcaption></figure>
