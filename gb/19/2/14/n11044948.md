<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：悦读，在竹林里</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Fotolia_103287945_Subscription_L-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>悦读，在竹林里<br />
傍着七贤和渊明<br />
竹叶飘落如雨<br />
鸟鸣飘落如雨<br />
世事飘落如雨</p>
<p>隐隐发现<br />
书里仿佛有光<br />
称奇不已<br />
勇敢地走进去<br />
走进书里</p>
<p>循着光走<br />
走过千山万水<br />
不知疲累与艰困<br />
不理纷至沓来的讥嘲<br />
探向茫然不知处</p>
<p>任竹叶飘落<br />
任鸟鸣飘落<br />
任世事飘落<br />
任时光飘落<br />
任青春飘落……</p>
<p>竟然发现那里<br />
有许多花绽放<br />
有许多诗绽放<br />
啊，别是一个天地<br />
现世的一个世外桃源</p>
<p>嗜赏缤纷的众花<br />
嗜赏缤纷的众诗<br />
嗜闻花香与诗香<br />
以致沉迷<br />
以致流连忘返……</p>
<p> <strong>Enjoy reading in the bamboo forest</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>Enjoy reading in the bamboo forest<br />
Accompany with The seven Sages and Yuanming<br />
Bamboo leaves floating down as rains<br />
Bird chirpings floating down as rains<br />
The affairs of the world floating down as rains</p>
<p>I find indistinctly<br />
As if there is the light in the book<br />
It’s very wonderfully<br />
Then enter bravely enter<br />
Into the book</p>
<p>Walk following the light<br />
Walk across thousands mountains and ten thousands of rivers<br />
In spite of tired and difficulty<br />
In spite of ridicule come thick and fast<br />
I search into the ignorance in the dark</p>
<p>Let the bamboo leaves floating down<br />
Let the bird chirpings floating down<br />
Let the affairs of the world floating down<br />
Let the time floating down<br />
Let youth floating down</p>
<p>Unexpectedly, I find<br />
There are many flowers blossoming there<br />
There are many poems blossoming there<br />
Oh, it’s another world<br />
The youyang land of idyllic beauty of modern world</p>
<p>Appreciate greatest numerious of the flowers<br />
Appreciate greatest numerious of the poems<br />
Smell greatest the scent of the flowers and poems<br />
I indulge in them<br />
And to linger on with no thought of leaving@</p>
