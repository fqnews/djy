<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：喜欢</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1402040331492153-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>原野牧场（伊罗逊／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>喜欢那片宽广的牧场<br />
那片宽广的牧场<br />
每天磁吸着我前来</p>
<p>看到那一片青青的牧草<br />
吹着那一阵阵不羁的风<br />
和那些蹦蹦跳跳的小人物<br />
玩那些玩不完的游戏<br />
可以不上学，甚至不吃饭<br />
啊，什么都可以不要</p>
<p>可以毫无顾忌地开怀大笑<br />
可以翻滚在那绿毯似的草坪上<br />
可以在那烂泥里捉青蛙，抓鱼……<br />
可以斗蟋蟀，摔角，赛跑……<br />
而牛儿自己前来，自己吃草，自己回去<br />
啊，放松呀！任心情去驰骋呀！<br />
没有谁人来干扰<br />
再没有比这更自由自在更合心意了<br />
天下有什么比这更好？</p>
<p>起初或许是被迫前来的<br />
后续却是自愿的<br />
而且是整天巴望着的<br />
恨不得每时每刻都在那里</p>
<p>嗯，就是喜欢那片牧场<br />
就是喜欢那样的日子！<br />
请问可以回去吗？</p>
<p><strong> I Prefer</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>I prefer that wide pasture<br />
That wide pasture<br />
Absorbs me every day</p>
<p>Looking at one piece of the green forage grass<br />
Blows with a spell after a spell of untamed wind<br />
As well as the game playing ceaselessly<br />
By the boys who frisk about everywhere<br />
They dare not to go to school, even not take meal<br />
Oh, I need not anything else</p>
<p>I can have a jolly laugh without any trouble<br />
I can roll on the carpet-like green lawn<br />
I can catch frog and fish in the muddy<br />
I can cricket fighting, professional wrestling, racing…<br />
And let the cattle go to by themselves, grazing, and turn back by themselves<br />
Oh, be free! Let mind to be galloping!<br />
There is no anyone to disturb<br />
There is no more freedom for ones mind<br />
Is that any thing better than this around the world?</p>
<p>May it’s compelled at first<br />
But it’s self-willing in succession<br />
And also thirst all the day<br />
Itching to be there every hour and every quarter</p>
<p>Um, I just to prefer this piece of pasture<br />
I just to prefer thus days!<br />
May I go back there?@</p>
