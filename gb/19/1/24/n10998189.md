  <p><strong>完整影片：</strong></p>
  <div class="video_fit_container epoch_player"><div class="player-container" id="player-container-652e395b-5dc5-4e28-41cb-2853c7f9d585" data-id="player-652e395b-5dc5-4e28-41cb-2853c7f9d585"></div></div><a src="//vs.youmaker.com/assets/player/652e395b-5dc5-4e28-41cb-2853c7f9d585?r=16x9&amp;s=1920x1080&amp;d=52&&cat=ncid1251282&api=7&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epochtimes.com%2Fgb%2F19%2F1%2F24%2Fn10998189.md#1"></a>  <p>太古这间没有旺角分店大，但装潢简洁干净，没有太多花巧装饰，可能是地区不同的关系吧！坐进去感觉也舒服，看到有日本人、西人光顾，相信食物质素应该有一定水准！</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10998263" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240106141366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10998263" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240106141366-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">餐厅装潢简洁干净，没有太多花巧装饰。（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>坐下看到左右隔邻都是吃越式汤粉，我没跟着点。喜欢试新及多种类食物的我点了一个椒盐软壳蟹、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%93%81%E8%B6%8A.md#1">品越</a>小食拼盘、芝士焗虎虾拼越式黑芝麻米饼和香杧软壳蟹炒饭。</p>
  <p>椒盐软壳蟹是一个铁煲上，内里刚好放上一只已切开两份的软壳蟹，软壳蟹下垫着沙律菜，上面铺上蟹子，份量够2人分吃，小吃来说份量可以。打开软壳蟹里面竟然充满蟹膏，很惊喜，入口外脆内软，充满蟹肉加椒盐香味，很不错。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10998199" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240106451366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10998199" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240106451366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">椒盐软壳蟹壳蟹里面竟然充满蟹膏，很惊喜，入口外脆内软。（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8A%9Dgi.md#1">米芝Gi</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>小食拼盘包括虾饼、春卷、扎肉、猪颈肉米纸卷和凤爪。春卷皮薄炸得香脆，配上鱼露更好吃。扎肉不像一般<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">越南餐厅</a>的扎肉，内里有一粒粒透明的东西，一问之下原来是软骨，难怪口感更爽。猪颈肉米纸卷米纸很薄里，包着猪颈肉、粉丝、青瓜、生菜还有香料九层塔，够饱肚又清新。凤爪是酸辣无骨凤爪，爱辣的朋友应该喜欢。虾饼反而一般。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10998212" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240106591366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10998212" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240106591366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">品越小食拼盘包括虾饼、春卷、扎肉、猪颈肉米纸卷和凤爪。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10998218" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240107091366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10998218" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240107091366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">扎肉与一般<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">越南餐厅</a>的不同，内里有一粒粒软骨，口感更爽。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10998224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240104381366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10998224" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240104381366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">猪颈肉米纸卷米纸够饱肚又清新。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>香杧软壳蟹炒饭，是饭面上放上一只软壳蟹，饭粒粒粒分明，也有很多杧果粒在内。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10998225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240105011366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10998225" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240105011366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">香杧软壳蟹炒饭，饭粒粒粒分明，也有很多杧果粒在内。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>最后上台的是要等25分钟的芝士焗虎虾拼越式黑芝麻米饼，上来先看到两块比头还大的黑芝麻米饼，然后另一碟是5只被满满芝士浸着的虎虾，侍应教这个食法是先将黑芝麻米饼搣开，沾上芝士吃。我看到芝士不像西式般杰身，而是比较稀像汁一样，芝士里面混了火腿丝，为了入味虎虾已经去壳。我先试吃黑芝麻米饼，一吃便吃出黑芝麻没有炒香过，因此有一阵“行”味，可幸沾上芝士后被浓郁的芝士盖过就吃不出来了！而且芝士略咸，我发觉越食越咸，之后一直猛喝水。虎虾不够爽口，可能因为焗很久的关系。到最后芝士吃完了，还剩下一片黑芝麻米饼，个人觉得这个试一次就够了。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_10998236" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240107281366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10998236" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240107281366-600x414.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">芝士焗虎虾拼越式黑芝麻米饼。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10998250" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240107511366.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10998250" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240107511366-600x431.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="431" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">虎虾为了入味已经去壳，可能因为焗很久，不够爽口。（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_10998265" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240105421366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10998265 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/01/1901240105421366-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（米芝Gi提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>**********************</p>
