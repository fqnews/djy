<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>酸甜好滋味 快乐做橘酱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/f0ec49b3b1669c0f326ee504691ba2d4-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>大家认真切橘子。（林宝云／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年03月18日讯】（大纪元记者林宝云台湾新竹报导）冬季是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A9%98%E5%AD%90.md#1">橘子</a>盛产的季节，酸酸甜甜的滋味，总是让人忍不住一口接一口。然而，不管怎么吃，似乎总有吃不完的橘子，不是朋友赠送，就是过年买多的。该如何处理呢？</p>
<p>阿钦家的仓库就有不少“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%81%AB%E7%83%A7%E6%9F%91.md#1">小火烧柑</a>”，他们俩夫妻认为那些又小又丑，不好意思拿出去卖，曾经想要送给慈善机构，但该机构不太领情，让他们觉得挫折，从此也不敢送人了。日前笔者和前同事提到此事，同事和先生在北埔乡开了间咖啡书店“拾乐坊”，提供我们一个好点子：可以用那些又小又丑的桶柑，教大家做果酱。</p>
<p>拾乐坊老板娘高美玉说，自己制作的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A9%98%E9%85%B1.md#1">橘酱</a>安全卫生，没有添加任何防腐剂或糖精，建议一周内食用完毕。使用前、后要放置冰箱冷藏。完成的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A9%98%E5%AD%90.md#1">橘子</a>果酱，除了直接抹在吐司上，也可搭配优格，或是用热水冲泡，或加入红茶中就变成浓郁香气的橘子果茶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11121553" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/3981bc96ab163a9ba926529f403743ca.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11121553 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/3981bc96ab163a9ba926529f403743ca-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">拾乐坊老板陈万成与老板娘高美玉及流浪狗。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>●准备器材：</strong></span></p>
<p>煮锅<br />
勺子<br />
卡式炉（加瓦斯罐）<br />
抹布<br />
小刀（切橘肉用）<br />
砧板<br />
干净的玻璃瓶数个<br />
围裙<br />
盛物容器3个（装洗净的桶柑、装切过的橘肉、装桶柑皮）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11121562" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/0e5837f902b35c80a81a4eda11aa5905.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11121562 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/0e5837f902b35c80a81a4eda11aa5905-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">需要的器材。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>●步骤：</strong></span></p>
<p>1.橘子洗净后剥好，要将白色纤维和籽去除。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11121551" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/42f64d1ac877add5048d2924a5dd55d2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11121551 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/42f64d1ac877add5048d2924a5dd55d2-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">先把橘子洗干净。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11121557" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/5a326844105a75172e09e878290e0dbb.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11121557 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/5a326844105a75172e09e878290e0dbb-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">洗净的橘子。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11121549" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/bbd78bd9104cf7e1729014143da5c457.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11121549 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/bbd78bd9104cf7e1729014143da5c457-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大家认真切橘子。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11121561" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1c9eb380eb58ac7550d6cd4e3965560a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11121561 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/1c9eb380eb58ac7550d6cd4e3965560a-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">剥好的橘子要将白色纤维和籽去除（之后再打成汁）。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.把橘肉先切丁，再继续切更小块，放入锅内一起熬（果酱内有颗粒更有口感）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11121556" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/9a2ce2d92637f0c989c95152d9fb31c9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11121556 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/9a2ce2d92637f0c989c95152d9fb31c9-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">把橘肉切更小块放入锅内一起熬，果酱内有颗粒更有口感。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 把汁倒入锅内加糖，用中火熬煮（要不断的搅拌，时间约1小时）。橘肉与糖的比例为10：1。甜度可随自己喜好增减。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11121555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/246c784da136d193f27448f1e731f936.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11121555 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/246c784da136d193f27448f1e731f936-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">把汁倒入锅内加糖，用中火熬煮，要不断的搅拌。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 待煮到果酱越来越浓稠，果胶完全释放出来后，拌匀装入消毒过的玻璃瓶即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11121558" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/81d1631b838296c1c1da0d0cc5aa9120.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11121558 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/81d1631b838296c1c1da0d0cc5aa9120-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">锅内黄橙橙的泡沫。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11121554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/5c12d6a7b59c6554ef12e0f8531a0752.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11121554 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/5c12d6a7b59c6554ef12e0f8531a0752-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">完成的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A9%98%E9%85%B1.md#1">橘酱</a>。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>※小提醒：</strong>制程中，最花时间的就是搅拌，需要有耐心，若焦掉就功亏一篑了。</p>
<p>看着完成的新鲜美味果酱，大家很有成就感。但看着地上还堆满一箱箱的橘子，让人发愁，怎么办呢？不能再退回去吧？大家集思广益，最后每人认养一箱，一起送给邻近的弱势机构及学校师生分享，无形中又做了一桩善事。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11121550" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/f44c7325d2fd1054df9a05adca7bd16f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11121550 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/f44c7325d2fd1054df9a05adca7bd16f-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">北埔乡卫生局特别前来宣导食安方面知识。（林宝云／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
