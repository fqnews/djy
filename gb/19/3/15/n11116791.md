<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>配送员见1尺深雪中“有件黑外套” 直觉让他救下3只幼猫</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49671111_1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>一片白茫茫大地中，他忽然看到一个奇怪的黑点。待靠近时，他意识到需要冲出驾驶座，勇敢向“那东西”走去——其中像是有小生命。（Courtesy of Tanya Wight／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年03月20日讯】（大纪元记者林宜君编译报导）配送员日夜穿梭在大街小巷中，将货物包裹按时交递，人们很容易就忽略了这些无名英雄的存在。然而，他们却能在紧要关头化身超人，完成超级救援任务。</p>
<p>一念之间，平凡的加拿大牛奶配送员拯救了三只差点冻僵的幼猫，让它们的生命没有在寒冬中凋零。</p>
<p><span style="color: #008080;"><strong>半埋雪中的“黑色外套”</strong></span></p>
<p>纽芬兰西部的一场暴风雪中，克莱德．康普顿（Clyde Compton）正在运送牛奶。白茫茫大地中，他忽然看到一个奇怪的黑点；待靠近时，他意识到需要冲出驾驶座，勇敢向“那东西”走去——其中像是有小生命。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125328" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/Clyde-Compton.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125328 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/Clyde-Compton-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Clyde Compton)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“当我看到它们时，它们就像是一件外套。”康普顿接受动物新闻媒体“The Dodo”采访时说，“但是看第二眼，我知道那是什么了。”</p>
<p>三只孱弱无助的黑色幼猫正在雪地里挤成一团，周围没有爪印，显示它们可能被某个开车司机扔弃在路上。</p>
<p>“从大卡车上看下去，我可以看到它们。”康普顿表示，“但其他人可能无法看到，毕竟他们正开在积了1尺深新雪的路面上。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125324" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49671111_10156564813340709_8475476050023809024_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125324 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49671111_10156564813340709_8475476050023809024_n-450x451.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="451" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Clyde Compton)</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="color: #008080;"><strong>浪浪的最佳守护者</strong></span></p>
<p>康普顿迅速捞起小猫，带着它们驶回近20公里外的家中，他的妻子和女儿已经在家里准备好温暖这些小浪猫，奋力帮它们与死神拔河。</p>
<p>这次大雪中的惊心巧遇并非康普顿第一次接触浪猫救援，他家中的四只猫狗，全都是由善心人士帮忙救助的幸运生命。</p>
<p>除了先为小猫保暖，康普顿也与“鹿湖小猫救援组织”（<span style="color: #008080;"><a style="color: #008080;" href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/164056717133339/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Deer Lake Kitten Rescue，DLKR</a></span>）取得联系，该组织能帮忙康普顿一家联系兽医帮小猫复健，将它们放在寄养家庭，随后再转移到认养中心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125323" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49614668_10156564813365709_4676229893598478336_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125323 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49614668_10156564813365709_4676229893598478336_n-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Clyde Compton)</figcaption></figure>
<p>该组织志工谭雅．怀特（Tanya Wight）说：“小猫的身躯被冰雪覆盖，但还是挤在一起努力求生??它们一定是被人载来抛弃的。这种对动物的残忍实在令人无法理解。”</p>
<p>命悬一线的三个小可怜总算脱离了生命危险，还各自有了新名字：短毛公猫叫做“乱乱”（Flurry），两只长毛和短毛母猫分别叫做“抖抖”（Shiver）与“暴风雨”（Stormy）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125325" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49674062_10156568680080709_5756430094281211904_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125325 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49674062_10156568680080709_5756430094281211904_n-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">短毛公猫“乱乱”（Flurry）。(Courtesy of Tanya Wight)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11125326" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49753054_10156568680225709_8570007459870015488_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125326 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49753054_10156568680225709_8570007459870015488_n-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">短毛母猫“暴风雨”（Stormy）。(Courtesy of Tanya Wight)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这三只大难不死的幸运猫儿普遍体重不足，兽医也用抗生素帮抖抖治疗呼吸道感染。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125327" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49947790_10156568679995709_837082498855862272_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125327 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49947790_10156568679995709_837082498855862272_n-450x408.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="408" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“抖抖”（Shiver）。(Courtesy of Tanya Wight)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11125400" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49828887_10156568680320709_1727128799239208960_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11125400" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/49828887_10156568680320709_1727128799239208960_n-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Tanya Wight)</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然三只小猫经历了外人难以想像的磨难，但两个半月大的它们都非常友善亲人，也很活泼好动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125386" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/50053166_10156585178510709_4726769387950833664_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11125386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/50053166_10156585178510709_4726769387950833664_n-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">谭雅怀抱猫咪。(Courtesy of Tanya Wight)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11125387" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/50230647_10156585178700709_1727209596163981312_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11125387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/50230647_10156585178700709_1727209596163981312_n-450x688.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="688" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“抖抖”在谭雅先生怀中。(Courtesy of Tanya Wight)</figcaption></figure>
<p>谭雅接手照养小猫后，乱乱、抖抖与暴风雨变得更有活力，“它们已经搬进豪华套房（也就是我的空余房间），因此会有更多的活动空间”。</p>
<p>她也告诉大纪元，“三喵”近日已被转移到“西纽芬兰防止动物虐待协会”（<span style="color: #008080;"><a style="color: #008080;" href="https://nlwestspca.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">NL West SPCA</a></span>），并已全部被正式收养，去到了新家。如此温暖的结果，真要感谢配送员康普顿的细心，以及他与谭雅两个家庭的爱心！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11125401" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/snow-kittens.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11125401" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/snow-kittens.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="489" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of Tanya Wight)</figcaption></figure>
