  <p>驻芝台北经济文化办事处黄钧耀处长、新闻组组长李唯诚、侨教中心主任王伟赞、副主任赖贞利出席宴会。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_11099825" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11099825 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/dd85e01eda972f5078cbd8517f0e1544-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄钧耀处长、徐曙明会长与贵宾合影。（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E8%81%94%E4%BC%9A.md#1">台联会</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>黄钧耀处长感谢台联会过去一年对中华民国政府的支持及对团结侨心的贡献，并祝大家万事如意、身体健康。王伟赞主任、赖贞利副主任也送上新年祝福。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_11099822" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11099822 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/3dddc6e01d5af0fd2203be2ba9587f60-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">驻<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E5%8A%A0%E5%93%A5.md#1">芝加哥</a>台北经济文化办事处处长黄钧耀致词。（台联会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>台联会长徐曙明欢迎理事顾问们与会，感谢黄处长带来美酒，也感谢赖志鸿连络餐馆、王庆敏带来猜谜节目及手工包子做猜谜奖品。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_11099826" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11099826 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/5d8676c263bcd680b36dd5129f87afb0-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">徐曙明会长致欢迎词。（台联会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_11099829" style="width: 384px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11099829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/db99b77b7b33d8839c44357e67b11e39-450x468.jpg" alt="" width="384" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">王庆敏带着Hello Kitty 拜年。（台联会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>徐会长并介绍当天的贵宾，包括前侨务委员长吴英毅及夫人、前加拿大周莉音副代表及夫婿黄登亮医师、101岁的赵鸿光将军，台大校友会会长谢邦基及多位校友均来参加盛会。</p>
  <p>享受美味佳肴后，由赖志鸿及郭正崇主持有奖猜谜游戏，奖品是王庆敏专门制作的手工包子，80个包子让“人人有奖拿”，众人饭后开怀大笑。</p>
  <p>当天黄处长及王主任提供红包抽奖，该会顾问王全民及来宾赵妈妈、傅家瑛均幸运中奖。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_11099823" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11099823 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/70a623dc4b18809bb27615ef6ec5c6ca-600x516.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="516" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄钧耀发红包给幸运中奖的两位女士。（台联会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_11099828" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-small wp-image-11099828" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/9b2a129243693377514cd13cef0673f5-300x414.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="414" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">王伟赞主任发红包给幸运中奖的王全明顾问。（台联会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>新春聚餐在大家合唱“恭喜发财”“贺新年”及发包子的欢呼声中完满成功，并合影留念。 ◇</p>
  <figure id="attachment_11099824" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11099824 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/780cc30b806f8c7857c3c83f54918302-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄钧耀处长，王伟赞主任、徐曙明会长与来宾敬酒。（台联会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>责任编辑：温文清</p>
