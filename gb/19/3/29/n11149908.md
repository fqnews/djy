<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>甘蔗地里豹崽哭叫妈妈 好心人巧让母子团圆</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/Wildlife-SOS-vet-Dr.-Ajay-Deshmukh-carrying-out-aan-on-site-examination-of-the-leopard-cub-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>高级兽医德赫姆（Ajay Deshmukh）在检查豹宝宝。(Courtesy of <a href="http://wildlifesos.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wildlife SOS
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Video-link.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10743778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/Video-link.jpg" alt="" width="578" b="65" /></a></p>
<p>【大纪元2019年03月30日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳编译报导）多亏一群好心人及时伸出援手，而且很有智慧和经验，不然，一只<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B0%E5%BA%A6%E8%B1%B9.md#1">印度豹</a>幼崽怕是很难再有机会和妈妈团聚。</p>
<p>本月初的一天，印度马哈拉施特拉邦（Maharashtra）一群农民正在地里收割甘蔗，快要收工时，它们发现地上躺着一个毛茸茸的小东西。</p>
<p>凑近一看，美丽的毛皮斑斑点点，原来是一只2个月大的豹崽。在保证小豹子安全方面，这些农民看来很有经验，他们马上联系森林管理部门官员，也叫<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%87%8E%E7%94%9F%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E6%95%91%E6%8F%B4.md#1">野生动物救援</a>团体“<ahref="https://wildlifesos.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wildlife SOS</a>”赶来支援。</p>
<p>由于农田和城市与豹子的栖息地相交叠，当地人不时会遇见豹子。Wildlife SOS已协助救援了该地区很多野生动物。该组织在新闻稿中说，“扩大农田和砍伐森林，导致这一地区人类与豹子的冲突越来越多。”</p>
<p>目前是豹子产崽季节，怀孕的豹子经常在甘蔗地里寻找庇护所，可它们不知道，现在也正是收割甘蔗的季节。</p>
<p>“在收割前和收割季节，人们会更频繁看到豹子，或与之发生冲突，因为茂密高大的甘蔗林为猎豹生养幼崽提供了一片便利的栖息地。”Wildlife SOS联合创始人兼首席执行官萨亚纳拉扬（Kartick Satyanarayan）说。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11149931" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/leopard-cub.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11149931" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/leopard-cub-600x337.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;http://wildlifesos.org/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Wildlife SOS&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="http://wildlifesos.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wildlife SOS</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>救援人员很清楚，豹崽与妈妈多分开一刻，母豹就多一分紧张不安。于是他们迅速看查看了幼崽的健康状况，然后开始制定计划让母子团聚。</p>
<p>高级兽医德赫姆（Ajay Deshmukh）检查豹宝宝后发现，小豹健康状态极佳，完全可以第一时间还给妈妈。</p>
<p><center><a style="border: none; overflow: hidden;" src=></a><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/attcqb2944/www/master/t/ntdtv/facebook.jpg" ><h3 align=center>(facebook视频或图片.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/post.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fwildlifesosindia%2Fphotos%2Fa.76535498525%2F10156282950258526%2F%3Ftype%3D3&amp;width=600&amp;show_text=false&amp;height=497&amp;appId" width="600" b="497" frameborder="0" scrolling="no"></a></center>&nbsp;</p>
<p>于是，他们物色好了一个放置豹崽的好地方，设置了一个隐藏摄像头，以便观察豹妈妈是否回来寻找幼崽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11149929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/The-team-safely-placed-the-cub-in-a-crate.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11149929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/The-team-safely-placed-the-cub-in-a-crate-600x396.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="http://wildlifesos.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wildlife SOS</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/The-leopard-cub-was-placed-in-a-crate-so-it-could-be-reunited-with-its-mother.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11149926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/The-leopard-cub-was-placed-in-a-crate-so-it-could-be-reunited-with-its-mother-600x398.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="398" /></a></p>
<p>果然，在夜色掩护下，一只成年豹在附近转来转去寻找着什么。</p>
<p>“晚上9:30左右，一只母豹终于从旁边的树林中现身。”德赫姆说。镜头中可见，当母豹意识到塑料盒子里有东西的时候，忽然立地不动了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11149928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/The-mother-leopard-finds-her-lost-cub.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11149928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/The-mother-leopard-finds-her-lost-cub-600x359.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;http://wildlifesos.org/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Wildlife SOS&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="359" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="http://wildlifesos.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wildlife SOS</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>之后，它做了猫科动物最典型的举动——把塑料盒子打翻在地。当盖子上压的石块滚落一旁、盖子被掀掉时，小豹子开始一点点往外爬了。</p>
<p>母子重逢一刻，双方都有点吃惊，不过很快，它们就明白了对方是谁。只见母豹一口将幼崽衔在嘴里，开心地一路小跑回到了树林中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11149927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/The-leopardess-walking-away-with-her-cub.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11149927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/03/The-leopardess-walking-away-with-her-cub-600x545.jpg" alt="(Courtesy of &lt;a href=&quot;http://wildlifesos.org/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Wildlife SOS&lt;/a&gt;)" width="600" b="545" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of <ahref="http://wildlifesos.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wildlife SOS</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们可以想见，它发现小家伙安然无恙时有多么宽慰！”德赫姆表示，对于豹子来说，在初生的前两年和妈妈一起学习生存技能是至关重要的。</p>
<p>Wildlife SOS创立14年来，德赫姆已经参与过数不清的豹子救援行动。他还说，“这样的救援行动在我们心中占有非常特殊的地位，因为我们知道，这只幼崽可以继续在野外让母亲养大，有机会在野外自由过生活，这对我们是巨大的安慰与回报。”</p>
