<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>《马前课》解卦新探 第一课天意难违</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/3b4401d2b3eaefad96785a2455f9338c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>月夜 (pixabay)
</h6>
<hr>
<figure id="attachment_11256478" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/549267680832aaac1d824f495c6968cb.png"><img class="wp-image-11256478 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/549267680832aaac1d824f495c6968cb-600x393.png" alt="" width="600" b="393" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">颐卦（道奇博士／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【当时已知朝代历史】</p>
<p>黄帝—<span lang="en-US">&gt;</span>夏—<span lang="en-US">&gt;?</span>商—<span lang="en-US">&gt;?</span>周—<span lang="en-US">&gt;?</span>春秋<span lang="en-US">/</span>战国—<span lang="en-US">&gt;</span>秦—<span lang="en-US">&gt;</span>汉—<span lang="en-US">&gt;</span>三国分立—<span lang="en-US">&gt;?</span>？</p>
<p>【设想场景】</p>
<p>蜀汉五出祈山、无功而返，第六次再度北伐曹魏，司马懿隔着渭水、坚守不出战。成败未卜、情急之下，做为当事人——诸葛孔明诚心占卦，求测目前及此后天下大局。首先他想预测<b>蜀汉</b>能否成功伐魏吞吴、一统天下？</p>
<figure id="attachment_11256482" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/9c9d89cc562aa1c05b3b18ac3944a8ee.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11256482" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/9c9d89cc562aa1c05b3b18ac3944a8ee-450x295.png" alt="" width="450" b="295" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E5%89%8D%E8%AF%BE.md#1">马前课</a>第一课颐卦（道奇博士／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>【课卦】?第一课　○●●●●○　中下 ??阳阴阴 阴阴阳 在卦为颐<span lang="en-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p>《易经》颐卦卦辞爻辞：</p>
<p>山雷颐<span lang="en-US">—</span><b>颐、贞吉、观颐、自求口实。</b></p>
<p><b>初九：舍尔灵龟、观我朵颐、凶。</b></p>
<p><b>六二：颠颐、拂经，于丘颐、征凶。</b></p>
<p><b>六三：拂颐、贞凶，十年勿用、无攸利。</b></p>
<p><b>六四：颠颐、吉，虎视眈眈，其欲逐逐、无咎。</b></p>
<p><b>六五：拂经、居贞吉，不可涉大川。</b></p>
<p><b>上九：由颐、厉吉、利涉大川。</b>（古文看不懂、没关系，暂时放下，耐心等待笔者引导解读，我们只专看“重点”。）</p>
<p><b>【</b><b>第一</b><b>课卦资讯】</b></p>
<p><span lang="en-US">1.?</span>卦象：山下雷声，不动如山。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">2.?</span>游魂卦：有去无回。（颐卦在巽宫系列八卦中排序?<span lang="en-US">7</span>，统称“游魂卦”）</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">3.?</span>卦辞：自求口实：自求多福，口字不成回字。</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">4.?</span>第五爻爻辞：行拂乱所为，不可涉大川。（军国大事看第<span lang="en-US">5</span>爻、“九五之尊”）</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">5.?</span>卦象图：阴爻居九五之尊阳位。（第<span lang="en-US">1</span>、<span lang="en-US">3</span>、<span lang="en-US">5</span>爻为阳位，“<span lang="en-US"><strong>– –</strong>?</span>”为阴爻数六、“—”为阳爻数九）</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">6.?</span>巽卦属木表鸡、巽宫系列变卦成颐卦（如何看衰变？看附图——《后天卦》巽宫变卦），君王“呆如木鸡”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11256498" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/ed20a601-58d0-447b-9aec-0c45dce60ed0.png"><img class="wp-image-11256498 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/ed20a601-58d0-447b-9aec-0c45dce60ed0-600x397.png" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图 ：《后天卦》巽宫变卦（道奇博士／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span lang="en-US">7.?</span>六五爻变卦成风雷益，益卦损上益下。（<strong>第</strong><strong><span lang="en-US">5</span></strong><strong>爻如果是阴爻、叫做“六”五，若是阳爻就称之“九”五</strong>）</p>
<p><span lang="en-US">8.?</span>由已知求未知——丞相食少事烦，岂可久乎。</p>
<h2>【第一课卦原断】</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>无力回天、</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>鞠躬尽瘁、</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>阴居阳拂、</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>八千女鬼。</strong></span></p>
<p>【第一课断卦新解】</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>蜀汉能否成功伐魏吞吴、一统天下？</strong></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11256496" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/9797583941b71539b1a04c8144d611f5.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11256496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/9797583941b71539b1a04c8144d611f5-450x453.png" alt="" width="450" b="453" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第一课的本卦、互卦和变卦（道奇博士／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 454px;"></figure>
<p>仓颉造字六书遵，文字内涵如有神；</p>
<p>自求口实惠不至，卦叹“回”难名游魂；</p>
<p>互卦（如上第一课互卦图）得出坤逆“天”，变卦风雷益上损；</p>
<p>（<span style="color: #800000;"><b>“互卦”看出事情发生的过程</b><strong>：</strong><strong>天亡蜀汉也</strong></span>）</p>
<p>木鸡君王“无”能“力”，拼命相父瘁辛勤；</p>
<p>敌军坚守挂免战，蜀兵雷动讥女人；（事实符合卦象——司马懿坚守不战“不动如山”，孔明送女衣给他，蜀兵在阵前嘲笑曹魏他们不是男人，“八千女鬼”何谓？合成一字就是“魏”。）</p>
<p>食少烦多岂可久，事必躬亲累坏身；</p>
<p>“鞠躬尽瘁”言本分，死而后已语成真；</p>
<p>爻辞不可涉大川，出师未捷哭英魂；</p>
<p>之后蜀主亲小人，随即阿斗安乐臣；（阴爻居第<span lang="en-US">5</span>爻九五阳位）</p>
<p>阴居阳位在卦象，八千女鬼“大魏”尊。（四阴爻八小段、千数成大）</p>
