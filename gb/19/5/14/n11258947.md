<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>创意转行开启新人生 “气球天使”舞热台湾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/48418857_2089862677718621_5590756455302037504_n-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>获封“气球天使”，“台湾首席气球表演达人”的陈荧家，凭借对气球的热爱与努力自学，创作出各种可爱造型的作品，其气球表演艺术走红台湾街头。（<a target="_blank" ="https://www.facebook.com/pg/balloon.mrevaeox/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">气球天使/脸书
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年05月24日讯】（大纪元记者李潇综合报导）一位台湾全职<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A1%A5%E6%95%99%E4%B8%9A%E8%80%81%E5%B8%88.md#1">补教业老师</a>，为照顾2个孩子，选择以折气球表演来补贴家用，不想竟无心插柳，月收入增加近5倍；获封“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%94%E7%90%83%E5%A4%A9%E4%BD%BF.md#1">气球天使</a>”、“台湾首席气球表演达人”的同时，也开启快乐人生新阶段。</p>
<p>很多朋友欣赏过街头折气球，但您欣赏过“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%94%E7%90%83%E5%A4%A9%E4%BD%BF.md#1">气球天使</a>”的表演吗？身材颀长的她，穿着绚丽的公主裙，一边翩翩起舞，一边娴熟地将手中的长条气球轻快折叠，变化出各种可爱俏皮的卡通造型……</p>
<p>这位“气球天使”就是享誉台湾的“街头艺人”<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E8%8D%A7%E5%AE%B6.md#1">陈荧家</a>，很多喜爱她表演的朋友，亲切称之为“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%AE%B6.md#1">家家</a>”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11259083" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/46998197_2052895234748699_8585072542992039936_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11259083 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/46998197_2052895234748699_8585072542992039936_n-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美丽的气球天使，备受台湾各界观众欢迎。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/pg/balloon.mrevaeox/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">气球天使/脸书</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11259031" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/48957879_2089862741051948_8945877837697515520_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11259031 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/48957879_2089862741051948_8945877837697515520_n-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">万千妙思在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E8%8D%A7%E5%AE%B6.md#1">陈荧家</a>一双巧手的运作下，创造出各种可爱的气球作品。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/pg/balloon.mrevaeox/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">气球天使/脸书</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每逢节假日，在台湾的旅游景点，都会看到<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%AE%B6.md#1">家家</a>忙碌的身影。在搭建得宛如童话世界的舞台上，家家以《美女与野兽》中“贝儿”公主的经典形象出场，伴随着欢乐的乐曲起舞，手中气球瞬间变化出一朵朵盛开的“玫瑰”，梦幻般的表演赢得阵阵热烈掌声。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11259160" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/38126015_1889387801099444_4524373344799162368_o.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11259160 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/38126015_1889387801099444_4524373344799162368_o-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">气球天使与育幼院的小朋友在一起。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/pg/balloon.mrevaeox/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">气球天使/脸书</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11259053" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/52595335_2165880133450208_2457484921317359616_o.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11259053 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/52595335_2165880133450208_2457484921317359616_o-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">气球在灵巧的玉手下，变化成美丽的花朵，备受小朋友欢迎。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/pg/balloon.mrevaeox/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">气球天使/脸书</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>家家她原本是一名全职<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A1%A5%E6%95%99%E4%B8%9A%E8%80%81%E5%B8%88.md#1">补教业老师</a>，为高中及国中学生进行数学科补教，平均月收入约45,000台币（约1400美金）左右。后来，家家历经恋爱、结婚、生子的“人生三部曲”，成为2个孩子的妈妈，为了照顾幼小的宝贝，只好将补课时间大幅减少，仅仅选择傍晚至夜间的时段，同时改教压力较小的国小低年级课程。</p>
<p>这样的改变也令家家的收入大幅缩水；为维持家计，她开始思考新的工作方式；同时，为了拉近与小学生的心灵距离，她想选择一些更亲切的教学道具。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11259072" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/40039776_1930148513690039_3699290490647085056_o-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11259072 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/40039776_1930148513690039_3699290490647085056_o-1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">家家选择色彩鲜艳、造型可爱的气球玩具，送给小朋友。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/pg/balloon.mrevaeox/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">气球天使/脸书</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当家家在网路上无意中看到那些色彩鲜艳、造型各异的气球时，不由眼前一亮，一个新的创意闪现在脑中：“我要学习折气球。”为了折出漂亮的气球，家家开始大量阅览相关书籍、视频和图像教程，没想到越折越爱，最后竟然折气球“成瘾”！</p>
<p>皇天不负有心人，渐渐地，她不仅能娴熟地折出各种常见的卡通人物，还可根据灵感自行创新。2年后，家家特地去考取了“街头艺人”的证照，精湛的折叠技巧与全新的表演风格令她一举成名，受到各界民众喜爱，并获得“气球天使”的美誉。</p>
<p>随着知名度提升，她开始接洽商演，并在节庆、婚宴、尾牙和企划活动中登台。在此过程中，家家的收入经常达到月赚25万台币（约8000美元），比先前多了近5倍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11259028" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/46950137_2052895928081963_2309125468034433024_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11259028 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/46950137_2052895928081963_2309125468034433024_n-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">家家在商演活动中受到小朋友的喜爱。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/pg/balloon.mrevaeox/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">气球天使/脸书</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11259099" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/47110673_2052896014748621_1562926865163747328_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11259099 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/47110673_2052896014748621_1562926865163747328_n-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">家家化身公主，受到小朋友的喜爱。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/pg/balloon.mrevaeox/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">气球天使/脸书</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>家家对气球表演的热爱，让女儿<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%82%B1%E5%AE%A5%E5%A7%97.md#1">邱宥姗</a>自小耳濡目染，继承了妈妈的表演艺术天赋。现在女儿也经常在寒暑假期学业空档时，化身“小天使”与妈妈一起登台，会在表演后和妈妈切磋技艺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11259032" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/48960165_2089862711051951_1469989888911736832_n.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11259032 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/48960165_2089862711051951_1469989888911736832_n-600x450.jpg" alt="女儿邱宥姗继承了妈妈的表演天赋，化身“小天使”与妈妈一起登台表演。" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">女儿<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%82%B1%E5%AE%A5%E5%A7%97.md#1">邱宥姗</a>继承了妈妈的表演天赋，化身“小天使”与妈妈一起登台表演。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/pg/balloon.mrevaeox/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">气球天使/脸书</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于折气球表演，邱宥姗表示：“每份工作都会有它辛苦的地方，虽然我没有比妈妈（表演得）好，可是我应该还可以更进步一些。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11259108" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/53472607_2191343750903846_3461590778394968064_o.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11259108 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/53472607_2191343750903846_3461590778394968064_o-600x450.jpg" alt="两位天使在活动中为观众表演。（气球天使/脸书）" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">两位“天使”在活动中为观众表演。（<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/pg/balloon.mrevaeox/photos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">气球天使/脸书</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于“小天使”的表演，观众们则说：“将来一定雏凤清于老凤声啦！”“很期待小天使未来更亮眼的表现……”热情的粉丝并送上祝福，祝福这对“天使”母女“未来表现更成功，可以走出台湾，走向世界！”</p>
