<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>MOMOLAND《I&#8217;m So Hot》摘公信榜单日冠军</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/05/181023024508100649-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩国人气女团MOMOLAND参与第55届大钟奖电影节资料照。（全景林／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
