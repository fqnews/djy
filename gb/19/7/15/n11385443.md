<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>旧屋翻新 室内防水工法8要领</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190716_shouhuihsu_waterproof_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>浴室磁砖填平缝隙可防潮，避免累积污垢。（图/棠韵室内设计提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E5%B1%8B%E7%BF%BB%E6%96%B0.md#1">旧屋翻新</a>最常遇见的就是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E6%B0%B4.md#1">防水</a>问题，老屋面临外墙龟裂、窗户边角渗水、厕所墙面壁癌、厕所<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E5%9D%AA.md#1">地坪</a>漏水至楼下层等等渗水问题，本文从实做经验探讨<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A7%E5%B1%8B%E7%BF%BB%E6%96%B0.md#1">旧屋翻新</a>防水工法的要点。</p>
<p><strong>一、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9C%B0%E5%9D%AA.md#1">地坪</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%98%B2%E6%B0%B4.md#1">防水</a>需做二次</strong></p>
<p>原则上地坪拆除见底后，隔天必需于全室的地坪做二次复合式弹性乳胶防水材涂布施作。因为旧屋翻新基本上窗户会全拆换，在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%96%BD%E5%B7%A5.md#1">施工</a>中遇见气候变化刮风下雨实属正常，就算窗口用帆布遮盖，也无法全面防止雨水溅入室内；若遇连续多日雨季、泥作砌砖前需浇水时，更容易产生渗水到下楼层的工程问题，所以建议全室施作二次防水，以防止地坪打底前产生渗漏问题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11389298" style="width: 469px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190715_shouhuihsu_waterproof_01.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-11389298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190715_shouhuihsu_waterproof_01.jpg" alt="" width="469" b="352" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图为旧屋拆除地坪见底。（图棠韵室内设计提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11389292" style="width: 493px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190717_shouhiuhsu_waterproof_02.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-11389292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190717_shouhiuhsu_waterproof_02-493x400.jpg" alt="" width="493" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图为经过防水处理的地面。（图/棠韵室内设计提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>二、水电配管</strong></p>
<p>水电进场配管，需按照<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%96%BD%E5%B7%A5.md#1">施工</a>图及法规放样。设计师需每日于现场与各工种人员一同会勘，讨论放样及调整施作材料、尺寸、高差、数量、泄水坡度等事项。为使放样精准，使用雷射仪、水平垂直校准器、水线、米尺、等必备工具。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11385481" style="width: 1114px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_02.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-11385481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_02-1114x400.jpg" alt="" width="1114" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图棠韵室内设计提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>三、泥作打底</strong></p>
<p>水泥打底前，需使用砂轮机、吸尘器、不銹钢桃镘抹刀、铁锤、凿子、扫把等相关器具进行整地清洁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11385485" style="width: 1150px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_03.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-11385485" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_03-1150x400.jpg" alt="" width="1150" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">水泥打底工程。（图棠韵室内设计提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>四、第一道防水工程</strong></p>
<p>泥作地坪进行第一道复合乳胶防水底漆涂料的打底工程。范围包括厕所、厨房、阳台与使用给水、排水区块之地坪，壁面防水打底高度施作于梁下。<strong>?</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11385489" style="width: 913px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_04.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-11385489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_04-913x400.jpg" alt="" width="913" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第一道防水工程。（图棠韵室内设计提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>五、落水头及孔洞防水处理</strong></p>
<p>地坪落水头置入专利管边防水套盖器，另外，孔洞处涂布防水胶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11385490" style="width: 1135px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_05.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-11385490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_05-1135x400.jpg" alt="" width="1135" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">地坪落水头及孔洞防水处理。（图棠韵室内设计提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>六、第二道防水工程</strong></p>
<p>第二道防水工程，包括壁面、地坪墙缝、给排水区块墙面，防水网壁面高度约15公分为一个基准，交接处需连续转折不可断接处理，尾端相接处请叠加10公分为一单位。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11385494" style="width: 1152px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_06.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-11385494" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_06-1152x400.jpg" alt="" width="1152" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第二道防水工程。（图棠韵室内设计提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>七、第三道工程</strong></p>
<p>第三道抗正负防水涂料施作于墙壁与地坪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11385593" style="width: 935px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_07.jpg"><img class="size-medium_vertical wp-image-11385593" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_07-935x400.jpg" alt="" width="935" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第三道工程。（图棠韵室内设计提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>八、磁砖填缝剂</strong></p>
<p>地坪贴磁砖完成后，使用防水型抗污磁砖填缝剂填平缝隙避免堆积污垢。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11385596" style="width: 193px" class="wp-caption alignright"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_08.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11385596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/2019075_shouhuihsu_waterproof_08.jpg" alt="" width="193" b="250" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">磁砖填缝剂。（图棠韵室内设计提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11387194" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190716_shouhuihsu_waterproof_01.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11387194 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20190716_shouhuihsu_waterproof_01-450x709.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="709" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">浴室磁砖填平缝隙可防潮，避免累积污垢。（图/棠韵室内设计提供）</figcaption></figure>
