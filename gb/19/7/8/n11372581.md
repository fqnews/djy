<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>巨大海狮光顾鱼市讨点心 耐心礼貌萌翻网友</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/huge-sea-lion-enters-a-fish-market-and-asks-for-a-snack-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>海狮和鱼市工人的有趣互动吸引了摄像者在旁观看，温馨一幕全被录入镜头。（Reddit:<a href="https://www.reddit.com/user/Tylers_Twin/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tylers_Twin
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2019年07月09日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳综合报导）Reddit网友“<ahref="https://www.reddit.com/user/Tylers_Twin/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tylers_Twin</a>”4月13日发布一段超萌视频，南美洲一头毛茸茸的大<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E7%8B%AE.md#1">海狮</a>等待鱼市工人赏食，耐心有礼让网友直呼太可爱。</p>
<p>从粗壮的头颈来看，这是一头成年的公<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E7%8B%AE.md#1">海狮</a>。循着美食飘散的气息，它发现了大快朵颐的捷径。它跟定一位很和善的工人，蹲守在他的工作台边翘首以待，显得冷静自律。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11372598" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/sea-lion-fisherman-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11372598" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/sea-lion-fisherman-3-450x563.jpg" alt="（Reddit:Tylers_Twin授权视频截图）" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Reddit:<ahref="https://www.reddit.com/user/Tylers_Twin/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tylers_Twin</a>授权视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管工人手上忙不停，但他没有冷落坐地巴望的来客——它的耐心乖巧获得了回报。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11372597" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/sea-lion-fisherman3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11372597" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/sea-lion-fisherman3-450x448.jpg" alt="（Reddit:Tylers_Twin授权视频截图）" width="450" b="448" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Reddit:<ahref="https://www.reddit.com/user/Tylers_Twin/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tylers_Twin</a>授权视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当工人发现这位大朋友，马上转身扔给它一条鱼。吃到零嘴的海狮很开心，希望得到更多恩惠……工人想继续工作，可旁边蹲着大海狮让他显然难以专心……</p>
<figure id="attachment_11372596" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/sea-lion-fisherman2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11372596" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/sea-lion-fisherman2-450x563.jpg" alt="（Reddit:&lt;a href=&quot;https://www.reddit.com/user/Tylers_Twin/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Tylers_Twin&lt;/a&gt;授权视频截图）" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Reddit:<ahref="https://www.reddit.com/user/Tylers_Twin/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tylers_Twin</a>授权视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们的有趣互动吸引了摄像者在旁观看，温馨一幕全被录入镜头。</p>
<p><center><a src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/video.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2FNatureHeavenOfficalPage%2Fvideos%2F359481534700218%2F&amp;show_text=0&amp;width=380" width="380" b="476"></a></center></p>
<p>只见渔夫又向海狮抛出一条鱼，海狮回身转悠几下，衔起鱼，一脸享受地仰头吞了下去……</p>
<figure id="attachment_11372600" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/sea-lion-fish-market-video1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11372600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/sea-lion-fish-market-video1-450x507.jpg" alt="（Reddit:&lt;a href=&quot;https://www.reddit.com/user/Tylers_Twin/&quot; target=&quot;_blank&quot; rel=&quot;noopener noreferrer&quot;&gt;Tylers_Twin&lt;/a&gt;授权视频截图）" width="450" b="507" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Reddit:<ahref="https://www.reddit.com/user/Tylers_Twin/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tylers_Twin</a>授权视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>影片到此戛然而止，但海狮在码头的愉快逗留想必会继续，毕竟，他找到了获得“免费午餐”的好地方！</p>
<p>这只海狮看起来块头够大，不过，据世界土地信托基金会（World Land Trust）介绍，成年雄性海狮可以长到3米，体重可达300公斤。</p>
<p>渔夫和海狮的视频经环境保护组织“<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/NatureHeavenOfficalPage/videos/359481534700218/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Nature Heaven</a>”在脸书转贴之后迅速传开，已获得193万次观看！</p>
<p>网友们非常喜爱这段视频，很多人留言夸赞海狮聪明可爱。还有网友好奇，海狮回到海里会不会和同类分享讯息，“如果它告诉朋友们会发生什么？”</p>
<p>也有网友提醒，海狮并不总是看起来那么友善，它们也会表现出攻击性。2017年12月，旧金山的著名景点——海狮云集的水上公园湾（Aquatic Park Cove）就曾因海狮屡次咬人而禁止游客下水游泳。</p>
<p>此前，还有网友分享海狮在鱼市吃下脚料、享受免费午餐的几段视频，地点是厄瓜多尔的科隆群岛。而海狮不是唯一访客，它们身边还有些特别的朋友，比如鹈鹕和鬣蜥（<ahref=""></a><a href="https://git.io/Jvy9G"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/oeiyl2369/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K5pWczZMdkE" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点阅</a>）。</p>
