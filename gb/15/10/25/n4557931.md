  <p>22号路在联合市的繁华地段，中心有零售商店分隔东西行车道。这条近一英里长路段去年有246起车祸，2013年为262起，前两年一直列为高危路段榜首。常去海边的人都熟悉的37号路四英里路段，去年撞车236起，排名第二。普拉克斯高速路（Pulaksi Skyway）基础设施老化，已列入拆迁名单。 </p>
  <p>近零售中心帕拉姆斯的17号路两英里路段和奎克桥路（Quaker Bridge）附近、西温莎默瑟购物中心（Mercer malls）的1号路，购物人流多也发生多起车祸。对数百条高速公路统计，车祸多发生在交通量大但相对较短的地方。<br />下面列出新州十条最危险路段：</p>
  <p>高危排名	公路	路段	所在城镇	发生车祸次数<br />1	Route 22 	Jefferson Avenue to Madison Avenue	Union Township	246<br />2	Route 37	Thomas Street to Barnegat Bay,	Toms River	236<br />3	Route 17 	Powers Drive to Linwood Avenue	Paramus	231<br />4	Route 27 	Stuart Place to Thompson Avenue	between Linden and Roselle	219<br />5	Route 1	Quaker Bridge Road to Alexander Road	West Windsor	218<br />6	Route 1	Pulaksi Skyway,Broadway to Route 139,	Jersey City	194<br />7	Route 9	Pond Road to Covered Bridge Boulevard	Manalapan	155<br />8	Route 130	New Albany Road to Rancocas Creek	between Delran and Delanco	143<br />9	Route 46	Browertown Road to Notch Road	Little Falls	142<br />10	Route 130	Route 38 to Myrtle Avenue	Pennsauken	136</p>
