<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】金蒜金勾蒸双蔬</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509041845272768-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（许心如/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2015年09月05日讯】有40多年料理经验，曾任美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨、海城酒楼 （Ocean City）主厨的粤菜大厨<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%BF%97%E7%94%9F.md#1">梁志生</a>先生，本周为大家带来──金蒜金勾蒸双蔬。</p>
<p><h2> 材料:</h2>
<p>丝瓜：8oz<br />茄瓜：8 oz<br />虾米 ：1 oz<br />蒜粒：1 oz<br />姜粒：1茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_6496494" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509041845272768-600x374.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="374"
	class="size-large wp-image-6496494" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2>调味料: </h2>
<p>盐：1/4茶匙<br />美极酱油：少许<br />胡椒粉：少许<br />麻油：少许</p>
<figure id="attachment_6496504" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509041845292768-600x400.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="400"
	class="size-large wp-image-6496504" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2> 做法:</h2>
<p>1.丝瓜去除硬皮， 留下少量绿色，斜刀切成厚片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6496515" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509041845322768-600x388.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="388"
	class="size-large wp-image-6496515" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.茄子间开刨掉部分紫色皮，同样斜刀切厚片，用清水浸泡着。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6496527" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509041845342768-600x438.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="438"
	class="size-large wp-image-6496527" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.虾米用暖水浸泡软身切碎、蒜粒切碎，用油把它们炸香至微黄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6496550" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509041845362768-600x488.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="488"
	class="size-large wp-image-6496550" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.准备一深碟，把丝瓜、茄子捞出晾干，排放在碟上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6496571" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509041845392768-600x397.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="397"
	class="size-large wp-image-6496571" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.把炸过的蒜粒虾米连油与姜粒，铺在瓜片上，洒上盐和胡椒粉，大火隔水蒸8分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6496587" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509041845412768-600x399.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="399"
	class="size-large wp-image-6496587" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.取出后淋上美极酱油、麻油，便可食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_6496602" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/1509041845442768-600x441.jpg" alt="（许心如/大纪元）" title="（许心如/大纪元）" width="600" b="441"
	class="size-large wp-image-6496602" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（许心如/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p></p>
<h2> 梁师傅小贴士</h2>
<p>?这道菜色彩鲜艳，健康又好味，是夏天很好的菜肴。<br />?虾米、蒜粒用中火慢炸至微黄，否则会太干及有苦味。</p>
