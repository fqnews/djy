  <figure id="attachment_12435363" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8007735b8727b6abccee7ff74f3eca31.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12435363" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8007735b8727b6abccee7ff74f3eca31-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">玫瑰棕色营造温柔又诗意的眼妆。（ETUDE提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>今年秋冬<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%BD%A9%E5%A6%86.md#1">彩妆</a>撷取落日的乱子草概念，细致的淡粉红乱子草，与夕阳轻吻乱子草的玫瑰棕色，混搭成既温柔又诗意的眼妆，或是搭配橙棕色变化，营造复古落日光辉。质感则是重视细致度、能贴合眼皮，呈现出干净的淡雅光芒。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12435364" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/31194a6b82d1b3b6700a45b6ef69c199.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12435364" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/31194a6b82d1b3b6700a45b6ef69c199-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">唇釉营造鲜嫩欲滴般的果汁色调。（ETUDE提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12435362" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/390432c2de76e64d95b7b2f7b431d97e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12435362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/390432c2de76e64d95b7b2f7b431d97e-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">柔滑的丝绒唇膏为趋势。（ETUDE提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>今年秋冬唇妆质感以丝绒和唇釉两种截然不同的质感为主轴，柔滑的丝绒唇膏搭配珊瑚色与奶茶色，透出恋爱感的韩星MLBB（My lips but better）唇色；轻透型的唇釉则营造鲜嫩欲滴般的果汁色调，让妆容有更加多元的表现。◇</p>
