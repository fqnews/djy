  <p>一口咬下“软而不烂”的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8C%84%E5%AD%90.md#1">茄子</a>让很多人放了心，搭配酿在茄子里的Ｑ弹花枝浆，口感有咬劲却不需要用力咀嚼，相当于粤菜的“心太软”食谱；烹饪过程茄子炸、回锅煮的时间必须注意才能掌握好口感，其中调味酱汁更是整道菜的关键，咸甜够味的酱汁突出混搭新吃法的特色滋味。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>  <p>花枝浆 10安士（Cuttlefish paste 10 oz）<br />  日本茄子两条约10安士（Japan Eggplant 10 oz）<br />  四季豆10安士（Green Bean 10 oz）<br />  葱花少许（Green onion）<br />  姜蓉少许（Minced ginger）<br />  胡椒粉少许（pepper）<br />  麻油少许（sesame oil）</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【调味】</span></h2>  <p>味噌汁2汤匙（Miso sauce 2 T）<br />  水1杯（Water 1 C）<br />  糖半茶匙（Sugar ? t）<br />  麻油少许（Sesame oil ）<br />  粟粉1汤匙开水勾芡用（Cornstarch solution 1 T）</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413728" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-28.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-28-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">味噌汁、葱花及姜蓉、粟粉（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>  <p>1.花枝浆拌入葱花、姜蓉、胡椒粉和麻油，搅拌均匀。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413730" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-19.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413730" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-19-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">花枝浆拌入葱花、姜蓉、胡椒粉和麻油，搅拌均匀（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2.接着用汤匙挖出小粒花枝浆备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413731" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-18-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用汤匙挖出小粒花枝浆。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3.茄子斜切三刀，不要切断。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413732" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-12-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">茄子斜切三刀，不要切断。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4.在切好的茄子上洒少许生粉。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413733" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413733" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">切好的茄子上洒少许生粉。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5.酿入花枝浆，抹平滑后备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413734" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413734" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6-10-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">酿入花枝浆，抹平滑后备用。 （梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>6.四季豆切除头尾，洗干净后用油盐水烫熟备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413735" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413735" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">四季豆切除头尾。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>7.热锅后下2杯油，油烧滚后放入茄子，放入时花枝浆的部分朝下，炸1分钟，再反转两面炸30秒，盛出后备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413736" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413736" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8-10-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">热锅后下2杯油，油烧滚后放入茄子。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>8.锅中剩下少许油，爆香姜、蒜蓉和味噌汁，再加入水和糖。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413737" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413737" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">锅中剩下少许油，爆香姜、蒜蓉和味噌汁，再加入水和糖。（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>9.将茄子放回锅中，中火煮一分半钟，最后用粟粉水勾芡。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413738" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413738" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将茄子放回锅中，中火煮一分半钟，最后用粟粉水勾芡（梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>10.茄子放在碟中央，四季豆围在旁边即完成。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h2>  <p>选购花枝浆时，可选择台湾制造的。其囗感爽脆嫩滑，用来酿蔬菜类最适合。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【主厨简介】</span></h2>  <p>姓名：梁志生<br />  厨龄：40余年<br />  经历：<br />  1、美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />  2、海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />  3、作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />  4、美国富商George Bowles御用厨师</p>
