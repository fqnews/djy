  <p>把家里的吹风机拿来做料理，就是做出好吃<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%83%A1%E6%A4%92%E8%99%BE.md#1">胡椒虾</a>的诀窍，因为需要把外层酱汁收干。因此热炒后用吹风机马上吹干，可以让胡椒粉附着在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%99%BE.md#1">虾</a>子的表面，更加入味。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【腌<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%99%BE.md#1">虾</a>食材】</span></h2>  <p>草虾 12只<br />  白胡椒粉适量<br />  盐适量<br />  香油适量<br />  米酒适量</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【调味料食材】</span></h2>  <p>蒜头碎 适量<br />  辣椒碎1根<br />  葱花适量<br />  糖少许<br />  盐巴 1大匙<br />  白胡椒粉大量<br />  黑胡椒粉适量<br />  香油 少许</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>  <p>1. 首先将白虾去须、开背、去肠泥，下适量盐、白胡椒、香油搅拌均匀。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413389" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6c572674724715162668a71e5b57d1b3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6c572674724715162668a71e5b57d1b3-450x315.jpg" alt="胡椒虾,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白虾去须、开背、去肠泥，下适量盐、白胡椒、香油搅拌均匀。（Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 取一锅开大火下油，放入虾子煎至双面稍微上色，下蒜头碎、辣椒碎略炒。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413392" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/34ce6009c2f0349b2f1235206a8dabe4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413392" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/34ce6009c2f0349b2f1235206a8dabe4-450x315.jpg" alt="胡椒虾,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">取一锅开大火下油，放入虾子，加盖为闷煎至双面稍微上色。（Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 下盐、白胡椒、黑胡椒翻炒均匀，起锅前下香油、葱花。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12413390" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/911dd622765f3938907a65fbd2a22efa.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413390" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/911dd622765f3938907a65fbd2a22efa-450x315.jpg" alt="胡椒虾,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">虾子熟时下盐、白胡椒、黑胡椒翻炒均匀。（Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12413391" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8dbce4cd9c4c7fa6d27085dbafd644af.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12413391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8dbce4cd9c4c7fa6d27085dbafd644af-450x315.jpg" alt="胡椒虾,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">起锅前下香油、葱花。（Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4. 最后用吹风机将虾子吹干即可。</p>
