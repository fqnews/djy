  <p>中医记载，南瓜从叶、苗到果实，全部都可以入药。《本草求原》载：“蒸晒浸酒佳。其藤甘苦、微寒。平肝和胃，通经络，利血脉。”</p>
  <p>南瓜富含人体必需的氨基酸、蛋白质、钾、磷、钙、铁、锌、钴、糖类、淀粉、胡萝卜素、维生素B1、维生素B2、维生素C和膳食纤维等。南瓜里的“钴”含量在各类蔬菜中居首位。钴能活跃人体的新陈代谢，促进造血功能。</p>
  <p>南瓜中含有的多糖是一种非特异性免疫增强剂，能提高机体免疫功能，促进细胞因子生成，通过活化补体等途径对免疫系统发挥多方面的调节功能。所以把它誉为天赐的免疫增强剂。</p>
  <p>红烧肉可以说是承载我们儿时记忆的一道菜，不同的人有不同的做法，也可以添加各种配料以增加风味。</p>
  <p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E8%82%89.md#1">南瓜红烧肉</a>，将红烧肉放入南瓜盅中，风味独特，赏心悦目，堪称宴请宾客的上等菜肴。</p>
  <p>好味道需要真功夫，要保证五花肉香而不腻，需要经过几个步骤。首先，要将肉皮煎至金黄，然后放入水中蒸煮，放入调料后，还要慢火炖煮2个小时，最后，放入南瓜盅后，还要再蒸45分钟。</p>
  <p>打开南瓜盅盖后，香气四溢。红烧肉肉质香滑、肥而不腻，南瓜吸收肉汁后别有一番风味。</p>
  <h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E8%82%89.md#1">南瓜红烧肉</a></h2>  <p>主料：五花肉、南瓜</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4778fbe10db6e2ec185ef99c57fb8f85.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433148" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4778fbe10db6e2ec185ef99c57fb8f85-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/dfb21f7a5087b9e2380335474ec9ff9a.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/dfb21f7a5087b9e2380335474ec9ff9a-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  配料：葱、八角、姜片、干辣椒、桂皮、月桂叶<br />  调料：白酒、黄酒、蜂蜜、红烧汁、海鲜酱、生抽、老抽、蚝油</p>
  <p>做法：</p>
  <p>1. 将五花肉用热镬煎至金黄色。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-38.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-38-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  2. 用少许油爆香葱、花椒等配料。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-24.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  3. 添加水和少许白酒，然后放入五花肉。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-24.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  4. 加盖煮约40分钟至五花肉熟。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433154" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  5. 将五花肉切10公分见方肉块待用。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  6. 将南瓜切开取出南瓜瓤。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6-13.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433157" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6-13-450x264.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="264" /></a><br />  7. 用少许油爆香葱、八角和辣椒等配料。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  8. 放入所有调料，将五花肉加盖慢火炖煮2小时。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  9. 将肉汁收汁至浓厚。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  10. 将肉放入南瓜盅中，然后放入原汁。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  11. 将南瓜和肉一起放入蒸箱中蒸45分钟。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/11-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12433166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/11-16-450x291.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="291" /></a></p>
