  <p>中国很多地方都有以桂花鱼制成的菜式，清蒸方式较为普遍，也有较特别的做法，如苏州的松鼠鳜鱼、徽州的臭鳜鱼等。</p>
  <p>中医认为，桂花鱼味甘、性平、无毒，归脾、胃经。具有补气血、益脾胃的滋补功效。</p>
  <p>桂花鱼含有蛋白质、脂肪、维生素、钙、钾、镁、磷、硒等营养元素，肉质细嫩，极易消化，对儿童、老人及体弱、脾胃消化功能不佳的人来说，吃桂花鱼既能补虚，又不必担心消化困难。</p>
  <p>另外，吃鳜鱼有“痨虫”的作用，也就是说有利于肺结核病人的康复。</p>
  <p>桂花鱼的热量不高，而且富含抗氧化成分，对于喜欢海鲜美味、想美容又怕肥胖的女士是极佳的选择。</p>
  <p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C%E6%A1%82%E8%8A%B1%E9%B1%BC.md#1">蜂蜜桂花鱼</a>色香味俱全，看起来高端大气，而且做法非常简单。罗厨强调，煎制鱼块之前要用黑胡椒和海盐腌制一下。炸鱼块时，先用180度油温将鱼块炸硬，然后降低油温将鱼块炸至七分熟，再升高油温将鱼块完全炸熟。这样做出的鱼块，外脆里嫩，鲜美多汁。</p>
  <h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C%E6%A1%82%E8%8A%B1%E9%B1%BC.md#1">蜂蜜桂花鱼</a></h2>  <p>主料：鱼块</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/72b75002213145fcd09b9ebccf0cdc40.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/72b75002213145fcd09b9ebccf0cdc40-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>配料：姜茸、葱碎、红葱头、蒜茸、香菜</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6cbd7a30f971dfc25cefa95a09b89021.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375821" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6cbd7a30f971dfc25cefa95a09b89021-450x261.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="261" /></a></p>
  <p>调料：黑胡椒、海盐、鸡蛋、生粉、生抽、老抽、蜂蜜、蚝油、鸡汤、生粉水</p>
  <p>做法：</p>
  <p>1. 将去骨的的鱼块吸去水分后，撒上盐、黑胡椒。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375822" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>2. 然后在鱼块面上撒上一些生粉。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375824" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>3. 鱼块上再放一些蛋液。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>4. 鱼块腌制一下再涂上生粉待用。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375827" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>5. 用180度的油温把鱼块的表层炸硬。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>6. 然后将油温降低浸炸至七成熟。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>7. 用高温将鱼块炸熟后捞起并吸干油分。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>8. 将葱、姜、蒜等配料炒香。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>9. 放蜂蜜等调料、清鸡汤调味勾芡。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>10. 将芡汁淋在鱼块上面。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>11. 最后撒上香菜粒、葱粒、红葱头粒。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/11-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12375842" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/11-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
