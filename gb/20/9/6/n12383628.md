  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>  <p>烟熏<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E6%96%87%E9%B1%BC.md#1">三文鱼</a>3片（Smoke salmon 3 slices）<br />  嫩菠菜叶6片装饰用（Baby spinach leaf 6 pieces）<br />  大鸡蛋两颗（Extra Large Egg 2 ）<br />  水10安士（Cold water 10 oz）<br />  菠菜叶适量（装饰用）</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12383649 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-5-450x364.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="364" /></a></p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【调味】</span></h2>  <p>盐四分之一茶匙（Salt ? t）<br />  白胡椒粉少许（white pepper a little）</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>  <p>1.鸡蛋打散后加入冷水和盐。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12383650 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><br />  2.搅拌均匀后，用网筛滤去杂质后备用。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12383651 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><br />  3.在蒸锅中放入1个蒸盘，开大火煮至水滚，把预先做好的鸡蛋液倒入蒸盘内， 盖上锅盖蒸10分钟，关火让<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%92%B8%E8%9B%8B.md#1">蒸蛋</a>在锅内闷20分钟。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12383652 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><br />  4.取出后放上三文鱼，做成三朵玫瑰花形状，最后再放上菠菜叶做装饰便可。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12383655 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-3-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h2>  <p>· 蒸鸡蛋时不用盖上保鲜膜，直接蒸便可以。<br />  · 这道蒸蛋蒸好之后非常嫩滑，适合做给家中的长辈享用。◇</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【主厨简介】</span></h2>  <p>姓名：梁志生<br />  厨龄：40余年<br />  经历：<br />  1、美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />  2、海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />  3、作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />  4、美国富商George Bowles御用厨师</p>
