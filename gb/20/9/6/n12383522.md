  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材 料】</span></h2>  <p>猪绞肉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 150g<br />  虾仁 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 100g<br />  红萝卜丁 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 30g<br />  洋葱 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230; 10g<br />  姜 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 1 片<br />  青葱 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 5g</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12383530" style="width: 248px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/P145-1.png"><img class="wp-image-12383530" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/P145-1-150x120.png" alt="" width="248" b="198" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">猪绞肉（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12383533" style="width: 267px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/P145-2.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-12383533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/P145-2.png" alt="采实文化" width="267" b="199" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">虾仁（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【调味料】</span></h2>  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C%E9%BB%84.md#1">姜黄</a>粉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230; 0.5g<br />  玫瑰盐 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230; 1.5g<br />  白胡椒粉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 1g<br />  无糖酱油 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 5ml<br />  酪梨油 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 5ml</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>  <p>1 取出1/4 的虾仁切丁备用，其余虾仁连同全部食材与调味料都放入<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B0%83%E7%90%86%E6%9C%BA.md#1">调理机</a>中。<br />  2 启动调理机搅打，将食材切碎并充分融合至产生些微黏性。<br />  3 取出虾肉泥，加入步骤1预留的虾仁丁，用汤匙稍微混合均匀增加口感。<br />  <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/P145-3.png"><img class="wp-image-12383534 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/P145-3-450x400.png" alt="" width="342" b="304" /></a><br />  4 起油锅（材料分量外），用汤匙挖50g虾肉泥放入，利用锅铲轻轻下压成稍扁的形状，以中小火慢慢煎至上色定型后可翻面。<br />  <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/P145-4.png"><img class="wp-image-12383535 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/P145-4-450x382.png" alt="" width="349" b="296" /></a><br />  5 翻面后继续煎至全熟、微微焦黄即可盛盘。<br />  <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/P145-5.png"><img class="wp-image-12383536 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/P145-5-450x380.png" alt="" width="372" b="314" /></a></p>
