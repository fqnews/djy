  <p>滋补肝肾、延年益寿的东星斑，多自然生长于100米以下的深海，污染少，生长时期长，故其肉质有咬劲，营养价值更是其它海产品所不能比拟的。</p>
  <p>东星斑肉不仅营养价值丰富，而且人体吸收率极高。鱼肉含有二十多种人体所需要的微量元素、维生素等，是高蛋白、低脂肪、低胆固醇的养生佳品。</p>
  <p>东星斑鱼皮的胶质层含有大量的“角沙烯”成分，是特效美容元素，对修复皮肤非常有效，堪称女士的养颜恩物。鱼骨内还有珍贵的“脑黄金”成分，能激活脑细胞、提高记忆力、延缓大脑老化。</p>
  <p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A5%E7%83%AB%E4%B8%9C%E6%98%9F%E6%96%91.md#1">粥烫东星斑</a>，完全用热粥的高温将鱼肉烫熟，可将东星斑鱼片最鲜美的味道完全激发出来，加之香浓纯滑的靓粥汤底、配料的自然清香脆嫩，一起入口，可以体会到无以言表的幸福感！</p>
  <p>罗厨强调，切东星斑鱼片的时候很有讲究，两片鱼不要完全切断，要连在鱼皮上，这样可以保证鱼肉连在一起，不会散落锅中，也可以同时品尝到鱼皮的好味道。</p>
  <h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A5%E7%83%AB%E4%B8%9C%E6%98%9F%E6%96%91.md#1">粥烫东星斑</a></h2>  <p>主料：东星斑、大米</p>
  <p>配料：葱丝、姜丝、姜片、香菜、红枣、玉米、肉排</p>
  <p>调料：胡椒粉、白芝麻</p>
  <p>做法：</p>
  <p>1. 东星斑起肉切薄片。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-15.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12408827" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-15-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  2. 玉米取玉米粒。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-25.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12408829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-25-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  3. 姜、葱切细丝待用。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-15.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12408830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-15-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  4. 将肉排放入砂煲。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12408832" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  5. 将玉米粒、玉米埂段、红枣放入砂煲。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12408833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  6. 将浸泡1个小时的香米放入砂煲。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12408835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  7. 添水煲30分钟成香喷喷的靓粥。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12408838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  8. 粥煲好后取出肉排和玉米梗。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12408839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  9. 将东星斑片慢慢放入煲中粥的上面。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12408841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  10. 然后放入姜丝、葱丝、芝麻和胡椒粉。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12408843" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>11. 被热粥烫熟的鱼片鲜美嫩滑。</p>
