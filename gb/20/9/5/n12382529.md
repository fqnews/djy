  <figure id="attachment_12382537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/e82643e698fda6d13a20ff91cf2c062c.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12382537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/e82643e698fda6d13a20ff91cf2c062c-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%95%E5%88%BB%E5%8D%B0%E8%AE%B0.md#1">雕刻印记</a>-蔡荣兴师傅创作个展。（宜兰市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>蔡荣兴师傅生于宜兰<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9C%A8%E9%9B%95.md#1">木雕</a>世家，父亲蔡火土为薪传奖知名木雕艺师。自小蔡师傅便与兄长师承父亲手艺，更专精于木雕粿印、糕印等传统糕饼模具制作数十载。蔡师傅的木艺，展现了木头印模特有的温润质感。木雕粿印上每一个特殊的印记，都是透过蔡荣兴师傅的双手一刀一刀慢慢雕刻而成。深刻地在精准的雕、刻之间，凿出传统糕饼上细腻而华美的纹饰。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12382534" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/adca89a1f377ef2ddbab45cacdca6d7d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12382534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/adca89a1f377ef2ddbab45cacdca6d7d-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">雕刻印记-蔡荣兴师傅创作个展。（宜兰市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12382535" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/93cdd34d5c568f087f9d567fe62740b6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12382535" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/93cdd34d5c568f087f9d567fe62740b6-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">雕刻印记-蔡荣兴师傅创作个展。（宜兰市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>本次个展《雕刻印记-蔡荣兴师父创作个展》一部分展出多件蔡师傅最为人赞叹的各式传统<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9C%A8%E9%9B%95.md#1">木雕</a>糕饼模具；另一方面，也展出了许多结合传统与创新，匠心独运的立体木雕作品。蔡师傅刻满岁月的双手，一手抚着木块，一手拿着凿刀，在敲打间，敲出了围绕着蔡师傅自身新与旧的生活记忆，以及台湾传统木雕工艺的面貌。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12382536" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3627f873b1169b75ac299694e1111da2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12382536" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3627f873b1169b75ac299694e1111da2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">雕刻印记-蔡荣兴师傅创作个展。（宜兰市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12382531" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/08a86be9e0ffbcd9c591310b21f6adcd.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12382531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/08a86be9e0ffbcd9c591310b21f6adcd-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">雕刻印记-蔡荣兴师傅创作个展。（宜兰市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>市长江聪渊表示，自上任以来即致力于推展艺文活动，以期让宜兰市成为拥有丰厚文化底蕴的艺文城市。此次在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9C%E5%85%B0%E4%BA%BA%E6%95%85%E4%BA%8B%E9%A6%86.md#1">宜兰人故事馆</a>的《雕刻印记》蔡荣兴师傅作个展，从蔡师傅众多的创作中挑选出最具代表性的作品，从传承百年的粿印工艺到日常生活印象的饭桌、镜台等，每件作品都充满岁月的淬炼及精华，具体呈现蔡荣兴先生的木雕艺术成就。</p>
