  <p>活动于上午9点开始，许多老师和家长利用周末假日，带着孩子们走出户外，从写生观察中激发儿童和少年的创意，举凡水池中饲养的锦鲤，公园里的小桥、木栈道及公共艺术造景，还有休闲凉亭、赏花步道、观湖平台、水圳广场等，都可成为入画题材。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12382610" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/d31a3c9b6db0b8943d5850a46c30beaf.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12382610 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/d31a3c9b6db0b8943d5850a46c30beaf-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">竹北市公所举办<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%86%99%E7%94%9F%E6%AF%94%E8%B5%9B.md#1">写生比赛</a>，鼓励亲子共同出游、尽情挥洒创作。（竹北市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12382611" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/acc1caef4bf7350e3316da3f30e8976a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12382611 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/acc1caef4bf7350e3316da3f30e8976a-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">竹北市长何淦铭(右)与参加活动的小朋友合影。（竹北市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>竹北市公所表示，今天参赛者都能获得经典的绘画专用套装组，及四款手绘DIY免费体验，写生比赛是竹北一年一度的美学盛会，希望以写生比赛的方式，鼓励亲子健康出游，让小朋友和青少年尽情挥洒，画出竹北高铁与幸福宜居的城市生活。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12382612" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8f73ae1404fb1f0ef3e57531b2969d54.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12382612 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8f73ae1404fb1f0ef3e57531b2969d54-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">新竹县多位民意代表到场参加活动、共襄盛举。（竹北市公所提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>责任编辑：玉珍</p>
