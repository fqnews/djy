  <p>伊面，又称“伊府面”，台湾叫做“意面”，是一种油炸的蛋面。伊面是中国著名面食之一，也是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A4%E8%8F%9C.md#1">粤菜</a>经典主食，因在民间广泛地流传与改良，传至世界各地。</p>
  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E8%99%BE%E4%BC%8A%E9%9D%A2.md#1">龙虾伊面</a>是一道经典的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C.md#1">海鲜</a>面食，通常的做法是把经过上汤烹调的龙虾放在伊面之上，龙虾的鲜甜汁液流入伊面，令伊面都充满海鲜的滋味。这道面食一般称为“龙虾伊面”或“伊面烩龙虾”。</p>
  <p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E8%99%BE%E4%BC%8A%E9%9D%A2.md#1">龙虾伊面</a>，添加了黑胡椒，用黑胡椒爆炒姜段和葱头，香气四溢，罗厨又画龙点睛地添加了一点蜂蜜，使得以鸡汤为底的龙虾汤汁味道极其鲜美。</p>
  <p>罗厨将伊面煮软后，将淋干水分的伊面放在热油锅里煎至香脆，这样使得伊面外脆内软，即Q弹又有嚼头。</p>
  <p>成品菜肴摆上桌，色、香、味俱全。龙虾肉质滑嫩、香浓可口，加入黑胡椒虾汁后拌面，鲜香四溢，堪称人间美味。</p>
  <h2>黑胡椒龙虾伊面</h2>  <p>主料：龙虾、伊面</p>
  <p>配料：姜条、葱段、葱头</p>
  <p>调料：盐、黑胡椒、生粉、蜂蜜、鸡汤、蚝油、生抽、胡椒粉</p>
  <p>做法：</p>
  <p>1. 将龙虾洗干净切成块。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-17.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12392320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/1-17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>2. 用调料将龙虾块腌好。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12392323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/2-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>3. 用160度的油温干炸龙虾，捞起滤干油。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12392325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/3-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>4. 将伊面用水煮软后捞起。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12392326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/4-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>5. 把姜条炒香捞起。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12392328" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/5-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>6. 利用热油锅将伊面煎至香脆，过程中添加少许盐。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12392329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/6-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>7. 将面剪成几大块。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12392331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/7-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>8. 利用热锅余油将配料用黑胡椒、蚝油和蜂蜜爆香。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12392332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/8-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>9. 添加鸡汤后放入龙虾和生抽、胡椒粉等调料。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12392334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/9-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>10. 加盖焗30秒后，加入3汤匙生粉水。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12392337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/10-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>11. 最后将龙虾摆放在伊面上，然后淋上汤汁。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/11-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12392338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/09/11-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
