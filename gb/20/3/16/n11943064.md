<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【武汉肺炎】亚省启动COVID-19自我评估网站</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月16日讯】（<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">记者陈安综合报导）</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">自</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">13</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">(</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">周五</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">)</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">起，亚省启动针对武汉肺炎（新型冠状病毒</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%87%AA%E6%88%91%E8%AF%84%E4%BC%B0%E7%A8%8B%E5%BA%8F.md#1">自我评估程序</a>，</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;"><ahref="https://myhealth.alberta.ca/Journey/COVID-19/Pages/COVID-Self-Assessment.aspx">https://myhealth.alberta.ca/Journey/COVID-19/Pages/COVID-Self-Assessment.aspx</a></span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">，以帮助人们确定是否应致电健康热线811（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Health Link 811）</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">及进行</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">测试。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">该在线服务是加拿大首例此类服务。在线调查表询问用户症状，逐步确定是否需要电话咨询或测试。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">自周五下午启动该评估表后的两个小时内，超过</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">10,000</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">人使用。截至周六下午，已有</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">30</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">万人使用了评估工具。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">省卫生部长尚德罗（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Tyler Shandro</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）表示，它减少了对健康热线811</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">的压力。此前拨打健康热线811</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">的电话每天增加</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">152</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％，每天有大约</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">6,300</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">个呼叫者。 健康热线811</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">工作人员从</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">35</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">名增加了一倍，达到</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">72</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">名，电话线路从</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">60</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">名增加到</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">182</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">名。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">省卫生部（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">AHS</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）表示，如果个人未曾出过加拿大以外的地方或在过去</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">14</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">天内未与被确诊的人接触，则无需进行</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">测试。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">对于需要测试的人来说，</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">测试程序也很简单。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">通过健康热线811</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">信息确定需要进行拭子测试后，在</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">个不同的时间段选择一个测试时间。</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">到达测试地点后，治安人员在门口接待，给你洗手液，请你戴上医用口罩，然后在待诊室等候。</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">进行鼻拭子测试的过程很快，不会很痛苦。</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">最多需要</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">5</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">天出结果。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">亚省周六宣布，迄今为止已对</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">7,069</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">名患者进行了检测，其中</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">39</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例呈阳性。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">卫生部建议任何有咳嗽，流鼻涕，发烧或嗓子疼的症状的人隔离</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">14</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">天。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">在公共场合，与他人保持约</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">2</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">米的距离，掩盖咳嗽和打喷嚏，并避免触摸脸部。并要求人们经常洗手。</span></p>
