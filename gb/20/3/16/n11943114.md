<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【武汉肺炎】卡尔加里的大学和学校如何应对</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月16日讯】（<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">记者陈安综合报导）</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">随着在亚省被诊断出<span lang="ZH-CN">武汉肺炎（新型冠状病毒</span><span lang="EN-CA">COVID-19)</span></span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">的人数增加到</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">39</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">，卡尔加里的大学和</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">K-12</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">学校正在采取以下措施，以减少感染的机会。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">K-12</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">学校</span></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">省卫生局尚未决定关闭学校，关闭一所学校，整个部门或所有学校仍在讨论中，但认为关闭</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">8</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">至</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">12</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">周才能有效。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">卡尔加里教育局（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">CEB</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）表示，它直接与省卫生部和卡尔加里紧急事务管理局（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">CEMA</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）合作，并成立了内部工作组。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">如果孩子生病或直系亲属最近去过旅行或出现</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">症状，</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">CBE</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">要求父母将这些学生留在家中并致电健康热线</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">811（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Health Link 811）</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">CBE</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">计划提醒学生洗手的重要性，并已加强清洁和消毒程序。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">CBE</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">已取消团体活动，团体租赁，实地考察和学生旅行。志愿者活动推迟。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">卡尔加里天主教学区取消了所有实地考察和旅行，并表示父母可以选择将孩子留在家中。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">卡尔加里大学（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">University of Calgary</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）</span></h3>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">13</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日（星期五），卡尔加里大学通知学生，课程暂停，周一起，通过远程教学学习。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">请教职员工在本周制定应急计划，以便在必要时提供在线课程，并在必要时采用替代评估形式，以替代期末考试。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">校园将保持开放，教职员工将继续工作。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">任何有症状的人都被要求远离校园并致电健康热线</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">811</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">该大学暂停了对受影响地区的出国旅行和留学计划，直到</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">2020</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">年</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">9</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月，并要求最近从某些地区（校网站可查询）返回的人员进行自我隔离</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">14</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">天。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">皇家山大学（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Mount Royal University</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）</span></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">该校在3月14日（周六）表示，从周一起不再面授课程，采用其他方式教学。校园和服务将保持开放。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">大学暂停了学业方面的旅行，制定了“在家工作”指南，并且正在重点清洁高接触区域。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">弓谷学院（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Bow Valley College</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）</span></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">该学院从周四开始，所有原本在校园举行的活动都将被推迟或取消。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">员工以网络方式或通过电话召开会议，如非重要的大学业务，不允许在省南部以外进行任何旅行。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">课程将在网络进行，如远程工作。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">亚伯塔艺术大学（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Alberta University of the Arts</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）</span></h3>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">亚伯塔艺术大学</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">表示，其危机管理团队正在监视局势，目前尚无取消活动或课程的计划。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">圣玛丽大学（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">St. Mary&#8217;s University</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）</span></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">大学要求员工，教职员工和学生暂停前往加拿大全球事务部指定为</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">级或</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">级风险的任何国家，直到另行通知。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">所有省外旅行活动都将被暂停。鼓励成员在制订个人旅行之前评估风险。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">该大学表示，由于面积很小，没有必要限制公共聚集，但是已经制定了在校园关闭的情况下的课程计划。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">南亚伯塔省理工学院（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">SAIT</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）</span></h3>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">SAIT</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">表示将暂停课程，直到</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">18</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日为止，</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日将过渡到在线课程和替代课程。校园将保持开放。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">SAIT</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">还取消了周二生效的出国留学计划，并禁止雇员去某些地区旅行（政府名单上的国家）。它还暂停了校外计划及其烹饪专业校区。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">安布罗斯大学（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Ambrose University</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）</span></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">安布罗斯大学说，它正在监视这种风险，并制定了应急计划，以应对取消课堂教学。</span></p>
