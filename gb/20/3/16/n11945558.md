<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【武汉肺炎】埃德蒙顿设立车内测试COVID-19诊所</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月17日讯】（<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">记者陈安综合报导）</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">为避免武汉肺炎（新型冠状病毒COVID-19）</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">进一步传播，亚省卫生部（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">AHS</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）在埃德蒙顿设立车内测试</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">诊所，埃德蒙顿的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B4%AD%E7%89%A9%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">购物中心</a>开始减时或关闭。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">直通车诊所</span></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">亚省卫生部（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">AHS</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）在埃德蒙顿开设了</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">2</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">家全新的直通诊所，在车内测试</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">诊所面向有症状，打过健康热线811（Health Link 811）</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">咨询电话的人。被测试者只需在车道上停车，在车上接受测试，不向步行者开放。测试需预约。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">亚省北部健康服务部医学总监乔夫（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Mark Joffe</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）博士说，这种直通车将加快测试过程并降低传播病毒的风险。“他们不必离开汽车。他们不必坐在候诊室。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">到达测试现场后，医务人员穿着工作服，戴手套，口罩和面罩迎接来访者。一名护士会从患者的鼻子中取样，并将样品进行测试。告诉患者几天之内就可以期待结果，并在等待结果的同时保持自我隔离。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">乔夫介绍，最好的标本实际上是从鼻子到咽喉后部取样。这不舒服，但必要。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">埃德蒙顿第一个测试站点于</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">7</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日开放，位于圣约东南校园（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Covenant Southeast Campus</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）的体育馆。诊所第一天筛查了</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">180</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">名患者。自开放以来，截至周日，诊所已进行了</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">1,783</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">项测试。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">15</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日（周日），第二个诊所开诊。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">卡尔加里也有一个直通车站点。截至周日，该站点自</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">10</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日开放以来已筛查了</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">604</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">名患者。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">急诊室筛查分区</span></h3>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">亚省卫生部</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">已要求亚省人不要访问急诊室，如果他们担心有</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">，可使用在线评估，致电健康热线</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">811</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">为了保护易受伤害的患者免受其他急诊科访客的感染，埃德蒙顿的医院现已开始对患者进行预筛查，将有</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">症状的患者分流到一个专门区域。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B4%AD%E7%89%A9%E4%B8%AD%E5%BF%83.md#1">购物中心</a>调整时间</span></h3>
