<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走进巴黎香宫 阿尔萨斯名酒邂逅粤菜大厨</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/ea13ab123cdc90a46aeabb31b398dad6-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>粤式烤鸭（林丽霞/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月02日讯】（大纪元记者林丽霞<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E9%BB%8E.md#1">巴黎</a>报导）“琼瑶浆白<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84%E9%85%92.md#1">葡萄酒</a>号称是‘四轮驱动’，能够无所不行，可以超常规地和外来菜肴很好地搭配。”Philippe Bouvet如是说。2月28日，来自<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%B0%94%E8%90%A8%E6%96%AF.md#1">阿尔萨斯</a>五家酒庄把琼瑶浆带进巴黎香格里拉大酒店，由米其林<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A4%E8%8F%9C%E5%A4%A7%E5%8E%A8.md#1">粤菜大厨</a>Samuel Lee精心打造了一次创新的尝试。</p>
<p>琼瑶浆（Gewurztraminer）是法国东部<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E5%B0%94%E8%90%A8%E6%96%AF.md#1">阿尔萨斯</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84%E9%85%92.md#1">葡萄酒</a>产区的一个葡萄品种，以其酿制的白葡萄酒拥有葡萄柚、玫瑰、荔枝和芒果的芬芳。</p>
<p>Philippe Bouvet是阿尔萨斯葡萄酒专业委员会（Conseil interprofessionnel des vins d&#8217;Alsace）的市场总监，他表示，这次专门选择了法国唯一一家拥有米其林星级的中餐厅——香宫（Shang Palace）来展示琼瑶浆白葡萄酒与中式烹调的般配。</p>
<p>“中国美食极为丰富多样，找到适合的葡萄酒搭配并非容易。”Philippe Bouvet认为，酒体丰满的琼瑶浆白葡萄酒“带轻微的辣味，是阿尔萨斯葡萄酒中最具热带风味的酒，能够与亚洲菜绝佳地搭配。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11913028" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Philippe-Bouvet.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11913028 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Philippe-Bouvet-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">阿尔萨斯葡萄酒专业委员会市场总监Philippe Bouvet（林丽霞/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11913024" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Bernard-Wagner.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11913024 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/Bernard-Wagner-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">合作社酒庄La Cave Vinicole de Beblenheim的市场负责人Bernard Wagner（林丽霞/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>合作社酒庄La Cave Vinicole de Beblenheim的市场负责人Bernard Wagner也表示：“白葡萄酒的味觉十分广泛，含较低的单宁酸，口感细腻柔和，而中餐（粤菜）的本身也是非常精细，讲究食材的搭配和食物的芳香，所以我觉得白葡萄酒可以很好搭配这样的烹调风格。”在米其林<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A4%E8%8F%9C%E5%A4%A7%E5%8E%A8.md#1">粤菜大厨</a>Samuel Lee的手下，松鼠鱼、烤鸭、粤式点心等与<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%90%8D%E9%85%92.md#1">名酒</a>相得益彰，连扬州炒饭都显得别有风味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11913034" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4aeb74f0c73de74e6b42ef62287672a9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11913034 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4aeb74f0c73de74e6b42ef62287672a9-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">粤式烤鸭（林丽霞/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11913038" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/5750d35715dfae2bb9fadd88d1a06bcf.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11913038 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/5750d35715dfae2bb9fadd88d1a06bcf-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">糖醋松鼠鱼 （林丽霞/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11913057" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2aabbc3dd7ca224e75198ecc874e0e33.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11913057 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2aabbc3dd7ca224e75198ecc874e0e33-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">粤式点心（林丽霞/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11913060" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/cc11b532982b5cff3f4469dab08bf31f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11913060 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/cc11b532982b5cff3f4469dab08bf31f-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">精美甜品 （林丽霞/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11913062" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/82ec38fe4611185f0d343f7609b57b67.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11913062 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/82ec38fe4611185f0d343f7609b57b67-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">扬州炒饭（林丽霞/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该合作社酒庄于1952年由几位葡萄种植者联合创办。50多年后的今天，该酒庄集合了阿尔萨斯产区150多酿造葡萄酒者，葡萄园面积超过500公顷，成为阿尔萨斯第六大酒庄，产品出口全球20多个国家。</p>
<p>据Philippe Bouvet介绍，阿尔萨斯葡萄产区拥有4000葡萄种植户，800家公司酿造和销售葡萄酒。本次活动旨在寻求创新的市场策略和活力，把阿尔萨斯传统的酿酒工艺面向亚洲市场打开大门。</p>
