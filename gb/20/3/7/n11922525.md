<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手作提拉米苏～唤醒爱情 苦甜在心</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/90e5a759e810eb211cee4427a86895d8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>代表爱与幸福的甜点～提拉米苏（图／C2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>迎接白色情人节的到来，和心爱的人一起下厨做一道代表“爱与幸福”的浪漫<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%82%B9.md#1">甜点</a>吧！准备食材是用泡过咖啡、莱姆酒的手指饼干与玛斯卡彭起司混合物，层层堆叠，再洒上一层可可粉，滑顺浓郁的口感，充满甜蜜滋味。</p>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%8F%90%E6%8B%89%E7%B1%B3%E8%8B%8F.md#1">提拉米苏</a>“Tiramisù”意大利原文代表着“带我走”的意思，背后是一段浪漫的爱情故事；在战争的年代，妻子为了即将远行的丈夫将家里仅剩的乳制品做成一份充满爱的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E7%82%B9.md#1">甜点</a>，希望丈夫可以带他一起走。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【食材】</strong></span></h3>
<p>糖30g<br />
蛋黄3颗<br />
马兹卡朋起司250g<br />
鲜奶油250g<br />
手指饼干<br />
咖啡<br />
蓝姆酒<br />
可可粉</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【做法】</strong></span></h3>
<p>1. 蛋黄和砂糖搅拌，蛋黄打到微微淡黄色。<br />
2. 砂糖溶解后停止，加入马兹卡彭起司混合均匀，等到看不见明显颗粒后放到一边。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11924814" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11924814 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/e2033a1905ec76bc4c96e178393ceca6-450x315.jpg" alt="提拉米苏,C2食光" width="450" b="315" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蛋黄和砂糖搅拌后，加入马兹卡彭起司混合均匀。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 换上打蛋器，拿出冰箱的鲜奶油。<br />
4. 打发鲜奶油到有明显的纹路，湿润并且打蛋器上的鲜奶油尖角微弯曲。<br />
5. 把鲜奶油先挖1/3到起司糊中，用切拌的方式混合均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11924815" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11924815 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/f53b834f4ea4e8655863ab3d4a48a1c9-450x315.jpg" alt="C2食光,提拉米苏" width="450" b="315" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11924813" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/be2c27589f61dca44773807ca5de88fc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11924813" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/be2c27589f61dca44773807ca5de88fc-450x315.jpg" alt="提拉米苏,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 拿出器皿，先铺一层馅料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11924816" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8b6ca9c70c81f17619af17f704e4f403.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11924816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8b6ca9c70c81f17619af17f704e4f403-450x315.jpg" alt="提拉米苏,C2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 手指饼干两面沾湿在咖啡和蓝姆酒液，放一层在馅料上。<br />
8. 再铺上一层馅料，放上一层沾湿的手指饼干，最后铺上一层馅料。<br />
9. 冷藏之后，洒上可可粉即可食用。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11924817" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/ea450caf25615f8b468b1c4a8f8c7fa7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11924817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/ea450caf25615f8b468b1c4a8f8c7fa7-450x315.jpg" alt="提拉米苏,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
