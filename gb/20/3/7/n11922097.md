<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>鲑鱼毛豆炊饭～一锅到底 轻松上桌</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/0d988ee6ce4f1752ac8a13df7b994404-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>鲑鱼毛豆炊饭（图／C2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%A8%E6%B0%B4.md#1">雨水</a>季节，天气变化不定，忽冷忽热的要注意个人保健工作，增强抵抗力，预防疾病的发生！</p>
<p>针对这样的气候特点，饮食调养应侧重于调养脾胃和祛风除湿，又由于此时气候较阴冷，可以适当地进补。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11922134" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/d5dfa2874d7cff9ae3c8dd24397c5088.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11922134 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/d5dfa2874d7cff9ae3c8dd24397c5088-600x338.jpg" alt="雨水，C2食光" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%A8%E6%B0%B4.md#1">雨水</a>节气。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>雨水建议食材 ｜蜂蜜、大枣、山药、银耳</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%91%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲑鱼</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%AF%9B%E8%B1%86.md#1">毛豆</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%82%8A%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炊饭</a></strong></span></h3>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%82%8A%E9%A5%AD.md#1">炊饭</a>是日本家庭常见的料理，对于忙碌的现代人来说是道简单又方便的料理。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【高汤食材】</strong></span></h3>
<p>水400ml<br />
昆布1片<br />
柴鱼10g</p>
<h3><strong><span style="color: #008000;">【食材】</span></strong></h3>
<p>米2杯<br />
味醂1大匙<br />
日式淡酱油1大匙<br />
清酒2大匙<br />
红萝卜50g<br />
牛蒡1/2枝<br />
鸿禧菇1包<br />
<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%91%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲑鱼</a>2片<br />
<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%AF%9B%E8%B1%86.md#1">毛豆</a>100g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【做法】</strong></span></h3>
<p>1. 先准备高汤：将昆布和水放入锅中烹煮，在快要煮沸前取出昆布、放入柴鱼片，汤汁沸腾后即可关火，用滤网过滤后即完成高汤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11922152" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/c794d072e6f1ec7d29c08b388f594e7c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11922152 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/c794d072e6f1ec7d29c08b388f594e7c-450x315.jpg" alt="C2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将昆布倒入锅中和水一起烹煮。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11922155" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/40da070ebbc0e26ce6f2cdae126f6cdb.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11922155 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/40da070ebbc0e26ce6f2cdae126f6cdb-450x315.jpg" alt="C2食光）" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">汤汁沸腾后即可关火，用滤网过滤后即完成高汤。（图／C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 米洗好后沥干，放入电子锅内锅，加入淡酱油、盐巴、酒和味醂等调味料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11922159" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2979c9548c224755356c853d2601f875.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11922159 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2979c9548c224755356c853d2601f875-450x315.jpg" alt="米,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">米洗好后沥干，放入电子锅内锅后再放调味料。（图／C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 处理配料食材：鲑鱼切块后用酒稍微腌过，红萝卜削皮后切丝，鸿喜菇切成适合入口的大小，牛蒡用刀背刮表面去皮，再像削铅笔一样削成牛蒡丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11922156" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/52ffb93278ba92d66ff82ca4b3f41dc8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11922156 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/52ffb93278ba92d66ff82ca4b3f41dc8-450x315.jpg" alt="红萝卜,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红萝卜削皮后切小块或切丝，可以根据家人喜欢的口感自行调整。（图／C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 将高汤倒入锅中，把配料铺在米饭上头，即可按下开关炊饭。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11922171" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/52c923af3961eb12ae2f63333555c9fb.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11922171 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/52c923af3961eb12ae2f63333555c9fb-450x315.jpg" alt="鲑鱼毛豆炊饭,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲑鱼记得把鱼刺去干净才和其他配料一起铺在米饭上头。（图／C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 开关跳起后，先闷10分钟再开锅，用饭杓先将米饭和配料均匀拌松，再盛入饭碗，即可上桌！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11922193" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/01a3ec05e6d4b5b72609a44e6c780965.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11922193 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/01a3ec05e6d4b5b72609a44e6c780965-450x315.jpg" alt="鲑鱼毛豆炊饭,C2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">记得先闷锅10分钟后再开锅，会更好吃喔！（图／C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><strong><span style="color: #008000;">“营养师的贴心小叮咛”-by 陈小薇营养师</span></strong></h3>
<p>鲑鱼含有不饱和脂肪酸omega-3，有助于释放褪黑激素，让褪黑激素作用，减轻焦虑症状，进而改善睡眠品质，有纾压助眠的效益！另外，搭档的毛豆则含有蛋白质、膳食纤维以及特殊营养成分大豆异黄酮（Isoflavone），大豆异黄酮因为类似人体的雌激素，也被称为植物雌激素！科学研究指出大豆异黄酮，可以缓解女性更年期症状，具有降低胆固醇、预防骨质疏松、减少心血管疾病…等！营养师小叮咛，可以将部分米饭用糙米取代，增加维生素B群摄取，营养更加分喔！◇</p>
