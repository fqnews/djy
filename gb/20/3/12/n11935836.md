<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>嫩煎骰子牛～在家也有居酒屋的气氛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/686fb3d8dff2812db3df2a5843ac9b07-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>嫩煎骰子牛（图／C2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%AA%B0%E5%AD%90%E7%89%9B.md#1">骰子牛</a>是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B1%85%E9%85%92%E5%B1%8B.md#1">居酒屋</a>很受欢迎的一款料理，牛肉切成大小适中的正方形，总是让人忍不住一口接一口，停不下来。现在想吃不用出门，在家也能轻松做，软嫩多汁的牛肉搭配香酥的蒜片，周末端上桌再小酌一杯，一扫整周工作的辛劳。</p>
<p>【<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%AA%B0%E5%AD%90%E7%89%9B.md#1">骰子牛</a>食材】<br />
牛排1片<br />
蒜油适量<br />
玫瑰盐 适量<br />
黑胡椒 适量<br />
奶油 适量</p>
<p>【<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%92%9C%E9%85%A5.md#1">蒜酥</a>食材】<br />
蒜头 5颗<br />
沙拉油适量</p>
<p>【作法】<br />
1.将牛肉的血水擦干，两面洒上玫瑰盐以及黑胡椒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11935873" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/e52a0a48c8802367f90d26323ad6152b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11935873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/e52a0a48c8802367f90d26323ad6152b-450x315.jpg" alt="骰子牛,Ｃ2食光,居酒屋,蒜酥" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将蒜头切片走水。</p>
<p>3.将蒜片擦干，在锅中倒入沙拉油（够淹过蒜片量即可）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11935875" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/7734a27ac8885c0c08e8735fd329de88.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11935875 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/7734a27ac8885c0c08e8735fd329de88-450x315.jpg" alt="骰子牛,Ｃ2食光,居酒屋,蒜酥" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将蒜片擦干（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.油炸蒜片，捞起备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11935877" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/f1bd52a05efe59b5a5f6e4950f9831a4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11935877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/f1bd52a05efe59b5a5f6e4950f9831a4-450x315.jpg" alt="骰子牛,Ｃ2食光,居酒屋,蒜酥" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.将牛肉多余血水擦干，切成骰子状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11935876" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/dbf0e4c48f9a620702e419f99bb70197.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11935876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/dbf0e4c48f9a620702e419f99bb70197-450x315.jpg" alt="骰子牛,Ｃ2食光,居酒屋,蒜酥" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.热锅，倒入些许蒜头油，倒入牛肉，拌炒至上色，下些许奶油，拌炒一下起锅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11935879" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/57f4d024e5a01a03641fd389d6090dd9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11935879 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/57f4d024e5a01a03641fd389d6090dd9-450x315.jpg" alt="骰子牛,Ｃ2食光,居酒屋,蒜酥" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">拌炒骰子牛时，下方放些许奶油。（图／C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
