<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Airbnb势头旺 西澳加强监管</h2>

<h6>去年有26万Airbnb客人到访西澳。（JOHN MACDOUGALL/AFP/Getty Images)
</h6>

	<p>【大纪元2020年03月21日讯】<span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者</span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">在线住宿平台</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Airbnb</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">2019</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">年有</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">450</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">万人在其澳洲在线住宿平台上预订了房间；在西澳，去年有</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">26</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">万</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Airbnb</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">客人到访。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western" lang="en-US" align="justify"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">据商业新闻网报导，</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Airbnb</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">公共政策负责人诺兰（</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Derek Nolan</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">）表示，澳洲当地的宝藏现在正受到国际游客的认可。他说：“澳洲为国内和国际旅客提供了很多服务，包括世界一流的景点，友善、热情、好客的当地人，独特的历史和全澳最好的标志性景观。”</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western" lang="en-US" align="justify"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">“我们为</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Airbnb</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">房东在向全球游客开放区域方面发挥的作用感到非常自豪。”诺兰，“</span></span></span> </span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Airbnb</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">房东创造的额外旅游能力为生活在全澳大城市以外的许多社区提供了至关重要的经济增长。”</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western" lang="en-US" align="justify"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">他表示，</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Airbnb</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">的客人已经向全澳成千上万的小企业注入了宝贵的旅游资金。但是，行业内一些组织，包括西澳旅游局和澳洲酒店协会，都曾倡导对</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Airbnb</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">和其它短期住宿提供商进行更严格的监管。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western" lang="en-US" align="justify"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">去年，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E6%94%BF%E5%BA%9C.md#1">西澳政府</a>宣布了加强对</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Airbnb</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">等短期住宿提供者监管的计划，以解决包括规划和便利性在内的问题。西澳议会经济产业常务委员会听取了各利益相关方提交的</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">350</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">多项意见书，包括</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Airbnb</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">房主、客人、邻居和竞争对手。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western" lang="en-US" align="justify"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">委员会主席肖沃（</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Jessica Shaw</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">）在去年</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">9</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">月份的新闻发布会上表示，委员会已收到有力证据，表明短期租赁应在全西澳范围内进行监管。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western" lang="en-US" align="justify"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">西澳旅游局和澳洲酒店协会均表示支持注册计划，并称这将使住宿业重新获得公平。西澳工商业联合（</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">CCIWA</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">会</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">却对此进行批评。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western" lang="en-US" align="justify"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">CCIWA</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">首席执行官罗德韦尔（</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Chris Rodwell</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">）说，“州政府似乎开始接受呼吁，要求在共享经济中增加繁文缛节，这只会压缩游客的选择空间，使住宿更加昂贵，并阻碍游客来西澳。”</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="western" lang="en-US" align="justify"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">西澳规划厅厅长萨菲奥蒂（</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">Rita Saffioti</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">）对此辩解说，政府需要指定最低的透明度措施，为住宿业创造一个更加公平的竞争环境。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
