<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>首次购房首付款新政备受东部欢迎   缘何在西澳遇冷？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/GettyImages-1188708561-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年03月21日讯】<span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者张筱甜、杨新云澳洲珀斯综合报导）自</span></span><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">从首次购房首付款优惠政策于今年</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">1</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">1</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">日生效以来</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">,</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">截止目前联邦政府已经收到超过</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">4000</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">份的购房申请，然而，来自西澳的首次购房者对此优惠的申请比例低于</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">5%</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">，新政的优势是否并不吸引西澳购房者还是另有起因？</span></span></span></span></p>
<p lang="zh-TW" align="left"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">根据以上新规，符合条件的首次购房者在申请房贷买房时，首付款仅需房价的</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">5%</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">，而其余</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">95%</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">由贷款支付，实际上联邦政府为首次购房者担保</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">15%</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">，从而他们可免交贷方抵押贷款保险费金（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">Lenders mortgage insurance</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">，简称：</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">LMI)</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p lang="zh-TW" align="left"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">在此政策推出之前，购房者的首付款通常为房价的</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">20%</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">，否则就需要支付抵押贷款保险，才可能获得房贷审批。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p lang="zh-TW" align="left"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">目前有</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">27</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">家贷款机构参与该优惠政策，其中四大银行中的联邦银行（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">CBA</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">）和国民银行（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">NAB</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">）开始接受首次购房者申请。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p lang="zh-TW" align="left"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">澳洲国民银行发言人表示，迄今为止这类贷款的需求“非常强劲”。然而，这个计划设置了不同的价格门槛，具体取决于人们在哪里购买。例如，在珀斯的房价门槛为</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">40</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">澳元，西澳其它地区的房价门槛为</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">30</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p lang="zh-TW" align="left"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">在今年</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">1</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">1</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">日，联邦政府已经发放了</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">1</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">万份的申请配额，在</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">7</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">月，政府将在增加</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">1</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">万个配额。现在澳洲每年约有</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">10</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">万个首次购房者，需要注意的是，此购房优惠仅限于澳洲公民。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p lang="zh-TW" align="left"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">尽管许多首次购房者积极利用此首付款新政入市买房，其中悉尼的购房优惠申请最多，为</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">970</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">个，而西澳的申请只有</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">208</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">个（珀斯的申请数量</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">192</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">个，西澳偏远地区的申请为</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">16</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">个）。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p lang="zh-TW" align="left"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">面对申请数量如此的悬殊，业界人士认为并非西澳人不青睐首付款新政，而是许多首次购房者已经申请了西澳政府推出的</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, lang="zh-TW">日开始，西澳政府提高了</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">Keystart</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span <p lang="zh-TW" align="left"><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">Alcock</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">表示，鉴于今年年初购房活动的增加，他预计首次购房者对此计划的需求将有所增加。</span></span></span></span></p>

<p lang="zh-TW" align="left"><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">Alcock</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">还说，那些使用首付款计划的人可能是更高收入的人，因为该计划的收入上限更为“慷慨”。据澳洲信贷网站</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">Finder</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">显示，此项计划的首次购房者的收入上限是，单身为</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">12.5</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元，一对夫妇为</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">20</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></span></span></span></p>
<h2 lang="zh-TW" align="left"><sub><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">珀斯青年买房比例高</span></span></sub></h2>
<p lang="zh-TW" align="left"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">最新数据还显示，在西澳，年龄介于</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">18</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">至</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">24</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">岁的首次购房者申请该计划的比例</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">-44%</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">，高于澳洲其他地区。在全澳，该比例为</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;">20%</span></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: 新细明体, PMingLiU, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></span></span></p>
