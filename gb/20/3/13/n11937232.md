<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亚省冠状病毒一周览</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/tu1-outbreak-coronavirus-world-1024x506px-600x400-1.jpg" />
<h6>图为美国疾病与预防中心刊出的新型冠状病毒的示意图。（CDC网站）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月13日讯】（<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">记者陈安报导）</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">12</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日（ 周三），加拿大联邦卫生部长哈吉杜（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Patty Hajdu</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）表示，专家们已经暗示，武汉肺炎（新型冠状病毒COVID-19）可以感染</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">30</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％至</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">70</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">％的人口。世界卫生组织已正式宣布疫情为大流行病。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">1</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">25</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日，加拿大确诊了第一例官方武汉肺炎</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">病例，当时一名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">50</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的男子从中国武汉抵达多伦多，这是新型冠状病毒爆发的始发地。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">截至</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">12</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日，加拿大有</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">140</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多例确诊的新型冠状病毒</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">病例。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">自</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">5</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日出现第一例病人以来至</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">12</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日，在亚省已确诊病例达</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">23</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">一周内</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">23</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例</span></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">第一例新型冠状病毒病例是一名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">50</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的妇女，在大公主号游轮感染了</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">。两个</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">ATB</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">分行（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Sage Hill</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">和</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Creekside</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）因此而暂时关闭。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">6</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日，埃德蒙顿发现一例新型冠状病毒病例。一名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">40</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的男子。在商务旅行中，他去了美国的伊利诺伊州，密歇根州和俄亥俄州。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">8</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日，埃德蒙顿地区一名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">60</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的男子和卡尔加里地区的</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">30</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的女性被诊为新型冠状病例。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">9</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日，全省又增</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例病例。埃德蒙顿附近发现</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">1</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例，一名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">70</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁妇女，与一名男患者密切接触。两人都曾在大公主号游轮旅行。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">卡尔加里地区确认了</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">2</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例。一位从加勒比海游轮返回的</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">50</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁妇女。一名男子与第四例患者密切接触，且去乌克兰，土耳其和荷兰旅游。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">10</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日，病例翻倍。卡尔加里地区</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例，包括</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">1</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">50</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的男性，</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">2</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">30</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的女性和</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">1</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">40</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的女性。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">埃德蒙顿地区</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例，包括一名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">70</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的男性和一名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">60</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的女性，他们最近一起旅行。一名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">30</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的女性，最近也曾出国旅行。</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">11</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日，全省新增</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">5</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">例。所有新病例最近都是从来自伊朗，埃及，西班牙，墨西哥或美国等多个国家旅行回来的。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">一名埃德蒙顿地区</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">30</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的男子。曾于</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">6</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日和</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">7</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日在</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Misericordia</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">医院接受了一项与新冠无关的手术。亚省卫生部已与医院相关工作人员和患者联系。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">另外</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">个新病例位于卡尔加里地区。一个</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">20</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的男人，两个</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">30</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">岁的女人。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">亚省中部地区也有一例个案，涉一名</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">30</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多岁的妇女。</span></p>
<h3><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7%E6%80%BB%E7%90%86%E7%89%B9%E9%B2%81%E5%A4%9A.md#1">加拿大总理特鲁多</a>的妻子新型冠病毒检测呈阳性</h3>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E6%8B%BF%E5%A4%A7%E6%80%BB%E7%90%86%E7%89%B9%E9%B2%81%E5%A4%9A.md#1">加拿大总理特鲁多</a>办公室的一份声明说，加拿大总理贾斯汀·特鲁多的妻子索菲亚·格雷戈雷·特鲁多（Sophie Gregoire Trudeau）的新型冠状病毒检测呈阳性。</p>
<p>声明说，她感觉良好，只有轻微症状，并且将保持隔离状态。</p>
<p>声明说，特鲁多没有症状，但将被隔离14天。由于他没有症状，因此目前未接受检测，并将继续正常工作。</p>
<p>加拿大总理办公室表示，总理夫人索菲亚最近曾前往英国演讲，返回后出现了轻微类似流感症状，周三深夜发低烧。</p>
<p>周四上午，在妻子测试结果出来前，特鲁多已经选择自我隔离并在家工作。</p>
<p>本周特鲁多与部长们首次会面的议程已被取消，原定于周四和周五在渥太华与加拿大省长的会晤，已改为通过电话进行。</p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">老人病人更严重</span></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">加拿大公共卫生局（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">PHAC</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）表示，对于</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">65</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">岁以上的人以及免疫力低下或慢性病、健康状况较差的人，发生严重后果的风险较高。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">亚省有</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">1</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">人正在医院接受治疗。此人患有慢性疾病。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">对于大多数人来说，新型冠状病毒只会引起轻度或中度症状，例如发烧和咳嗽。对于某些人，特别是老年人和存在健康问题的人，它可能引起更严重的疾病，包括肺炎。绝大多数人会从病毒中恢复过来。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">因此，建议</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">65</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">岁以上的人不要出国旅行。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">亚省首席卫生官肖欣（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Deena Hinshaw</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）指出，如果感到不适，且不确定是否应该待在家中，或平时患有疾病，或年纪较大的人，应考虑与医疗机构联系以得到帮助。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日托关闭</span></h3>
<p><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">11</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日，卡尔加里市中心森科（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Suncor</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）能源中心大楼中日托所的一名儿童被确诊新冠病例。</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日托所于</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">12</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日关闭，并计划于</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">23</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日重新开放。</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">20</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">多名员工及家人被要求自我隔离，直到</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">20</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日托病例是亚省首例确诊的儿科病例，也是加拿大首例儿童病例。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">卡尔加里大学卡明医学院的传染病医师和病毒学研究员凡迈耶·斯蒂芬森（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">Vanessa Meier-Stephenson</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）博士说，儿童患冠状病毒的风险并未增加。“就迄今为止的证据来看，儿童受到的影响比成年人少得多。”</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">但儿童更有可能将病毒传播给他人。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">此外，卡尔加里市中心的视觉艺术与设计学院（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">VCAD</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）已关闭，此前一名与</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">测试呈阳性的人接触的学生进入自我检疫。官员们证实学校将关闭至周六。</span></p>
<h3><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%9E%8B%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">大型活动</a>取消</span></h3>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">鉴于疾病传播情况，全国各地的音乐会，会议，体育赛事和其他公共聚会已被取消。</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">12</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日上午，国家冰球联盟（</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">NHL</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">）因</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">暂停了比赛。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">肖欣在周四的新闻发布会中宣布，所有超过</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">250</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">人的大型集会将被取消，但教堂礼拜和学校暂时不受影响。</span></p>

<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">周三，总理特鲁多表示，有必要确保工人在需要自我隔离时得到保护。他宣布成立</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">article content begin -->0</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">亿的</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">应急基金。该基金将支持省级卫生工作，研究和测试，还将免除为期一周的就业保险等待期。政府还准备促进企业获得商业信贷可用性计划。</span></p>
