<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>法拉盛部分华人超市继续营业</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/3e52aaa85f9dae61c1b51dc0bea5c56f-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>法拉盛德昌超市内的收银员全副武装。（林丹／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月31日讯】（大纪元记者林丹美国纽约报导）纽约的疫情凶猛，不少华人<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E5%B8%82.md#1">超市</a>为避疫而关门。今天（30日，周一），位于<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B.md#1">法拉盛</a>中心区最大的华人超市新世界超市暂停营业，不过，部分华人超市仍继续开门营业，超市外现排队的人龙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11990712" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/c389b51a528dbaa8221cac640baffe20.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11990712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/c389b51a528dbaa8221cac640baffe20-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E6%8B%89%E7%9B%9B.md#1">法拉盛</a>41大道德昌<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E5%B8%82.md#1">超市</a>外排队的顾客。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11990718" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/98d4f42b2f5410fb53aa13e8137c6219.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11990718" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/98d4f42b2f5410fb53aa13e8137c6219-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大中华超市外排队的顾客，自觉拉开距离。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在法拉盛，包括德昌（41大道）、大中华（缅街店、北方大道店）、昌发（缅街老店）、华联（Parsons店）仍继续营业；店外排队的顾客，比周末少，排队的人均自觉保持较疏的距离。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11990721" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/cfbfb4be68ea40f345dddbeaa062ff84.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11990721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/cfbfb4be68ea40f345dddbeaa062ff84-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法拉盛德昌超市内的肉类供应，还算充足。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11990710" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/a1af4edbe5830f9b6eeab3470f5ebc93.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11990710" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/a1af4edbe5830f9b6eeab3470f5ebc93-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">肉类卖得很快。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11990714" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/49435e0baaf4b0cf5098c76499132eb4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11990714" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/49435e0baaf4b0cf5098c76499132eb4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">肉类卖得很快。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>41大道的德昌超市员工表示，该店会每天开门，肉类、蔬菜的供应都是充足的，但为保持“社交距离”，会限制店内顾客人数，出来一个顾客再进入一个；开门时间缩短，从早上9点营业到下午6点半。</p>
<p>记者见到，德昌超市店内4台收银机全部运作，收银员全副武装——穿工作服，戴口罩、护目镜，并要求顾客进内也要戴上口罩。肉、菜的供应充足，但卖得很快；该店依然提供快餐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11990716" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/156b905faa7231e2776792032fecb632.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11990716" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/156b905faa7231e2776792032fecb632-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法拉盛缅街昌发超市（老店）仍开业。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于部分店员不敢上班，人手不足，该店表示不提供加工服务，而且张贴招聘人手的告示。</p>
<p>长江超市在周一也短时间开门，但店内食品不多。收银员只有老板娘一个人，她说，因缺收银员，所以也不敢进太多货，否则放在店内会烂掉，现在急招收银员，有收银员就维持开门。</p>
<p>另一方面，主流及韩裔的超市坚持开业，如BJ&#8217;s、Costco、HMark等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11990709" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8256b281c1872985a1f4fe03db58c01c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11990709" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8256b281c1872985a1f4fe03db58c01c-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法拉盛中心区行人稀少。 （林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11990715" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/343a90e7db6ac438b65dbded1ed1c5b8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11990715" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/343a90e7db6ac438b65dbded1ed1c5b8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法拉盛中心区行人稀少。 （林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11990722" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/17723e198a3e5e1928104f75d7feaa8c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11990722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/17723e198a3e5e1928104f75d7feaa8c-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法拉盛中心区行人稀少。 （林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在餐馆外卖方面，开门的餐馆寥寥无几，偌大的法拉盛中心区只有“旺角”、“豫园”餐馆继续提供外卖；开门的饼店也仅剩“飞达饼屋”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11990719" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/ce6cbc9dbf7ea9e80b6ec0279988e9ad.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11990719" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/ce6cbc9dbf7ea9e80b6ec0279988e9ad-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">街上行人零零星星。（林丹／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>法拉盛街头行人零零星星，商店绝大多数拉闸，马路上主要是公交车在行驶，整个市区变得寂静，昔日车水马龙、门庭若市、熙熙攘攘的景象不再，也不知何时才能恢复。◇</p>
