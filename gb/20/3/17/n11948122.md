<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>亚省公园露营地在线预订开始</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年03月18日讯】<span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">3</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">17</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日（周二）早上，亚省公园开启<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E9%A2%84%E8%AE%A2.md#1">在线预订</a>，但同时警告，如果</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">COVID-19</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">危机持续下去，未来可能关闭。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">为了最大程度地降低<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%AD%A6%E6%B1%89%E8%82%BA%E7%82%8E.md#1">武汉肺炎</a>（又称新型冠状病毒，COVID-19）</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">的风险和传播，除<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F%E5%AD%A3%E9%9C%B2%E8%90%A5%E5%9C%B0.md#1">夏季露营地</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E9%A2%84%E8%AE%A2.md#1">在线预订</a>外，公园的所有娱乐设施，计划，活动和预订均关闭或取消。规定立即生效，直到</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">5</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">1</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">公园将继续评估对露营季节的影响，如果风险没有解除，将在</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">2020</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">年</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">月</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">30</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">日前应要求取消预订并免除取消费。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">希望退款的人联系</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">1-877-537-2757</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">，而不是在线取消或更改预订。</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">各个露营地的预订将全天按地区错开。可以在预定抵达日期之前</span><span lang="EN-CA" style="font-family: SimSun;">90</span><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family: SimSun;">天进行预订。</span></p>
<p>责任编辑：赵明月</p>
