<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>民歌9月再攻蛋 施孝荣自曝写谱让歌手都冒汗</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240527012122-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>“民歌45高峰会巨蛋感恩场”记者会8月24日在台北举行。（黄宗茂／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年08月24日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E6%AD%8C.md#1">民歌</a>45高峰会”2月落幕，为了感谢歌迷不畏疫情相挺，演唱会制作人<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%96%BD%E5%AD%9D%E8%8D%A3.md#1">施孝荣</a>今天（24日）率领14组歌手阵容齐聚一堂，宣布9月13日再唱进台北小巨蛋，他也自曝为了呈现完美演出，写的合声谱难度太深奥，让大家排练时冷汗直流。</p>
<p>歌手们现场演唱经典曲目《小草》与《奔放奔放》，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%96%BD%E5%AD%9D%E8%8D%A3.md#1">施孝荣</a>表示，因为<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E6%AD%8C.md#1">民歌</a>首首经典，每次在排演唱歌单的时候，内心都先天人交战一番，才能决定歌单，因此将特别开放歌迷点歌，喊话不怕罚款“不怕唱超时，只怕没唱到！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12354082" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240527132122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240527132122-ss1.jpg" alt="施孝荣" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12354082" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">施孝荣将特别开放歌迷点歌。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>双姝周子寒、陈艾湄首次加入</strong></p>
<p>相较于2月演出，9月的阵容从8组增至14组。除了原班人马施孝荣、李明德、王瑞瑜、殷正洋、叶佳修、邰肇玫、于台烟、王海玲之外，还新增了潘越云、王梦麟、许景淳、罗吉镇等歌手，其中周子寒、陈艾湄更是首次加入，让众男歌手欣慰笑说：“期许未来能有更多新血投入民歌。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12354083" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240526372122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240526372122-ss1.jpg" alt="王海玲" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12354083" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">王海玲出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12354084" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240527092122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240527092122-ss1.jpg" alt="王梦麟" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12354084" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">王梦麟出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12354086" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240526412122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240526412122-ss1.jpg" alt="潘越云" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12354086" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">潘越云出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12354087" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240526492122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240526492122-ss1.jpg" alt="于台烟" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12354087" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">于台烟出席记者会。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>合声难度再升级 歌手练唱冒冷汗</strong></p>
<p>施孝荣透露新演出会结合灯光效果与惊喜组合，让经典歌曲也能有新的演绎方式，也自曝他写的合声谱难度太深奥，让大家排练时都冷汗直流反复练习，于台烟更直接“Call out”施孝荣到她家楼下教唱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12354093" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240527172122-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2008240527172122-ss1-600x354.jpg" alt="民歌45高峰会" width="600" b="354" class="size-large wp-image-12354093" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E6%AD%8C45%E9%AB%98%E5%B3%B0%E4%BC%9A.md#1">民歌45高峰会</a>巨蛋感恩场”记者会8月24日在台北举行，众歌手出席现场。（黄宗茂／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
