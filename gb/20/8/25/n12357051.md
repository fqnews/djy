<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】番茄炒牛肉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/c7de1ca8e11b3e45bcf2c02c36cf0b9b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道番茄炒牛肉，甜酸可口、略带鲜辣，成品菜肴香气四溢、色泽诱人。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>番茄营养价值非常丰富，还含有丰富的抗氧化物，不仅可以生吃，还可以入馔，是中西料理中都被广泛运用的食材。</p>
<p>根据美国农业部（USDA）的资料显示，每100克的生番茄含有18卡路里和0.2克脂肪，热量比苹果低。番茄中还含有丰富的维生素A及维生素C。维生素C是制造胶原蛋白的重要元素，有助于体内骨胶原的修复和增生。因此番茄是女士美颜及延缓衰老的好帮手。</p>
<p>番茄中的茄红素是类胡萝卜素的一种，人体内无法自行合成。它除了能使食物呈现鲜红的色彩之外，也是强力的抗氧化物。在所有的类胡萝卜素中，茄红素的抗氧化能力最强，因此有强效抗氧化的功效。</p>
<p>番茄的维他命A、B、C、E（果色愈红含量愈多）与茄红素一起作用时，能抑制自由基对细胞染色体基因的损坏，是防癌圣品。此外其热量低又有丰富的维生素，是瘦身美容的最佳选择。</p>
<p>牛肉中的维生素A和B群可以预防贫血，其高含量的蛋白质、氨基酸有相当高的营养价值。牛肉与番茄搭配而成的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84%E7%82%92%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">番茄炒牛肉</a>，即营养健康又不用担心吃后发胖。</p>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%95%AA%E8%8C%84%E7%82%92%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">番茄炒牛肉</a>是一道老少咸宜的家常美味，不同的人做出的菜肴口味完全不同，罗厨制作的这道番茄炒牛肉，甜酸可口、略带鲜辣，成品菜肴香气四溢、色泽诱人。</p>
<h2>番茄炒牛肉</h2>
<p>主料：牛肉0.5磅、大番茄3个</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/6ad01efc7aabc4f8defa8637efc75859.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/6ad01efc7aabc4f8defa8637efc75859-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/318ffe8c3326434cf7ae90715d99c505.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/318ffe8c3326434cf7ae90715d99c505-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：蒜片、红葱头、青辣椒、葱粒、香菜叶<br />
调料：鸡蛋、白酒、生抽、老抽、蚝油、盐、糖、生粉</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将番茄洗干净后切小块。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/1-32.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/1-32-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 牛肉切薄片。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2-27.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357327" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2-27-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 用鸡蛋、生抽、胡椒粉和生粉腌制牛肉片。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/3-23.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/3-23-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>4. 猛火阴油，将蒜片爆香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4-22.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357332" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4-22-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>5. 放入牛肉大火爆炒。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/5-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/5-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 加白酒、生抽和老抽将牛肉炒至五成熟捞起。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/6-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357335" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/6-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 用盐将红葱头、青辣椒、葱段炒香后捞起。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/7-13.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/7-13-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 用锅底余油将番茄大火炒香后添加盐、糖和胡椒粉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/8-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357338" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/8-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 放入牛肉后添加生粉水。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/9-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357340" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/9-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 添加已经炒香的红葱头、青辣椒和葱段。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/10-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357341" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/10-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 包尾油装碟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/11-17.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12357342" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/11-17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
