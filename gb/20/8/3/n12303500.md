<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>行善天下 3501地区举办扶轮基金研讨会</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/5ca7b79123a17990ef4727f4ad9f6b83-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>扶轮基金研讨会主办社-中坜明星扶轮社，社友开心合影。（赖月贵／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年08月03日讯】（大纪元记者赖月贵台湾桃园报导）为了让各社的社长及社友对<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%89%B6%E8%BD%AE%E5%9F%BA%E9%87%91.md#1">扶轮基金</a>能有充份了解，进而捐献与运用，透过<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A5%96%E5%8A%A9%E9%87%91.md#1">奖助金</a>计划造福社区、行善天下，国际扶轮3501地区于7月25日举办扶轮基金研讨会，吸引三百多名社友踊跃报名参加，活动在中坜平镇高中热闹登场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12303503" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/8b4c528fb930d584254bd2e157fd4918.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303503" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/8b4c528fb930d584254bd2e157fd4918-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">总监办公室宣导，介绍社友认识扶轮网站，有助于社务之推动。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12303504" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/aabe97d1b95d72868eaf56325dbd9a6d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303504" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/aabe97d1b95d72868eaf56325dbd9a6d-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">社友相见欢、开心合影。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12303506" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/54388ae4e76136d98ba36b57c28b36fd.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303506" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/54388ae4e76136d98ba36b57c28b36fd-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">竹梦社友相见欢、开心合影。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12303509" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/d1dcf6fc07a21fbadfcbfe2acb41e1b4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303509" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/d1dcf6fc07a21fbadfcbfe2acb41e1b4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">社友相见欢、开心合影。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>研讨会首先由筹备主委王元甫DGE Medico开场，他致词表示，在全世界疫情仍然严峻的情况下，大家能共聚一堂探讨<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%89%B6%E8%BD%AE%E5%9F%BA%E9%87%91.md#1">扶轮基金</a>，弥足珍贵！扶轮基金的构想是在1917年由阿奇．柯蓝夫（Arch C. Klumph）所提出，第一笔捐款是26.5美元，相隔一百多年后，目前每年的捐款总额已达4亿多美元，成果非凡、令人振奋！</p>
<p>DGE Medico说，今年度扶轮基金会做了几项重大改变：1.全球<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A5%96%E5%8A%A9%E9%87%91.md#1">奖助金</a>国际伙伴资助将由30%调降至15%。2.、现金自筹款将不再有世界基金1：0.5的配合款。3.原本六大焦点领域，增加第七项焦点领域“环境保护”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12303510" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/01ca1a9b2aab0bc7f14587033168173a.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303510" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/01ca1a9b2aab0bc7f14587033168173a-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">国际扶轮3501地区于7月25日举办扶轮基金研讨会，吸引三百多名社友踊跃参加并开心大合照。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>地区总监吴得坤DG Peter表示，举办研讨会的目的，是希望各社领导人在新年度开始前能对扶轮基金充份了解，先明白再规划，下年度就能得心应手，他期勉社友仔细用心听讲并做笔记，相信对年度扶轮基金服务的推动有所帮助，明年能更有效率执行服务。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12303511" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/fb2bca642e107b4a5c35607c489a2774.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303511" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/fb2bca642e107b4a5c35607c489a2774-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">国际扶轮3501地区总监吴得坤DG Peter致词。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12303512" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/74ef803b4471ae9636af5c7131410065.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/74ef803b4471ae9636af5c7131410065-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">国际扶轮基金理事刘启田RID Surgeon Liu致词。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>RI理事刘启田RID Surgeon Liu致词表示，区域(Region 9)有RRFC区域扶轮基金协调人PDG E.N.T.及E/MGA区域永久/巨额捐献顾问PDG Ortho，他们都曾在RI 总部接受过RLTI(Regional Leader&#8217;s Training Institute)，他们就是地区的智库。他们已经举办了区域团队训练会(Regional Team Training Seminar)，台湾十二个地区（港澳及大陆未来）的ARRFC（助理区域机扶轮基金协调人）及地区扶轮领导人已经受了训练。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12303513" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/bbadd07680927bfac757b9b357f5326c.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303513" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/bbadd07680927bfac757b9b357f5326c-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">研讨会主办社-中坜明星扶轮社，社长许孟纪致词。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12303515" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4894aa718c29e2c774e94e54e9f57b18.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303515" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4894aa718c29e2c774e94e54e9f57b18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">活动进行中、社友听演讲神情专注。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12303516" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/9afe83b0f5726a5c94966f4cd0b3fc0e.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303516" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/9afe83b0f5726a5c94966f4cd0b3fc0e-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">研讨会筹备主委王元甫DGE Medico（左）致赠感谢状给专题演讲讲师-陈弘修RRFC E.N.T（右）。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>为让社友对扶轮基金有更深入的了解，研讨会中也安排办专题演讲，邀请到第九地带扶轮基金协调人陈弘修RRFC E.N.T.演讲“扶轮基金会概况与年度目标”；地区扶轮基金主委、多年担任扶轮基金会财务稽核Audit的前总监郑桓圭PDG Peter 主讲“奖助金管理监督”；在奖助金申请的实务操作上，则邀请到地区奖助金主委陈礼龙CP SOHO主讲“全球奖助金申请”，及台湾网路社前社长陈韵如PP Teddy介绍“扶轮基金”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12303517" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2915fa0e4693bc754306683f87f6c8a9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2915fa0e4693bc754306683f87f6c8a9-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">研讨会筹备主委王元甫DGE Medico（左）致赠感谢状给专题演讲讲师-陈礼龙CP SOHO（右）。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12303518" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/e9af5f2cf4bbe221eabf57583b14185d.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303518" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/e9af5f2cf4bbe221eabf57583b14185d-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">研讨会筹备主委王元甫DGE Medico（左）致赠感谢状给专题演讲讲师-陈韵如PP Teddy（右）。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>前总监郑桓圭PDG Peter讲述申请奖助金应遵守的法律、奖助金种类和地区及扶轮社认证等内容，他也强调自己审查非常严格，希望社友提出的计划是真正为社区谋福利，而不了为了发展个人事业。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12303519" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/6f449411756e98347354fd836a57a3ed.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12303519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/6f449411756e98347354fd836a57a3ed-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">前总监郑桓圭PDG Peter（左）致赠感谢状给研讨会筹备主委王元甫DGE Medico（右）。（赖月贵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>研讨会在最后也安排了提问及解答，许多社友热烈讨论，分享各自的实务经验，希望能将听到的理论化成实际行动，凝聚善的力量，造福社区、行善天下。</p>
