<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】鲜奶炖蛋～香滑奶香 简易上手</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/11-5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>港式饭后甜点“鲜奶炖蛋”，犹如布丁般的滑顺口感，又有浓郁的奶香。（图：梁志生）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【小编按】<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E5%A5%B6.md#1">鲜奶</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%82%96%E8%9B%8B.md#1">炖蛋</a>是一道港式饭后甜点，在甜点店容易买到，在家也容易制作。简单营养、工序不繁复，主要是料理过程要注意炖煮的时间，还有蛋、牛奶和糖的比例。做了原味的炖蛋后可以增添姜汁就有了日式甜点的味道。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>
<p>大鸡蛋2个（Large Egg 2）<br />
糖3茶匙（Sugar 3 t）<br />
<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%9C%E5%A5%B6.md#1">鲜奶</a>8安士（Milk 8 oz）</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/1-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12323008 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/1-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【用具】</span></h2>
<p>小炖盅两个<br />
锡泊纸两小块</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12323010 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2-12-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【做法】</span></h2>
<p>1.鲜奶用微波炉热20秒，之后再热20秒。用手指放进去感觉有温暖便可，不能太热。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/3-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12323013 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/3-14-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
<p>2.取出之后加入糖搅拌均匀。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12323014 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4-12-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
<p>3.将鸡蛋打散，将“做法2”加入鸡蛋液内。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/5-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12323016 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/5-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
<p>4.用过滤网过滤“做法3”。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/6-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12323021 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/6-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
<p>5.将做好的鲜奶蛋液倒入两个炖盅内。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/7-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12323022 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/7-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
<p>6.炖盅上盖上小锡箔纸。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/8-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12323024 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/8-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a></p>
<p>7.炖盅放入已经烧滚水的蒸锅内，中小火炖10分钟便可。炖好后打开锡箔纸，轻轻摇动一下炖盅，如果只有少许震动便是熟了！</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/9-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12323031 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/9-4-450x600.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="600" /></a></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【梁厨小贴士】</span></h2>
<p>1. 如果你发现你做的鲜奶<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%82%96%E8%9B%8B.md#1">炖蛋</a>还没有熟，可以再盖上锅盖多热2分钟，但只要用余温即可，不要开火。<br />
2. 可以磨少许姜汁加入，以增添香味，如果觉得成品还不够甜的话，可加少许黄糖配着吃。<br />
3. 用微波炉分两次加热20秒，是因为一次加热40秒可能会太热。</p>
