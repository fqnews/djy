<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】姜葱水浸鲩鱼</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/db6760a538f4f29a7b40aade30fdfc84-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>这道菜不仅味道鲜美，而且营养非常丰富，鱼肉的营养一点也没有流失，口感爽、滑、鲜、嫩，还有一丝姜葱的香味。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>鲩鱼肉质特别鲜美，且营养丰富，含有维生素B1、B2、烟酸、不饱和脂肪酸，以及钙、磷、铁、锌、硒等。常吃可以预防癌症、预防骨质疏松等。</p>
<p>中医认为，鲩鱼味甘性温，有平肝、祛风、暖胃的作用。鲩鱼含有的不饱和脂肪酸对血液循环有利，是心血管病人的良好食物。鲩鱼含有丰富的硒元素，经常食用有抗衰老、养颜的功效，对肿瘤有一定的防治作用。身体瘦弱、食欲不振的人食用鲩鱼，可以开胃、滋补。</p>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C%E8%91%B1%E6%B0%B4%E6%B5%B8%E9%B2%A9%E9%B1%BC.md#1">姜葱水浸鲩鱼</a>是一道经典家常菜，做法简单，但处理食材时间较长。这道菜不仅味道鲜美，而且营养非常丰富，鱼肉的营养一点也没有流失，口感爽、滑、鲜、嫩，还有一丝姜葱的香味。</p>
<p>这道菜在色彩的配置上也很讲究，以葱白、葱叶和生姜的辅助的色彩来衬托、突出主料，形成菜肴色彩的均匀柔和，外观看起来谐和悦目。</p>
<p>罗厨特别强调，姜和葱要切成丝，首先，因为鱼是长型的，如果切成粒或是切成块就不好看了。另外，切成丝容易出味道，把油烧得很热，浇到鱼肉、姜丝和葱丝的上面时，很高的油温可以把姜丝和葱丝炸出汁水，那么葱汁和姜汁就是会浸到鱼肉里面。</p>
<p>罗厨认为，这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C%E8%91%B1%E6%B0%B4%E6%B5%B8%E9%B2%A9%E9%B1%BC.md#1">姜葱水浸鲩鱼</a>的做法，不仅是鲩鱼的经典做法之一，也是替代清蒸鱼的很好的做法，可以解决没有高温蒸汽蒸鱼的难题。</p>
<h2>姜葱水浸鲩鱼</h2>
<p>主料：鲩鱼<br />
配料：姜丝、葱丝<br />
调料：生抽、胡椒粉、香油、盐</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将鲩鱼洗净起肉切大件。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/1-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12309607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/1-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 用油镬爆香姜葱后放入开水。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12309608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 放入一汤匙盐后把水煮开。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/3-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12309609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/3-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 放入鱼块，调至慢火。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12309610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 浸约10分钟至熟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/5-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12309611" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/5-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 捞出之前，用筷子试一下。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/6-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12309612" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/6-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 捞起鱼块放入热碟中排开。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/7-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12309613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/7-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 放入姜丝、葱丝、胡椒粉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12309618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 淋上热油将葱姜淋熟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/9-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12309620" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/9-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 最后放入4～5汤匙生抽即可。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12309621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴爽滑鲜嫩、香气四溢。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/11-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12309624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/11-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
