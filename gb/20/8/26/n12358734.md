<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】花胶小刺参伴青江菜 美颜润肤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/8-11-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>海味餐馆菜，吃了养颜美肤～花胶小刺参伴青江菜。（梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #000000;">【小编按】<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E8%83%B6.md#1">花胶</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">海参</a>汤是著名的养生汤；以中医的角度此汤品有促进新陈代谢、养颜美容和滋补养身的作用，对于身体虚弱的人尤为适合。中医说：冬病夏治，这一道汤品非常适合夏天滋补，一点都不会上火。海参肉嫩滑软，炖煮汤头时鲜甜回甘，在节庆时也是宴客常见佳肴。现在，不用等到特地节日或是上馆子，只要准备好食材，跟着梁厨料理，也能做出一道滋补的养生汤。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>
<p>湿<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E8%83%B6.md#1">花胶</a>10oz<br />
小刺参6oz<br />
大棵<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E6%B1%9F%E8%8F%9C.md#1">青江菜</a>五棵，切成玫瑰花形状。<br />
姜、蒜茸、干葱、酒各适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_12358737" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/1-35.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12358737 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/1-35-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">湿花胶、小刺参。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12358746" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/9-9.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12358746 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/9-9-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大棵<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E6%B1%9F%E8%8F%9C.md#1">青江菜</a>五棵，切成玫瑰花形状。</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【调味】</span></h2>
<p>鸡汤1杯<br />
蚝油1汤匙<br />
糖半茶匙<br />
xo酱油1汤匙<br />
胡椒粉、麻油少许<br />
粟粉2汤匙开水勾芡用</p>
<figure id="attachment_12358739" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2-28.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12358739 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2-28-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">xo酱油、栗粉、鸡汤、糖、麻油、胡椒粉、蚝油。（由左至右）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1.<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">海参</a>和花胶先用姜汁酒烫8分钟，捞出沥干备用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/3-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12358740 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/3-24-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><br />
2.热镬下油，爆香姜、蒜茸和干葱，加一点酒之后再加入调味汁液，放入花胶和海参滚10分钟，最后再用粟粉水勾芡。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12358741" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4-23.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12358741 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/4-23-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">花胶和海参滚10分钟之后才用粟粉水勾芡。</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.青江菜用油盐水烫熟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/5-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12358742 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/5-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><br />
4.烫好的青江菜摆盘在旁边，中间放入巳焖好的花胶海参，便可食用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【梁师傅小贴士】</span></h2>
<p>花胶和海参的骨胶原非常丰富，有补钙补水之作用，且胆固醇为零，适合女性食用，有美颜润肤之功效，常吃对皮肤及骨骼有益。◇</p>
