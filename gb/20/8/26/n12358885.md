  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【营养成分】</span></h2>  <p>净碳水化合物 6.3 g<br />  脂肪 41.5 g<br />  热量 517 cal<br />  膳食纤维 2.9 g<br />  蛋白质 28.2 g</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>  <p>酪梨油 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;10ml<br />  <ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%94%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲔鱼</a>罐头 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 75g<br />  高丽菜 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 50g<br />  红萝卜 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 10g<br />  洋葱 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230; 10g<br />  鸡蛋 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 1 颗</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【粉类材料】</span></h2>  <p>烘焙杏仁粉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 20g<br />  洋车前子粉 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 2g<br />  玫瑰盐 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;.. 1g</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12367943" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_1116127259.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12367943" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_1116127259-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">洋车前子皮壳（右）磨制成粉末是洋车前子粉（左）呈白色粉状，因为洋车前子种子富含丰富的膳食纤维质，适量摄食并多喝水，可以维持肠道蠕动、促进废物排出。（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【装饰】</span></h2>  <p>无糖酱油 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 15g<br />  无糖美乃滋 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;. 20g<br />  海苔丝 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230; 适量<br />  柴鱼片 &#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230;&#8230; 适量</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12367951" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_1765714835.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12367951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/shutterstock_1765714835-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">海苔和柴鱼片搭配起来风味特别咸甜，入口时浓浓的海味难以散去（shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <p>1 将粉类材料搅拌混合均匀。<br />  2 将高丽菜、红萝卜、洋葱洗净切成丝状，再加入酪梨油、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%94%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲔鱼</a>、鸡蛋搅拌均匀。<br />  3 将步骤1、2的材料混合均匀。<br />  4 平底锅热油（材料分量外），倒入步骤3的粉浆，以中小火慢慢煎熟并一边利用锅铲塑形，定型后翻面再煎至上色熟透即可盛盘。<br />  5 在表面装饰美乃滋、酱油、海苔丝及柴鱼片即可。</p>
