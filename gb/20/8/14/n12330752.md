<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>快速上桌“三杯小卷”  简单调味咸甜下饭</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JJ7F1"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/alniap312/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>夏夜小酌的最佳选择～三杯小卷。（图：Ｃ2食光）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【小编按】咬一口软嫩的酱香<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%8D%B7.md#1">小卷</a>，搭配清凉啤酒，咕噜下肚，一种满足的滋味从口中直达温暖的胃。夏天怎么能错过小卷呢？这时正是小卷盛产期，鲜美价廉，热炒起来咸甜滋味超级下饭，香气十足更是一口接着一口！周末时朋友来家用餐，“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E6%9D%AF.md#1">三杯</a>小卷”可是快速上桌的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%8B%E9%85%92%E8%8F%9C.md#1">下酒菜</a>名单之一，赶快趁着产季来炒一盘吧！</p>
<p>料理小技巧：先用热盐水浸泡，入锅后才不会出水影响浓度<br />
**千万不能拿去水龙头下冲水，会使甜味流失**</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【食材】</span></h2>
<p>熟<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E5%8D%B7.md#1">小卷</a> 200g<br />
辣椒 1根<br />
青葱 1根<br />
姜片 7～8片<br />
蒜头 7～9颗<br />
九层塔 1大把</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【调味料】</span></h2>
<p>黑麻油 1大匙<br />
香油 1大匙<br />
素蚝油 2大匙<br />
米酒 2大匙<br />
糖 1小匙</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>（1）烧一锅热水，水滚后加入少许盐巴，熄火，放入熟小卷浸泡2分钟，再捞起沥干备用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/029716164377000df34c9a54994533e8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12330770 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/029716164377000df34c9a54994533e8-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><br />
（2）蒜头切片、辣椒斜切片、青葱切段备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12330769" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/90bb11208b7b599a463d6662c46b6860.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12330769 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/90bb11208b7b599a463d6662c46b6860-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">姜片也需要切片备用。</figcaption></figure>
<p>（3）起锅煸姜片，小火煸至姜片干皱、卷曲，再加入蒜片炒至金黄。<br />
（4）加入葱白段、辣椒爆香，再加入蚝油、米酒和糖炒至浓稠。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/cb9db847d4844bd3ac197e333dc57d36.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12330771 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/cb9db847d4844bd3ac197e333dc57d36-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><br />
（5）放入小卷、葱绿翻炒均匀至收汁。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/a50aa2b5f484ed3dc9c3d853bf006662.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12330772 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/a50aa2b5f484ed3dc9c3d853bf006662-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><br />
（6）最后放入九层塔快速拌炒一下即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12330773" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/7876164762798ba7a7e6e8c706f539e1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12330773 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/7876164762798ba7a7e6e8c706f539e1-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放入九层塔后，别忘了再加盖呛米酒，让米酒慢慢渗透散发出酒香，这样才能上桌喔！</figcaption></figure>
