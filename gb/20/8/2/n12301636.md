<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>动手做：“十全十美”黏土父亲卡</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/IMG_5469-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>祝福天下辛劳的爸爸们，父亲节快乐！（图／张心慧提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>有着坚强臂膀的父亲，可以帮弱小的孩子们撑起一片天，在父亲的关爱下，子女能得以快乐的成长。有的人喜欢在父亲节时送太阳花，因为想到父亲，就会联想到太阳，父爱如阳光般温暖、可靠。</p>
<p>妈妈可以带着孩子一起为父亲做张黏土花朵卡片，送给辛劳的父亲一个祝福，让他感受到这份亲情的温暖吧！</p>
<p><strong>材料：</strong><br />
铁质的礼物盒盖、剪刀、彩色或白色黏土、水彩颜料、塑胶材质的牙签棒、彩色纸张、黑色签字笔、提带或是强力磁铁、白胶及胶带。</p>
<p><strong>做法：</strong><br />
黏土在湿度较高时，可以在完成配件形状后直接互相黏住组合，不需借助白胶等黏着剂黏合。</p>
<p><strong>?一、做花朵：</strong></p>
<p>1、做花瓣：1朵花需10片花瓣。</p>
<p>取一小团黄色黏土(约如手指的小指头指甲大小)，搓圆球→搓成水滴状→压扁。（如下图）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12301646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-1-600x419.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">步骤从右到左</figcaption></figure>
<p>用塑胶材质的牙签棒尖端在压扁的水滴形黏土花瓣上尖端侧压2道凹痕（如下图），这样是一片花瓣。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301647 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-2-600x426.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="426" /></a><br />
将完成的花瓣放在塑胶布或具有蜡光质的纸张或厚纸板上晾干，一般约静置2至3小时即可干燥到花瓣不黏、不变型的状态。本例有10朵花，所以做了100片花瓣，晾干，备用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-3.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-12301648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-3-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>2、搓一个小圆球→压扁成圆形→将5片花瓣摆上。（如下图）</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301649 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><br />
3、在2片花瓣间的下面那层位置上再黏上一片花瓣，花瓣正面(有2道压痕的)朝上。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-5.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-12301650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-5-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>4、搓一个约如花瓣的土球一半大小的红色黏土球，置于花朵中央处→稍微压扁→用牙签棒的尖端在红色圆形的花芯上搓小凹洞(如下图)。这样一朵花就完成了，共做10朵花备用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-6.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-12301651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-6-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301652 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-8.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-12301653" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-8-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><br />
<strong>二、做绿叶：</strong>本例的绿叶有2款样式。</p>
<p><strong>?1、太阳花的绿叶</strong>：取约与花瓣土球一样大小的绿色小土块→搓圆球→搓成水滴状→压扁→将绿叶放在手掌上，用牙签棒的尖端画出一道叶脉的痕迹→用手指整理形状，晾干，备用。（如下图）</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301654 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-9-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301655 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301657 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301658 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301659 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-13-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><br />
为了让作品的绿叶有大、小的变化，绿色土块的大小要略有差异，整理后的形状也要略有不同(如下图)。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-14.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301660 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-14-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><br />
<strong> 2、卷玫瑰的小绿叶</strong>：方法步骤与太阳花的绿叶做法前段一样，叶脉就要画出主脉(中间的)与2侧的小叶脉。画叶脉前将叶片至于手掌侧边，这样画好叶脉后叶片即会呈现某种程度的弯曲状，近似于自然状态，画好叶脉后约略整理形状，晾干，备用。(如下图)</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-15.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301661 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-15-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-16.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301662 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-16-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-17.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301663 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-17-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><br />
<strong>三、做卷玫瑰</strong>：取红色小土块→搓圆球→搓成约6公分左右的长条状(不要太细)→压扁→由一端往另一端将土片条卷曲成一个圆形，从正面看就像一朵小玫瑰。(如下图)</p>
<figure id="attachment_12301664" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-18.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301664" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-18-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">步骤从左到右</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-19.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301665 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-19-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><br />
<strong>四、做螃蟹</strong>：用2只螃蟹代表向父亲说声：“谢谢！”</p>
<p>1、蟹身背甲：取近金黄色土块→搓圆球→压成中央稍微凸起、周边较扁的型状→整理成略近椭圆形→用原子笔笔盖的圆弧压出螃蟹身上的凹痕。(如下图)</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-20.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301667 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-21.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301668 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-21-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-22.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301669 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><br />
<strong>2、头顶大螯</strong>：搓3个大小不一的圆球。最大的用剪刀剪开一些，并整理形状使其成螯的样子，将3个黏成一支大螯，再黏于蟹的背甲上，为了让螃蟹有造型，将蟹螯跨于卫生纸条上晾干。1只螃蟹有2支大螯，因此需要做2组。(如下图)</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-23.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301670 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-24.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301671 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><br />
<strong> 3、脚</strong>：两个一组，分别搓3组大小不一的圆球→再一一搓成两端略尖的形状→稍微将其弯曲像蟹脚状。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-25.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301672 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-25-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><br />
4、趁着蟹脚还潮湿将背甲与蟹脚组合成一只螃蟹，晾干。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-26.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301673 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-27.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301674 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-27-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><br />
<strong>五、土条88</strong>:　取2块小蓝色黏土→分别搓圆球→搓成细土条→分别将两端往中间黏合成一个小圈圈即成一个8字型（如下图）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-28.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-12301675" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-28-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a></p>
<p><strong>??? 六、做小卡片：</strong><br />
1、剪一张约6公分宽9公分长的米黄色纸张，先直向对折再横向对折，外侧剪一个圆弧，内侧也剪一个圆弧，剪好后摊开横向对折即是一个圆圈。（如下图）</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-29.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301676 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-29-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><br />
2、 剪一张约7公分宽10公分长的浅蓝绿色纸张，直向对折并置于米黄色圆圈内侧，剪一个比米黄色圆圈略大一些的圆形，摊开。（如下图）</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-30.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301677 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-30-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-31.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301678 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-31-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><br />
3、用白胶将两张纸黏住，再用色笔描出米黄8，并写上谢谢爸爸等字样。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-32.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12301679 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-32-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><br />
七、将配件组合成卡片：将花朵摆于盒盖周边，放上螃蟹及卡片，用卷曲小玫瑰装饰，适切的放上绿叶陪衬花朵及小玫瑰。此时所有配件都已干燥，不具黏合性，所以需要用白胶黏合。完成。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-33.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-12301680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/wenhua1-33-600x408.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="408" /></a></p>
<p>可于铁盒盖背面黏上强力磁铁，将“十全十美父亲卡”贴在冰箱门上，或是利用提带将父亲卡挂在门上。</p>
