<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【高力国际珀斯房地产专栏】 锂加工业推动Kwinana工业地块需求</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/Screen-Shot-2020-08-03-at-3.49.45-pm-320x200.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年08月02日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">商业房地产服务公司<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E5%8A%9B%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85.md#1">高力国际</a>（</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Colliers</span> <span lang="en-AU">International</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）的研究显示，西澳成为锂加工基地，是导致</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Kwinana</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">工业区</span></span><span lang="en-AU">1</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万平方米以上的工业地块供不应求的其中一个因素。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">该公司销售主管</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Justin</span> <span lang="en-AU">Mabarrack</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">表示，最近对西澳锂矿产品感兴趣的买家主要来自回收运营商、工程公司、工业用地开发商、运输和物流部门以及农业和建筑业。“推动工业地块需求的动力部分来自锂矿业，我们在为几个买家找地块时，发现库存短缺。”他说。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">澳洲天齐锂业公司（</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Tianqi</span> <span lang="en-AU">Lithium</span> <span lang="en-AU">Australia</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）在</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Kwinana</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">建立了氢氧化锂加工厂。全球对氢氧化锂的需求快速增长，氢氧化锂是用于电动汽车、家用储能和锂可充电电池的主要成分。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">Mabarrack</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">表示，</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Kwinana</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">私营工业地块相对较少，并且对靠近其它工业场地的迫切要求加剧了工业地块的短缺。“</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Kwinana</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">工业区是西澳西海岸贸易地区（</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Western</span> <span lang="en-AU">Trade</span> <span lang="en-AU">Coast</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）的物业之一，属综合工业区，为石油、天然气、资源和农业提供服务，这些产业是西澳经济的主要推手。”他说。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">2018</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E5%8A%9B%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85.md#1">高力国际</a>在</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Kwinana</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">销售的两个地块都属于大块工业基地。在去年</span></span><span lang="en-AU">9</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月的一次市场外交易中，</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Mabarrack</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">以</span></span><span lang="en-AU">385</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元出售了</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Kwinana</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">海滨</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Yeates</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">路</span></span><span lang="en-AU">1</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">号</span></span><span lang="en-AU">11,000</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">平米的工业物业，其中带有一个</span></span><span lang="en-AU">2400</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">平米的仓库。该物业属业主自用，计划用以工程和制造。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">去年早些时候，位于</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Kwinana</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">海滨</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Butcher</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">街</span></span><span lang="en-AU">1</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">号占地</span></span><span lang="en-AU">2.62</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公顷的工业物业由工业器材供应商</span></span><span lang="en-AU">J&amp;P</span> <span lang="en-AU">Group</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">以</span></span><span lang="en-AU">450</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元收购，之前曾有多人出价。该物业拥有</span></span><span lang="en-AU">5000</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">平米的库房及开发潜力。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="en-AU">Mabarrack</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">说，锂加工业需要相应供应商的投入，包括制造、工程和化学产业，他们需要靠近锂加工设施。“人们越来越意识到，西澳处于锂加工业的独特地理位置，这将使</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Kwinana</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">和西海岸贸易区有机会成为一个更大的工业基地。”他接着说。</span></span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">西澳目前拥有</span></span><span lang="en-AU">7</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">座锂矿，西南部</span></span><span lang="en-AU">Greenbushes</span><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">镇拥有世界上最高级别的锂矿资源。</span>此外，全球化工集团</span><span lang="en-AU">Albemarle</span> <span lang="en-AU">Corporation</span><span lang="zh-CN">获得了</span><span lang="en-AU">Bunbury</span><span lang="zh-CN">附近</span><span lang="en-AU">Kemerton</span><span lang="zh-CN">工业园区建立锂加工厂的环境许可。</span></p>
<p><span lang="zh-CN">本专栏由高力国际</span>-Colliers International<span lang="zh-CN">珀斯分部供稿 ， 欲查阅更多房地产投资报告及相关资讯，请登录高力国际官方网站：</span><ahref="https://www.colliers.com.au/">https://www.colliers.com.au</a><span lang="zh-CN">。</span></p>
