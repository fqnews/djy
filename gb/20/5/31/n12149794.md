<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：母鸭带着小鸭 暖心母子情展露无遗</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3a7abf859ce7b0bc2bf1d7571c72fef6.jpg" />
<h6>母鸭带着小鸭，暖心母子情展露无遗 。（大纪元合成图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年06月02日讯】（大纪元记者王玫心综合报导）在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E5%B7%9E.md#1">加州</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E6%8B%89%E7%B1%B3%E8%BE%BE%E5%B8%82.md#1">阿拉米达市</a>（Alameda）一个风和日丽的早晨，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%AF%8D%E9%B8%AD.md#1">母鸭</a>和一群<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E9%B8%AD%E5%AD%90.md#1">小鸭子</a>们正在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%BA%AA%E6%B0%B4.md#1">溪水</a>旁小憩。柔和的阳光下清风徐徐的吹着，潺潺的水流声仿佛天然的催眠曲，如此惬意的光景，让<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E9%B8%AD%E5%AD%90.md#1">小鸭子</a>们安心地靠在鸭妈妈身边甜甜地睡着了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12149807" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_-SWEET-DREAMS_duck_01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12149807" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_-SWEET-DREAMS_duck_01-600x383.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="383" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E5%B7%9E.md#1">加州</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E6%8B%89%E7%B1%B3%E8%BE%BE%E5%B8%82.md#1">阿拉米达市</a>的鸭妈妈与小鸭子。图中的小鸭子们睡着了。（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不一会儿，周围的动静让它们纷纷醒来。鸭妈妈警觉地靠近小鸭们并查看四周，似乎在问：“是谁在看着我们？”小鸭子们紧紧地依偎着鸭妈妈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12149809" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_SOMEONE-IS-WATCHING_duck_02.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12149809 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_SOMEONE-IS-WATCHING_duck_02-600x376.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="376" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸭妈妈警觉的样子像是在问：“谁在看我们？”，小鸭子们也紧紧依偎着鸭妈妈。（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当确定无危险后，鸭妈妈将身体压低靠近小鸭们像是在安抚它们：“别担心，妈妈在！”然后便带着孩子们沿着岸边往前走。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12149810" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_3-DONT-WORRY_duck_03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12149810" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_3-DONT-WORRY_duck_03-600x403.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸭妈妈将身体压低，靠近小鸭们像是在安抚它们说：“别担心，妈妈在！”（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12149812" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_-MAYBE-WE-SHOULD-LEAVE_duck_04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12149812" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_-MAYBE-WE-SHOULD-LEAVE_duck_04-600x395.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="395" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸭妈妈将小鸭子们带走。（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12149814" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_GO-AHEAD_duck_05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12149814" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_GO-AHEAD_duck_05-600x397.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图中鸭妈妈带着小鸭子们往前走。（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<p>行进间，好奇的小鸭子们不时地会停下来，鸭妈妈时而在队伍前面勘查带路，时而留意小鸭子们的安全，轻声地呼唤着小鸭们，似乎提醒着：“快点跟上喔！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12149816" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_-DONT-STAND-THERE_duck_06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12149816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_-DONT-STAND-THERE_duck_06-600x397.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小鸭子逗留，鸭妈妈呼唤着，像是在说：“别站在那儿！”。?（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12149820" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_-MOMMY-WILL-LEAD_duck_07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12149820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_-MOMMY-WILL-LEAD_duck_07-600x388.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸭妈妈在队伍前勘查带路，并呼唤小鸭们，似乎提醒着：“快点跟上喔！”（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12149821" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_FOLLOW-ME_duck_08.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12149821" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_FOLLOW-ME_duck_08-600x399.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸭妈妈边走边呼唤小鸭们：“跟我来！”（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不久，它们来到水边，鸭妈妈跳进水里缓缓转过身来，温柔地看着小鸭子们像是在鼓励着它们：“这儿很安全！快点跳下来吧！”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12149822" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_READY-TO-JUMP_09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12149822" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_READY-TO-JUMP_09-600x370.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="370" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图为鸭妈妈带着小鸭们勘查后，准备跳水。（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12149841" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_JUMP-NOW_10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12149841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_JUMP-NOW_10-600x391.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸭妈妈先行跳进水里。（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当小鸭子跳进水里时，鸭妈妈一样轻声叫着，像是在说：“好棒！继续游！” 最后，小鸭子们一一跳进水里跟随着妈妈一同畅游在清凉的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%BA%AA%E6%B0%B4.md#1">溪水</a>里。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12149828" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_GOOD-KEEP-GOING_11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12149828" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_GOOD-KEEP-GOING_11-600x391.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸭妈妈回头就像示意小鸭子们：“这儿很安全！快点跳下来吧！”，当小鸭子下水后又轻声叫着像在鼓励：“好棒！继续游！”（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看着鸭妈妈一路上对小鸭子的细心呵护，以及小鸭子们对妈妈的信赖与依赖，这温暖的一幕，是不是让人想起什么，而感到很暖心呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_12149830" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_-GOODBYE_12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12149830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/20200531_shouhui_-GOODBYE_12-600x372.jpg" alt="Richard Linn, duck" width="600" b="372" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图为小鸭子跟随鸭妈妈畅游在清凉的溪水里。（图片提供/Richard Linn）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再见！鸭妈妈和小鸭子们！</p>
