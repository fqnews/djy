<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：静静地等待</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/1511251527522483-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（fotolia）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>怎么会那么巧<br />
一走进鸟园<br />
天空便突然乌云密布<br />
落起倾盆大雨？<br />
无奈呀无奈！<br />
我只得找一处屋檐躲雨</p>
<p>雨是一种恶势力<br />
横行霸道<br />
将我囚禁着<br />
如那些被囚禁的众鸟<br />
逸失原来的天空<br />
纵有雄心壮志，一飞冲天之能<br />
一展翅便要撞头碰壁<br />
只得举目向雨<br />
难有作为<br />
更且，雨犹不知足<br />
不时借着风势<br />
泼将进来，泼湿我身<br />
使我极为难受</p>
<p>未带雨具<br />
我又能如何？<br />
只得缩着头颈<br />
郁郁寡欢，闷闷不乐<br />
如那些被囚的众鸟<br />
举目向雨<br />
任雨借着风势，泼洒肆虐<br />
默默地等待</p>
<p>等待雨尽快停歇<br />
等待天尽快放晴<br />
（天雨必然是短暂的<br />
天晴才真是恒久的）<br />
我可以自由发挥</p>
<p><strong>Wait Silently </strong>&emsp; Hsu chiCheng</p>
<p>——Meet with rain at a Bird Park</p>
<p>Why does so coincidence<br />
The clouds gather in the sky suddenly<br />
And rain cats and dogs<br />
As soon as I step into the bird park?<br />
I can not help<br />
Just to keep away from the rain under the eaves</p>
<p>The rain is a kind of vicious power<br />
Tyrannize<br />
Imprison me<br />
Let me like those birds<br />
To lost their sky original<br />
Even they possess the lofty aspirations, the ability to soar up into the sky with one start<br />
They will bump against the wall while spread the wings to fly<br />
Just to lift my eyes to the rain<br />
Difficult to do something<br />
Farther more, the rain doesn’t content<br />
They make use of the trend of the wind<br />
Spill forward, spill me into wet<br />
Let me feel unwell extremely</p>
<p>For not bring the rain gear<br />
How can I do?<br />
I just shrink my neck<br />
Made me blue, feel depressed<br />
Like the birds<br />
Lift my eyes to the rain<br />
Let the rain spill in, spill play havoc with<br />
Waiting silently</p>
<p>Waiting the rain will stop quickly<br />
Waiting the weather will be fine quickly<br />
(Rain must be short<br />
Fine must be forever truly)<br />
I can do my best as I will@</p>
