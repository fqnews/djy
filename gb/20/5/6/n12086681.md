<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>动手做：母亲节黏土花朵卡</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/IMG_5634-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>亲手精心制作的卡片，温暖妈妈的心。（图／张心慧提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>母亲节又将来到，为表达对母亲的爱与感谢母亲默默的为家庭的付出，许多人都费尽心思想送什么礼物给母亲。礼物不在轻重，母亲最想收到的就是那份心意！</p>
<p>无论小孩还是大人，您亲手精心制作的卡片，就足以温暖妈妈的心，快来动手做一张吧！此篇教授的形似金针花的花与叶的制作，您也可以按照自己的组合方式，也可以用纸张取代铁盒盖做背景铺垫……发挥您的创意吧！</p>
<p><strong>材料：</strong>礼物盒盖或卡纸、剪刀、彩色或白色黏土、水彩颜料、塑胶材质的牙签棒、彩色纸张、蓝色签字笔、白胶。</p>
<p><strong>做法：</strong>处于高湿度状态下的黏土，在完成配件形状后还具有很强的黏性，可以直接互相黏住组合，不需借助白胶等黏着剂黏合。</p>
<p><strong>一、做花朵：</strong>本例做了12朵颜色不同，大小也略有差异的花朵。做法都是一样的，您可根据自己喜欢的颜色做搭配。</p>
<p><strong>1.做花瓣：</strong>1朵花需6片花瓣（如下图步骤）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12087172" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">1.做一个卫生纸晾干架</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2.取6小团金黄色黏土(约如手指的小指头大小)，搓圆球。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-3-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">3.将圆球搓成水滴状→将水滴状两端分别再搓使其变长、另端也略呈尖状(步骤从左到右)。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">4.将水滴压扁，放在手掌外侧近边缘处。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087176" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087176" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-5-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">5.用塑胶材质的牙签棒尖端在压扁的花瓣上中央压划出1道凹痕。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087177" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087177" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-6-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">6.完成一朵花的6片花瓣。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-7-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">7.将3片花瓣组成第一层花。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087179" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-8-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">8.于2片花瓣间再于下层黏上一片花瓣。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-10-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">侧面视角。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">9.搓一个比花瓣小的圆球，置于花朵中央当花芯。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087185" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087185" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">10.用牙签棒尖端将花芯戳出几个小洞。花朵组合完成后在卫生纸晾干架上晾干，备用。</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>注：</strong>完成等待组合的花瓣要暂放在塑胶布或具有蜡光质的纸张或厚纸板上，这样可避免黏土花瓣黏住晾干的垫子无法拿起。</p>
<p><strong>二、做绿叶：</strong></p>
<p>1. 取绿色小土块→搓圆球→搓成水滴状→将水滴状两端分别再搓使其变长、另端也略呈尖状→压扁(下图步骤从上到下)。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087187 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-13-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<ol start="2">
<li>将绿叶放在手掌上，用牙签棒的尖端画出3道叶脉的痕迹，运用双手手指整理形状，晾干，备用。为了让作品的绿叶有大、小的变化，绿色土块的大小要略有差异，整理后的形状也要略有不同。(如下图)<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-14.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087188 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-14-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></li>
</ol>
<p>三、 做茎：取绿色小土块→搓圆球→搓成水滴状→搓成长条状的细土条→将土条对折，让2段长短不一，约略成弯曲状(如下图)。晾干，备用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-15.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087189 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-15-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>四、组合：将花朵、叶片、茎在蛋糕盒盖内或卡纸上、外摆出满意的样子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12087191" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-16.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087191" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-16-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">先不要黏贴，先试摆看看效果。</figcaption></figure>
<p>五、</p>
<p><strong>1、做字卡：</strong>“母亲节快乐”5张字卡。步骤如下图。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12087192" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-17.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087192" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-17-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">1. 剪一张长约8公分、宽约4公分的淡粉红色纸张。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087193" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-18.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087193" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2.对折。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087194" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-19.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087194" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">3. 剪出像康乃馨花瓣的形状后将对折处剪开，成2片花瓣。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087195" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-20.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087195" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">4.剪一张长约10公分、宽约6公分的粉红色纸张，对折，然后放上康乃馨花瓣。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087196" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-21.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087196" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-21-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">5. 剪出比花瓣大一些的形状，剪好后摊开。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12087197" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-22.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087197" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">6.将对折处剪开，成2片大花瓣。共做5片(组)。</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2、剪绿色花托：</strong>剪一张长约6公分、宽约5公分的绿色纸张，对折后剪成约略像梯型；在梯形底部(较宽的那端)再剪出像草的样子，并将对折处剪开，成2片草。(如下图)<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-23.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087198 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a> <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-24.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087199 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-24-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>3.将5张字卡组合，试摆于花朵间的空白处，检视字卡大小的适合度，若不适合，就加以修改调整。</p>
<p>4.在5片淡粉红色康乃馨花瓣上别写出“母亲节快乐”后描粗字体，将字卡黏于粉红色大花瓣上并黏上绿花托。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-25.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12087200 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/wenhua1-25-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a></p>
<p>将所有制作好的花朵与字卡等黏于盒盖或卡纸上，母亲节快乐卡即完成。</p>
