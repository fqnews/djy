<h6>古早味蜂蜜蛋糕，质感扎实、入口松软又充满浓浓的蜂蜜香气，令人陶醉不已！（图：C2食光提供）

	<h3><span style="color: #000080;"><strong><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B0%B7%E9%9B%A8.md#1">谷雨</a>节气</strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_12079122" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5ebb6a613fd72d5fbcba24d42f6d7951.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12079122" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/5ebb6a613fd72d5fbcba24d42f6d7951-450x253.jpg" alt="谷雨,Ｃ2食光,节气料理" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B0%B7%E9%9B%A8.md#1">谷雨</a>节气（<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“谷雨”是春季最后一个节气，过了这一个节气就进入炎炎夏日。除了气温逐渐升高，降雨量也会增加。因此，谷雨养生注重祛湿利水，建议多吃一些健脾除湿的食材，比方吃<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C.md#1">姜</a>可以有效帮助排除体内的湿气。</p>
<p>* 谷雨建议食材：山药、薏仁、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A7%9C.md#1">姜</a>、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a></p>
<h3><span style="color: #000080;"><strong><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a>蛋糕</strong></span></h3>
<p>母亲节快到了，想要送什么给妈妈呢？自制<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蜂蜜蛋糕</a>是一份代表回忆与爱的礼物！它是我们小时候在街角杂货店买一小块蛋糕的回忆。蜂蜜蛋糕质感扎实有弹性、松软口感、迎面扑鼻的蜂蜜香气，令人陶醉不已。出外购买有时候都要排队，在家自己动手做看看，实惠又不用担心添加人工添加物！</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">* 料理小技巧：</span></h3>
<p>1. 打蛋白要分次加入砂糖，因为一次加入太多的砂糖会不容易成型。<br />
2. 加入面粉要分次加，才不会结块。<br />
3. 面糊放入模具要抬到一定的高度才倒，进烤箱前要震一下，这是为了要震出面糊的大气泡，大气泡会让蛋糕内部组织不够细致，而造成塌陷。<br />
4. 出烤箱后也要震一下，让热气出来再放凉。</p>
<h3>【蛋黄面糊】</h3>
<p>蛋黄5颗<br />
细砂糖25g<br />
高筋面粉100g</p>
<h3>【蛋白霜】</h3>
<p>蛋白4个<br />
细砂糖75g</p>
<h3>【蜂蜜水】</h3>
<p>蜂蜜50g<br />
温水7cc</p>
<h3>【做法】</h3>
<p>1. 蛋黄加上糖混合均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079126" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/68be0de1eb2cf5585e37435c7a304c16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12079126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/68be0de1eb2cf5585e37435c7a304c16-450x299.jpg" alt="C2食光,蛋糕,蜂蜜蛋糕" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 蛋白打发，其中分次加入砂糖，直到硬性打发。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079127" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/f30e86b829b07085005ac20b5b7c8049.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12079127" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/f30e86b829b07085005ac20b5b7c8049-450x324.jpg" alt="C2食光,蛋糕,蜂蜜蛋糕" width="450" b="324" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将蛋黄液加入蛋白霜混合均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079144" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/ad3afe7a8b5e85db1c9d3f447129e239.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12079144" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/ad3afe7a8b5e85db1c9d3f447129e239-450x356.jpg" alt="C2食光,蛋糕,蜂蜜蛋糕" width="450" b="356" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 高筋面粉分3次过筛进入，以切拌方式混合均匀以成面糊。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079145" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/f2c99a0878a4589ee32d8245717c94ee.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12079145" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/f2c99a0878a4589ee32d8245717c94ee-450x319.jpg" alt="C2食光,蛋糕,蜂蜜蛋糕" width="450" b="319" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 面糊倒入事先混合好的蜂蜜水。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079146" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/639cf14dcfc8acdc15a23b36154fa6e2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12079146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/639cf14dcfc8acdc15a23b36154fa6e2-450x390.jpg" alt="C2食光,蛋糕,蜂蜜蛋糕" width="450" b="390" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 以切拌方式确实混合均匀。<br />
7. 由高处将面糊倒入烤模中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079152" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/f1d07f9346715b3cdce71fff31c31586.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12079152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/f1d07f9346715b3cdce71fff31c31586-450x315.jpg" alt="C2食光,蛋糕,蜂蜜蛋糕" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 放入已经预热到160℃的烤箱中烘烤60-65分钟至表面呈现金黄色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079153" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/a2d14c9d2bbbf2e3537f0b7b8f65c2fc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12079153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/a2d14c9d2bbbf2e3537f0b7b8f65c2fc-450x316.jpg" alt="C2食光,蛋糕,蜂蜜蛋糕" width="450" b="316" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
