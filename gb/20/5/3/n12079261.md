<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>客家美食米苔目 农村阿嬷的“手路菜”</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/g"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/er2596/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>古早味米苔目。（C2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3><span style="color: #008000;">【小编语】</span></h3>
<p>入口弹牙的平民小吃<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8B%94%E7%9B%AE.md#1">米苔目</a>，在台湾早期社会，它是农家子弟的成长料理，甜咸皆宜。咸食搭配<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B2%B9%E8%91%B1%E9%85%A5.md#1">油葱酥</a>、虾米、韭菜、芹菜、豆芽菜和猪绞肉同煮，可以区分成干、湿两种口味；甜食佐以糖水、花生等其他配料，相当消暑。</p>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E8%8B%94%E7%9B%AE.md#1">米苔目</a>来自客家传统菜，因为制作过程犹如用米筛筛米，客家人称为“米筛目”，台语则是“米苔目”。客家人做菜讲究油、酥、香，因此煮成咸食的话，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B2%B9%E8%91%B1%E9%85%A5.md#1">油葱酥</a>肯定不会缺席。如果想要放入汤头食用，可以加入<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8F%87.md#1">香菇</a>水、香虾米会添加回甘滋味。这是一道阿嬷（台语：外婆的意思）的手路菜，也是很多人记忆中的幸福滋味。</p>
<h3>【食材】</h3>
<p>米苔目 300g<br />
开阳（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B2%E8%99%BE%E7%B1%B3.md#1">干虾米</a>）1大匙<br />
干<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%8F%87.md#1">香菇</a> 适量<br />
猪肉丝150g<br />
猪高汤 500ml<br />
香菇水 适量<br />
红葱酥 适量<br />
豆芽菜 100g<br />
韭菜 50g<br />
红萝卜丝 适量<br />
芹菜末 少许</p>
<h3>【腌肉食材】</h3>
<p>猪肉丝150g<br />
白胡椒粉 少许<br />
盐 少许<br />
太白粉 适量<br />
香油 少许</p>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;">【做法】</span></h3>
<p>1. 香菇泡水。<br />
2. 所有食材洗净后，芹菜切末、韭菜切段、泡软的香菇切丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079294" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/eeab71f53dfd75034b0b1a28a58c8d9d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12079294 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/eeab71f53dfd75034b0b1a28a58c8d9d-450x315.jpg" alt="米苔目,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">韭菜切段（<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将猪肉抓腌（太白粉水、香油、盐、胡椒）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079296" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/b86e154a51dca50ccbb1303f2c587479.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12079296" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/b86e154a51dca50ccbb1303f2c587479-450x315.jpg" alt="米苔目,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热锅下油，香菇丝、开阳，炒出香气后。<br />
5. 放入肉丝拌炒开炒熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079297" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/b098aa0879f711ea1609cf14966f776b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12079297 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/b098aa0879f711ea1609cf14966f776b-450x315.jpg" alt="米苔目,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">热锅下油，倒入香菇丝、开阳炒出香气之后，再放入猪肉丝拌炒。（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 倒入香菇水、油葱酥、高汤，煮滚调味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079298" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/d4247d400b7ae05028032ba0782481e9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12079298" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/d4247d400b7ae05028032ba0782481e9-450x315.jpg" alt="米苔目,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 下米苔目，滚一下后，下豆芽菜、韭菜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12079299" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/0f25daf96b071ff50dc6497ebdc214f4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12079299" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/0f25daf96b071ff50dc6497ebdc214f4-450x315.jpg" alt="米苔目,Ｃ2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 盛碗后，撒上芹菜末以及白胡椒粉，完成</p>
