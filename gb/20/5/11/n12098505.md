<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>赴素里医院捐帽 枫情读书会集资献爱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/05/S__59400264-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图：空中枫情读书会面对疫情，大家出谋划策帮助社区，为前线医务人员提供捐款与手工帽子，赢得社会赞赏。（空大枫情读书会提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年05月11日讯】<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">始于</span></span>2019<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">年底的世纪疫情“新冠肺炎”迅速爆发成为全球厄耗，加拿大亦无幸免，从</span></span>2020<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">年三月扩散全国，警戒状态至今未歇。</span></span></p>
<p>4<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>8<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">日，空大学生社团<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9E%AB%E6%83%85%E8%AF%BB%E4%B9%A6%E4%BC%9A.md#1">枫情读书会</a>举办视讯干部会议，梁玉燕会长在临时动议中提出为感谢第一线医疗团队不眠不休为全民守护健康，因而建议捐款支持。经讨论后决议由本会提拨＄</span></span>550<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">元加币，另由会长、副会长们认捐＄</span></span>450<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">的配合款，共计＄</span></span>1000<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">元。</span></span>4<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>9<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">日网上捐赠素里医院基金会</span></span>(Surrey Hospital Foundation)<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>

<p>4/18<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">再增购</span></span>32.3 meters <span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">布疋，继续由</span></span>Nelly <span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">和 </span></span>Lily <span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">共同制作</span></span>180<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">顶，</span></span>Vivian <span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">何亦提供私人材料自制</span></span>10<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">顶。手工帽子制作工序包括：制版、排版、裁剪、裁片手烫定型、上松紧带、拼布车缝、折叠包装。其间，</span></span>Joyce <span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">捐赠</span></span>6<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">卷松紧带和养生茶及</span></span>Kuo Yen <span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">提供</span></span>75<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">度酒精和消毒湿巾，还有老师、学长和</span></span>Annie <span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">等三位副会长赞助经费。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">大家散居在本拿比、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>和白石，透过分工合作，热情贡献专长，克服既要运送，又要保持距离讨论、加工、会合等困难。之后一一化零为整，逐件完成实用又美观的工作帽。</span></span></p>
<p>5<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span>8<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">日由</span></span>Nelly <span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">和</span></span>Lily<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">两位副会长送</span></span>220<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">顶工作帽，到 </span></span>Surrey Hospital Foundation,<span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">由</span></span>CEO Jane Adams <span style="font-family: 新细明体;"><span lang="zh-TW">女士代表接受，完成这项自发性用爱接力的任务。</span></span></p>
