<p>【单字/词解析】<br />
● recovery，名词/r??k?v.?.i/恢复健康<br />
自然发音：re-CO-ve-ry<br />
英解：the process of becoming well again after an illness or injury<br />
例句：The doctor said she&#8217;d make a quick recovery.（医生说她很快就会康复。）<br />
用法：If someone is in recovery, they are being given a course of treatment to help them recover from something such as a drug habit or mental illness.<br />
（如果某人正在康复中，通常有一个疗程，来帮助他们从毒瘾或精神疾病之类的问题中康复。）</p>
<p>● monument，名词/?mɑ?n.j?.m?nt/纪念碑<br />
自然发音：MO-nu-ment<br />
英解：a structure or building that is built to hono(u)r a special person or event<br />
例句：This pillar is a monument to all those who died in the American Civil War.（这根纪念碑柱是悼念所有在美国内战／南北战争中丧生的人。）<br />
用法：be a monument to sth（是&#8230;的成果），例：The annual arts festival is a monument to her vision and hard work.（一年一度的艺术节展示了她广阔的视野和勤奋创作的成果。）</p>
<p><a name="_Toc2020052801"></a><br />
● half-staff，动词/?h?f?st?f/降下半旗<br />
自然发音：half-STAFF<br />
英解：a flag that is at half-staff has been lowered to the middle of a pole, often in order to show respect for someone who has died<br />
例句：When should the flag be flown at half staff?（什么时候应该降半旗？）<br />
用法：又可以叫 half-mast，例：All national flags will fly at half-mast at home today. （所有自家置挂的国旗将在今天降下半旗。）</p>
<p>● spokesman，名词/?sp??ksm?n/发言人<br />
自然发音：SPO-kes-man<br />
英解：someone who is chosen by a group or organization to speak officially to the public for them<br />
例句：The spokesman never gave a direct answer to any question of the reporters.（发言人从未直接回答记者的任何问题。）<br />
用法：也可以用spokesperson，例：A government spokesperson made a statement to the press.（政府发言人在记者会发表了声明。）</p>
<p>● deflect，动词/d??flekt/使&#8230;转向<br />
自然发音：de-FLE-ct<br />
英解：to (cause to) change direction, or to prevent something from being directed at you<br />
例句：He deflected the ball away from the goal.（他把球挡出了球门外。）<br />
用法：The committee is seeking to deflect criticism by blaming me.（委员会试图通过指责我来转移批评。）</p>
<p>● carnage，动词/?kɑ?r.n?d?/大屠杀<br />
自然发音：CAR-nage<br />
英解：the violent killing of large numbers of people, especially in war<br />
例句：Refugees crossed the border to escape the carnage in their homeland.（难民越过边境逃离来自家园的大屠杀。）<br />
用法：拼法类似的单字：carnation，名词，“康乃馨”。carnival，名词，“嘉年华会”。</p>
<p>● disinformation，名词/?d?s?nf?r?me???n/假消息<br />
自然发音：dis-in-for-MA-tion<br />
英解：false information spread in order to deceive people<br />
例句：It was a very plausible piece of disinformation.（这是一个非常合理的假消息。）<br />
用法：类似单字：untruth, misinformation, fabrication, etc.<br />
<a name="_Toc2020052802"></a><br />
另外，white lie 为“善意谎言” ，例句：When the truth hurts , a little white lie can heal.（当真相造成伤害时，一个小的善意谎言可以平缓。）<br />
其他口语上的“谎言”有：bullshit（BS）, baloney, nonsense, bluff, etc</p>
<p>● propaganda，名词/?pr?p??ɡ?nd?/宣传、思想<br />
自然发音：pro-pa-GAN-da<br />
英解：information, especially false information, that a government or organization spreads in order to influence people&#8217;s opinions and beliefs<br />
例句：At school we were fed communist propaganda.（就学期间，我们被强制灌输共产党的宣传/思想。）<br />
用法：There has been so much propaganda against smoking that many people have given it up.（反对吸烟的宣传很多，促成许多人成功戒烟。）</p>
<p>● disgrace，名词/d?s?ɡre?s/不光彩的行为（丢脸）<br />
自然发音：dis-GRACE<br />
英解：the loss of other people&#8217;s respect because of something bad that you have done<br />
例句：Andrew behaved so badly on the school trip that he was sent home in disgrace early.<br />
（安德鲁在学校旅行中表现很差，以至于提前丢脸地被送回家了。）<br />
用法：可以当动词使用，例：No one can disgrace us but ourselves.（除了我们自己，没有人可以让我们丢脸。）</p>
<p>● plague，名词/ple?ɡ/瘟疫<br />
自然发音：PLA-gue<br />
英解：any serious disease that spreads quickly to a lot of people and usually ends in death<br />
例句：The plague has spread over many states.（瘟疫已经扩散到许多州了。）<br />
用法：当动词时，意思为“使折磨、困扰”，例：The fatigue has been plaguing me several months.<br />
（疲劳已经折磨我数月之久。）</p>
<p>【背景知识】</p>
<p>● 美国“纽约大都会运输局”（MTA）<br />
全名 Metropolitan Transportation Authority，提供快慢速巴士、地铁、大纽约地区的区域铁路和营运纽约市许多收费桥梁和隧道。</p>
<p>● 美国交通部（USDOT）<br />
全名 U.S. Department of Transportation，是美国联邦政府的交通行政部门。</p>
<p>● 《新冠病毒援助、救济和经济安全法》（CARES ACT）<br />
全名 Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act，简称 CARES ACT。川普于2020年3月27日签署规模2.2兆美元（$2.2 Trillion）的经济援助法案。（详细内容参：<ahref=><a href="/gb/20/4/4/n12002733.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">川普维生法案概要（The CARES ACT）经济援助看这里</a>）</p>
<p>● 纽约地铁<br />
俗称 metro NYC，又称 NYC Subway。是美国纽约市拥有的地铁系统。</p>
