  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%8F%9C%E5%A4%B4.md#1">红菜头</a>栗米牛骨汤</span></h2>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>  <p>红菜头10oz<br />  <ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%90%9D%E5%8D%9C.md#1">红萝卜</a>8oz<br />  <ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%92%A1.md#1">牛蒡</a>8oz<br />  <ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E8%90%9D%E5%8D%9C.md#1">白萝卜</a>1磅<br />  玉米2条<br />  牛肋骨2.5磅<br />  姜3片<br />  水16碗</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12626674" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-24.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12626674" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-24-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%90%9D%E5%8D%9C.md#1">红萝卜</a>、玉米、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E8%90%9D%E5%8D%9C.md#1">白萝卜</a>、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%92%A1.md#1">牛蒡</a>、红菜头、姜。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12626676" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-19.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12626676" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-19-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">牛肋骨2.5磅。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>  <p>1. 所有材料削皮后切成大块，牛肋骨切除多余的肥膏。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12626678" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12626678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-17-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">材料削皮后切成大块。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 牛肋骨用姜汁酒烫过，冲洗干净后备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12626679" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12626679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-15-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">牛肋骨用姜汁酒烫过，冲洗干净后备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 汤煲内放入16碗水，把所有材料放入，大火煮滚10分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12626680" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12626680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-10-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">汤煲内放入16碗水，把所有材料放入，大火煮滚10分钟。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4. 转成小火炖2个小时，最后放入适量盐即完成。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12626681" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12626681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-13-450x600.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">转成小火炖2个小时，最后放入适量盐即完成。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5. 汤里头的料可以捞出来，沾点酱油更好吃！</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12626682" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12626682" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-9-450x338.jpg" alt="牛肋骨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">汤里头的料可以捞出来沾酱油吃！（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【小贴士】</span></h3>  <p>如果想要喝比较清爽不油的牛肉汤，可以去除汤面上的肥油。先捞出汤料，放凉后将牛肉汤放入冰箱过夜，隔夜后油脂便会凝结在汤面上，再用汤匙捞去油脂即可。这么做很容易清除油脂，而且也不会浪费汤水。◇</p>
