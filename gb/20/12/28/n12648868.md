  <p>相思树的花金黄色，闻起来有轻淡的香味。果实为荚果。木材可供制枕木、坑木及农具，更供作薪炭及种植为行道、园景树。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12648877" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/402317_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/402317_medium-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12648875" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/402315_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12648875" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/402315_medium-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">树皮可治跌打损伤。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>相思树是台湾最好的薪炭材，早期政府还曾将相思炭发给公务人员，作为家庭炊食的柴火用，您想不到吧！嫩枝叶行血散瘀，袪腐生肌，治跌打，毒蛇咬伤，野外皮肤擦伤，可用嫩芽，放在嘴里嚼烂后敷在受伤的地方，很快就可痊愈。树皮可治跌打损伤。</p>
