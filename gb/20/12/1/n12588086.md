  <p>锁在一起的?是你我的双眼<br />  你的眼睛缤纷异彩<br />  呵?是街上的路灯<br />  让它放出绚丽的光芒！</p>

  <p>那是一个完美的夜晚<br />  天空?熠熠星光<br />  你我?目光一对接<br />  瞬间?就夺走了我的心！</p>
  <p>呵?在那样迷人的时刻<br />  你我的心?热切地渴望<br />  美美的一个吻<br />  万般恋欲的井喷<br />  就在那个一眨眼的瞬间！</p>
  <p>你的感情?是那样的强和烈<br />  你的秋波?激荡我的心潮澎和湃<br />  你我?双双坠入情海<br />  这种莫名的冲动<br />  就在那个一眨眼的瞬间！</p>
  <p>你我?这次的相遇是缘<br />  命中注定 不可避免<br />  呵 情感的大潮<br />  紧紧地拥抱着你我<br />  向神秘莫测的尽头冲去！</p>

  <p><strong>Just A Wink Of An Eye</strong></p>
  <p>Last night I saw you<br />  On the waterfront<br />  You glanced at me<br />  And I looked your way!</p>
  <p>Our eyes locked together<br />  And your eyes shifted colors<br />  As they caught the light<br />  Installed at the waterfront!</p>
  <p>Though your alluring lips<br />  Were sealed<br />  Yet those lovely eyes spoke<br />  A thousand words&#8230;!</p>
  <p>The night was perfect<br />  With star studded sky<br />  You winked at me<br />  And snatched my heart right away!</p>
  <p>It was a wonderful moment<br />  Our hearts craved for a kiss<br />  With the bliss<br />  Everything happened<br />  In just a wink of an eye!</p>
  <p>You were utterly crazy<br />  Your glance made me crazy<br />  We were both swept away<br />  By this undefined ecstasy<br />  In just a wink of an eye!</p>
  <p>We soared the inevitable<br />  We could not resist<br />  The high tides of emotion<br />  It knocked us down<br />  To the very end!</p>
