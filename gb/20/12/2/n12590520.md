  <figure id="attachment_12592028" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/shutterstock_345372449.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12592028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/shutterstock_345372449-450x300.jpg" alt="燕麦饼干" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">如果喜欢吃酥脆感的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%87%95%E9%BA%A6.md#1">燕麦</a>饼可以尝试加入坚果、水果干碎片，并延长烘烤时间。(图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">有咀嚼口感的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%BC%8F%E8%BD%AF%E9%A5%BC.md#1">美式软饼</a></span></h2>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%87%95%E9%BA%A6%E7%89%87.md#1">燕麦片</a>100g<br />  燕麦粉40g<br />  <ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%95%89.md#1">香蕉</a>1 根（100g）<br />  甜菊糖20g<br />  葡萄籽油15 ～ 20g（或花生酱）<br />  肉桂粉1/2 小匙<br />  泡打粉1/8 小匙<br />  香草精1/2 ～ 1/4 小匙（可省略）</p>
  <figure id="attachment_11305878" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/oat-powder_521620303.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11305878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/06/oat-powder_521620303-450x300.jpg" alt="被蚊子咬得全身痒，不妨泡个燕麦浴。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%87%95%E9%BA%A6%E7%89%87.md#1">燕麦片</a>100公克，燕麦粉40公克（图／Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">推荐这些配料！</span></h3>  <p>? 巧克力碎片<br />  ? 坚果类<br />  ? 果干</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12450244" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2020-10-03_222235.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12450244" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2020-10-03_222235-450x231.png" alt="每天适量吃坚果，有助减少癌症发生率。(Shutterstock)" width="450" b="231" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">坚果有好的油脂和香气，作为燕麦饼干的配料也很对味！（图／Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>  <p>1 将<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A6%99%E8%95%89.md#1">香蕉</a>放入碗中压碎。<br />  * 烤箱预热170 度。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12600053" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/660aa5a77d3f202e25e9bb22b15808c3.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12600053" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/660aa5a77d3f202e25e9bb22b15808c3.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将香蕉放入碗中压碎（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2 将配料以外的所有材料放入碗中，搅拌均匀后，加入配料稍微混合。<br />  3 将烤盘铺上料理纸，用冰淇淋挖勺（或汤匙）挖起面团，以5cm 的大小铺在烘培纸上。<br />  * 烤箱预热170 度。<br />  * 用挖勺挖起面团后，一定要用力压平。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12600055" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/92298e0f84daf638076b12f511f15936.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12600055" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/92298e0f84daf638076b12f511f15936.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="300" b="360" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用冰淇淋挖勺（或汤匙）挖起面团，约5cm 的大小。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12600056" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/eca3afb7bcad5118c97fb153757260eb.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12600056" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/eca3afb7bcad5118c97fb153757260eb.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用挖勺挖起面团后，一定要用力压平（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4 放入预热170 度的烤箱中，烤约20 ～ 25 分钟，烤至变成黄褐色，表面些许裂开的状态。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12600052" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2f10415439b3cecf7e45f8f578aebe33.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12600052" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2f10415439b3cecf7e45f8f578aebe33-450x540.jpg" alt="高宝书版" width="450" b="540" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放入预热170 度的烤箱中，烤约20 ～ 25 分钟。（图／高宝书版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p><span style="color: #000080;">Tip 用气炸锅代替烤箱制作</span>：进行到步骤3 时，将面团放入气炸锅中，并紧紧压平面团，使其厚度为0.5cm。先以160 度烤10 分钟，翻面后再烤5 ～8 分钟，直到呈现黄褐色为止。<strong>@</strong></p>
