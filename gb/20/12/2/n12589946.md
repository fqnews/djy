  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">咖喱酥饼</span></h2>  <p>黄澄澄的咖喱酥饼，烘烤时飘来阵阵浓郁的咖喱香，令人食欲大增。</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">份量</span></h3>  <p>每个60克(油皮20克，油酥10克，内馅30克 )，可做20个</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <p>油皮：中筋<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E7%B2%89.md#1">面粉</a>105克、低筋面粉105克、糖粉20克、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E6%B2%B9.md#1">猪油</a>70克、水100克<br />  油酥：低筋<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E7%B2%89.md#1">面粉</a>130克、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E6%B2%B9.md#1">猪油</a>70克、咖喱粉10克<br />  馅料：马铃薯150克、红萝卜50克、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BB%9E%E8%82%89.md#1">绞肉</a>100克、盐5克、味素10克、咖喱粉15克、白胡椒5克、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BB%BF%E8%B1%86%E6%B2%99.md#1">绿豆沙</a>300克<br />  擦拭：蛋黄液3颗</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>  <h4><strong>外皮</strong></h4>  <p>1.油皮材料全部混合制成油皮面团后，静置20分钟。<br />  2.油酥材料全部混合制成油酥，分割成每个20克。<br />  3.将油皮分成每个20克，包入油酥。<br />  4.包好后卷起擀成长方形，再次卷起再擀一次。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12590202" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/93cbbe283a51b7759a39aa10cea5b5c1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12590202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/93cbbe283a51b7759a39aa10cea5b5c1-450x338.jpg" alt="油皮" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">卷起擀成长方形，再次卷起再擀一次。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5.将油皮擀圆也擀大一点，包入30克咖喱馅。<br />  6.包好后放在手上捏成圆形。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12590203" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/b9bd6123b50d50f064746114a80acf45.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12590203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/b9bd6123b50d50f064746114a80acf45-450x338.jpg" alt="油皮" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">油皮包入30克咖喱馅，在手上捏成圆形。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>7.放入直径4公分的圆模中。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12590204" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4359824ee748f8ea2ba175fe0f3530a0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12590204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4359824ee748f8ea2ba175fe0f3530a0-450x338.jpg" alt="馅料" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">包好的馅料放入直径4公分的圆模中。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>8.从圆模取出排入烤盘。<br />  9.重复所有动作至全部材料包完。<br />  10.先擦蛋黄液，待略干后再擦一次。<br />  <span style="color: #000080;">Tips 蛋液请擦两次(干后再擦)，表皮才会亮丽。</span><br />  11.待干后盖上红印章。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12590205" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5758129e8b2f1f3f6a00e2b688f3c96c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12590205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5758129e8b2f1f3f6a00e2b688f3c96c-450x338.jpg" alt="咖喱" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">擦蛋黄液一次后，待略干后再擦一次，等全干盖上红印章。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>12.放入烤箱以上火180度、下火170度，烤15分钟，至表皮呈金黄色即可取出。<br />  <span style="color: #000080;">Tips 本配方油皮放较多的糖粉，容易上色，所以烤箱温度不宜过高。</span></p>
  <p>咖喱酥与<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%92%96%E5%96%B1%E9%A5%BC.md#1">咖喱饼</a>有点不同，咖喱酥表皮酥脆，咖喱饼表皮柔软。</p>
  <h4><strong>内馅</strong></h4>  <p>1.马铃薯去皮洗净切细丁。<br />  Tips 马铃薯和红萝卜切丁时请切细丁，以免包时刺破皮不好看。<br />  2.红萝卜也去皮洗净切细丁。<br />  3.取一平底锅，倒入沙拉油热锅。<br />  4.先放入马铃薯丁、红萝卜丁炒熟。<br />  5.再放入<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BB%9E%E8%82%89.md#1">绞肉</a>及所有调味料炒熟。<br />  Tips绞肉绞细的就好。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12590198" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/a4179d318a1fc058b755ef9afd81aafa.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12590198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/a4179d318a1fc058b755ef9afd81aafa-450x338.jpg" alt="咖喱" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">取一平底锅，倒入沙拉油热锅，先放入马铃薯丁炒熟，再放红萝卜丁，最后放绞肉和调味料炒熟。（图／橘子文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>6.取出放凉，备用。<br />  7.加入绿豆馅拌成团，分割成每个30克，备用。</p>
