  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E5%BC%8F.md#1">日式</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%82%B8%E9%B8%A1%E6%8E%92.md#1">炸鸡排</a>饭</span></h2>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>  <p>鸡胸肉2块 8oz<br />  洋葱半个<br />  鸡蛋2个<br />  葱2棵<br />  盐、胡椒粉少许（腌<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E6%8E%92.md#1">鸡排</a>用）<br />  煮熟白饭2碗</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【炸<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E6%8E%92.md#1">鸡排</a>用材料】</span></h3>  <p>面粉、鸡蛋液、面包糠各适量</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味】</span></h3>  <p>柴鱼汤1杯（或者用烹大师1茶匙加开水1杯）<br />  酱油2汤匙<br />  味醂2汤匙<br />  糖1茶匙<br />  盐半茶匙</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12606623" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12606623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">调味材料：柴鱼汤、味醂、糖、盐、酱油。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>  <p>1. 鸡肉轻轻拍扁后，用刀子在肉上划刀纹。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12606624" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12606624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸡肉轻轻拍扁后，用刀子在肉上划刀纹。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 鸡肉放在碗中，洒上盐和胡椒粉腌一下。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12606625" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12606625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸡肉放在碗中，洒上盐和胡椒粉腌一下。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 腌好的鸡肉按照顺序沾上面粉、鸡蛋和面包糠。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12606626" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12606626" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">腌好的鸡肉按照顺序沾上面粉、鸡蛋和面包糠。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4. 用中大火油温，将鸡肉炸至金黄色。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12606627" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12606627" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用中大火油温，将鸡肉炸至金黄色。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5. 待鸡排熟透后，取出切块备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12606628" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12606628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-4-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">待鸡排熟透后，取出切块备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>6. 洋葱切长条，葱切段，鸡蛋打匀。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12606629" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/8-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12606629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/8-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">洋葱切长条，葱切段。 （图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>7. 取一锅加入所有调味煮滚，接着加入洋葱和葱煮一段时间。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12606636" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/9-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12606636" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/9-3-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">取一锅加入所有调味煮滚，接着加入洋葱和葱煮一段时间。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>8. 放入鸡排，再放入鸡蛋液，蛋液半熟时，再撒上剩下的葱。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12606638" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/10-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12606638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/10-3-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放入鸡排，再放入鸡蛋液，蛋液半熟时，再撒上剩下的葱。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>9. 将鸡排和酱汁从锅中取出，淋在热饭上面即完成。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12606642" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/13-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12606642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/13-3-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E5%BC%8F.md#1">日式</a>炸鸡排淋上咸甜酱汁与蛋液，非常好下饭。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【小贴士】</span></h3>  <p>下鸡蛋液时要从锅的中间部分倒入，蛋液的厚度才会均匀。◇</p>
