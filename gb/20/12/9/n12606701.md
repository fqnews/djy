  <figure id="attachment_12619030" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-5-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B2%E5%AD%90.md#1">莲子</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">海参</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%AB%A5%E5%AD%90%E9%B8%A1%E6%B1%A4.md#1">童子鸡汤</a>。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8E%B2%E5%AD%90.md#1">莲子</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">海参</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%AB%A5%E5%AD%90%E9%B8%A1%E6%B1%A4.md#1">童子鸡汤</a>?</span></h2>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>  <p>淮山 1.5 oz<br />  杞子 1 oz<br />  茨实 1.5 oz<br />  莲子 1 oz<br />  百合 1 oz<br />  冬菇 5个<br />  无花果 4粒<br />  童子鸡 1只（约 1.5磅）<br />  湿海参 1.5磅<br />  陈皮 1块<br />  姜 3片<br />  水 16碗</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12619023" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619023" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸡汤炖煮食材。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12619022" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619022" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">准备童子鸡、湿海参、姜片、陈皮。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>  <p>1. 所有材料用水洗干净后，浸泡 20分钟后备用。<br />  2. 童子鸡去皮去除脂肪，切成四大块，用姜汁酒烫过，再清洗干净。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12619024" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619024" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">童子鸡去皮去除脂肪，切成四大块，用姜汁酒烫过，再清洗干净。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 海参切块，用姜汁酒烫过后沥干备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12619025" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-11-450x388.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">海参切块，用姜汁酒烫过后沥干备用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4. 汤煲内放入16碗水，把海参和杞子外的所有材料都放入，用大火煮滚15分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12619026" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">汤煲内放入16碗水，把海参和杞子外的所有材料都放入，用大火煮滚15分钟。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5. 接着转为小火再多炖2个小时，然后加入海参和杞子续煮20分钟，便可食用。◇</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12619027" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12619027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-9-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加入海参和杞子续煮20分钟。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【主厨简介】</span></h3>  <p>姓名：梁志生<br />  厨龄：40余年<br />  经历：<br />  1、美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />  2、海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />  3、作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />  4、美国富商George Bowles御用厨师</p>
