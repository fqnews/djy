  <figure id="attachment_12645147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476396.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12645147" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476396-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">校长与获奖学生合影。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12645149" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476397.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12645149" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476397-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">校长于贤华颁发敢谢状予嘉南药理大学餐旅管理系专案讲师王明煌。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12645156" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476403.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12645156" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476403-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">嘉南药理大学餐旅管理系专案讲师王明煌与母亲合影。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12645151" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476399.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12645151" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476399-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">部分餐饮科学生合影。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>淡水商工于贤华校长表示，本次以“淡水八景菜色研发”为主题办理成果发表会，主要以中餐烹调课程为基础运用烹调原理与切割技巧提升学生厨艺技巧，以说菜服务技巧提升服务品质。将在地食材融入烘培领域增能，运用北海岸冬季盛产的山药、万里蟹、金山地瓜等食材，并融合淡水小吃&#8211;阿给，增添菜色趣味与特色，并且搭配师生设计之陶瓷美盘，使学生技能与产业契合，达到跨越增能的学习成效。</p>
  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A4%90%E9%A5%AE%E7%AE%A1%E7%90%86.md#1">餐饮管理</a>科李岳明同学表示，假日参加说菜技巧课程能提高餐饮服务自信，林育婷与曾奕嘉同学说参加“加速器增能课程”有助于提升厨艺与烘焙技巧，以及艺术鉴赏的能力。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12645155" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476402.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12645155" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476402-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">餐饮科学生表演饮料调制。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12645153" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476401.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12645153" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476401-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">餐饮科学生的部分作品娇艳可口。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12645152" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476400.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12645152" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476400-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">会场内莺歌工商陶瓷工程科老师指导淡水商工学生制作的最新创作的餐饮类美观陶瓷作品展示。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12645150" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476398.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12645150" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/476398-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">北海岸农业、渔业特产创作菜肴。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>学校表示，在新北教育局创新加速器竞争型计划经费挹注下，以“淡水八景”为核心，辅以美学讲座，并纳入餐旅群中餐、西餐、饮料调制、餐饮服务等餐饮科实务课程，借此丰富学生专业知识与技能的学习，认识在地人文风貌与物产特性，利用所学创作陶瓷，融合北海岸风格的料理，扩展跨域课程学习的深度与广度。</p>
