  <figure id="attachment_12598927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-1-2Abortion2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12598927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-1-2Abortion2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">与会者也悼念因为堕胎而去世的妇女。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12598929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-1-3Abortion.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12598929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-1-3Abortion-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">与会者呼吁珍惜生命，反对堕胎。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12598930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-1-4Abortion2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12598930" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-1-4Abortion2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在寒冷的风雨中，与会者为因堕胎而受伤害的妇女和婴儿祈祷。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>  <p>在阴冷的风雨中，与会者们在州政府楼前的阶梯上摆满红白玫瑰，在主持人的带领下诵念祷词。他们认为，堕胎的行为既杀死了婴儿，又对堕胎的妇女本身造成严重伤害，绝大多数有过这种经历的妇女都对堕胎深感后悔。在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%86%E4%BC%9A.md#1">集会</a>的前一天，麻州议会刚刚通过了新的财年预算法案，其中包括放宽堕胎限制的条文。</p>
  <p>麻州共和党在社交媒体上支持这次<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E5%A0%95%E8%83%8E.md#1">反堕胎</a>集会。“今天在州政府大楼前，以这卓越的悼词，向成千上万因为堕胎而丧失生命的无辜未出生婴儿致意。”麻州共和党在脸书上说。◇</p>
