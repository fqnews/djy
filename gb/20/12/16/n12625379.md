  <p>Punch in the Heart<br />  &#8211; To Miss LIU Yanli (刘艳丽)<br />  by Yiyan Han (c) 2020-12-10</p>

  <p>Your poem “torture” pens the pain of a heart in bleeding<br />  the weight that a human conscience has been enduring<br />  The Sacred Heart of Jesus is on the cross<br />  and your heart bleeds on the land under communists</p>
  <p>I knew nothing when you called out<br />  and when I come you were already locked up<br />  Waiting for the prison gate opens<br />  I shall present you a piece of bloodred rose</p>
