  <p>除了独特的甘甜之外，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C.md#1">南瓜</a>也含有丰富的营养成分。它有极丰富的氦基酸、胡萝卜素、维生素，尤其它胡萝卜素的含量，在所有瓜类中可说是翘楚。南瓜性温味甘，具补中益气的功效。清代名医陈修园说：“南瓜为补血之妙品。”</p>
  <p>一般而言，糖尿病、高血压患者适合食用南瓜，南瓜也有促进消化排便的作用。南瓜对爱美的女性而言，可说是非常适合的食物。因为其不仅有美容功效，且南瓜中含有果胶，能调节胃内食物的吸收速度，使得糖类吸收减缓。食用南瓜也比较容易获得饱足感，想控制身材者可藉食用南瓜降低淀粉的摄取量。</p>
  <p>糯米含有蛋白质、脂肪、糖类、钙、磷、铁、维生素B1、维生索B2、烟酸及淀粉等，营养丰富，为温补强壮食品，具有补中益气、健脾养胃、止虚汗之功效。</p>
  <p>秋冬时节正是吃南瓜的好季节，也是进补的好时机。罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C%E5%B0%8F%E9%B8%A1%E7%82%96%E7%B3%AF%E7%B1%B3.md#1">南瓜小鸡炖糯米</a>，将营养美味的南瓜、滋补养生的小鸡和补中益气的糯米汇聚在一起，清香、甘甜，堪称经典菜肴，非常适合节日全家享用。</p>
  <h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C%E5%B0%8F%E9%B8%A1%E7%82%96%E7%B3%AF%E7%B1%B3.md#1">南瓜小鸡炖糯米</a></h2>  <p>主料：南瓜、小鸡、糯米<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3282d7715234f77a63a25e5fb337a2c9.png"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12594577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3282d7715234f77a63a25e5fb337a2c9.png" alt="" width="398" b="261" /></a></p>
  <p>配料：胡萝卜、葱段、红枣、枸杞、姜片、当归</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/76f2be868fa5abb9263c5b960e89b39a.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/76f2be868fa5abb9263c5b960e89b39a-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>调料：盐、鸡汤</p>
  <p>做法：</p>
  <p>1. 把小鸡用开水烫过，去血迹和杂质。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-2.png"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-2-450x262.png" alt="" width="450" b="262" /></a></p>
  <p>2. 在糯米中加入盐、黄酒。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>3. 在糯米中放入红枣。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>4. 将糯米塞入小鸡肚中。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>5. 把小母鸡放入砂煲清鸡汤中。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>6. 放入配料慢火煲45分钟。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>7. 将南瓜里的瓜肉挖出。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>8. 将南瓜盅用开水烫熟。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594587" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>9. 将小母鸡放入南瓜盅中。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594588" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>10. 在南瓜中加入原汤。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594589" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>11. 将南瓜盅放入蒸箱炖约40分钟。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/11-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12594591" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/11-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
