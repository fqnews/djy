  <p>星级俱乐部配备酒吧，餐厅，健身中心，瑜伽馆，芭蕾舞训练场，互动游戏室，高尔夫模拟练习场，并配有私人教练，让你在工作之余放松身心。豪华露天泳池，让你在忙碌了一天后，徜徉在碧水蓝天之下。这一切仿佛让你置身于坎昆的五星级酒店。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12592292" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-HD-GOLF-EXPERIENCE.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12592292" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-HD-GOLF-EXPERIENCE-450x515.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="515" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">高尔夫模拟练习场。（Park Central提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12592297" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-Pool.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12592297" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-Pool-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">游戏室。（Park Central提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>其实不必远走，海因斯公园中心（Hines&#8217; Park Central）就能让你体验24小时的五星级享受。坐落于卡尔加里市中心12街的中心纪念公园之上，这一座32层高的现代化<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%BA%E7%A7%9F%E5%85%AC%E5%AF%93.md#1">出租公寓</a>汇集了462套单元，包括单室公寓，一居室，两居室和三居室套房，将为你提供一个全新的生活方式。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12592302" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-living-room-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12592302" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-living-room-01-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">起居室。（Park Central提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12592319" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-kitchen.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12592319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-kitchen-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Park Central提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>客户体验经理Tareq Merali表示，海因斯公园中心这个独特而全新的设计理念赢得了非常广泛的关注，尤其是年轻的上班族。“公园中心确实是卡尔加里租赁住宅中的第一家。它提供世界一流的设施，纽约式风格，及宠物友好型公寓。”</p>
  <p>这里最引人注目的地方肯定是室外露台上设有游泳池，并设有餐饮设施，将城市天际线，公园和落矶山一览无余。宽敞的屋顶Skylounge与室外露台相对应，居民可以在其中享用私人酒吧和餐厅，并从高处眺望卡尔加里的城市景色。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12592324" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-THE-SKYLOUNGE.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12592324" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-THE-SKYLOUNGE-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宽敞的屋顶Skylounge。（Park Central提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12592331" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-skyline.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12592331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-skyline-600x393.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="393" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">城市天际线。（Park Central提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>Merali说：“每位居民入住时都会收到一张VIP卡，让居民享受优先附近的服务和设施，如Metrovino, Native Tongues, Petropolitan, Woofly等等。” 为了增强社区意识，海因斯公园中心还提供聚集空间，人们可以在那里进行个人或工作事宜，如品酒会，世界美食之夜，咖啡社交，等等，海因斯公园中心每月不同的社区活动更让居民的生活多姿多彩。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12592349" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-CREATIVE-CO-WORKING-HUB.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12592349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/20201202-Park-Central-CREATIVE-CO-WORKING-HUB-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">富有创意的工作空间。（Park Central提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>欲了解更多信息，请访问<ahref="http://www.parkcentralcalgary.com">www.parkcentralcalgary.com</a>或致电403-888-0680。</p>
