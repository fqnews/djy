  <p>美食总是节日里的重要角色， 火鸡、蔓越梅酱、土豆泥、蛋酒、姜饼都是圣诞节不可或缺的传统食物，香气诱人的菜肴既带来享受，也增添了浓浓的节日气氛。</p>
  <p>今天我们为您介绍罗厨制作的<strong><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B1%89%E6%B2%B9%E9%B8%A1.md#1">豉油鸡</a></strong>，希望这酥香浓郁的好味道，为您的圣诞大餐增色添味。</p>
  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B1%89%E6%B2%B9%E9%B8%A1.md#1">豉油鸡</a>又称酱油鸡，是<ahref="https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E7%B2%A4%E8%8F%9C/306654">粤菜</a>中的传统名菜。这道菜因用料简单、做法简单又美味可口而备受大家的喜欢。这道名菜的主要食材是<ahref="https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E9%B8%A1/416172">鸡</a>和<ahref="https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E9%85%B1%E6%B2%B9/1246">酱油</a>，用小火煮至吸干汤汁即可，十分美味。</p>
  <p>鸡肉肉质细嫩，滋味鲜美，由于其味较淡，因此可使用于各种料理中。鸡肉不但适于热炒、炖汤，而且是比较适合冷食凉拌的肉类。</p>
  <p>鸡肉的蛋白质的含量颇多，在肉之中，可以说是蛋白质最高的肉类之一，是属于高蛋白低脂肪的食品。</p>
  <p>罗厨制作的这道<strong>豉油鸡，</strong>色泽鲜亮，鸡肉香滑浓厚，肥而不腻，冰糖的清甜和玫瑰露酒的芬香完美结合，使得鸡肉具备特殊的质地和口感。</p>
  <p>罗厨强调，要保证好味道，也需要一些小技巧。首先，三黄鸡不要太大，也不要太小。为了让鸡肉充分入味，要在鸡的腹腔内塞入葱、蒜和红葱头。在汤汁中熬煮的时候，要用汤勺把汤汁淋到未浸到的鸡身上，保证色泽和味道均匀。</p>
  <h2>豉油鸡</h2>  <p>主料：三黄鸡</p>
  <p>配料：葱、姜、蒜、红葱头、香菜。</p>
  <p>调料：生抽、老抽、冰糖、玫瑰露酒。</p>
  <p>做法：</p>
  <p>1 .将三黄鸡洗干净用开水烫过。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-31.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12643613" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/1-31-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>2.将洗干净的鸡用生抽上色。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-28.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12643614" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/2-28-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>3.鸡内腔放入葱、姜、蒜和红葱头。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-23.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12643616" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/3-23-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>4.将鸡半煎炸至金黄色。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-20.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12643617" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/4-20-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>5.沥干油份捞起。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12643618" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/5-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>6.冰糖放入煮开的四碗开水中一起熬煮。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-17.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12643620" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/6-17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>7.再放入老抽2汤匙。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12643621" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/7-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>8.汤汁煮香后放入煎好的两只鸡。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/8-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12643623" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/8-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>9.慢火煮20分钟后翻面。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/9-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12643624" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/9-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>10.再放入葱段2根。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/10-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12643625" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/10-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
  <p>11.慢火收汁后装盘，上面点缀些香菜。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/11-29.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12643628" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/11-29-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
