<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】年菜系列之四：荷叶龙虾糯米蒸鸡</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/516ce38f824b2f94d4cfd64b85c8531b-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道荷叶龙虾糯米蒸鸡，堪称色香味形的完美组合，在满满年味的氛围中，定会让你吃出满口幸福的滋味。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>中国人过年的饮食讲求吉祥寓意，“鸡”通“吉”，在年菜中预示吉利吉祥之意，是过年餐桌上不可或缺的一道菜肴。而龙虾因形似龙而被视作吉兆的象征，在年节期间也是备受欢迎。</p>
<p>更难得的是，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8D%B7%E5%8F%B6%E9%BE%99%E8%99%BE%E7%B3%AF%E7%B1%B3%E8%92%B8%E9%B8%A1.md#1">荷叶龙虾糯米蒸鸡</a>，不仅寓意吉利，还营养丰富：鸡肉含丰富的蛋白质，其脂肪中含不饱和脂肪，是老年人和心血管疾病患者较好的蛋白质食品。</p>
<p>糯米即黏稻，俗称江米、元米，富含蛋白质、糖类、脂肪、碳水化合物、钙、磷、铁、维生素B1、B2、烟酸、多量淀粉，还含硫胺素、核黄素、尼古酸等人体所必需的微量元素。</p>
<p>龙虾味道鲜美，其优质的蛋白质很容易消化，非常适合体弱的病人和老年人吃，丰富的虾青素可以延缓衰老，还有钾和镁等微量元素，能够保护心脏以及血管系统。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8D%B7%E5%8F%B6%E9%BE%99%E8%99%BE%E7%B3%AF%E7%B1%B3%E8%92%B8%E9%B8%A1.md#1">荷叶龙虾糯米蒸鸡</a>，荷叶一拆开便清香扑鼻、鲜味四溢，荷叶的清香、糯米的黏软、鸡肉的嫩滑，加上香菇与龙虾的鲜美，相互交糅相互渗透，融为一体，堪称色香味形的完美组合，在满满年味的氛围中，定会让你吃出满口幸福的滋味。</p>
<h2>荷叶龙虾糯米蒸鸡</h2>
<p>主料：龙虾1只约1.5磅、鸡半只、糯米2碗、荷叶1.5张。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/89a07500f6e40b1c709e52a945b19ac0.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801888" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/89a07500f6e40b1c709e52a945b19ac0-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：小姜片1汤匙、葱粒1汤匙、红枣4颗、香菇2个。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/019abcffe5983194f7cfb27bb66cd982.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801889" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/019abcffe5983194f7cfb27bb66cd982-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
调料：盐、老抽、生抽、蚝油、胡椒粉、糖、油、生粉、香油。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 龙虾切件。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-15.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-15-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 鸡切件。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801891" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 用小姜片等配料和老抽等调料腌制鸡块。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801894" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 干荷叶用清水浸一晚，切成蒸笼大小。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 糯米用清水浸一晚，晾干。然后用盐、糖、胡椒粉、香油等腌制。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 把剪好的荷叶放入蒸笼里，把腌好的糯米放在荷叶上面，均匀铺平，蒸25分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801897" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 把腌制好的鸡块放到蒸熟的糯米上面。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 把鸡块摊开，再大火蒸6分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801899" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 龙虾上面撒上0.5汤匙的生粉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801900" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 把龙虾摆在已蒸熟的鸡上面，龙虾上放姜丝，蒸3分钟至熟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/10-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801902" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/10-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 龙虾上加特制私房酱及葱粒、姜丝，用热油淋香，再淋上生抽即可。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/11-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/11-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/49f9bf310836130d7509c368891b29ec.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11801907" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/49f9bf310836130d7509c368891b29ec-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
