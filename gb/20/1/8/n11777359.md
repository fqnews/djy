<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】年菜系列之三：红烧圆蹄</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/13598daccdc946cd520ddde98808e916-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道红烧圆蹄肥而不腻、松软可口。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>传统新年即将到来，亲朋好友欢聚一堂，炮制美味可口又健康营养的年菜一直是主妇们的头痛事，年年过节做的菜都一样，怎样才能有新意？</p>
<p>其实只要食材选择得当，烹调讲究一些小技巧，再加上小有创意的装盘，一定可以做出令人称道的美味佳肴。今天我们就介绍罗厨制作的这道肥而不腻、松软可口的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E5%9C%86%E8%B9%84.md#1">红烧圆蹄</a>。</p>
<p>圆蹄营养很丰富，含较多的蛋白质，特别是含有大量的胶原蛋白，和肉皮一样，是使皮肤丰满润泽、强体增力的冬日食补佳品。但用它来烹制的菜式，常给人肥腻的感觉，而能烹制出肥而不腻的圆蹄，才算“上品”。</p>
<p>圆蹄寓意全家“团圆和气”，是一道传统的年菜，通常以红烧的做法为多。罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E5%9C%86%E8%B9%84.md#1">红烧圆蹄</a>肥而不腻、松软可口。</p>
<p>制作红烧圆蹄的火候极为重要，慢火的扣炖可“逼”出蹄内的脂肪，使其“丰腴可口”。焖制前加入适量的黄酒，可降低其肥腻的口感，而适当运用香料来焖制，便可达肥而不腻、松软可口的效果。</p>
<p>时至今日，圆蹄除了红烧，做法早已花款尽出，包括椒盐圆蹄，以花椒、姜、冰糖等腌制后，下油锅炸至酥香。另外还有以越式酱汁调味，配以拧檬粉等。什么变化都好，正宗的红烧圆蹄还是不可取替的。</p>
<h2>红烧圆蹄</h2>
<p>主料：圆蹄4只。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/66a805071a244c5ba5a62a3b188ba662.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793575" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/66a805071a244c5ba5a62a3b188ba662-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：老姜2块，葱2根。<br />
调料：海鲜酱2汤匙、南乳1块（约2汤匙）、蚝油3汤匙、黄酒4汤匙、蜂蜜2汤匙、肉桂粉2汤匙、香叶5片、花椒粒10粒。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将圆蹄用水烫过，沥干水分。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793576" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 用老抽涂抹圆蹄均匀上色。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793577" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 将腌制好的圆蹄用油煎香至金黄色。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793578" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 利用锅底余油爆香老姜、葱，添加海鲜酱、南乳、蚝油和黄酒。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793579" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 加入生抽，添加4碗开水。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793580" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 放入蜂蜜、肉桂粉、香叶、花椒粒后，盖锅盖煮开。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793581" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 煮开后放入圆蹄。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793582" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 加盖慢火炖约2.5小时。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793583" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 时间可以根据喜好而定，软一点，时间要长一点。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793584" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 成品圆蹄松软可口、甘香润滑。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/10-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793585" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/10-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/10-2-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11793586" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/10-2-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
