<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>庆新年 马州州长夫妇官邸宴请亚裔领袖</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年01月22日讯】（大纪元记者林帆马州安纳波利斯报导）1月16日晚﹐<a href="/gb/tag/%E9%A9%AC%E9%87%8C%E5%85%B0%E5%B7%9E.md">马里兰州</a>州长霍根（Larry Hogan）夫妇在位于首府安纳波利斯的官邸招待<a href="/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94.md">亚裔</a>社区领袖﹐共庆传统<a href="/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md">新年</a>——鼠年的到来。当晚出席的上百位嘉宾中﹐主要是华裔和韩裔等社区人士﹐也有<a href="/gb/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E8%A3%94.md">亚裔</a>的马州州议员﹑州政府官员﹐及韩国使馆﹑台湾驻美代表处官员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11810764" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200116Governor03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11810764" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200116Governor03-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马州州长霍根祝福大家鼠年成功快乐。（林帆/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11810765" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200116Governor02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11810765" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200116Governor02-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马州州长官邸内招待会现场。（林帆/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>州长霍根在招待会致词中表示﹐这是他和夫人<a href="/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E6%9C%89%E7%BE%8E.md">金有美</a>第六次在官邸举办传统<a href="/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B4.md">新年</a>庆祝活动。在马州﹐亚裔人口已经超过40万。他的夫人是美国有史以来首位亚裔州长夫人﹐对于美国提供人们实现梦想的机会由衷感恩。经由夫人﹐他也更加了解亚裔的传统文化和社会需求。他特别提到﹐晚宴中的<a href="/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A1%E8%8F%9C.md">泡菜</a>都由夫人亲手制作﹐并开玩笑说﹐马州的事务由他负责﹐但官邸内事务则由夫人说了算。</p>
<p>谈到鼠年﹐霍根说﹐老鼠代表着聪明而有活力﹐懂得寻找机会。他祝福大家鼠年都能成功﹑快乐。招待会中准备了丰盛的亚洲美食。最后﹐州长夫妇还一一与宾客们合影留念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11810767" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200116Governor07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11810767" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200116Governor07-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">招待会上﹐马州州长霍根品尝亚洲美食。（林帆/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11810772" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200116Governor06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11810772" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/20200116Governor06-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马州州长官邸内招待会现场。（林帆/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
