<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>车评：超越53MPG 2020 Toyota Corolla Hybrid LE</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_03-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2020 Toyota Corolla Hybrid。〈李奥／大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年01月10日讯】Toyota的长青车系Corolla，已迈进第十二代，去年已率先测试了被Toyota称为“Haute Hatch”的Hatchback揭背版本，动力不错又有动感外形，与Mazda3 Hatchback及Honda Civic Hatchback成为最受欢迎的三款日系Hatchback，其实还有Subaru Impreza的，差点忘了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781638" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_07-e1578633924671.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11781638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_07-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020 Toyota Corolla Hybrid。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>这周测试的Corolla Sedan，也不单单是一辆四门房车，而是装有Toyota闻名的Hybrid系统，务求在耐用、舒适的基础上，再把低耗这一优点加上去，可以完全把同级对手比下去。Corolla Hybrid的Hybrid系统，是由一副1.8公升四汽缸VVT-i引擎，与一台53kW的电动马达联合，前者输出马力121匹，峰值扭矩为105磅呎，而马达的马力约71匹及105磅呎扭矩，透过CVTi无段变速箱输出至车轴，再传递到前轮。令人惊讶的是Corolla Hybrid仍使用Ni-MH电池作为电力储存，而不是更高一级的Lithium锂离子电池。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781642" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_14-e1578633950313.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11781642" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_14-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020 Toyota Corolla Hybrid。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>外形设计，与Corolla Hatchback一样，采用流线形的设计，头灯是刀锋形设计，中间伸延至厂彻，构成“领结（Bow Tie）”的形状，下方整个大面积的梯形进风口，网纹则是鳞片状。Hybrid LE配备了15吋的铝合金轮圈，以及P195/65R15四季轮胎。车侧下方有少许裙脚，挺好看的，反而尾部设计平实，不及Hatchback版的精彩。在载货量当然是Sedan更优胜，载货空间为13.1立方呎，约为370公升，而且是整个空间也是可用，并没有被电池组占用了部分空间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_12-e1578633940993.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11781641" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020 Toyota Corolla Hybrid。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11781647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_23-e1578634066673.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11781647" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020 Toyota Corolla Hybrid。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>车厢内部设计虽然简约，但是并不落伍，首先仪表板是以三个半圆形为主要设计，中间其实是一个7吋彩屏，虚拟的仪表板可显示更多不同资讯，最大好处是在美加两地穿梭，可即时由“英里／时”转换至“公里／时”，或是倒过来，不用再看内圈那细小刻度，超速也不知道；两侧则仍是实体仪表，左方是引擎转速，而右方上半部是油缸存量，下方是水温。Hybrid版为了减轻车重吧，所以只有LE版，装备不及去年测试的XSE，换来是极佳的耗油量，厂方的数据为53MPG。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781643" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_16-e1578633963189.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11781643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_16-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020 Toyota Corolla Hybrid。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>中控台的轻触萤幕尺寸达到8吋，内里运行EnTune 3.0 Audio系统，附有6个扬声器，以蓝牙技术接驳手机，让驾驶者可透过车内音响通话或播放手机内的音乐。此外，可支援Amazon Alexa及Apple?CarPlay，并没有Android Auto的份儿，另外还支援Siri Eyes Free。Scout GPS Link有三年试用，另有Verizon提供的Wi-Fi Connect，也有6个月试用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781644" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_17-e1578633975685.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11781644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_17-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020 Toyota Corolla Hybrid。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>手刹车是电子式，可减少手刹车占用空间，而且又可自动启动及解开，当变速杆进入P档时便会启动，转到D或R档便会解开。HOLD键是当汽车停下时，便会自动保持刹车，放开刹车踏板也不会动，只会在踏下油门一刻才会解开，对于上坡路停红灯时，格外有用，因不用担心后溜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781645" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_20-e1578634048827.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11781645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020 Toyota Corolla Hybrid。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然Corolla Hybrid有各种优点，但也不是没有缺点，其中之一是新Corolla的B柱巨大，在Shoulder Check的时候，往往看到的只是一片黑，尤其是看左边的时候，要格外留神。另外，这车没有电动加热座椅，在冬天出门时要忍耐长一点时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11781646" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_22-e1578634056859.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11781646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2020_Toyota_Corolla_Hybrid_22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2020 Toyota Corolla Hybrid。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于选择Corolla Hybrid的人来说，最为关心的应该还是实际耗油量，笔者开了此车一星期，汽车电脑显示是53.1MPG，比厂方的数据还要好，走了约175英里并用了约3.3加仑而已，非常神奇！</p>
