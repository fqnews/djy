<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】年菜系列之五：潮州碌鸭</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/b5998ff150dba28915d1678cc5ab4d16-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的潮州碌鸭，口感甘香，肉质超有质感，家乡味十足，真正的让人唇齿留香。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>鸭肉的蛋白质含量非常丰富，比畜肉含量还高一些。鸭肉的脂肪含量比较适中并且分布均匀，而且鸭肉中的脂肪酸熔点比较低，有助于消化。鸭肉中还含丰富的铁质和各种维他命，脂肪中的不饱和脂肪酸比青鱼类还多，是可以安心食用的健康食物。</p>
<p>鸭肉中富含女性常缺乏的铁质，以及维持活力不可或缺的维他命B1、B2和A等营养素。中医认为，鸭子吃的食物多为水生物，故其肉性味甘、寒，入肺胃肾经，有滋补、养胃、补肾、消水肿、止热痢、止咳化痰等作用。</p>
<p>广东碌鸭好吃入味，是地道的特色美食。广东话中的“碌”，如同北方的石碌，是在磨盘上翻来覆去地滚动的意思。碌，就是在油镬里来回滚动。乡下每年冬至时分，芋大莳甜、鹅强鸭肥，人们都会杀鸭宰鹅，作为一年辛劳的犒劳。因为普通家庭不能像烧腊店那样做烧鹅、烧鸭，那碌鹅、碌鸭应需而生。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%BD%AE%E5%B7%9E%E7%A2%8C%E9%B8%AD.md#1">潮州碌鸭</a>，口感甘香，肉质超有质感，家乡味十足，真正的让人唇齿留香。</p>
<h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%BD%AE%E5%B7%9E%E7%A2%8C%E9%B8%AD.md#1">潮州碌鸭</a></h2>
<p>主料：鸭子1只。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/523d8f9e8924a066dcbb2265e3014ecc.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826468" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/523d8f9e8924a066dcbb2265e3014ecc-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：葱、姜块、红葱头2个、香叶4片、八角1个。<br />
调料：蚝油1汤匙、海鲜酱1汤匙、生抽2汤匙、老抽1/3汤匙、高度白酒1汤匙。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将鸭子去内脏洗干净，并用开水汆烫。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-23.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826469" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1-23-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 去掉表皮脏物和油，涂上生抽腌制。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-28.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826472" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/2-28-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 用少量油半煎炸至金黄色。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3-18-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 用少量的油将配料和调料爆炒至香浓。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/4-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 放入3碗开水。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 加五香粉、生抽和老抽调色。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/6-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 把鸭子放进去加盖慢火烧35分钟，老鸭可烧45分钟至1小时。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826669" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-1-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826706" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7-1-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 过程中不断用汤汁淋鸭身。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8-1-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8-1-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 直至汤汁足够包住鸭身即可。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11826673" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/9-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
