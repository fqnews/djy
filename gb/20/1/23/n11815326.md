<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>汉阳超市热闹庆祝成立34周年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/a96f977cb61a7dff31d3e8d51e981952-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>新泽西Palisades Park 市长Christopher Chung（右）颁奖给汉阳超市总裁韩宅善（左）。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年01月23日讯】（大纪元记者大纪元讯报导）2020年新年伊始，当人们正沉浸于新年的喜庆气氛时，闻名社区的“<a href="/gb/tag/%E6%B1%89%E9%98%B3%E8%B6%85%E5%B8%82.md">汉阳超市</a>”迎来了喜庆日子。1月22日，汉阳超市34周年庆典活动于法拉盛大同宴会厅隆重登场。当晚，新泽西Palisades Park韩裔市长Christopher Chung，新泽西Ridgefield韩裔市议员Dennis Shim以及三百多位嘉宾，出席了庆祝活动，并给汉阳超市总裁韩宅善颁奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11815330" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/bfa6634276d6ee26bd306c2922580ebb.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11815330" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/bfa6634276d6ee26bd306c2922580ebb-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">韩国农水产食品流通公社 美洲本部长金珖镇（右）获得最佳机构奖。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11815333" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3ac0282e6f371a374801b498b7b7c0a9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11815333" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3ac0282e6f371a374801b498b7b7c0a9-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="/gb/tag/%E6%B1%89%E9%98%B3%E8%B6%85%E5%B8%82.md">汉阳超市</a>总裁韩宅善（中）和最佳顾客奖获得者合影。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11815334" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/24d1947211d11236427967d4cc113322.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11815334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/24d1947211d11236427967d4cc113322-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">社会各界贺汉阳超市34周年庆。 （林青／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“韩国农水产食品流通公社”（ Korea Agro-Fisheries &amp; Food Trade Corporation，简称 aT）美洲本部长金珖镇，代表“韩国农林畜产食品部”光临庆典，给予庆贺，“韩国农林畜产食品部”获得了汉阳超市颁发的最佳机构奖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11815329" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/fae4451fc70b20fb97eafc462cedc393.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11815329" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/fae4451fc70b20fb97eafc462cedc393-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">新泽西Ridgefield 市议员Dennis Shim（右）颁奖给汉阳超市总裁韩宅善（左）。（Lucy Lee／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11815331" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/73b60af89f85ea276422ed39d30b5af5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11815331" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/73b60af89f85ea276422ed39d30b5af5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">汉阳超市总裁韩宅善（后左三）和优秀员工奖获得者合影。（林青／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>汉阳超市34周年庆设置了多个奖项：最佳机构奖，最佳顾客奖，最佳员工奖。总裁韩宅善亲自给超市年度优秀员工颁发奖状，同时也给员工子女颁发了奖学金。汉阳超市员工代表随着欢快的音乐声互相有竞舞表演，抽奖和乐器演奏等，场面热烈。</p>
<p>汉阳超市目前在法拉盛、长岛和新泽西都有分店。</p>
<p>汉阳超市（H&amp;Y）的英文全称是“Health &amp; Youth”，它表达了汉阳超市致力于健康与年轻的经营理念。“H&amp;Y”的商标由绿色和蓝色构成，其中绿色代表绿色食品，而蓝色代表着海鲜产品。◇</p>
