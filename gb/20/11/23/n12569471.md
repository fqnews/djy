  <figure id="attachment_12569475" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470767.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12569475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470767-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“重过月明桥”<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%88%B7%E5%A4%96%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">户外音乐会</a>，吸引许多人专程到场聆赏。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12569477" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470776.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12569477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470776-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“重过月明桥”<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%88%B7%E5%A4%96%E9%9F%B3%E4%B9%90%E4%BC%9A.md#1">户外音乐会</a>吸引上千人聆赏（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12569476" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470770.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12569476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470770-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">钢琴家罗芳华、王雪萍联合演奏。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%89%E7%A7%80%E5%9B%AD.md#1">三秀园</a>位于大埤乡怡然村，占地一万多坪，当地村民称之为“三间仔”，清朝末年，由张添盛先生所建，是诗人张祯祥故居，因园内树木寄生灵芝，因而取灵芝俗名“三秀”为名。</p>
  <p>三秀园在日治时期，曾是人文荟萃、骚人墨客雅集的一处私人园林，园内花木扶疏、风景秀丽，进入园区仿佛进入与世隔绝的桃花源，让人留连忘返，春天时百花齐放、虫鸟齐鸣，秋天则有落叶翻红堆积，因此四季来此观赏园景，都有不同的感受。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12569473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470762.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12569473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470762-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">花木扶疏、绿意盎然的三秀园入口，是游客必拍的景点。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12569474" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470763.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12569474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470763-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">园内充满诗情画意的池塘，让人流连忘返！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12569478" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470777.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12569478" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470777-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">园内曲径通幽，景色宜人！（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12569480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470779.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12569480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/470779-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在地居民悠闲坐在草坪上欣赏音乐。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>三秀园也是全台仅存保留完整的日治时期文人花园，因年久失修，一度荒芜，五年前由张祯祥的后人、也是国内科技界闻人苏元良、林主瑶夫妇接手后，斥钜资修复，让园内重新恢复往昔生机。</p>
  <p>喜爱艺文的苏元良夫妇，每年中秋节后都会邀请国内知名音乐家在园内举办户外音乐会，吸引许多喜爱音乐的朋友专程前来聆赏。</p>
  <p>这次“重过月明桥”音乐会以“怀念”为主题，邀请东海音乐系创系主任罗芳华、长荣大学钢琴家王雪萍副教授、东海大学声乐家徐以琳同台演出，并有钢琴家四手及八手联弹的国际级演奏。希望人们在忙碌的生活中，仍不忘过去的点滴，让流淌的音乐抚慰大家的心情！</p>
