  <p class="tgt" data-section="2"><span class="tgt highlight" data-section="2" data-sentence="0" data-group="2-0">允许或禁止的活动如下：<br />  </span></p>
  <h2 class="tgt" data-section="4"><span class="tgt" data-section="4" data-sentence="0" data-group="4-0">有组织的公共活动，社会聚会，宗教服务，典礼和仪式：</span></h2>  <p class="tgt" data-section="10"><span class="tgt" data-section="10" data-sentence="0" data-group="10-0">除同一家庭成员外，禁止在室内组织公共活动和社交聚会。</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="11"><span class="tgt" data-section="11" data-sentence="0" data-group="11-0">户外聚会的人数限制为10人，必须保持身体距离。</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="12"><span class="tgt" data-section="12" data-sentence="0" data-group="12-0">允许虚拟和免下车的聚会及活动。</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="13"><span class="tgt" data-section="13" data-sentence="0" data-group="13-0">婚礼、葬礼和其它宗教仪式、典礼，允许室内10人或室外10人，必须保持身体距离。</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="14"><span class="tgt" data-section="14" data-sentence="0" data-group="14-0">允许以虚拟和免下车方式举行仪式和典礼。</span></p>
  <h2 class="tgt" data-section="17"><span class="tgt" data-section="17" data-sentence="0" data-group="17-0">学校、儿童保育和高等教育机构</span></h2>  <p class="tgt" data-section="19"><span class="tgt" data-section="19" data-sentence="0" data-group="19-0">学校和幼儿中心仍然开放。</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="20"><span class="tgt" data-section="20" data-sentence="0" data-group="20-0">学院及大学只可接受虚拟教学，如果课程要求亲身授课（例如临床培训、技工)可豁免。</span></p>
  <h2 class="tgt" data-section="21"><span class="tgt" data-section="21" data-sentence="0" data-group="21-0">餐馆、酒吧和食品或饮料店<br />  </span></h2>  <p class="tgt" data-section="23"><span class="tgt" data-section="23" data-sentence="0" data-group="23-0">禁止在室内和室外提供服务。<br />  </span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="24"><span class="tgt" data-section="24" data-sentence="0" data-group="24-0">允许外卖、汽车快卖（Drive-thru）和送货上门，包括卖酒。</span></p>
  <h2 class="tgt" data-section="25"><span class="tgt" data-section="25" data-sentence="0" data-group="25-0">健身房，运动和娱乐健身</span></h2>  <p class="tgt" data-section="27"><span class="tgt" data-section="27" data-sentence="0" data-group="27-0">关闭所有室内设施，包括室内球场、游泳池和溜冰场。</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="28"><span class="tgt" data-section="28" data-sentence="0" data-group="28-0">允许社区中心及多用途设施(例如：YMCA)开放，以做幼儿服务等获批准的活动。</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="29"><span class="tgt" data-section="29" data-sentence="0" data-group="29-0">不允许室内个人和团体项目(包括训练)；大型赛事，如残疾人运动员和职业联赛(如NHL、CFL、MLS、NBA)的训练可豁免。</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="30"><span class="tgt" data-section="30" data-sentence="0" data-group="30-0">户外运动、课程和设施的使用限制在10人以内。</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="31"><span class="tgt" data-section="31" data-sentence="0" data-group="31-0">之前的其它措施不变。</span></p>
  <h2 class="tgt" data-section="32"><span class="tgt" data-section="32" data-sentence="0" data-group="32-0">会议及活动场地</span></h2>  <p class="tgt" data-section="38"><span class="tgt" data-section="38" data-sentence="0" data-group="38-0">全部关闭，以下几类例外：法院服务，政府服务，精神健康和成瘾支助服务（如匿名戒酒会）最多允许10人参加</span></p>
  <h2 class="tgt" data-section="39"><span class="tgt" data-section="39" data-sentence="0" data-group="39-0">购物中心、超市、卖酒商店、零售店、车行<br />  </span></h2>  <p class="tgt" data-section="41"><span class="tgt" data-section="41" data-sentence="0" data-group="41-0">零售商店只允许在路边取货或送货上门，</span><span class="tgt" data-section="41" data-sentence="1" data-group="41-1">禁止进店购物，但以下情况除外:</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="43"><span class="tgt" data-section="43" data-sentence="0" data-group="43-0">允许进店购物的地方包括：超市、杂货店、便利店、五金店以及其它经营杂货、啤酒、葡萄酒和酒类的零售商店、药店和安全用品店。</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="44"><span class="tgt" data-section="44" data-sentence="0" data-group="44-0">零售专卖商店（outlet）允许进店购特，顾客容量上限为50%。</span></p>
  <p class="tgt" data-section="45"><span class="tgt" data-section="45" data-sentence="0" data-group="45-0">在商场，专卖店，只允许路边提取货或送货上门，</span><span class="tgt" data-section="45" data-sentence="1" data-group="45-1">禁止亲自购物。商场内的</span><span class="tgt" data-section="45" data-sentence="2" data-group="45-2">像牙医诊所和药店这样的必要服务店，顾客可以进入；</span><span class="tgt" data-section="45" data-sentence="3" data-group="45-3">美食广场开放，只做外卖。</span></p>
