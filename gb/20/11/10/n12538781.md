  <p>副县长谢淑亚10日化身酸菜推广大使与大埤乡长林森宝、大埤乡代表会主席陈秋庆、大埤乡农会总干事吴锦凉等人出席宣传记者会，邀请乡亲作伙到大埤品尝酸菜好滋味。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12538785" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12538785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468497-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">活动当天有酸菜制作DIY体验。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>谢淑亚表示，酸菜是家家户户料理必备的食材，无论是酸菜鸭、刈包、牛肉面，都能拌煮出道道美味。大埤乡的酸菜产量占全国的80%，是全国品质最好、产量最大的产地，有“酸菜的故乡”之美誉，要吃健康美味的酸菜，就一定要来大埤，邀请大家来场酸菜产地之旅，了解酸菜产业文化，认识在地农友。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12538786" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12538786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468499-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">副县长谢淑亚(右)化身酸菜推广大使，邀请大家场来场酸菜产地之旅。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>大埤乡长林森宝表示，大埤<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%85%B8%E8%8F%9C%E8%8A%82.md#1">酸菜节</a>暨<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%86%9C%E4%BA%A7%E5%93%81.md#1">农产品</a>推广活动，将于11月21日登场，有酸菜鸭、炒米粉免费品尝，现场还有酸菜搓搓乐、叠叠乐、手作亲子饭团DIY体验，以及限量50桌，每桌1000元创意料理办桌。11/11日上午8点起开放报名，每人限报一桌，以报名顺序决定预约序位，报名后二天内须汇款至大埤乡公所账户，经确认入账后才完成报名。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12538787" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12538787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468501-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">价值3000元的创意酸菜料理，一桌只要1000元。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>报名方式采：网路报名及亲自到大埤乡公所报名二种方式，详情请上大埤乡公所脸书粉丝页查询。https：/www.facebook.com/tapi.gov.tw</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12538788" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12538788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468511-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大埤酸菜文化馆。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12538789" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12538789" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468515-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">记者会现场由名厨师宋和鸿烹调的酸菜料理，让大家赞不绝口。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12538790" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12538790" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468518-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大埤酸菜文化馆。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12538791" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12538791" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468519-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">令人垂涎的名厨宋和鸿的酸菜料理。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12538792" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12538792" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/468521-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">由名厨宋和鸿烹调的酸菜料理令人垂涎。（廖素贞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>责任编辑：郑桦</p>
