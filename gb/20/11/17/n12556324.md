  <h2><span style="color: #188638;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9D%90%E5%A7%BF%E5%89%8D%E5%BC%AF.md#1">坐姿前弯</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8.md#1">运动</a>（全身后侧<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%82%8C%E8%82%89.md#1">肌肉</a>伸展）</span></h2>  <p>15～30次/1次</p>
  <p><strong>训练部位：</strong></p>
  <p>大腿后侧、阔<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%83%8C%E8%82%8C.md#1">背肌</a>、肩膀柔软度</p>
  <p><strong>作法：</strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_12556354" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/foward-bending-pose-1-rrxf-health11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12556354" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/foward-bending-pose-1-rrxf-health11-600x443.jpg" alt="坐姿前弯动作一。（日日幸福提供）" width="600" b="443" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">坐姿前弯动作一。（日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>1. 坐姿双脚伸直，双手抓着毛巾绕过脚掌。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12556355" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/foward-bending-pose-2-rrxf-health11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12556355" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/foward-bending-pose-2-rrxf-health11-600x291.jpg" alt="坐姿前弯动作二。（日日幸福提供）" width="600" b="291" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">坐姿前弯动作二。（日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 双手慢慢往前抓，直到轻微紧绷的程度。</p>
  <p>NOTE：膝盖保持伸直至轻微紧绷即可，收下巴、肩颈放松。</p>
