  <p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">同时，在该协会网站登记的待售物业也比去年同期减少</span></span>20%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
  <p lang="en-AU">REIWA<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">主席</span></span>Damian Collins<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">表示，促使房产销售速度加快的因素包括待售房源吃紧、低利率以及强劲的人口增长回升。</span></span></p>
  <p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-TW">他说：“尽管市场总体上有所改善，但某些地区房市表现更为亮眼，例如：在</span>9<span lang="zh-TW">月，珀斯房产销售最快的前十区，其销售天数都少于珀斯房产销售中位天数</span>-28<span lang="zh-TW">天。”</span></p>
  <p lang="en-AU">Subiaco<span lang="zh-TW">区与</span>Kingsley<span lang="zh-TW">区的房产销售速度最快，仅需</span>12<span lang="zh-TW">天，比去年</span>9<span lang="zh-TW">月少</span>16<span lang="zh-TW">天。</span></p>
  <p lang="en-AU">Rivervale<span lang="zh-TW">紧随其后，房产销售中位时间为</span>14<span lang="zh-TW">天，比去年同期少</span>11<span lang="zh-TW">天。</span></p>
  <p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN">其余</span>7<span lang="zh-CN">个房产销售最快的区（按</span>2019<span lang="zh-CN">年</span>9<span lang="zh-CN">月和</span>2020<span lang="zh-CN">年</span>9<span lang="zh-CN">月房产交易量超过</span>10<span lang="zh-CN">个的地区进行筛选）依次为</span>Darch<span lang="zh-CN">、</span>Woodvale<span lang="zh-CN">、</span>Swan View<span lang="zh-CN">、</span>Beechboro<span lang="zh-CN">、</span>Coolbellup<span lang="zh-CN">、</span>Mount Hawthorn<span lang="zh-CN">和</span>Greenwood<span lang="zh-CN">。</span></p>
