  <p>常常设法闪避<br />  还是拢闪避不开<br />  只好把目睭眯起来</p>
  <p>哪会知影目睭一眯起来<br />  反倒转看到记忆深处有什么离我而去<br />  譬如蓝天、绿野、青山、碧水<br />  譬如鸟只、蝴蝶、没喷农药的蔬菜水果<br />  譬如童年、乡情、纯真、欢笑<br />  譬如安详、诚恳、朴实、平和……</p>
  <p>离我而去，离我而去<br />  唉，愈去愈远，愈去愈渺茫<br />  渐渐沉入怀念里……</p>
  <p>1996/1/19台湾时报副刊</p>
  <p><strong>Sink into the memory</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
  <p>At the present tine<br />  I often feel there are many things rush toward me, dash toward me<br />  For instant, the vehicle, the trash, the noise, the dust<br />  For instant, the mountain which is fell in sick, the water which in polluted<br />  For instant, the sexual advertisement, the scrabble wall<br />  For instant, the defraud, prerogative, rob, unlawful gun</p>
  <p>I often try to keep off it<br />  But I always can’t keep off it<br />  As soon as to narrow my eyes</p>
  <p>How can I know as soon as I narrow my eyes<br />  There are something get away from my deep memory on the contrary<br />  For instant, the blue sky, the green field, the vernal mountain, the pure water<br />  For instant, the birds, butterflies, vegetables and fruits which no farm insecticide<br />  For instant, the childhood, country feelings, innocence, laugh heartily<br />  For instant, the easiness, honest, simple, gentle…</p>
  <p>Get away from me, get away from me<br />  Alas, more far more get away, more dimming more get away<br />  Sink into the memory slowly…@</p>
