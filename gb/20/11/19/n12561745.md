  <p>人体出现疾病的主要原因是免疫力低下，只有提高自身的免疫力才能预防疾病，而常吃虾就有这样的功效。虾不但具有增强免疫力的功效，而且还能抗早衰。</p>
  <p>虾中含有丰富的镁，镁对心脏活动具有重要的调节作用，能很好地保护心血管系统，它可减少血液中的胆固醇含量，防止动脉硬化，同时还能扩张冠状动脉，有利于预防高血压及心肌梗死，是血管和心脏的天然保护伞！</p>
  <p>现代社会已经进入网络社会，电子产品融入人们的生活中，人们工作、学习都离不开手机、电脑等，但长期使用这些电子产品对眼部的危害极大，这时可以多吃虾。因为虾中富含维生素A，可保护眼睛。</p>
  <p>现代人生活琐事的繁多及工作压力的增大，易出现疲惫的现象，除了睡眠和按摩的方式可去除疲劳感之外，也可以多食用虾，因为它含有维生素B群，不但能消除疲劳，而且还能增强体力。</p>
  <p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E9%85%A5%E5%A4%A7%E8%99%BE.md#1">黄金酥大虾</a>，不仅色泽亮丽、外形美观，而且又酥又脆、鲜香无比。罗厨先将虾开背，然后去虾的纱线，这样不仅能保证鲜香的味道，也更容易将虾炸透炸脆。</p>
  <h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%BB%84%E9%87%91%E9%85%A5%E5%A4%A7%E8%99%BE.md#1">黄金酥大虾</a></h2>  <p>主料：明虾6个</p>
  <p>配料：葱粒、香菜粒、炸面碎</p>
  <p>调料：胡椒盐、生粉</p>
  <p>做法：</p>
  <p>1. 先将大明虾开背。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561760" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  2. 去明虾沙肠后冲洗干净。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561761" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2-18-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  3. 在明虾的背面撒上一点椒盐做底味。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3-13.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561763" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3-13-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  4. 再撒上一层比较厚的生粉。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  5. 用约180度的油温把明虾炸定型。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  6. 将明虾里的油份吸出来。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/6-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/6-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  7. 放入香菜粒、葱粒炒香。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/7-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561768" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/7-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  8. 再放炸面碎。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561771" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  9. 放入炸好的明虾。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/9-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561772" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/9-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  10. 将面碎拌明虾炒匀。</p>
