  <p>中医认为，牛肉有补中益气、滋养脾胃、强健筋骨、化痰息风、止渴止涎的功效。牛肉蛋白质所含的人体必需氨基酸较多，而且脂肪和胆固醇的含量较低，因此，特别适合胖人和高血压、冠心病、血管硬化和糖尿病病人适量食用。</p>
  <p>洋葱被誉为“蔬菜皇后”，它所含的微量元素硒是一种很强的抗氧化剂，能清除体内的自由基，增强细胞的活力和代谢能力，具有防癌抗衰老的功效。</p>
  <p>洋葱中含有植物杀菌素，如大蒜素等，因而有很强的杀菌能力。洋葱也是蔬菜中唯一含前列腺素A的，能扩张血管，降低血液黏度，因而可以降血压、增加冠状动脉的血流量，从而预防血栓的形成。</p>
  <p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B4%8B%E8%91%B1%E7%82%92%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">洋葱炒牛肉</a>色香味俱全，不仅好吃有营养，而且健康不长胖，适合老人、小孩及全家人食用。</p>
  <p>罗厨强调，为保证牛肉软嫩香浓的口感，腌制牛肉非常重要，不仅要加鸡蛋，还添加了一点油。另外，炒牛肉时要添加一点白酒，这样牛肉会更加鲜嫩可口。最后菜肴出锅时添加几滴香油，使原本就色香味俱全的美味香气四溢，令人垂涎。</p>
  <h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B4%8B%E8%91%B1%E7%82%92%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">洋葱炒牛肉</a></h2>  <p>主料：牛肉、洋葱</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/d4f7595f27f20ce75e39515aa8696a6d.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561731" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/d4f7595f27f20ce75e39515aa8696a6d-450x263.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="263" /></a></p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/c250b55979d81dc5d3e2d255c6cf18d7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561732" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/c250b55979d81dc5d3e2d255c6cf18d7-450x260.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="260" /></a><br />  配料：葱段、姜片、蒜片、鸡蛋<br />  调料：盐、砂糖、生抽、生粉、油、白酒、老抽</p>
  <p>做法：<br />  1. 将洋葱切成均匀的小块。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1-15.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561712" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1-15-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  2. 牛肉按横纹切薄片。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2-17.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2-17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  3. 用生抽、白糖、鸡蛋腌制牛肉片。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561717" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  4. 添加一些油后将牛肉片搅拌均匀。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561721" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  5. 将蒜片、姜片炒香。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561722" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  6. 放入腌好的牛肉片一起翻炒。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/6-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561724" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/6-7-450x264.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="264" /></a><br />  7. 加入白酒、生抽、蚝油炒匀即出镬。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/7-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561725" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/7-8-450x274.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="274" /></a><br />  8. 倒入洋葱炒几下后加盖。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561727" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  9. 放入牛肉后添加老抽、生粉水炒匀。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/9-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561728" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/9-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  10. 放入葱段后添加一点香油。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/10-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12561729" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/10-5-450x257.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="257" /></a><br />  11. 起锅盛盘，成品菜肴色、香、味俱全。</p>
