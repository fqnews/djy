  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a>牛奶小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">吐司</a></span></h2>  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E5%A5%B6%E5%90%90%E5%8F%B8.md#1">牛奶吐司</a>，是单吃或搭配各种食材都很好吃的吐司。</p>
  <p>这款面团使用蜂蜜代替细砂糖，而蜂蜜口味可以选用自己喜欢的，例如甜度跟香气都最浓郁的龙眼蜜，或者尾韵带着花香的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8D%94%E6%9E%9D%E8%9C%9C.md#1">荔枝蜜</a>，打造出自己的特色吐司。</p>
  <p>另外，在吐司面团中添加蜂蜜，能让吐司的口感更湿润，也让烤色更好看。</p>
  <p>份量 ?: 4条/每条150g</p>
  <p>模具 ?: 银色水果条模 4个</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">前置作业?</span></h3>  <p>将银色水果条模内部均匀涂上奶油备用。</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <h4><strong>面团?A</strong></h4>  <p>高筋面粉 295g<br />  低筋面粉 35g<br />  盐 6g<br />  奶粉 10g<br />  酵母粉 3.3g<br />  水 100g<br />  牛奶 115g<br />  蜂蜜 33g</p>
  <p><strong>B</strong></p>
  <p>奶油 26g</p>
  <p><strong>表面装饰</strong></p>
  <p>奶油 适量</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法 Steps</span></h3>  <p>1、将A材料倒入钢盆中，搅拌至面团表面光滑有扩展的状态，再加入奶油，搅拌至面团有薄膜后，基本发酵60分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12531411" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/ad2281c2e7fec407d9fd5a97e886dc6c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12531411 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/ad2281c2e7fec407d9fd5a97e886dc6c-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将A材料倒入钢盆中，搅拌至面团表面光滑有扩展的状态，再加入B奶油搅拌。（图文／日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2、将面团分割成12个（每个50g），分别滚圆后，进行中间发酵15分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12531409" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/31fc2992da382ae6e144ed4fb7e74a92.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12531409 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/31fc2992da382ae6e144ed4fb7e74a92-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将面团分别滚圆后，进行中间发酵15分钟。 （图文／日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3、将面团开成长方形，左右两边往中间折并用手掌根轻压排气。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12531410" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4a697722f017f991978f903e23f3c659.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12531410 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4a697722f017f991978f903e23f3c659-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用手掌根轻压排气面团。（图文／日日幸福）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4、由上往下卷，最后将面团收口，放入烤模型中，做最后发酵30～40分钟，或发酵至满模。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12531431" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5884743eadac82abea42b837c7665ef6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12531431 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/5884743eadac82abea42b837c7665ef6-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">由上往下卷，最后将面团收口。（图文／日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5、烤箱预热190℃，放入烤箱烤20分钟，烤好后取出并在面团表面刷上奶油即可。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12531408" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/11879e20b8e118bfd32b4316242e54b8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12531408 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/11879e20b8e118bfd32b4316242e54b8-450x298.jpg" alt="吐司" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在烤好的吐司上方涂抹奶油。（图／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3>&lt;本文摘自：<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href="https://www.books.com.tw/products/0010861180" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">小面包 Little Bread：食材天然低负担，兼具美味营养！大小刚刚好，佐餐主食都适合！</a></span>，日日幸福提供。&gt;</h3>  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">· <a style="color: #008000;" href=><a href="/gb/20/1/23/n11816355.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> 6种食材搭配食谱 教你轻松制作“免煮餐”</a></span><br />  <span style="color: #008000;"> · <a style="color: #008000;" href=><a href="/gb/20/10/15/n12477808.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">【在家咖啡厅】手作外酥内软“奶油蜜糖吐司”</a></span><br />  <span style="color: #008000;"> · <a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/18/6/18/n10493544.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">享受早晨 来个滑嫩顺口的火腿蛋杯吧！</a></span></h3>  <p>责任编辑：伊芙</p>
