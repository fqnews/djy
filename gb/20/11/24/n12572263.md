  <figure id="attachment_12572336" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/20201124-pts-05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12572336" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/20201124-pts-05-600x400.jpg" alt="禾浩辰" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E6%97%A5%E6%A2%A6%E5%A4%96%E9%80%81%E7%8E%8B.md#1">白日梦外送王</a>》由擅长动作片拍摄的洪子烜执导，禾浩辰（左）与巫建和（右）剧中有多场精彩逗趣打闹戏码。（公视提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">?浩辰在剧里饰演个性正直外送员“宋达彦”，他表示当初看完剧本认为这是个很热血的角色，还说“跟我的人生有点像，一开始对所有的事情、这世界都有想像、憧憬”。</span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">回顾近三周拍摄期，<span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;">?浩辰</span>说“每天都很欢乐”，还表示“好快喔！不像来工作，每天现场都是很像在校园里学个新东西，不太想这么快杀青啦”。</span></span></p>
  <figure id="attachment_12572325" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/20201124-pts-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12572325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/20201124-pts-03-600x900.jpg" alt="孔令元" width="450" b="900" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AD%94%E5%AD%90.md#1">孔子</a>第76代子孙、新生代演员<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AD%94%E4%BB%A4%E5%85%83.md#1">孔令元</a>剧中诠释高冷的Youtuber。（公视提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">而孔子第</span></span><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;">76</span><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">代孙、孔令奇的远房堂妹孔令元饰演女主角“小天”，私下个性热情的她表示角色跟自己反差大。为诠释剧中高冷的</span></span><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;">Youtuber</span><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">，她形容“不是用我的脑袋在运作”，而是让自己身体跟生活里去实现一种全新的状态；在扮演这个角色之后，孔令元也觉得“啊！好舍不得离开这角色”。</span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;">饰演</span><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;">?浩辰</span>父亲的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%9C%E5%AD%A6%E4%BA%AE.md#1">卜学亮</a>，在剧中是知名甜点师，却因故落魄守在小面店里，他也认为这次不用刻意营造笑点，“生活化的演法反倒带来意外的放松与收获”。</span></span></p>
  <figure id="attachment_12572334" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/20201124-pts-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12572334" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/20201124-pts-04-600x400.jpg" alt="卜学亮" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">卜学亮与禾浩辰剧中饰演感情好、相依为命的父子。（公视提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">贺岁片《白日梦外送王》搭配时兴的外送员、甜点大赛话题，在今年武汉肺炎（中共病毒）疫情冲击全球，逼着各行各业出招改变现状，导演洪子烜希望以全新动作喜剧，让观众们获得正能量、迎接充满变化的</span></span><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">年。</span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: 细明体-ExtB, serif;"><span lang="zh-TW">洪子烜形容故事核心表示“平凡的人们如何在被迫改变的时刻突破困境”，也藉由怀抱理想的外送员与不得志的厨师父亲切入，带出亲人间相互的矛盾与关怀。</span></span></p>
  <figure id="attachment_12572337" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/20201124-pts-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12572337" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/20201124-pts-02-600x400.jpg" alt="郭源元、巫建和" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">郭源元、巫建和主演公视贺岁喜剧《白日梦外送王》。（公视提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12572339" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/20201124-pts-01.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12572339" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/20201124-pts-01-600x400.jpg" alt="曾少宗、巫建和" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">曾少宗、巫建和主演公视贺岁喜剧《白日梦外送王》。（公视提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>责任编辑：辛幼安</p>
