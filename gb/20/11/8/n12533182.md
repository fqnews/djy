  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">前置作业</span></h2>  <p>腰果事先以150℃烤15分钟至有香气，略切至葡萄干大小。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">份量 </span></h2>  <p>10个 / 每个60g</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">面团材料?</span></h2>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">A</span></h3>  <p>高筋<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E7%B2%89.md#1">面粉</a> 255g<br />  低筋<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E7%B2%89.md#1">面粉</a> 65g<br />  细砂糖 20g<br />  盐 5g<br />  低糖<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%85%B5%E6%AF%8D%E7%B2%89.md#1">酵母粉</a> 3.2g<br />  <ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>果肉 130g<br />  水 95g<br />  初榨<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A9%84%E6%A6%84%E6%B2%B9.md#1">橄榄油</a> 10g</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">B</span></h3>  <p>腰果 50g</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">表面装饰</span></h3>  <p>高筋面粉˙˙˙˙ 适量</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">做法 Steps</span></h2>  <p>1、将A材料倒入搅拌机的钢盆中搅拌面团至可扩展的状态，取出面团后包入B材料切拌均匀，基本发酵大约60分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533207" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/b5733fe63057535cbb266a7e3243d87c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533207 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/b5733fe63057535cbb266a7e3243d87c-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将A材料倒入搅拌机的钢盆中搅拌面团至可扩展的状态，取出面团后包入B材料切拌均匀，基本发酵大约60分钟。 （图文／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2、将面团分割成10个，滚圆后进行中间发酵20分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533209" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2e85c6983b8ae8fb5ac24a3e8289a57b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533209 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/2e85c6983b8ae8fb5ac24a3e8289a57b-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">面团分割成10个。（图文／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3、轻拍面团排掉大气泡，将一半面团再次滚圆；另一半做成椭圆形，再做最后发酵30～40分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533205" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3f9823d533c7b4272f5905f0d13f471c.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533205 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3f9823d533c7b4272f5905f0d13f471c-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">轻拍面团排掉大气泡，将一半面团再次滚圆；另一半做成椭圆形。（图文／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4、烤箱预热220℃；烤焙前面团表面撒高筋粉、划线，再放进烤箱烤15分钟即可。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533204" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4fd79494c864172c149b35ef2454dd98.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533204 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4fd79494c864172c149b35ef2454dd98-450x298.jpg" alt="面团" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烤箱预热220℃；烤焙前面团表面撒高筋粉、划线。（图文／日日幸福提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12533198" style="width: 150px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8c81ada9c01afd1e016e8f44abca4f42-e1604798600330.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533198" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/8c81ada9c01afd1e016e8f44abca4f42-e1604798794607.jpg" alt="小面包" width="150" b="201" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《小<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9D%A2%E5%8C%85.md#1">面包</a> Little Bread》书封（图／日日幸福出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3>&lt;本文摘自：<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href="https://www.books.com.tw/products/0010861180" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">小面包 Little Bread：食材天然低负担，兼具美味营养！大小刚刚好，佐餐主食都适合！</a></span>，日日幸福提供。&gt;</h3>  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">· <a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/16/3/21/n4667684.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">烘焙点心：香橙蛋糕</a></span><br />  <span style="color: #008000;"> · <a style="color: #008000;" href=><a href="/gb/20/6/18/n12195451.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">酪梨生起司蛋糕 无奶 无蛋 无麸质 素食</a></span><br />  <span style="color: #008000;"> · <a style="color: #008000;" href=><a href="/gb/20/7/1/n12224964.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">橄榄油的质感与风味 正确使用5个关键点</a></span></h3>  <p>责任编辑：伊芙</p>
