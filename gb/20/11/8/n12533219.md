  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9F%94%E5%AB%A9.md#1">柔嫩</a>鸡胸便当</span></h2>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">主菜：<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9F%94%E5%AB%A9.md#1">柔嫩</a>鸡胸（冷藏保存5天）</span></h2>  <figure id="attachment_12533266" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/51dbdbb990df92f577945f9e0ea40aa6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533266" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/51dbdbb990df92f577945f9e0ea40aa6.jpg" alt="鸡胸肉" width="500" b="328" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a>要不柴，处理方法很重要！（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E8%83%B8%E8%82%89.md#1">鸡胸肉</a> 1 副（300g）<br />  粗盐、胡椒粉少许<br />  酱料<br />  <ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%98%86%E5%B8%83.md#1">昆布</a>高汤 400ml<br />  淡口酱油 120ml<br />  <ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%91%B3%E9%86%82.md#1">味醂</a> 120ml<br />  萝卜新芽（或生菜） 适量</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>  <p>1. 鸡肉撒上粗盐、胡椒粉各少许，揉按一下。<br />  2. 平底锅不放油，干煎鸡肉。皮朝下，煎到酥黄再翻面，肉色一转白即取出。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533265" style="width: 499px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/cf4cc29102b477062cc9d831ebd1fa15.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/cf4cc29102b477062cc9d831ebd1fa15.jpg" alt="平底锅" width="499" b="329" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">平底锅不放油，干煎鸡肉。皮朝下，煎到酥黄再翻面，肉色一转白即取出。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 酱料注入深锅，以中火煮滚，放入鸡肉，冒出小泡泡时立刻熄火，盖上锅盖，藉由余温慢慢渗透，最好浸泡至少8 小时（一个晚上），使之入味。肉嫩味醇，做法极简，只需要时间。斜切鸡片使口感更滑嫩，淋酱汁，添生菜一道享用。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">副菜：烤镶豆糯米椒</span></h2>  <figure id="attachment_12533322" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4dc9c86cf2180a14d52c84cea52b6604.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533322" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/4dc9c86cf2180a14d52c84cea52b6604.jpg" alt="糯米椒" width="500" b="328" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烤镶豆糯米椒，看起来非常精致美味。（图／商周出版）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <p>糯米椒 10 条<br />  煮熟或蒸熟的大豆 70g<br />  味噌 2 小匙<br />  <ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%91%B3%E9%86%82.md#1">味醂</a> 1 小匙<br />  麻油 1 小匙</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>  <p>1. 豆类不拘，将煮熟或蒸熟的大豆、黑豆、甜豆等与味噌、味醂、麻油一起拌匀。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533267" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/f79e995cd0b5a2a360749d86f0987b6a.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533267" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/f79e995cd0b5a2a360749d86f0987b6a.jpg" alt="味增" width="500" b="328" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将煮熟或蒸熟的大豆、黑豆、甜豆等与味噌、味醂、麻油一起拌。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 纵剖糯米椒一刀，将拌好的豆类塞入，送烤箱（180 度）烤7 ～ 8 分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533323" style="width: 524px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/ffe61fac33d00b5c00c2be9413c47533.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12533323" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/ffe61fac33d00b5c00c2be9413c47533.jpg" alt="糯米椒" width="524" b="344" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">纵剖糯米椒一刀，将拌好的豆类塞入，送烤箱（180 度）烤7 ～ 8 分钟。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">常备菜：橙香红萝卜（冷藏保存4 天）</span></h2>  <figure id="attachment_12533276" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3990753ef291e2cf0febaa0042a7ef17.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533276" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/3990753ef291e2cf0febaa0042a7ef17.jpg" alt="红萝卜" width="451" b="312" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">橙香红萝卜，冷藏保存可以4天。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <p>红萝卜 1 条（200g）<br />  香菜 适量<br />  粗盐 适量<br />  糖 1.5 大匙<br />  柳橙汁 2 大匙</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>  <p>1. 红萝卜用削皮器削成条状。香菜摘取菜叶。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533296" style="width: 465px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/010f63d7fbfbde15d34be92d35751426.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533296" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/010f63d7fbfbde15d34be92d35751426.jpg" alt="红萝卜" width="465" b="275" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红萝卜削成条状。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 钵里放入红萝卜、香菜，盐抓适量，再加入糖、柳橙汁，浸渍至少10 分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533301" style="width: 466px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/b241915a0c26cf5a90b0b6db286acdc8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533301" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/b241915a0c26cf5a90b0b6db286acdc8.jpg" alt="红萝卜" width="466" b="247" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">钵里放入红萝卜、香菜，盐抓适量，再加入糖、柳橙汁。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 入饭盒前，先拧一下水分。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">时雨煮牛肉便当</span></h2>  <figure id="attachment_12533304" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/f03d0a6a71927ff576468e53050ba683.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533304" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/f03d0a6a71927ff576468e53050ba683.jpg" alt="牛肉" width="450" b="693" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">时雨煮牛肉便当，配菜可以佐芥末芦笋、梅香鸿禧菇。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">主菜：时雨煮牛肉（冷藏保存5天）</span></h2>  <figure id="attachment_12533312" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1e068b1e4b34a8ffa884afbf09603dba.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533312" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/1e068b1e4b34a8ffa884afbf09603dba.jpg" alt="牛肉" width="451" b="286" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">时雨煮牛肉。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <p>牛肉( 薄片) 300g<br />  大葱 1 根<br />  姜 约25g<br />  酱料<br />  水 200ml<br />  酒 100ml<br />  酱油、味醂 各50ml<br />  糖 1 ～ 1.5 大匙</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>  <p>1. 大葱斜切成片。牛肉切成4 公分长。姜切丝。<br />  2. 酱料入锅煮滚后，放入牛肉、姜丝，以中火煮到缩汁八分，若出现浮沫请捞除。最后投入葱片，稍微煮一下。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533309" style="width: 524px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/980b12e32ef587175a0ce208f346f643.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12533309" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/980b12e32ef587175a0ce208f346f643.jpg" alt="牛肉" width="524" b="327" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">酱料入锅煮滚后，放入牛肉、姜丝，以中火煮到缩汁八分，若出现浮沫请捞除。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>注：<span style="color: #0000ff;">“佃煮”</span>是日本人用来保存食物的烹调法，以酱油、酒、糖煨煮，食材入味后，外表裹上一层浓稠酱汁，收干水分后的食材能减缓腐坏速度，适宜存放。“时雨煮”是佃煮的一种，它的料理时间如及时雨一般短暂，故有其名。除了酱油、酒、糖等基本调味外，还加了姜，宜于冷藏常备。<span style="color: #0000ff;">（推荐阅读：<a style="color: #0000ff;" href=><a href="/gb/20/9/2/n12375397.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">【低糖版食谱】日式小菜野菇佃煮 轻松上菜</a>）</span></p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">副菜：芥末芦笋</span></h2>  <figure id="attachment_12533275" style="width: 501px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/466e2f65bdfc9e20a489749339bb07b4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12533275" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/466e2f65bdfc9e20a489749339bb07b4.jpg" alt="芦笋" width="501" b="311" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">芥末芦笋。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <p>芦笋 70g<br />  盐 少许<br />  酱料<br />  淡口酱油 1 小匙<br />  芥末酱粒 1 小匙<br />  蛋黄 1 颗分</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>  <p>1. 芦笋削除茎部硬皮，放入加盐的滚水中汆烫，烫到色泽转艳绿，软度适中，充分渗透盐分时捞起。<br />  2. 切成适当长度，与酱料拌合。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533318" style="width: 523px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/fca5c524b53c8cb566bf10ca36fc52de.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12533318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/fca5c524b53c8cb566bf10ca36fc52de.jpg" alt="芦笋" width="523" b="328" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">芦笋切成适当长度，与酱料拌合。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">常备菜?:?梅香鸿禧菇（冷藏保存1周）</span></h2>  <figure id="attachment_12533288" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/ad8d2d78230b3ceef8b653d83798832d.png"><img class="wp-image-12533288 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/ad8d2d78230b3ceef8b653d83798832d-450x390.png" alt="鸿禧菇" width="450" b="390" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">梅香鸿禧菇。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <p>鸿禧菇 100g<br />  盐 适量<br />  梅干 1 颗<br />  橄榄油 1 小匙</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>  <p>1. 煮一锅水，汆烫鸿禧菇，一熟即捞起，沥干后，撒入盐适量。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12533280" style="width: 524px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/197ddb0d35bb322066cfdb424106802e.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12533280" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/11/197ddb0d35bb322066cfdb424106802e.jpg" alt="鸿禧菇" width="524" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">煮一锅水，汆烫鸿禧菇，一熟即捞起。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 梅干去籽，剁成泥，与橄榄油混匀后，加入鸿禧菇拌合。</p>
