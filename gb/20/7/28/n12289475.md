<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>研究：西澳Claremont公寓持续保持高价位</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/959f74ca57ea3d2750f6a080283adede-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年07月28日讯】<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">（大纪元记者杨新云澳洲珀斯编译报导）</span><span lang="zh-TW">最近，一项关于珀斯都市区公寓销售的分析表明，</span></span>Claremont<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的公寓持续保持高价位，该区公寓市场表现强劲，超过邻近地区如</span></span>Subiaco<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，</span></span>Floreat<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，</span></span>Jolimont<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">和</span></span>Shenton Park<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">据商业新闻网报导，</span><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%BE%B3%E6%88%BF%E5%9C%B0%E7%94%A2.md#1"><span lang="zh-TW">西澳房地产</span></a><span lang="zh-TW">研究机构</span></span><ahref="https://www.businessnews.com.au/Company/PropertyESP">Property ESP</a><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">分析了从</span></span>2017<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>1<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月至</span></span>2020<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>4<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月以上五个地区</span></span>593<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">个公寓销售情况，其中</span></span>Claremont<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">区的公寓销售量占比</span></span>53%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">根据</span></span>PropertyESP<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的数据显示，在相同户型公寓房销售中，</span></span>Claremont<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公寓房成交价要比</span></span>Subiaco<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公寓房的价格高出</span></span>8<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万</span></span>-10<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">万澳元。</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU">PropertyESP<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">还发现，在这三年期间，五个地区的公寓转售数量相对较低（总计</span></span>11<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">笔）。在租金收益方面，截至</span></span>2020<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年</span></span>5<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月，</span></span>Claremont<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">与</span></span>Subiaco/Shenton<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的公寓房租金收益率分别为</span></span>4%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">和</span></span>4.5%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">此外，</span></span>PropertyESP<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公司的执行董事</span></span>Samantha Reece<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">女士表示，在</span></span>3<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">卧室</span></span>2<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">卫浴的公寓房销售中，</span></span>Claremont<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">区的公寓房占一半。</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">“自</span></span>2017<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年以来，</span></span>Claremont<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的公寓房中位价也上涨了</span></span>2.7%<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，这表明高档地区的房价逆势而上，房子还会升值。”她还说，“毫无疑问，</span></span>Claremont<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">椭圆形开发地带的开发以及其便利设施对房地产价值产生了重大影响，该地区买家应该对房产（增值潜力）充满信心。此外，该地区的开发项目还配备了咖啡厛与屋顶露台，完全与生活方式和便利性联系在一起。”</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">西澳政府城市开发规划机构</span><span lang="zh-TW">—</span><span lang="zh-TW">西澳开发局（</span></span><ahref="https://www.businessnews.com.au/Company/DevelopmentWA">Development WA</a><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">）在几年前启动了开发项目</span><span lang="zh-TW">——</span></span>Claremont on the Park<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，占地</span></span>9.4<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">公顷，计划新开发的房产超过</span></span>750<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">套。</span></span></p>
<p lang="en-AU"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">项目第一块地皮于</span></span>2012<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年卖给了开发商</span></span><ahref="https://www.businessnews.com.au/Company/Georgiou-Living">Georgiou Living</a><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，目前建成的公寓楼名为</span></span>The Pocket<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">，共有</span></span>95<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">套公寓。在此地区的其它项目还包括开发商</span></span><ahref="https://www.businessnews.com.au/Company/Blackburne">Blackburne</a><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">承建的项目</span><span lang="zh-TW">—</span></span>Essence<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">、开发商</span></span><ahref="https://www.businessnews.com.au/Company/Mirvac">Mirvac</a><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">的两个项目</span><span lang="zh-TW">—</span></span>The Grandst<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">和</span></span>The Reserve<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">以及开发商</span></span><ahref="https://www.businessnews.com.au/Company/Celsius-Property-Group">Celsius Property Group</a><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">承建的项目</span><span lang="zh-TW">—</span></span>Lucent Claremont<span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">。</span></span></p>
