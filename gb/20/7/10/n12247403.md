<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】栉瓜扣花菇</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/0cce9652f946384c0fea969650389ec1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>成品菜肴香气四溢、口味独特。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>栉瓜原产于墨西哥，是从南瓜变形栽种而来的瓜果，在亚洲其它地区还有西葫芦、美洲葫芦、矮南瓜及嫩南瓜等别名。栉瓜有深绿色、浅绿色、黄色以及绿色带浅色条纹等多样瓜皮色泽，形状亦分成类似小黄瓜的长条状及南瓜形的圆弧状，造型多元，吃起来也有些微差异。</p>
<p>栉瓜是一款低热量、低淀粉、低GI（升糖指数）、高纤维的蔬菜，在“世界健康食物”中排名极佳。因为吃了不会变胖，栉瓜也是减肥时期的好帮手。</p>
<p>栉瓜也含丰富的抗氧化物，如胡萝卜素、叶黄素、玉米黄素等，对皮肤和眼睛有益。其它如铜、锰、镁、磷、钾、维生素C、叶酸、维生素B6及维生素K含量也极佳，是不可多得的超级食物。</p>
<p>花菇是香菇的一种，有“植物皇后”之誉。历来被认为是延年益寿的补品。据《本草纲目》载：花菇性平、味甘，补脾胃，益气，能益气不饥，治风破血，化痰理气，益味助食，理小便不禁。主治食欲减退、少气乏力。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A0%89%E7%93%9C%E6%89%A3%E8%8A%B1%E8%8F%87.md#1">栉瓜扣花菇</a>，栉瓜环淋滑软嫩，花菇香滑而富有弹性，蒸的过程可以大大去掉栉瓜的菜青味，同时也可以使栉瓜充分吸收花菇自然独特的香气和汁水，再配上秘制的鲍鱼汁，为这道菜画龙点睛，提升了菜品尊贵的气息。</p>
<h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A0%89%E7%93%9C%E6%89%A3%E8%8A%B1%E8%8F%87.md#1">栉瓜扣花菇</a></h2>
<p>主料：栉瓜2条、花菇6朵</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/0ca194ab9fc70972b064a8c6146d83a3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247407" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/0ca194ab9fc70972b064a8c6146d83a3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2b97a2dd1f44c282bb6c82d4589de127.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247408" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2b97a2dd1f44c282bb6c82d4589de127-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>配料：姜片、葱段、虾米、腊肉皮</p>
<p>调料：海盐、糖、胡椒粉</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将栉瓜去皮洗净改为瓜环。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-17.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 去掉栉瓜环中的瓜瓤。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 将发好的日本花菇放在瓜环中间。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/3-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247411" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/3-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 将姜、葱、虾米用油炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/4-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/4-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 锅内放入清水。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/5-5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247413" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/5-5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 锅内放入盐、糖、胡椒粉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/6-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247414" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/6-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 在栉瓜和香菇上面放两条腊肉皮。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/7-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247416" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/7-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 水煮沸后倒入钢盘，浸住瓜环蒸20分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 蒸好后将瓜环花菇放入碟中。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/9-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247420" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/9-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 将秘制鲍鱼汁淋在上面。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/10-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/10-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴香气四溢、口味独特。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/11-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12247422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/11-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
