<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>花椰菜米炒饭 高纤低糖～吃多也不发胖！</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/JJU9c"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/yktzsx335/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>低卡低糖的花椰菜米炒饭，是零厨艺的新手都能轻松驾驭的料理。
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编按】</strong>想要吃饭又怕胖的朋友有福了！现在有一种像“米”的食材，低卡、高纤维又有维生素，那就是运用<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E6%A4%B0%E8%8F%9C.md#1">花椰菜</a>做成的细碎米粒状。花椰菜米饭少了碳水化合物，和蔬菜、肉类一起拌炒仍然香味迷人，这是零厨艺也能轻松驾驭的一道食谱。重要的是，用花椰菜米代替米饭零淀粉，吃多了也没有罪恶感！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【食材】</span></h2>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E6%A4%B0%E8%8F%9C.md#1">花椰菜</a>米 100g<br />
鸡胸肉 100g<br />
鸡蛋 2颗<br />
洋葱 1/2颗<br />
红萝卜 1/2根<br />
芦笋 1小把<br />
盐 适量<br />
白胡椒粉 适量</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1. 所有的食材都切成丁</p>
<figure id="attachment_12227383" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/41ac1acfb9a2383fffc2779b652b8e9f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12227383" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/41ac1acfb9a2383fffc2779b652b8e9f-450x315.jpg" alt="C2食光," width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">芦笋切丁（<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 热油锅，炒蛋液炒到半熟取出备用</p>
<figure id="attachment_12227384" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/adedc753405a65969c12950490ba7edd.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12227384" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/adedc753405a65969c12950490ba7edd-450x315.jpg" alt="C2食光" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">炒蛋液炒到半熟（<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 鸡胸肉炒到七分熟取出备用</p>
<figure id="attachment_12227386" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2edebfe6b3d228988e606b7239d7b43d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12227386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2edebfe6b3d228988e606b7239d7b43d-450x315.jpg" alt="鸡胸肉" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸡胸肉炒到七分熟（C2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 倒入洋葱丁、红萝卜丁、芦笋丁、花椰菜米炒香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12227387" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8c233274a5768429c9a197af5e5d6f7e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12227387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8c233274a5768429c9a197af5e5d6f7e-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再放入鸡胸肉、炒蛋、芦荀炒香，最后调味即可</p>
<figure id="attachment_12227389" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/d7600c81cb9c01db25f10a7eb499adcb.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12227389" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/d7600c81cb9c01db25f10a7eb499adcb-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（C2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">“营养师的贴心小叮咛”-by 陈小薇营养师</span></h2>
<p>花椰菜米炒饭非常适合进行低糖饮食的朋友；每100g约65大卡，总糖约3.7g，膳食纤维为1.2g左右。花椰菜是十字花科植物，含有许多有益人体健康的特殊营养成分如：槲皮酮、谷胱甘肽，以及硫配糖体，也可以提升身体保护力，就像防护罩一样守护身体健康。</p>
