<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>屏东LOG IN 6种色彩缤纷玩法</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/595d791f4fc89c37bd3ebdd147659bf7-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>水上活动。（业者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年07月31日讯】（大纪元记者林紫馨台湾<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>报导）尽管因为疫情关系，政府实施边境管制，民众无法出国旅游，但随着国内疫情稍缓，7月1日开始安心旅游上路，搭上暑假旅游旺季，台湾国旅市场将迎来前所未有的热潮！</p>
<p>以<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B1%8F%E4%B8%9C.md#1">屏东</a>来说，6月国旅订单量较4月暴增6倍，光是订房数量，6月就比4月暴增7倍，今年1月到6月的业绩也比去年同期成长五成，屏东俨然成为消费者出游的当红炸子鸡！</p>
<p>“还是那个熟悉的屏东，只是你从未想过可以这样玩！”用6色主题建立旅游品牌，将屏东玩得更深入、更缤纷。这次，让你提到屏东时不再只是想到垦丁，而是用“最强彩点王”的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%89%B2%E5%BD%A9.md#1">色彩</a>新玩法，让颜色代表不同的路线特色，包括：</p>
<h2>一、蓝色：运动的</h2>
<p>“代表海平面的蓝——独木舟、潜水、单车、SUP、冲浪、轻航机、滑翔翼、飞行伞”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12297821" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1999a2ce4088eb29b8fab82ddc1ee177.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297821 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1999a2ce4088eb29b8fab82ddc1ee177-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蓝色——小琉球独木舟。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12297819" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/9904c74f66d02eeb239b48c766dd7cb4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297819 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/9904c74f66d02eeb239b48c766dd7cb4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蓝色——公路单车。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蓝色，令人心旷神怡，踏进屏东从蔚蓝的蓝天到湛蓝的海底世界，从海平面上的轻航机、滑翔翼、飞行伞，至海平面下感受屏东蓝色海洋的冲浪、潜水、独木舟，还有单车体验迎面而来不同风采的南国气息。</p>
<h2>二、黄色：传承的</h2>
<p>“代表信仰的黄——迎王祭、抢孤、土地公、五路财神、王爷”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12297827" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/5999969602cdbd4320f43dafbf3fdb81.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297827 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/5999969602cdbd4320f43dafbf3fdb81-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄色——恒春古城。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12297828" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/d9dff7c82ed650c9ee03324c3e4f0144.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297828 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/d9dff7c82ed650c9ee03324c3e4f0144-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄色——东港镇海宫。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黄色，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%89%B2%E5%BD%A9.md#1">色彩</a>明度最高的颜色，五行中为土，象征宇宙中央，黄色包含了“智慧、睿智、知识”，信仰可视为一个记忆保存库，保存从古至今传承下来的鬼神故事，当地的祭典节庆，往往都是旅游地不可被取代的一环。</p>
<h2>三、绿色：生态的</h2>
<p>“代表探索在地生态之美的绿——登山、草原、古道、赏鹰、伯劳鸟、梅花鹿”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12297826" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/f8064c182192e0bdfb170aa8a0977e03.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297826 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/f8064c182192e0bdfb170aa8a0977e03-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">绿色——社顶自然公园。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12297824" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/35723d61e3b7670df69d6a365c4fceb3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297824 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/35723d61e3b7670df69d6a365c4fceb3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">绿色——社顶自然公园。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>绿色，为我们带来平静与安定的感受，从海拔低至高不同风采，水上草原的青青草原绿、阿塱壹古道的丛绿、满州赏鹰的草地绿、社顶公园的原始林翠绿，都是展现自然生命力的色彩。</p>
<h2>四、橙色：人文的</h2>
<p>“代表多元族群融合的橙——客家、原乡部落、闽南、眷村”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12297823" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/50784339028792c67ae95a83c814ff40.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297823 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/50784339028792c67ae95a83c814ff40-600x414.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="414" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">橙色——原住民文化园区。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12297822" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1876f0d6e30d126d86bb8d7705a463ef.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297822 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1876f0d6e30d126d86bb8d7705a463ef-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">橙色——原住民文化园区。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>橙色，是色彩中感受最暖的颜色，具有创造力的能量，屏东的多元文化融合、并存，创造出独特的文化及故事。包括历史悠久的胜利新村，过去是军官眷舍，经过整理保存后再融入许多有意思的艺术装置，或者前往屏东县雾台乡大武村，体验部落文化。</p>
<h2>五、红色：美味的</h2>
<p>“代表美食的米其林红——小吃、美食、水果、渔产及在地风味”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12297832" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/24f244122cb175d42f021a7f1fe435f4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297832 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/24f244122cb175d42f021a7f1fe435f4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红色——屏东晒可可。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12297833" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/cf579a23fe7d5d9b949e787596386685.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297833 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/cf579a23fe7d5d9b949e787596386685-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红色——恒春绿豆蒜。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12297834" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8f10a8c602995f7de539420b5ef4d42d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297834 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8f10a8c602995f7de539420b5ef4d42d-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红色——枋寮鱼干。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12297835" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/96fedea74aae533b95f634085714791f.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297835 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/96fedea74aae533b95f634085714791f-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红色——东港汤饭。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>红色，可以激发我们身体的活力，当旅人遇到美食更能活化旅程的记忆，屏东物产及美食皆爆棚，从莲雾、芒果、红龙果到黑鲔鱼、油鱼子、饭汤里的樱花虾，在在都是颜色品味屏东的热情。</p>
<h2>六、粉红色：生活的</h2>
<p>“代表开心愉悦的粉——文学、建筑走读、渔港”</p>
<figure id="attachment_12297829" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/c5a13659dc9e7a0cba77baac9f438ff8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297829 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/c5a13659dc9e7a0cba77baac9f438ff8-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">粉红色——枋寮蜗牛巷。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12297830" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/69e30d3f6d8139a5d0b2c4ef61df039b.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297830 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/69e30d3f6d8139a5d0b2c4ef61df039b-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">粉红色——职人町。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12297831" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/c35874591af75ed10ea33051cab0ed85.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12297831 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/c35874591af75ed10ea33051cab0ed85-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">粉红色——青创园区。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>粉红色—粉色，带有正面情绪，且有安抚浮躁的效果，代表着青春又温和的能量，体验屏东生活的惬意、怀旧与慢步调的街头，都是屏东圈粉的本事。以景点来说，许多怀旧的建筑融合新的元素成为热门景点，在新旧建筑结合、再生的空间里，感受时光走过的痕迹，也是体验一种在屏东的生活方式。</p>
<p>屏东县县长潘孟安表示，“后疫情时代已正式启动，旅游玩法也迎来转型，国旅首选就是屏东。”并亲自领军各处行销，与<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%84%E7%8B%AE.md#1">雄狮</a>旅行社还有KLOOK合作，从团体及自由行两大行销通路，推出6色主题旅游路线，预计年吸600万人次，缔造百亿元消费商机。</p>
<p>国境之南利用6种颜色拉出多种游程作为引路，推广深度的精致旅游，不但更能了解在地人员、餐食体验，也带动经济发展，尤其年轻人更为地方注入新活力。</p>
<p>除了让大家看见宝岛还有很多未被发掘的优秀元素外，也带大家看见很不一样、有故事的屏东。还在想着今夏该去哪里晃晃吗？不妨到屏东走走看看吧！◇</p>
