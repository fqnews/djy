<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】菠萝鸡球</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/cafc53a093412179d8a2420ba1fb37a0-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>菠萝鸡球是一道令人百吃不厌的家常菜，酸酸甜甜，开胃又下饭。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>鸡肉肉质细嫩、滋味鲜美，适合多种烹调方法。鸡肉不但适于热炒、炖汤，而且也是适合冷食凉拌的肉类。</p>
<p>鸡肉蛋白质含量较高，脂肪含量较低，而且鸡肉蛋白质中富含人体必需的氨基酸，其含量与蛋乳中的氨基酸谱式极为相似，因此为优质蛋白质的来源。鸡肉也是磷、铁、铜和锌的良好来源，并且富含丰富的维生素B12、维生素B6、维生素A、维生素D和维生素K等。</p>
<p>菠萝是夏季人们喜爱的水果之一，不仅可生吃，还可入菜，菠萝与肉一起烹煮，可以使肉类变得香滑软嫩。</p>
<p>菠萝含丰富的营养物质，包括膳食纤维、有机酸、维生素B1、维生素C、类胡萝卜素、菠萝蛋白酶、钾及多种矿物质等，当中维生素C的含量是苹果的5倍。</p>
<p>菠萝生吃可解暑助消化，也可以捣为酱食用。在菠萝的果汁中，含有一种跟胃液相类似的酵素，可以分解蛋白、帮助消化。因此，美味的菠萝不仅可以减肥，而且对身体健康有着不同的功效。</p>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8F%A0%E8%90%9D%E9%B8%A1%E7%90%83.md#1">菠萝鸡球</a>是一道令人百吃不厌的家常菜，酸酸甜甜，开胃又下饭。罗厨选用的是鸡腿肉，所以口感更加嫩滑。菠萝和鸡肉炒好后，放在菠萝船里，非常好看，人见人爱！</p>
<h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8F%A0%E8%90%9D%E9%B8%A1%E7%90%83.md#1">菠萝鸡球</a></h2>
<p>主料：熟菠萝1个、去骨鸡腿1只<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/35f37d78405de6bb3a9d33d911c2877a.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261749" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/35f37d78405de6bb3a9d33d911c2877a-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/6a8761c6a9059ffd5298b717defcdc2d.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261750" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/6a8761c6a9059ffd5298b717defcdc2d-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>配料：尖椒、红葱、姜</p>
<p>调料：生抽、老抽、盐、糖、蛋清、香油、生粉</p>
<p>1.?将鸡大腿去骨切成厚片。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-28.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261742" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-28-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2.?用生抽、盐、糖、蛋清腌制鸡肉半小时待用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2-20.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261744" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/2-20-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>3.?把熟菠萝取出，切件，形成菠萝船状容器。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/3-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261751" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/3-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>4.?用热水将菠萝船容器烫出香味。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/4-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261752" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/4-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>5.?猛火阴油将鸡球煎香至熟，放入调好芡汁爆香后捞出。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/5-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261753" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/5-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>6.?利用猛火余油，把菠萝快速炒香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/6-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/6-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>7.?放入炒好的鸡球。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/7-3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261755" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/7-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>8.?放入尖椒件、姜片和红葱件。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261756" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>9.?放入3汤匙清水，然后加盖焗30秒。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/9-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261757" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/9-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>10.?加盐、红葱和生粉水，猛火收汁炒香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/10-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261759" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/10-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>11.?最后加老抽和油炒香，起锅盛盘。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/11-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12261762" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/11-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
