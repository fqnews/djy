<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>不同血型个性挑礼物 打造帅爸装备</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/8204d527762fc621cc6cd7c00b53c29c-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>温昇豪代言刮胡刀，自豪是家庭强壮靠山。（飞利浦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年07月27日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）无论是身为父亲犒赏自己，或是作为太太与孩子，想要表达心意，可从<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A1%80%E5%9E%8B.md#1">血型</a>窥探不同的个性，挑选最适合的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%A4%BC%E7%89%A9.md#1">礼物</a>。</p>
<figure id="12285723" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/e9285c1ae3ddb68193fbc38d3d5db833-450x338.jpg" alt="宥胜一家露营，太太与小孩送德国百灵刮胡刀。" width="500" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宥胜一家露营，太太与小孩送德国百灵刮胡刀。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_12285720" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/0a7334dbce6020c7bb476d3691614f5a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12285720" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/0a7334dbce6020c7bb476d3691614f5a-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蓝色西装是绝对不会出错的经典色系，易于配搭亦适合任何场合。（BOSS提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>O：乐观且热情奔放，重视生活享受，适合有个人特色又不失实用的礼物，例如太阳眼镜、咖啡机，都能满足老爸的虚荣心，来顿大餐祭五脏庙也很好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12285729" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/a3cf766cec7dd125974fca72a1f580b8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12285729" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/a3cf766cec7dd125974fca72a1f580b8-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Nespresso咖啡机适合享受生活的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%88%B8%E7%88%B8.md#1">爸爸</a>。　（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
