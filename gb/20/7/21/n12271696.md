<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【蔬食烘焙】家人也爱不释手的柠檬司康</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200804_shouhui_lemon_cake_02-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>柠檬司康。（如何出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>因为我喜欢酸酸甜甜的滋味，所以一下子就迷上<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9F%A0%E6%AA%AC.md#1">柠檬</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B8%E5%BA%B7.md#1">司康</a>的味道。用全麦面粉制作的司康味道十分清淡，再加上柠檬的清爽口感与香气，让家人也对这款司康爱不释手，这样的反应也使我非常满意，让我以这款柠檬司康为傲。</p>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">食材－6个份（直径6公分）</span></strong></h2>
<p>全麦面粉 170 克，泡打粉 2 克，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9F%A0%E6%AA%AC.md#1">柠檬</a>皮 40 克，柠檬汁 10 克，</p>
<p>植物油、糖浆各 20 克，豆浆 80 克，盐 2 克</p>
<p>装饰用豆浆跟手粉（全麦面粉）适量，装饰用柠檬切片 6 片（约 1 颗柠檬）</p>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">事前准备</span></strong></h2>
<p>柠檬洗净后擦干，再用削皮刀把柠檬皮削成细丝，接着用柠檬果肉挤出柠檬汁。</p>
<p>装饰用的柠檬切开前要先洗净、擦干。</p>
<p>面团放入烤箱前 20 分钟先用 180 度 C 预热。</p>
<p>先将<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%83%98%E7%84%99.md#1">烘焙</a>纸或烤盘布铺在烤盘上。</p>
<p>先把要涂抹在面团上的豆浆及硅胶刷准备好。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">制作面团</span></h2>
<p>1-1 全麦面粉与泡打粉分别用细网眼的筛子筛入调理盆中，加入植物油后用手将面粉抓松。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12272384" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12272384" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_02-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1-2 把柠檬皮、柠檬汁、豆浆、糖浆、盐倒入步骤 1-1 的调理盆中，以刮刀轻轻搅拌至完全看不见粉末颗粒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12272386" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12272386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_03-450x337.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1-3 用手将步骤 1-2 的面团揉成团。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">醒面</span></h2>
<p>2-1 在工作桌上洒一点手粉，将步骤 1-3 的面团放在桌上，并以双手压扁。</p>
<p>2-2 将面团放入容器，盖上盖子放入冰箱休息 30 分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12272399" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12272399" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_04-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">塑形</span></h3>
<p>3-1 把面团从冰箱里拿出来，放在工作桌上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12272402" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12272402" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_05-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3-2用饼干模把步骤3-1的面团切出6等分并放到烤盘上，每一块之间留固定的间隔。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12272403" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12272403" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_06-450x296.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="296" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3-3 用刷子在面团表面刷上薄薄一层豆浆。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12272404" style="width: 386px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_07.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12272404" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_07.jpg" alt="" width="386" b="259" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3-4 在每一个面团上放一片柠檬切片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12272405" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_08.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12272405" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_08-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">烘烤</span></strong></h2>
<p>4-1 将步骤 3-4 的面团放入以 180 度 C 预热好的烤箱中烤 20 至 25 分钟，烤好后取出、放到冷却网上散热。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12272409" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_09.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12272409" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_09-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12272410" style="width: 386px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_10.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12272410" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/20200721_sally_food_10.jpg" alt="" width="386" b="259" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（如何出版社提供）</figcaption></figure>
