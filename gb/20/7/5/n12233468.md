<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年07月05日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）7月4日，数十位热心<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%BA%BB%E5%B7%9E.md#1">麻州</a>居民来到波士顿的旧州府大楼(Old State House)庆祝美国独立日。他们回顾开国先贤事迹，谴责当今极左势力破坏美国价值的行为，并且朗诵《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%AE%A3%E8%A8%80.md#1">独立宣言</a>》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12233512" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-2July4-e1593924657302.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12233512" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-2July4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">先贤们曾用火鸡毛笔签署《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8B%AC%E7%AB%8B%E5%AE%A3%E8%A8%80.md#1">独立宣言</a>》和美国宪法。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12233514" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-3July4-e1593924691347.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12233514" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-3July4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“Camp Constitution”夏令营的美国宪法讲师Richard Howell朗诵《独立宣言》。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>过去，波士顿每年都会在7月4日举办盛大的游行。在市长和其他政要的带领下，礼枪队、乐队和民众从市政府大楼出发，来到Granary墓园向先贤致敬，而后游行到旧州府大楼朗读《独立宣言》。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12233515" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-4July4-e1593924725584.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12233515" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-4July4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%BA%BB%E5%B7%9E.md#1">麻州</a>民众自发庆祝独立日。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12233517" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-5July4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12233517" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-5July4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">庆典组织者合影。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12233519" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-6July4-e1593924799143.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12233519" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-6July4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市政府大楼挂起巨大国旗，庆祝独立日。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12233521" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-7July4-e1593924827751.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12233521" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-7July4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一名路人向《独立宣言》致敬。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于中共病毒疫情，波士顿今年没有举办独立日大游行。但在7月4日上午11点，仍有数十位爱国民众自行组织来到旧州府大楼庆祝。组织者称，不希望看到2020年成为第一个没有独立日庆典的年份。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12233522" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-8July4-e1593924861146.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12233522" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/07/1-1-8July4-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">麻州联邦众议员参选人Rayla Campbell致敬美国。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我要向美国说生日快乐！”身穿国旗裙装的麻州联邦众议员参选人坎贝尔(Rayla Campbell)说。</p>
<p>在旧州府大楼的金色时钟和白色阳台下，“Camp Constitution”夏令营的美国宪法讲师豪尔(Richard Howell)抑扬顿挫地朗诵《独立宣言》。还有与会者举牌支持川普总统连任，以及反对共产主义，支持敬信神明的传统理念。◇</p>
