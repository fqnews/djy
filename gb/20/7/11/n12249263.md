<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：人若鸟仔</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/bird-2686359_1280-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>鸟儿们依自己的作息，安居于广阔的天地。(Pixabay)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>人若鸟仔，有时飞高有时飞低，或者搬东或者搬西，这阵在这明日可能已经在天涯。</p>
<p>人若鸟仔，拢总嘛要歇困。不管是都市的水泥丛林、庄下的草茅或是有巢氏筑的巢，拢总嘛是人歇困的巢；最后的巢，显然是四支钉仔钉落去的……</p>
<p>人为财死，鸟为食亡。人若鸟仔，每日剌剌撞，大粒小粒汗涔涔流，只是为了顾性命。</p>
<p>不过，人和鸟仔有一个真大的不同，彼就是：</p>
<p>人要活得有意义。</p>
<p><strong>Human likes The Bird </strong>&emsp; Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>Human likes the bird, sometimes soars either high or low, sometimes moves either east or west, this time be here and may be in the ends of the earth tomorrow.</p>
<p>Human likes the bird, all will takes a rest. In spite of the cement forest in the city, the thatched cottage in the countryside or the nest built by Youchao, all are the nests for human to rest; and the last nest apparently is the place which nailed by four nails…</p>
<p>Human struggles to death for property, bird struggles to death for food. Human like bird, rushes everyday, streaming with sweats either big or small, just for life.</p>
<p>However, there is a most difference between human and bird, it’s:</p>
<p>We human must live meaningful.@</p>
