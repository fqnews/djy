  <figure id="attachment_12502817" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_794860645.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502817" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_794860645-450x300.jpg" alt="鲭鱼" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%AD%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲭鱼</a>营养成分高，价格不贵，是很多家庭喜欢的食材。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B3%B0%E5%BC%8F.md#1">泰式</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%AD%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲭鱼</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%8B%92.md#1">罗勒</a>饭（2人份）</span></h2>  <figure id="attachment_12502754" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/eb64fc0685d100de92e123d33595a812.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502754" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/eb64fc0685d100de92e123d33595a812-450x567.jpg" alt="泰式" width="450" b="567" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">泰式鲭鱼<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%8B%92.md#1">罗勒</a>饭，鲭鱼取代猪肉的打抛鱼料理，风味独特。（图／大是文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>材料</strong></span></h3>  <p>鲭鱼的鱼肉片：1片（100公克）<br />  彩椒（红椒、黄椒）：各1／2个<br />  洋葱：1／2个<br />  沙拉油：2大匙<br />  罗勒叶（切末）：5片<br />  热饭：2碗份<br />  荷包蛋：2颗蛋</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12502820" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1721163883.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1721163883-450x300.jpg" alt="彩椒" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">彩椒有各种颜色，红、黄、橘、绿等颜色；图为红椒和黄椒。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12502816" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_554601856.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_554601856-450x300.jpg" alt="罗勒叶" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">罗勒叶不是九层塔，类似茴香的气味，多用于南亚料理。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">A</span></h3>  <p>蒜（切末）：1小匙<br />  生姜（切末）：1小匙<br />  红辣椒（去籽切圆片）：1根</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">B</span></h3>  <p>鱼露：2小匙<br />  蚝油：1大匙</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法</span></h3>  <p>1. 鲭鱼、彩椒、洋葱切成1公分大小。<br />  2. 沙拉油入锅加热炒A，香气出来后放入1炒软后，加入B和罗勒叶快速翻炒调味。<br />  3. 将2放入已盛好饭的盘子里，依个人喜好放入荷包蛋。</p>
  <p><strong>用这些鱼也可以！鲣鱼、生鲑鱼、剑旗鱼、大西洋鲑。</strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_12502819" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1631991265.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502819" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1631991265-450x298.jpg" alt="剑旗鱼" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">剑旗鱼是一种大型鱼类，多用于煮汤或是制作成罐头食品。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">涮<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E8%82%89.md#1">牛肉</a>沙拉</span><span style="color: #008000;">（2人份）</span></h2>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <p>牛肉（切薄片）：100公克<br />  豆瓣菜（和兰水芹）：1束<br />  香菜：1／2束</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12502815" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_343406270.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12502815" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_343406270-450x298.jpg" alt="豆瓣菜" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">豆瓣菜是一种西洋菜，料理方式炒食、川烫、煮汤。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">A</span></h3>  <p>鱼露：2小匙<br />  橄榄油：2大匙</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">做法</span></h3>  <p>1. 在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B3%E5%BA%95%E9%94%85.md#1">平底锅</a>里烧一锅热水，把牛肉放入热水中汆烫30秒捞起，放入滤勺。<br />  2. 豆瓣菜和香菜切成易入口的大小。<br />  3. 将1盛盘放上2，淋上混合均匀的A。</p>
