  <p>疫情蔓延全球，旅游业首当其冲。没有了国际游客，国内游客也骤减，有人说：“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%90%BD%E5%9F%BA%E5%B1%B1.md#1">落基山</a>又重新回到了亚省人的手中。”</p>
  <p>如果你问我落基山最美的景观在哪里，我会告诉你：<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%81%E5%B3%B0%E5%B1%B1%EF%BC%88ten-peaks%EF%BC%89.md#1">十峰山（Ten Peaks）</a>、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%A6%E8%8E%B2%E6%B9%96%EF%BC%88moraine-lake%EF%BC%89.md#1">梦莲湖（Moraine Lake）</a>当属第一。而<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%85%B0%E7%B1%8D%E6%B9%96%EF%BC%88consolidation-lakes%EF%BC%89.md#1">慰籍湖（Consolidation Lakes）</a>就在梦莲湖的旁边。</p>
  <h3>入山 步道</h3>  <p>尽管去梦莲湖的山路依然限行，但每隔20分钟或半个小时，路口的工作人员会根据出来的车辆多少，开放几分钟让等待的车辆进去。通常下午5点以后，路障就会被撤掉，那时就畅行无阻了。</p>
  <p>早上一直在下雨，到梦莲湖停车场时天开始放晴。阳光下的梦莲湖映出醉人的翡翠色，十峰山如花瓣镶嵌四周。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12509890" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200909-SUNNY-Moraine-Lake-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12509890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200909-SUNNY-Moraine-Lake-02-600x436.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="436" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">梦莲湖景色第一。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>从碎石堆背面拾级而上，行至一半时看到左侧有一条向下的步道，这是去慰籍湖的起始点。下行数十米，经过一些散落的大大小小的砾石。这些砾石源于一次山体滑坡，其中一部分堆积成一个俯瞰梦莲湖的最佳观赏点。</p>
  <p>有两家人坐在路边的砾石上野餐，顺便晒湿透的衣服。闲谈中得知他们才从慰籍湖拖家带口下来，一路大雨，淋了个透。他们很热心地告诉我们步道只有3公里，非常易走。</p>
  <p>雨后空山，正午的阳光射入松林，温润的空气蒸出郁香，渗入心肺。充沛的雨水让植物恣意生长，到处野花繁盛，绿意欣然。</p>
  <p>山路果然易行，但因为经常有熊出没，所以游人还是要非常小心。顺流而上，及至流水渐缓，山谷豁然开朗，慰籍湖便现于眼前。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12509893" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200723-SUNNY-Consolidation-Lakes-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12509893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200723-SUNNY-Consolidation-Lakes-03-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">慰籍湖清澈见底。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12509896" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200723-SUNNY-Consolidation-Lakes-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12509896" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200723-SUNNY-Consolidation-Lakes-04-600x423.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">慰籍湖清澈见底。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12509912" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200909-SUNNY-Consolidation-Lakes-05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12509912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200909-SUNNY-Consolidation-Lakes-05-600x412.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="412" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">慰籍湖清澈见底。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <h3>看湖 观冰</h3>  <p>慰籍湖实际上是两个湖，由四冰川（Quadra Glaciers）融冰形成。站在湖边，时不时还有隆隆的雪崩之声传来。</p>
  <p>湖的右侧是无数的砾石，并不难爬，站在不同高度下，更看出湖水的变幻莫测。白云从山顶飞速掠过，湖面忽明忽暗，印照着深深浅浅的凝碧湛蓝。飞瀑悬于峭壁，雪峰直入云间，慰籍湖果然慰籍人心。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12509898" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200723-SUNNY-Consolidation-Lakes-06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12509898" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200723-SUNNY-Consolidation-Lakes-06-600x379.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="379" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">慰籍湖的凝碧湛蓝。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12509915" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200723-SUNNY-Consolidation-Lakes-07.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12509915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200723-SUNNY-Consolidation-Lakes-07-600x397.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="397" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">慰籍湖的凝碧湛蓝。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>闯过砾石，继续沿湖向四冰川行进，到达上慰籍湖，在此可近距离观察冰川。千年的寒光，剔透的翠玉，怎一个美字了得？</p>
  <p>下得山来，顺道去梦莲湖，在湖中拍桨是怎样的体验？桨板运动在落基山方兴未艾，泛板在世界级无敌的景色中让人怎样陶醉？</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12509916" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200723-SUNNY-Moraine-Lake-08.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12509916 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/20200723-SUNNY-Moraine-Lake-08-450x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在梦莲湖上泛板。（Sunny/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>有道是：残冰卧雪映碧空，盈盈翡翠凝湖中。子骥遗恨不得遇，隐入十峰山万重。（注：刘子骥,晋时人。《桃花源记》载：南阳刘子骥，高尚士也，闻之，欣然规往。未果，寻病终。）</p>
