  <figure id="attachment_12444809" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/c9c16304e26f4cb2f3ef08f66211d0b2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444809 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/c9c16304e26f4cb2f3ef08f66211d0b2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">建在百年老<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A6%95%E6%A0%91.md#1">榕树</a>上的台东延平乡卡米莎度<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A0%91%E5%B1%8B.md#1">树屋</a>。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12444810" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/ebf0fbaba1b030770058beabd053ae45.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444810 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/ebf0fbaba1b030770058beabd053ae45-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">卡米莎度树屋下，荡秋千，吹山风，享原乡风味餐。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <h2>树屋观星揽月?? 部落金刚守护</h2>  <p>延平乡<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E5%86%9C%E6%97%8F.md#1">布农族</a>属内本鹿后裔，群山叠翠中藏着5个山地部落。上里部落以前是日本警察驻在所，目前只有4户人家。伊蓝?明基努安从小在这里长大，他说，“上里，从日语翻译过来，有‘上面的部落’的意思，也指‘上帝的国度’的地方。老人家称这里是很多野蜂蜜的地方。”</p>
  <p>这棵拥有百年历史的大榕树是日据时代日本人种的，不但粗壮结实，而且最特别的是气根很少，“很干净，生命力很强。”伊蓝?明基努安指出，根据老一辈传说，日本政府撤离时，曾在树下埋有黄金，口耳相传，老榕树成为传说中的黄金树。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12444808" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/51da4f66c6c23ff47e1e4333f8e49508.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444808 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/51da4f66c6c23ff47e1e4333f8e49508-600x704.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="704" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">百年老榕树的树根，综横盘错密布。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12444815" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1c71a444fecdfac1e32d59ab2266e2de.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444815 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1c71a444fecdfac1e32d59ab2266e2de-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">树屋旁露台小憩，享受树顶的静谧。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>“小时候就常想带着木板爬到树上去住。4年前，决定开始打造树屋。很多朋友说他们也有同样的梦，希望我分享出来。”于是伊蓝?明基努安将树屋盖好后，还将周边规划成可以露营的休闲园区，游客在这里烤肉、射箭、学习<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E5%86%9C%E6%97%8F.md#1">布农族</a>的八部合音，充分体验布农族的传统生活。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12444811" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/ad716422577aaeb351b4776290fbe5bc.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444811 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/ad716422577aaeb351b4776290fbe5bc-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">卡米莎度树屋园区的烤猪体验。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12444812" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/dbfb8fa22b592c87d7fcf9e5cf3c46c2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444812 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/dbfb8fa22b592c87d7fcf9e5cf3c46c2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">布农族向导带领游客体验八部合音。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>沿着树屋木梯盘旋而上，恍如走进童话世界。小小树屋可住4人，里面有冷气设施，隔窗树影婆娑，屋内观星赏月或在屋外露台上品茗，静静享受着山间的微风岁月。树屋从去年10月开幕至今，已经吸引不少游客前来探访，体验在树屋里过夜、远离尘嚣的山野滋味。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12444813" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/71c6383181c0b681d7fcc5bd1de4f698.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444813 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/71c6383181c0b681d7fcc5bd1de4f698-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">树屋内，隔窗树影婆娑，尽览日月风华。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12444814" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/d24a43414adef2eb007e8207415451d6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444814 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/d24a43414adef2eb007e8207415451d6-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">树屋内上下铺，可容纳4人。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>榕树下，盘错密布的榕树根环抱着一个大石头，生物化石专家曾来现场探勘，认为这个大石头不是一般石头，可能是有千万年以上历史的木化石。伊蓝?明基努安说，“因为木头久了会烂掉，所以我们研判它是被岩浆喷到，有可能是几千万年以前，板块从海底挤上来时造成的。”</p>
  <p>由于岩浆喷发的时候，同时也会喷到其他石头上，所以附近还有好几个大小不一的木化石。触摸时，许多人都明显感觉大石头十分清凉，直透手心。伊蓝?明基努安笑着说，“很奇妙，里面一定有它的意义，很多人说它会带来好运。”</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12444816" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/c18de86a225c1f57de118c2c29d00737.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444816 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/c18de86a225c1f57de118c2c29d00737-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">树屋主人伊蓝?明基努安指榕树下的木化石可能已有千万年历史。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12444806" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/0b9ce55c205ce5f6528f40509d3a5a61.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444806 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/0b9ce55c205ce5f6528f40509d3a5a61-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">从树屋园区远眺，上里部落入口处，仿佛一个大金刚伫立守护。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>园区深处，还有一个很大的苏铁林区，“苏铁是属于恐龙时代的植物，其他县市没有，这边的空气、磁场、水跟其他地方不一样，都跟苏铁林区息息相关。”伊蓝?明基努安描述电影中金刚大战恐龙的场景，可能史前也曾在这里发生。从园区远眺，部落口的山头就像金刚屹立，似乎正在守护着这块土地。</p>
  <h2>百年<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%83%E9%87%8C%E9%A6%99.md#1">七里香</a>原始林 每株编号列国宝</h2>  <p>10年前，伊蓝?明基努安当桃源村长时，在上里部落发现一大片罕见的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%83%E9%87%8C%E9%A6%99.md#1">七里香</a>原始林，“总共有6百棵左右，其中300多棵树龄达百年以上，都是土生土长的。”这些七里香形态各异，苍劲有力，最大的有两层楼高。</p>
  <p>七里香原始林属国有地，伊蓝?明基努安指出，每棵树都有上百万的价值。后来，延平乡公所特别清查并着手保护这片七里香原始林，为每颗树编上号码，同时建造了七里香步道，供游客沿途观赏。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12444807" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/e9325b39be4bd738f5627e6cfe5d8a14.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444807 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/e9325b39be4bd738f5627e6cfe5d8a14-600x424.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台东延平乡的七里香原始林，成为树屋游客顺道必探访的秘境。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12444817" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/a160864d349242d8da77e40cc863619e.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12444817 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/a160864d349242d8da77e40cc863619e-600x373.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="373" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">每株七里香都有编号。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>步道长约900公尺，随着布农族向导在步道阶梯上上下下走完一趟后，往往汗流浃背。但漫步在七里幽香间，体验原乡部落的山林野趣，心里充满对大地的尊重与珍惜，也特别能感受到大自然处处蕴藏着瑰宝与神奇。</p>
