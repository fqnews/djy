  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】</span></h2>  <p>淡<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E6%B2%B9.md#1">牛油</a> 8oz（放室温至软）<br />  面粉7oz<br />  发粉1茶匙<br />  白砂糖4oz<br />  鸡蛋4个<br />  盐半茶匙<br />  鲜奶4汤匙<br />  香草精一茶匙</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>  <p>1. 预热烤箱350℉。取一烤<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md#1">蛋糕</a>长型盆（8寸乘8寸），涂上<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E6%B2%B9.md#1">牛油</a>备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12484015" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-20.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12484015" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-20-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">预热烤箱350℉，取一烤蛋糕长型盆（8寸乘8寸），涂上牛油备用。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 面粉、发粉和盐拌匀之后，过筛备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12484016" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12484016" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-16-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">面粉、发粉和盐拌匀之后，过筛备用。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 用打蛋器将糖和牛油打至淡黄色。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12484017" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12484017" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-15-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用打蛋器将糖和牛油打至淡黄色。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4. 接着再逐一加入鸡蛋，分4次加入，打至顺滑。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12484018" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12484018" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-10-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">分4次加入鸡蛋，用搅拌器打至顺滑。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5. 加入香草精混和，接着再加入面粉，轻轻翻入蛋浆内，最后加鲜奶拌匀。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12484019" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12484019" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加入香草精混和，接着再加入面粉，轻轻翻入蛋浆内，最后加鲜奶拌匀。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>6. 将“做法5”倒入烤盘内，轻轻用手摇匀，放入预热好的烤箱内。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12484020" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/6-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12484020" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/6-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将混合均匀的面团倒入蛋糕模内。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>7. 蛋糕烤约40分钟，烤到蛋糕呈金黄色熟透为止。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12484021" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12484021" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-9-450x303.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蛋糕烤约40分钟。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>8. 取出蛋糕放凉5分钟后，趁热切开来享用。◇</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12484022" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12484022" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">取出蛋糕放凉5分钟后切开享用。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">【主厨简介】</span></h2>  <p>姓名：梁志生<br />  厨龄：40余年<br />  经历：<br />  1、美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />  2、海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />  3、作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />  4、美国富商George Bowles御用厨师</p>
