  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%89%E6%8B%8C.md#1">凉拌</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E8%9C%87.md#1">海蜇</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E4%B8%9D.md#1">鸡丝</a></span></h2>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【材料】</span></h3>  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E8%9C%87.md#1">海蜇</a>丝6oz<br />  鸡胸肉12oz<br />  苹果1个<br />  西芹1条<br />  青瓜半条<br />  红萝卜1条<br />  青葱2条</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12483970" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12483970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-18-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">准备材料有海蜇丝、鸡胸肉、苹果、西芹、青瓜、红萝卜、青葱。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【调味料】</span></h3>  <p>芝麻酱3汤匙<br />  花生酱3汤匙<br />  生抽2汤匙<br />  米醋2汤匙<br />  砂糖1汤匙<br />  盐半茶匙<br />  美极鲜酱油半茶匙<br />  热开水半杯</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">【作法】</span></h3>  <p>1. 海蜇丝剪开袋口，取出冲洗干净。如果买到的海蜇丝是用盐水浸泡的，洗完还得再用水浸泡，每30分钟换水一次，共六次，直至无咸味。</p>
  <p>2. 准备一盆冰水，另外再烧一锅热水，水滚后关火，把海蜇丝放入后立即捞出，放到冰水中浸泡，这样能避免海蜇丝过韧或者不够爽脆。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12483971" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12483971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-14-450x311.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="311" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烧一锅热水，水滚后关火，把海蜇丝放入后立即捞出，放到冰水中浸泡。（图／<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 海蜇丝浸泡到变冰后捞起沥干备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12483972" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12483972" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-13-450x338.jpg" alt="海蜇丝" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">海蜇丝浸泡到变冰后捞起沥干。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4. 芝麻酱和花生酱先用少许油拌开。另外在一个大碗中放入生抽、米醋、糖、盐和美极酱油，以开水搅拌均匀之后，再加入芝麻酱搅拌均匀，便可备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12483973" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12483973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-8-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">混合调味酱料。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5. 青瓜、红萝卜、西芹切丝备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12483974" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12483974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-9-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">青瓜、红萝卜、西芹切丝。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>6. 苹果洗净切片后，再切成丝备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12483975" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/6-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12483975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/6-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">苹果洗净切片后，再切成丝。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>7. 鸡胸肉连同卤水包和姜葱，放进一锅水中浸熟，大约13分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12483978" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12483978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-7-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸡胸肉连同卤水包和姜葱，放进一锅水中浸熟。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>8. 煮好的鸡胸肉放凉后切丝备用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12483979" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12483979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">煮好的鸡胸肉放凉后切丝。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>9. 最后将所有的材料装盘，把各类蔬果和海蜇丝排放在旁边，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B8%A1%E4%B8%9D.md#1">鸡丝</a>放在碟中间， 淋上芝麻酱汁，放上葱丝装饰，便可食用。◇</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12483980" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12483980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">最后摆盘让凉拌菜更美味。（图／梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">【主厨简介】</span></h3>  <p>姓名：梁志生<br />  厨龄：40余年<br />  经历：<br />  1、美心饮食集团日本首间中菜旗舰店副主厨<br />  2、海城酒楼（Ocean City）主厨<br />  3、作为特邀嘉宾参与著名烹饪节目主持人甄文达的《Yan Can Cook》<br />  4、美国富商George Bowles御用厨师</p>
