  <p>罗厨制作的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B2%A9%E9%B1%BC.md#1">鲩鱼</a>系列菜肴包括鱼肉酿秋葵、姜葱水浸鲩鱼、红烧划水、宽粉炖鱼头和<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A4%92%E7%9B%90%E9%B1%BC%E9%AA%A8.md#1">椒盐鱼骨</a>等。鱼肉酿秋葵和姜葱水浸鲩鱼是选用剔骨的鱼肉烹制，剩下的鱼头可以用来做宽粉炖鱼头，鱼骨则可以制作椒盐鱼骨。</p>
  <p>日本人喜欢吃鱼，不光是吃鱼肉，几乎鱼的所有部位都吃，就连鱼刺和鱼骨也都会吃得干干净净，这并不是因为他们自古以来就有吃鱼骨头的习惯，日本很多营养学家对此做过充分的研究，认为经常吃鱼骨头对身体是非常有好处的。</p>
  <p>鱼骨里含有丰富的钙质和微量元素，经常吃可以防止骨质疏松，对于处于生长期的青少年和骨骼开始衰老的中老年人来讲，都非常有益处。 而且，经过适当软化处理的鱼骨，营养成分都成为水溶性物质，很容易被人体吸收。 所以，多吃鱼骨对身体非常有益。</p>
  <p>很多人不吃鱼骨是因为不会做，做鱼骨有几种常见的做法。可以把鱼骨晒干、碾碎，和肉馅一起做成炸丸子。另外，炖鱼的时候可以用高压锅，里面多放一点醋，这样鱼骨和鱼刺会软化，可以直接食用。</p>
  <p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A4%92%E7%9B%90%E9%B1%BC%E9%AA%A8.md#1">椒盐鱼骨</a>，将鱼骨完全炸酥炸脆，鱼味纯正、甘香可口，外酥内嫩，骨香浓郁。</p>
  <p>罗厨强调，制作这道椒盐鱼骨，要反复炸鱼骨三次，这样炸出的鱼骨酥脆，而且油分非常少，男女老少都可健康享用。</p>
  <h2>椒盐鱼骨</h2>  <p>主料：鲩鱼脊骨排一条<br />  配料：葱花、柠檬、香菜<br />  调料：椒盐、黑胡椒、生粉、泡打粉、蛋清、油</p>
  <p><strong>做法：</strong><br />  1. 鲩鱼起骨切段。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-30.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12509078" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1-30-450x294.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="294" /></a><br />  2. 用椒盐和黑胡椒腌制鱼骨。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-24.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12509080" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2-24-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  3. 添加葱花后将鱼骨腌渍30分钟。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-20.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12509082" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3-20-450x287.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="287" /></a><br />  4. 用生粉、泡打粉、蛋清制作脆浆。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-15.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12509083" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4-15-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  5. 将腌制好的鱼骨放入脆浆中搅拌上浆。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12509084" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5-14-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  6. 将柠檬切成小块摆盘备用。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/6-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12509086" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/6-10-450x277.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="277" /></a><br />  7. 用180度油温将鱼段浸炸至熟。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12509087" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  8. 炸熟鱼段捞起后淋干油分。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12509088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  9. 再次炸鱼段，反复三次。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12509089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />  10. 淋干油分摆盘。</p>
  <p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/10-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12509090" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/10-12-450x261.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="261" /></a><br />  11. 最后放入香菜点缀。<br />  <ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/11-19.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12509091" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/11-19-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
