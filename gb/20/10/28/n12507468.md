  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8E%89%E5%AD%90%E7%83%A7.md#1">玉子烧</a>除了基本款的高汤口味，还有蔬菜玉子烧，营养多元，只要注意烹饪时蛋心是否熟透。玉子烧的“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E6%B6%B2.md#1">蛋液</a>比例”也是重要秘诀，日月累积经验摸索出的黄金比例，让<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%B8%B8%E8%8F%9C.md#1">家常菜</a>有高级食堂的等级，松软滑顺、层层润厚、没有粗糙气孔、无破裂的蛋皮堆叠，犹豫艺术品般的食物，让人难忘。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">锦丝蛋（冷藏保存4 天）</span></h2>  <figure id="attachment_12510207" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/edf54aad10f2ef7818fa923290ad54c0.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12510207 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/edf54aad10f2ef7818fa923290ad54c0-450x465.jpg" alt="锦丝蛋" width="450" b="465" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">锦丝蛋口感温润顺滑，料理过程时间、温度、油量都需要注意，是不折不口的纯朴却细致的料理。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <p>蛋 2 颗<br />  盐 1 小撮<br />  糖、酒 各1 小匙<br />  油 适量</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>  <p>1. 蛋打散，加盐、糖、酒，充分打匀。用滤网过筛<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E6%B6%B2.md#1">蛋液</a>，煎出更细腻光滑的质感。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12510208" style="width: 520px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/a9e3ed649128c91a040dd0ec049a413b.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12510208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/a9e3ed649128c91a040dd0ec049a413b.jpg" alt="锦丝蛋" width="520" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用滤网过筛蛋液，煎出更细腻光滑的质感。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 以中火热平底锅，倒入油，使锅底均匀覆上油膜一层就好，多余的油以厨纸巾拭除（煎出的蛋才<br />  会光滑细致）。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12510209" style="width: 520px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5e593cb7b1383bde8b8c9c89e8d33dc2.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12510209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/5e593cb7b1383bde8b8c9c89e8d33dc2.jpg" alt="锦丝蛋" width="520" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">以中火热平底锅，倒入油，使锅底均匀覆上油膜一层就好，多余的油以厨纸巾拭除。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 倒入蛋液，摇一下锅子，使蛋液均匀扩张，呈现凝固状态立即熄火，盖上锅盖，把锅子移到湿抹<br />  布上，持续焖1 ～ 2 分钟。等蛋完全凝固，掀盖用手触按一下，表面有弹性即可盛起，切丝。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12510210" style="width: 520px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/36c49f35ce61fe44ee0a1921f3b0efe3.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12510210" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/36c49f35ce61fe44ee0a1921f3b0efe3.jpg" alt="锦丝蛋" width="520" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">等蛋完全凝固，掀盖用手触按一下，表面有弹性即可盛起，切丝。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">高丽菜玉子烧（冷藏保存5 天）</span></h2>  <figure id="attachment_12512357" style="width: 518px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/b6879f1c87757a2367dc3245d7d321b5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12512357" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/b6879f1c87757a2367dc3245d7d321b5.jpg" alt="玉子烧" width="518" b="536" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">等蛋皮包裹的高丽菜放凉，才能依所需切成数等分。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">材料</span></h3>  <p>高丽菜 3 片（约120g）<br />  蛋 3 颗<br />  油 1 大匙<br />  粗盐、糖 各1/2 小匙<br />  麻油 1 小匙</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>  <p>1. 高丽菜切丝。<br />  2. 把高丽菜、蛋、粗盐、糖和麻油全放入大钵中，打匀。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12512359" style="width: 524px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2888007658cd4a84824cc4fca6bb9b39.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12512359" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2888007658cd4a84824cc4fca6bb9b39.jpg" alt="玉子烧" width="524" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">把高丽菜、蛋、粗盐、糖和麻油全放入大钵中，打匀。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 锅中放入油1 大匙，摇一下锅子，使锅缘锅底充分覆油，多余的油以厨纸巾吸取。<br />  4. 把蛋液一口气倒入锅里，小火慢煎，盖上锅盖焖10 分钟，等蛋香飘出，见到锅缘蛋液也凝固了，翻面。将蛋倒扣盘上，翻面再回锅煎熟。熄火，放凉，依所需切成数等分。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12512360" style="width: 520px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/c9702cdeaa966cfeffb40021468edaf2-e1604020682244.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12512360 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/c9702cdeaa966cfeffb40021468edaf2.jpg" alt="玉子烧" width="520" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">把蛋液一口气倒入锅里，小火慢煎，盖上锅盖焖10 分钟。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12512361" style="width: 518px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4421651eb1fd5ce6ad18895ee124d0d5-e1604020550375.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12512361" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/4421651eb1fd5ce6ad18895ee124d0d5.jpg" alt="玉子烧" width="518" b="312" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">见到锅缘蛋液也凝固了，翻面。将蛋倒扣盘上，翻面再回锅煎熟。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">四季豆玉子烧（冷藏保存4 天）</span></h2>  <figure id="attachment_12513911" style="width: 518px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/08de2a764ca6f1ed9cb520e09ebc6f2d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12513911" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/08de2a764ca6f1ed9cb520e09ebc6f2d.jpg" alt="四季豆玉子烧" width="518" b="536" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">四季豆玉子烧。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3>材料</h3>  <p>四季豆 4 ～ 5 根<br />  蛋 2 颗<br />  油 1 大匙<br />  材料<br />  盐、糖 各1/4 小匙<br />  淡口酱油 数滴</p>
  <h3>作法</h3>  <p>1. 四季豆放入加了少许盐的沸水中汆烫，烫熟捞起，依玉子烧锅的宽度把四季豆切齐（四季豆预先煮好，浸泡高汤一晚尤佳）。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12513912" style="width: 520px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9bc068f09ab8f44073454503b4a505e6.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12513912" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9bc068f09ab8f44073454503b4a505e6.jpg" alt="四季豆" width="520" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">四季豆预先煮好，浸泡高汤一晚。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 蛋打散，以盐、糖、淡口酱油调味。<br />  3. 中火热玉子烧锅，下油1 大匙，让锅子四周充分走油，以厨纸巾吸取多余油分。<br />  4. 蛋液分三次注入。先倒1/3，摇一下锅子，使之分布均匀，四季豆摆在锅子的1/3处（如下图），边煎边卷下来。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12513914" style="width: 524px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3df338d8a54e62419b11de12510c68c6.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12513914" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/3df338d8a54e62419b11de12510c68c6.jpg" alt="四季豆" width="524" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">四季豆摆在锅子的1/3处。（图文／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5. 煎好的蛋往上推，再注入1/3 蛋液，布匀锅底，等蛋液凝固再卷起来。如此重复一遍。若油分不够，注蛋液之前请补油，用刚才拭油的纸巾涂一下，便能煎得更顺手。</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">海带芽玉子烧（冷藏保存4 天）</span></h2>  <h3>材料</h3>  <p>蛋 2 颗<br />  干海带芽 2 小匙<br />  乳酪片 1 片<br />  高汤 2 大匙<br />  盐 2 小撮<br />  油 1 大匙</p>
  <h3>作法</h3>  <p>1. 干海带芽用水泡开，约5 分钟后取出拧干。乳酪片切丁。<br />  2. 钵里放入海带芽、乳酪、高汤、盐和蛋，充分打匀。<br />  3. 热玉子烧锅，下油1 大匙，让锅子充分走油，以厨纸巾吸取多余油分。<br />  4. 蛋液分三次注入。先倒1/3，摇一下锅子，使之均匀分布，边煎边卷。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12513916" style="width: 520px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/22dd07d709487f081a8c975840ab3dfa.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12513916" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/22dd07d709487f081a8c975840ab3dfa.jpg" alt="玉子烧" width="520" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">先倒蛋液1/3，让其在锅内均匀分布，边煎边卷。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12513918" style="width: 520px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/e076aca4bb7f34043295cb9c06c8be73.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12513918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/e076aca4bb7f34043295cb9c06c8be73.jpg" alt="玉子烧" width="520" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蛋液均匀分布后，边煎边卷。（图／商周出版提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5. 煎好的蛋往上推，再倒入1/3 蛋液，布匀锅底，等蛋液凝固再卷起。如此重复一遍。若油分不够，注蛋液之前再补油，用刚才拭油纸巾涂一下，便能煎得顺手。</p>
