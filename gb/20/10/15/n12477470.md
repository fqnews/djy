  <figure id="attachment_12477473" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464284.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477473" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464284-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">吊放板车。（林铁及文资处提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>百年前制造的<ahref="/gb/tag/21%E5%8F%B7%E8%92%B8%E6%B1%BD%E7%81%AB%E8%BD%A6.md#1">21号蒸汽火车</a>，停用后于嘉义公园展示40年，108年移回林铁及文资处，车体多已锈蚀损坏，为修复原有功能及外观，将启动整修计划。因限于现阶段自有维修技术人力断层及汰换零件需委外制作加工，15日由嘉义车库园区启程，林铁及文资处将派维修人员驻厂共同进行18个月维修作业，要让21号蒸汽火车头重新回到阿里山铁路运行，再现风华。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12477474" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464285.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477474" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464285-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">吊运前置作业。（林铁及文资处提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>为建立核心维修技术，将解构所有零件，重新绘制零件标准尺寸图，以建立资料库，管控零件组装精确性，确保机件功能正常及行车安全；并派遣技术人员全程参与整修工作，学习维修核心技能并编撰维修手册，以传承技术，培养更多自有维修人力。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12477475" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464286.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477475" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464286-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">吊放板车。（林铁及文资处提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>未来所有修复计划将进行影像纪录留存，并保存重要零组件旧品文物。另一方面邀请台铁局、台糖公司、罗东及花莲林管处等国内蒸汽火车管理单位，藉由共同参与21号蒸汽火车修复活动，共享技术、彼此交流经验，深化国内技术层面，以扩大发挥整修效益。◇</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12477476" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464287.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477476" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464287-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">21号蒸汽火车现况。（林铁及文资处提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12477477" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464288.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477477" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/464288-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">吊运前置作业。（林铁及文资处提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>责任编辑：李悦</p>
