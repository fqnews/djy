  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">必要材料</span></h3>  <p>厚片吐司1个<br />  <ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">奶油</a> 2?汤匙<br />  蜂蜜 2?汤匙<br />  糖1／2杯</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">选择性材料</span></h3>  <p>糖粉 0.5 汤匙<br />  肉桂粉少许</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">步骤</span></h3>  <p>1.把吐司切成厚片。<br />  (把2到3片普通土司叠在一起也OK！也可买整条吐司，请店家切取需要的大小。)</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12477831" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/b5b44126f52fdcfff336cf8d01f6d58f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/b5b44126f52fdcfff336cf8d01f6d58f-450x299.jpg" alt="采实文化,蜜糖吐司" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">取一个厚片土司，也可以将很多片薄片吐司叠在一起。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2.<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E6%B2%B9.md#1">奶油</a>放在室温下融化。在距离吐司边缘1cm的地方用刀子划X，在隙缝处挤入蜂蜜。<br />  (使用装在压挤式容器里的蜂蜜较方便！)</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12477832" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/878b56b527164c0388b585c29bc2a53d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477832" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/878b56b527164c0388b585c29bc2a53d-450x299.jpg" alt="采实文化,蜜糖吐司" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">奶油放在室温下融化。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12477833" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/121a92fee505fcbdbe64dc4b9fc8e0f9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/121a92fee505fcbdbe64dc4b9fc8e0f9-450x299.jpg" alt="采实文化,蜜糖吐司" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在距离吐司边缘1cm的地方用刀子划X，在隙缝处挤入蜂蜜。奶油放在室温下融化。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3.在吐司表面涂上奶油，最后再涂一层糖。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12477834" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/ca6e23aec1ea460c16ea22278fc8b3d3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12477834" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/ca6e23aec1ea460c16ea22278fc8b3d3-450x299.jpg" alt="采实文化,蜜糖吐司" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在吐司表面涂上奶油，最后再涂一层糖。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4.烤箱以200℃预热，放入吐司烤5～10分，最后洒上糖粉和肉桂粉。<br />  （可以用水果或鲜奶油装饰，美味令人难以抗拒！）</p>
