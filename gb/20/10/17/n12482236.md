  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">必要材料（2人份）</span></h2>  <p>切丁蔬菜 1杯<br />  芝麻油 1汤匙<br />  白饭 1碗<br />  烤<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E8%8B%94.md#1">海苔</a> 1/2张</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">调味酱</span></h2>  <p>酱油 2汤匙<br />  芝麻油 1汤匙<br />  捣碎的芝麻粒 少许</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">步骤</span></h2>  <p>1. 将<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C.md#1">南瓜</a>、马铃薯、洋葱、红萝卜等蔬菜切成小丁。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/afce37a2082233b769805bc2bfa4dd23.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/afce37a2082233b769805bc2bfa4dd23-450x338.jpg" alt="洋葱" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%97%E7%93%9C.md#1">南瓜</a>、马铃薯、洋葱、红萝卜等蔬菜切成小丁。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 在锅内倒入芝麻油后，以中火翻炒。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482258" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/846d8c0be1a7b9046554b92d60aa41b8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482258" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/846d8c0be1a7b9046554b92d60aa41b8-450x337.jpg" alt="芝麻油" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在锅内倒入芝麻油后，以中火翻炒综合蔬菜。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 加入2杯水与白饭，以中火继续煮。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482259" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/bee113b87717491c2268d4ef8c5745ce.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/bee113b87717491c2268d4ef8c5745ce-450x337.jpg" alt="粥" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加入水、白饭，以中火煮。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4. 煮至饭粒变软后，将火关掉。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482260" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/db556c86fc6a21f956fc48b45f6d7a55.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/db556c86fc6a21f956fc48b45f6d7a55-450x338.jpg" alt="粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">煮至饭粒变软后，将火关掉。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>5. 起锅后加点捏碎的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E8%8B%94.md#1">海苔</a>，配上一碟小菜。（就算只是煮白<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B2%A5.md#1">粥</a>，加调味酱跟海苔就非常好吃了！）</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482261" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/f946e7664624ae478392bd62dfd25e3c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/f946e7664624ae478392bd62dfd25e3c-450x338.jpg" alt="海苔" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">起锅后加点捏碎的海苔或酱油就很好吃！（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3>&lt;本文摘自：<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href="https://www.books.com.tw/products/0010716814?loc=P_br_r0vq68ygz_D_2aabd0_C_2" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">10分钟做早餐：一个人吃、两人吃、全家吃都充满幸福的120道早餐提案</a></span>，采实文化提供&gt;</h3>  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">· <a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/19/12/13/n11720907.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">【梁厨美食】花生眉豆火鸡骨粥 养生又暖胃</a></span><br />  <span style="color: #008000;"> · <a style="color: #008000;" href=><a href="/gb/20/8/4/n12305391.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">厚切鸡肉豆腐汉堡 早午餐最佳减脂轻食</a></span><br />  <span style="color: #008000;"> · <a style="color: #008000;" href=><a href="/gb/20/9/18/n12413613.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">【省时家常菜】帕玛森松子四季豆 咸甜清脆</a></span></h3>  <p>责任编辑：伊芙</p>
