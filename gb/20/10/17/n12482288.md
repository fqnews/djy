  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">必要材料（1人份）</span></h2>  <p>鸡<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B.md#1">蛋</a> 1颗<br />  盐巴 0.2汤匙<br />  胡椒粉 少许<br />  白饭 1/2碗</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">选择性材料</span></h2>  <p>葱花 少许</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">步骤</span></h2>  <p>1. 打一颗<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B.md#1">蛋</a>加1杯半的水打匀，以盐巴、胡椒粉调味。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482318" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9fa75f49f11920e7b4d5d24e9ff5e80f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482318" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/9fa75f49f11920e7b4d5d24e9ff5e80f-450x337.jpg" alt="蛋" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">打一颗蛋加1杯半的水打匀，以盐巴、胡椒粉调味。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 把饭、蛋液倒入小锅子。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482319" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/595f1e0b3127c1480672aea6ccb17c09.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482319" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/595f1e0b3127c1480672aea6ccb17c09-450x346.jpg" alt="蛋液" width="450" b="346" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">把饭、蛋液倒入小锅子。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3. 将小锅子放进大锅子内隔水加热，锅盖要盖上。<br />  （在小锅子下方垫毛巾，水滚后就不会有碰撞的声音。）</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482320" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/72fb35a2a410b5b2bd801931b8f5fbb7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482320" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/72fb35a2a410b5b2bd801931b8f5fbb7-450x338.jpg" alt="蛋液" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将小锅子放进大锅子内隔水加热，锅盖要盖上。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4. 水滚后放入葱花，转小火继续闷煮10分钟即完成。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482321" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2b1918646ec9d3a2db8f56e7dcd4c5b8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482321" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2b1918646ec9d3a2db8f56e7dcd4c5b8-450x337.jpg" alt="蛋液" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">水滚后放入葱花，转小火继续闷煮10分钟。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h3>&lt;本文摘自：<span style="color: #0000ff;"><a style="color: #0000ff;" href="https://www.books.com.tw/products/0010716814?loc=P_br_r0vq68ygz_D_2aabd0_C_2" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">10分钟做早餐：一个人吃、两人吃、全家吃都充满幸福的120道早餐提案</a></span>，采实文化提供&gt;</h3>  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">· <a style="color: #008000;" href=><a href="/gb/20/10/15/n12477808.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">【在家咖啡厅】手作外酥内软“奶油蜜糖吐司”</a></span><br />  <span style="color: #008000;"> · <a style="color: #008000;" href=><a href="/gb/20/10/17/n12482197.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">清爽甘甜“豆腐汤”早上迅速煮好高汤的秘诀！</a></span><br />  <span style="color: #008000;"> · <a style="color: #008000;" href=><a href="/gb/20/10/9/n12464945.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">迪士尼食谱：在家做“米奇椒盐蝴蝶饼”</a></span></h3>  <p>责任编辑：伊芙</p>
