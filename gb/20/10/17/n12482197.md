  <figure id="attachment_12482213" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/53915397316eb0b26aad827d169d0100.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482213" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/53915397316eb0b26aad827d169d0100-450x338.jpg" alt="豆腐汤" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90%E6%B1%A4.md#1">豆腐汤</a>起锅后放凉，搭配调味料就可以上桌。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">必要材料（2人份）</span></h2>  <p>蒜苗5cm<br />  小鱼干5尾<br />  豆腐1块</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">调味料</span></h2>  <p>辣椒粉 1汤匙<br />  酱油 2汤匙<br />  葱 1汤匙<br />  蒜泥 1汤匙<br />  芝麻油少许</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">步骤</span></h2>  <p>1.先调好调味料，将蒜苗斜切。（在前一晚就准备好）</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482210" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/ef28dffa17556536768ff1ed7781d49c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482210" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/ef28dffa17556536768ff1ed7781d49c-450x338.jpg" alt="酱汁,豆腐汤" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">前一晚就可以先调好酱汁；辣椒粉、酱油、葱、蒜泥、芝麻油。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12482209" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/a7c0d938125cef65c8ee7786d3209545.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/a7c0d938125cef65c8ee7786d3209545-450x337.jpg" alt="豆腐汤" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">前一晚先备妥蒜苗5公分长然后斜切。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2.锅内加2杯水和5尾小鱼干煮5分钟，水滚之后把小鱼干捞起就可以跑去睡觉。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482211" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/fa67327601807bf827d8a67b69d40fcd.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482211" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/fa67327601807bf827d8a67b69d40fcd-450x338.jpg" alt="小鱼干,高汤" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">早上不用花时间煮<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%AB%98%E6%B1%A4.md#1">高汤</a>的秘诀就是“前一晚先煮好”！只要在锅子加2杯水、5尾小鱼干煮5分钟，水滚之后把小鱼干捞起就可以跑去睡觉。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3.把豆腐、蒜苗放进高汤里，用大火滚煮2分钟。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12482212" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/f685a69bca3de6f8231fc9e863d70fac.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12482212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/f685a69bca3de6f8231fc9e863d70fac-450x337.jpg" alt="豆腐" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">早上起来，将高汤取出放入豆腐、蒜苗，用大火滚煮2分钟。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>4.起锅后稍微放凉，跟搭配的调味料一起上桌。（加入一点捏碎的海苔也不错！）</p>
