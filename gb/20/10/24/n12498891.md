  <p>宣信国小校长黄金木表示:“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A3%E4%BF%A1%E9%99%84%E5%B9%BC.md#1">宣信附幼</a>与吴凤科大合作来办理这一次代间共融活动，今天有30位祖孙一起来参加这个活动，共同跳舞，共同游戏，也特地作了一个奉茶的活动，希望藉由奉茶游戏交流这样的活动，让孩子认识<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%87%8D%E9%98%B3%E8%8A%82.md#1">重阳节</a>，甚至在家里或任何地方都能尊重长者，这是社会和学校应该让孩子学会的事情。”</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12498894" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465713.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12498894 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465713-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宣信国小校长黄金木接受联访。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12498895" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465714.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12498895 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465714-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">吴凤科大幼保系助理教授黄珮书表示，11/7在中央广场还有一场活动式的成果展，会邀请制作陀螺的师傅上台与市长一起打陀螺，希望大家也能来共襄盛举。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>吴凤科大幼保系助理教授黄珮书表示，办理这个活动，让我们看到阿公阿嬷的神情不一样，小朋友会发现阿公阿嬷不是老坐在家里，或只会操持家务的，因为这个活动，他们与孙子有更多的交流，更多的互动或更多的话题，这是非常有意义，有价值的活动。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12498896" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465715.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12498896 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465715-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">破冰-欢喜恰恰恰，来参与的30位祖孙共舞。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>活动开场，祖孙一起舞动“欢喜恰恰恰”，随着音乐每个人都很用心的跳起舞来，现场充满欢笑声。接下来，老师透过“旧时光机”照片播放，回顾事先搜集的祖孙合照，一张一张细数祖孙间的温馨故事；接着，老师播放孙子要给阿公阿嬷的祝福语影片，长者们看着自己的孙子用着稚嫩的言语说着温馨的祝福，每位都好感动。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12498897" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465716.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12498897 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465716-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">欣赏祖孙合照影片。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>附幼小朋友也为阿公阿嬷佩戴胸花，奉上象征长寿的感恩菊花茶给阿公阿嬷喝，多位长者感动的热泪盈眶。为抓住温馨的画面，老师为每一个家庭的祖孙，现场拍个“温馨祖孙照”。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12498898" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465717.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12498898 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465717-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小朋友在影片中表达对阿嬷的感谢。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>紧接着以即拍即印的相印机将“温馨祖孙照”列印出来。下一步祖孙围在一木桌前共同为温馨祖孙照加框，此时祖孙动用剪刀、色纸、胶水等材料合力制作、装饰相框，祖孙爱同框作品大功告成。温馨祖孙照装上外框后，整个价值显现出来了，可长久保存作纪念。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12498899" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465718.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12498899 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465718-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小朋友在现场拿起麦克风表达对阿公阿嬷的感谢。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>黄珮书最后表示，11/7在中央广场还有一场活动式的成果展，会邀请制作陀螺的师傅上台与市长黄敏惠一起打陀螺，希望大家也能来共襄盛举。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12498900" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465719.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12498900 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465719-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小朋友在重阳敬老节前夕的奉茶活动，以感恩菊花茶表达对阿嬷的谢意。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12498901" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465720.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12498901 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465720-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小朋友对阿嬷奉上一杯感恩菊花茶，阿嬷又惊又喜。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12498902" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465721.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12498902 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465721-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">老师为每一个家庭的祖孙，现场拍个“温馨祖孙照”。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12498903" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465722.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12498903 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465722-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">老师以即拍即印的相印机将“温馨祖孙照”列印出来。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12498904" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465723.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12498904 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/465723-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">最后共同创作温馨祖孙照&#8211;祖孙爱同框。（李撷璎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>责任编辑：玉珍</p>
