  <figure id="attachment_12447639" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414281487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447639" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414281487-600x369.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="369" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">本届<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%A9%AC%E5%A5%96.md#1">金马奖</a>星光大道主持人杨千霈（左）、林鹤轩于日前共同宣布今年的24项专属客制贵宾礼品，为所有<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%A5%E5%9B%B4%E8%80%85.md#1">入围者</a>喝采。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>日前（9月30日）金马执委会邀请本届星光大道主持人杨千霈、林鹤轩共同宣布今年的24项专属客制贵宾礼品，为所有入围者喝采。今年金马贵宾礼品以“生活”为主题，严选众品牌打造极具质感商品。</p>
  <p>除了先前曝光的客制金色限量款大同联名电锅、迷你纪念电锅置物盒，以及CSD中卫口罩礼盒等影人心中的梦幻逸品，还有第六年打造专属瓶身的海洋深层气泡水、泡泡骚手机气囊支架、台电文创以旧公文纸环保再生的扩香石笔记本，以及著名钢笔品牌的联名玫金钢笔。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12447640" style="width: 454px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414241487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12447640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414241487.jpg" alt="" width="454" b="680" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台湾特有种五木精油香气礼盒。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12447683" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446071487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12447683" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446071487.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="677" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">CSD中卫联名口罩礼盒。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12447646" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414171487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12447646" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414171487.jpg" alt="" width="455" b="681" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">JINS金马联名墨镜。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>考量入围者在外拍戏需求，金马也贴心准备来自美国的HYDY联名两用随行保温杯、日本品牌JINS联名墨镜、文青指定plain-me旅行小包及托特包。还有肌肤香氛品牌KP女王香氛的花灿光耀香氛洗沐组、星光保养礼盒等。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12447684" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446031487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447684" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446031487-600x387.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="387" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">星光保养礼盒。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12447643" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414201487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447643" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020414201487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">花灿光耀香氛礼盒组。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>另外，还有疫情之下，更针对环境防护推出多款商品，包含肯园“疗愈之岛”台湾特有种五木精油香气礼盒、大稻埕品牌大春炼皂的耀净金马皂礼盒、ible Airvida联名穿戴式空气清净机。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12447686" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446111487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447686" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446111487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">联名穿戴式空气清净机。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>食品部分则有带起台湾“黑糖鲜奶”风潮的联名黑糖礼盒、有机食品专家的芝麻礼盒、超夯团购的客制爆米花，以及来自台中的8结蛋卷，行政院农委会与彰化县埔盐乡大有社区合作的金碳稻礼盒，还有老协珍熬鸡精。此外，陪所有人一起长大的零食“乖乖”也加入联名行列，一连推出两款商品。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12447687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446151487.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12447687" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2010020446151487-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">老协珍联名熬鸡精。（台北金马影展执行委员会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>今年除了金马奖入围者可以获得全套贵宾品，部分商品也将在品牌通路贩售，11月4日起更可直接在指定的便利商店购买，让更多人搜藏<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E9%A9%AC57.md#1">金马57</a>的限量联名商品，共享盛会。</p>
  <p>第57届金马奖颁奖典礼将于11月21日在台北国父纪念馆举行。</p>
