  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">必要材料</span></h2>  <p>糯米糕2个<br />  糖少许</p>
  <h2><span style="color: #008000;">步骤</span></h2>  <p>1.前一晚睡前将<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B9%B4%E7%B3%95.md#1">年糕</a>拿出来解冻。<br />  （在前一晚就准备好，如果前一晚忘记拿出来解冻，当天再用微波炉加热1分钟就OK啰！）</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12480554" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7db5ad158b8d9a64576f08a9338ae384.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12480554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/7db5ad158b8d9a64576f08a9338ae384-450x337.jpg" alt="年糕" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">前一晚睡前将年糕拿出来解冻。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2.开小火热锅，倒少许油后，把年糕放进锅里煎。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12480555" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/03d79245fe89df7bd32b558a80d70e82.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12480555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/03d79245fe89df7bd32b558a80d70e82-450x338.jpg" alt="年糕" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">开小火热锅，倒少许油后，把年糕放进锅里煎。（图／采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>3.用汤匙以按压的方式煎，等两面呈金黄色时起锅，最后洒上一些糖即可。<br />  （搭配小菜一起享用也很好吃！）</p>
