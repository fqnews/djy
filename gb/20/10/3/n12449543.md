  <figure id="attachment_12451433" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_517264075.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12451433" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_517264075-450x229.jpg" alt="栗子" width="450" b="229" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">秋天是吃烤栗子的季节。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #0000ff;">栗子巧克力<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A5%B6%E8%8C%B6.md#1">奶茶</a></span></h2>  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">材料</span></h3>  <p>栗子<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%8C%B6.md#1">红茶</a>6g<br />  杏仁奶180ml<br />  巧克力酱20ml<br />  装饰用可可粉</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12451434" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_602988734.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12451434" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_602988734-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">可可粒、巧克力块、巧克力酱、巧克力粉（由左至右）都很适合巧妙的和奶茶做搭配，苦甜的滋味特别有青春活力的气息。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">作法</span></h3>  <p>1. 在牛奶锅中放入<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E5%8F%B6.md#1">茶叶</a>和杏仁奶，均匀搅拌使茶叶充分浸润。<br />  Tip：煮茶的同时，在杯中倒入热水温杯后，再把水倒掉。煮茶的途中就算需要加入其它食材混合，也不需要额外增加浸泡的时间，茶的味道和香气还是会充分释出。浸泡太久反而会泡出<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E5%8F%B6.md#1">茶叶</a>中苦涩的味道，影响奶茶的风味，请务必小心。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12451436" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1072313420.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12451436" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/shutterstock_1072313420-450x300.jpg" alt="栗子" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">栗子<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E8%8C%B6.md#1">红茶</a>跟香醇的杏仁奶也很搭！（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2. 将1. 以小火加热，待牛奶锅边缘开始出现小气泡，茶叶舒展开来之后，倒入巧克力酱均匀混合后关火。<br />  3. 用滤网过滤泡好的茶叶，将奶茶倒入奶泡杯中，再使用电动奶泡器打出奶泡。<br />  4. 将打出奶泡的奶茶倒入杯中，撒上装饰用可可粉，完成。</p>
  <h3><span style="color: #008000;">NOTE</span></h3>  <p>? 栗子红茶是加入栗子香气的调味红茶，跟香醇的杏仁奶很搭。如果不用栗子红茶，可以使用适合搭配杏仁滋味，香气浓郁的中国红茶或者印度红茶。<br />  ? 用一般牛奶代替杏仁奶，也能和栗子的香气调和得很好。</p>
