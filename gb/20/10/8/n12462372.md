  <p>新北市<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB%E5%B8%88%E5%85%AC%E4%BC%9A.md#1">中医师公会</a>做公益不落人后，继今年初捐赠660份药膳包及前往双溪举办<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B9%89%E8%AF%8A.md#1">义诊</a>外，8日又再加码捐赠800份药膳包给泰山、五股地区银发俱乐部长辈及弱势民众，并举办义诊充份展现善心与爱心。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12462377" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12462377" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/c682599db5c3134727252c09ba65ab8e-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">市府回赠感谢奖牌。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>中医师公会理事长詹益能表示，该会长期以来秉持“医者父母心”的精神，每月办理养生讲座，更成立全国第一个由中医师组成的志愿服务队，经常深入偏区义诊，另外也举办“健脑护鼻操”校园巡回教学、小神农探险营等公益活动。这次捐赠的四神药膳包是由公会开药方，由科达制药公司所帮忙提炼而成。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12462378" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12462378" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/e525378a3a3cd23ce36499e359b01b17-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">全体贵宾合影。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>中医师公会今年7月底为响应新北市政府“OK旅游”，带领理监事到双溪淡兰古道出游，同时在当地举办义诊。今年1月才捐赠660份药膳包，这次再加码捐赠800份药膳包，以提供五股、泰山关怀据点的长辈，同时在现场举办义诊活动，帮长辈把脉、针灸，有20多位长辈接受服务。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12462379" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12462379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/b45545bd3cae8b7d781dfff6750672cb-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">新北中医理事长詹益能。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>社会局专委林坤宗表示，新北市中医师公会长年从事公益活动，去年获得侯友宜市长颁赠人民团体领航金奖最高荣耀的“最佳领航奉献奖”，成为新北市5,400多家团体的公益先驱。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12462381" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12462381" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/76165d132b4e6f23920ce37e7fce9099-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">桂圆银耳露药膳。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>林坤宗表示，“好日子爱心大平台”成立一年余，已获得民众捐物市值及捐款合计超过10亿元，这些资源都投入照顾弱势，欢迎更多人加入“圆梦英雄”。泰山和五股2位区长也感谢中医师公会的爱心。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12462382" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12462382" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/d2cfa5fab6472b830a5fd49b292779cb-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">四神汤药膳。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>参与活动的贵宾有社会局专门委员林坤宗、泰山区长林庆丰、五股区长洪崇琏、中医公会理事长詹益能，监事长陈文丰，副理事长邱定，理事陈明树、中医师林曾翠霞、洪汝欣、顾问卓汶庆、社会人文课课长胡建文、科达制药股份有限公司副总经理陈美玲、社区发展协会理事长蔡铃木、贵子里里长戴联树，以及多位社区发展协会理事长、多位里长等。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_12462383" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12462383" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/ca5d36d5867baeae0b2512332349573e-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">泰山区长林庆丰。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12462384" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12462384" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/1d1ad3fe2dad46784401ab638a8c1997-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中医师洪汝欣为民众义诊。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12462386" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12462386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/2756fe8812ec75a8f4e8be9f1a1c521a-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中医师公会捐800袋药膳，暖心暖胃。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_12462387" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12462387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/10/8c05ee26d29de157216df9e9e1cb985b-450x298.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="298" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中医师林曾翠霞为民众义诊。（宋顺澈／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>责任编辑：郑桦</p>
