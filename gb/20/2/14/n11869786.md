<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>说走就走的隐世之旅  Berkshire East</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/DJI_0127-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>位于麻萨诸塞州西部的Berkshire East ，是一家四季度假村，可谓是东岸冒险运动（Adventure Sports）的发源地，并且也是文化气息浓厚的地区。（本文图片均由Berkshire East 提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年02月15日讯】<strong><a href="https://berkshireeast.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Berkshire East</a></strong> 是一家四季<a href="/gb/tag/%E5%BA%A6%E5%81%87%E6%9D%91.md">度假村</a>，位在麻萨诸塞州西部，靠近伯克希尔县（Berkshire County）、北安普敦（Northampton）、阿默斯特（Amherst）、格林菲尔德（Greenfield）等地。此地可谓是东岸冒险运动（Adventure Sports）的发源地，却也是文化气息浓厚的一带，因为这里与美国著名小说著作《白鲸记》的诞生地相去不远，该书作者Herman Melville就在附近完成此巨著创作。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/DJI_0048.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11869808 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/DJI_0048-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="738" b="553" /></a></p>
<p>Berkshire East 拥有麻州最适合滑行的雪道，除了提供冒险刺激的雪道体验，位于<a href="/gb/tag/%E6%BB%91%E9%9B%AA%E5%9C%BA.md">滑雪场</a>的东侧，还有孩子最爱的雪上气垫船（Snow Tubing）。冬季结束后，这里也有丰富的户外体验活动欢迎您尽情探索。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-11869856 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/49255462_2716575495051344_1611358097509449728_o-600x369.jpg" alt="" width="736" b="453" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-11869857 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/53155015_2834424899933069_2966700538898415616_o-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="742" b="418" /></p>
<p><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/54368144_2843458842363008_1984599139308011520_o.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11869854 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/54368144_2843458842363008_1984599139308011520_o-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="736" b="552" /></a></p>
<p>这里有美东最好的越野自行车公园，即“<a href="https://berkshireeast.com/summer/thunder-mountain-bike-park/overview" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Thunder Mountain Bike Park</a>”所在地；有三条滑行钢索路线（Zip），适合在秋季欣赏橙黄橘绿的山林美景；夏天适合体验刺激的急流泛舟之旅，河流范围包含麻州西部以及佛州、康州；这里有世上其中一座最长的山地云霄飞车（过山车）；有冒险公园，有各式各样的障碍物挑战；有乡村农场旅馆以及婚宴中心，还有许多专门举办宴会的设施。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11869861" style="width: 723px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/70197217_3292507040791517_523150677627633664_o.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11869861 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/70197217_3292507040791517_523150677627633664_o-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="723" b="482" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">越野自行车公园（Thunder Mountain Bike Park）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11869821" style="width: 496px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/Bike.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11869821" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/Zipline-6-600x900.jpg" alt="" width="496" b="743" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">滑行钢索路线（Zip）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11869831" style="width: 711px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/Coaster.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11869831 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/Coaster-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="711" b="474" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">过山车</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_11869820" style="width: 731px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-11869820 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/Adventure-Park-12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="731" b="487" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">冒险公园</figcaption></figure>
<p>Berkshire East 为每一位造访者带来出走山林的野趣。夏季适合带上孩子来趟天伦之旅，冬日里约好三五好友共赴雪地畅快体验滑雪乐趣，这里也很适合公司包场举办员工之旅。夏季时，Berkshire East 开放露营地，也提供精美的客房，周边还有数家餐厅和酒吧供您享用。</p>
<p>从波士顿出发，驱车需2.5小时；若从纽约市出发，则需将近4小时。造访Berkshire East的同时，您还可顺道规划周边行程，此区有许多美丽的自然风光，周边有Shelburne瀑布，也有数间艺术博物馆，还有许多美食餐厅供您发掘与探索。</p>
<p class="has-text-align-center">注：“雪上气垫船”今年由于天气因素，已提早关闭。</p>
