<h2><strong><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%86%A0%E9%B9%AB.md#1">大冠鹫</a>（Spilornis cheela）鹰科</strong></h2>
<p>又名蛇雕、蛇鹰、冠蛇雕，为中型猛禽。高约55至75公分，成鸟背面和后颈为黑褐色，胸部和腹部和翼下为淡褐色，胸腹有白色斑点，停栖时可见明显冠羽，在高空时翼开展保持以浅Ｖ字型翱翔，尾巴和翼下后缘为黑色有一条白色横带。筑巢在森林近水边的树冠，鸟巢以树枝条构成，且一巢只生一个蛋。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11892784" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/393249_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11892784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/393249_medium-600x898.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="898" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">筑巢在森林近水边的树冠，鸟巢以树枝条构成，且一巢只生一个蛋。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11892785" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/393250_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11892785" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/393250_medium-600x908.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="908" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%A7%E5%86%A0%E9%B9%AB.md#1">大冠鹫</a>分布很广，从thomas南部的印度和斯里兰卡到亚洲东南部的中国南方，以及印度尼西亚。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11892783" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/393247_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11892783" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/393247_medium-600x434.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="434" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在高空时翼开展保持以浅Ｖ字型翱翔，尾巴和翼下后缘为黑色有一条白色横带。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11892786" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/393245_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11892786" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/393245_medium-600x908.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="908" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大冠鹫主要以蛇这类爬虫类为食，多在森林里猎捕，也捕食蜥蜴、小型鸟、鼠类。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>大冠鹫分布很广，从thomas南部的印度和斯里兰卡到亚洲东南部的中国南方，以及印度尼西亚。大冠鹫鸣叫声是“呼、呼、悠悠阿”，声音高昂，远处可听闻，尤其上午时分，靠着热气流盘旋飞上高空。大冠鹫主要以蛇这类爬虫类为食，多在森林里猎捕，也捕食蜥蜴、小型鸟、鼠类。</p>
