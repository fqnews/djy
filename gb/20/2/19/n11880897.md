<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】芋头排骨芥菜汤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/1c028497f448b96ed79cd0a10f8deaa5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>芋头、排骨、芥菜三种食材汇聚一处熬制汤品，不仅醇香味美， 而且还可以增进食欲、提高免疫力！（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>芋头含有糖类、膳食纤维、维他命C、维他命B群、钾、钙、锌等，常吃能增强人体的免疫功能。芋头也是防治癌瘤的常用药膳主食，还能解毒。中医认为，芋头有开胃生津、消炎镇痛、补气益肾等功效，可治胃痛、痢疾、慢性肾炎等。</p>
<p>芥菜微苦中带甘味，具有“长年菜”之称，是公认的健康食材。芥菜的热量低，膳食纤维丰富，有助于排便，也是抗癌的好蔬菜。芥菜含有大量的抗坏血酸，有提神醒脑、解除疲劳的作用。</p>
<p>芥菜还是富含维生素A的蔬菜，含有丰富的β胡萝卜素，与肉类一起烹煮，有提升免疫力、预防感冒、保眼、护肤等效果，有明目、消肿、消炎功能，可用来辅助治疗感染性疾病 。</p>
<p>猪肉性平味甘，可润肠胃、生津液、补肾气、解热毒；芋头可以益胃宽肠、通便解毒、补益肝肾；芥菜尤其芥菜头带有一股清甜的味道。芋头、排骨、芥菜三种食材汇聚一处熬制汤品，不仅醇香味美， 而且还可以增进食欲、提高免疫力！</p>
<h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%8B%E5%A4%B4%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8%E8%8A%A5%E8%8F%9C%E6%B1%A4.md#1">芋头排骨芥菜汤</a></h2>
<p>主料：大芋头1颗、芥菜1棵、猪脊骨3磅。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/ee946f1e9fdb93e3aa0c923409da8da7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912770" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/ee946f1e9fdb93e3aa0c923409da8da7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/d8341e15c73d0a7688f8ded56efeecb2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912771" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/d8341e15c73d0a7688f8ded56efeecb2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>配料：姜6块。<br />
调料：胡椒粉0.5汤匙、蜂蜜2汤匙、盐2茶匙。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将香芋头洗净，去皮后切成小粒。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/1-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912775" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/1-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2. 将大芥菜洗净，切成小块。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912776" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 猪脊骨用开水煮过，去杂质及血水，捞起沥干水。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/3-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912777" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/3-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 猛火阴油把姜片炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912778" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 放入排骨，煎香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/5.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912780" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/5-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 加入已烧开的水。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912781" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 加盖慢火煲35分钟，将骨头捞出。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912782" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 把香芋粒加进去一起慢火煮。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912783" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 将芥菜加入汤中，焖煮5分钟。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912784" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 添加胡椒粉、蜂蜜、盐和炒香的面粉水。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912787" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴清香四溢，嫩滑可口。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/11-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11912788" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/11-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<h2>罗厨烹饪经验谈</h2>
<p>罗厨特别强调，制作这道汤品的关键是火候。一定要熬制到排骨骨肉分离，骨髓熬出流入汤中，这时骨汤浓白香甜。</p>

<h2>粤菜名厨罗子昭</h2>
<p>入行近40年，获得过许多荣誉。曾任锦江集团北方公司厨师评委、昆仑饭店中餐出品总监、沈阳市富虹酒店行政总厨，被业内的同行推为“北京粤菜四大名厨”之一。“中国烹饪大师”、“粤港澳名厨”、“亚太厨皇勋章”等头衔都是对他本人精湛厨艺的肯定。2011年，在新唐人电视台主办的第四届全世界中国菜厨技大赛上，他以传统的中国菜一举摘得粤菜金奖。</p>
