<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】红烧牛尾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7617d026de324a2d623e322ec2c766d9-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨烹制的红烧牛尾，色泽金红，牛尾嫩、滑，软而不烂，肥而不腻，咸鲜醇厚，堪称是一道营养丰富、老少皆宜的滋补佳肴。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E7%89%9B%E5%B0%BE.md#1">红烧牛尾</a>”是一道传统名菜，早在周朝就是关中人的主要菜肴。相传秦始皇统一六国后，一次出巡路上，感到腹中空空，便走进一家餐馆。事有凑巧，此日店里牛肉已卖完，只有一条牛尾，店主人炖在锅里，准备自己食用，此时只得胆战心惊地端出来进献给秦始皇。谁知歪打正着，秦始皇从来未吃过这样味美的牛尾，不但不怪罪店主，反而给予重赏。此菜后经历代不断改进，流传至今。</p>
<p>牛尾，是牛身上活动最为频繁的部分。多筋骨、少脂肪、富含胶质，因而其肉的味道最为鲜美，而且营养价值极高，含有丰富的钙质和胶原蛋白，能益血气、补精髓、强体魄、滋容颜。现代营养学因其低脂肪、高蛋白、富含多种营养物质，而视其为健康营养食品。</p>
<p>胡萝卜含有非常丰富的维生素和矿物质。具有健脾消食、润肠通便、行气化滞、明目等功效。其所含有的胡萝卜素，经常食用能够治疗夜盲症，保护呼吸道和促进儿童生长，一般人都可食用。</p>
<p>将胡萝卜与牛尾搭配，既能用蔬菜来吸收牛尾中的油脂，又能促进胡萝卜素的吸收，口感和营养都更加均衡。</p>
<p>罗厨烹制的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BA%A2%E7%83%A7%E7%89%9B%E5%B0%BE.md#1">红烧牛尾</a>，色泽金红，牛尾口感嫩、滑，软而不烂，肥而不腻，咸鲜醇厚，香醇背后又带清香甜美的胡萝卜味道，堪称是一道营养丰富、开胃清爽、老少皆宜的滋补佳肴。</p>
<h2>红烧牛尾</h2>
<p>主料：牛尾3磅。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1d59573c6854979f97b742bf139bcaaa.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878367" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1d59573c6854979f97b742bf139bcaaa-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：干香菇、紫洋葱、红葱头、生姜、胡萝卜、西洋芹、小番茄、蒜头。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/353de3f893cc994bd8f7d6671a176f77.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878370" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/353de3f893cc994bd8f7d6671a176f77-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/22e13aaec4a1da41580c6f01221d52db.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878371" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/22e13aaec4a1da41580c6f01221d52db-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>调料：生抽、蚝油、老抽、冰糖、胡椒粉。</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将牛尾用开水煮过，祛除杂质和血水后，沥干水分。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-27.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878373" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-27-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2. 用七成油温，将生姜、胡萝卜块、香菇、红洋葱、芹菜炸至浅黄色捞起。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-20.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878375" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-20-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 利用锅底余油（油要很少），放半碗水，冰糖1/3碗，炒好糖色。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-20.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878376" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-20-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>4. 添加水，将牛尾倒入锅中，牛尾要全部浸入水中。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878379" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4-18-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>5. 添加料酒、生抽、胡椒粉等调料，将配料加入锅中。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878380" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 中火加盖焖煮30～40分钟，至牛尾嫩滑而软。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-2-1.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878381" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6-2-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 将煲烂的芹菜、洋葱捞起，令牛尾汤汁透亮。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878382" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 将胡萝卜块捞起，如果色泽不够，加适量生抽、老抽调色。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878383" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 最后加入紫洋葱、葱丝、黄椒丝、炸过的蒜粒。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878384" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/9-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>10.?翻搅、勾芡，收汁后就可起锅落盘了。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/10-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878385" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/10-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴肉烂汁浓、肥而不腻。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8aa071f4565bb822b5a2542fc0d49474.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878391" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8aa071f4565bb822b5a2542fc0d49474-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/11-13.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11878386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/11-13-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>

<h2>粤菜名厨罗子昭</h2>
<p>入行近40年，获得过许多荣誉。曾任锦江集团北方公司厨师评委、昆仑饭店中餐出品总监、沈阳市富虹酒店行政总厨，被业内的同行推为“北京粤菜四大名厨”之一。“中国烹饪大师”、“粤港澳名厨”、“亚太厨皇勋章”等头衔都是对他本人精湛厨艺的肯定。2011年，在新唐人电视台主办的第四届全世界中国菜厨技大赛上，他以传统的中国菜一举摘得粤菜金奖。</p>
