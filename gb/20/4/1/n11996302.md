<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>24小时内中共病毒感染集群数增一倍</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年04月02日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;">（大纪元记者筱康<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>编译报道）短短</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">24</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">小时内，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B0.md#1">新西兰</a>重要<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%84%9F%E6%9F%93.md#1">感染</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%86%E7%BE%A4.md#1">集群</a>的数量就增加了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个，这给卫生系统带来了进一步的压力。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">据《新西兰先驱报》报道，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">31</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日，新西兰的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%85%B1%E7%97%85%E6%AF%92.md#1">中共病毒</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%84%9F%E6%9F%93.md#1">感染</a>人数增加到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">600</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">人，而感染<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9B%86%E7%BE%A4.md#1">集群</a>数则增加了一倍，达到个</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">14</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">本次增加的感染集群中，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">感染人数最多的是与新西兰皇家空军<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A9%84%E6%A6%84%E7%90%83%E9%98%9F.md#1">橄榄球队</a>（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Royal New Zealand Air Force rugby team</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）有关</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，这支<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A9%84%E6%A6%84%E7%90%83%E9%98%9F.md#1">橄榄球队</a>曾在</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月赴美参加美国锦标赛。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">另一个新的感染集群与布拉夫（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bluff</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）的婚礼有关，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">该</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">婚礼在新西兰</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">宣布</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">禁止大型聚会之前举行。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在新西兰，被确定为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">属于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">感染集群的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">患者</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">不一定是新近发生的病例，而是因为他们曾经在一起，因此相互关联。大多数这些感染集群病例都与最近出国旅行的一个人或几个人有联系。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">七个新感染集群包括：</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Living Earth</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，基督城：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个病例</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">新西兰皇家空军橄榄球队，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Blenheim</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">9</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个病例</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">布拉夫（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bluff</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）的婚礼，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bluff</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个病例</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Marlborough</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">集群，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Marlborough</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个病例</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hutt </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">市集群</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hutt</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个病例</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hutt </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">市集群</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hutt</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个病例</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">科罗拉多（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Colorado</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）滑水艇制造商，奥克兰：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个病例</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">频谱护理（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Spectrum care</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">），奥克兰：</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">个病例</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">最大的感染集群仍然是奥克兰马里斯特女子学院（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Marist College</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">），该校有</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">48</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">例确诊病例和一些</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">疑似</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">病例。</span></p>
