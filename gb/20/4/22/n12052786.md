<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>山中瑰宝 石碇</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8b1f4095e5a6b005592d5855a80120e1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>山城石碇（王知涵／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>喧嚣都会车水马龙，上班日总免不了与高噪音、高车烟废气为伍，很想找一个深幽僻静的地方，好好放松，欣赏高山绿水，那么台北市近郊――<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E7%A2%87.md#1">石碇</a>，绝对是最佳选择。</p>
<p><strong>淡兰吊桥与淡兰古道</strong></p>
<p>在木栅捷运站搭乘公车666，约莫半小时就到达绿意盎然小镇<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9F%B3%E7%A2%87.md#1">石碇</a>。首先安排色彩艳丽朱红色淡兰吊桥，这里风景秀丽，俯瞰石碇溪溪谷，大小溪石散落，流水遇到溪石时而湍急，时而潺潺，各有其美！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12052801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7010ff710dc3a203b614c2d525d6eb28.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052801" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7010ff710dc3a203b614c2d525d6eb28-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">淡兰吊桥（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12052804" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/b8457c47b76f3a5f6615ea2a3bfaeed7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/b8457c47b76f3a5f6615ea2a3bfaeed7-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">溪流中大小石头散布各处（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>沿溪流前行是石碇人气最旺的亲山步道淡兰古道。昔日的淡兰古道由艋舺出发，是连结淡水到宜兰的交通要道，现在仅存的一段由淡兰吊桥到石碇老街，全长约两公里。古道由碎石小径、石板块、木栈道组成，大部分的步道上方有北宜高速公路遮蔽，或是大树的葱郁垂荫，大幅减少炙阳酷热感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12052812" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/87b9bd52d1d6f66e9d3c32875e751dd7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052812" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/87b9bd52d1d6f66e9d3c32875e751dd7-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">淡兰古道（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>步道全程平坦宽敞，不到一小时路程设有两个休息处――凉亭和平台休息区，很适合一家老老少少健行。山景溪流美不胜收，只是讶然还意犹未尽，却已经到了目的地石碇老街。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12052813" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/f73c0c2a620b47c0c48524309357a095.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052813" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/f73c0c2a620b47c0c48524309357a095-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">淡兰古道的风景（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>石碇老街之一：石碇东街</strong></p>
<p>淡兰古道走到底就是石碇东街，沿乌涂溪而建，乌涂溪上的桥桥墩有23层高，非常壮观。以万寿桥区分石碇东街与石碇西街，石碇西街是昔日茶坊，现在多成为住户民宅，观光客主要聚集石碇东街的乌涂溪溪畔木板栈道，欣赏清澈见底的绿色潭水，众多溪石错落各处，几只水鸟傲然挺立，美得像一幅画。站在溪畔享受淡淡微风，沁入溪水凉意，整个身心都被疗愈了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12052816" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1361c9d47db43e8ba398d61097efe0e4.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052816" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1361c9d47db43e8ba398d61097efe0e4-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">左侧是乌涂溪溪畔步道（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12052820" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6a309978d32bd2dad53f452ef1f2de31.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6a309978d32bd2dad53f452ef1f2de31-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">乌涂溪上的桥桥墩有23层高（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>早期石碇是以煤矿起家，用乌涂溪清洗煤矿是矿工们每日例行公事，在乌涂溪的“人推煤矿台车雕像”纪念昔时矿工辛苦工作。煤矿台车对面是石碇东街另一个卖点“石碇一粒粽”，为端午节最佳伴手礼，不论肉粽、素粽、甜粽、桂花粽，各种口味一应俱全。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12052829" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/e3bd3fc7f86c7fb133285635c57052e1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12052829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/e3bd3fc7f86c7fb133285635c57052e1-600x665.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="332" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">煤矿台车（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>石碇老街之二：不见天街</strong></p>
<p>不见天街沿着崩山溪而建，因为整条街上方都有遮蔽，不仅遮阳蔽雨，冬季还可以挡风，所以命名“不见天街”，尚保留许多历史痕迹供游人细细参观。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12052838" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/562e0a9d2abd52ee015aaa0b75f2cb33.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12052838" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/562e0a9d2abd52ee015aaa0b75f2cb33-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="533" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">不见天街（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吊脚屋：台湾硕果仅存的吊脚建筑，以前是矿工宿舍，特色是一楼全部打通只留柱子支撑，为了便利当时挖矿疲惫的工人好通行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12052840" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/52e0ce7c6679b4213c898f54e9664ee8.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052840" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/52e0ce7c6679b4213c898f54e9664ee8-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">传统建筑特色吊脚屋（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>石头屋：建于民国前六年（公元1905年），最早是林恒春中药铺，屋内保存一些石器和古老制药器具。石头屋也曾经经营五金和杂货，在二次世界大战期间被当作防空洞用途。屋舍后方拾级而上有一个充满古意的小花园。民国95年石头屋由乡公所保管使用，才开始对外开放。</p>
<p>打铁铺：百年老店迄今还保留传统打铁技艺，现任老板叶先生打铁经验超过35年。店内放置着超过一世纪的鼓风炉和打铁炉，在台湾很难找到这种古董级的器材了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12052841" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/727e0f7cd939ccefe55e0a450ccd76df.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052841" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/727e0f7cd939ccefe55e0a450ccd76df-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">百年打铁店（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>袜子娃娃专卖店：穿在脚上的袜子被拿来做成可爱袜娃，每一个袜娃都是独一无二的，还有自己的出生编号。现场有DIY袜娃体验，让游人可以开心地把自己亲手缝制的袜娃带回家。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12052848" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/91d9186cef1bc4a77054feedb94fc782.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052848" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/91d9186cef1bc4a77054feedb94fc782-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">袜子娃娃（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>必尝美食：好山好水好空气孕育出香滑爽口的豆腐和面线。豆腐花样繁多――臭豆腐、清蒸豆腐、豆腐蛋糕、豆腐奶酪、豆腐起司、豆腐冰淇淋……这么多款豆腐美食只有石碇才见得到！石碇水质佳，好吃的面线就是用石碇山泉水制作，拉好的面线放在阳光下曝晒，清新空气造就外观嫩白又美味的Q弹面线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12052849" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/dcdc6093f0e1ab1063e62b4d5f53c0f0.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/dcdc6093f0e1ab1063e62b4d5f53c0f0-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">石碇名产（王知涵／大纪元）豆腐</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12052852" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6952985eb679685ec2df717a413084e0.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6952985eb679685ec2df717a413084e0-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">有名的手工面线（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12052853" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/d63f70843f654939867c23d3de07e9d6.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12052853" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/d63f70843f654939867c23d3de07e9d6-600x328.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="328" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">各式各样豆腐食品（王知涵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>石碇不大，古朴小山城却拥有令人流连忘返的私房山景，高山巍巍，山岚环绕，潺潺溪流，清净澄澈，大大小小石头点缀其中。坐邻溪畔，远眺高山，享用美食，置身在山里大自然，享受芬多精洗涤，生活也可以如此轻松惬意！@*</p>
