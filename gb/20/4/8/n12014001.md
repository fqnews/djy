<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】白木耳甜豆炒带子?爽脆鲜甜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>白木耳甜豆炒带子。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>【小编语】</strong></span>中共肺炎（武汉肺炎）防疫期间，如果不方便出门，在家下厨也可以品尝家常热炒菜。这一道“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E6%9C%A8%E8%80%B3.md#1">白木耳</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E8%B1%86.md#1">甜豆</a>炒<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B8%A6%E5%AD%90.md#1">带子</a>”，先将带子解冻后腌渍，再加入冬笋、白木耳、甜豆在锅中翻炒，淋上调味汁后快手兜匀即完成；一盘色彩缤纷、春意盎然的时蔬佳肴即可装碟。这一道菜口感偏清爽鲜脆，带子软嫩甘甜、冬笋肉质细致具清香，搭配甜豆、白木耳为营养加分也增添食欲。</p>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B8%A6%E5%AD%90.md#1">带子</a>富有丰富蛋白质，平时可以多买一些冷冻起来，只要解冻后进行腌制就可以烹煮，腌料可以多方尝试，为了去腥味又不影响食材品质可以使用姜汁酒。姜汁酒在粤菜地位昭然，是很多餐馆常用的炒腌酱汁，适用去除有血腥味的鱼类、肉类，不妨尝试看看！</p>
<h3>【材料】</h3>
<p>发好的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E6%9C%A8%E8%80%B3.md#1">白木耳</a> 6安士（White Fungus 6 oz）<br />
鲜冬笋4安士（Bamboo Shoots 4 oz）<br />
<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E8%B1%86.md#1">甜豆</a>3安士（Sugar peas 3 oz）<br />
红萝卜花和姜蒜蓉各少许（Carrot slices, ginger &amp; garlic a little）<br />
急冻带子（16-20）10安士（Scallop 10 oz）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014026" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-21.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12014026 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-21-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,白木耳,甜豆,带子" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">姜蒜蓉、红萝卜花、鲜冬笋、甜豆、白木耳、急冻带子。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【腌料】</h3>
<p>姜汁酒、胡椒粉和栗粉各适量</p>
<h3>【调味料】</h3>
<p>鸡汤1/4杯（Chicken Broth ?C）<br />
糖1/4茶匙（Sugar ?t）<br />
盐1/8茶匙（Salt 1/8t）<br />
胡椒粉和麻油各少许（Pepper &amp; Sesame oil a little）<br />
粟粉1/2茶匙（Constarch 1/2t）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014028" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-12014028 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-19-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,白木耳,甜豆,带子" width="450" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">胡椒粉、麻油、鸡汤、粟粉、糖、盐（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【做法】</h3>
<p>1. 带子解冻后用少许姜汁酒、胡椒粉和粟粉腌10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014029" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-15-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,白木耳,甜豆,带子" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">带子解冻后用少许姜汁酒、胡椒粉和粟粉腌10分钟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 腌好的带子用姜汁酒烫过后沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014030" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-11-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,白木耳,甜豆,带子" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">腌好的带子用姜汁酒烫过后沥干备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 冬笋切成三角型，甜豆摘除头尾旁边的粗纤维，洗干净备用。白木耳、甜豆和冬笋用油盐水烫熟，捞出沥干。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014031" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014031" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-5-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,白木耳,甜豆,带子" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白木耳、甜豆和冬笋用油盐水烫熟，捞出沥干。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 热镬下油，爆香料头，放入所有材料翻炒30秒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014032" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2080cd58003f3589a2a6341caf7e0380.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014032" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2080cd58003f3589a2a6341caf7e0380-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,白木耳,甜豆,带子" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">热镬下油，爆香料头，放入所有材料翻炒30秒。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 搅匀调味汁液，快手倒入锅中兜匀，便可上碟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014033" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014033" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-4-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,白木耳,甜豆,带子" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">搅匀调味汁液，快手倒入锅中兜匀，便可上碟。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【梁师傅小贴士】</h3>
<p>纯白木耳稀少，属于东南亚某些地方的农产品。建议可以使用“白背木耳”代替。木耳是很好的营养食材，对容易腰腿疼痛、抽筋麻木的人有帮助，亦可降血压和血糖。◇</p>
