<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】西洋菜猪横脷汤?润肺健脾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-5-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>西洋菜猪横脷汤是一道具有食疗的汤品，香气浓郁。（梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><strong>【小编语】</strong>俗话说：“饭前先喝汤，胜过良药方。”饮用“煲汤”调节身体、美颜润肤，增强免疫力，不同汤品因为食材各异有不同功效。“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E6%A8%AA%E8%84%B7.md#1">猪横脷</a>”是广式汤品常见食材之一，在中医食疗角度有养肺润燥、助消化、去肝火的功效，适合工作忙碌、经常要熬夜的人；熬煮时加入蜜枣、陈皮可以提味增加清香，融合着肉质在锅中慢慢炖煮释放各类营养素精华，滋补养身上选之品。</p>
<p>春夏交际之时，保养好呼吸道避免过敏和感染病菌，这一道“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%B4%8B%E8%8F%9C.md#1">西洋菜</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8C%AA%E6%A8%AA%E8%84%B7.md#1">猪横脷</a>汤”综合各种食疗材料，熬煮过程不困难，备料、掌握好火侯和时间，料理成功率高，不妨跟着食谱做看看！</p>
<h3>【材料】</h3>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A5%BF%E6%B4%8B%E8%8F%9C.md#1">西洋菜</a>2扎（Watercress）<br />
鸭胗3个 （Duck Gizzard）<br />
猪蹄筋12oz （Pork Tendon）<br />
猪横脷3条（Pork Pancreas）<br />
旧陈皮1块（Dried Tangerine Peel）<br />
姜3片（Ginger）<br />
南北杏共2oz （Almonds）<br />
蜜枣4粒（Dates）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014158" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-22.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014158" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-22-450x300.jpg" alt="梁厨美食、西洋菜、猪横脷" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【做法】</h3>
<p>1. ?西洋菜先用清水加少许盐浸泡约15分钟，再捞起洗两次左右，完成后捞起备用。猪蹄筋切成大块，猪横脷切除肥油，与鸭胗用姜汁酒烫过，捞出再冲洗干净备用。南北杏、蜜枣和旧陈皮洗干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014161" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-20.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-20-450x300.jpg" alt="梁厨美食、西洋菜、猪横脷" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">西洋菜先用清水加少许盐浸泡约15分钟，再捞起洗两次左右，完成后捞起备用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. ?汤煲内放入16碗水，先放入鸭胗、猪蹄筋、猪横脷、南北杏、蜜枣、旧陈皮和姜，大火煲滚15分钟之后用中火煲 45分钟，捞出鸭胗切片后再放回汤煲内。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014163" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-16-450x674.jpg" alt="梁厨美食、西洋菜、猪横脷" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">汤煲内放入16碗水，再放入时才熬煮。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. ?接着放入西洋菜，再煲多90 分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014165" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-12.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-12-450x300.jpg" alt="梁厨美食、西洋菜、猪横脷" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">放入西洋菜。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 汤渣可以捞出，沾点酱油吃，因为鸭胗有些咸味，试过味道后再下盐调味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014167" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-6-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食、西洋菜、猪横脷" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">汤渣可以捞出，沾点酱油吃，因为鸭胗有些咸味，试过味道后再下盐调味。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【梁师傅小贴士】</h3>
<p>1. 猪横脷是猪的胰脏，可以增强人体胰岛素的分泌，有预防疾病的作用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014169" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-3-450x300.jpg" alt="梁厨美食、西洋菜、猪横脷" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">猪横脷是猪的胰脏，可以增强人体胰岛素的分泌，有预防疾病的作用。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 陈皮和鸭胗有止咳化痰的效果。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12014171" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12014171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-3-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食、西洋菜、猪横脷" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">陈皮有止咳化痰的效果。（梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 西洋菜有清热降火的作用。<br />
4. 这次制作的汤品香味浓郁，汤渣全部都可以吃。◇</p>
