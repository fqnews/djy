<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】蟹粉烧豆腐</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/389eeff809571576a5bb37245c1c15ce-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>蟹肉的鲜甜加入葱、蒜油的结合，会使豆腐更嫩滑更鲜美。色、香、味简单明快，是这道菜的最大特点。 （视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>豆腐口感细滑、软嫩，是人们餐桌上的常客，做法很多，怎么做都很好吃。今天罗厨为我们做了一道味道特别鲜美的“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9F%B9%E7%B2%89%E7%83%A7%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90.md#1">蟹粉烧豆腐</a>”，浓醇香滑的蟹肉和蟹膏与豆腐一起烧制，豆腐的醇香搭配蟹肉的鲜香，那一抹鲜美的味道令人入口难忘。</p>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9F%B9%E7%B2%89%E7%83%A7%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90.md#1">蟹粉烧豆腐</a>是江南地区的名菜之一，将小块状豆腐用油煎黄，放入蟹肉、蟹黄及调味料，之后加生粉水勾芡即可。</p>
<p>豆腐是中国的传统食品，味美而养生。也是我国素食菜肴的主要原料，在先民记忆中刚开始很难吃，经过不断的改造，逐渐受到人们的欢迎，被人们誉为“植物肉”。豆腐可以常年生产，不受季节限制，因此在蔬菜生产淡季，可以调剂菜肴品种。</p>
<p>蟹粉，是蟹经过煮熟或蒸熟后拆取出的蟹肉和蟹黄的统称，味道鲜美，适宜于多种烹饪用途。可蒸、炒、制馅或作为提味助鲜的原料。代表菜式如蟹粉狮子头、炒蟹粉、蟹黄包子等。</p>
<p>罗厨特别强调，制作“蟹粉烧豆腐”无需加香料调味，用料从简，为的是突出原汁原味。蟹肉的鲜甜加入葱、蒜油的结合，会使豆腐更嫩滑更鲜美。色、香、味简单明快，是这道菜的最大特点。</p>
<h2>蟹粉烧豆腐</h2>
<p>主料：滑豆腐2盒、螃蟹（大约1磅）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/d7936a30976ec41f2fc14497f9f26faf.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040025" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/d7936a30976ec41f2fc14497f9f26faf-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6cb7d0db845f52d594544ef2dc6bdc55.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040026" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6cb7d0db845f52d594544ef2dc6bdc55-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：葱、姜、蒜。<br />
调料：盐、老抽、生抽、胡椒粉。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 两盒滑豆腐切成小块备用，螃蟹拆肉取蟹黄。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-51.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040027" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-51-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 猛火阴油爆香蒜粒，捞蒜粒留蒜油。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-37.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040028" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-37-450x313.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="313" /></a><br />
3. 小块豆腐下油锅，爆香。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-32.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040029" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-32-450x304.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="304" /></a><br />
4. 放入盐、胡椒粉。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040030" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-18-450x341.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="341" /></a><br />
5. 放入老抽、生抽。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-15.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040032" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-15-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 放入一碗水后加盖焖煮5分钟。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040034" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 开盖放入蟹肉和蟹黄。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-11.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040035" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-11-450x308.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="308" /></a><br />
8. 放入葱花。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040036" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/8-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 加生粉水，再焖煮5分钟。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-9.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040037" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/9-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 开锅盛盘。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-18.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040038" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-18-450x343.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="343" /></a><br />
11. 成品菜肴清香鲜甜，滑嫩味美。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/11-23.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12040039" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/11-23-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a></p>
