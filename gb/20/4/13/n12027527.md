<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台式葱油干拌面～传承祖先手艺的好滋味</h2>
<a href="https://rawcdn.githack.com/wopdxm256/vd/master/jump2.htm?id=hWXes"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wopdxm256/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>煮熟的面，加入油葱酥、酱油、辣油、葱花拌匀，就完成一道香味四溢的“葱油干拌面”。（图：C2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3><span style="color: #008000;">清明节气</span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_12027713" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7a1b9ae4169da2d06ad1dff24707fff6.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027713" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7a1b9ae4169da2d06ad1dff24707fff6-450x254.png" alt="红葱头,Ｃ2食光,干拌面" width="450" b="254" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>节气来到清明时，代表春季已过了一大半。清明除了扫墓、踏青外，同时也需要注重身体的保养。清明时节饮食宜温，建议多多进食蔬果。</p>
<p>清明建议食材：大米、大多数谷物、瓜类、黄瓜、山药和鸡蛋。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>葱油干<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%8B%8C%E9%9D%A2.md#1">拌面</a></strong></span></h3>
<figure id="attachment_12027703" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/0707dfd884309e6ea0b14db9d04f34db.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027703" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/0707dfd884309e6ea0b14db9d04f34db-450x253.jpg" alt="红葱头,Ｃ2食光,干拌面" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：<ahref="/gb/tag/c2%E9%A3%9F%E5%85%89.md#1">C2食光</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在家想要轻松煮出记忆中的“古早味”，红葱头酥是最佳选择；红葱头有助防疫、加强抵抗力，春季正是采收的季节。炸红葱头酥要注意火侯和时间，如果炸得过老会有苦味、焦味，等放凉后可能有油耗味。用餐时间先煮一碗干面或白饭、烫炒青菜之后，加入充满香气的红葱油卤肉，就会成就一桌滋味俱全的料理。</p>
<h3>【食材】</h3>
<p>红葱头 约30颗<br />
猪油 5大匙<br />
酱油 1小匙<br />
葱花 适量<br />
辣油 1/2小匙</p>
<h3>【做法】</h3>
<p>1. 红葱头切碎备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027609" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/044069303c6c9fa1477fe7cf5cf68fc8.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/044069303c6c9fa1477fe7cf5cf68fc8-450x315.png" alt="红葱头,Ｃ2食光,干拌面" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红葱头切碎（图：C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 热锅放入猪油，微温时放入红葱头，然后用小火慢慢煸成金黄色的红葱头酥油。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12027610" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/97779d6175259205642e10d6a3069e1e.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027610" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/97779d6175259205642e10d6a3069e1e-450x315.png" alt="红葱头,Ｃ2食光,干拌面" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">热锅放入猪油，微温时放入红葱头（图：C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12027611" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/63165b2be1ab74162415ef9d477c1180.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027611" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/63165b2be1ab74162415ef9d477c1180-450x315.png" alt="红葱头,Ｃ2食光,干拌面" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用小火慢慢煸成金黄色的红葱头酥油。（图：C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12027612" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/c3ba49c549905858ea206b7fe9178556.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12027612" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/c3ba49c549905858ea206b7fe9178556-450x315.png" alt="红葱头,Ｃ2食光,干拌面" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红葱头酥不要炸太久会有苦味。（图：C2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 取一汤锅，将面条煮熟后捞起备用。<br />
4. 煮熟的面，加入<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B2%B9%E8%91%B1%E9%85%A5.md#1">油葱酥</a>、酱油、辣油、葱花拌匀即完成。</p>
<h3>【料理小技巧】</h3>
<p>炸红葱头的时候不要炸过头，否则会带出苦味。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #008000;">“营养师的贴心小叮咛”-by 陈小薇营养师</span></h3>
<p>调配好的台式葱油酱约215公克，换算成每100公克热量约438大卡，除了使用在面条上调味，也可以拌入烫好的青菜，如高丽菜、空心菜、菠菜…等，增加菜肴风味。</p>
<p>营养师小叮咛因为油脂受热容易氧化裂变，随着保存时间变长，会产生不好的气味，也是我们讲的油耗味；因此，台式葱油酱建议制作完毕，必须尽早使用，并且纳入每日的油脂摄取量。</p>
<p>另外，台式葱油干<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%8B%8C%E9%9D%A2.md#1">拌面</a>的面条，可以用蒟蒻面替代，这样就成为一道减糖的主食，再搭配葱油高丽菜、葱油菠菜，最后淋上些许酱油的古早味荷包蛋，就成为饮食均衡的一餐。</p>
