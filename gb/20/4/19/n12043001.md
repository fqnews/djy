<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蒜蓉酱汁搭配皮蛋～私房家常小菜</h2>
<a href="https://rawcdn.githack.com/puj222/vd/master/jump2.htm?id=FDDQJ"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/puj222/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>这一道“蒜蓉酱汁搭配皮蛋”有独特风味，十分下饭。（图： Ｃ2食光）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>吃腻了<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%89%E6%8B%8C%E5%B0%8F%E8%8F%9C.md#1">凉拌小菜</a>“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9A%AE%E8%9B%8B.md#1">皮蛋</a>豆腐”吗？简单变化一下料理又是美味的家常开胃小菜，这一道“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%92%9C%E8%93%89%E9%85%B1.md#1">蒜蓉酱</a>汁搭配<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9A%AE%E8%9B%8B.md#1">皮蛋</a>”有独特风味，十分下饭，是在家也能品尝的精致小点。</p>
<h3>【食材】</h3>
<p>皮蛋 3颗<br />
青葱 2根<br />
蒜头 4瓣<br />
辣椒 1根<br />
香菜 适量<br />
酱油1/2大匙<br />
蚝油 适量<br />
白胡椒粉 适量<br />
糖 适量</p>
<h3>【作法】</h3>
<p>1. 皮蛋冷水下锅，约煮10分钟左右。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12045368" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/e56cb3c15f23913013cd946b2df2007c.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12045368" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/e56cb3c15f23913013cd946b2df2007c-450x304.png" alt="Ｃ2食光,皮蛋,蒜蓉酱" width="450" b="304" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">皮蛋冷水下锅，约煮10分钟左右。（图： Ｃ2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 煮好的皮蛋捞出来，放清水里泡一下，然后去壳，把皮蛋切成4半，最后装盘备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12045364" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/c43260b9ca7487c6e0ee21c623c3fc65.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12045364" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/c43260b9ca7487c6e0ee21c623c3fc65-450x306.png" alt="Ｃ2食光,皮蛋,蒜蓉酱" width="450" b="306" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">煮好的皮蛋捞出来，放清水里泡一下。（图： Ｃ2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12045348" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1814863009c25a26ad3edf2530298ad6.png"><img class="wp-image-12045348 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1814863009c25a26ad3edf2530298ad6-450x333.png" alt="Ｃ2食光,皮蛋,蒜蓉酱" width="450" b="333" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">把皮蛋切成4半。（图： Ｃ2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 倒入沙拉油（或是香油）开火烧至微热，下蒜末、葱白先爆香，再加入辣椒、葱绿拌炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12045343" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-12045343 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6978b0c3e5d5fb45ef5cd88200ceccec-450x350.png" alt="Ｃ2食光,皮蛋,蒜蓉酱" width="450" b="350" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图： Ｃ2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 倒入酱油、蚝油、白胡椒粉、糖小火拌炒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12045347" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/29986f062308ec1a620bd236141819b1.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12045347" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/29986f062308ec1a620bd236141819b1-450x294.png" alt="Ｃ2食光,皮蛋,蒜蓉酱" width="450" b="294" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图： Ｃ2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 再将所有炒料淋在皮蛋上即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12045349" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/a1b268517dc18aa4ee1c05e4235e6b0b.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12045349" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/a1b268517dc18aa4ee1c05e4235e6b0b-450x252.png" alt="Ｃ2食光,皮蛋,蒜蓉酱" width="450" b="252" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图： Ｃ2食光）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【料理小技巧】</h3>
<p>皮蛋先稍微煮过能让蛋黄凝固，去除异味。</p>
