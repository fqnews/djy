<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】海参无花果小米粥～养胃助消化</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/10-23-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>海参无花果小米粥，是一道养胃健脾、助消化的粥品。（图：梁志生提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<h3>【小编语】</h3>
<p>早上来一碗养胃的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%B1%B3.md#1">小米</a>粥，一整天能量满满，还可以清肠润便！如果想要吃甜的，加一点枸杞、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E8%8A%B1%E6%9E%9C.md#1">无花果</a>，可以达到清热解毒的食疗效果；想吃咸的滋味，烫一些蔬菜、添加一点盐巴调味，也很好入口。这一道“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">海参</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%97%A0%E8%8A%B1%E6%9E%9C.md#1">无花果</a>小米粥”有高蛋白、低脂的海参，处理过程添加姜丝可以去腥，再配合鸡汤和其他食材共同熬煮，会释出甘甜汤头，是养胃健脾助消化的早餐粥。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #003300;">【材料】</span></h3>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%B1%B3.md#1">小米</a>1杯（Millet 1C）<br />
阿拉斯加<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E5%8F%82.md#1">海参</a>10安士（Sea cucumber 10oz）<br />
无花果3个（Figs 3pieces）<br />
姜丝一茶匙（Ginger 1t）<br />
杞子20粒（Goji berries 20pieces）<br />
葱花一条（Green onion 1strip）<br />
鸡汤一杯（Chicken borth 1C）<br />
水一杯（Water 1C）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12048654" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-65.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12048654 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/1-65-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,无花果海参小米粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图为小米粥、海参、枸杞、葱花、姜丝、无花果（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #003300;">【调味料】</span></h3>
<p>胡椒粉、美极酱油各少许（Pepper &amp; Maggie）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12048658" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-46.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12048658 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/2-46-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,无花果海参小米粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图为小米粥一碗、美极酱油、鸡汤一杯。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #003300;">【做法】</span></h3>
<p>1.小米洗干净后用两杯水浸泡20分钟，再煮15分钟备用。阿拉斯加海参切片，用姜汁酒烫过后沥干备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12048684" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-41.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12048684" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/3-41-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,无花果海参小米粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小米洗干净后用两杯水浸泡20分钟，再煮15分钟。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.无花果切小粒，姜切丝，杞子用水冲洗一下，沥干后备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12048689" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-24.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12048689 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/4-24-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,无花果海参小米粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">无花果切小粒；杞子用水冲洗一下，沥干后备用（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.锅内放入小米，再加入鸡汤和水一起煮开，接着加入无花果、海参和姜丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12048699" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-22.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12048699" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/5-22-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,无花果海参小米粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.轻轻搅拌均匀后，用中慢火煮约10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12048712" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-13.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12048712 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/6-13-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,无花果海参小米粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">轻轻搅拌均匀后，用中慢火煮约10分钟。（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.然后加入调味料、杞子及葱花，拌匀之后便可食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12048715" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12048715" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/7-14-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,无花果海参小米粥" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁志生提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3>【梁师傅小贴士】</h3>
<p>1. 小米是很好的食材，其含有维生素B并有清肠胃的作用。<br />
2. 用鸡汤作为小米粥的基底就已经很有风味了！稍为加入少许胡椒粉和美极酱油提味即可，记得不要加盐。<br />
3. 这道海参无花果小米粥是一道健康的营养早餐， 海参不仅零胆固醇又可清毒素，是一种非常好的海鲜食材。◇</p>
