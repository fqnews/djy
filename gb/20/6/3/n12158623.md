<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：草人</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/264822-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>稻草人变模特儿。（谢月琴／大纪元） 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>你我，谁是草人？<br />
你我，谁不是草人？</p>
<p>予人制造出来<br />
和真的人一模一样<br />
也穿得花红柳绿<br />
却无血无肉无目屎<br />
无肠无肚无心肝</p>
<p>不由自己而企在田间<br />
吓惊彼些不时<br />
想要偷食的鸟仔<br />
没暝没日，没吃没困</p>
<p>古早的时阵，草人到处可见<br />
因为彼阵是农业时代<br />
现在，稻田已经慢慢缩减<br />
草人渐渐没去了</p>
<p>草人渐渐没去了？<br />
真的吗？<br />
为主人虚张声势的草人<br />
真的会从人间没去吗？</p>
<p>你我，谁是草人？<br />
你我，谁不是草人？</p>
<p><strong>The Scarecrow</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>Either you or me, am not the scarecrow?<br />
Either you or me, am not the scarecrow?</p>
<p>Created by human<br />
The same as the true man<br />
Also clothes in flower red and willow green<br />
In the contrary, they are neither blood, flesh, tear<br />
Nor bowel, belly and liver</p>
<p>He can’t help to stand on the field by himself<br />
And scares the birds<br />
Which often come to take food on the shy<br />
In spite of day and night, neither takes meal nor sleep</p>
<p>There were scarecrows everywhere in ancient time<br />
Due to it’s the time of agriculture<br />
But the paddy field is reducing slowly now<br />
The scarecrow is passing away slowly</p>
<p>Is the scarecrow passing away slowly?<br />
Is it true?<br />
Will the scarecrow which barking dogs for the host<br />
Truly pass away from the world?</p>
<p>Either you or me, am the scarecrow?<br />
Either you or me, am not the scarecrow? @</p>
