<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽约Woodbury重开首日 华人访客占半数</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_124622-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>深受华人热捧的纽约上州Woodbury购物中心9日开始恢复营业。（萧静／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年06月10日讯】（大纪元记者萧静纽约报导）纽约市8日才开始第一阶段复工，纽约上州一些地区已进入第二阶段复工。9日，深受<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E4%BA%BA.md#1">华人</a>热捧的Woodbury购物中心也开始恢复营业。虽然只有不到一半的商家正式营业，访客也寥寥无几，但华人的身影占到了一半。</p>
<p>9日，暂停营业近三个月的Woodbury终于开始迎客，客流比刚关闭时还要冷清得多。平时最繁忙的Woodbury访客服务中心仍贴着暂时关闭的标签；公交站台没有停靠任何接驳巴士，周遭的访客停车场几乎九成被空置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12173864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_124141-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12173864 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_124141-2-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">纽约上州Woodbury访客服务中心仍处于暂时关闭，公交站台没有停靠接驳巴士。（萧静／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>购物中心内，几家<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E4%BA%BA.md#1">华人</a>热门的商铺几乎都未正式营业，有的还没拆掉封门的木板；有的虽没对外营业，但已有店员在店内整理柜台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12173868" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_120450-4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12173868 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_120450-4-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">KATE SPADE店仍是木板封门。（萧静／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12173870" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_120625-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12173870" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_120625-3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">COACH店仍是木板封门。（萧静／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>只有BURBERRY店门前，不少人顶着烈日在商店门口排起长队，其中华人占了一半。一位华人说，不管有没有折扣，就是想要来逛逛买买。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12173880" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_120745-7.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12173880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_120745-7-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">人们顶着烈日在BURBERRY店前排队。（萧静／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有两位华人边逛边驻足聊上几句，时而贴近玻璃橱窗往里看。他们说，听到Woodbury开业的消息很高兴，没有购物计划；知道不是全部商家开业，但还是想来看看商家情况。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12173885" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_120432-9.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12173885" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_120432-9-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">访客驻足看店内陈列。（萧静／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12173884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_115850-8.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12173884 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_115850-8-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一位华人趴在玻璃橱窗上看NIKE店内。（萧静／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>过去一周，纽约市爆发暴力骚乱，曼哈顿不少商家遭到洗劫，尤其6月1日当晚。之后网上热传，劫匪的下一个目标可能是纽约上州的Woodbury购物中心。</p>
<p>据Woodbury购物中心一位零售店工作人员透露，她们管理层上周三曾通知员工，为阻止商店遭洗劫，决定当晚连夜加装木板封店，原定的开业时间可能有变化。</p>
<p>Woodbury开业当天，有不少商家仍是木板封门，如COACH、SWAROVSKI、KATE、PANDORA、UGG。受华人欢迎的MK店，虽未用木板封门，但店内仍是清空状态。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12173890" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_114308-10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12173890" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_114308-10-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">CUCCI店内商品陈列已基本完成。（萧静／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12173893" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_121406-13.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12173893" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_121406-13-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">UGG仍木板封门。（萧静／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12173895" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_123834-12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12173895" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/20200609_123834-12-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">SWAROVSKI仍木板封门。（萧静／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
