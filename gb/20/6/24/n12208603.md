<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>浓醇酪梨凯萨沙拉～独家凯萨酱 减压轻食料理</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/c45d1f278ed1022227957ed781701158-600x400.png" />
<h6>夏季减压轻食，在家也可以轻松做出餐厅前菜料理～酪梨凯萨沙拉。（图：高宝出版社提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【小编按】</strong></span><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%AF%E8%90%A8%E6%B2%99%E6%8B%89.md#1">凯萨沙拉</a>的三大灵魂：凯萨酱、萝蔓莴苣、面包丁，缺一不可。凯萨酱先以腌鳀鱼和蒜头磨成泥状，再加入起司、沙拉油或奶油使用食物搅拌器打成均质浓稠酱汁（本道食谱有进行调整配方与比例），搭配上淸脆的莴苣与湿润的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>，不腻味又营养解热，一片一片的蔬菜让人不自觉的吃下去。这就是减压轻食的独特风味，很难抗拒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209860" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_272997344.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12209860" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_272997344-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">腌鳀鱼是意大利常见的食材，一般作为开胃菜、搭配生菜沙拉或是欧式面包食用，再淋上柠檬汁或加点罗勒就是地中海料理，因为味道浓郁，份量不用过多。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【材料】（2人份）</span></h2>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%85%AA%E6%A2%A8.md#1">酪梨</a>?1颗<br />
莴苣?1/2颗<br />
小番茄?1颗<br />
温泉蛋?1颗<br />
墨西哥玉米片（可用面包丁代替）?1片<br />
莳萝?依个人喜好</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209825" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_562218118.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12209825" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_562218118-450x363.jpg" alt="莴苣" width="450" b="363" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">莴苣又称为生菜，在西方习惯生吃，在亚洲地方大多是煮熟食用。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12209829" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_1235187985.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12209829" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_1235187985-450x298.jpg" alt="莳萝" width="450" b="298" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">莳萝味道清凉香甜，是北欧料理的灵魂食材，一般会磨碎后加入汤品、拌入生菜沙拉或海鲜中；可以去腥、装饰摆盘。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【A】</span></h2>
<p>腌鳀鱼（切碎）?1片<br />
半熟蛋（或是生蛋）?2颗<br />
白酒醋?2大匙<br />
格拉娜．帕达诺起司（或帕马森干酪）?2大匙<br />
Tabasco?1大匙（塔巴斯科辣椒酱）<br />
蒜头（磨成泥）?1/2瓣<br />
沙拉油?适量</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209826" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_1020269764.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12209826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_1020269764-450x300.jpg" alt="辣椒酱" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">塔巴斯科辣椒具有独特香气和辣度，酱汁除了烹饪、淋在熟食上，也可以用于腌渍或混合其他调酱，风味会更多层次。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1. 酪梨斜切成片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12208666" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_1284691024-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12208666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_1284691024-1-450x300.jpg" alt="酪梨" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">先将酪梨切成片状。（图／shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将【A】用食物调理机磨碎，打成酱料。<br />
3. 莴苣放上容器，将一半的酪梨插入莴苣中间，洒上切成4等分的小番茄。<br />
4. 在莴苣旁边打上温泉蛋，淋上步骤2。<br />
5. 剩下的酪梨摆至莴苣最上面，再洒上捏碎的墨西哥玉米片，最后用莳萝点缀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12209859" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_415089574.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12209859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/shutterstock_415089574-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">莴苣类蔬菜品种多样，西方人制作<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%AF%E8%90%A8%E6%B2%99%E6%8B%89.md#1">凯萨沙拉</a>主要以萝蔓莴苣作为搭配，因为它的口感清脆、水分较多；色白部分味清甜，绿色菜办偏苦，作为沙拉在风味上具变化性。（图：shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
