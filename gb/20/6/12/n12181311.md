<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>无法接受小三 蔡黄汝自曝断开闺密</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120920572384-ss1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（左起）乔雅琳、臧芮轩、程予希、蔡黄汝（豆花妹）、陈敬宣、谢翔雅6月12日在台北出席新戏《我的青春没在怕》卡司发布会。（陈柏州／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2020年06月12日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E9%BB%84%E6%B1%9D.md#1">蔡黄汝</a>（豆花妹）12日与<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E7%BF%94%E9%9B%85.md#1">谢翔雅</a>、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%A8%8B%E4%BA%88%E5%B8%8C.md#1">程予希</a>、陈敬宣、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%87%A7%E8%8A%AE%E8%BD%A9.md#1">臧芮轩</a>及乔雅琳亮相新戏《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%88%91%E7%9A%84%E9%9D%92%E6%98%A5%E6%B2%A1%E5%9C%A8%E6%80%95.md#1">我的青春没在怕</a>》记者会，众人畅聊闺密情谊与恋爱，也开气泡酒预祝收视开红盘，并为16日生日的程予希提前庆生。<br />
<figure id="attachment_12181393" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120920262384-ss1-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120920262384-ss1-1-600x399.jpg" alt="乔雅琳（左至右）、臧芮轩、程予希、蔡黄汝（豆花妹）、陈敬宣、谢翔雅" width="600" b="399" class="size-large wp-image-12181393" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">众人为16日生日的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%A8%8B%E4%BA%88%E5%B8%8C.md#1">程予希</a>（左3）提前庆生。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>聊起青春往事，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%94%A1%E9%BB%84%E6%B1%9D.md#1">蔡黄汝</a>表示每人在青春时期都会有一票闺密，大家可以相互倒垃圾，但以前曾有位闺密当了小三，那时她毫不考虑就切断关系，“可能我个性很直，加上感情有洁癖，无法接受小三，现在已经慢慢会看人了。”<br />
<figure id="attachment_12181336" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120923572384-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120923572384-ss1-600x399.jpg" alt="蔡黄汝" width="600" b="399" class="size-large wp-image-12181336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蔡黄汝（豆花妹）自认个性很直，感情有洁癖。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>外型亮丽的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B0%A2%E7%BF%94%E9%9B%85.md#1">谢翔雅</a>与蔡黄汝第一次合作拍戏，两人的男友正巧分别是乐团“八三夭”的主唱阿璞与鼓手阿电，而且两人的闺密也都曾介入别人感情。谢翔雅坦言曾有闺密不小心当了小三，气得她把闺密骂到哭。</p>
<p>程予希则是很有义气，高中好友向一位乐团学长告白被拒，这位学长却想追程予希，她当时虽然欣赏男方，但为了避免让好友尴尬，还是拒绝学长了；<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%87%A7%E8%8A%AE%E8%BD%A9.md#1">臧芮轩</a>也相当重视好友，曾开车赴桃园接对方到台北，一起喝酒听朋友说委屈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12181345" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120922402384-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120922402384-ss1.jpg" alt="谢翔雅" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12181345" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">谢翔雅出席新戏《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%88%91%E7%9A%84%E9%9D%92%E6%98%A5%E6%B2%A1%E5%9C%A8%E6%80%95.md#1">我的青春没在怕</a>》卡司发布会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12181343" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120923322384-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120923322384-ss1.jpg" alt="程予希" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12181343" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">程予希出席新戏《我的青春没在怕》卡司发布会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12181358" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120923022384-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120923022384-ss1.jpg" alt="臧芮轩" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12181358" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">臧芮轩出席新戏《我的青春没在怕》卡司发布会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12181351" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120923122384-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120923122384-ss1.jpg" alt="陈敬宣" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12181351" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">陈敬宣出席新戏《我的青春没在怕》卡司发布会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12181348" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120922292384-ss1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2006120922292384-ss1.jpg" alt="乔雅琳" width="450" b="600" class="size-full wp-image-12181348" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">乔雅琳出席新戏《我的青春没在怕》卡司发布会。（陈柏州／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>陈敬宣读书时期欣赏一位男同学，两人有些暧昧，她和闺密透露秘密。没想到有一天她发现闺密和男方越来越好，不仅每天晚上都通电话，甚至还把她的秘密告诉男生，让她好生气。</p>
<p>乔雅琳则笑说，拍戏时大家都会把一半便当饭给她，而且她的包包随时准备各种糖果，加上爱吃宵夜，都瘦不下来。</p>
