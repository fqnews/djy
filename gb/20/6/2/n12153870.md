<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>太空、军事、登山元素  腕表探险展MAN味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/78a5c5baaa3de9c719089a3cd15a0cca-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>万宝龙1858系列运动复古表款，向世界七大高峰致意。（品牌提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年06月02日讯】（大纪元记者林苏菲台湾台北报导）探险永远是男性的向往，以手表凸显个人帅劲风格，无论是跑车联名、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%AA%E7%A9%BA.md#1">太空</a>、航海还是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%86%9B%E4%BA%8B.md#1">军事</a>元素，在在展现狂肆不羁的想像力和登峰造极的设计美学。</p>
<p><strong>Roger Dubuis联名蓝宝坚尼</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12153872" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4f1baafc0bde9b797b729c11a702876e.png"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12153872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4f1baafc0bde9b797b729c11a702876e-450x636.png" alt="" width="450" b="636" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Roger Dubuis 罗杰杜彼再度与蓝宝坚尼合作，“Excalibur Huracán”自动上炼<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%85%95%E8%A1%A8.md#1">腕表</a>披着黑色盔甲上阵。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一向展现霸气的Roger Dubuis?罗杰杜彼，与意大利跑车蓝宝坚尼再度合作，为专属强劲“引擎”RD630机芯创造全新演绎，“Excalibur Huracán”自动上炼<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%85%95%E8%A1%A8.md#1">腕表</a>披着黑色盔甲上阵，以碳原子俐落的六角形形式为设计主轴，配上了独树一帜的12度倾角单游丝摆轮，运用缩影形式诠释<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E8%B7%91.md#1">超跑</a>的强烈视觉。</p>
<p><strong>ZENITH真力时联名LAND ROVER</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12153880" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/b2d8070eb56c6a2cba553b67b8c63e84.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12153880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/b2d8070eb56c6a2cba553b67b8c63e84-450x563.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">ZENITH真力时DEFY EL PRIMERO 21 LAND ROVER DEFENDER联名限量腕表。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12153881" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/15c9f861c7662ba47be5ee60d81201a7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12153881" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/15c9f861c7662ba47be5ee60d81201a7-450x643.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="643" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">ZENITH真力时DEFY EL PRIMERO 21 LAND ROVER DEFENDER联名限量腕表。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>ZENITH真力时则与英国汽车制造商LAND ROVER搭档，推出“DEFY EL PRIMERO 21 LAND ROVER DEFENDER”联名限量腕表，搭载原厂自制的高振频自动计时机芯，具有整合式结构、双炼机制和专利摆轮游丝，并配备最新的调节器和专利计时控制机制，这枚现代化机芯获得天文台认证，彰显了DEFENDER强悍坚韧的精神。</p>
<p><strong>MB&amp;F星际座钟探险</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12153879" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/26754b0271b55a9df823182914c12a0a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12153879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/26754b0271b55a9df823182914c12a0a-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">MB&amp;F与瑞士高级座钟制造商L’EPEE 1839 合推Starfleet Explorer<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%AA%E7%A9%BA.md#1">太空</a>站座钟。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>MB&amp;F与瑞士高级座钟制造商L’EPEE 1839?合作推出新作“Starfleet Explorer”，太空站座钟外型增加亮色细节，加入由三艘小型太空船组成的宇宙舰队，可以围绕太空站轨道运行，绕行一圈为五分钟，具备高达八天的动力储存，宛如穿梭银河间的星际探险队。</p>
<p><strong>万宝龙山岳探险</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12153876" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8f779226c3f9f7330aedc8385debe7b0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12153876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8f779226c3f9f7330aedc8385debe7b0-450x720.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="720" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">万宝龙1858系列腕表，结合自制游丝与手工整饰技术。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1858系列腕表为运动复古表款，设计灵感源于Minerva军用计时码表，结合自制游丝与手工整饰之高级制表技术，营造冷凛而前卫的工具腕表风格，向世界七大高峰致意。</p>
<p><strong>Victorinox瑞士刀<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%86%9B%E4%BA%8B.md#1">军事</a>美学</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_12153877" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6982945d73dc6e9f441e4deba1dd5afc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12153877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6982945d73dc6e9f441e4deba1dd5afc-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">瑞士刀品牌Victorinox推出全新FieldForce系列GMT腕表。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12153878" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3bf2361cc72b1196f645c227a09c94a6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12153878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3bf2361cc72b1196f645c227a09c94a6-450x305.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="305" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">瑞士刀品牌Victorinox推出全新FieldForce系列GMT腕表。（品牌提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>瑞士刀品牌Victorinox推出全新FieldForce系列GMT腕表，五大系列表款风格各异，包括独特的数字显示、加大指针、耐刮、防水可达100米，展现军事野战血统与硬派风格。◇</p>
