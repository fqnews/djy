<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>日式茶碗蒸 黄金蛋液比例 蒸出滑嫩口感</h2>

<h6>滑嫩口感的日式茶碗蒸，秘诀就在蛋液比例。（图：Ｃ2食光提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E7%A2%97%E8%92%B8.md#1">茶碗蒸</a>”是日本妈妈必备的家常料理，汤汁里锁进满满食材，香菇、海鲜、鱼板或山茼莴，小小一碗就有浓郁的鲜味。滑嫩细致口感可是小朋友的最爱呢！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12203418" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/b7c56c2d3bc3ea8b39d9de4f5b0a13b4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12203418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/b7c56c2d3bc3ea8b39d9de4f5b0a13b4-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【料理小诀窍】</span></h2>
<p>蛋与高汤的比例约莫1：2，调和后过筛，蒸蛋会更滑嫩细致喔！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【食材】</span></h2>
<p>香菇适量<br />
鱼板3~4片<br />
蛤蛎3~4颗<br />
山茼莴 1小片</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【蛋液比例】</span></h2>
<p>蛋3颗<br />
酱油 15cc<br />
味醂 10cc<br />
昆布高汤300cc</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">【作法】</span></h2>
<p>1. 将蛋打散过筛，加入上述所有调味料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12203415" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/38b0f4451a352872ec4a88847c5bcf10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12203415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/38b0f4451a352872ec4a88847c5bcf10-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将香菇切丝。</p>
<p>3. 煮热水，将蛤蛎、银杏、鱼板烫过。</p>
<p>4. 拿<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8C%B6%E7%A2%97%E8%92%B8.md#1">茶碗蒸</a>杯，放入香菇、蛤蛎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12203416" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/a8082da2352e5d68262c582aed47b67e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12203416" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/a8082da2352e5d68262c582aed47b67e-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 倒入蛋液。<br />
6. 蒸熟后约6-8分，将鱼板、山茼莴放在表面即可。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12203417" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/643534abcfb345350da3e86bbe9a76fa.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12203417 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/643534abcfb345350da3e86bbe9a76fa-450x315.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：Ｃ2食光提供）</figcaption></figure>
