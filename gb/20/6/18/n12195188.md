<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【梁厨美食】奶油蒜蓉酱拌鱼面～百搭主厨沾酱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/11-8-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>夏日容易食欲不振吗？这一道“奶油蒜蓉酱拌鱼面”蒜香与奶香交织，浓郁可口抚慰你浮躁的心。（图：梁厨美食提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>【小编按】</strong></span>炎热难耐的夏日，总是有点食欲不振！来一盘味道香浓、蒜头与鲜奶的交织，有点辛香辣却带点甜的面食。这一道“奶油<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%92%9C%E8%93%89%E9%85%B1.md#1">蒜蓉酱</a>拌<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E9%9D%A2.md#1">鱼面</a>”兼具中式鱼面和西式调酱，却一点都不违和。奶油<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%92%9C%E8%93%89%E9%85%B1.md#1">蒜蓉酱</a>汁也可以和鸡肉、意大利面共同料理；或是抹在吐司、法国面包上烘烤，也是相当对味！一举多得，赶快把“主厨<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B2%BE%E9%85%B1.md#1">沾酱</a>”收纳入家常料理名单吧！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【主材料】</strong></span></h2>
<p>蒜蓉酱1.5杯（Gralic Sauce 1.5 C）<br />
小西兰花3安士（Sweet Baby Broccoli 3 oz）<br />
<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%B1%BC%E9%9D%A2.md#1">鱼面</a>6安士（Fish Noodles 6 oz）</p>
<figure id="attachment_12195253" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-26.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12195253" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-26-450x338.jpg" alt="梁厨美食,奶油蒜蓉酱拌鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【蒜蓉酱材料】</strong></span></h2>
<p>蒜头三颗（Whole Garlic 3 pieces）<br />
新鲜蒜粒2.5安士（Fresh cloves of garlic 2.5 oz）<br />
鲜奶1.5杯（Milk 1.5 C）<br />
熟马铃薯1个切小块（Steamed potato 1 piece cut in diced）<br />
橄榄油、胡椒、盐适量<br />
鸡粉1茶匙<br />
糖1茶匙</p>
<figure id="attachment_12195255" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-18.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12195255" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-18-450x338.jpg" alt="奶油蒜蓉酱,梁厨美食,鱼面" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A2%81%E5%8E%A8%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md#1">梁厨美食</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【蒜蓉酱作法】</strong></span></h2>
<p>1. ?整颗蒜头切去尾部四分之一，剥去多余的外皮，滴上橄榄油，撒上少许黑胡椒和盐，用锡箔纸包着，放入烤箱用400℉烤45分钟，把烤好的蒜子肉取出来， 放在碗中备用。</p>
<p>2. ?马铃薯放入蒸锅蒸约30分钟到熟透，准备一个果汁搅拌机。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12195256" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12195256" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-14-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马铃薯放入蒸锅蒸到熟透。（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. ?把鲜奶及新鲜蒜粒放在锅中煮约15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12195257" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12195257" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-11-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲜奶及新鲜蒜粒放在锅中煮约15分钟。（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. ?将鲜奶和蒜粒倒进搅拌机中，加入烤好的蒜粒和马铃薯，打成绵滑状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12195259" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12195259" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. ?取出再加入盐半茶匙、鸡粉1茶匙和糖1茶匙拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12195260" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12195260" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-8-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>【奶油蒜蓉酱拌鱼面】</strong></span></h2>
<p>1. ?小西兰花清洗干净，只摘取花的部分，用油盐水烫过后，捞出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12195261" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12195261 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. ?鱼面用水煮3分钟后捞出沥干。加热奶油蒜蓉酱，把菜及鱼面放进拌匀后便可上碟食用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12195262" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-12195262 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-5-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（图：梁厨美食提供）</figcaption></figure>
