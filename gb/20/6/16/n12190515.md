<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】脆皮溏心咕咾肉</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5677db310848a6d0794b6105e6dc7519-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>肉卷爽口香浓，而且没有肥腻的感觉，加之中间部位的菠萝鲜甜可口，配以蔗糖、柠檬天然的甜味和酸味，使整道菜再现传统的风味。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>据说，咕咾肉是国外知名度排行榜位列第一的中国菜。咕咾肉，也称“咕噜肉”、“古老肉”，这个名称的由来还有些典故。说法一：这道菜甜酸开胃，端上桌时香气四溢，令人禁不住“咕噜咕噜”地吞咽口水，因而得名。说法二：这道菜式里的肉块，外酥里嫩，鲜甜溜滑，乍一入口，未及咀嚼，便已“咕噜”一声穿喉入腹，唯唇齿留香，令人惊叹不已。</p>
<p>这道古老的菜肴演变至今，主料、配料、做法都有很大的选择空间，譬如主料中的肉，有人选脱骨排骨、里脊肉。罗厨习惯选用肥廋相宜的五花肉。因为五花肉的肥肉可以增加滑嫩的口感，加入糖炸过以后的肥肉部分，也不会显得太油腻。</p>
<p>罗厨将传统的咕咾肉增添了新的风味，制作成口味独特的“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%84%86%E7%9A%AE%E6%BA%8F%E5%BF%83%E5%92%95%E5%92%BE%E8%82%89.md#1">脆皮溏心咕咾肉</a>”。将菠萝夹在经过高度白酒与砂糖腌制过的五花肉片中，炸至酥脆，放入菠萝制成的容器中，色泽鲜亮，香气四溢。肉卷爽口香浓，而且没有肥腻的感觉，加之中间部位的菠萝鲜甜可口，配以蔗糖、柠檬天然的甜味和酸味，使整道菜再现传统的风味。</p>
<p>罗厨介绍：“咕咾肉是甜酸口味，要求酸甜适中。要想达到‘酸甜适中’的话，没有底味、没有盐，就做不到‘适中’，没有盐的时候糖会腻，那个酸会让人觉得接受不了，所以酸甜要有盐，要达到它们溶在一起恰到好处，所以这就考验厨师对调味、对底味的处理等等方面的功夫。”</p>
<p>罗厨特别强调，做这道“咕咾肉”有两个必不可少的小窍门。首先，五花肉片要先腌制半小时，这样肉炸过后会变得酥脆。另外，糖醋汁要先放，再将肉下锅，不然会吸附太多调料，影响酥脆感。</p>
<h2><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%84%86%E7%9A%AE%E6%BA%8F%E5%BF%83%E5%92%95%E5%92%BE%E8%82%89.md#1">脆皮溏心咕咾肉</a></h2>
<p>主料：菠萝1个、五花肉1块（0.5磅）。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/c831558d6505f104307a09b938630291.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190523" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/c831558d6505f104307a09b938630291-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/86787a4f5946f89f1bf543598d5f0d6e.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190524" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/86787a4f5946f89f1bf543598d5f0d6e-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
配料：小姜片、红椒、黄椒</p>
<p>调料：<br />
1. 生粉、盐、白酒、白糖、蛋清<br />
2. 清水、白醋、柠檬、片糖、老抽</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将菠萝洗干净后去皮。<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-25.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190525" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/1-25-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
2. 将菠萝肉取出切成小块，然后将菠萝变成一个容器。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-17.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190528" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2-17-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
3. 将五花肉去皮，直刀切成薄片。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190529" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/3-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
4. 用调料1腌制五花肉片30分钟。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-10.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190530" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/4-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
5. 用五花肉包住菠萝卷成小卷。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190531" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/5-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
6. 将五花肉小卷滚上生粉。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190533" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/6-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
7. 将肉卷炸至浅金黄色，外面酥脆为好。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190534" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/7-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
8. 将配料用盐炒香后捞起待用。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190537" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/8-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
9. 将调料2调成糖醋汁。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190538" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/9-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
10. 把所有原料放入锅中炒匀。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/10-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190539" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/10-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><br />
11. 将炒好的食材放入菠萝容器中。<br />
<ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/11-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12190540" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/11-7-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
