<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>看新闻，学英语(65)中国雪灾(四)</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/f47a53f77b07e0b855f012440393667b-320x200.png" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>大家好：</p>
<p>中国这次发生的雪灾，其严重性很可能远远超出我们所能想像的。官方在2月2日所公布的死亡数字是60人。但是在湖南省，高速公路上被冰冻的汽车长达数十公里，很多停在中间的汽车已经和地面冻成了一体，仅仅一条公路上，车祸及在长途客车上死亡的人员就已经超过二百人。</p>
<p> 今天我们来讲解1月30日新唐人电视台报导中国雪灾打击经济的英语新闻。大家先自行看一下新闻播报稿，看看自己能了解多少。</p>
<p><B>Market Report &#8211; China Snows Hit Economy</B><br /> Wed, 30 Jan 2008 10:04:00<br /> <ahref=http://english.ntdtv.com/authors/?authorID=7>NTDTV </a> </p>
<p><B>ZHANG: </B><br />Snow, ice and freezing temperatures. Chaotic winter weather is reeking havoc on China&#8217;s economy. Millions of people are suffering power cuts and water shortages in the winter chill. </p>
<p>Analysts say the current fuel shortages in more than half of China&#8217;s provinces are partially due to misplaced government policy. The state-controlled low electricity tariffs have left power generators running at a loss, with little excess funds to tackle the extreme weather. The electricity grid, a relic of earlier times, is collapsing under the pressure while limited market supplies are pushing up inflation. </p>
<p>Transportation is also taking a hit, as China&#8217;s roads, supplies and passengers are snowed under by the brutal winter weather.</p>
<p>Zhuang Jian, Senior Economist at the Asia Development Bank predicts that the snow will have a domino effect on other aspects of the economy.</p>
<p>[Zhuang Jian, Senior Economist at Asia Development Bank]:<br />&#8220;I think its comparable to the 1998 flooding or 2003 SARS and the past two crises had a great influence on the Chinese economy, of course it was a short term impact.&#8221; </p>
<p>Inflation in China already stands at an 11 year high, but many supplies are unable reach their destinations. </p>
<p>While state controlled fuel prices remain low, prices not under government control are set to rise. </p>
<p>China&#8217;s economy is experiencing an inconvenient truth&#8211;when disasters such as the extreme winter weather combine with failed government policy, many are left victims at the mercy of the market.</p>
<p><B>【主播稿讲解】</B></p>
<p> Snow, ice and freezing temperatures. Chaotic winter weather is reeking havoc on China&#8217;s economy. Millions of people are suffering power cuts and water shortages in the winter chill.<br /><B>【中译】</B>中国大陆正在发生的是雪、冰与零下的温度。混乱的冬季气侯正在造成对中国经济的损害。数百万人正在寒冷的冬季中遭逢停电、缺水。</p>
<p> <B>【讲解】</B>To reek means to send out vapor or fumes, usually with a strong unpleasant odor. “reek”可以当名词，也可以当动词。当名词时就是水汽（vapor）、蒸气（steam）或臭气（a disagreeable odor）；当动词时就是“发散不好的臭气”之意。</p>
<p>Havoc: Wide and general destruction; devastation. 广泛与普遍的损害；重创。</p>
<p>所以 to reek havoc 也就是造成损害之意。</p>
<p><B>【原文】</B>Analysts say the current fuel shortages in more than half of China&#8217;s provinces are partially due to misplaced government policy. <br /><B>【中译】</B>分析家说，目前在中国一半以上的省份燃料短缺的情况，部分是由于不当的政府政策。</p>
<p><B>【讲解】</B>To misplace means to put in a wrong place. 所以 misplaced 就是错放的、误植的。</p>
<p><B>【原文】</B>The state-controlled low electricity tariffs have left power generators running at a loss, with little excess funds to tackle the extreme weather. <br /><B>【中译】</B>国营的低电费已让发电厂的营运亏损，只有很少的剩余资金可以应付这样极端的气候。</p>
<p><B>【讲解】</B> Tariff一般作“关税”解，这里是当“Any schedule of rates, charges, etc.”因此electricity tariff就是“电费费率”的意思。</p>
<p>To tackle means to solve, to settle, to cope with, to deal with. 因此“tackle”这个动词就是解决、处理、应付之意。</p>
<p><B>【原文】</B>The electricity grid, a relic of earlier times, is collapsing under the pressure while limited market supplies are pushing up inflation. <br /><B>【中译】</B>在大雪的压力之下，如同是早期遗物的电力输送系统正在崩塌，然而同时间有限的市场供给，却正在推高通膨。</p>
<p><B>【讲解】</B>“ grid ” 一般当“栅栏”解，这里是指“高压线输电系统”。英文解释为：A grid is a system of high-voltage power lines serving a large area.</p>
<p> “relic” 就是遗迹、遗物、古迹之意。Historic relic就是古迹。The electricity grid isa relic of earlier times. 就是说“高压线输电系统已是如同早期遗物一般非常老旧”。</p>
<p><B>【原文】</B>Transportation is also taking a hit, as China&#8217;s roads, supplies and passengers are snowed under by the brutal winter weather.<br /><B>【中译】</B>交通也正受到打击，因为中国的道路、供给与乘客，在严酷的冬季气侯下被雪所覆盖。</p>
<p><B>【原文】</B>Zhuang Jian, Senior Economist at the Asia Development Bank predicts that the snow will have a domino effect on other aspects of the economy.<br /><B>【中译】</B>亚洲开发银行的资深经济学家Zhuang Jian预测，大雪将会对经济的其他方面造成骨牌效应。</p>
<p><B>【讲解】</B>The Domino effect：when something, usually something bad, happens and causes other similar events to happen, like each of a set of dominoes knocking the next one over. 骨牌效应。</p>
<p><B>【原文】</B>&#8220;I think its comparable to the 1998 flooding or 2003 SARS and the past two crises had a great influence on the Chinese economy, of course it was a short term impact.&#8221; <br /><B>【中译】</B>“我认为这场雪灾可比拟1998年的大洪水，或是2003年的蕯斯，过去这两场危机，对中国经济都造成了很巨大的影响，当然这是短期冲击。”</p>
<p><B>【原文】</B>Inflation in China already stands at an 11 year high, but many supplies are unable reach their destinations. <br /><B>【中译】</B>在中国，通膨已站上11年来的最高点，但是许多的供给却无法到达目的地。</p>
<p><B>【原文】</B>While state controlled fuel prices remain low, prices not under government control are set to rise. <br /><B>【中译】</B>同时国营燃料价格仍然很低，不受政府控制的价格预定会提高。</p>
<p><B>【原文】</B>China&#8217;s economy is experiencing an inconvenient truth&#8211;when disasters such as the extreme winter weather combine with failed government policy, many are left victims at the mercy of the market.<br /><B>【中译】</B>中国经济正在经历一场不愿面对的真相：当像这样极端气侯伴随无效的政府政策所酿成的灾难，许多人将在市场的摆布下沦为受害者。</p>
<p><B>【讲解】</B>“An inconvenient truth”就是前美国总统高尔所拍的纪录片的片名：不愿面对的真相。Convenient是“方便的”，那么加上否定字首in-就是“不方便的”，Disadvantageous; giving trouble or annoyance; inopportune. </p>
<p>“An inconvenient truth”就是指不方便说的真相，说了会带给人麻烦或让某些人恼怒的真相，因此一般就译成“不愿面对的真相”，也有译成“难以忽视的真相”。</p>
<p>At the mercy of: in the power of; defenceless against. 任由&#8230;摆布；完全受&#8230;左右。</p>
