  <p>面粉 300克</p>
  <p>葱4-5根 切成葱花</p>
  <p>橄榄油 (有的会放猪油，会很香，很酥，也很油…为了健康自己来做)</p>
  <p>盐</p>
  <p><b>做法： </b></p>
  <p>(一) 活面：</p>
  <p>170cc 的水 (2/3 在锅中烧开) 1/3的冷水，将面活好，揉到面光均匀，搁置一小时以上 (醒面)。</p>
  <p>(二) 前制工作：</p>
  <p>1.分成六等份，每一等份压扁，杆面棍杆到摊成最薄状态。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/804040728121983-450x346.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="346"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7845832" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">要杆出的薄面皮（摄影：祝大姐/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>2.将油均匀的沾到每一处(边缘一定要有油)，用手抓一小撮盐，由上均匀的洒在面皮上，葱花也均匀的洒在面皮上。<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/804040728131983-450x356.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="356"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7845833" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">洒上油+盐+葱花（摄影：祝大姐/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>3.自边缘细细的卷成一长条状，再轻轻的拉的更长些。<br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/804040728141983-450x324.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="324"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7845834" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">由上往回卷（摄影：祝大姐/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>4.一端平面的卷回成一小圆饼，尾端用杆面棍杆平后压在底层，成小圆饼状不会散开。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/804040728151983-450x356.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="356"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7845835" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">卷好的样子（摄影：祝大姐/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --><br /><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/804040728161983-450x314.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="314"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7845836" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">拉长的程度（摄影：祝大姐/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/804040731581983-450x314.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="314"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7845837" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">卷妥的葱油卷（摄影：祝大姐/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>(三) 收藏方法：	</p>
  <p>六个全做完后，自第一开始用杆面棍，杆成所要的大小，大约15公分的圆，用分隔纸做隔层，可以叠起来收藏，大小可随意，叠起来收入冷冻柜。</p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/804040731591983-450x335.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="335"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7845838" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">六个饼都卷妥（摄影：祝大姐/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <table border=0 align=center>  <tr valign=top>  <td>  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/804040732001983-450x358.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="358"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7845839" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">家常饼杆出15公分大小（摄影：祝大姐/大纪元）</span></div>  </td>  <td>  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/804040732011983-450x358.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="358"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7845840" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">杆出15公分大小（摄影：祝大姐/大纪元）</span></div>  </td>  </tr>  </table>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p><!--image v 1.0--></p>
  <div style="line-height: 90%; text-align: center;">  	<img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2008/09/804040732021983-450x358.jpg" alt="" title="" width="450" b="358"  	class="size-medium wp-image-7845841" /></a><br /><span class="bn12">分隔储存放入冷冻柜（摄影：祝大姐/大纪元）</span></div>  <p><!-- --></p>
  <p>(四) 现做现吃：	</p>
  <p>也可同时杆好一张，即时下锅，中小火，少许的油，将饼放在锅内，饼面变色时，翻一个面待金黄色时即完成了。(重点是面皮杆的薄，卷的层次较多，相对就有好口感了)冷热均可口呢！(家常饼，不需葱花，做法相同。)＠＊</p>
