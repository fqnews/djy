  <p>BASIC EXPRESSIONS基本句型表达</p>
  <p>1) How do you think of your English?<br />你认为你的英语水准如何？</p>
  <p>2) How do you think of your proficiency in written and spoken English?<br />你认为你的书面英语和口语熟练程度如何？</p>
  <p>3) I think my English is good enough to communicate with English speaking people.<br />我认为我能用英语和说英语国家的人很好的交流。</p>
  <p>4) What other foreign language do you speak?<br />你还能说什么其他外语吗？</p>
  <p>5) I have a good command of <br />我精通……</p>
  <p>6) Have you obtained any certificate of technical qualifications?<br />你获得过什么技术证书吗？</p>
  <p>7) What special skills do you have, can you tell me?<br />你能告诉我你有什么特殊技能吗？</p>
  <p>8) Have you gotten any special training in &#8230;?<br />你接受过……方面的特殊培训吗？</p>
  <p>CONVERSATIONS会话<br />（A=Applicant I=Interviewer）<br />Dialogue 1<br />I:Our advertisement says English competence is a key requirement of this position. Then how do you think of your proficiency in written and spoken English?<br />A:I have learned English for 10 years, and I have passed College English Test Band 4 and 6.My spoken English is fairly good enough to express myself fluently.<br />I:What other foreign language do you speak?<br />A:I did self-study of Japanese in college, and I can carry on some simple conversations in Japanese.<br />I: 我们的招聘广告要求这个职位的应聘者应当具有相当好的英语水准，那么，你认为你的书面英语和口语能力如何呢？<br />A: 我已经学英语10年了，而且我通过了大学英语四级和六级。我能用英语口语把自己的想法流利地表达出来。<br />I: 你还能说其他别的外语吗？<br />A: 我在大学时自学过日语，我能用日语进行一些简单的对话。</p>
  <p>Dialogue 2<br />I:Have you obtained any certificate of technical qualifications or license?<br />A:Yes, I have received an Engineer抯 Qualification Certificate and a driver抯 license.<br />I:How many years have you had the driver抯 license?<br />A:I have two yearsdriving experience.<br />I:That抯 good. What special skills do you have, can you tell me?<br />A:I have experience in computer operation，proficiency in Microsoft Windows，Microsoft Word and Microsoft Excel．<br />I:What computer languages have you learned?<br />A:Visual C++, C and Delphi.<br />I:Have you gotten any special training in programming?<br />A:No, but I have database programming experience and network knowledge.<br />I: 你获得过技术资格证书或者执照吗？<br />A: 是，我有工程师资格证书和驾照。<br />I: 你获得驾照多长时间了？<br />A: 我已经有两年的驾龄了。<br />I: 好，那你能告诉我你有什么特殊技能吗？<br />A: 我有电脑操作经验，熟悉微软Windows，Word和Excel。<br />I: 你学过哪种电脑语言？<br />A: Visual C++, C 和Delphi。<br />I: 你在编程方面受到过特殊培训吗？<br />A: 没有，但是我有资料库编程经验并具有网路知识。</p>
  <p>proficiency熟练程度；精通<br />fluently 流利地，通畅地<br />carry on进行<br />qualification 资格<br />certificate证书；证明书 <br />license 许可证，执照<br />programming 编程 <br />datebase 资料库</p>
  <p>在叙述个人技能时,，如果是熟悉的话，还应该恰到好处地回答你用C++做过什么，这是他们最感兴趣的。在回答做过哪些专案时，要讲清楚当时是担任什么职务、承担哪部分工作，用过哪些硬体、软体等。<br />1) I can speakEnglish.<br />French<br />Spanish<br />German<br /> 我会讲 英语。<br /> 法语<br /> 西班牙语<br /> 德语</p>
  <p>2) I have received an Engineer Qualification Certificate.<br /> an Accountant Qualification Certificate<br /> a Computer Operation Qualification Certificate<br /> a Tourist Guide Qualification Certificate</p>
  <p> 我获得了 工程师资格证书。<br /> 会计师资格证书<br /> 电脑操作证书<br /> 导游证书</p>
  <p>3) Iam accomplished in programming.<br />am well up in<br />am proficient in<br />have a good command of<br /> 我编程很熟练。<br /> 我精通编程<br /> 我精通编程<br /> 我精通编程</p>
  <p>4) I have experience incomputer operation.<br />am experienced in<br />am skillful with<br /> 我具有电脑操作经验。<br /> 我对电脑操作很有经验。<br />我的电脑操作技术很好。</p>
