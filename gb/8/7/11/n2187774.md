  <p>BASIC EXPRESSIONS基本句型表达</p>
  <p>1) What is your name，please？<br />请问，你叫什么名字？</p>
  <p>2) Can you tell me what your full name is, please?<br />能把你的全名告诉我吗？</p>
  <p>3) How do you spell your full name?<br />你的全名怎么拼写？</p>
  <p>4) I was born on June 22,1980.<br />我生于1980年6月22日。</p>
  <p>5) You look very young．How old are you?<br />你看上去很年轻，你多大了？</p>
  <p>6) I am just over twenty-two.<br />我刚过22岁。</p>
  <p>7) Where are you from？<br />你是哪里人？</p>
  <p>8) Where is your native place？<br />你的籍贯是哪里？</p>
  <p>9) Where do you live now?<br />你现在住哪里？</p>
  <p>10) I live at 606 Zhongguancun Road, Apt 802,Beijing.<br />我住在北京市中关村大街606号，802单元。</p>
  <p>11) Can you tell me something about your family?<br />能介绍一下你的家庭情况吗？</p>
  <p>12) Are you married？<br />你结婚了吗？</p>
  <p>13) How long have you been married?<br />你结婚多长时间了？</p>
  <p>14) I have been married for two years.<br />我结婚两年了。</p>
  <p>CONVERSATIONS会话（A=Applicant I=Interviewer）</p>
  <p>Dialogue 1<br />I:What is your name, please?<br />A:My name is Sun lin.<br />I:How old are you?<br />A:I am twenty-five years old.<br />I:May I ask your birth date?<br />A:Yes, my birth date is September 22, 1977.<br />I:Where do you live now?<br />A:I live at 606 Zhongguancun Road, Apt 802,Beijing.<br />I:Then tell me your birthplace.<br />A:My birthplace is Nanjing.<br />I:Ok,I know you are not a resident of Beijing. Where is your domicile place then?<br />A:My domicile place is Nanjing,too.<br />I: 请问，你叫什么名字？<br />A: 我叫孙林。<br />I: 你多大了？<br />A: 25岁。<br />I: 我可以问问你的生日吗？<br />A: 可以，我的生日是1977年9月22日。<br />I: 你现在住哪里呢？<br />A: 我住北京市中关村大街606号，802单元。<br />I: 你的出生地是？<br />A: 我的出生地是南京。<br />I: 好的，我知道你不是北京人。那你的原籍是哪里？<br />A: 我的原籍也是南京。</p>
  <p>Dialogue 2<br />I:Mr.Sun,would you mind if I ask you some questions, which may sound impolite?<br />A:Ah,never mind, please.<br />I:Can you tell me how old you are?<br />A:I am 25 years old.<br />I:Are you married?<br />A:No, I am still single.<br />I:Can you tell me something about your family?<br />A:Ok.There are four persons in my family, my parents, my elder sister and me.<br />I: 孙先生，我可以问你几个听起来可能不是很有礼貌的问题吗？<br />A: 啊，没关系，请问吧。<br />I: 你能告诉我你多大了吗？<br />A: 我25岁了。<br />I: 你结婚了吗？<br />A: 没有，我还是单身。<br />I: 那你能告诉我你家里的一些情况吗？<br />A: 好的，我家四口人，我父母，我姐姐和我。</p>
  <p>Dialogue 3<br />I:Tell me a little bit about yourself, please.<br />A:My name is Sunlin and I live in Beijing. I was born in 1980. I will graduate from Peking University this July. I have majored in accounting.<br />I:Would you tell me something about your family?<br />A:There are three in my family, my parents and myself. My father is a teacher and my mother is a worker.<br />I: 能告诉我一些你自己的情况吗？<br />A: 我叫孙林，住在北京。我出生于1980年。今年七月将从北京大学毕业，我主修的专业是会计学。<br />I: 能说说你的家庭情况吗？<br />A: 我们家三口人，我父母和我。我父亲是一位教师，我母亲是一名工人。</p>
  <p>WORDS AND EXPRESSIONS关键字<br />birthplace出生地 resident居民，居住者<br />domicile （法律）原籍sound听起来<br />single单身的married结婚的 <br />NOTES注释<br />根据这些资讯，主考官可以初步断定应试者是否符合他们的要求，比如有些工作只适合本地人来做；有些工作需要经常出差，单身的人就有优势。但是有些涉及个人隐私，自己不便于说的，比如主考官问你：I noticed you抮e wearing a wedding ring. Do you have any children?这时你最好的回答就是Why do you ask?，这样可以使你掌握主动，使主考官把他真正的意图说出来，比如也许他是在试探你是否能长时间留在他们公司。<br />1) Can you tell meyour name，please？<br /> Can I have<br /> What is<br /> 请问你能告诉我你的名字吗？<br /> 请问我能知道你的名字吗？<br /> 请问你叫什么名字。</p>
  <p>2) How old are you？ 你多大了？<br /> What is your age？<br />May I ask your age?</p>
  <p>3)What抯 your birthday? 你的出生日是？<br />When is your birth date? 你哪一年出生？<br />When were you born? 你哪一年出生？ </p>
  <p>4) Whereare you from？<br /> Wheredo you come from?<br /> Whatis your hometown? <br /> 你来自哪里？<br /> 你来自哪里？<br />你的老家是哪里？</p>
  <p>5) Where is your nativeplace?<br /> domicile<br /> permanent<br /> 你老家是哪里？<br /> 你现在住在哪里？<br /> 你永久性的居住地是哪里？</p>
