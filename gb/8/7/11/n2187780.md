  <p>兴趣爱好有助于工作的胜任愉快，而且热爱本职，从事的专业岗位与自己的兴趣爱好相吻合也更有利于出成果。</p>
  <p>BASIC EXPRESSIONS基本句型表达</p>
  <p>1) What kind of character do you think you have?<br />你认为自己是什么性格的人？</p>
  <p>2) Are you introverted or extroverted?<br />你内向还是外向？</p>
  <p>3) What kind of person would you like to work with?<br />你喜欢和什么样的人一起工作呢？</p>
  <p>4) -How do you get along with others?<br />你和别人相处得怎样？<br /> -I get on well with others.<br />我和别人相处得很好。</p>
  <p>5) Do you have any particular strengths or weaknesses?<br />你有什么特别的优点和缺点吗？</p>
  <p>6) What basic principles do you apply to your life?<br />你生活中的基本原则是什么？</p>
  <p>7) How do you spend your spare time?<br />你的业余时间怎么度过？</p>
  <p>8) What kind of sports do you like most?<br />你最喜欢什么运动？</p>
  <p>9) What are your hobbies?<br />你的业余爱好是什么？</p>
  <p>10）I have an interest in traveling.<br />我对旅游非常感兴趣。</p>
