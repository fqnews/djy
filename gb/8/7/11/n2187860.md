  <p>BASIC EXPRESSIONS基本句型表达</p>
  <p>1） What degree will you receive?<br />你将拿到什么学位？</p>
  <p>2） I will receive a Bachelor’s degree.<br />我将获得学士学位。</p>
  <p>3） How about your academic records at college?<br />你大学的成绩如何？</p>
  <p>4） I have been doing quite well at college.<br />我在大学时学习很好。</p>
  <p>5） My specialization at the university is just in line with the areas your institute deals with.<br />我在大学所学的专业和你们研究所所涉及的范围刚好对口。</p>
  <p>6） I was one of the top students in the class.<br />我是班里最优秀的学生之一。</p>
  <p>7） Which course did you like best?<br />你最喜欢哪门课程？</p>
  <p>CONVERSATIONS会话<br />（A=Applicant I=Interviewer）<br />Dialogue 1<br />I:Which school are you attending?<br />A:I am attending Hebei University of Technology.<br />I: When will you graduate from that university?<br />A:This coming July.<br />I:What degree will you receive?<br />A:I will receive a Bachelor抯 degree.<br />I:What is your major?<br />A:My major is Business Administration.<br />I:How have you been getting on with your studies so far？<br />A:I have been doing quite well at college. According to the academic records I抳e achieved so far, I am confident that I will get my Bachelor of Business Administration this coming July.<br />I:How do you think the education you抳e received will contribute to your work in this institution？<br />A:I have already learned a lot in the classroom and I hope to be able to make practical use of it in your company. My specialization at the university is just in line with the areas your institute deals with. I am sure I can apply what I have learned to the work in your institute.<br />I: 你在哪个学校上学？<br />A: 我在河北工业大学上学。<br />I: 你什么时候毕业？<br />A: 今年七月。<br />I: 你能拿到什么学位？<br />A: 学士学位。<br />I: 你是什么专业的？<br />A: 我的专业是商业管理。<br />I: 到目前为止，你在校的学习情况如何？<br />A: 我学得不错。根据至今我所取得的学习成绩，我敢肯定今年7月份我一定能获得商业管理学士学位。<br />I: 你认为你接受的教育将如何有助于本机构的工作？<br />A: 我在课堂上学了很多东西，我希望能把它实际运用到贵公司的商务活动中去。我在大学所学的专业和你们研究所所涉及的范围刚好对口。 我相信我能够把我所学到的东西运用到你们研究所的工作之中。</p>
  <p>Dialogue 2<br />I:Which university did you graduate from?<br />A:I graduated from Hebei University.<br />I:What subject did you major in at university?<br />A:I majored in Economics.<br />I:Tell me about the courses of your major in university.<br />A: I take more than 50 courses in university, including microeconomics, macroeconomics, marketing principles, sales management, statistics, and so on.<br />I:How did you get on with your studies in university?<br />A:I did well in university.   <p>Dialogue 3<br />I:Have you received any degrees?<br />A:Yes.   <p>I: 你有获得学位吗？<br />A: 有，1996年我在河北大学获得了理学学士学位，2001年在北京大学获得了工商管理硕士学位。<br />I: 你大学时学习成绩如何？<br />A: 事实上我每门课都非常优秀，我的总平均成绩按10分制是9分，是班里最高的。<br />I: 那可真不错哦。你最喜欢哪门课程？<br />A: 英语。因为这门课既有趣又实用，我对它很感兴趣。<br />I: 那你能告诉我你读研究生时为什么要换专业吗？<br />A: 因为我对管理非常感兴趣，而且我也学过这方面的课程，而且成绩非常好。因此，我相信我能做好这个职位。<br />I: 你大学时获得过荣誉和奖励吗？<br />A: 获得过。每年我都获得学校奖学金，在1995年我参加了国家数学建模考试，并得了奖。</p>
