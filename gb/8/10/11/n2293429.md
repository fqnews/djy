  <p>本画我们画单瓣茶花。中间的蕊柱就是它的特色，呈圆柱形，是茶花最重要的表征。画的上方我们加一圆满月，使稍远的花在月光下显得朦胧，所谓“月朦胧、鸟朦胧”，也即是“秋中花月夜”吧。</p>
  <p>Camellia（茶花）</p>
  <p>I&#8217;ve heard a camellia grower say that there are more than a thousand varieties of camellia in the world. There are all kinds of colors; some are purebred, some are mutants, some have a single petal, and some have multiple petals. There are a nearly countless number of types. The only common characteristic of these camellias is their beauty. The erudite camellia grower says that a flowergod dwells within them. The camellia is thus considered to have a kind of intelligence.<br />This painting depicts a single-petal camellia. The biggest identifying characteristic of camellias is their cyiindrical columns. Adding a full moon to the upper part of the painting makes the slightly distant flower appear blurry in the moonlight; this gives the painting a poetic feel and evokes &#8220;moon and flowers on a fall night.&#8221;＠</p>
