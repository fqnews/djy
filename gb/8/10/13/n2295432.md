  <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />1.	<b>exclusive</b>: adj. 独特的<br />2.	<b>surfer</b>: n. 冲浪者<br />3.	<b>contact</b>: v. 接触<br />4.	<b>ethos</b>: 风气<br />5.	<b>equipment</b>: n. 设备<br />6.	<b>reign</b>: v. 统治</p>
  <p>STORY:<br />It&#8217;s a small club, but not an <b>exclusive</b> one.It is made up of the 100 or so <b>surfers</b> in Cuba.<br />新闻主播：这虽然只是个小型俱乐部，却是独一无二的，大约聚集了ㄧ百多名古巴的冲浪好手。</p>
  <p>With no money and with little <b>contact </b>with the outside world, Cuban surfers have developed a cooperative<b> ethos</b>, sharing boards and <b>equipment </b>that once <b>reigned</b> among surfers around the globe.<br /> 古巴的冲浪人没什么钱，和世界其他地方也少有接触，但是他们自己形成了ㄧ股合作风气，分享全球冲浪好手的冲浪板及设备。</p>
  <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />7.	<b>foreign</b>: adj. 国外的<br />8.	<b>polyurethane</b>: n. 聚亚安酯<br />9.	<b>fiberglass</b>: n. 玻璃纤维<br />10.	<b>resin</b>: n. 树脂</p>
  <p>They learned surfing the hard way, by imitating what they saw in <b>foreign</b> magazines and making boards anyway they could.<br /> 他们的学习过程十分刻苦，只能模仿外国杂志，用尽各种方法制作冲浪板。</p>
  <p>[Surfer Eduardo Nunez, Surfer]: &#8220;Back in the day, knowledge about making surfboards was scarce and we started experimenting with <b>polyurethane</b> foam from old refrigerators when old refrigerators were thrown out, and we used the <b>fiberglass</b> that&#8217;s used here and the <b>resin</b>.&#8221;<br />冲浪好手Eduardo Nunez说：“ 以前没什么人知道如何制作冲浪板，刚开始，有人丢掉旧冰箱，我们就用冰箱的聚酯安酯做，这里也有人用玻璃纤维，还有树脂。”</p>
  <p><b>【新闻关键字】</b><br />11.	<b>donate</b>: v. 捐赠<br />12.	<b>solidarity</b>：n. 团结<br />13.	<b>universe</b>: n. 宇宙<br />14.	<b>cooperation</b>: n. 合作</p>
  <p>Now they ride the waves on secondhand surfboards <b>donated </b>by surfers in other countries, whose <b>solidarity</b> is keeping afloat one of the least known tribes in the surfing<b> universe</b>.<br /> 现在他们使用其他国家冲浪好手捐赠的二手冲浪板乘风踏浪，虽然在全球冲浪界中没没无闻，却是最团结的ㄧ群。</p>
  <p>So far this year, Cubans have received 20 boards and another 40 are expected in coming months.<br /> 今年到目前为止，古巴人已收到20个冲浪板，几个月后还会收到40个。</p>
  <p>The boards are given out for free to the surfers, with one condition: no one can sell them. They can be shared, handed down or given away, but years of necessity taught Cuban surfers that things like<b> cooperation </b>can be more important than money.<br />这些冲浪板会免费送给爱好冲浪人士，但有一个条件：不得转售，只能分享、移交或转送，这些年来，古巴的冲浪人都学到了合作比金钱更重要的道理。（translated by Wen-Chi Liao）</p>
