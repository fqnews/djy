	<p>【大纪元2016年07月26日讯】（<span id="E117" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">记者唐风<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>报导</span><span id="E118" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">）</span><span class="qowt-font2-SimSun">大<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>在北<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>市举行，数十个来自世界各地不同族裔的团队在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>中精彩的表演引来万余观众的欢呼与喝</span><span id="E128" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">采</span><span id="E129" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">，加勒比海游行给北温带来<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F%E6%97%A5.md#1">夏日</a>的快乐时光。</span><span id="E130" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">?</span></p>
