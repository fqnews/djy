<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>温市计划明年兴建流动泳池</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/06/2ab01538b791708dcde8e541f106247c-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年07月26日讯】<span id="E192" class="qowt-font2-SimSun"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">公园</a>局就兴建流动泳池地点展开</span><span id="E193" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">咨</span><span id="E194" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">询。<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">公园</a>局指出</span><span id="E195" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">, </span><span id="E196" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">有市民反映</span><span id="E197" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">, </span><span id="E198" class="qowt-font2-SimSun"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>的室外<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E6%B3%B3%E6%B1%A0.md#1">游泳池</a>资源短缺，</span><span id="E199" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">所以他们</span><span id="E200" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">计</span><span id="E201" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">划</span><span id="E202" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">明年兴建流动泳池给市民享用。</span><span id="E203" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">?</span></p>
<p><span id="E206" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">公园局主席</span><span id="E207" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">Sarah Kirby-Young</span><span id="E208" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">表示，相比起一般的坐地式固定<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E6%B3%B3%E6%B1%A0.md#1">游泳池</a></span><span id="E209" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">, </span><span id="E210" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">这些流动泳池将会在地面上兴建</span><span id="E211" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">, </span><span id="E212" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">可以随时<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%A7%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">移动</a>到其他地点</span><span id="E213" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">, </span><span id="E214" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">更重要的是兴建费用比较低。</span><span id="E215" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">?</span></p>
<p><span id="E219" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">Kirby-Young </span><span id="E220" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">表示，流动泳池一般由混凝土做底座，周边由各种不同物料制造，成本大约</span><span id="E221" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">100</span><span id="E222" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">至</span><span id="E223" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">200</span><span id="E224" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">万，寿命大约是</span><span id="E225" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">5</span><span id="E226" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">年，而现时的永久户外泳池成本大约是</span><span id="E227" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">500</span><span id="E228" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">万元。</span><span id="E229" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">?</span></p>
<p><span id="E232" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">她表示，永久户外泳池需要更多金钱维修，亦需要较多成本更换。一个流动泳池可以容纳数百人。</span><span id="E233" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">?</span></p>
<p><span id="E236" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">公园局会考虑在现时没有泳池的社区开设，</span><span id="E237" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">Kirby-Young</span><span id="E238" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">说，现时只知道温哥华的东区和南区拥有较少室外泳池，他们会在秋季，职员复工后再作详细讨论和研究。</span><span id="E239" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">?</span></p>
<p><span id="E242" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">现时温哥华市内一共有三个室外游泳池</span><span id="E243" class="qowt-font3-TimesNewRoman">, </span><span id="E244" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">而多个室内游泳池在过去十年都没有进行过</span><span id="E245" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">检</span><span id="E246" class="qowt-font2-SimSun">修工程。◇</span></p>
