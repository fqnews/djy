  <p>【大纪元2016年07月03日讯】<span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">（大纪元记者余欣然美国休斯顿编译报导）大众汽车公司就尾气排放造假案与美国司法部周二</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">6</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">月</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">28</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">日在旧金山达成和解，赔偿金额为</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">147</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">亿美元，创美国汽车史上最昂贵民事和解记录。该赔偿涵盖问题车辆回购费及两项环境基金。德州初步获赔</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">5</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">千万美元，用以支付现有或潜在州消费者保护理赔费用。</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">?</span></p>
  <p class="\&quot;p2\&quot;"><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">大众汽车期望此番庭外和解可一次性解决</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">600</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">余个集体索赔诉讼。公司将支付</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">100</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">亿美元回购或修理</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">48</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">万</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">7</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">千辆</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">2.0</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">升引擎的问题柴油车，向美国国家环保局（</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">EPA</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">）运营的信托支付</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">27</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">亿美元环境修复款，以及</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">10</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">年内投资</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">20</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">亿美元协助美国研发零排放技术。</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">?</span></p>
  <p class="\&quot;p2\&quot;"><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">拥有问题柴油车的车主将得到</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">5</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">千</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">1</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">百至</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">1</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">万美元不等赔偿，弥补车辆贬值损失以及大众汽车“干净柴油”的欺骗性宣传行为。受影响车型包括</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">2009-2015</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">年的大众及</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">2.0</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">升奥迪，回购价格在</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">1</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">万</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">2</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">千</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">5</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">至</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">4</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">万</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">4</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">千美元之间。车主可在</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">2018</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">年</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">5</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">月前选择让大众回购或将原车修理过关。</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">?</span></p>
  <p class="\&quot;p2\&quot;"><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">此外，大众汽车还同意赔偿美国</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">44</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">州，波多黎各，及首都华盛顿共</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">6</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">亿</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">3</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">百万美元，协助调解各地的消费者保护理赔案。据美国专业汽车评价公司凯利蓝皮书报告，加州，德州，佛罗里达，伊利诺和纽约州注册最多</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">。德州除了分到</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">5</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">千万理赔金份额，还将获得</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">1</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">亿</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">9</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">千</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">2</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">百万环境修复费。</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">?</span></p>
  <p class="\&quot;p2\&quot;"><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">2015</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">年</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">9</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">月，大众汽车在美国环保部门的压力下承认，使用特殊软体操纵</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">2.0</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">升柴油引擎的尾气测量值。当汽车接受尾气排放检测时，排放系统显示达标，但日常使用时，废气排量可高达法定标准</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">40</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">倍。全世界有约</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">1100</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">万辆大众汽车受到影响。美国司法部副部长</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">Sally Yates</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">称大众汽车的欺诈行为乃“美国史上针对消费者和环境保护法最恶劣的违法行为之一”。</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">?</span></p>
  <p class="\&quot;p2\&quot;"><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">司法部与大众均表示周二的协议仅迈出庭外和解第一步，下次听证日期是</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">7</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">月</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">26</span><span class="\&quot;s1\&quot;">日。德州政府亦表示将继续针对大众汽车违反本州环境保护法而积极索赔。</span><span class="\&quot;s2\&quot;">?</span></p>
