<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>能在周末完成的十大卫浴升级方案</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/g"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tofjt245/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年08月31日讯】（大纪元记者陈洁云编译报导）浴室往往是家中最小的房间，但这不意味着你不能在其中呈现“大家风范”。美国家居网站Hometalk近日介绍了可显着提升浴室品位的十大<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%87%E7%BA%A7.md#1">升级</a>方案，均可利用一个周末轻松完成。</p>
<p><strong>1. 修补瓷砖</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8251947" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301412592669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251947 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301412592669-300x309.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="309" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">修补瓷砖(Accent Tile Wall via Paint Speckled Paw Prints on Hometalk)</figcaption></figure>
<p>更换瓷砖可能要耗费几个月，而在短短的一个周末，你就可以通过修补瓷砖（涂一层特殊涂料）让过时的浴室瓷砖焕发出现代美感。不要被浴室早先瓷砖的铺排和颜色障碍，你可以随心所欲地转换为你喜欢的样子！</p>
<p><strong>2. 给浴室镜装框</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8251946" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301413022669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251946 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301413022669-300x450.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">给浴室镜装框(Framed Bathroom Mirror via The Everyday Home on Hometalk)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%A4%E5%86%85%E8%AE%BE%E8%AE%A1.md#1">室内设计</a>的最佳技巧之一。您可以通过为镜子装框，轻松赋予浴室以风格和美感。市场上有很多现成的镜框套装，但为获得高度个性化的效果，你也可以利用3M无痕胶条等工具自己动手加装订制的木框。</p>
<p><strong>3. 新的盥洗台和水槽</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8251945" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301413042669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251945 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301413042669-300x453.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="453" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">新的盥洗台和水槽(Weathered Wood Vanity via Everyday Enchanting on Hometalk)</figcaption></figure>
<p>无论你是想要用大号的盥洗台增添趣味，还是换用时尚的下沉式水槽设计以腾出空间，你都可以在一个周末轻松完成这一重大翻新。</p>
<p>你甚至还可以把漂亮的梳妆台改造成盥洗台，还有时间来把它安装到位！</p>
<p><center><a src=""></a><a href="https://git.io/g"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tofjt245/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/HUTP3pQ_gKU" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></center>请务必注留意抽屉拉手，考虑到效果，要选择最美观的零件。如果已有嵌入式的浴室柜，您也可以将其重新粉刷，加装新零件。</p>
<p><strong>4. 别致的照明</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8251944" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301413072669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251944 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301413072669-300x478.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="478" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">别致的照明(Bathroom Chandelier via Mommy is Coo Coo on Hometalk)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A3%85%E4%BF%AE.md#1">装修</a>浴室时不要忽略照明，别忘记把无趣的标准灯具换成好看的吊灯或壁灯。一副顶灯可以真正提升浴室的装饰品位。</p>
<p><strong>5. 改装护壁板</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8251943" style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301413102669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8251943" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301413102669.jpg" alt="" width="480" b="320" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">改装护壁板(Beadboard in the Bathroom via Golden Boys and Me on Hometalk)</figcaption></figure>
<p>护壁板是扮靓浴室、创造通风和宽敞感的好方法。其安装简便，也可以用壁纸来代替。</p>
<p><strong>6. 翻新浴缸</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8251942" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301413132669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251942 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301413132669-300x338.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">浴缸美容(Wood Tub Paneling via The Savvy Stager on Hometalk)</figcaption></figure>
<p>更换浴缸可能需要花几周，但“浴缸美容”只需一天半就能完成。</p>
<p>内置式浴缸可以在外壁加铺瓷砖，甚至是木围，以求得更精致的外观。如果选用易粘的瓷砖，浴缸改造只需几个小时。</p>
<p><strong>7. 重刷墙壁</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8251948" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301412532669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251948 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301412532669-450x343.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="343" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">重刷墙壁(Bright Bathroom via Heirloom Design Build on Hometalk)</figcaption></figure>
<p>让浴室改头换面的最简单有效的办法是重刷墙壁或加铺壁纸。不妨大胆选用靓丽的色彩或图案。即便是简单的家具，在具高度设计感的墙壁衬托下也会显得很时尚。</p>
<p>为了实现更好的视觉趣味，你还可以把天花板一起刷一刷。</p>
<p><strong>8. 更新浴室柜</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8251950" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301412472669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251950 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301412472669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">更新浴室柜(Painted Bathroom Counter via At The Picket Fence on Hometalk)</figcaption></figure>
<p>更换或重新粉刷浴室柜，会对浴室的整体外观有相当大的影响。</p>
<p><strong>9. 装饰墙壁</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8251949" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301412562669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8251949 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301412562669-450x361.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="361" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">装饰墙壁(Bathroom Mirror Art via A Button Tufted Life on Hometalk)</figcaption></figure>
<p>别浪费了浴室的墙壁！这个周末，挂上一些靓丽的镜子、架子或墙上工艺品，使洗手间和家里的其它区域一样富有装饰感。</p>
<p>镜子是特别棒的浴室装饰，因为它可创造出空间错觉。</p>
<p><strong>10. 玻璃淋浴门</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8251951" style="width: 480px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301412502669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8251951" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608301412502669.jpg" alt="" width="480" b="639" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">玻璃淋浴门(Glass Shower Door via Texas Home and Garden on Hometalk)</figcaption></figure>
<p>玻璃墙和淋浴门会赋予整个<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%AB%E6%B5%B4.md#1">卫浴</a>以设计感，同时也创造出更多的空间错觉。</p>
<p>玻璃门易于清洁，且永远不会过时。</p>
