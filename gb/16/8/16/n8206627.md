  <p>活动征件时间为<span lang="EN-US">105</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日起至<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，参赛对象为就读台南市及嘉义县市的国中小学生，分为国中组<span lang="EN-US">（7</span>年级至<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年级<span lang="EN-US">）</span>及国小组<span lang="EN-US">（4</span>年级至<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年级学生<span lang="EN-US">）</span>。报名经审查后符合参赛资格者，可获得精美小礼物乙份。各组将选出特优等奖项致赠礼券，得奖作品将择期于乌山头水库风景区旅客服务中心公开展示，参赛获奖者将可于展示期间免费进入乌山头水库风景区。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8206742" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608160529252357.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8206742 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608160529252357-450x303.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="303" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">嘉南田野景观&#8211;采收白萝卜。（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E5%8D%97%E5%86%9C%E7%94%B0%E6%B0%B4%E5%88%A9%E4%BC%9A.md#1">嘉南农田水利会</a>嘉义分会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8206743" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608160529212357.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8206743 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/08/1608160529212357-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">乌山头水库旧送水口。（嘉南农田水利会嘉义分会提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>欢迎有兴趣参加比赛活动的学童踊跃报名，活动简章与相关讯息可至嘉南农田水利会或乌山头水库风景区官方网站、脸书粉丝团查询或电洽<span lang="EN-US">06)2200622#259</span>。</p>
  <p>嘉南农田水利会网址：<span lang="EN-US">http://www.chianan.gov.tw/</span>；乌山头水库风景区网址：<span lang="EN-US">http://www.chianan.gov.tw/wusanto</span>。</p>
