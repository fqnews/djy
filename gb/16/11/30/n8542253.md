  <figure id="attachment_8542319" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542319 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC06029-450x253.jpg" alt="dsc06029" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%99%BD%E5%AE%AB.md#1">白宫</a>北侧正门口的圣诞树。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542317" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542317 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC06024-450x253.jpg" alt="dsc06024" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">工作人员往白宫内运送圣诞树。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542255" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542255 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC05964-300x376.jpg" alt="dsc05964" width="300" b="376" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫东厢大门的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E8%A3%85%E9%A5%B0.md#1">圣诞装饰</a>。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542267" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542267 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC05977-300x376.jpg" alt="dsc05977" width="300" b="376" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫一楼东厢的圣诞树与国旗装饰。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542265" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542265 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC05974-450x253.jpg" alt="dsc05974" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫一楼东厢走廊的屋顶装饰。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>每年圣诞节前﹐美国白宫内都会布置得富丽堂皇﹐充满节日的欢乐气氛。今年最显眼的就是摆放在一楼东厢的两只巨大的小狗模型﹐它们就是第一家庭的宠物阿波和Sunny。它们端坐在走廊尽头的窗下﹐仿彿在乖乖地迎接宾客。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8542263" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542263 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC05972-450x253.jpg" alt="dsc05972" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫一楼东厢内﹐第一家庭的宠物阿波和Sunny的模型。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542276" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542276 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC05988-450x253.jpg" alt="dsc05988" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫一楼的图书室中央的两棵圣诞树﹐分别用铅笔和蜡笔排列组成。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542303" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542303 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC06001-450x253.jpg" alt="dsc06001" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫二楼东厅内的圣诞装饰。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542275" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542275 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC05986-300x533.jpg" alt="dsc05986" width="300" b="533" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">摆放在白宫一楼朱红房间的、以“芭蕾”为主题的圣诞装饰。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542272" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542272 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC05984-450x253.jpg" alt="dsc05984" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">摆放在白宫一楼朱红房间的、以“芭蕾”为主题的圣诞装饰。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>圣诞节当然离不开圣诞树﹐白宫内每个房间里﹐都配有大小不同﹑装饰精美的圣诞树。在二楼蓝厅内﹐19英尺高﹑来自宾州的冷衫﹐是今年的“白宫圣诞树”。上面缀满闪亮的金属和玻璃装饰﹐玻璃瓶内的贺词寄托着军人家属们对海外驻军亲人的思念。旁边绿厅和红厅的圣诞装饰﹐则以健康为主题。＃</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8542305" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542305 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC06006-300x533.jpg" alt="dsc06006" width="300" b="533" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫二楼蓝厅内﹐19英尺高﹑来自宾州的冷衫﹐是今年的“白宫圣诞树”。上面缀满闪亮的金属和玻璃瓶装饰﹐瓶内的贺词寄托着军人家属们对海外驻军的亲人的思念。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542304" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542304 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC06003-450x253.jpg" alt="dsc06003" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫二楼绿厅内的圣诞装饰。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542308" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542308 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC06010-450x253.jpg" alt="dsc06010" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫二楼红厅内的圣诞装饰。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542270" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542270 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC05981-300x505.jpg" alt="dsc05981" width="300" b="505" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫一楼东厢的圣诞树。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>圣诞节的另外一个传统就是制作姜饼屋。白宫国宴厅里巨大的姜饼屋﹐超过300磅重，仿照白宫的建筑式样，包括东西厢房﹐外面涂满诱人的白巧克力﹐周围的装饰也都是用姜饼和糖霜等制成。在另外两棵圣诞树上﹐还装饰有56个用乐高做成的小姜饼屋﹐分别代表着美国50个州和6个不同地区。组成这些美丽的小屋总共用了20万块乐高。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8542316" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542316 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC06017-450x253.jpg" alt="dsc06017" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫国宴厅里巨大的姜饼屋﹐超过300磅重，仿照白宫的建筑式样，包括东西厢房﹐外面涂满诱人的白巧克力﹐周围的装饰也都是用姜饼和糖霜等制成。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_8542313" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8542313 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/11/DSC06014-300x533.jpg" alt="dsc06014" width="300" b="533" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白宫国宴厅的圣诞树上﹐装饰有56个用乐高做成的小姜饼屋﹐分别代表着美国50个州和6个不同地区。组成这些美丽小屋总共用了20万块乐高。（林帆／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>&nbsp;</p>
