<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台故宫推“嘉庆君游台湾”文物特展</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年05月09日讯】（大纪元记者钟元台湾报导）台湾国立<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%95%85%E5%AE%AB.md#1">故宫</a>博物院推出的“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E5%BA%86%E5%90%9B.md#1">嘉庆君</a>?游台湾——<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%85%E4%BB%81%E5%AE%97.md#1">清仁宗</a>文物特展”，将于5月10日在国立<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%95%85%E5%AE%AB.md#1">故宫</a>博物院南部院区盛大展出，展期自5月10日起至9月4日。故宫表示，“嘉庆君?游台湾－<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%85%E4%BB%81%E5%AE%97.md#1">清仁宗</a>文物特展”是该院继康熙、雍正、乾隆三帝后再次以一位君王为主轴策划的大型展览。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7877396" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090919142378-e1462802104557.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7877396 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090919142378-e1462802104557.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%98%89%E5%BA%86%E5%90%9B.md#1">嘉庆君</a>游台湾”是台湾流传甚广的民间故事，台湾<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%95%85%E5%AE%AB%E5%8D%97%E9%99%A2.md#1">故宫南院</a>将自5月10日起展出嘉庆君文物特展。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>台湾故宫表示，策展团队集合众人之力，依据史实与文物从正面诠释这位站在大清王朝由盛转衰关键时期的君王，看他如何匡时杜弊，反转乾坤，以及他与台湾深厚的情源。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7877470" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924402378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7877470 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924402378-450x336.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">清_嘉庆_明黄地绣彩云金龙十二章纹龙袍。（沈阳故宫博物院）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7877434" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924472378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7877434 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924472378-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">清_嘉庆_金镶黄碧玺吉服带。（台湾故宫）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冯明珠院长表示，“嘉庆君游台湾”是一则深植社会流传极广的传说，传言嘉庆皇帝为太子时曾游历台湾，台湾中南部许多名胜古迹因攀附嘉庆君曾经到访，成旅游景点。故宫南部院区座落于嘉义，正是“嘉庆皇帝游台湾”传说源头所在。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7877435" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090922032378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7877435 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090922032378-300x400.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“嘉庆君．游台湾─清仁宗文物特展”5月10日起在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%95%85%E5%AE%AB%E5%8D%97%E9%99%A2.md#1">故宫南院</a>展出，共展出嘉庆龙袍、朝珠（图）、吉服带、传位诏书等151组件文物。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>冯明珠指出，一年多以前，故宫组成跨四处庞大策展团队，展开研究，从追踪嘉庆君游台湾的文本源头、传说形成与影响，到嘉庆皇帝与台湾、清仁宗其人、其事、其事功、其艺术以及其文化事业等，进行探索，并寻找可铺陈展览的展件，共展出计151组件文物，以“嘉庆皇帝与台湾”、“嘉庆皇帝其人”、“宫廷收藏与艺术”、“文化事业”与“安内靖乱”等五单元，铺陈展览。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7877447" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924252378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7877447 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924252378-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《清_黄钺_画龢丰协象“土牛鞭春”》。（台湾故宫）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7877451" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924162378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7877451 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924162378-450x320.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="320" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《清_冯宁_画赏葉瑶精》。（台湾故宫）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7877458" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090923532378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7877458 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090923532378-450x232.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="232" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《钦定秘殿珠林石渠宝笈三编》清嘉庆间内府朱丝栏写本。（台湾故宫）</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘉庆君(1760-1820)，是满族入关后第五位皇帝，清高宗乾隆皇帝第十五子，全名爱新觉罗?颙琰，庙号仁宗，谥号睿皇帝，在位二十五年(1796-1820)，嘉庆是他的年号。台湾故宫表示，证之史册，他在位期间，先后平定许多内部动乱，抑制社会危机的蔓延，尤其是他剿灭了海盗蔡牵，维护东南沿岸海防与台湾的安定。</p>
<figure id="attachment_7877437" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924012378.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-7877437" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924012378-600x109.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="109" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《唐_杨昇_画山水》。（台湾故宫）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7877442" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924312378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7877442 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924312378-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">清_嘉庆_白玉凤首壶。（台湾故宫）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_7877455" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924072378.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7877455 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/1605090924072378-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">清_干隆_金瓯永固》、玉烛长调烛台。（台湾故宫）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另一方面，他继续推动乾隆朝的文化事业，整理皇室收藏，编纂官书典籍。嘉庆皇帝的才华与名气被皇父乾隆皇帝掩盖，然其诗文创作丰富，数量虽次于其父，但创作水平犹胜一筹，足称为中国历史上第二位多产皇帝诗人。</p>
