  <p>网路上<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%8F%E8%82%A5.md#1">减肥</a>方法琳琅满目，有人靠节食<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%98%A6%E8%BA%AB.md#1">瘦身</a>，一天只吃一餐，有人不吃淀粉类食物，这些不健康的减肥方式，不但会让健康急速下滑，复胖概率也高达9成。其实，减肥不必挨饿，只要吃对食物，也可以让你健康地吃成一个瘦子！</p>
  <p>以下几种食物，可以让你越吃越瘦，提供给正准备<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%98%A6%E8%BA%AB.md#1">瘦身</a>的女性朋友参考：</p>
  <p><strong><strong>1.酸奶</strong></strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_5736447" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1211091222502382.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5736447 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1211091222502382-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">酸奶和饼。（Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>  <p>酸奶是一种非常好的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%8F%E8%82%A5%E9%A3%9F%E5%93%81.md#1">减肥食品</a>，可以有效的帮助清除肠道里淤积的毒素，促进消化，减少体内脂肪比例，达到快速减肥瘦身的目的。</p>
  <p><strong><strong>2.玉米</strong></strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_5738496" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1401312341432483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5738496 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/06/1401312341432483-450x360.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="360" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">玉米。（Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>  <div class="mceTemp"></div>  <p>玉米含有丰富的膳食纤维，多吃有助于加速毒素的排出，并且能够产生饱腹感。玉米须煲汤还能消除水肿，是减肥的不二选择。</p>
  <p><strong><strong>3.鸡胸肉</strong></strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_7739777" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1406121135342483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7739777 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1406121135342483-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸡胸肉与蔬菜沙拉。（Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>  <p>鸡胸肉几乎不含脂肪，同时完整具备我们体内不可或缺的“必需氨基酸”及“蛋白质精华”。这类低卡路里的纯蛋白质，非常适合瘦身的朋友。早上吃富含蛋白质的食物，可有效降低整天食欲、增加饱足感。</p>
  <p><strong><strong>4.燕麦片</strong></strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_5798838" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1410022322482483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5798838 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1410022322482483-450x306.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="306" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">燕麦片。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>  <p>燕麦营养丰富，又没有过高的热量，其降血脂、控制血压以及胆固醇的功效奇佳。</p>
  <p><strong><strong>5.鱼肉</strong></strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_5867191" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1505051311562730.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5867191 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/05/1505051311562730-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">夏威夷海域长大的野生鲳鱼，肉块紧致，搭配的蛤蛎藏红花汤汁，还有春季豌豆和红椒丝。一道菜色，融合了酸、甜、咸、辣，又没有掩盖食材的清新原味。（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>鱼肉鲜美可口，低热量，含有的氨基酸能最佳补给身体蛋白质，让女性变得美丽又窈窕。</p>
  <p><strong><strong>6.红豆薏仁</strong></strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_7739804" style="width: 375px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-7739804" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/3825642903_21a4dbf0ef_z.jpg" alt="红豆绿豆薏仁汤（图片来源：pan vanessa/flickr）" width="375" b="500" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红豆绿豆薏仁汤。（pan vanessa/flickr）</figcaption></figure>  <p>红豆跟薏仁是绝佳的减肥组合，红豆薏仁汤不但可以消除水肿、改善体寒，同时能拥有红润气色跟白皙肌肤。</p>
  <p><strong>7.</strong><strong><strong>蘋</strong>果</strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_6912796" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/02/0912191321421892.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6912796 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2010/02/0912191321421892-450x299.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">苹果。（连震黎／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>苹果的热量低，又含有丰富的维生素C，有助于胶原蛋白的生成、帮助铁质的吸收，可让肌肤变好。每天一个苹果，是最简单的饮食塑身。</p>
  <p><strong>8.黄</strong><strong>瓜</strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_7728856" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/06/1106200956031839.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7728856 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2011/06/1106200956031839-450x330.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="330" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄瓜。（苏玉芬／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>黄瓜的含水量高达96％～98％，生吃、熟炒，或榨成黄瓜汁，是夏天减肥美容利器。</p>
  <p><strong>9.食</strong><strong>醋</strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_5798812" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1401021032462483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5798812 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/12/1401021032462483-450x301.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="301" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">苹果醋。（大纪元资料室）</figcaption></figure>  <p>食醋中富含的氨基酸能够干扰脂肪的形成，起到消耗脂肪的作用。又可以促进摄入的糖类与蛋白质的新陈代谢，增强机体对脂肪的代谢作用，排泄过多的“膘”，而达到良好的瘦身减肥作用。</p>
  <p><strong>10.西兰花</strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_7428638" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603150059432669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7428638 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603150059432669-450x356.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="356" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">西兰花沙拉。(eluxemagazine.com)</figcaption></figure>  <p>西兰花热量低助消化，富含矿物质、维生素、蛋白质等营养成分，还有促进肝脏解毒的作用，是非常好的健康蔬菜。</p>
  <p><strong>11.冬</strong><strong>瓜</strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_5715723" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1211301215022382.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5715723 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/04/1211301215022382-450x265.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="265" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">冬瓜。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>  <p>冬瓜不含脂肪、利尿排湿，帮助排除体内多余的水分，消除浮肿的现象。</p>
  <p><strong>12.青</strong><strong>菜</strong></p>
  <figure id="attachment_6575395" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204060354471839.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6575395 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/04/1204060354471839-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">烫青菜。（江柏逸 ／ 大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <p>青菜能补充水分和维生素，有美容保健、解毒消肿的作用，是减肥不可少的食物。</p>
  <p>想维持好身材，除了注重饮食之外，若能搭配运动，使身体线条更加紧实，你也能成为一个亮丽的健康美人！@*#</p>
