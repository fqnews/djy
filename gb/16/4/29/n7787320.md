  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">美国卫生和公众服务部长布维尔（</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Sylvia Burwell</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）说，她在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E5%A4%9A%E9%BB%8E%E5%90%84.md#1">波多黎各</a>待了</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">2</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">天，那里有</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">475</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">人<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%B9%E5%8D%A1%E7%97%85%E6%AF%92.md#1">兹卡病毒</a>测试为阳性，<span lang="zh-CN">并且</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">48</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">名孕妇也感染了兹卡病毒。尽管所有感染都出现在该国境内，但卫生专家还是担心兹卡病毒会在全美</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">30</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">多个州传播。科学家目前还在研究蚊虫携带的兹卡病毒是如何起作用的，但是很显然性接触会导致进一步传播。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">和西尼罗河病毒（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">West Nile virus</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）一样，兹卡病毒主要通过感染病毒的蚊子叮咬传播，</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span id="gv_article_lbl_content_1"><span lang="ZH-TW">怀孕妇女感染病毒也可能会传给胎儿，</span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">造成</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">新生儿</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">小头症</span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">。感染兹卡病毒的</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">症</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">状</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">为发烧，出红色疹子，关节疼痛等。据疾控中心（</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;">CDC</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">说</span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">，尽管该病导致的死亡例子很少，但约五分之一的兹卡感染者病情会很严重。目前还没有疫苗或有效药物能预防或治疗兹卡感染。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">布维尔说，联邦官员将不得不动用</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">5</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">亿美元的埃博拉基金，以便调配资源到波多黎各阻击兹卡病毒的传播。截至本周，美国参议院已接近通过</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">19</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">亿美元奥巴马总统在</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">2</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">月所提的大部分拨款要求。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">在最近的一篇社论中，众议员希夫（</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Adam Schiff</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）敦促国会尽快采取行动，尽快<span lang="zh-CN">批准</span>该项拨款。他说，最终必须调配新的资金来阻击兹卡，不能再拆东墙补西墙了，他希望国会能尽快通过这项紧急资助。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">目前，全美几个州<span lang="zh-CN">已</span>发现携带兹卡病毒<span lang="zh-CN">的</span>蚊子<span lang="zh-CN">，</span>其中包括<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E5%B7%9E.md#1">加州</a>，有</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">35</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">人在旅行时感染了该病毒。据上周五的州数据，一名到病毒爆发的国家旅行的人<span lang="zh-CN">因</span>性接触<span lang="zh-CN">感染了</span>该病毒。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">据<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E5%B7%9E.md#1">加州</a>公共卫生部，全州</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">30%</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">的兹卡病例出现在圣地亚哥县，洛县</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">8</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">例，圣贝纳迪诺县和橙县各</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">2</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">例，河滨县目前还没有发现感染者。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">联邦官员说，在全美范围内，有</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">388</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">人感染兹卡病毒，他们全都是在前往病毒爆发国家期间感染<span lang="zh-CN">了</span>兹卡病毒。全美目前没有本地传播的报告。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">全美传染病和过敏研究院（</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）主任福西博士（</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Dr. Anthony Fauci</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">）说，科学家们正在研制兹卡疫苗，但是最快也要到</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">9</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">月才能进行临床第一阶段测试。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">今年早些时候，</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">CDC</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">列出了几个中南美洲、亚洲和非洲<span lang="zh-CN">的</span>国家，警告孕妇或考虑怀孕的人<span lang="zh-CN">尽量避免</span>前往这些国家<span lang="zh-CN">旅行</span>。◇</span></span></span></p>
