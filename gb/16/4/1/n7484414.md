<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美国3月汽车销售强劲 但制造商困境隐现？</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1401140701242120-600x400-e1459552113492.jpg" />
<h6>美国3月汽车销售似乎迎来了10多年来最好的月份。（Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年04月02日讯】<span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">（大纪元记者张纯之编译报导）近日，<span lang="zh-TW">大多数主要<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B1%BD%E8%BD%A6%E5%88%B6%E9%80%A0%E5%95%86.md#1">汽车制造商</a>发布</span><span lang="zh-CN">的</span><span lang="zh-TW">大美国地区</span></span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">月销售利润报告显示，汽车工业似乎迎来了</span></span></span>10<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span lang="zh-TW">年多来最好的月份。但也有迹象显示，一些公司销售艰难，被迫提高<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B1%BD%E8%BD%A6%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE.md#1">汽车销售</a>折扣，或者以更便宜的价格将更多车卖给租车公司。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">据《圣盖博谷论坛报》报导，主要受到</span></span>SUV<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">和卡车销售强劲的带动，福特（</span></span>Ford<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）、本田（</span></span>Honda<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）、菲亚特克莱斯勒（</span></span>Fiat Chrysler<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）和尼桑（</span></span>Nissan<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）销售报告都显示出较大的增长。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">尼桑销售增加了</span></span>13%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">，创下了单月历史纪录。本田受到全新的</span></span>Civic compact<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">销售带动，销售增长了</span></span>9%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">。菲亚特克莱斯勒和福特销售增加了</span></span>8%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">，这是两公司</span></span>10<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">年来的最好数据。通用汽车（</span></span>General Motors<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）销售轻微增长</span></span>0.9%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">，该公司一直在减少对租车公司的销售，其报告说，该公司对私人买家的零售增加了</span></span>6%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">汽车评估和研究公司</span></span>Kelley Blue Book<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">说，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B1%BD%E8%BD%A6%E5%88%B6%E9%80%A0%E5%95%86.md#1">汽车制造商</a></span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月的总体销售量大约为</span></span>166<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">万辆小车和卡车，这是自</span></span>2005<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">年</span></span>7<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月以来的单月最大销售。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="color: #330000;"><span lang="zh-CN">分析认为，汽车行业销售增长可能是受到相对低的油价、较好的租赁、低利率、贷款容易以及老化车增加等因素的推动。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">不过，丰田（</span></span>Toyota<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）销售降低近了</span></span>3%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">，该公司</span></span>SUV<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">和卡车销售有所增长，但是小车销售下降了近</span></span>10%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">。大众（</span></span>Volkswagen<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）受到排放丑闻困扰，销售下降了</span></span>10.4%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">然而汽车行业整体销售呈向上趋势，预期增长将达到</span></span>7%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">。这表明，尽管一些分析家和经销商预测销售已经见顶了，但美国人仍在大量买车，</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B1%BD%E8%BD%A6%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE.md#1">汽车销售</a>数据，加上周五公布的数据说美国</span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月增加了</span></span>21.5<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">万个工作，显示尽管股市震荡，但经济仍然很健康。尽管失业率增长了</span></span>5%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">，但这是因为美国找工作的人数增加了。通用首席经济师</span></span>Mustafa Mohatarem<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">说，这些都表明汽车销售将继续领导经济增长。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">但一些分析家说，靓丽的数据背后，也有迹象显示汽车制造商的困境。上个月比一年前增加了</span></span>2<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">个打折日，是折扣带动的消费在增加。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">尽管折扣消费对新车买家来说是好事，却削减了制造商的利润，伤害了二手车的价格。高激励措施引导的消费是导致</span></span>2008<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">年经济困境的重要原因之一，汽车销售价格网站</span></span>TrueCar.com<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">说，</span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月，汽车制造商给予每辆车的折扣平均为</span></span>3,000<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">美元，比一年前多了</span></span>10%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">著名汽车行业评鉴公司</span>J.D. Power and Associates<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">认为</span>，汽车制造商增加折扣主要是因为小车销售有困难，美国市场正在转向卡车和</span></span>SUV<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">。</span></span>J.D. Power<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">副总裁金（</span></span>Thomas King<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）说，打折销售也压低了二手车的价格，所以拿自己旧车换购抵价的买家减少</span>。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">金说，上月受激励措施推动的消费已接近大衰退时期的水平，小车销售折扣已经达到了制造商标示价格的</span></span>12.3%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">，不过卡车和</span></span>SUV<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">的折扣仍然维持在</span></span>8.2%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">，这意味着准备买小车的消费者今年可能会找到好交易，但卡车和</span></span>SUV<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">买家不会看到大的折扣。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">菲亚特克莱斯勒的小车销售</span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月减少了</span></span>34%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">，但卡车和</span></span>SUV<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">销售增长了</span></span>23%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">。美国最畅销车，丰田凯美瑞（</span></span>Toyota Camry<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）的销售</span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月降低了</span></span>9%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">，还不到</span></span>3.7<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">万辆。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">汽车交易网站</span></span>Autotrader.com?<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">的资深分析师</span></span>Michelle Krebs?<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">说，</span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月汽车销售仍然很强劲，但是汽车制造商被迫向租车公司卖更多车，增多激励措施，使用租赁和延长贷款期等方式将月付控制在消费者预算之内等。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000; font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">福特公司美国区总裁</span>Mark LaNeve<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">说，他看到汽车折扣在增加，因为消费者在转向卡车和</span></span>SUV<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">，但这不是什么值得担心的事。◇</span></span></span></p>
