<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加州6月总统初选 民调：川普略胜克鲁兹</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/04/1604020358451758-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>6月7日，加州将举行总统大选初选。图为2月25日，川普（左）和科鲁兹（右）在德州举行的共和党大选电视辩论会上激烈交锋。(Michael Ciaglo-Pool/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年04月08日讯】<span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">（大纪元记者张纯之编译报导）</span></span>6<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月</span></span>7<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">日，加州将举行总统大选初选。一份最新民调显示，共和党总统候选人<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B7%9D%E6%99%AE.md#1">川普</a>（</span></span>Donald Trump<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）在这场初选中稍稍领先德克萨斯州参议员<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%8B%E9%B2%81%E5%85%B9.md#1">克鲁兹</a>（</span></span>Ted Cruz<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">）。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;">4<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月</span></span>7<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">日发布的民调显示，</span></span>39%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">的加州共和党选民支持<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%B7%9D%E6%99%AE.md#1">川普</a>，</span></span>32%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">支持<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%85%8B%E9%B2%81%E5%85%B9.md#1">克鲁兹</a>，</span></span>18%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">支持俄亥俄州州长卡西奇（</span></span>John Kasich<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">），还有</span></span>11%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">的选民或者未做出决定，或者支持其他候选人。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">但是，民调还显示出了显着的地区差异。这是一个值得注意的因素，因为加州共和党共有</span></span>172<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">张党代表票，其中</span></span>159?<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">张党代表票来自加州</span></span>53<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">个国会选区，这意味着每个选区都有</span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">张党代表票，它们将各归各选区赢家所有。</span></span>另13张“奖励党代表票”（Bonus delegates）将归全州获得最多选票的参选人。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">民调显示，克鲁兹在洛县、中央山谷</span></span>/<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">内华达地区领先，而川普在南加其它地区以及旧金山湾区领先。卡西奇在旧金山湾区获得了他最强的支持率。</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">民调主任</span>Mark DiCamillo<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">写道，如果这些区域差别一直持续，这会</span><span lang="zh-TW">冲淡</span></span></span>6<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月加州初选</span>赢家的优势。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">民调报告还说，许多川普的支持者和</span></span>2003<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">年施瓦辛格竞选加州州长时的支持者是同一拨人。报告显示高达</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span id="gv_article_lbl_content_3">75%的</span>这群选民</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">喜欢川普胜于克鲁兹。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">不过，川普还需要进一步争取女性选票。民调显示，只有</span></span>31%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">的共和党女性选民支持他，而相比之下，克鲁兹则获得</span></span>35%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">共和党女性选民的支持。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #330000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">这次民意调查从</span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月</span></span>24<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">日开始，</span></span>4<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">月</span></span>4<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">日结束。调查者用英语和西班牙语电话访问了</span></span>558<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">位可能的共和党选民，选民误差率在正负</span></span>4%<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-CN">左右。◇</span></span></span></p>
