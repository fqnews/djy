src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/12/f258f47b3d77ac2c3b447ced06a5eef5-320x200.jpg" />  <h6></h6>  <hr>  	<p>【大纪元2016年04月22日讯】（<span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">大纪元记者袁玫艾尔蒙地市报导）为提倡海外华人<span lang="zh-CN">注</span>重户外体育活动，发扬体育精神，中华体育联谊会积极持续举办运动大会，今年第</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">26</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">届海华体育季暨第</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">36</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">届<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E5%8D%8E%E4%BA%BA%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8%E4%BC%9A.md#1">美国华人运动会</a>，即将于</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">4</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">月</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">23</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">日在核桃市<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A0%B8%E6%A1%83%E5%B8%82%E9%AB%98%E4%B8%AD.md#1">核桃市高中</a><span lang="zh-CN">（</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">400 N.Pirre Rd.</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">，</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Walnut</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">，</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">CA 91789</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN">）</span>体育场举行，侨务委员会为了提倡<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">民俗活动</a>，本次运动会特别增加了民俗活动表演。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">中华体育联谊会理事长高启正</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">19</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">日表示，美国中华体育联谊会极尽所能，倡导运动风气，期望海外侨胞及青年朋友，均能养成爱好运动的良好精神，无论是为自己或是为家人锻炼身体，华人念书念得好，才艺不错，然而华人家长及孩子们似乎都未将体育活动摆在优先，希望华人在运动方面能更加强，对孩子未来健康及生活习惯都很重要。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">今年为推广中华文化中的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8.md#1">民俗活动</a>，<span lang="zh-CN">又</span>加入两项有趣的项目<span lang="zh-CN">——</span>有特色的扯铃、体能训练的踢毽子<span lang="zh-CN">。</span>这是除田赛、径赛、拔河外新增项目，</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">23</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">日请了本地著名团体将以表演方式，来引起大家对这两项随时随地都可以做到的运动的兴趣，明年联谊会将耗资<span lang="zh-CN">准备</span>器材来提供选手使用。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">主办大会总干事张正欧说明，本届运动会是近十年来南加州各地区中文学校参与最多，参加人数最多的一次<span lang="zh-CN">。</span>为了提升青少年对运动的兴趣，</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">2015</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">年新创优胜现金奖，由专门出售运动鞋公司</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">Shoe City</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">负责人应礼迪按往例提供。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">优胜现金奖限在</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">100</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">公尺项目<span lang="zh-CN">。</span>凡男子破</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">10.90</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">秒，女子破</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">11.40</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">秒大会<span lang="zh-CN">纪</span><span lang="zh-CN">录</span>者，可获</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">1000</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN">美元奖金</span>，若有成绩相等则奖金平分<span lang="zh-CN">；</span>其它各年龄男<span lang="zh-CN">、</span>女组，凡得本年度最佳成绩者</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">(</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">即各年龄组分男，女各取一名</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">)</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">，<span lang="zh-CN">获</span>奖金</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">100</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN">美</span>元<span lang="zh-CN">；</span>凡各分组第一名者获奖金</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">20</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW"><span lang="zh-CN">美</span>元，亦不限组别<span lang="zh-CN">。</span>但是若已经领取该年龄组最佳奖金者，将不可重复领取。</span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">运动大会上午</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">8</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">时</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">30</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span lang="zh-TW">分举行开幕典礼，主办单位美国中华体育联谊会与协办单位南加州中文学校联合会，南加州中国大专联合校友会，将邀请驻洛杉矶台北经济文化办事处夏季昌处长以及各侨团领袖，地方首长共同揭幕。</span></span></span></p>
