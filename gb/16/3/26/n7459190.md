  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">按计划，</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">5</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">月</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">11</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">日，加州将要求商店在收银台张贴告示，警告</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">BPA</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">的危险，提示一些罐装和瓶装产品的内衬含有毒化学品。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">加州环保局（</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">California Environmental Protection Agency</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">）的这一决定使社区和公共健康组织很愤怒。内科医生社会责任洛杉矶组织的执行经理阿桂洛（</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">Martha Dina Arguello</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">）说，不让人们知道食品中有些什么，这太荒唐了。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">很多食品制造商在罐头、瓶子和坛子的环氧树脂内衬中使用</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">BPA</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">。但是，一些研究已经确定，</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">BPA</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">是一种类雌激素化学物质，含量高时会伤害女性生殖系统。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">这个警告规定来自加州</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">1986</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">年通过的</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">65</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">号法案，该法案要求商家通知公众食品中和其它地方所含的化学品。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">去年，加州官员决定将</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">BPA</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">加到</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">65</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">号法案要求的化学品通知清单中，该清单列出了</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">800</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">多种危险化学品。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">但是这一决定是有争议的。美国食品和药品管理局（</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">FDA</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">）在</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">2012</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">年禁止了在婴儿奶瓶和鸭嘴杯中使用</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">BPA</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">，但该局也说食品中的</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">BPA</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">浓度是安全的。联邦机构还在等待更多研究结果。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><a name="result_box1"></a><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="color: #66cc00;"><span style="font-size: medium;">加州</span></span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;">EPA </span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">环境健康和有害物质评估办公室（</span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">EPA&#8217;s Office of Environmental Health Hazard Assessment</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">）</span></span></span><span style="color: #66cc00;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-TW">首席副主任</span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;">郝什（</span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: medium;">Allan Hirsch</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><span style="font-size: medium;">）说，州府会通常要求制造商在罐头上贴警告标识，或者让<span style="color: #cc0000;">零售商</span>在罐装食品的货架上张贴警告：某个牌子的西红柿酱或某个品牌的绿豆的外罐含有害化学品等等。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">然而，州官员说，这可能有些混乱，零售商可能会因此而将罐装和瓶装食品全部下架。这对没有好杂货店的社区来说是个坏消息。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">郝什说：“我们想确保居民，特别是低收入社区居民仍旧能买到罐装水果和蔬菜。”他还承认听取了一些零售商对实施警告的担心。</span></span></span></span></p>
  <p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">罐装行业的北美金属包装联盟执行经理罗伯特（</span></span></span><span style="font-size: medium;">Kathleen Roberts</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span lang="zh-CN">）说，这些警告会进一步限制健康选择，特别是内城社区和农村地区低收入家庭的健康选择。 ◇</span></span></span></span></p>
