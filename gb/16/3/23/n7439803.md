<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>巡视组杀“回马枪” 湖南1天3厅级官员落马</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/06/1206241122452054-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>中国传统文化中素来有善恶有报之说。法轮功修炼团体反迫害13年来，中共各级大小官员都有人参与迫害，其中迫害的主要几个省、市、县的政法委系统、公安系统的相关书记，公安局长，这些年来遭到恶报的被披露出来的也越来越多。 (Feng Li/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年03月23日讯】<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">（大纪元记者张顿综合报导）<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%BA%AA%E5%A7%94.md#1">中纪委</a>巡视组今年</span></span></span>2<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月对湖南省杀“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9B%9E%E9%A9%AC%E6%9E%AA.md#1">回马枪</a>”后，</span></span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月</span></span></span>23<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">日，中共湖南省</span></span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">名厅级官员落马。分析认为，湖南作为中央巡视组“回头看”的四个省份之一，可能将会有“重量级人物”落马。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><b>湖南</b></span></span></span><b>3</b><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><b>厅官同一天落马</b></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">中共湖南纪委官网</span></span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月</span></span></span>23<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">日发布通报，湖南日报报业集团有限公司（湖南日报社）党组副书记、社务委员、总经理皮林涉嫌“严重违纪”被调查。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">湖南日报报业集团有限公司（湖南日报社）党组成员、社务委员<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E6%A0%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">刘树林</a>涉嫌“严重违纪”被调查。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">湖南省政府办公厅原副厅级干部<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E7%88%B1%E5%9B%BD.md#1">欧爱国</a>涉嫌“严重违纪”被调查。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">今年</span></span></span>6<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月将满</span></span></span>57<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">岁的皮林是湖南湘乡市人。曾任湘潭市宣传部副部长，湘潭市文化局党委书记、局长，湖南日报社社务委员、副总编辑等职务。</span></span></span>2015<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">年</span></span></span>5<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月至今任湖南日报报业集团有限公司（湖南日报社）党组副书记、社务委员、总经理。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">今年</span></span></span>12<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月将满</span></span></span>60<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">岁的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%88%98%E6%A0%91%E6%9E%97.md#1">刘树林</a>也是湖南人。曾任湖南日报印刷厂副厂长，湖南日报传媒公司副总经理、湖南日报发行有限公司董事长等职务，</span></span></span>2009<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">年</span></span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月至今任湖南日报报业集团有限公司（湖南日报社）党组成员、社务委员。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">现年</span></span></span>62<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">岁的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%AC%A7%E7%88%B1%E5%9B%BD.md#1">欧爱国</a>是湖南衡阳人。曾任湖南宾馆党委书记、总经理，省政府办公厅接待办副主任等职务，</span></span></span>2011<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">年</span></span></span>4<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月至</span></span></span>2014<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">年</span></span></span>6<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月任省政府办公厅副厅级干部，</span></span></span>2014<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">年</span></span></span>6<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月退休。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><b><ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%BA%AA%E5%A7%94.md#1">中纪委</a>对湖南杀“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9B%9E%E9%A9%AC%E6%9E%AA.md#1">回马枪</a>” 或牵出大“老虎”</b></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">中纪委在</span></span></span>2<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月</span></span></span>23<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">日宣布，中央首轮巡视对辽宁、安徽、山东、湖南等</span></span></span>4<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">个省进行“回头看”。这是“十八大”后中央巡视组首次杀“回马枪”。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">评论认为，巡视组选中上述</span></span></span>4<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">个省进行“回头看”，应该是“检查该发现的问题有没有遗漏”，因为上述</span></span></span>4<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">省中，仅有</span></span></span>6<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">名副省部级官员落马，其中政协副主席就有</span></span></span>4<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">个。尤其作为江派势力窝点的辽宁、山东和湖南，跟随中共前党魁江泽民“闷声发大财”，自然贪腐严重，也是最容易与习当局唱反调的地方之一，岂止仅有</span></span></span>4<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">副省部级官员贪腐，4省</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">或有“重量级官员落马”。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">今年中共两会期间，中纪委<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;">“回头看”</span></span>的辽宁省，</span></span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">先后</span></span></span>有2<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">名“老虎”落马，他们是中共辽宁省人大副主任王阳和中共前辽宁省委书记王珉。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">有评论认为，如今，中共湖南纪委一天之内宣布</span></span></span>3<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">名官员落马，也算是在<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;">湖南</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;">“</span></span>打虎<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;">”</span></span>取得了初步成果。后续或牵连出更大的“老虎”。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><b><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%96%E5%8D%97%E5%AE%98%E5%9C%BA.md#1">湖南官场</a>被清洗 </b></span></span></span><b>12</b><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN"><b>名常委被撤换</b></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">湖南是江泽民集团的一个重要窝点，也是迫害法轮功的重灾区。江派大佬周永康的心腹、原河北省委书记周本顺曾执掌湖南政法系统。现任中共湖南省委书记徐守盛被指与江派关系密切。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">习近平上任后，展开对江派窝点清洗，湖南也有多名官员落马，仅去年被查的就有湖南日报社党组书记、社长覃晓光，湖南日报社党组成员、社务委员薛伯清，湖南省委组织部原副部长、省委老干部局原局长常世雄，徐守盛的大秘之一、湖南省委副秘书长马勇等。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">同时，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B9%96%E5%8D%97%E5%AE%98%E5%9C%BA.md#1">湖南官场</a>高层人事也不断地震。去年</span></span></span>10<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月，中共湖南省常务副省长陈肇雄被调任工信部副部长，至此与</span></span></span>2011<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">年</span></span></span>11<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">月这届湖南省委人员相比，除了组织部长郭开朗仍担任原职外，其余</span></span></span>12<span style="font-family: SimSun;"><span style="font-size: small;"><span lang="zh-CN">名常委已全部易人。#</span></span></span></p>
