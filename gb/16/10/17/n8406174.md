<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【黏土捏塑】漂亮的玫瑰胸针</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/04/Eisenhower_visits_Taiwan_June_1960-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>玫瑰是爱情之花，西方人于情人节送上玫瑰表达心意，不同数量的玫瑰代表不同含义的爱情。</p>
<p>我喜欢玫瑰，不在它的花语而在它的花瓣层次感，层层圈圈略有变化却又都遵守着生长规律，这样的特质让它在我的眼中非常耐看。找个胸针，将玫瑰做成可以别在衣服上装饰的胸针，别有韵味。动手做一个永不凋谢的玫瑰胸针，或送给爱的人，让爱更保鲜。</p>
<p><strong>材料：</strong>质地较细的超轻土、美工刀、保丽龙胶、亮粉胶水、水彩笔、L形资料夹或塑胶板（这是避免桌面有油渍不好处理）、压克力颜料、压板（本范例采用一片椭圆形小木板）、润肤乳。</p>
<p><strong>做法：</strong></p>
<p><strong> 1. </strong>取一份约7克左右的超轻土，先切下3个约小指头大的小土球，再分别搓成圆球。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406208" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1382-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1382" width="324" b="216" /></p>
<p><strong>?? 2. </strong>用约1粒绿豆大小的润肤乳量，将压板双面涂一涂、L形资料夹使用面上也涂上润肤乳，避免圆球压平后黏住压板及资料夹。</p>
<p><strong>?3. </strong>将小圆球放在资料夹涂了润肤乳的地方，用压板略为倾斜的一一将3个圆球压出一边较薄一边稍厚的圆形。圆形取下后，可以用手指在将薄处稍为捏整一下，让花办呈现更美的边缘。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406209" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1385-450x304.jpg" alt="img_1385" width="350" b="237" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406212" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1393-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1393" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><strong>4.</strong> 将3片圆形依序叠在一起，这里要注意的是薄的一端在上，厚端在下，并呈放射状。这是国立台中女中教师郑如娟透露的技巧诀窍，这可以让玫瑰卷起后的内圈有漂亮的花形，奠定后续花瓣卷起花苞形状的基础，以免卷成包心菜的样子。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406213" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1394-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1394" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406214" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1395-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1395" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><strong>5. </strong>将上图中最右端的圆形像卷甜筒那样卷起来。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406215" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1397-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1397" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><strong>6. </strong>再依序将中间与最左端的圆形卷起，3片圆形都卷合好后，可将下部看起来有点多余的土捏起来，让花苞较短一些，因最后要将花苞末端切下部分土，以黏上胸针。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406216" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1401-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1401" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406217" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1405-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1405" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<figure id="attachment_8406218" style="width: 342px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8406218" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1407-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1407" width="342" b="228" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">用手指将花瓣边缘稍为往外拨、整形。</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>7. </strong>若想做渐层的玫瑰花朵，每增一片花瓣就要加入白色土调和。方法是用第1次小圆球的2/3量加第1次小圆球量的白色土调色。越后面增加的花瓣都要比上一片花瓣稍为大一点，这样花形会比较漂亮。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406219" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1409-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1409" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><strong>8. </strong>用压片将调好色的圆球斜向压平(同步骤三)、整形薄边，与成形花苞的最后一片花瓣略重叠后继续卷花苞，用食指将花瓣边缘往外拨整，后续的调色、搓圆球、压平、整薄边、卷花苞、拨整花办动作都是重复的。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406220" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1411-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1411" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406223" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1415-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1415" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406221" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1413-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1413" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406226" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1420-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1420" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406200" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1421-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1421" width="334" b="222" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406201" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1422-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1422" width="333" b="222" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1424-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1424" width="322" b="215" /></p>
<p><strong>9. </strong>花朵完成后，放着待干，隔4小时后或是隔天才做切除多余土，再与与胸针做黏合。胸针必须先将4个尖角用尖嘴钳往黏合面翻折使其立起，以便夹住花朵底部，如下图。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406205" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1477-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1477" width="341" b="227" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1429-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1429" width="341" b="227" /></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406204" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1474-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1474" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><strong>10. </strong>于胸针黏合面涂上一点保丽龙胶，将胸针插入花朵底部，黏合，如下图。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406206" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1480-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1480" width="342" b="228" /></p>
<p><strong>11. </strong>用水彩笔沾亮粉胶水将花瓣一一涂上一些，使花朵更吸睛、具有高雅感，切勿涂太多，以免看起来很俗气喔。完成。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-8406207" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/IMG_1481-450x300.jpg" alt="img_1481" width="342" b="228" /></p>
