  <p>白鹭鸶<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%90%88%E5%94%B1%E5%9B%A2.md#1">合唱团</a>原本是<wbr />一个女声合唱团，<span lang="AR-SA">由指挥陈慧中创团于</span><span lang="EN-US">1996</span><span lang="AR-SA">年，借着歌声散播音<wbr />乐与友情的种子。经常参加<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>社区各种多元文化活动。</span></p>
  <p><span lang="AR-SA">先后通过与</span><span lang="EN-US">Elektra Women’s Choir</span><span lang="AR-SA">、日裔</span><span lang="EN-US">Sakura Singers</span><span lang="AR-SA">、兰韵中乐团、</span><span lang="EN-US">Turning Point Ensemble</span><span lang="AR-SA">、</span><span lang="EN-US">Vancouver Chamber Choir</span><span lang="AR-SA">、</span>Borealis String Quartet<span lang="AR-SA">等交流演出，将台湾合唱音乐，介绍给其他族裔爱乐<wbr />人士。并多次参与<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>亚裔传统月</span><span lang="EN-US">explorASIAN</span><span lang="AR-SA">、<wbr />台湾文化节与西雅图美国台湾传统周演出。</span><span lang="EN-US">2002</span><span lang="AR-SA">年荣获台湾教育<wbr />部，评选为“九十一年度推展社会教育有功团体”，<wbr />是唯一获此殊荣的海外团体。</span><span lang="EN-US">2007曾</span><span lang="AR-SA">受邀代表台湾参加</span>Musi<wbr />cFest Vancouver<span lang="AR-SA">“</span><span lang="EN-US">Sounds of Asia</span><span lang="AR-SA">”。</span></p>
  <div>  <div><span lang="AR-SA">?</span><span lang="EN-US">2004</span><span lang="AR-SA">年水牛男声合唱团成军，</span><span lang="EN-US">2007</span><span
