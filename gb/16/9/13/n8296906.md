<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>纽约客推荐 来大都会这25件事值得做(上)</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/03/081886a9518cf1a9a36f51655b55d5a7-600x400-1-1-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年09月14日讯】（大纪元记者陈洁云报导）造访纽约无疑是激动人心的体验。无论你来过多少次，这座世界最大都会总有成千上万的新奇事物等你去探寻。听听当地人的建议，无疑可省去很多踌躇。下面就是纽约客眼中最值得做的一些事。</p>
<p><strong>1. 花一下午，到布鲁克林跳蚤市场（Brooklyn Flea）闲逛淘宝，品尝街食。百余个旧货摊位和三十多个小吃摊位定期聚集在布鲁克林的几个社区和公园，是当地人很喜光顾之地。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8296989" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131856312669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8296989" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131856312669-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">布鲁克林跳蚤市场。(NilsPix/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="http://brooklynflea.com/" target="_blank">详情点此</a>（英文官网，下同）。</p>
<p><strong>2. 在中央公园西侧占地6万多平方米的绵羊草原（Sheep Meadow）晒太阳、聚餐，地点靠近西66街到69街之间。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8296993" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131849192669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8296993" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131849192669-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中央公园绵羊草原。(ep_jhu/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="http://www.centralparknyc.org/things-to-see-and-do/attractions/sheep-meadow.md#1" target="_blank">详情点此。 </a></p>
<p><strong>3. 到皇后区法拉盛吃广式早茶。君豪酒楼的榴莲点心，敦城酒家的流沙包、凤爪和豆腐花，明都大酒楼的叉烧包，还有东溢丰大酒楼的姜汁豆花，都受到中西食客推荐。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8296994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131854352669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8296994" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131854352669-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法拉盛的广式早茶。(lulun &amp; kame/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>4. 到布鲁克林植物园（Brooklyn Botanic Garden）漫步。这里种有二百多棵樱桃树，还有玫瑰园和日式山水花园。每周六上午和周二可免票进入。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8296990" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131850392669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8296990" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131850392669-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">布鲁克林植物园。(Steven Severinghaus/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="http://www.bbg.org/" target="_blank">详情点此。 </a></p>
<p><strong>5. 到上西城的烘焙坊Levain Bakery，品尝“全世界最好吃的饼干”。开业22年，店内只出售四款饼干，包括巧克力核桃、燕麦葡萄干、黑巧克力片和黑巧克力花生酱风味，还有另外数种点心。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8297736" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131850542669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8297736" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131850542669-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Levain Bakery的饼干。(Gary Wong/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="http://www.levainbakery.com/" target="_blank">详情点此。 </a></p>
<p><strong>6. 在联合广场（Union Square）的农夫市场（Greenmarket）买在地农产品。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8297029" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131850272669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8297029" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131850272669-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">联合广场的农夫市场。(Mat McDermott/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="http://www.grownyc.org/about" target="_blank">详情点此。 </a></p>
<p><strong>7. 逛逛藏品丰富的布鲁克林博物馆，到邻近的展望公园（Prospect Park）散步。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8297011" style="width: 384px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131853012669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8297011" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131853012669.jpg" alt="" width="384" b="316" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">布鲁克林博物馆馆藏萨金特画作。（公有领域）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8296998" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131853202669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8296998" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131853202669-600x237.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="237" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">展望公园。(Garry R. Osgood, Wikimedia Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p>详情点<ahref="https://www.brooklynmuseum.org/" target="_blank">这里</a>和<ahref="https://www.prospectpark.org/" target="_blank">这里</a>。</p>
<p><strong>8. 2017年1月中旬，</strong><strong>一票难求的</strong><strong>“世界第一秀”神韵演出会再度于</strong><strong>林肯表演艺术中心上演七场。平日里，林肯中心的爵士乐社（Jazz at Lincoln Center）有现场表演，中央公园和哥伦布圆环区（Columbus Circle）的景色也可收于眼底。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_7409775" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603131733551973.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-7409775" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/03/1603131733551973-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2016年3月13日，神韵纽约艺术团在纽约林肯中心大卫寇克剧院的演出再次爆满。（戴兵／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8297030" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131850162669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8297030" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131850162669-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">林肯中心爵士乐社的演出。(Ilya S. Savenok/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="http://zh-tw.shenyunperformingarts.org/nynj" target="_blank">神韵演出详情点此。 </a><br />
<ahref="http://www.jazz.org/" target="_blank">爵士乐社详情点此。 </a></p>
<p><strong>9. 到位于布鲁克林皇冠高地（Crown Heights）的Berg&#8217;n Food Hall吃夜宵。这座大啤酒屋云集了纽约著名街食，包括麦笛昆烧烤（MIGHTY QUINN&#8217;S BBQ）、意大利Tramezzini三明治、菲律宾风味春卷Lumpia Shack等等。（早上10点就开门营业。）</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8297014" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131849102669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8297014" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131849102669-600x449.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="449" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Edsel Little/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="http://www.bergn.com/" target="_blank">详情点此。</a></p>
<p><strong>10. 租辆脚踏车，沿哈德逊河畔骑行。沿途有很多地方可以逗留，从事各种活动的地点可从<ahref="https://www.hudsonriverpark.org/explore-the-park/map#search=activities" target="_blank">这张地图</a>中搜寻。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8297032" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131849272669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8297032" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131849272669-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">哈德逊河畔。(gigi_nyc/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://www.hudsonriverpark.org/explore-the-park/map#search=activities" target="_blank">详情点此。 </a></p>
<p><strong>11. 参观布鲁克林区威廉斯堡的Mast Brothers巧克力工厂。游客可在这家手工巧克力工厂“一日游”，观摩巧克力的制作过程，并品尝美味的产品。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8297036" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131850492669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8297036" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131850492669-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Mast Brothers巧克力工厂。(Premshree Pillai/Flickr )</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://mastbrothers.com/" target="_blank">详情点此。 </a></p>
<p><strong>12. 在曼哈顿翠贝卡（Tribeca）街区的艾尔古浴场（Aire Ancient Baths）放松休憩。这座异国风情的水疗中心，灵感来自古代罗马的浴场。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8297022" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131849232669.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-8297022" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609131849232669-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">艾尔古浴场。(AIRE Ancient Baths/Facebook)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="http://www.ancientbathsny.com/" target="_blank">详情点此。 </a></p>
