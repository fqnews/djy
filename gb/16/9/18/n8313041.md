  <h3>1. 弗吉尼亚州雪兰多<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">国家公园</a>天际线大道</h3>  <p>Skyline Drive, Shenandoah National Park, VA</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8313065" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422222669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8313065 size-small" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422222669-300x454.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="454" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">弗吉尼亚州雪兰多<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%85%AC%E5%9B%AD.md#1">国家公园</a>天际线大道(Flickr: premierehdr)</figcaption></figure>  <p>这条大道沿雪兰多国家公园蓝岭山脉（Blue Ridge Mountains）的顶峰绵延105英里（约170公里）。这是贯穿整个园区的唯一公路。沿途共有75个眺望点，可供游客<span class="st">“停车坐爱枫林晚”</span>；且车辆限速35英里，这样就保证不会错过任何华美的秋色。</p>
  <h3>2. 佛蒙特州斯托</h3>  <p>Stowe, Vermont</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8313071" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181442162669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8313071 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181442162669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">佛蒙特州斯托(Flickr:Anthony Quintano)</figcaption></figure>  <p>离美国乡村音乐之家纳什维尔不远的斯托（Stowe）拥有众多滑雪场，是著名的山区度假胜地，那里的秋天红叶也是一绝。<ahref="http://www.gostowe.com/thingstodo/seasonal-activities/autumn/foliage" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点此查看斯托的秋季游乐项目</a>，也可关注<ahref="http://www.vermont.com/foliage.cfm" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">佛蒙特州实时更新的秋色报告</a>，以确定最佳观赏时间。</p>
  <h3>3. 田纳西州大雾山国家公园</h3>  <p>Great Smoky Mountains National Park, TN</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8313067" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422262669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8313067 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422262669-450x294.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="294" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">田纳西州大雾山国家公园(Timothy Wildey CC BY-NC / Via Flickr: timothywildey)</figcaption></figure>  <p>大雾山公园被认为是美国南部秋色最美的景区之一。你还可通过<ahref="http://www.facebook.com/GreatSmokyMountainsNPS" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">公园脸书</a>及<ahref="https://www.nps.gov/grsm/planyourvisit/fallcolor.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">网站</a>上的网络摄像头画面实时观察色调的变化。</p>
  <p>近日，公园网站还推出全美秋叶变色地图（<ahref="https://smokymountains.com/fall-foliage-map/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点阅查看</a>），各色图标中，红色为最佳观赏时段，褐色则为末后时段。</p>
  <h3>4. 加利福尼亚莫诺县</h3>  <p>Mono County, CA</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8313068" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422292669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8313068 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422292669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">加利福尼亚莫诺县(Howard Ignatius CC BY-NC-ND / Via Flickr: howardignatius)</figcaption></figure>  <p>没错，加州确实有秋天，而且美得惊人。莫诺县位于加州东部山脉（Eastern Sierra）区域，是西海岸秋景最佳的地带之一。<ahref="http://www.monocounty.org/things-to-do/sightseeing/fall-colors/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">点击这里可查看秋色报告</a>。</p>
  <h3>5. 纽约州阿迪朗达克</h3>  <p>The Adirondacks, NY</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8313070" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422312669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8313070 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422312669-450x281.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="281" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">纽约州阿迪朗达克(Decaseconds CC BY-NC / Via Flickr: decaseconds)</figcaption></figure>  <p>点击可查看<ahref="http://www.iloveny.com/seasons/fall/#.V97KrTX5rIV" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">阿迪朗达克秋季游玩项目一览</a>。其中的亮点是10月间在普莱西德湖（Lake Placid）举办的<ahref="http://www.iloveny.com/includes/calendar-of-events/Flaming-Leaves-Festival/28571/#.V97KszX5rIV" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">红叶节（The Flaming Leaves Festival）</a>。同样，你可通过追踪纽<ahref="http://www.iloveny.com/seasons/fall/foliage-report/#.V97KtTX5rIV" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">约州秋色报告</a>确定最佳造访时机。</p>
  <h3>6. 新罕布什尔州白山地区</h3>  <p>White Mountain Region, NH</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8313074" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422352669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8313074 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422352669-450x338.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">新罕布什尔州白山地区(Jennifer Cochran CC BY-NC-ND / Via Flickr: cochranhumbird4)</figcaption></figure>  <p>新罕布什尔州的白山山脉是北阿巴拉契亚山的一部分，面积覆盖全州的四分之一（也延伸到缅因州）。游客沿山间高速公路<ahref="http://www.kancamagushighway.com/foliage/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Kancamagus Highway</a>开车，可将秋色尽收眼底。这里也被公认为全美观赏秋景的最佳地点之一。</p>
  <h3>7. 科罗拉多州阿斯彭市</h3>  <p>Aspen, CO</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8313075" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422392669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8313075 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422392669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">科罗拉多州阿斯彭市马隆贝尔斯(j.casey.oneill CC BY-NC-ND / Via Flickr: dirkoneill)</figcaption></figure>  <p>图中这些金黄色的树木居然叫白杨树（Aspen tree）！白杨是科罗拉多州典型的林木。而让它们得名的市镇阿斯彭（Aspen）可称得上观赏白杨的最佳地点了。<ahref="http://www.aspenchamber.org/explore-aspen/trip-highlights/fall-foliage" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">当地众多秋景区域</a>中，图中的马隆贝尔斯（Maroon Bells）是其一，那里有埃尔克山的两座山峰。</p>
  <h3>8. 麻州波士顿公园</h3>  <p>Boston Common, MA</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8313076" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422422669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8313076 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422422669-450x336.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">麻州波士顿公园(Bill Ilott CC BY-NC-ND)</figcaption></figure>  <p>作为该州历史最悠久的公园，波士顿公园的秋叶也一直美不胜收。何时成行？请参看<ahref="http://www.leafpeepers.com/mass.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">麻州秋色报告</a>。</p>
  <h3>9. 俄勒冈州威拉梅特谷</h3>  <p>Willamette Valley, OR</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8313079" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422452669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8313079 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422452669-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">俄勒冈州威拉梅特谷(Robert Crum/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>  <p>威拉梅特谷长150英里（约240公里），是俄勒冈州人口的主要聚居地，分布着200多个葡萄酒庄园，秋季到来时，色彩十分艳丽。点阅<ahref="https://oregonfallfoliage.wordpress.com/regions/willamette-valley/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">葡萄酒之乡的一些游览胜地</a>。</p>
  <h3>10. 缅因州阿卡迪亚国家公园</h3>  <p>Acadia National Park, ME</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8313080" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422482669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8313080 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422482669-450x336.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">缅因州阿卡迪亚国家公园(Flickr: heipei)</figcaption></figure>  <p>山脉，海洋，新鲜空气，阿卡迪亚国家公园可说应有尽有，北大西洋海岸最高峰凯迪拉克山（Cadillac Mountain）也在公园里。攀到山顶可“一览众山小”，沿途也有很多观景点。这里是<ahref="http://www.maine.gov/dacf/mfs/projects/fall_foliage/index.shtml" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">缅因州秋色观测的官方网站</a>。</p>
  <h3>11. 缅因州卡姆登山国家公园</h3>  <p>Camden Hills State Park, ME</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8313084" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422502669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8313084 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1609181422502669-450x336.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">缅因州卡姆登山国家公园(Holly Ladd CC BY-NC-ND)</figcaption></figure>  <p>尽管阿卡迪亚揽去很多人气，但靠近罗克波特的卡姆登山国家公园也美得惊人。园内自然小径长达30英里（约48公里），和阿卡迪亚一样，也是个山海交会之地。</p>
