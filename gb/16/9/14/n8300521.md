  <p><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E4%BC%A6%E5%A4%9A.md#1">多伦多</a>地区演出时间为<span lang="EN-US"><span style="font-family: Calibri;">9</span></span>月<span lang="EN-US"><span style="font-family: Calibri;">16</span></span>日（星期五）晚上<span lang="EN-US"><span style="font-family: Calibri;">7</span></span>时在<span lang="EN-US"><span style="font-family: Calibri;">Toronto Centre For The Arts</span></span>，地址：<span lang="EN-US"><span style="font-family: Calibri;">5040 Yonge St., Toronto, ON M2N 6R8</span></span></span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-size: medium;">。</span> <span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">欢迎全侨各界及社区人士购票欣赏，票价<span lang="EN-US"><span style="font-family: Calibri;">$40</span></span>，<span lang="EN-US"><span style="font-family: Calibri;"> $25</span></span>，<span lang="EN-US"><span style="font-family: Calibri;"> article content begin -->5.</span></span>售票联络处：邵良昊<span lang="EN-US"><span style="font-family: Calibri;">416-688-5064</span></span>，杨俊宏<span lang="EN-US"><span style="font-family: Calibri;">416-567-7568。</span></span></span></span></p>
