<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Grand Hyatt 凯悦酒店的法式料理</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2016年09月04日讯】(文章来源纽约GPS提供)</p>
<p><strong>每年，纽约很多知名餐厅都会推出优惠活动。除了一年两季的纽约餐馆周(NYC Restaurant Week) ，这些餐厅还会提供价格优惠的限时早午餐套餐（Brunch）或者下午茶（Happy Hour)。今天我们来到世界知名的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%AF%E6%82%A6%E9%85%92%E5%BA%97.md#1">凯悦酒店</a>Grand Hyatt 品尝他们的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A4%8F%E5%AD%A3.md#1">夏季</a>最新菜单。</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8265823" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8265823 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/GRAND-HYATT2-450x300.jpg" alt="GRAND HYATT Photo by Annie Zhuo／黄瓜薄荷冷汤" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Photo by Annie Zhuo／黄瓜薄荷冷汤Cool Creamy Cucumber Mint Gazpacho</figcaption></figure>
<p>第一道前菜我们选了开胃冷汤，刚入口是有点咸咸的黄瓜味道，接下去奶香便在口中扩散。清爽的薄荷味打底，顿时让你口味大开，迫不及待下一道主菜上席。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8265826" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-8265826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/GRAND-HYATT3-450x300.jpg" alt="芝士番茄Greenmarket Heirloom Tomatoes and Burrata" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Photo by Annie Zhuo/芝士番茄Greenmarket Heirloom Tomatoes and Burrata</figcaption></figure>
<p>Burrata是法式料理中经常出现的一种芝士，不过跟番茄一起食用，小编也是第一次尝试。甜甜的番茄加上弹牙的芝士，不禁赞叹厨师的巧思创意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8265830" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-8265830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/GRAND-HYATT4-450x300.jpg" alt="Photo by Annie Zhuo／Mac n Cheese" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Photo by Annie Zhuo／Mac n Cheese</figcaption></figure>
<p>Mac n Cheese在美国应该是大众美食，从小孩到大人无不对其着迷。大厨Daron Lee又是怎么让这道家常菜变成顶级法式料理的呢？除了常见的食材，溏心蛋是这道主菜的精华，高温后的蛋黄没有完全凝固，配上浓浓的芝士，跟通心粉一起送入口中。。。想起来，小编又要开始流口水了。。。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8265832" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-8265832" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/GRAND-HYATT5-450x450.jpg" alt="Photo by Annie Zhuo／龙虾卷Lobster Benedict" width="450" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Photo by Annie Zhuo／龙虾卷Lobster Benedict</figcaption></figure>
<p>龙虾班式迪克：刚刚烘烤好的面包加上新鲜的龙虾肉，再洒上鲜嫩的蛋液，味道好极了！</p>
<figure id="attachment_8265834" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-8265834" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/GRAND-HYATT6-450x369.jpg" alt="Photo by Annie Zhuo／秘制茄子煲Baked Eggplant Skillet" width="450" b="369" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Photo by Annie Zhuo／秘制茄子煲Baked Eggplant Skillet</figcaption></figure>
<p>这道秘制烤茄子有点像中国的鱼香茄子煲，口感却更加细腻，只是味道略咸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8265835" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-8265835" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/GRAND-HYATT7-450x300.jpg" alt="Photo by Annie Zhuo/三文鱼披萨Smoked Salmon Flatbread" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Photo by Annie Zhuo/三文鱼披萨Smoked Salmon Flatbread</figcaption></figure>
<p>烟熏三文鱼加薄片披萨面包，全是自然味道。再配点红酒，就能把你的味蕾推向高潮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8265836" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-8265836" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/GRAND-HYATT8-450x300.jpg" alt="Left:Brown Butter Nutella &amp;Summer Peaches Right:Bruleed Raisin French Toast with Banana Photo by Annie Zhuo" width="450" b="300" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Left:Brown Butter Nutella &amp;Summer Peaches Right:Bruleed Raisin French Toast with Banana<br />Photo by Annie Zhuo</figcaption></figure>
<p>甜点从来都是我们这些食客最关心的部分：特质的黄油面包以及劲道的口感，再配上香蕉冰激凌和黄桃与巧克力酱，真是完美的搭配！</p>
<figure id="attachment_8265839" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-8265839" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/GRAND-HYATT9-450x541.jpg" alt="Chef de Cuisine Daron Lee Photo by Annie Zhuo" width="450" b="541" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Chef de Cuisine Daron Lee<br />Photo by Annie Zhuo</figcaption></figure>
<p>你一定想不到，这些富有创意的法式料理，居然是由一位名叫Daron Lee的年轻华裔主厨发明的。</p>
