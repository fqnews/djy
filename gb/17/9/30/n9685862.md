<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>金钟红毯剪影 罗志祥格纹时尚 宏正丝绒稳重</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/1709301114441487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>第52届电视金钟奖红毯于30日晚间在台北国父纪念馆登场，图为男星（左起）蓝正龙、张赫、罗志祥与SpeXial团长宏正。（许基东／大纪元合成）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年09月30日讯】（大纪元记者郑宜芬台湾台北报导）第52届<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%B5%E8%A7%86%E9%87%91%E9%92%9F%E5%A5%96.md#1">电视金钟奖</a>于30日在台北国父纪念馆登场，今年星光大道男星，多以深蓝、深红、深绿色系，以及丝绒、皮革、缇花等材质展现秋冬时尚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9686317" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/170930101155100088.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9686317" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/170930101155100088.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">韩星<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E8%B5%AB.md#1">张赫</a>。（许基东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>韩星<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%BC%A0%E8%B5%AB.md#1">张赫</a>黑色丝绒西装散发熟男帅气，这次访台与金钟影后柯佳嬿担任颁奖嘉宾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9686316" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/170930101121100088.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9686316" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/170930101121100088.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小猪（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%BF%97%E7%A5%A5.md#1">罗志祥</a>）身穿紫红色系的格纹西装与高领内搭。（许基东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BD%97%E5%BF%97%E7%A5%A5.md#1">罗志祥</a>（小猪）身穿紫红色系的格纹西装与高领内搭；王仁甫、孙协志、许孟哲《饥饿游戏》节目团队。三帅以皮裤、紫红色营造秋冬神秘感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9686324" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/170930101023100088.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9686324" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/170930101023100088.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="677" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（左起）许孟哲、孙协志、王仁甫以皮裤、紫红色营造秋冬神秘感。（许基东／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
