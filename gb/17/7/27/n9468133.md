<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>这些奇珍异果 你吃过几个？</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年07月27日讯】（大纪元记者宋清宁澳洲墨尔本编译报导）很多人的人生目标是游遍世界，这应该算是个很普遍的梦想。但有人想尝遍天下的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A5%87%E7%8F%8D%E5%BC%82%E6%9E%9C.md#1">奇珍异果</a>吗？如果这是你在此生想要完成的一件事，那下面这些<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>你可要尝尝。</p>
<p><center><strong>Cherimoya番荔枝（又称释迦）</strong></center></p>
<figure id="attachment_9846622" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/Cherimoya_cut_hg-e1510809411734.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-9846622" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/Cherimoya_cut_hg-e1510809411734.jpg" alt="（Hgrobe,Wikimedia Commons）" width="450" b="258" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Hgrobe,Wikimedia Commons）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这种<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B0%B4%E6%9E%9C.md#1">水果</a>尝起来很像冰淇凌。它会像奶油一样在你的口中融化，据称有退烧和治痢疾的功效。唯一的缺点是它的籽有毒。</p>
<p><center><strong>Jackfruit菠萝蜜</strong></center></p>
<figure id="attachment_9468352" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9468352" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/jackfruit-1087108_1920-450x253.jpg" alt="（Pixabay）" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>菠萝蜜树长出的果实很大，最重可达70磅（约32千克）。其果肉混合了香蕉、菠萝和芒果的味道。菠萝蜜的籽炒过之后尝起来像巧克力。</p>
<p><center><strong>Jabuticaba嘉宝果</strong></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BLWFMOxAyWq/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A1138.95%7D" width="450" b="606" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>嘉宝果的样子和味道都很像葡萄，会被用来制作果冻、葡萄酒和甜酒。在巴西人们流行用其晒干的果皮治疗哮喘。</p>
<p><center><strong>Miracle berry神秘果</strong></center><center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BTYxB02lE4f/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A910.58%7D" width="450" b="493" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center></center>果如其名，这种浆果似乎有神奇的功效：吃一粒神秘果，接下来的一小时内无论吃什么尝起来都会是甜的。</p>
<p><center><strong>Red bananas红香蕉</strong></center></p>
<figure id="attachment_9846629" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/red-banana-2614316_1920-e1510809546789.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-9846629" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/red-banana-2614316_1920-e1510809546789.jpg" alt="（Pixabay）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这种香蕉吃起来像覆盆子（raspberry）或芒果，比普通的香蕉含有更多的钾、β-胡萝卜素和维生素C。</p>
<p><center><strong>Kiwano火参果</strong></center><center></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BS3OM3wAegV/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A879.6550000000001%7D" width="450" b="454" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>又名刺角瓜、非洲蜜瓜或非洲角黄瓜。成熟的火参果尝起来像黄瓜、猕猴桃和香蕉的混合体。</p>
<p><center><strong>Tamarillo树番茄</strong></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BTZ_sT8li1b/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A406.02500000000003%7D" width="450" b="493" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>树番茄是番茄、茄子和辣椒的“亲戚”，吃起来很像酸甜的番茄和百香果（passion fruit），适合做甜点和菜肴装饰。</p>
<p><center><strong>Buddha’s hand佛手柑</strong></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/wDNeVOEjxK/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A948.8200000000002%7D" width="450" b="493" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>这种水果的果皮有紫罗兰的芳香。佛手柑的果肉很少，味道像柠檬皮。其黄色的“手指”在国外多被用于沙拉和烹调鱼，在国内通常用作中药。</p>
<p><center><strong>Salak蛇皮果</strong></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BRdNliSDVyY/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A374.765%7D" width="450" b="493" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>它名字的由来大家一看就明白了吧。蛇皮果闻起来很香，但吃起来有点怪，很像菠萝、香蕉和坚果混合在一起的味道。另外，它的果皮会扎到人，所以剥皮时要小心。</p>
<p><center><strong>Black sapote巧克力布丁果</strong></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BTDeq0VFgX1/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A934.1750000000001%7D" width="450" b="493" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>这种水果的另一个名字不及上面的可爱，叫“黑肉柿”。这应该是唯一一种“选择”用棕黑色来代表成熟的水果了。它无论是味道还是质感，都很像巧克力布丁，但却健康很多。</p>
<p><center><strong>Melothria scabra拇指西瓜</strong></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BKdh0IfAPHI/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A380.79%7D" width="450" b="493" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>这种水果看起来真可爱，像个迷你小西瓜。虽然是水果，但拇指西瓜并不甜，味道更像黄瓜，还带有一丝柠檬汁的味道。</p>
<p><center><strong>Biriba罗林果</strong></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BS7f09qFPTo/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A512.475%7D" width="450" b="518" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>这种水果成熟时，果皮会从绿色变成黄黑色。果肉吃起来像柠檬派。</p>
<p><center><strong>Sapodilla人心果</strong></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BMadTrJBilP/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A611.5250000000001%7D" width="450" b="606" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>成熟的果实味道很甜，营养价值很高。有些人心果的果核上带钩，吃之前不剔除的话，可能会钩到喉咙上。</p>
<p><center><strong>Breadfruit面包果</strong></center><center></p>
<figure id="attachment_9846637" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/ARS_breadfruit49-e1510809723802.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-9846637" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/ARS_breadfruit49-e1510809723802.jpg" alt="（AlbertCahalan~commonswiki,Wikimedia Commons）" width="450" b="257" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（AlbertCahalan~commonswiki,Wikimedia Commons）</figcaption></figure>
<p></center>这种水果可以在成熟的不同阶段吃。完全成熟时，很香甜滑润；而不太成熟的面包果吃起来像刚烤好的面包。</p>
<p><center><strong>Australian finger lime澳洲手指青柠</strong></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BTVR0CZgyUf/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A414.40500000000003%7D" width="450" b="493" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>其果实形似鱼卵，因而博得“鱼子青柠”（caviar lime）的别称。小小的果粒咬一下会爆开，汁水味道像普通的青柠。</p>
<p><center><strong>Cupua?u古布阿苏</strong></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BRKLEzIA9Ts/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A584.6700000000001%7D" width="450" b="493" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>古布阿苏是可可的“近亲”。可用来代替巧克力，但味道更复杂。</p>
<p><center><strong>Akebia木通果</strong></center><center><a id="instagram-embed-0" class="instagram-media instagram-media-rendered" src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BMMcnsrjh8k/embed/?cr=1&amp;v=7&amp;wp=658#%7B%22ci%22%3A0%2C%22os%22%3A383.84000000000003%7D" width="450" b="493" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" data-instgrm-payload-id="instagram-media-payload-0"></a></center>果肉甜香，很像覆盆子（raspberry）。吃时可以用手挖出豆?状果囊，塞入口中，然后爽快地吐籽。</p>
<p><center><strong>Hala fruit哈拉果</strong></center><center></p>
<figure id="attachment_9846645" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/Hala_tree_fruit_ripe-e1510809854253.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-9846645" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/Hala_tree_fruit_ripe-e1510809854253.jpg" alt="（Howcheng,Wikimedia Commons）" width="450" b="336" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（Howcheng,Wikimedia Commons）</figcaption></figure>
<p></center>这是一种像是来自外星的水果。果肉可以生吃，也可以烹调后吃，甚至可以被用作天然的洁牙线。果树的叶子据说有药用价值。</p>
