<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>安省议员表扬马偕牧师对台湾贡献</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年03月24日讯】马偕故乡牛津郡（Oxford County ）安省议员哈德曼<span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">（<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">?Ernie Hardeman），?</span></span>连续第二年<span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">表扬</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">马偕牧师对<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE.md#1">台湾</a>的贡献<span style="font-family: Calibri;">。</span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">今年<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">哈德曼于3 </span></span>月<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US"> 23 </span></span>日上午<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">11点45分</span></span>至下午<span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US">1点30分，</span></span>在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9C%81%E8%AE%AE%E4%BC%9A.md#1">省议会</a><span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span lang="EN-US"> Queen&#8217;s Park </span></span>正式表扬</span></span><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">马偕牧师对<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE.md#1">台湾</a>的贡献。他还发信邀请台湾乡亲参加并观礼。</span></span></p>
<figure id="attachment_8962887" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-8962887" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/03/4474b99d255bcd5ffe2441f02cdf94c1-450x188.jpg" alt="安省议员哈德曼（Ernie Hardeman）在省议会表扬马偕牧师对台湾的贡献后，出席人员合照。（梁清祥提供文图）" width="450" b="188" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">安省议员哈德曼（Ernie Hardeman）在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9C%81%E8%AE%AE%E4%BC%9A.md#1">省议会</a>表扬马偕牧师对台湾的贡献后，出席人员合照。（梁清祥提供文图）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;">出席嘉宾有驻多伦多台北经济文化办事处徐咏梅处长、<wbr />副处长洪松晖、刘宏明组长、文化中心主任李叔玲</span></span> <span style="color: #000000; font-size: medium;">、副主任欧阳群，台湾社团负责人及很多乡亲，都参加了这个非常有意义的表扬聚会。</span></p>
