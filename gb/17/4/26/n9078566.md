  <figure id="attachment_9078681" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/5d309912e49b92922f760b2903a6da5c.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-9078681" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/5d309912e49b92922f760b2903a6da5c-600x332.png" alt="台湾彰化田中稻站。（视频截图）" width="600" b="332" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台湾<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%BD%B0%E5%8C%96.md#1">彰化</a><ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%B0%E4%B8%AD%E7%A8%BB%E7%AB%99.md#1">田中稻站</a>。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>采收后的稻谷去哪儿了呢？田中的碾米厂，农民会将收获的稻谷秤重运往碾米厂，准备一连串的处理过程。诺大的碾米厂区，充满着米香，有着养活台湾人的机器！碾米厂的巨大的碾米机，选色机，正努力运作着，制造出让人幸福的优质稻米“台梗九号米”。“谁知盘中飧，粒粒皆辛苦”，一颗颗米粒的旅程，其实背后藏着很多的学问，很适合规划一趟寻米之旅。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9078680" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/fbe57553c02f0194805052ac0bd9e707.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-9078680" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/fbe57553c02f0194805052ac0bd9e707-600x335.png" alt="台梗九号米。（视频截图）" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台梗九号米。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p><strong>米学堂，制作一包自己的米回家</strong>（7分55秒处）</p>

  <p>除了可以参观壮观的大机具，还可以体验DIY制作<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E7%A6%8F%E8%A2%8B.md#1">米福袋</a>。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9078679" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/13dcaae57782db7b9b7cf066fec9038a.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-9078679" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/13dcaae57782db7b9b7cf066fec9038a-600x302.png" alt="将糙米放入碾米机。（视频截图）" width="600" b="302" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">将糙米放入碾米机。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>首先，从稻米开始，先放入古董级的碾米机来碾米去壳。黄澄澄的稻米，脱去外衣，就变成白米了，放在手中，仔细闻看看，还有一股的米糠的香气呢！</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9078678" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/e8f2f972308f6939657f83ed3a28e201.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-9078678" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/e8f2f972308f6939657f83ed3a28e201-600x332.png" alt="糙米经过碾米机变身白米。（视频截图）" width="600" b="332" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">糙米经过碾米机变身白米。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>接下来还要去芜存菁，过筛去碎米，经过精选后，留下晶莹剔透的白米小精灵！</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9078675" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/09f38be4605d121c9cb45a11a25ae833.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-9078675" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/09f38be4605d121c9cb45a11a25ae833-600x335.png" alt="过筛。（视频截图）" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">过筛。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>再来进行打包，最后装上米粒，就完成了自己专属的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E7%A6%8F%E8%A2%8B.md#1">米福袋</a>啦，好有成就感！</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9078672" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/2cd0fb4a8962e58616000c6d82a74b6e.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-9078672" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/2cd0fb4a8962e58616000c6d82a74b6e-600x335.png" alt="主持人翊绫开心完成米福袋八堡圳。（视频截图）" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">主持人翊绫开心完成米福袋。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>田中的风景，是适合放慢脚步、细细品味的农村之美。除了定点式的造访各个景点，也可以骑着协会准备的单车，沿着当地富有历史意义的八堡圳-认识田中的过去。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9078671" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/c5784f8740c5e36038a616936912f979.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-9078671" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/c5784f8740c5e36038a616936912f979-600x333.png" alt="八堡圳。（视频截图）" width="600" b="333" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">八堡圳。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>受八堡圳灌溉的土壤上，充满了各种不同的农家故事，土生土长的稻米收割后，都会送往当地的碾米厂，这些离开土地与阳光的榖粒，为了做好上市的准备，都要经过一段充满轰隆隆机械声的必经旅程。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9078670" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/2940ab091ea9df240a9ebc26b02b13e0.png"><img class="size-large wp-image-9078670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/2940ab091ea9df240a9ebc26b02b13e0-600x333.png" alt="彰化田中旅游秘笈。（视频截图）" width="600" b="333" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">彰化田中旅游秘笈。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>这本田中的武林秘笈，就在前面提到的车站旁的黄色小屋“<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%94%B0%E4%B8%AD%E7%A8%BB%E7%AB%99.md#1">田中稻站</a>”陈列，各种游玩田中的方式，任君挑选！上面记载着好吃、好玩的地方，而且还有详尽的地图，喜欢自由行的人，可以照着上面玩，还有英文翻译唷！</p>
  <p>每个地方都有它独特的故事，用他们的大自然和人文写自己的故事。受八堡圳滋养的田中镇，特产超丰富，带一包亲手碾制的新米，亲身体验稻榖的秘密旅程，到田中感受充满泥土味的古朴小镇吧。</p>
