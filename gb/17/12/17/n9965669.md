  <h3>On Vegetables Cookbook素食菜谱</h3>  <figure id="attachment_9965727" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain1_OnVeg_800-e1513496010460.png"><img class="wp-image-9965727 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain1_OnVeg_800-e1513496010460.png" alt="" width="600" b="309" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片由INFORM提供</figcaption></figure>  <p>全球闻名的加州素食大厨Jeremy Fox终于推出了众所周盼的On Vegetables素食菜谱。在这本处女作中，Fox将自己的饮食哲学融入160道食谱内，手把手教每个家庭的大厨用新颖的食材搭配和处理手法，烹调出凸显时蔬质感和鲜味的高端素菜。</p>
  <h3>HAY Time Hourglasses沙漏</h3>  <figure id="attachment_9965730" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain3_Hay_800-e1513496045806.png"><img class="wp-image-9965730 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain3_Hay_800-e1513496045806.png" alt="" width="600" b="349" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片由INFORM提供</figcaption></figure>  <p>造型柔和的HAY玻璃沙漏通过不同大小、颜色沙漏的组合摆放，将普通沙漏转变成为充满现代气息的装饰艺术。沙漏有30分钟时长、15分钟时长和3分钟时长三种尺寸可供选择。</p>
  <h3>Iittala Sarpaneva Cast Iron Pot生铁锅</h3>  <figure id="attachment_9965728" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain2_Iittala-e1513496031948.png"><img class="wp-image-9965728 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain2_Iittala-e1513496031948.png" alt="" width="600" b="315" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片由INFORM提供</figcaption></figure>  <p>微微弯曲的活动木提手、黝黑的生铁锅身，The Sarpaneva铁锅的经典设计既是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8E%A8%E6%88%BF.md#1">厨房</a>中的实用功臣，也是餐桌上的吸睛皇后。发明人Timo Sarpaneva在1960年从铁匠祖父处获得灵感，将设计感与传统生铁锅相结合，创造出The Sarpaneva铁锅，并在当年的米兰博览会（Milan Triennial）上一举拿下银奖。</p>
  <p>The Sarpaneva铁锅在灶台与烤箱内均可使用。其搪瓷内表面不会沾染食物气味，保证在多次烹饪间不串味。</p>
  <h3>Tecta M4R Console Cart桌案</h3>  <figure id="attachment_9965734" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain6_Trolly_800-1-e1513496113970.png"><img class="wp-image-9965734 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain6_Trolly_800-1-e1513496113970.png" alt="" width="600" b="283" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片由INFORM提供</figcaption></figure>  <p>Tecta M4R推车是德国制造商Tecta于2002年设计的现代多功能桌案。这件经典家具既可以当作餐车使用，又是一张帅气利落的靠墙桌台。</p>
  <h3>Bob Kramer Euroline Damascus Knives大马士革厨刀</h3>  <figure id="attachment_9965731" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain4_Knife_800-e1513496059170.png"><img class="wp-image-9965731 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain4_Knife_800-e1513496059170.png" alt="" width="600" b="375" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片由INFORM提供</figcaption></figure>  <p>6英寸大马士革刀是大师级刀匠Bob Kramer在日本关市（Seki）手工打造的全能型厨刀。刀片沉重硬朗、刀刃细巧锐利、刀柄符合人体工学，无论是剁碎蔬菜还是鱼肉切片，大马士革厨刀都是专业大厨和家庭厨师们的“梦中厨刀”。</p>
  <h3>Edward Wohl Cutting Boards 手工砧板</h3>  <figure id="attachment_9965733" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain5_Boards_800-e1513496092949.png"><img class="wp-image-9965733 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain5_Boards_800-e1513496092949.png" alt="" width="600" b="320" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片由INFORM提供</figcaption></figure>  <p>Edward Wohl凤眼枫木砧板由手工打磨而成，触感几近丝滑，每一块都有自身独特的木调与木纹，每一块也都是充满美感的艺术品。</p>
  <h3>Alessi Valerio Juicer + Muddler 榨汁搅拌棒</h3>  <figure id="attachment_9965735" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain7_Muddler_800-e1513496135947.png"><img class="wp-image-9965735 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain7_Muddler_800-e1513496135947.png" alt="" width="600" b="375" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片由INFORM提供</figcaption></figure>  <p>一间工具，两样功能。Guido Venturini榨汁搅拌棒一端为柠檬、柑橘榨汁棒，另一段则是研杵。使用任何一端时，另一段便可作为手柄，舒适抓握。由于重心在研杵一端，整件工具在桌面上能够竖直站立。</p>
  <h3>Georg Jensen Manhattan Bar Collection 乔治杰生曼哈顿系列酒器</h3>  <figure id="attachment_9965736" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain8_Jensen_800-e1513496150101.png"><img class="wp-image-9965736 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain8_Jensen_800-e1513496150101.png" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片由INFORM提供</figcaption></figure>  <p>乔治杰生曼哈顿系列酒器以镜面不銹钢打造，展现出大胆的几何线条，富有装饰作用。曼哈顿系列酒器的复古和考究，展现出调酒、品酒和享酒的精致生活态度。</p>
  <h3>Tom Dixon Tank Glassware 玻璃酒具</h3>  <figure id="attachment_9965724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain9_Glasses_800-1-e1513495991391.png"><img class="wp-image-9965724 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Entertain9_Glasses_800-1-e1513495991391.png" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">图片由INFORM提供</figcaption></figure>  <p>英国设计品牌Tom Dixon 用技术手段将透明玻璃与黑玻璃锻造熔合，创造出黑色渐变烟熏玻璃酒器，成为Tank系列口吹玻璃酒具的最新主打风格，以期使该系列成为“桌上的建筑艺术”。?◇</p>
