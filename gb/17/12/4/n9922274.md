<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年12月04日讯】你会怎么处置用过的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E7%89%B9%E7%93%B6.md#1">宝特瓶</a>呢？一般人可能会用个两三次之后就丢到资源<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9B%9E%E6%94%B6.md#1">回收</a>桶。</p>
<p>但是以下这段视频将告诉你几个超级实用的方法，绝对让你轻轻松松地就能发挥<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E7%89%B9%E7%93%B6.md#1">宝特瓶</a>的最大效用唷！</p>
<p>将瓶盖烧融。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9922415" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0015.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9922415" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0015-600x328.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="328" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">让瓶盖融化。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再用牙签戳出一个尖尖的形状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9922416" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0016.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9922416" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0016-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">利用牙签戳出尖尖的造型。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这样就能做出一个装胶水的罐子，方便不沾手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9922417" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0017.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9922417" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0017-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">制作出装胶水的瓶子。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>就连瓶底的凹槽剪裁后。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9922418" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0018.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9922418" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0018-600x392.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="392" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宝特瓶底座，可制成小勺子。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也能制作出一个小勺子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9922421" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0019.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9922421" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0019-600x344.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="344" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宝特瓶底座，可制成小勺子。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>就连杯子也不是问题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9922422" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0020.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9922422" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0020-600x393.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="393" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">宝特瓶底，可制成小杯子。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最厉害的就是制作玻璃瓶的标签。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9922423" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0021.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9922423" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0021-600x354.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="354" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">可利用宝特瓶制作出防水标签。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>只要将宝特瓶中间段剪裁下来，再将标签纸放入玻璃瓶外，套上宝特瓶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9922424" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0022.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9922424" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/20171204-SIJING-PARROT0022-600x349.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="349" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">利用吹风机，让宝特瓶遇热收缩，制作出防水标签。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>然后利用吹风机的热度，使塑料宝特瓶产生收缩的反应。这样就可以制作出防水的瓶装标签了！</p>
