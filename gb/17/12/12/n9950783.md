  <p>为什么安武花（昵称小花）能够做到其他5岁孩子做不到的事呢？这是她对罹癌早逝母亲的承诺。2015年，一家三口的感人故事被拍成了一部电影——《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A6%88%E5%A6%88%E7%9A%84%E5%91%B3%E5%99%8C%E6%B1%A4.md#1">妈妈的味噌汤</a>》（<em>Hana&#8217;s Miso Soup</em>），感动日本千万观众。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950807" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl3-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950807 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl3-1-600x333.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="333" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>小花出生之前的2000年，她的母亲——25岁的千惠被诊断出患有乳腺癌。尽管如此，小花的父亲——担任新闻记者的安武信吾还是毅然将千惠娶回了家。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950809" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl2-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950809 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl2-1-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>这份无私的爱带来了奇迹，千惠逐渐康复，并且怀上了小花。医生告诉她，生下孩子之后，癌症还是可能复发，但千惠满怀勇气，在她心中，“来此走一遭，就是为了和女儿相见。”</p>
  <p>2003年2月，小花降生了，整个家庭顿时充满了幸福。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950811" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl1-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950811 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl1-1-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>不幸的是，快乐相当短暂。小花9个月大时，妈妈的癌症复发了。医生这次说，几年后她就将阔别人世，这让千惠开始思考：“小花生活中最重要的事情是什么呢？”</p>
  <p>癌末妈妈决定教小花做饭、做家务，由此将来她可以自食其力。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950805" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl5-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950805 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl5-1-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>“我想让自己的女儿尽可能多地掌握些技能。”千惠在她的部落格写道，“我希望我的女儿能够学会坚强，顽强茁壮地活下去。”</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950803" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl11-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950803 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl11-1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>小花4岁生日到了，千惠送了她一套围裙作礼物。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950801" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl14-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950801 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl14-1-600x333.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="333" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>就在4岁这一年，小花学会了如何煮食糙米、烹饪纳豆和味噌汤。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950834" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl15-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950834 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl15-1-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>千惠在2008年2月写道：“无论我是否患有癌症，我都会先她而去。这就是为什么我必须毫无遗憾地死去。”</p>
  <p>那时，癌症已经扩散到千惠的肝脏和肺部。2008年7月11日，33岁的她告别了人世。她去世后不久的一天早上，爸爸信吾看到小花正在炉灶前煮味噌汤，味道尝起来和千惠煮的一个样。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950802" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl13-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950802 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl13-1-600x331.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="331" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>此后每个清晨，父女俩都会由飘香的味噌汤开始新的一天。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950806" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl4-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950806 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl4-1-600x335.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>小花也会做所有的家务，是千惠亲手教给她的。小花坚强的存在，让信吾渐渐走出了悲伤。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950824" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl12-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950824 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl12-1-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2012年，信吾出版散文集《<ahref="https://www.amazon.com/Hana-chan-misoshiru-Shingo-Yasutake/dp/4167901692" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">小花的味噌汤：安武家面对生命的8堂课</a>》，收入了千惠的部落格文章，将爱妻与爱女真实的生命故事展现给了读者。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950826" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl7-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950826 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl7-1-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>他在书中写道，“我最要感谢的是你生了小花，你是冒着生命危险生下她的。”</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950804" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl8-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950804 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl8-1-600x333.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="333" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>小花则对妈妈说：“我想告诉你，我现在会做整份便当了。你不觉得惊讶吗？我不再哭了。我正尽力做好。”</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950827" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl6-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950827 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl6-1-600x332.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="332" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">信吾展示小花做的便当。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>这位母亲对孩子深挚的爱真是美得令人动容，也引人思考：我们要为后辈留下些什么呢？</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9950825" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl9-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9950825 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DJY-Little-girl9-1-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>  <p>2014年，这个真实故事被改编成日剧搬上萤幕。次年推出的电影版演绎得更加成功，除了广末凉子饰演安武千惠、泷藤贤一饰演安武信吾，还有从千人选拔中胜出的童星赤松爱美饰演小花。该片感动日本千万观众，去年4月、5月也相继在台湾、香港感动上映。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_7821799" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/6b14873fc4bb827d4a0cecf40dd16713.jpg"><img class="wp-image-7821799" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/05/6b14873fc4bb827d4a0cecf40dd16713-450x321.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="428" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A6%88%E5%A6%88%E7%9A%84%E5%91%B3%E5%99%8C%E6%B1%A4.md#1">妈妈的味噌汤</a>》由真人真事改编，催人泪下。（电影公关提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>下面的短片，则是信吾和小花父女俩温馨朴素的生活记录。</p>
