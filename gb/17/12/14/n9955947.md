<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>9种天然高钙食物！第一名不是牛奶</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1701092228352483-e1513261803100-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>多吃钙质丰富的食物，对保护骨质和肌肉有很大帮助。芝麻是含钙最丰富的天然食物之一。(Fotolia)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>人体有99%<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%92%99%E8%B4%A8.md#1">钙质</a>都在骨头中。我们的身体无法自行制造钙，必须从食物中摄取。然而，许多人饮食习惯不均衡，又爱吃速食，不仅吸收不到足够的钙，还容易造成体内钙质的流失；加上骨质流失速度随年龄增长不断加快，长时间下来，就会导致“骨骼危机”。</p>
<p>平时摄取<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%92%99%E8%B4%A8.md#1">钙质</a>丰富的食物，对于预防骨质疏松、帮助肌肉收缩、保护心血管和血液凝结功能有很大的作用。以下几种富含钙质的食物，不妨加入到日常的饮食中。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">9种帮你<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A1%A5%E9%92%99.md#1">补钙</a>的高钙食物</span></h2>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">1. <ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB.md#1">芝麻</a></span></strong></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9958216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/shutterstock_229605109-e1513269051960.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9958216 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/shutterstock_229605109-e1513269051960-600x304.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="304" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">富含钙质的食物之一：<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB.md#1">芝麻</a>。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>据美国农业部（USDA）数据，100g芝麻中含钙约975mg，可以说是含钙最高的非加工食物之一。另外，芝麻营养丰富，经常吃不仅能防止头发过早变白或脱落，且还有很好的润肠通便、抗老护肤的作用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>2. 无花果</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9958183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/shutterstock_224018086-e1513269083551.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9958183 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/shutterstock_224018086-e1513269083551-600x346.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="346" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">富含钙质的食物之二：无花果。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>无花果不仅含钙质丰富，还富含膳食纤维与钾、镁等矿物质。其中镁也能强化骨骼、肌肉，还能帮助身体制造蛋白质、调节心跳。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>3. 黑豆</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9957994" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/5684475740_8ea70b4c89_z-e1513269105995.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9957994 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/5684475740_8ea70b4c89_z-e1513269105995-600x357.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">富含钙质的食物之三：黑豆。(Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>黑豆的钙含量约为菠菜的1.5倍。多吃黑豆，能软化血管，延缓衰老。特别是对高血压、心脏病等患者有益。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>4. 海带</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9955962" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1707181800482483-e1513263422394.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9955962 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1707181800482483-e1513263422394-600x300.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">富含钙质的食物之四：海带。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>海带是高钙食物，它的钙质容易被人体所吸收。海带含铁量也很高，并且有降低血脂、预防动脉硬化的功效。平时可以多吃一些凉拌海带，或者也可以多喝海带排骨汤，对于<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A1%A5%E9%92%99.md#1">补钙</a>补铁都有好处。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>5. 紫菜</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9955963" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1606131655442483-e1513269180475.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9955963 size-large" title="Korean seaweed" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/1606131655442483-e1513269180475-600x358.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="358" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">富含钙质的食物之五：紫菜。(shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>紫菜和海带一样，都是含钙丰富的食品。除了保护视力，还可维持皮肤、口腔黏膜的机能，对于抗癌、增强免疫力都有作用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>6. 黑木耳</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9955964" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/161114070614100692-e1513263925784.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9955964 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/161114070614100692-e1513263925784-600x354.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="354" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">富含钙质的食物之六：黑木耳。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>黑木耳也是帮助轻松补钙的食物之一。它还有降血压、增强智力及抗癌等功效，同时能够清理肠胃的灰尘和杂质。黑木耳富含蛋白质、维他命、纤维素与植物胶原，不仅能促进肠胃蠕动、减少脂质吸收，并有助缓解便秘。（<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;" href="/gb/14/10/22/n4278344.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">血管清道夫！黑木耳的四大养生功效</a></span></span>）</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>7. 芥菜</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11122872" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Chinesemustardgreens-e1513264343245.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11122872" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/Chinesemustardgreens-e1513264343245-600x304.jpg" alt="富含钙质的食物之七：芥菜。(Wikipedia)" width="600" b="304" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">富含钙质的食物之七：芥菜。(<ahref="https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Chinesemustardgreens.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wikimedia commons</a>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>芥菜营养纷繁，富含维他命A、C、E、K，有良好的抗氧化功效。素食者补钙，不妨多吃芥菜。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>8. 牛奶</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9750449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1709131638102483-e1513269214366.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9750449 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1709131638102483-e1513269214366-600x363.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">富含钙质的食物之八：牛奶。(shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>牛奶和起司等奶制品，是钙的优质来源。美国退伍军人会营养主任王咪咪表示，若她一日喝够三杯牛奶或吃起司，当日她就不再吃钙片来补充钙质。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>9. 虾米</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9958185" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/shutterstock_496459501-e1513269239798.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9958185 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/shutterstock_496459501-e1513269239798-600x340.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="340" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">富含钙质的食物之九：虾米。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>鱼干、虾米、虾皮含钙相当丰富。在饮食中适当加入，不仅可以调味，还能够有效补钙。</p>
<p>补钙还有一个关键，就是<span style="color: #800000;">光靠吃高钙食物是不够的，体内必须有足够的维他命D，钙才能顺利转化和吸收。</span>维他命D可以从鱼肝油、肝脏、蛋黄、鱼类等食物中摄取。另外，每日应有适度的户外运动与日晒，让阳光帮助身体产生维他命D。如果长期在室内工作，接触日光的机会少，还应考虑通过营养补充剂来补充。</p>
