<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>动手做：漂亮的卷纸圣诞卡</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/08/2e76a3133702f3508443cc6a9e17de94-320x200.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E8%8A%82.md#1">圣诞节</a>是西方很重要的节日，流传到世界各地后，现在连东方各个国家的人们也很重视这个节日。不仅孩子们期待着能收到圣诞礼物，年轻人也很喜欢送圣诞卡片给朋友。</p>
<p>亲手制作的卡片尤其温馨，除了手绘，今天教大家制作一款<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%B7%E7%BA%B8%E8%8A%B1.md#1">卷纸花</a>的圣诞卡片，简单又漂亮，快动手做一张吧！</p>
<p><strong>? 材料：</strong>丹迪纸或粉彩纸或是云彩纸等彩色纸张、剪刀、美工刀、直尺、白胶、切割垫、无墨水原子笔笔芯。</p>
<p><strong>?做法</strong><strong>：</strong></p>
<p>1、将丹迪纸或是云彩纸等稍有厚度的彩色纸张画剪成0.5公分的细纸条备用。</p>
<p>2、运用无墨水原子笔笔芯将细纸条卷起，在纸条第1圈与第2圈交接处沾上一点白胶黏合，继续卷纸条，在纸条末端同样沾上一点白胶黏合，用手指压住黏合点约5秒钟，黏合点已黏合牢固后取下笔芯，这样就是一个“密实圆”。若觉得密实圆不够大，可继续黏接上纸条再卷。多做几个不同色彩、大小不一的密实圆备用（如下图）。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4055.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977614 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4055-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a>3、剪一块长方形的绿色纸张，对折，在原侧边剪出3个不太一样的小山尖，运用无墨水原子笔笔尖在叶片上画出叶脉。用不同的绿色纸张多剪几张大小不一的叶片，备用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4059.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977619 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4059-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="301" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4060.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977616 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4060-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4053.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977618 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4053-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="300" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4050.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977617 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4050-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="272" /></a></p>
<p>4、运用无墨水原子笔笔芯将绿色细纸条卷起，卷几圈（圈数不定，依需要及纸条长度决定，通常卷5圈左右比较有视觉上的效果）后，一手抓住笔芯与纸条，另一手的拇指与食指略微一前一后的夹住纸条，并往纸条末端刮滑出去，使纸条呈现某种弧度（见下图），此即为“嫩芽卷”。若纸条呈现弧度不美可多做几次直至满意为止。做几条长短不一的嫩芽卷备用。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4063.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977626 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4063-600x444.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="296" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4056.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977627 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4056-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="402" b="302" /></a></p>
<p>5、将一张A4大的云彩纸长宽各割下2公分，黏置于另一张A4的彩色纸张上，作为圣诞卡的底纸。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4058.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977662 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4058-600x432.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="288" /></a></p>
<p>6、组合圣诞花，试摆卡片造型。黏合配件时，建议只在叶片中间折痕背面上涂白胶，让叶片有不同方向的翘起或紧靠底纸，这样看起来圣诞花比较活。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4079.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977666 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4079-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="267" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4076.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977667 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4076-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="266" /></a></p>
<p>7、 取黄色细纸条，运用无墨水原子笔笔芯将细纸条卷起，卷10圈左右，黏合纸卷，用手指压住黏合点约5秒钟，黏合点已黏合牢固后取下笔芯；换纸条另一末端卷，使纸卷像一个8。笔者预计做4组来装饰卡片。（如下图）</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4066.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977669 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4066-600x433.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="288" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4073.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977670 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4073-600x407.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="272" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4054.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977668 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4054-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="301" /></a>? 8、将8字卷试摆后，在底部沾上一点白胶黏合在摆放位置后固定。纸卷卡片完成。您可以在卡片空白处写字，就可以装进卡片袋送人啰！</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4078-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9977677 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/IMG_4078-1-600x391.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="260" /></a></p>
<p>做卷纸最有趣的是过程中的挑战，各配件的组合，就是一项美感的游戏与挑战，这就是动手做的快乐之处吧。</p>
