<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大雪节气尾巴 吃法式盐烤乌鱼</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/Jv14W"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/onzhi266/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>冬季的第三个节气“大雪”（现在已是尾巴），这个名称在台湾（和其它南方地区）可能不是那么符合实际。在台湾除了高山，还要加上寒流来袭才会有机会看到雪花纷飞的景气。</p>
<p>从中医的角度来看，节气到了大雪就是“进补”的好时机了。这个时候进补能够提升人体的基础免疫力，促进新陈代谢、调整原来畏寒的体质。但进补需得当。</p>
<p><strong>饮食小叮咛：</strong>多吃蔬菜防止上火、进补需得当<br />
<strong>时令蔬菜：</strong>白萝卜、木耳、芹菜、莲藕、冬笋<br />
<strong>当季水果：</strong>梨、苹果、柿子、甘蔗、葡萄柚、橘子</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>教你做“法式<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9B%90%E7%83%A4%E4%B9%8C%E9%B1%BC.md#1">盐烤乌鱼</a>”</strong></span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">大雪到冬至期间捕获的乌鱼肉质肥厚，纹理结构清晰、不会太过松散。</span>进烤箱前用海盐和多种香料提味，用简单的调味衬托出当季乌鱼的鲜美。</p>
<p><strong>食材：</strong></p>
<p>乌鱼1条、柠檬1颗、蛋3颗</p>
<p>新鲜迷迭香数支、新鲜百里香数支、大茴香籽约5g、海盐1kg</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><div class="video_fit_container"><a width="635" b="356" class="video_frame" src=""></a><a href="https://git.io/Jv14W"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/onzhi266/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/M5DNSuWQS54?wmode=transparent&#038;wmode=opaque" allowfullscreen></a>
	</div><br />
<span style="color: #808080;"><em>（完整制作视频，制作步骤如下。）</em></span></p>
<p><strong>步骤：</strong></p>
<p>1. 乌鱼不去鱼鳞，洗净后擦干水分。柠檬切片，海盐及蛋白混合均匀备用；</p>
<figure id="attachment_9975251" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2-50.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9975251" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/2-50-600x343.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="343" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法式<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9B%90%E7%83%A4%E4%B9%8C%E9%B1%BC.md#1">盐烤乌鱼</a>步骤之一：海盐及蛋白混合均匀备用。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 将部分黄柠檬及香料（茴香籽、迷迭香和百里香）塞到鱼肚里；</p>
<figure id="attachment_9975254" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/3-41.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9975254" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/3-41-600x343.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="343" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法式盐烤乌鱼步骤之二： 将部分柠檬及香料塞进鱼肚。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 烤盘铺上烘焙纸，铺上一层海盐。放上乌鱼后，用海盐盖满乌鱼，再放上新鲜香料枝、柠檬片；</p>
<figure id="attachment_9975261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/4-33.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9975261" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/4-33-600x343.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="343" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法式盐烤乌鱼步骤之三：用海盐盖满乌鱼。（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 烤箱预热180～200度，烤40分钟。鲜嫩肥厚、香气蒸腾的法式盐烤乌鱼就静待享用了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9975265" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/5-30.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9975265" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/5-30-600x342.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="342" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法式盐烤乌鱼出炉了！（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
