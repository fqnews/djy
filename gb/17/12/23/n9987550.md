<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美丰银行桃树角（Peachtree Corner）分行开张</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DSC_3224-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>董事长Nack Paek（正中系红领带者）率领董事会成员一行参加剪彩仪式。（毕倩／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p class="NoSpacing1" align="left">【大纪元2017年12月24日讯】<span lang="ZH-CN">（</span><span lang="ZH-CN">大纪元记者毕倩亚特兰大报导）</span>12<span lang="ZH-CN">月</span>19<span lang="ZH-CN">日上午</span>11<span lang="ZH-CN">时，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E4%B8%B0%E9%93%B6%E8%A1%8C.md#1">美丰银行</a></span> (Metro City Bank)<span lang="ZH-CN">董事会主要成员、</span>Peachtree Corner<span lang="ZH-CN">分行职员以及华裔、韩裔社区嘉宾聚集在</span>Peachtree Corner<span lang="ZH-CN">分行，为新开业的</span>Peachtree Corner<span lang="ZH-CN">（<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E6%A0%91%E8%A7%92.md#1">桃树角</a>）分行举办开张庆典。<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E4%B8%B0%E9%93%B6%E8%A1%8C.md#1">美丰银行</a>董事长</span>Nack Paek <span lang="ZH-CN">率领总裁</span>Howard Kim<span lang="ZH-CN">、总执行长（</span>CEO<span lang="ZH-CN">）</span>Farid Tan<span lang="ZH-CN">、总财务长（</span>CFO<span lang="ZH-CN">）</span>Benton Gunter<span lang="ZH-CN">、会计总监（</span>Controller<span lang="ZH-CN">）</span>Allan Miller<span lang="ZH-CN">、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%A1%83%E6%A0%91%E8%A7%92.md#1">桃树角</a>分行长杜静、主任</span>Francis Lai <span lang="ZH-CN">、</span>Ajit Patel<span lang="ZH-CN">、</span> Don Leung Sam Shim<span lang="ZH-CN">一行，并邀请</span>Peachtree Corner<span lang="ZH-CN">（桃树角）市议员</span>Jeanne Aulbach<span lang="ZH-CN">以及华人侨领曾超汉、韩裔社区的</span>Ki Sung Bae<span lang="ZH-CN">、</span>II Hong Kim <span lang="ZH-CN">等参加剪彩仪式。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_9987554" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DSC_3212-e1514067230790.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9987554" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DSC_3212-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">桃树角（Peachtree Corner）分行行长杜静（中）与职员及桃树角市议员Jeanne Aulbach（左二）相见欢。（毕倩／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="NoSpacing1" align="left"><span lang="ZH-TW">据董事长</span>Nack Paek<span lang="ZH-TW">介绍，美丰银行从</span>2006<span lang="ZH-TW">年</span>4<span lang="ZH-TW">月以</span>1500<span lang="ZH-TW">万为初始资金和</span>9<span lang="ZH-TW">名员工开始，发展到</span>2007<span lang="ZH-TW">年底</span><span lang="ZH-CN">就</span><span lang="ZH-TW">增加了一倍的资本收入。目前，该银行已发展到拥有上百名员工，总资产</span>7<span lang="ZH-TW">亿</span>9<span lang="ZH-TW">千万、净资产</span>9600<span lang="ZH-TW">万、存款余额</span>6<span lang="ZH-TW">亿</span>5<span lang="ZH-TW">千万美元的规模。</span></p>
<p class="NoSpacing1" align="left"><span lang="ZH-TW">据</span><span lang="ZH-CN">总执行长（</span>CEO<span lang="ZH-CN">）</span>Farid Tan<span lang="ZH-TW">介绍，</span>Peachtree Corner<span lang="ZH-TW">（桃树角）分行自</span>11<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>8<span lang="ZH-TW">日试营业</span><span lang="ZH-CN">，</span><span lang="ZH-TW">到目前已累计</span><span lang="ZH-CN">吸引</span><span lang="ZH-TW">存款</span>500<span lang="ZH-TW">万美元，希望一年后达到</span>5000<span lang="ZH-TW">万</span>-6000<span lang="ZH-TW">万存款的目标。</span>Farid <span lang="ZH-TW">说：“</span>Peachtree Corner<span lang="ZH-TW">（桃树角）地区</span>IT<span lang="ZH-TW">业分布密集，各族裔也越来越集中，所以分行的开业会为周边的居民、商家提供便利，大家不必跑去</span>Doraville<span lang="ZH-TW">了”。</span>Peachtree Corner<span lang="ZH-TW">（桃树角）分行</span><span lang="ZH-CN">经理</span><span lang="ZH-TW">杜静女士跟记者介绍</span><span lang="ZH-CN">说</span><span lang="ZH-TW">，美丰银行</span>Peachtree Corner<span lang="ZH-TW">（桃树角）分行已是公司的第</span>14<span lang="ZH-TW">家分行，乘着这股良好的发展趋势，另一家位于</span>East Cobb—Marietta<span lang="ZH-TW">分行即将于</span>2018<span lang="ZH-TW">年</span>1<span lang="ZH-TW">月底开业。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_9987555" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DSC_3216-e1514067315464.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9987555" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/DSC_3216-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美丰银行的中、韩职员 。左一为East Cobb-Marietta分行经理 Cynthia Meng。（毕倩／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="NoSpacing1" align="left">Peachtree Corner<span lang="ZH-TW">（桃树角）分行开业之初，为新客户提供</span>14<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>CD<span lang="ZH-TW">年溢率</span>1.65%<span lang="ZH-TW">（</span>APY<span lang="ZH-TW">）的优惠（只限于乔州、阿拉巴马州最低开户额</span>1000<span lang="ZH-TW">美元）以及</span>18<span lang="ZH-TW">月</span>CD<span lang="ZH-TW">的</span>1.85% APY<span lang="ZH-TW">（最低开户额</span>1000<span lang="ZH-TW">美元）的促销活动。位于</span>Doraville<span lang="ZH-TW">市的美丰银行大厦总部已建成，预计将于</span>2018<span lang="ZH-TW">年上半年正式投入使用。◇</span></p>
