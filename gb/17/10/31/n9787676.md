<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>悉尼BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅 举办婚礼的好地方</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-13-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>美丽的新娘（BIVIANOS提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年10月31日讯】悉尼的Dural区是一个非常迷人的好地方，一栋栋漂亮的庄园别墅、丘岭起伏、绿茵连绵，宽广的土地，悠闲地时不时丢丢尾巴吃着青草的骏马，一派令人心旷神怡的美丽的田园风光景象。在Dural，有一家远近闻名的海鲜餐厅，那里的美食、High Tea非常吸引人，但凡品尝过那里菜品的人，总是会想再回去品尝更多佳肴。很多人交口称赞说“Bivianos is my favourite restaurant”（比维亚诺是我最喜欢的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C%E9%A4%90%E5%8E%85.md#1">海鲜餐厅</a>）。餐厅每3个月就要更换一次菜谱，不断推陈出新，即使是常客也时时可以品尝到新菜品。从前菜到主菜，最后的甜点都是棒棒的。精致美味的佳肴是留住客人的绝招。</p>

<h2>High Tea</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9787826" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-06.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9787826" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-06-600x400.jpg" alt="High Tea（BIVIANOS提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">High Tea（BIVIANOS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>BIVIANOS的High Tea与众不同，是传统的英式High Tea。如果相见识什么是英式High Tea，那就去BIVIANOS。餐厅里坐着服饰讲究的绅士女士，一边品尝精美的英式红茶和茶点，一边轻言细语交谈，悠闲度过时光。</p>
<h2>婚礼</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9787832" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-11.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9787832" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-11-600x400.jpg" alt="BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅婚里庆典（BIVIANOS提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅婚里庆典（BIVIANOS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>BIVIANOS豪华餐厅外的庄园庭院式花园是想拥有一个浪漫的室外婚礼的最佳选择，除了庭院式花园风格外，还可以极目远眺蓝山风景。BIVIANOS每周都要承办数场婚礼，让无数对佳人在那里完成了人生最重要的仪式。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9787820" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-01.jpeg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9787820" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-01-600x400.jpeg" alt="BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅婚礼庆典（BIVIANOS提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅婚礼庆典（BIVIANOS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9787824" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9787824" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-05-600x450.jpg" alt="BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅佳肴（BIVIANOS提供）" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅佳肴（BIVIANOS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>豪华餐厅的外草坪区域也可用于自助餐或鸡尾酒会，而草坪区域亦设有座椅，可以观景赏景享受美好时光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9787823" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9787823" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-04-600x450.jpg" alt="BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅婚宴（BIVIANOS提供）" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A9%9A%E5%AE%B4.md#1">婚宴</a>（BIVIANOS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9787831" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-10.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9787831" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-10-600x400.jpg" alt="BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅婚宴（BIVIANOS提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A9%9A%E5%AE%B4.md#1">婚宴</a>（BIVIANOS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9787833" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-12.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9787833" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-12-600x400.jpg" alt="婚里庆典蛋糕（BIVIANOS提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">婚里庆典蛋糕（BIVIANOS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>BIVIANOS有一组非常专业的团队，能创造令人难忘，即使放到皇家也不逊色的鲜花布景。她们能为客人量身定制，从婚礼蛋糕到鲜花布景和摄影，每一个细节都能照顾周到，能将一场婚礼操作到美丽精致的极致，而且情调温馨、高贵典雅令人难忘，让一生这个最重要的日子永远留在人们的记忆里。</p>
<h2>私密贵宾厅</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9787830" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-09.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9787830" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-09-600x400.jpg" alt="BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅婚宴（BIVIANOS提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅婚宴（BIVIANOS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9787822" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9787822" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/20171031-yichun-food-03-600x450.jpg" alt="BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅婚宴（BIVIANOS提供）" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">BIVIANOS海鲜餐厅婚宴（BIVIANOS提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>BIVIANOS有一个私密的贵宾厅是人们在那种时候常常选用的地方。私密贵宾室，那里可以是婚礼前重要贵宾的休息、饮酒和交谈的地方，也可以是一些公司或商业重要餐会的举办地。在私密贵宾厅里有一个长长的桌子，最多可容纳24人，也可以少至12人。私密贵宾厅有自己专属的通道，服务人员都是通过专门通道来递送餐点餐食和美酒。进入这个私密的贵宾厅有点像进入大酒庄的酒窖的感觉，有点与外界相隔的一种神秘感。<br />
地址 : 628 Old Northern Rd, Dural NSW 2158</p>
<p>电话 : 02 9651 2022</p>
