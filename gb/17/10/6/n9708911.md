Alfred和歌手Krissy则弹唱搭配，以充满张力的歌声唱出思乡之情，Krissy并带来热情洋溢的海地舞蹈。<br />  <figure id="attachment_9708928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC00045.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC00045-600x400.jpg" alt="三位乌克兰裔音乐家表演民族音乐。（温文清/大纪元）" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-9708928" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">三位乌克兰裔音乐家表演民族音乐。（温文清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />  <figure id="attachment_9708929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC00112.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC00112-600x400.jpg" alt="海地裔吉他歌手Gerald &#039;Toto&#039; Alfred和歌手Krissy表演海地音乐和舞蹈。（温文清/大纪元）" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-9708929" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">海地裔吉他歌手Gerald &#8216;Toto&#8217; Alfred和歌手Krissy表演海地音乐和舞蹈。（温文清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>　　<br />  两位华裔音乐家压轴演奏中华民乐，民乐家梁嘉驹和古筝演奏家关孟华首先合奏《牧羊曲》、《葬花吟》等经典曲目，随后二人分别独奏古筝名曲《高山流水》、琵琶曲《天山之春》等，让观众一一感受不同中华乐器之独特乐音。#<br />  <figure id="attachment_9708935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC00168.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC00168-600x400.jpg" alt="关孟华独奏古筝。（温文清/大纪元）" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-9708935" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">关孟华独奏古筝。（温文清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />  <figure id="attachment_9708936" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC00155.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/DSC00155-600x400.jpg" alt="梁嘉驹独奏琵琶。（温文清/大纪元）" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-9708936" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">梁嘉驹独奏琵琶。（温文清/大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />  责任编辑：唐明镜 </p>
