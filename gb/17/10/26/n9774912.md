  <p>据BBC报导，9月初的一天，32岁的肯尼亚女子万嘉（Wanja Mwaura）正在去买菜的路上，突然听见有人大喊她的名字。她抬头一看，一个形容枯槁的高个男子坐在路边，正瞪着布满血丝的大眼珠盯着她。他全身脏兮兮的，一顶破烂的羊毛帽压在头上。万嘉实在认不出他是谁，直到男子开口说，他是“辛伽”（Patrick &#8220;Hinga&#8221; Wanjiru）。</p>
  <p>万嘉惊呆了。眼前这个流浪汉，竟然是她从1992年上小学起就认识的好友。辛伽比万嘉大2岁，万嘉还记得，在学校里他球踢得很棒，同学们都叫他“贝利”。</p>
  <p>当护士的万嘉坐了下来，轻轻抚摸着辛伽的头。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9774968" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22089537_1215648768535783_3554163497578451888_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9774968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22089537_1215648768535783_3554163497578451888_n-450x600.jpg" alt="(Wanja Mwaura/Facebook)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/wanjam2/photos?lst=100004618500978%3A100002720158709%3A1509048681&amp;source_ref=pb_friends_tl" target="_blank">Wanja Mwaura/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>  <p>原来，辛伽自幼被父母抛弃，跟着祖母在庇护所长大。在祖母付不出学费后，他不得不翘课；最后，连庇护所也不让他们住了……即便如此，辛伽的学习成绩一直都很好，而在祖母去世后，他不得不辍学，命运由此急转直下。</p>
  <p>辛伽开始吸毒，最初是大麻，然后是海洛因，为了勉强维持流浪生活，他每天要拣数小时的垃圾。</p>
  <p>在和万嘉失联15年后，他们终于在街头邂逅，此时辛伽已经流浪了十余年，已经不再是那个驰骋球场的“贝利”。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9774967" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22007921_1215648831869110_5756269501664083600_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9774967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22007921_1215648831869110_5756269501664083600_n-450x600.jpg" alt="(Wanja Mwaura/Facebook)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/wanjam2/photos?lst=100004618500978%3A100002720158709%3A1509048681&amp;source_ref=pb_friends_tl" target="_blank">Wanja Mwaura/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>  <p>看到万嘉难过的样子，辛伽赶紧解释自己只想和她打个招呼而已。万嘉则带他到附近的咖喱馆，为他点了他小时候最喜欢吃的猪排和马铃薯泥。看着儿时好友眼神涣散、话不成句，万嘉十分担心，她把自己的电话号码留给他，让他有需要时随时打给她。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9774969" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/21462910_1206452019455458_5321586170275848563_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9774969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/21462910_1206452019455458_5321586170275848563_n-450x600.jpg" alt="(Wanja Mwaura/Facebook)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/wanjam2/photos?lst=100004618500978%3A100002720158709%3A1509048681&amp;source_ref=pb_friends_tl" target="_blank">Wanja Mwaura/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_9774962" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/21462435_1206452219455438_5203320501234315456_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9774962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/21462435_1206452219455438_5203320501234315456_n-450x600.jpg" alt="(Wanja Mwaura/Facebook)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/wanjam2/photos?lst=100004618500978%3A100002720158709%3A1509048681&amp;source_ref=pb_friends_tl" target="_blank">Wanja Mwaura/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>  <p>接下来的几天，辛伽不时会用借来的电话拨通万嘉的号码，听听她的声音，和她聊几句。他也告诉万嘉，他下定决心要戒掉毒瘾。“我当时就决定，我要做些什么帮助他。”万嘉说。</p>
  <p>由于在肯尼亚吸毒是个比较严重的问题，内罗毕当地的医院做不到免费<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%88%92%E6%AF%92.md#1">戒毒</a>，费用还相当昂贵：首期9天疗程需要10万先令（约960美元），万嘉于是借助社交平台向朋友们募款，但只筹到了4.1万先令（不到400美元）。万嘉不放弃，她径直将辛伽带去了医院，心里还不知剩余的多一半费用在哪里。</p>
  <p>医院<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%88%92%E6%AF%92.md#1">戒毒</a>中心发言人后来告诉媒体，辛伽是个非常有决心的戒毒者，咬牙完成了9天极为艰苦的排毒疗程。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9774965" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22366390_1224364984330828_1334803156553134806_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9774965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22366390_1224364984330828_1334803156553134806_n-450x600.jpg" alt="(Wanja Mwaura/Facebook)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/wanjam2/photos?lst=100004618500978%3A100002720158709%3A1509048681&amp;source_ref=pb_friends_tl" target="_blank">Wanja Mwaura/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>  <p>当他和万嘉见面时，两人紧紧拥抱彼此。这么短的时间里，辛伽的体重就增加了，注意力也获得了改善，看起来健康多了。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9774963" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22549879_1231869976913662_1022296970765283048_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9774963" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22549879_1231869976913662_1022296970765283048_n-450x600.jpg" alt="(Wanja Mwaura/Facebook)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/wanjam2/photos?lst=100004618500978%3A100002720158709%3A1509048681&amp;source_ref=pb_friends_tl" target="_blank">Wanja Mwaura/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>  <p>欣喜不已的万嘉在脸书上发帖，由衷为好友的奋斗感到骄傲。她写道：“一周之前，如果我不用手扶着辛伽的头，他还不能集中精神说话；今天他和我说话时，已经是带着自信的眼神注视着我。”</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9774966" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22448281_1224364820997511_5880710334031997325_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9774966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22448281_1224364820997511_5880710334031997325_n-450x600.jpg" alt="(Wanja Mwaura/Facebook)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/wanjam2/photos?lst=100004618500978%3A100002720158709%3A1509048681&amp;source_ref=pb_friends_tl" target="_blank">Wanja Mwaura/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_9774990" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22539920_1230662173701109_4249774630176419490_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9774990" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22539920_1230662173701109_4249774630176419490_n-450x338.jpg" alt="(Wanja Mwaura/Facebook)" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/wanjam2/photos?lst=100004618500978%3A100002720158709%3A1509048681&amp;source_ref=pb_friends_tl" target="_blank">Wanja Mwaura/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>  <p>一个名为卡利德（Fauz Khalid）的商人网友被两人真挚的友谊打动，在自己的推特上简短分享了这个故事，并配上照片，至今已被转推超过5万次。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9774989" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22491782_1230623087038351_5073515582468941294_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9774989" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22491782_1230623087038351_5073515582468941294_n-450x600.jpg" alt="(Wanja Mwaura/Facebook)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/wanjam2/photos?lst=100004618500978%3A100002720158709%3A1509048681&amp;source_ref=pb_friends_tl" target="_blank">Wanja Mwaura/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>  <p>万嘉发自内心感恩她和辛伽有此巧遇，认为这是上天的安排和赐福。而她也希望好友能够巩固戒毒的成果。为此，她又在为辛伽接下来三个月的疗程募款了！</p>
  <p>“她简直就是上天派来的天使，她救了我的命，她对我负责到底的态度胜过了亲兄妹。”辛伽告诉BBC记者。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9774964" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22449773_1227543817346278_5859695076844623009_n.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9774964" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/22449773_1227543817346278_5859695076844623009_n-450x600.jpg" alt="(Wanja Mwaura/Facebook)" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(<ahref="https://www.facebook.com/wanjam2/photos?lst=100004618500978%3A100002720158709%3A1509048681&amp;source_ref=pb_friends_tl" target="_blank">Wanja Mwaura/Facebook</a>)</figcaption></figure>  <p>而在推特上，众多网友也对万嘉赞叹有加：“我相信天堂里会有你一席！”有的说她是位“英雄”。</p>
  <p>“人们说我改变了辛伽的人生，但他也改变了我的。”万嘉说，“我现在明白，小小举动也可以翻转一个人的一生。”</p>
