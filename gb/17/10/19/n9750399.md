  <h2><strong><span style="color: #008000;">梨</span> </strong></h2>  <p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B6%A6%E8%82%BA.md#1">润肺</a>止咳、清热解毒，有“天生甘露饮”之称。对于秋燥引起的干咳、口渴和便秘效果不错。中国历代戏曲名家保护嗓音的秘诀就是，每日饮用鲜梨榨汁滋润喉咙。但脾胃弱者不宜多食。可生食或榨汁，若再加入甘蔗或<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%9C%82%E8%9C%9C.md#1">蜂蜜</a>，滋润效果更好。梨子加蜂蜜、冰糖川贝母炖煮，可用来缓解咳嗽症状。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9750409" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/161201233328100692.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9750409 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/161201233328100692-600x401.jpg" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">止咳保护气管可食用冰糖炖梨。(大纪元图库)</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB.md#1">芝麻</a> </strong></span></h2>  <p>秋季天气干燥，不妨吃点黑<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB.md#1">芝麻</a>防燥又润肤，可以磨粉泡茶或煮粥。但是若有发炎情形，如牙痛、肠胃炎或腹泻的人，不适合多吃。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_5702968" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/03/1401021036162483-e1508766882889.jpg"><img class="wp-image-5702968 size-large" title="黑芝麻" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/03/1401021036162483-e1508766882889-600x419.jpg" width="600" b="419" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">一般黑色食物多入肾，可补益肾精。可食用黑芝麻、黑木耳、黑豆、桑椹、黑糯米等。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>百合</strong></span></h2>  <p>百合清润甘甜，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%B6%A6%E8%82%BA.md#1">润肺</a>养颜，并有止咳、镇静安神效果。可将百合煮粥或拌炒其它食材。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9762094" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/shutterstock_728909299.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9762094" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/shutterstock_728909299-600x427.jpg" alt="百合清润甘甜，润肺养颜，并有止咳、镇静安神效果。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">百合清润甘甜，润肺养颜，并有止咳、镇静安神效果。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>茄子</strong></span></h2>  <p>茄子是秋季上市应季的蔬菜之一，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8C%BB.md#1">中医</a>认为，茄子性凉味甘，入肠胃经，有清热止血、消肿止痛、祛风通络等功能，因此，所以就有了立秋吃茄子去火除燥的说法。（<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #008000;"><a style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;" href="/gb/17/6/3/n9221772.md#1">茄子是良药别再小看它</a></span></span>）</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9762091" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/shutterstock_693208924.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9762091" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/shutterstock_693208924-600x400.jpg" alt="食用茄子可以预防秋燥或减轻秋燥引起的一些症状。(Shutterstock)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">食用茄子可以预防秋燥或减轻秋燥引起的一些症状。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>银耳</strong></span></h2>  <p>银耳味甘，是秋季润肺的圣品，既有补脾开胃的成效，又能滋阴润肺。适用于肺热咳嗽、肺燥干咳、胃肠燥热、便秘等症。另外，银耳还含有自然动物性胶质，是非常好的润肤食品。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_6517754" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/131022153240100087.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6517754 size-large" title="桂花银耳" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/09/131022153240100087-600x400.jpg" alt="桂花银耳" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">秋季来碗桂花银耳汤，让人备感温暖。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>海带</strong></span></h2>  <p>海带是化痰、消炎、平喘、排毒、通便的理想食物。这是因为海带性寒，其含有的碘被人体吸收后，促进有害物质、病变物和炎症渗出物的排除，同时海带含有一种叫硫酸多糖，能吸收血管中的胆固醇，并排出体外。昆布及发菜也有同样的效用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9750446" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/0a82a4c26e93bd6ce2497bb4e06e3ffa-e1508427375156.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9750446" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/0a82a4c26e93bd6ce2497bb4e06e3ffa-600x338.jpg" alt="海带。(limin_chiu/CC/Flickr)" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">海带。(limin_chiu/CC/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>牛奶</strong></span></h2>  <p>秋季容易燥热，饮用微寒的牛奶可以滋阴、去肝火、解热毒，同时还可以补充优质蛋白，增强人体抵抗力。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9750449" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1709131638102483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9750449 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1709131638102483-600x400.jpg" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">牛奶可以滋阴、去肝火、解热毒。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>山药</strong></span></h2>  <p>秋季天气较枯燥，容易出现口干、咽干、唇焦、干咳等病症，此时进补山药最为适合。山药不仅能够切片煎汁作为茶饮，还能够炝炒、熬粥，适合不同体质的人食用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8397296" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1605161425212483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8397296 size-large" title="山药" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/10/1605161425212483-600x400.jpg" alt="山药" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">山药为秋季滋阴养肺之上品。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>莲藕</strong></span></h2>  <p>民间早有“荷莲一身宝，秋藕最补人”的说法。秋令时节，此时天气干燥，吃些藕，能起到养阴清热、润燥止渴、清心安神的作用。（<span style="color: #008000;"><a style="color: #008000;" href="/gb/17/10/20/n9754853.md#1">6步做桂花糖藕，养脾胃又补气血</a></span>）</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9757672" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1708291425252483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9757672 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/10/1708291425252483-600x400.jpg" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">莲藕养阴清热、润燥止渴、清心安神。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>白萝卜</strong></span></h2>  <p>萝卜有止咳化痰，清热解毒的效果，含有丰富的维生素，对肺部及咽喉部位，有着很好的保养效果。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8268321" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1605241524352483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8268321 size-large" title="三个白萝卜" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/09/1605241524352483-600x400.jpg" alt="三个白萝卜" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">白萝卜具有消炎作用，并能缓和喉咙的肿胀感，止咳效果佳。（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>  <h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>荸荠</strong></span></h2>  <p>荸荠有很好的医疗保健效果，据中医记载：荸荠性味甘、微寒、无毒，有温中益气、清热开胃、消食醒酒的功效。临床上可用于缓解热病烦渴、痰热咳嗽、咽喉疼痛等症状。</p>
