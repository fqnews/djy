<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：这个非洲小村庄 每座房子都是艺术品</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/mudvillage_11-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年06月12日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳编译报导）虽然从经济发展的标准来看，多数非洲国家都非常贫穷，但在文化上，这些国家可谓多姿多彩，有着非常丰富的传统。</p>
<p>在西非国家布基纳法索的提埃贝勒（tiébélé）地方，有个叫卡塞纳（Kassena）的小村庄，那里传统的泥土小屋虽然都没有窗，却可跻身“非洲最美住房”。这些住宅的主人——古龙西（Gurunsi）人从15世纪起就居住在这里，是这一地区最古老的部族之一。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9252227" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9252227" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/3380738311_f576f0bce8_o-600x384.jpg" alt="布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr) " width="500" b="320" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9252230" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9252230" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/3381548340_9deeca1628_o-600x427.jpg" alt="布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr) " width="500" b="356" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9252268" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9252268" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/mudvillage_07-600x361.jpg" alt="布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr) " width="500" b="301" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>卡塞纳村的住宅上彩绘着精致的几何和人形、动物图案，有着神秘的寓意。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9252283" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9252283" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/mudvillage_14-600x400.jpg" alt="布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr) " width="500" b="333" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9252231" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9252231" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/3380364484_29c6b6cc9f_o-600x400.jpg" alt="布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr) " width="500" b="333" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">古龙西彩绘泥土住宅也运用了浮雕和阴线刻的手法。(Rita Willaert/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9252232" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9252232" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/3379178871_17ae9d2c10_o-600x400.jpg" alt="布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr) " width="500" b="333" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，房屋的彩绘和浮雕有着古老而神秘的寓意。(Rita Willaert/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>丰收的土地，飘摆的青草，男性的服饰……每座小屋的彩绘都是艺术品，体现出居住者的独特品味，也使得整座村庄都成为部族文化的丰富表达。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9252233" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9252233" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/3380036476_73ba03bb67_o-600x400.jpg" alt="布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr) " width="500" b="333" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">家家户户的彩绘都体现着自己的风格。(Rita Willaert/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9252235" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9252235" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/3380031574_1acbcdd0be_o-600x400.jpg" alt="布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr) " width="500" b="333" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">古老的陶缶与现代的搪瓷器皿（右上）。(Rita Willaert/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9252236" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9252236" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/3379124545_0259d4a5b0_o-600x400.jpg" alt="布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr) " width="500" b="333" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，古龙西彩绘泥土住宅成为部族文化的丰富表达。(Rita Willaert/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>每隔四五年，村里的妇女们就会用彩泥和白粉笔把这些墙面重画一遍。这是全村的工事，也是古龙西人文化传统的重要一部分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9252228" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9252228" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/3381601670_0cce9fc0cb_o-600x400.jpg" alt="布基纳法索的卡塞纳村，多彩的古龙西彩绘泥土住宅。(Rita Willaert/Flickr) " width="500" b="333" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">村中的妇女们是泥土住宅的彩绘者。(Rita Willaert/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于人迹罕至，这些墙画保存状况很好，透过西方摄影师和少数游客的镜头才渐为世人所知……</p>
