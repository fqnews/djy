<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>从深海到丛林 10种惊人有趣的透明动物</h2>
<a href="https://git.io/g"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/s2510/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg"title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年06月07日讯】（大纪元记者苏琳综合报导）深邃而广袤的海洋，对人类仍然是个谜。但在深海勘探之中，人类也发现了许多奇怪得惊人的海洋生命体。</p>
<p>如同其它生态系统，海洋对于很多生物来说可能是个危机四伏的地方，能否活命取决于能够避免成为大型捕食动物的口中餐。因此，许多海洋动物有着非常特殊的自我保护机制，比如说：生得通身透明。</p>
<p>当然，陆地上也有些不可思议的奇怪生物，如热带雨林深处的透明昆虫。下面就来逐一看看它们的样子。</p>
<h2>1. 半透明的海天使（sea angel）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9234170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9234170 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Sea_angel-600x398.jpg" alt="透明的海天使。（公有领域）" width="600" b="398" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">透明的海天使。（公有领域）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<figure id="attachment_9234178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9234178 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Sea_angels_-600x374.jpg" alt="透明的海天使。(Wikimedia Commons)" width="600" b="374" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">透明的海天使。(Wikimedia Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p>它生得如此超现实、如此梦幻，很难相信这些动物只是些小虫子。</p>
<h2>2. 樽海鞘（salpa maggiore）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9234182" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9234182 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/NTD-3-18-600x450.jpg" alt="(Imgur)" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">樽海鞘。(Imgur)</figcaption></figure>
<p>没有看到它游过来吗？这种类似海蜇的海洋无脊椎动物（Salps）可以在新西兰水域找到。</p>
<h2>3. 幼年箱鲀（Juvenile cowfish）</h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div class="getty embed image" style="background-color: #fff; display: inline-block; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif; color: #a7a7a7; font-size: 11px; width: 100%; max-width: 509px;">
<div style="padding: 0; margin: 0; text-align: left;"><a style="color: #a7a7a7; text-decoration: none; font-weight: normal !important; border: none; display: inline-block;" href="http://www.gettyimages.com/detail/73797748" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Embed from Getty Images</a></div>
<div style="overflow: hidden; position: relative; height: 0; padding: 66.601179% 0 0 0; width: 100%;"><a style="display: inline-block; position: absolute; top: 0; left: 0; width: 100%; height: 100%; margin: 0;" src="//embed.gettyimages.com/embed/73797748?et=EXWnJesNQF5xJNB3BlwZnQ&amp;tld=com&amp;viewMoreLink=on&amp;sig=93oNQANRGykz9WLjl_N-0YDHPqNnjROf8HLhOkReVAY=&amp;caption=true" width="509" b="339" frameborder="0" scrolling="no"></a></div>
<p style="margin: 0;">
</div>
<p>海洋生物的差异性真是太大了。这些晦暗发光的奇怪生物真是不可思议。</p>
<h2>4. 冰鱼（Ice fish）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9234198" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9234198 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/NTD-5-9-600x400.jpg" alt="NTD-5-9" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">冰鱼。(uwe kils, Wikimedia Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p>顾名思义，它们喜欢冰冷的水，而且有防冻功能，使其血液不会在寒冷水域冻上。</p>
<h2>5. 透明灯塔水母（Turritopsis nutricula）</h2>
<p>普通的水母在有生殖后就会死亡，但灯塔水母却能够再次回到水螅型，它可以无限次使出这种返老还童的本领，所以又叫长生不老水母（immortal jellyfish）。举例说，可以把它看成是一只蝴蝶，变成毛毛虫后，还会再作为蝴蝶获得重生。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9234201" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9234201 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/shutterstock_646161799-600x399.jpg" alt="灯塔水母。(shutterstock)" width="600" b="399" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">灯塔水母。(shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>6. 透明刺尾鱼（Transparent Juvenile Surgeonfish）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9234206" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9234206 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/NTD-9-3-600x600.jpg" alt="(MissLissa/Imgur)" width="600" b="600" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">透明刺尾鱼。(MissLissa/Imgur)</figcaption></figure>
<p>虽然有人说这条小鱼不可能是真的，但你确实可以在新西兰海岸地带看到它自在游泳。</p>
<h2>7. 幽灵虾（Ghost Shrimp）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9234210" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9234210 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Palaemonetes_pugio-600x400.jpg" alt="幽灵虾。(Brian.gratwicke, Wikimedia Commons)" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">幽灵虾。(Brian.gratwicke, Wikimedia Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这种通身体透明的虾几乎可以在海中隐形地游泳，不过明黄色的肠子还是清晰可见。</p>
<h2>8. 龟甲虫（Tortoise shell beetle）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9234215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9234215 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/NTD-17-2-2-600x450.jpg" alt="龟甲虫。(Charles Lam/Flickr)" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">龟甲虫。(Charles Lam/Flickr)</figcaption></figure>
<p>一见之下，龟甲虫像某种瓢虫，而在透明的外壳之下，其颜色和花纹都有所不同。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9234216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9234216 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/shutterstock_624570629-600x397.jpg" alt="龟甲虫。(shutterstock)" width="600" b="397" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">龟甲虫。(shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>9. 半透明壳蜗牛</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9234223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9234223 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/NTD-18-1-600x400.jpg" alt="半透明壳蜗牛。(J. Bedek, Alexander M. Weigand, Wikimedia Commons)" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">半透明壳蜗牛。(J. Bedek, Alexander M. Weigand, Wikimedia Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这只蜗牛直到2012年才被发现，如果不是生物学家深入洞穴探险，它还将不为人知——那可是深入地表743到1392米的克罗地亚地下洞穴。</p>
<h2>10. 透翅蝶（glasswinged butterfly）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_9234229" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9234229 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/Greta_oto-600x586.jpg" alt="透翅蝶。(David Tiller, Wikimedia Commons)" width="600" b="586" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">透翅蝶。(David Tiller, Wikimedia Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p>它的正名叫宽纹黑脉绡蝶，风格和亮丽的七彩蝴蝶全然不同。</p>
