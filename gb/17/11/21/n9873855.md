<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>双层巴士本周上路试运营3个月</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年11月21日讯】（<span lang="ZH-TW">记者余天白温哥华报导）</span><span lang="ZH-TW">本周起，二辆全新双层公车将首次踏上大温路面，开跑为期三月的试验性运营，以期缓解部分长途线路公车人满为患的现状。若试行成功，大温<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%BF%90%E8%81%94.md#1">运联</a>（</span><span lang="EN-US">Translink</span><span lang="ZH-TW">）计划于</span><span lang="EN-US">2019</span><span lang="ZH-TW">年前再新购</span><span lang="EN-US">30</span><span lang="ZH-TW">辆双层公车服务于本地。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%BF%90%E8%81%94.md#1">运联</a>表示，大温地区公共交通乘客人数正在快速增加，仅去年便上升了</span><span lang="EN-US">6%</span><span lang="ZH-TW">，其中以部分来往于城市之间的长途公共汽车最为拥挤，如来往于兰里市和本拿比市的</span><span lang="EN-US">555 </span><span lang="ZH-TW">路公车、连接素里市和列治文市的</span><span lang="EN-US">301</span><span lang="ZH-TW">路公车等。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">“我们跟不上需求量。”运联首席执行官</span><span lang="EN-US">Kevin Desmond</span><span lang="ZH-TW">表示，“长期以来，（</span><span lang="EN-US">301</span><span lang="ZH-TW">路公车）一直至少有</span><span lang="EN-US">11</span><span lang="ZH-TW">名站着的乘客。对于一辆行驶在高速公路上的公车，</span><span lang="EN-US">11</span><span lang="ZH-TW">名站立乘客是一个很大的数字，而且（对他们来说）这也不是一趟舒服的旅程。”</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">由于双层公车载客量为普通公车的两倍，“这些公车是我们对公车拥挤现象的解决方法之一。我们也希望用更舒适、更有趣的乘坐体验来吸引乘客、维持乘客数量。”</span><span lang="EN-US"> Desmond</span><span lang="ZH-TW">说。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">本次上路的二辆双层公车将在未来三个月内试行以下路线：</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">　　</span><span lang="EN-US">301</span><span lang="ZH-TW">路公交线：</span><span lang="EN-US">Newton Exchange</span><span lang="ZH-TW">（素里市）</span><span lang="EN-US">-Brighouse Station</span><span lang="ZH-TW">（列治文）</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span><span lang="ZH-TW">　　</span><span lang="EN-US">311</span><span lang="ZH-TW">路公交线：</span><span lang="EN-US">Scottsdale Exchange</span><span lang="ZH-TW">（三角洲）</span><span lang="EN-US">-Bridgeport Station</span><span lang="ZH-TW">（列治文）</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span><span lang="ZH-TW">　　</span><span lang="EN-US">351/354</span><span lang="ZH-TW">公交线：白石镇</span><span lang="EN-US">/</span><span lang="ZH-TW">南素里</span><span lang="EN-US">-Bridgeport Station</span><span lang="ZH-TW">（列治文）</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span><span lang="ZH-TW">　　</span><span lang="EN-US">555</span><span lang="ZH-TW">公交线：</span><span lang="EN-US">Carvolth Exchange</span><span lang="ZH-TW">（兰里市）</span><span lang="EN-US">-Lougheed Station</span><span lang="ZH-TW">（本拿比）</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span><span lang="ZH-TW">　　</span><span lang="EN-US">601/620</span><span lang="ZH-TW">公交线：南三角洲</span><span lang="EN-US">/Tsawwassen</span><span lang="ZH-TW">渡轮码头</span><span lang="EN-US">-Bridgeport Station</span><span lang="ZH-TW">（列治文）</span><span lang="EN-US"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span lang="ZH-TW">然而，对于同样面临拥挤难题的</span><span lang="EN-US">99</span><span lang="ZH-TW">路等市内公车，运联表示没有计划用<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8F%8C%E5%B1%82%E5%B7%B4%E5%A3%AB.md#1">双层巴士</a>代替当前的多节型长公车。“这些多节型公车还有很大的生命力。它们更适合于大批量的乘客上下。”</span><span lang="EN-US"> Desmond</span><span lang="ZH-TW">说。?</span><span lang="ZH-TW">　</span><span lang="EN-US">◇</span></p>
