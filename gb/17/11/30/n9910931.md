<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>不挨饿又想减肥 你需要这12种饱足感食物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/egg-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>饱足感强的食物能帮助大脑接收到吃饱信号，降低食欲，有助于减肥。(Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>想<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%8F%E8%82%A5.md#1">减肥</a>却不愿挨饿？那么特别有<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A5%B1%E8%B6%B3%E6%84%9F.md#1">饱足感</a>的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E7%89%A9.md#1">食物</a>对你来说是最佳选择。事实上，人们在吃完某些特定食物后，大脑更容易接收到吃饱的信号，降低食欲。想减肥的人可以从这几种食物中选择，不必刻意节食也不用担心发胖。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>1. 苹果</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9910939" style="width: 339px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1606151528192483-e1512059725304.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9910939 " title="Fresh red wet apple with drops isolated on white." src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1606151528192483-e1512059725304-600x516.jpg" alt="" width="339" b="292" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">苹果。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>苹果的果胶遇到水后会膨胀，所以吃苹果喝水，会很有<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A5%B1%E8%B6%B3%E6%84%9F.md#1">饱足感</a>。加之苹果营养丰富，是相当理想的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%87%8F%E8%82%A5.md#1">减肥</a>代餐。在餐前一个半小时吃一个苹果，可以补充纤维和水，在正餐时食量就会减少。（<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><ahref="/gb/17/10/25/n9770641.md#1"><span style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;">日本苹果10秒卖出1颗！这三种苹果最热销</span></a></span>）</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>2. 酪梨（鳄梨、牛油果）</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_8783750" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/1702061722272669-e1512059865351.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8783750 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/02/1702061722272669-e1512059865351-600x345.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="345" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">酪梨（鳄梨、牛油果）。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>酪梨又称鳄梨、牛油果，富含健康的单一不饱和<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%84%82%E8%82%AA.md#1">脂肪</a>，这种脂肪能增加我们的饱足感。如果早餐食用半个酪梨和一片吐司，直到中午你都不会感到饿。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>3. 毛豆</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_5789146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/141011020922836.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-5789146" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/141011020922836-600x401.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">毛豆。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>毛豆虽小，但它的膳食纤维能增加饱足感、促进肠道蠕动，而维生素C则有助伤口愈合与结缔组织的生长，还有烟碱素可以增进神经系统和黏膜的健康。除此之外，毛豆所含的蛋白质更是牛奶的12倍。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>4. 泡菜</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9910948" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9910948 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/kimchi-600x428.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="428" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">泡菜。(Pixabay)</figcaption></figure>
<p>酸黄瓜、酸菜、泡菜和其它发酵<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%A3%9F%E7%89%A9.md#1">食物</a>，含有短链<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%84%82%E8%82%AA.md#1">脂肪</a>酸。短链脂肪酸可强化大脑与内脏、肠道之间的连结，刺激某种荷尔蒙的分泌，增加给身体的饱足讯号。（<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #008000; text-decoration: underline;" href="/gb/16/11/16/n8500529.md#1">益生菌改善情绪！常吃发酵食品解心忧</a></span></span>）</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>5. 辣椒粉</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_6540292" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/1511191543412483-e1512060187412.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6540292 size-large" title="韩国辣椒粉和辣椒（fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2015/12/1511191543412483-e1512060187412-600x357.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">辣椒。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>辣椒里的辣椒素能激发消化。曾经有研究指出，在餐中加四分之一茶匙的辣椒能增加饱足感，达到控制食欲之效。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>6. 黑巧克力</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_8580938" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/1612110027522669.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8580938 size-large" title="巧克力（fotolia）" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/1612110027522669-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黑巧克力。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>嘴馋想吃甜食前，不妨先试试黑巧克力。研究指出，黑巧克力有降血压、保护心脏和大脑之效。和牛奶巧克力相比，黑巧克力也比较容易引发饱足感，同时还能抑制吃甜食与咸食的欲望。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>7. 鸡蛋</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9336286" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1610111427332483-e1512060426792.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9336286 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/06/1610111427332483-e1512060426792-600x350.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="350" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>鸡蛋是非常健康的食物，最主要的原因是在于蛋白。早餐吃鸡蛋，可以让你在午饭之前都充满饱足感。白煮蛋的热量也比炒蛋及煎蛋低。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>8. 坚果</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_10940704" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/food-2584022_1280.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-10940704" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/12/food-2584022_1280-600x401.jpg" alt="坚果和种子是素食者冰箱中的重要食材。" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">坚果。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>坚果是另一种可以让你吃得更少、有饱足感的食品。因为坚果富含不饱和脂肪，还有额外的蛋白质和纤维，能有效控制食欲。但注意最好选择没有调味的坚果，避免选择调味太重的、香咸的坚果，否则有可能食用过量。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>9. 燕麦粥</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_5758310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1402052350592483.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-5758310" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/08/1402052350592483-600x492.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="492" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">燕麦粥。（Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>一碗燕麦片能让你的饱足感维持更长时间。燕麦富含纤维、蛋白质等，可以增加体内调节食欲荷尔蒙胆囊收缩素的水平，能抑制一些人的饥饿感。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>10. 水</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_6762140" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/10/100717093002100445-e1523980779513.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-6762140" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/10/100717093002100445-600x471.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="471" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">水。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有时身体缺水会让你误以为自己饿了。为什么呢？饥饿的症状和脱水很类似：能量低，认知功能下降，情绪不佳。所以如果下次吃饭前，先喝一大杯水，你的饥饿感可能会消失；多食用富含水分的水果和蔬菜也有帮助。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>11. 马铃薯</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_9910980" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1708011731362483-e1512061077573.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9910980 size-large" title="土豆和马铃薯" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/1708011731362483-e1512061077573-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">马铃薯。(Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>
<p>马铃薯到底是肥胖头号杀手还是减重好帮手？不同于地瓜本身就具有甜味，马铃薯本身没什么味道，所以在烹调过程中多半要加料增添美味。关键因素在于你是怎么料理马铃薯的。如果加了很多料或油脂下去烹调的话，那马铃薯就是肥胖杀手；如果只是把它拿来煮汤或烤熟来吃，那么它就是减肥帮手。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>12. 鱼</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11281615" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/shutterstock_256621606-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11281615" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/04/shutterstock_256621606-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鱼。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果想通过吃鱼肉减肥，要特别注意鱼肉的烹调方式，尽量选择清淡的鱼肉，避免重口味食物，这样才能有助减肥。Omega-3脂肪酸在某些富含油脂的鱼类中特别丰富，像是鲑鱼、鲭鱼、鲱鱼等。除了帮助减重，Omega-3脂肪酸也有助预防高血压和阿兹海默症。</p>
