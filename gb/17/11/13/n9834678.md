<h3>不寻常的“狗”</h3>
<p>他把它带回家，取名尼奥（Neo），但很快就意识到这只“小公狗”不寻常。他没法训练尼奥上厕所，而且这小家伙总想吸引主人的注意。可作为低年级大学生的他需要全天上课，没有多少时间陪它，所以尼奥只能自己在后院玩。</p>
<p>尼奥会在地上挖洞，还会越过围栏去寻找邻家的狗。小主人发现后，建起了更高的围栏。但尼奥会用尖利的牙齿啃开一个大洞，然后又钻出去。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9637148" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170915-sulin-wolf5.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9637148 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170915-sulin-wolf5-600x345.jpg" alt="(Wolf Connection)" width="600" b="345" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: blue;"><ahref="https://wolfconnection.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wolf Connection</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>邻居们也觉得尼奥有点奇怪。一开始，他们带它进屋，让它和自家的狗一起玩，但尼奥却视这些狗不见，目光躲闪，还会藏在卫生间不出来。邻居们喂它狗吃的零食，它也不感兴趣。</p>
<p>这位毛访客总是这么奇怪，邻居们终于决定联系南亚利桑那州人道协会（Humane Society of Southern Arizona）——它的主人似乎无法尽到责任，需要有人来过问。</p>
<p>该协会前主席莫伦．奥尼尔（Maureen O&#8217;Nell）在办公室等待着这位不寻常的来客。她还记得第一眼看到尼奥时，它不敢和她对视。她也意识到，幼时的它被弃路边不是一件正常的事。</p>
<h3>找到原主人</h3>
<p>根据大学生提供的地址，她去了那栋房子，找到住在那里的夫妇，问道：“你们知道那不是一只狗，对吧？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_9637149" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170915-sulin-wolf4.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9637149 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170915-sulin-wolf4-600x535.jpg" alt="(Wolf Connection)" width="600" b="535" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: blue;"><ahref="https://wolfconnection.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wolf Connection</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这对夫妇说，他们当时也起疑了。事实上，尼奥是狼和狗杂交的后代——一只真正的狼狗，而且主要遗传了狼的特征，狼性占主导。</p>
<p>奥尼尔告诉宠物媒体“The Dodo”，她做了一番研究，在亚利桑那州不允许养这种杂交的狼狗，除非是北美土着人或持有特别许可证。如果动物收容所收留这类狼狗，也须向主管部门报告。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9637151" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170915-sulin-wolf2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9637151 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170915-sulin-wolf2-600x359.jpg" alt="(Wolf Connection)" width="600" b="359" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: blue;"><ahref="https://wolfconnection.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wolf Connection</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>奥尼尔与狼狗救援中心及庇护所取得了联系，看他们是否能够接收尼奥。而把它从房子边上捡回家的那位小主人，也同意这对尼奥来说是最好的解决办法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9637147" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170915-sulin-wolf6.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9637147 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170915-sulin-wolf6-600x450.jpg" alt="(Wolf Connection)" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: blue;"><ahref="https://wolfconnection.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wolf Connection</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>尼奥到庇护所的那个晚上，可说是欣喜若狂，马上加入了一众同伴的“夜嚎队”。尽管医疗人员第一天将它隔离起来接受体检，它还是逃掉了，跑到狼狗群中去和“主雌”玛雅（Maya）打招呼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9637152" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170915-sulin-wolf1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9637152 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/09/20170915-sulin-wolf1-450x450.jpg" alt="(Wolf Connection)" width="450" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">(Courtesy of?<span style="color: blue;"><ahref="https://wolfconnection.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wolf Connection</a></span>)</figcaption></figure>
<p>奥尼尔说：“它迫不及待地融入了狼狗群，它知道自己属于这个群体。”</p>
