  <figure id="attachment_9866094" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171120-yichun-food-05.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9866094" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171120-yichun-food-05-600x400.jpg" alt="静静停在河面上的船舶（Brooklyn Lifeboat Seafood 提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">静静停在河面上的船舶（Brooklyn Lifeboat Seafood 提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>因为这里是海流与河流交汇处，鱼虾海产异常丰富。而且蚝场就在不远之处，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%82%89%E5%B0%BC%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C.md#1">悉尼海鲜</a>市场的生蚝有10% 来自这一水域。我今天要说的能吃到当地渔民当天打捞的新鲜海鲜的餐馆——Lifeboat Seafood就位于布鲁克林的码头。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9866089" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171120-yichun-food-02.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9866089" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171120-yichun-food-02-600x400.jpg" alt="Brooklyn 河边有一家美味的海鲜店（Brooklyn Lifeboat Seafood 提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Brooklyn 河边有一家美味的海鲜店（Brooklyn Lifeboat Seafood 提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>Lifeboat Seafood出售的鱼虾全是当地渔民捕捞的。 老板Glen绝对自豪的说，他餐厅的鱼虾是悉尼最新鲜的。渔民们把当天捕捞的鱼虾存放在他的库房里，他有最先购买权！第二天一早渔民们才把鱼虾拿到<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%82%89%E5%B0%BC%E6%B5%B7%E9%B2%9C.md#1">悉尼海鲜</a>市场去拍卖。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9866091" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171120-yichun-food-03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9866091" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171120-yichun-food-03-600x400.jpg" alt="Brooklyn 河边最新鲜的食材（Brooklyn Lifeboat Seafood 提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Brooklyn 河边最新鲜的食材（Brooklyn Lifeboat Seafood 提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_9866092" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171120-yichun-food-04.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9866092" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/11/20171120-yichun-food-04-600x400.jpg" alt="Brooklyn 河边最新鲜的食材（Brooklyn Lifeboat Seafood 提供）" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Brooklyn 河边最新鲜的食材（Brooklyn Lifeboat Seafood 提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>估计悉尼没有第二家餐馆的海鲜能比Lifeboat Seafood更新鲜了。陈列在橱窗里的海鲜就像刚刚从水里捕捞出来，肉质新鲜带有光泽。一看就想来它一盘，大快朵颐。很多客人远程赶来这里享受悉尼最新鲜的海鲜，炸鱼薯条Fish&amp;Chips和海鲜盘是最受欢迎的。</p>
  <p>从悉尼到Brooklyn的交通方便，自驾开车从Hornsby起仅需25分钟，也可以从Centre火车站乘坐到Newcastle快车，在一个小时就到达，沿途景色美丽。Lifeboat Seafood餐馆距离Hawkesbury? River火车站仅600米。</p>
