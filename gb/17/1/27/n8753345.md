<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>跟着大厨学做菜【小葱烧豆腐】</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/124fdf301ad65bd66bb382193e06987f-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>豆腐外酥内嫩的小葱烧豆腐（新唐人提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2017年01月28日讯】要怎样把简单家常菜做出高级的感觉呢？一块烧热的铁板，把<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90.md#1">豆腐</a>的原汁原味，跟葱的香味完全释放出来！</p>
<p><center><a src=""></a><a href="https://git.io/JvXfI"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jmzkba221/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/d7cvYrkncnA" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></center><br />
<strong>主料：</strong>滑<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90.md#1">豆腐</a>半盒、小葱5根</p>
<p><strong>配料：</strong>蘑菇粒、葱粒各1汤匙、清汤</p>
<p><strong>调料：</strong>蚝油1汤匙、生抽半汤匙、白糖1茶匙、生粉水1汤匙、盐少许</p>
<p><strong>做法：</strong></p>
<p>1.滑豆腐切成扁长方状</p>
<figure id="attachment_8753469" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8753469 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/f3d211d394e39000076a66de8569c68e-600x338.jpg" alt="豆腐切成长方状（新唐人）" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">豆腐切成长方状。（新唐人提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.小葱切段 ，葱白要切开再切断。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8753473" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8753473 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/fe27891568c7c59639f9ba1ce2297d6b-600x338.jpg" alt="青葱切段 葱白部分中间拉一刀（新唐人提供）" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E8%91%B1.md#1">青葱</a>切段，葱白部分中间拉一刀。（新唐人提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.猛火阴油煎豆腐，把豆腐煎成黄色。翻过来就放盐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8753478" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8753478 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/9d4652f6c66bc05dbe9466bc8838c7c6-600x338.jpg" alt="豆腐一翻面 趁热加盐（新唐人提供）" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">豆腐一翻面，趁热加盐。（新唐人提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.豆腐煎完以后，放清汤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8753481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8753481 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/b83c25bd8f0c2db01bbea131c1a27aac-600x338.jpg" alt="豆腐加清汤（新唐人提供）" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">豆腐加清汤。（新唐人提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.蘑菇粒、葱粒各1汤匙。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8753483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8753483 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/895d3f01eda666a8da5b66e4b2effccf-600x338.jpg" alt="浓浓葱香味的葱烧豆腐（新唐人提供）" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">浓浓葱香味的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%91%B1%E7%83%A7%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90.md#1">葱烧豆腐</a>。（新唐人提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.调料放进去，用生粉水勾芡收汁。</p>
<p>7.烧铁板，葱段放到铁板上面。</p>
<p>8.把烧好的豆腐放到葱上面，把调味好的汁浇上去。</p>
<p>9.最后撒上葱花。</p>
<p><strong>温馨提醒：</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8753486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8753486 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/c058473ef7289c615910aab5c876436b-600x338.jpg" alt="小葱烧豆腐-13_CHN" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">简单的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%AE%B6%E5%B8%B8%E8%B1%86%E8%85%90.md#1">家常豆腐</a> 变成可以上餐桌的高级料理</figcaption></figure>
<p>豆腐在锅内煮得最烧的时候放盐，因为盐通过加温的热度，可以释放出它的香味。另外罗师傅做菜是不用味精的，他认为用盐、糖、酱油等等比例调配得宜，就可以达到一样的效果，所以他宁可不要用这些化学的调味剂。那一般家庭要怎么准备清汤呢？罗师傅说，做广东菜一定得有汤，一般家庭做菜，可以准备一锅汤，在做菜之前舀一些清汤起来备用，做菜用这些汤调味，味道更天然美味！</p>
