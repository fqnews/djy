<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>家长和小孩一起手工DIY玩具 好处多多</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/GettyImages-461419882-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>增加亲子互动（Matt Cardy/Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年01月25日讯】现在的家长们都忙于上班工作，再加上商场里的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8E%A9%E5%85%B7.md#1">玩具</a>琳琅满目，让人看的眼花撩乱 。家长都根本没有时间也没有兴趣带着小孩子来DIY自制手工玩具。其实，鼓励小孩动手自制玩具对孩子的好处是非常多的。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a src=""></a><a href="https://rawcdn.githack.com/sxrssb2693/oo/master/jump2.htm?id=jNTBR"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sxrssb2693/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/bannedbook/fanqiang/wiki">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/DHJvTSTDYuA" width="640" b="360" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<h2>锻炼小朋友动手能力</h2>
<p style="text-align: left;">在自制的过程中，孩子需要自己写写画画，可以锻炼动手能力，小孩子肌肉的灵活协调度也会得到发展。当小孩子专注地做手工的时候，也会变得比较容易集中精力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8742773" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8742773 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/GettyImages-461419882-600x367.jpg" alt="RADSTOCK, UNITED KINGDOM - JANUARY 06: A young girl draws at a playgroup for pre-school aged children in Chilcompton near Radstock on January 6, 2015 in Somerset, England. Along with the health and the economy, education and childcare are to be key issues in the forthcoming election. (Photo by Matt Cardy/Getty Images)" width="600" b="367" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">DIY能锻炼小孩子的动手能力。（Matt Cardy/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>激发小孩子的创造力</h2>
<p>在这个过程中，小朋友需要发挥创造力， 用手边的工具和材料来设计制作。如果家长对小孩子加以正面的引导，小孩子可以在制作的过程中不知不觉地学会很多东西。例如在玩煮饭的游戏的时候，可以告诉小朋友要和家长一起分享食物，久而久之小朋友就会记得分享这个动作。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8742771" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8742771 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/GettyImages-461419692-600x421.jpg" alt="RADSTOCK, UNITED KINGDOM - JANUARY 06: A young boy paints at a playgroup for pre-school aged children in Chilcompton near Radstock on January 6, 2015 in Somerset, England. Along with the health and the economy, education and childcare are to be key issues in the forthcoming election. (Photo by Matt Cardy/Getty Images)" width="600" b="421" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">DIY可以培养小孩的创造力。（Matt Cardy/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<h2>让小孩子学会珍惜</h2>
<p>小朋友自己动手做<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%8E%A9%E5%85%B7.md#1">玩具</a>的时候，会感受到过程中的艰辛，对花了好大力气好长时间做出来的玩具，会更加懂得珍惜，不会随意地搞破坏。</p>
<h2>增强<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%BA%B2%E5%AD%90.md#1">亲子</a>的互动</h2>
<p>现在家长忙于工作，忙于社交，忙着看手机看电脑，和小朋友交流玩耍的机会越来越少。趁着和小朋友一起做玩具的过程，可以增强<ahref="/gb/tag/%E4%BA%B2%E5%AD%90.md#1">亲子</a>之间的互动，让父母和子女的关系更加融洽。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8742772" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-8742772 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/01/GettyImages-461419718-600x400.jpg" alt="RADSTOCK, UNITED KINGDOM - JANUARY 06: A young boy plays with toys at a playgroup for pre-school aged children in Chilcompton near Radstock on January 6, 2015 in Somerset, England. Along with the health and the economy, education and childcare are to be key issues in the forthcoming election. (Photo by Matt Cardy/Getty Images)" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄金发育时期，小孩需多动脑。（Matt Cardy/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p style="text-align: left;">在家中DIY手工制作的玩具，虽然没有商场中买的那么专业，那么多元化，但毕竟是孩子自己动手、自己创造的。这样的玩具，在孩子成长过程中产生的作用和意义，都是市面上的玩具没法比的。所以抓住小孩子成长发育的黄金点，和孩子做一些有意义的手工吧。</p>
