<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：康乃馨</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/08121200392010003-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>康乃馨是适合送给母亲的花。（苏玉芬/大纪元）  　
</h6>
<hr>
<p>一朵康乃馨活现在我的眼前，活现在我的心中，辉光闪闪……。<br />
总在五月里开放，总开放得那么壮盛，那么严肃，那么深具意义……。<br />
它，到底是怎么样的一种花？<br />
它是一种充满赞美和感恩的花！<br />
所有的根茎，所有的枝叶，都在收敛，都在凝聚，都在输送，收敛凝聚一种异样的光 辉，然后输送给花，给花去开放，去表现，去欢呼……。<br />
那是一种赞美。<br />
那是一种感恩。<br />
那是一种自我期许。<br />
每一瓣花瓣都发出一句句赞美，每一瓣花瓣都写着一句句感恩，每一瓣花瓣都辉映着 一句句自我期许：<br />
——慈晖母爱，永世长存，人间永沐！<br />
——要有母爱，才有大地万物的发荣滋长！<br />
——母亲的恩惠，如山高，如海深，永难报答！<br />
——在母亲面前，无论长得多高，无论有多大成就，永远是小孩，永远渺小，永远要 感恩！<br />
——努力奋发，以报答亲恩！<br />
我希望，我祈求，我手中拿着的康乃馨，永远是红色的，不会是白色的。</p>
<p><strong>Carnation? ?</strong> Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>A carnation appears vividly before my eyes, appears vividly in my heart, with radiance…<br />
It always blossoms in May, always blossoms thus grandeur, thus severe, thus meaningful…<br />
What kind of flower is it?<br />
It’s a kind of flower full of praise and be graceful for kindness!<br />
The roots and stems, the branches and leaves, all are draws their horns, all are in coherent sheaves, all are transport, draws their horns and in coherent sheaves into a different radiance, then transports to flowers, let the flowers to blossom, to show, to hail…<br />
It’s a praise!<br />
It’s a graceful for kindness!<br />
It’s a self expectation!<br />
Every petal send off a praise, every petal write down a graceful for kindness, every petal shine brilliantly of self expectation:<br />
&#8211;Maternal love kindly, long live eternally, bath eternally all over the world!<br />
&#8211;It must the maternal love for everything of the earth to thriving and propagate!<br />
&#8211;The grace of mother, high like the mountain, deep like the sea, isn’t repay forever!<br />
&#8211;In front of mother, in spite of how tall we grow, how large our achievement, we are children forever, we are insignificant forever, and will graceful for kindness forever!<br />
&#8211;We must strive to show great animation, and repay for the grace of mother!<br />
I hope, I pray, the carnation hold in my hand, will be red forever, and never be white.@</p>
