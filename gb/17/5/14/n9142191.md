<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>缓解肌肉酸痛的7种食物</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/shutterstock_507140221-1-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>武汉男子为练出六块肌，每天锻炼2小时，第四天就发现酱油尿，查出罹患了急性肾衰竭。(Panumas Yanuthai/Shutterstock)
</h6>
<hr>
<p><ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%82%8C%E8%82%89%E9%85%B8%E7%97%9B.md#1">肌肉酸痛</a>很要命，我知道有人因长期肌肉酸痛而放弃了健身计划。锻炼后肌肉酸痛是正常的，特别是当你特别劳累的时候，其实，锻炼后选择吃某些食物，可以加速恢复。</p>
<p>下面介绍七种缓解<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%82%8C%E8%82%89%E9%85%B8%E7%97%9B.md#1">肌肉酸痛</a>、加速恢复的食物（饮水也包含在内），你既可以在锻炼之后马上吃，也可以当天晚些食用。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">一、蓝莓</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11378703" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/1611221531012669.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/1611221531012669-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-11378703" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蓝莓具有<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%8A%97%E6%B0%A7%E5%8C%96.md#1">抗氧化</a>性能，可缓解肌肉酸痛。（Jiri Hera/Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>研究已发现蓝莓可减少肌肉酸痛。这种水果含有<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%8A%97%E6%B0%A7%E5%8C%96.md#1">抗氧化</a>剂，有助于消除运动时肌肉中产生的自由基。</p>
<p>其它浆果也可以帮助你恢复：樱桃促进褪黑激素（睡眠激素）的分泌，会改善睡眠，使你更快恢复。黑莓、覆盆子和草莓也有缓解肌肉酸痛的抗氧化剂，具有<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%8A%97%E7%82%8E.md#1">抗炎</a>效力。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">二、鸡蛋</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11378709" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/food-breakfast-egg-milk-1.jpeg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/food-breakfast-egg-milk-1-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-11378709" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋富含氨基酸，且有抗氧化性能。（Tookapic/Pexels）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鸡蛋含有所有人体必需的氨基酸，可减少肌肉酸痛、促进蛋白质合成。它们还具有抗氧化性质，具有消炎功效。</p>
<p>你不必害怕吃下整颗鸡蛋，血液中的胆固醇水平并不会因此提高。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">三、水</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11378713" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/water-drink-glass-drinking-1.jpeg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/water-drink-glass-drinking-1-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-11378713" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">喝水少容易肌肉酸痛。（Kaboompics // Karolina/Pexels）</figcaption></figure>
<p>让身体保持水分有助于更快恢复。研究发现，与体内含水度正常的人相比，运动时、运动后脱水的人肌肉更酸痛。研究还表明，脱水可引起肌肉痉挛。</p>
<p>记住在锻炼时和锻炼后喝水，并且每天都喝2至3升水。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">四、蔬菜</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11378724" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/pexels-photo-115537-1.jpeg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/pexels-photo-115537-1-600x402.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="402" class="size-large wp-image-11378724" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">蔬菜<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%8A%97%E7%82%8E.md#1">抗炎</a>、抗氧化，可缓解肌肉酸痛。（surajith s/pexels）</figcaption></figure>
<p>蔬菜可以有效地抗炎和抗氧化。十字花科蔬菜还可调节雌激素水平，对抗减缓恢复的自由基。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">五、三文鱼</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11378737" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/iStock_000042523230_Large-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/iStock_000042523230_Large-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-11378737" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">吃三文鱼可缓解肌肉酸痛。（klenova/iStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>三文鱼含有蛋白质和健康的脂肪，可加速肌肉恢复；还富含ω?3脂肪酸，可以抗炎、促进血液流向肌肉。</p>
<p>剧烈锻炼后，将三文鱼与蔬菜和浆果一起吃，可以更快地恢复。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">六、姜</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11378742" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/iStock_000076191199_Medium-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/iStock_000076191199_Medium-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-11378742" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">吃了姜的人锻炼后较少肌肉酸痛。（Maya23K/iStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>姜可以缓解肌肉酸痛，因为它具有很强的抗氧化和抗炎特性。研究发现，与未吃姜者相比，吃了熟姜或生姜的人锻炼后较少疼痛。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">七、升糖指数高的水果及含淀粉蔬菜</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11378746" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/iStock_000047737786_Medium-1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/iStock_000047737786_Medium-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" class="size-large wp-image-11378746" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">吃西瓜等升糖指数高的水果可缓解肌肉酸痛。（Sergey Skleznev/iStock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在锻炼后吃升糖指数（GI）高的水果和含淀粉蔬菜，如西瓜、菠萝、土豆和红薯，有助于补充能量，增加胰岛素分泌——胰岛素在肌肉生长中起着至关重要的作用。</p>
