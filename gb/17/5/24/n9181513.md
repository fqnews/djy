  <p id="E85" class="qowt-stl-正文"><span id="E86" class="qowt-font4">前日的计票结果中，竞争最激烈的Courtenay-Comox</span><span id="E87" class="qowt-font4">选区</span><span id="E88" class="qowt-font4">，</span><span id="E89" class="qowt-font4">NDP候选人刚刚将其9票领先扩大至领先13票，昨日该选区</span><span id="E90" class="qowt-font4">又历经几番拉锯式争夺</span><span id="E91" class="qowt-font4">，自由党候选人</span><span id="E92" class="qowt-font4">先是</span><span id="E93" class="qowt-font4">以3票</span><span id="E94" class="qowt-font4">之差险超</span><span id="E95" class="qowt-font4">NDP，</span><span id="E96" class="qowt-font4">然而好景不长，截至周二晚八点，NDP</span><span id="E97" class="qowt-font4">候选人</span><span id="E98" class="qowt-font4">再度反超，并将优势从最初<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%82%B9%E7%A5%A8.md#1">点票</a>的</span><span id="E99" class="qowt-font4">9票领先扩大</span><span id="E100" class="qowt-font4">至101票。不过仍有数百张<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BC%BA%E5%B8%AD%E6%8A%95%E7%A5%A8.md#1">缺席投票</a>还在点算当中，所以该选区谁能胜出尚未尘埃落定。</span></p>
  <p id="E101" class="qowt-stl-正文"><span id="E102" class="qowt-font4">Courtenay-Comox</span><span id="E103" class="qowt-font4">选区，堪称今年<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%9C%81%E9%80%89.md#1">省选</a>的关键之争，破局所在。如果省自由党能在此翻盘，就可跨过</span><span id="E104" class="qowt-font4">单独执政的门槛-44席。</span><span id="E105" class="qowt-font4">如若不然，主动权就会交到绿党手中。</span></p>
  <p id="E107" class="qowt-stl-正文"><span id="E108" class="qowt-font4"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%91%E8%AF%97.md#1">卑诗</a>选举局（Elections BC）点算各选区<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%BC%BA%E5%B8%AD%E6%8A%95%E7%A5%A8.md#1">缺席投票</a>的</span><span id="E109" class="qowt-font4">最终<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%82%B9%E7%A5%A8.md#1">点票</a></span><span id="E110" class="qowt-font4">工作，星期三正式完成。截至星期二下午，卑诗87个选区中，7</span><span id="E111" class="qowt-font4">3</span><span id="E112" class="qowt-font4">个选区已经完成了最终计票。</span></p>
  <p id="E113" class="qowt-stl-正文"><span id="E114" class="qowt-font4">Coquitlam-Burke Mountain也是竞争激烈的选区之一，自由党候选人初期点票时超出170票，最终结果是超出215票。</span></p>
  <p id="E115" class="qowt-stl-正文"><span id="E116" class="qowt-font4">Richmond-Queensborough选区的选票也极其接近</span><span id="E117" class="qowt-font4">，</span><span id="E118" class="qowt-font4">最终自由党候选人比NDP竞争对手多出134票。</span></p>
  <p id="E119" class="qowt-stl-正文"><span id="E120" class="qowt-font4">Maple Ridge-Mission选区截至星期二</span><span id="E121" class="qowt-font4">晚</span><span id="E122" class="qowt-font4">虽然尚未结束点票，但NDP候选人领先自由党的微弱优势略有扩大。</span><span id="E125" class="qowt-font4">◇</span></p>
