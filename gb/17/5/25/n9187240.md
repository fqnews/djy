  <figure id="attachment_9189502" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/1705260146171487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9189502" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/1705260146171487-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="344" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%9C%B1%E7%9B%B8%E6%97%AD.md#1">朱相旭</a>（右）与<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%BD%A6%E8%89%BA%E8%8E%B2.md#1">车艺莲</a>25日在华克山庄举行结婚典礼，图为俩人婚礼照。（HB娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_9189508" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/1705260146091487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9189508" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/1705260146091487-600x437.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="364" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">朱相旭（右）与车艺莲25日在华克山庄举行结婚典礼，图为两人婚礼照。（HB娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>  <figure id="attachment_9189512" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/1705260146131487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9189512" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/1705260146131487-600x410.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="342" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">朱相旭（右）与车艺莲25日在华克山庄举行结婚典礼，图为两人婚礼照。（HB娱乐提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>两人日前特别飞到夏威夷拍婚纱，朱相旭透露，原本要在夏威夷求婚，但因为被她发现而作罢；车艺莲则娇嗔，朱相旭直到现在都还没向她求婚。</p>
  <p>这次婚礼没有请婚礼规划师，两人包办所有细节。准新人因为新婚紧张不已，朱相旭前一晚喝了两瓶烧酒助眠，车艺莲更是紧张到整晚失眠。</p>
  <p>之前车艺莲曾在S.E.S.团员Bada的婚礼上接到捧花，如今大婚，也特别请来Bada、wax、JeA演唱婚礼歌曲。婚礼由赵在允主持，并请到《来自星星的你》剧组造型师郑允基打点造型。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_9188992" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/1705252257001487.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9188992" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/05/1705252257001487.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="547" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">朱相旭（右）与车艺莲25日在华克山庄举行结婚典礼。（newsis提供）</figcaption></figure>  <p>两人在戏中虽然相爱，却被逼着嫁给他人，如今现实中有情人终成眷属。朱相旭透露，目前还没计划去哪里度蜜月，有可能六月初才会确定。《<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E4%B8%BD%E7%9A%84%E8%AF%B1%E6%83%91.md#1">华丽的诱惑</a>》目前正在台湾的电视台热播。</p>
