<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>DIY父亲节礼物：美丽的黄玫瑰笔筒</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4336-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（图／张铭芬）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>小时候，是父亲把我高高举起；在人群中看不到最热闹的事物时，也是父亲把我举高坐在他的肩膀上，让我可以高人一等，看见最吸引人的事物。那双有力的臂膀，是我的安全感、家人的依靠。</p>
<p>献身、护家是父亲的代名词，黄色玫瑰花有着类似的意涵，今年的<ahref="/gb/tag/%E7%88%B6%E4%BA%B2%E8%8A%82.md#1">父亲节</a>，想跟父亲大声说：“爸爸！我爱您！祝您父亲节快乐！”</p>
<p>在酷暑的假期里，到哪儿都热，不如请妈妈帮忙一起为父亲做个有爱心的笔筒当礼物，给平日打拼的爸爸一个夏日惊喜。</p>
<p><strong>?</strong></p>
<p><strong>材料:</strong>美工刀、剪刀、直尺、丹迪纸或其他色彩纸、白胶、切割垫、铅笔、卫生纸卷筒、厚纸板或礼盒、无墨水原子笔笔芯、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%BB%8F%E5%9C%9F.md#1">黏土</a>(超轻土、树脂土或造形土皆可，质地较细的超轻土效果会比较好)、亮粉胶水、彩色笔、牙签。</p>
<p><strong>做法:</strong>本作品由笔筒、<ahref="/gb/tag/%E9%BB%8F%E5%9C%9F.md#1">黏土</a>玫瑰及小卡片3部分组合而成，做法依这三部分一一介绍。</p>
<p>1、取一片厚纸板，描画并剪出与卫生纸卷筒一样大小的圆形。因为厚纸板比较不好剪，更不好用美工刀切割，尤其小朋友操作，危险性比较高，所以建议教学时一边剪一边将多余的纸板往下翻折，让剪刀比较容易前进。剪下圆形后，比比看有没有凸出圆周，若有凸出则再稍微修剪一下，在卫生纸卷筒一端的圆周边涂上一层薄薄的白胶，再将圆型纸板黏上待干备用，这是笔筒的“骨架”。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500458 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4192-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4192" width="399" b="266" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500459 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4193-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4193" width="399" b="266" /></p>
<p>2、取1张A4的丹迪纸或其他色彩纸，运用直尺与铅笔在纸上画出笔筒的高度，一端多出约5公分（笔筒上端）、另一端约多2公分（笔筒底端），这样可以将卫生纸卷包起来“换新装”，再分别用剪刀剪出约1公分宽的纸条段。若教导高年级小朋友制作，可让他们了解纸张剪成细条状就容易折、卷黏的包住圆柱状。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500470 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4198-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4198" width="398" b="265" /></p>
<p><img class="wp-image-9500466 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4201-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4201" width="398" b="265" /></p>
<p>3、在卫生纸卷筒外围涂上一层薄薄的白胶，将笔筒“躺”在步骤2剪好的纸张上，将笔筒黏卷起来，黏卷起笔筒后多余的纸张再涂上一些白胶，重叠地卷着纸筒，约等一下，在白胶比较干、卷黏的纸张较固定时再放开手，让它自然阴干。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500497 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4202-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4202" width="398" b="265" /></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>4、分别将笔筒上端内侧与底部涂上一层薄薄的白胶，一一将细纸条折黏好。黏好后待干备用。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500498 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4207-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4207" width="399" b="266" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500500 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4213-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4213" width="399" b="266" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500499 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4212-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4212" width="399" b="266" /></p>
<p>5、取1张卫生纸对折几次后卷起成一长条，再分别将两端往中间弯曲、互相缠绕卷成像甜甜圈的圆形，当作黏土玫瑰花的晾干架。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500476 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/1-58-450x338-450x338.jpg" alt="1-58-450x338" width="399" b="300" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500477 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/3-12-450x338-450x338.jpg" alt="3-12-450x338" width="398" b="299" /></p>
<p>6、取1小块约2立方公分的超轻土，在手指间充分揉合后，再于掌心上将土团搓成圆球→水滴状→用食指指腹压扁，即是花瓣。每片花瓣的基本做法都是这样。第1层用圆锥状的花芯或是花瓣卷起；第2层用3片花瓣，印掌纹的那面朝内侧卷黏花朵（黏土在新鲜水分足够时也就是很柔软的状态下，可直接黏合）；第3层的黏土团要比第2层的稍大一些，第3层以后的花瓣都是印着掌纹的那面朝外侧卷黏花朵，这样花瓣很像真的，仿佛上面有着花瓣的脉络，形状也会比较好看；其余依此类推，这样花朵才会比较漂亮逼真。</p>
<p>因为做成装饰笔筒的花朵，所以不适合太大朵，若要做成胸针或是其他的配件，可以继续做到第5层以上。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500480 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_3888-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_3888" width="398" b="265" /></p>
<figure id="attachment_9500481" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9500481 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_3889-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_3889" width="399" b="266" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">水滴状</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9500482" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9500482 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_3890-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_3890" width="398" b="265" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">手指接触面</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9500483" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9500483 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_3891-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_3891" width="398" b="265" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">掌心接触面</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9500479" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9500479 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_3885-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_3885" width="398" b="265" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">圆锥状花芯</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9500484" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9500484 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_3911-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_3911" width="399" b="266" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">花瓣卷起的花芯</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9500485" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-9500485 " src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_3913-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_3913" width="398" b="265" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">完成第1层花瓣的小玫瑰花朵</figcaption></figure>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500486 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_3914-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_3914" width="395" b="263" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500487 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_3918-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_3918" width="399" b="266" /></p>
<p>7、每卷黏好一片花瓣就要用拇指指腹侧边将花瓣边缘稍微往外侧拨整，使其看起来生动一些。</p>
<p>8、取一些调好的绿色黏土，也是搓揉成瘦瘦的水滴状→压扁成小托叶，每朵花做出3片花托的小绿叶，分别黏于花朵最外层适当位置（通常是花瓣交接处）上。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500489 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4022-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4022" width="396" b="264" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500490 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4023-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4023" width="399" b="266" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500488 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_3920-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_3920" width="399" b="266" /></p>
<p>9、<strong>做绿叶：</strong>取绿色黏土，土球要比小托叶大一点，也是搓揉成水滴状→压扁→置于小指侧的手掌心侧边上用牙签画出叶脉，这样做出的叶片会有自然的曲线形动态感，完成后再调整叶片姿态。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500491 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4024-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4024" width="398" b="265" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500493 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4025-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4025" width="398" b="265" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500495 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4026-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4026" width="396" b="264" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500496 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4027-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4027" width="399" b="266" /></p>
<p>10、等隔天后再将玫瑰花朵一一用白胶黏在笔筒周围，依着花朵黏的位置不同而用美工刀将花朵底部切去多余部分。</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500474 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4214-450x336.jpg" alt="IMG_4214" width="398" b="297" /></p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-9500475 aligncenter" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/08/IMG_4240-450x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4240" width="398" b="265" /></p>
<p>11、剪出一个爱心，画上父亲高高把孩子举起的剪影，写上心里想对爸爸说的话语，贴于笔筒上，放在爸爸书桌上，他一定很高兴。</p>
