  <p class="p1"><span class="s1">据都门（</span><span class="s2">Domain</span><span class="s1">）新闻报导，<ahref="/gb/tag/%E5%A2%A8%E5%B0%94%E6%9C%AC.md#1">墨尔本</a>住宅市场</span><span class="s2">2017</span><span class="s1">年的拍卖活动刚进行了三周，拍卖结果维持在积极水平，市场行情持续对卖方有利。春季通常是<ahref="/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%A7.md#1">房地产</a>的热卖季节，墨尔本住宅市场去年春季的平均拍卖成交率为</span><span class="s2">79.6%</span><span class="s1">。从目前拍卖行情来看，今年秋季的拍卖成交率将维持在相似水平。</span></p>
  <p class="p1"><span class="s3">通常，</span><span class="s4">2</span><span class="s3">月份卖方的势头会日益强劲。</span><span class="s1">上周六挂牌拍卖的住宅总数达到</span><span class="s2">469</span><span class="s1">栋，明显多于一周前的挂牌拍卖总数（</span><span class="s5">192</span><span class="s1">栋）。</span><span class="s3">拍卖住宅总数增加意味着区域分布扩大，其中，</span><span class="s1">内城区拥有较大份额，而且高价住宅所占比例增大。这种趋势在未来几周将继续加强。</span></p>
  <p class="p1"><span class="s1">在</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">日的拍卖市场上，东北区继续维持强劲的拍卖行情，其成交率达到</span><span class="s2">91.4%</span><span class="s1">，这与该区去年的热卖行情类似；北区取得了</span><span class="s2">87.5%</span><span class="s1">的拍卖成交率；内城区东部的成交率为</span><span class="s2">86.4%</span><span class="s1">；西区不仅取得了</span><span class="s2">80.3%</span><span class="s1">的成交率，而且取得了最高销售数（</span><span class="s2">61</span><span class="s1">栋）；东部远郊地区的拍卖成交率为</span><span class="s2">76.6%</span><span class="s1">；内城区南部取得</span><span class="s2">76.4%</span><span class="s1">的成交率；东南区的成交率为</span><span class="s2">72.4%</span><span class="s1">；内城区为</span><span class="s2">67.6%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
  <p class="p1"><span class="s1">比较引人瞩目的拍卖结果如下：</span></p>
  <p class="p1"><span class="s6">? Beaumaris</span><span class="s7">区一栋</span><span class="s1">3</span><span class="s7">居室住宅（<span class="s2">7 Cloris?Ave</span>）</span><span class="s1">: 191</span><span class="s7">万澳元</span></p>
  <p class="p1"><span class="s8">? </span><span class="s1">Kew</span><span class="s7">区一栋</span><span class="s1">3</span><span class="s7">居室住宅（</span><span class="s1">47 Brougham?St</span><span class="s7">）</span><span class="s1">: 166.8</span><span class="s7">万澳元</span></p>
  <p class="p1"><span class="s6">? Wantirna?</span><span class="s1">South</span><span class="s7">区一栋</span><span class="s1">4</span><span class="s7">居室住宅（<span class="s2">3 Kirrum?Cl</span></span><span class="s1">ose</span><span class="s7">）</span><span class="s1">: 166</span><span class="s7">万澳元</span></p>
  <p class="p1"><span class="s6">? Strathmore</span><span class="s7">区一栋</span><span class="s1">4</span><span class="s7">居室住宅（<span class="s2">69 Lloyd?St</span>）</span><span class="s1">: 163</span><span class="s7">万澳元</span></p>
  <p class="p1"><span class="s6">? Brighton</span><span class="s1">?East</span><span class="s7">区一栋</span><span class="s1">4</span><span class="s7">居室住宅（<span class="s2">4 Little Union</span><span class="s2"> St</span>）</span><span class="s1">: 163</span><span class="s7">万澳元</span></p>
  <p class="p2"><span class="s7">售价最高的是</span><span class="s1">Brighton East</span><span class="s7">区一栋</span><span class="s1">4</span><span class="s7">居室住宅（</span><span class="s1">200 South Rd</span><span class="s7">），其成交价高达</span><span class="s1">192</span><span class="s7">万澳元﹔售价最低的是</span><span class="s1">West Footscray</span><span class="s7">区的</span><span class="s1">1</span><span class="s7">居室单元房（</span><span class="s1">8/21 Hampton Parade</span><span class="s7">），其成交价为</span><span class="s1">24.1</span><span class="s7">万澳元。</span></p>
  <p class="p1"><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">日，墨尔本拍卖成交总额为</span><span class="s2">1.8422</span><span class="s1">亿澳元，已拍卖成交住宅的价格中位数为</span><span class="s2">75.25</span><span class="s1">万澳元，高于一周前的价格中位数（</span><span class="s2">65.25</span><span class="s1">万澳元），比一年前同期的价格中位数（</span><span class="s2">71</span><span class="s1">万澳元）高</span><span class="s2">6%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
  <p class="p1"><span class="s1">本周六（</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">18</span><span class="s1">日）的挂牌拍卖总数将达到近</span><span class="s2">900</span><span class="s1">栋，</span><span class="s3">卖方将面对更激烈的竞争</span><span class="s1">，而买方将有更多的选择。</span></p>
  <p class="p3"><span class="s1">澳洲储备银行（</span><span class="s2">RBA</span><span class="s1">）上周召开了今年第一次董事会议，决定维持基准利率不变。此举与此前的市场预期一致。但近期降息的可能性仍存在，除非澳洲经济出现明显改善。</span></p>
  <p class="p5">近期公布的经济数据不容乐观，失业率在<span class="s9">12</span>月份达到<span class="s9">5.8%</span>，这是过去一年内最高的失业率。而且，<span class="s9">12</span>月份的零售业销售额也有所下降。</p>
