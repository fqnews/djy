  <p>历经过去两年持续的联系与作业﹐大华府地区四大社团﹕老挝(寮国)民主社团﹐自由柬埔寨社团﹐自由中国社团﹐及大华府越南社团﹐在2006年7月底曾在维州Papaya 餐厅召开会议﹐讨论共产主义在亚洲的罪行和在中国风起云涌的退党大潮。会议发表正式公报﹐宣布成立大华府地区种族社团民主大联盟 (Alliance for Democracy of Ethnic Communities﹐ADEC )﹐为大华府各种族社团之间寻找出共同合作与相互支持的方法﹐一同见证中共的解体﹐迎接一个没有共产党的新亚洲。</p>
  <p>此次华府集会﹐种族社团大联盟(ADEC) 召集人兼越南代表﹑同时也是全美越南美裔社区同乡会总会长、阮文坛(Tan V. Nguyen) 先生率领各族裔代表参加并发言﹐以下是根据录音翻译的演讲全文。<br />_________________________________________________________________________________________________</p>

  <p>今天﹐才短短八个月后 ﹐我们再次前来参加你们声援1500万退出中共的勇士和庆祝九评传播两周年的集会。我以总会长的身份代表全美国30个大城市的越南美裔社区同乡会﹐为你们难以置信的成功而祝贺。</p>
  <p>我们非常相信退出中国共产党的人数会持续而快速的增加﹐直到所有的中共党员都退出了共产党组织。我们相信到那一天﹐中国老百姓为民主、自由、人权与平等而奋斗和等待的惊天胜利就一定会来临。</p>
  <p>女士先生们﹗在和你们自由中国社团同样的目标下﹐我们自由寮国社团﹐自由柬埔寨社团﹐以及大华府自由越南社团和你们结盟在一起﹐共同对抗共产党﹐为我们分别国家的民主、自由、人权与平等共同奋斗而努力。</p>
  <p>我们2006年7月在华盛顿DC和你们一起成立了大华府地区种族社团民主大联盟(ADEC) ﹐我们希望大家密切的合作﹐并且尽全力为仍然在共产党压迫下受苦难的国家及人民﹐争取自由捍卫人权推动民主。</p>
  <p>我们ADEC大联盟提出来一起筹备组织的第一个值得纪念的事情﹐就是支持自由寮国社团在2006年12月2日首都华盛顿共产寮国大使馆前的抗议示威集会。这个集会是为了纪念前自由寮国沦陷于共产党32周年﹐并大家团结一起为寮国人民争取自由和民主。我希望自由中国社团﹐自由柬埔寨社团﹐及自由越南社团的每一个人﹐都能来支持自由寮国社团﹐参加这个值得纪念的集会,使集会能够举办成功。</p>

  <p>Ladies and Gentlemen,</p>
  <p>In early March this year, the Vietnamese-American community of Washington DC, Maryland, and Virginia joined you at the Lincoln Memorial to show our respect and support to the 9 millions courageous Chinese people who quit the Chinese Communist Party (CCP).</p>
  <p>Eight months later, today, we join your public rally again.The number now has already exceeded 15 millions.On behalf of Vietnamese-American Communities in the United States, I, as the President of our organizations, would like to congratulate your fabulous success.</p>
  <p>We are sure that this wave of Chinese people quitting the CCP will rise continuously and rapidly until all members of CCP have quit their party association.And by that day, the fantastic victory for all Chinese people who have fought for democracy, liberty, human rights, and equality will arrive.</p>
  <p>Ladies and Gentlemen!Also under the very same purpose of the community of Free China, our communities of Free Laos, Free Cambodia, and Free Vietnam in Washington DC are joining you to show the solidarity, and work together to fight against the communist regimes for the democracy, liberty, human rights, and equality for our respective nations.</p>
  <p>With you all, we established the Alliance for Democracy of Ethnic Communities (ADEC) in Washington D.C. in July 2006.We hope to work closely together and with our collective competence, let’s fight for the democracy and freedom for the countries and the people that are still under the oppression of the Communist party.</p>
  <p>The first memorable event that our Alliance (ADEC) would like to organize is to assist the Free Laos community to hold a demonstration in front of Laos Communist Embassy of Washington DC on December 2nd, 2006. This event is to commemorate the 32 years of the fall of freedom in Laos under the Communist regime, and to raise the awareness of the freedom and democracy for Laotians.I hope that everyone in Chinese, Cambodian, and Vietnamese communities will join the Laos community and witness this memorable event and to make it a success.</p>
  <p>Before leaving I would like to speak loudly: “DOWN WITH THE COMMUNISM!”and I would like all of you to repeat after me: “DOWN! DOWN! DOWN!”</p>
