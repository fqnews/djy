  <p><b>玉米 </b>：玉米富含脂肪，其脂肪中的不饱和脂肪酸，特别是亚油酸的含量高达60%以上。有助于人体脂肪及胆固醇的正常代谢，可以减少胆固醇在<ahref="/gb/tag/%E8%A1%80%E7%AE%A1.md#1">血管</a>中的沉积，从而软化动脉血管。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_6709744" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/05/1305160318401459.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6709744 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2013/05/1305160318401459-450x473.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="473" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">玉米。(fotolia)</figcaption></figure>  <p><b> 番茄</b>：不仅各种维生素含量比苹果、梨高24倍，而且还含维生素芦丁，它可提高机体氧化能力，消除自由基等体内垃圾，保护血管弹性，有预防血栓形成的作用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_6407276" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2007/07/707040446021758.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6407276 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2007/07/707040446021758-600x394.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="394" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">番茄 (clipart.com)</figcaption></figure>  <p><b> 苹果</b>：苹果富含多糖果酸及类黄酮、钾及维生素E和C等营养成分，可使积蓄体内的脂肪分解，对推迟和预防动脉粥样硬化发作有明显作用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_8086696" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1511251042162483.jpg"><img class="wp-image-8086696 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/07/1511251042162483-600x420.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">篮子装满的红苹果（fotolia）</figcaption></figure>  <p><b>海带 </b>：海带中含有丰富的岩藻多糖、昆布素，这类物质均有类似肝素的活性，既能防止血栓又能降胆固醇、脂蛋白，抑制动脉粥样硬化。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_5770122" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1407052258002483.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-5770122" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/09/1407052258002483-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">海带（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>  <p><b> 茶叶</b>：含有茶多酚，能提高机体抗氧化能力，降低血脂，缓解血液高凝状态，增强红细胞弹性，缓解或延缓动脉粥样硬化。经常饮茶可以软化动脉血管。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_5793530" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1407241414032525.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-5793530" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2014/10/1407241414032525-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">茶叶（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>  <p><b>大蒜 </b>：含挥发性辣素，可消除积存在血管中的脂肪，有明显降脂作用，是主治高血脂症和动脉硬化的良药。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_7973426" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/67dd4241fd1ee0305e09eca0a46d8c81.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-7973426" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/06/67dd4241fd1ee0305e09eca0a46d8c81-600x418.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大蒜 (Fotolia)</figcaption></figure>  <p><b> 洋葱</b>：含有一种能使血管扩张的前列腺素A，它能舒张血管，降低血液黏度，减少血管的压力，同时洋葱还含有二烯丙基二硫化物和含硫氨基酸，可增强纤维蛋白溶解的活性，具有降血脂，抗动脉硬化的功能。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_6550925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/01/1511041613262483.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-6550925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/01/1511041613262483-600x413.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">洋葱（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>  <p><b>茄子 </b>：保护心血管、降血压，茄子含丰富的维生素P，是一种黄酮类化合物，有软化血管的作用，还可增强血管的弹性，降低毛细血管通透性，防止毛细血管破裂，对防止小血管出血有一定作用。</p>
  <figure id="attachment_6588553" style="width: 595px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/05/1205020848221778.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6588553 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2012/05/1205020848221778.jpg" alt="" width="595" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">茄子（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>  <p>文章来源: 网易健康网 南京晨报</p>
